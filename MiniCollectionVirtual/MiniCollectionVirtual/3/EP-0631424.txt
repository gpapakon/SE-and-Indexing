<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method and apparatus allowing the verification of accounting fees.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H04M1528	H04M1530	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H04M	H04M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H04M15	H04M15	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
To verify billing for a telecommunications connection, in particular a telephone or fax line, which has been prepared by the telecommunications network operator, independently of the telecommunications network operator at the subscriber end only, the electrical conditions prevailing or the operations in progress on the telephone lines of the telecommunications connection are recorded and are automatically stored in a memory (9) following automatic allocation of time data.
