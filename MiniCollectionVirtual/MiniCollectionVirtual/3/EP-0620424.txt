<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for measuring temperature and resistance thermometer for carrying out this method.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01K716	G01K718	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01K	G01K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01K7	G01K7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for measuring temperature and a resistance thermometer for carrying out this method. It is the object of the invention to make available a method and a device in which mechanical influences on the measuring resistor can be compensated. This object is achieved in that the resistance thermometer consists of at least two different materials with different temperature coefficients.
</ABSTRACT>
