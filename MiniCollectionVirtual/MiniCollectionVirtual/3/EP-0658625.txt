<INVENTION-TITLE>
Protein that can be biotinylated to be used for the germination stage determination.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C07K14415	C07K14415	C12N1529	C12N1529	C12N1582	C12N1582	C12P2108	C12P2108	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C07K	C07K	C12N	C12N	C12N	C12N	C12P	C12P	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C07K14	C07K14	C12N15	C12N15	C12N15	C12N15	C12P21	C12P21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
1. Pure protein of plant origin capable of being biotinylated. 2. It is characterised in that it is derived from the seed of a species of plant crop, in that it comprises at least one unit of 50 to 85 kDa, expressed in the seeds and in no other organ of the plant, and which disappears rapidly during the early phases of germination. 3. Use in agriculture as molecular marker for determining the stage of germination of seeds.
</ABSTRACT>
