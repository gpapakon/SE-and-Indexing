<INVENTION-TITLE>
Alcaline chlorite-based bleaching agent.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an alkaline chlorite bleach with a pH in the range from 7.5 to 10, preferably 7.5 to 9, and a chlorite:aldehyde ratio of 1 - 4:0.01 - 0.5 by weight. The invention furthermore relates to a process for bleaching textiles in which the textile product is treated with an aqueous liquor containing the chlorite bleach according to the invention at a temperature of 20 - 80 DEG C, preferably 40 - 60 DEG C.
</ABSTRACT>
