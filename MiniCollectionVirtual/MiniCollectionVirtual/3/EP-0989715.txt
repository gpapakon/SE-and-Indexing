<INVENTION-TITLE>
Calling number as access authorization to a telephone terminal
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H04M157	H04M157	H04M165	H04M1652	H04M166	H04M1665	H04M172	H04M1725	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H04M	H04M	H04M	H04M	H04M	H04M	H04M	H04M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H04M1	H04M1	H04M1	H04M1	H04M1	H04M1	H04M1	H04M1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The communications peripheral, e.g. a combined telephone and answering machine, incorporates access authentication for an incoming call by comparison of the caller number with at least one given number. The access authentication may be used for permitting the caller access to a telephone answering machine, a call diversion device, or an alarm device combined with the peripheral, e.g. for remote interrogation or on-off switching.
</ABSTRACT>
