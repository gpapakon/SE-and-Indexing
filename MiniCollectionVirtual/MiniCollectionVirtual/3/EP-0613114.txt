<INVENTION-TITLE>
Adhesive-free display means.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G09F1500	G09F1502	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G09F	G09F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G09F15	G09F15	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an aqueous mixture for wetting a display on paper comprising a thickener and a film-forming agent. It also relates to a support panel for a poster wetted with the aqueous mixture. Finally the invention relates to a method of placing a paper poster on a support panel without adding glue in situ.
</ABSTRACT>
