<INVENTION-TITLE>
Light stabilized polymer compositions.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08J518	C08J518	C08K500	C08K500	C08K53435	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08J	C08J	C08K	C08K	C08K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C08J5	C08J5	C08K5	C08K5	C08K5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to light-stabilised polymer compositions based on low-density polyethylene or an ethylene-vinyl acetate copolymer which contain, as stabiliser, at least one polymeric, sterically hindered amine and a compound from the benzotriazole class. The compositions are preferably suitable for the production of sheeting which is used in agriculture and whose resistance to sulphur, fungicides and the action of light is improved.
</ABSTRACT>
