<CLAIMS>
A process for continuously dyeing yarns with 
vattable dyes, which comprises, in continuous yarn dyeing 

ranges, eg. indigo dyeing ranges, metering an aqueous dye 
dispersion into the circulating dyeing liquor. 
A process as claimed in claim 1, wherein the dyes 
used have a vatting half-life of ≤ 20, preferably ≤ 5, 

minutes. 
A process as claimed in claim 1, wherein the dye 
is added in a readily vattable form. 
A process as claimed in claim 1, wherein the 
reducing agents used are sodium dithionite, thiourea 

dioxide, organic reducing agents or mixtures thereof. 
</CLAIMS>
