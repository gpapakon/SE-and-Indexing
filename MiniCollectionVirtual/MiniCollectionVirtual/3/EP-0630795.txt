Entry.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Entry, in particular in the side wall, of a track-bound coach for passenger transport, there being a difference in level between the floor surface (3) of the said coach and a perron (2) for the passenger, and a detachable hand rail (4) being provided which is arranged transversely with respect to the floor surface (3) and is fixed at the level of, in particular on the floor surface (3), and the entry being capable of being closed and opened with one or more doors, and the hand rail (4) being coupled in the region of one end and being capable of being fixed in the region of the other end in a detachable fashion in at least one position in which the hand rail (4) is swung into the entry (A).
