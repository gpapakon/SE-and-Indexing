<INVENTION-TITLE>
FUNGICIDAL TRIAZOLYLETHANOL DERIVATES.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A01N4364	A01N43653	C07D24900	C07D24908	C07D40500	C07D40506	C07D52100	C07D52100	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A01N	A01N	C07D	C07D	C07D	C07D	C07D	C07D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A01N43	A01N43	C07D249	C07D249	C07D405	C07D405	C07D521	C07D521	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
New triazolylethanol derivates have the formula (I), in which a stands for a ketone group or for a CH(OH) grouping; R stands for halogen alkyl or for halogen alkenyl and R1 stands for a residue having the formula (a), in which X stands for halogen, alkyl with 1 to 4 carbon atoms, halogen alkyl with 1 to 4 carbon atoms and 1 to 5 halogen atoms, alkoxy with 1 to 4 carbon atoms, halogen alkoxy with 1 to 4 carbon atoms and 1 to 5 halogen atoms, alkylthio with 1 to 4 carbon atoms, halogen alkylthio with 1 to 4 carbon atoms and 1 to 5 halogen atoms, cycloalkyl with 3 to 7 carbon atoms, cyano, possibly halogen-substituted phenyl or possibly halogen-substituted phenoxy, and m equals 0, 1, 2 or 3, or R1 stands for a residue having the formulas (b) or (c), in which X1, X2, X3 and X4 are the same or different and stand for hydrogen or halogen. Also disclosed are the acid addition salts and metal complexes of these derivates, a process for preparing these new substances and their use as fungicides.
</ABSTRACT>
