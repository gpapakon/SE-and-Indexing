<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for germ counting in the clinical field.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61L226	A61L228	G01N2147	G01N2153	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61L	A61L	G01N	G01N	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61L2	A61L2	G01N21	G01N21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a device for germ counting in the clinical field, in particular for operating theatres (1), for monitoring the germ content of the air in a room (1), having a measurement volume (15), to which air, to be monitored for germs, from the room (1) can be fed, a positionally mobile sensor arrangement (2) which detects the number of particles present in the air, and an evaluation unit (6) which determines the germ count from the detected number of particles.
