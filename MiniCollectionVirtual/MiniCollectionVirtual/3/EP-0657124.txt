<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a chair with a seat having a seat plate (2), a back (3) and an underframe (1). The underframe (1) is connected to the front end portion (2a) of the seat (2) via an elongated hole/peg guide, which is formed by a vertically upwardly extending peg (5) and an approximately horizontally extending elongate hole (6) cooperating therewith, as a result of which a particularly simple assembly of seat plate and underframe is achieved.
