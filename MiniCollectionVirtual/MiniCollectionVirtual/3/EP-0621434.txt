<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for renovating a non-accessible pipeline.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F16L55162	F16L551645	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F16L	F16L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F16L55	F16L55	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In the case of an apparatus for renovating with filler a pipeline (40) which is not accessible by foot, a manipulator (2), which contains a filling device (12) and can move in the pipeline (40), carries an exchangeable storage container (20) for the filler. According to the invention, the storage container (20) is arranged in a self-supporting manner at least indirectly on an end side of the manipulator (2). This measure reduces the overall length of the manipulator (2) and ensures that it can be easily introduced into the pipeline even in the case of constricted spatial conditions.
