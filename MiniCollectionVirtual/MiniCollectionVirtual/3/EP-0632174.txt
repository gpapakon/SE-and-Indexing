<INVENTION-TITLE>
Structure for connecting an aluminium mast onto a cast metal base
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E01F9018	E01F9011	E04H1222	E01F9011	E04H1222	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
Structure for connecting an aluminium post of a lamp post to a base-piece, characterised in that the base-piece (2) is made of SG cast iron and in that it is in the form of a flange (clamp) (4) mechanically gripping the base of the post, a heat-shrinkable sleeve (3) being interposed between the flange and the post over their entire area of mutual contact.

<CLAIMS>
Structure for connecting a lamp post formed from aluminium to the
ground, comprising a footing having one portion in the form of a clamp (4)

intended to grip the base of the post mechanically, characterised in that the
footing (2) is formed from GS cast iron, and in that the connecting

structure also comprises a heat-retractable sleeve (3) intended to be
interposed between the clamp (4) and the post (1) over the entirety of their

mutual area of contact.
Connecting structure according to claim 1, characterised in that the
clamp is an open clamp (4), co-operating with clamping screws (5, 6).
Connecting structure according to one of claims 1 to 2,
characterised in that the footing is galvanised.
</CLAIMS>
