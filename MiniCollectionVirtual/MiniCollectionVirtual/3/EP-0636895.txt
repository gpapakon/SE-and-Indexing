<INVENTION-TITLE>
Test grid for tester of unpopulated printed circuits.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01R3128	G01R3128	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01R	G01R	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a test grid for an unpopulated printed circuit, comprising test points (T) linked to measurement circuits. Each measurement circuit is linked to a group (G) of test points, the test points of each group being linked together by resistors (r) of non-zero values.
