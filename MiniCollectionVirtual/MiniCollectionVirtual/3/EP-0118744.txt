<INVENTION-TITLE>
LIGHTWEIGHT CONSTRUCTION ELEMENT AND PROCESS FOR MAKING IT
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C04B2800	C04B2832	C04B2834	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C04B	C04B	C04B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The lightweight construction material consists of expanded perlite as aggregate and a binder skeleton of Sorel cement or aluminium phosphate, foam structures and/or expanded pore structures being present in the binder skeleton. A process for producing the novel lightweight construction material is included.
