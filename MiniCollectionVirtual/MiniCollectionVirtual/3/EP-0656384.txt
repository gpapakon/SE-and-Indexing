<INVENTION-TITLE>
Polymer systems, method of producing same and their application in inks.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Amino-containing polymer systems which are suitable for the preparation of binders (vehicles) for printing inks can be obtained by synthesising a polymer or polymer mixture A from at least one compound from each of the following groups:     (a) epoxides, carbonates and epoxide carbonates,   (b) amines and   (c) phenols, and at least partially neutralising the resultant polymer or polymer mixture A and transferring it into water, and optionally preparing a water-insoluble polymer B by polymerisation of ethylenically unsaturated monomers in the presence of the polymer or polymer mixture A.
</ABSTRACT>
