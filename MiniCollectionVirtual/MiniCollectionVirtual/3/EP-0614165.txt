<INVENTION-TITLE>
Circuit for generating an analog output signal.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G09G320	G09G320	G09G336	G09G336	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G09G	G09G	G09G	G09G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G09G3	G09G3	G09G3	G09G3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A circuit arrangement for generating an analogue output signal from a digital data word and a method for driving data lines of a liquid-crystal display screen by means of such a circuit arrangement. In order to permit a liquid-crystal display screen to be driven with a high number of grey level values present in digital N-bit data words, the circuit arrangement has a signal memory (12) with N memory elements (C0, C1, ..., C(N-1)) and N switch elements (SP0, SP1, ..., SP(N-1)) as well as a digital/analogue converter (13).
