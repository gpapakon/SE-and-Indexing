Articles of foamed plastic material.

<ABSTRACT>
Article made of foamed (cellular) plastic, produced from a foamed plastic preform by thermoforming and compression of certain parts (10, 12, 14) of the preform to different degrees in order to form, in particular, sealing lips (10), a guide bearing (12) and lubricating bearing bushes (14).
