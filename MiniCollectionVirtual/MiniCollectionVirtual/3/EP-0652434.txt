<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for testing the thermal insulation of bodies, particularly thermally insulating bodies.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01J500	G01J500	G01N2518	G01N2518	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01J	G01J	G01N	G01N	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01J5	G01J5	G01N25	G01N25	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The quality of thermally insulating bodies is tested by determining the thermal relaxation behaviour at the thermally insulating body by means of contactless temperature measurement.
