<INVENTION-TITLE>
COFFEE CAPSULES AND PROCESS FOR THEIR MANUFACTURE
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A23F548	A23F524	B01J1302	A23F546	A23F538	B01J1302	A23F546	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A23F	A23F	B01J	A23F	A23F	B01J	A23F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A23F5	A23F5	B01J13	A23F5	A23F5	B01J13	A23F5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Coffee capsules are described, comprising a hardened 
shell of coffee and/or coffee substitute solids surrounding 

a droplet of coffee oil containing coffee aroma. 
They are made by contacting particles of an emulsion 
of coffee oil and aromatic coffee distillate with particles of 

instant coffee and/or coffee substitute powder. The capsules 
are added to instant coffee or an instant-coffee substitute. 
</ABSTRACT>
