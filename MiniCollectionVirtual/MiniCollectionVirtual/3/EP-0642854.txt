Machine tool with a control device for a functional part.

<ABSTRACT>
On a machine tool, in particular one for bending, punching and embossing, an adjusting device is provided; this adjusting device (20) is intended to correct the position of a functional component (22) of the machine tool relative to a functional-component carrier (10) in at least one position correction direction during the running of the machine. In this arrangement, the adjusting device (20) is designed with a stepper drive (42).
