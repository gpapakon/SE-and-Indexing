<INVENTION-TITLE>
Connection device for a cleaning trolley
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47L1310	A47L13512	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47L	A47L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47L13	A47L13	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The connector (3) comprises a holding element (6) and at least two opposing detent elements (5) which can be operated by sliders (4) and are designed to fit into the grooves (2) on the cleaning trolley. In the installed state the holding element is aligned vertical. The detent elements are pretensioned in their detent position through spring tension elements. Independent claim descries support system for trolley having a connector and a support profile (1) with opposing grooves.
</ABSTRACT>
