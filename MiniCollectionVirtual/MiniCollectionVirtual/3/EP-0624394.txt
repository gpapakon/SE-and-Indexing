<INVENTION-TITLE>
Mixer.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B28C500	B28C512	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B28C	B28C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B28C5	B28C5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A mixer is proposed, in particular a mobile mixer, for mixing at least two solids with a liquid, maintaining a flowable, preferably pumpable mixture, in particular a concrete mortar, having a mixing and conveying screw (4) preferably concentrically rotating in a shell, a solids introduction section, mixing section and conveying section being provided with a solids feed arranged above this and subsequently thereto a liquid feed section, mixing section and conveying section, in which the shell has at least one liquid throughway (14) for introducing liquid into the shell interior, which mixer is especially characterised in that each solids feed has, per solid, one feed hopper having an independently controlled metering device.
