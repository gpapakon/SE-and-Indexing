<INVENTION-TITLE>
DEVICE FOR GUIDING A PUNCTURE DEVICE AND ITS USE WITH A HAND-HELD APPLIANCE FOR LOCATING BLOOD VESSELS.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61B806	A61B806	A61B808	A61B808	A61B1734	A61B1734	A61M531	A61M531	A61M2502	A61M2502	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61B	A61B	A61B	A61B	A61B	A61B	A61M	A61M	A61M	A61M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61B8	A61B8	A61B8	A61B8	A61B17	A61B17	A61M5	A61M5	A61M25	A61M25	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A device (40) useful for carrying a puncture device (20) has a detector (10) for determining the location and/or the type of a blood vessel under the skin surface. An evaluation unit whose results are supplied to a display is connected to the detector (10). As soon as the display shows the appropriate position of the guiding device for carrying out the puncture, the puncture device is guided into the blood vessel to be punctured.
</ABSTRACT>
