<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an arrangement for reducing the moisture content of sludge, characterised by a spray-dryer (8) which can admit the sludge, a gaseous or vaporous atomising agent and hot gas, a separator unit (19) having a solids discharge (21) arranged downstream of the spray-dryer (8) by means of a transfer line (18) and a heater (31) bringing the gas arising in the separator unit (19) to a higher temperature, which heater is in a connection line (16, 30) between the separator unit (19) and the spray-dryer (8).
