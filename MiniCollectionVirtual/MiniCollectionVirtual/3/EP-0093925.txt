Azolyl butynes, process for their preparation and their use as fungicides and plant growth regulators.

<ABSTRACT>
The compounds of the general formula 
<
IMAGE
>
  which have the meaning given in the description, are obtained by reacting, for example, carbinols with azolides of polybasic inorganic acids and, if desired, subsequently reacting the azolylbutyne derivatives additionally with alkali metal hypohalite. The active compounds are suitable for use as plant protection agents and can be used particularly successfully for combating cereal diseases, rice diseases, Sphaerotheca species and Phytophora species. The compounds furthermore have plant growth-regulating properties.
