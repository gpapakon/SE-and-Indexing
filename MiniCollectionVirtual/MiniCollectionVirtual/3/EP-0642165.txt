<INVENTION-TITLE>
Hybrid frame.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H01L2302	H01L23057	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H01L	H01L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The subject-matter of the invention is a plastic hybrid frame having electrical connection elements (2). The elements (2) have contact lugs (3; 4) which are shaped substantially at right angles to one another and with a bend region in between the contact lugs (3; 4). The bend region is enclosed by the frame. At least one straight section (6a; 6b) is provided between the contact lugs (3; 4) and in the bend region. A reinforcement (9) in the form of an embossment is preferably provided on the section (6a) located closer to the internal space of the frame.
