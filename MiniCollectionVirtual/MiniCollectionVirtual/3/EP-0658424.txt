<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for manufacturing blanks of identity cards.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The subject of the invention is a method for manufacturing blanks of identity cards, in particular chip cards (smart cards), in which method the prepunched film for receiving identification or memory elements, for example, is laminated with further film layers, using a tool receiving the punching form (matrix).
