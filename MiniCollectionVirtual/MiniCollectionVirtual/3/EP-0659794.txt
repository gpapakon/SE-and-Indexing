<INVENTION-TITLE>
Bath and electrolytic method for coating an oxidizable metallic surface with polypyrrole by electropolymerization
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C25D1304	C25D1308	C25B300	C25B300	C08G6112	C08G6100	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C25D	C25D	C25B	C25B	C08G	C08G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
Process for the deposition of an adherent thin coating of polypyrrole on the surface of an article made of oxidisable metal by electropolymerisation of an aqueous or hydroalcoholic electrolytic solution of pyrrole and/or of one or a number of substituted pyrroles, where the anode of the electropolymerisation device consists of the said article made of oxidisable metal, characterised in that the said solution contains ions chosen from the ions of formula:
