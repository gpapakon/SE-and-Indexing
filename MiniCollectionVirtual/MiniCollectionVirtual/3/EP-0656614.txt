<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for confirming the authenticity or the absence of tampering with an object such as a document or a package.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G09F302	G09F302	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G09F	G09F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G09F3	G09F3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device for confirming the authenticity or the absence of tampering with an object such as a document or a package. It includes   - a base sheet (5) intended to be stuck by one of its faces to the object, - a flexible and transparent flap (6), integral with the base sheet (5) and capable of covering over the latter, the face of the flap (6) able to come into contact with the base sheet (5) being covered by an adhesive product, and - a removable protective film applied to the face of the flap (6) which is covered with the adhesive product.
