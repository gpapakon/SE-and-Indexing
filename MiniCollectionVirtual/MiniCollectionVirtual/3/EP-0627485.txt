<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the removal of impurities present in food products obtained by fermentation.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C12C500	C12C500	C12C502	C12C1200	C12C1204	C12G300	C12G308	C12H100	C12H104	C12H300	C12H300	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C12C	C12C	C12C	C12C	C12C	C12G	C12G	C12H	C12H	C12H	C12H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C12C5	C12C5	C12C5	C12C12	C12C12	C12G3	C12G3	C12H1	C12H1	C12H3	C12H3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In a process for the removal of undesirable constituents present in foodstuffs and/or confectionery obtainable by fermentation, while preserving the aroma spectrum of odorous substances and flavours, determining the value, in ready-to-consume foodstuffs and/or confectionery,   - either the odorous substances and/or flavours, determining the value, are removed from the foodstuff and/or confectionery to be treated, the undesirable constituent is or constituents are then removed, the odorous substances and/or flavours, determining the value, which have been removed from the foodstuff and/or confectionery are taken up in solvents which per se are bothersome, and added again to the foodstuff and/or confectionery, - or impurities are removed, the odorous substances and/or flavours, determining the value which have thus also been removed being separated off, taken up in solvents which per se are bothersome, and being added again to the depleted foodstuff and/or confectionery.
</ABSTRACT>
