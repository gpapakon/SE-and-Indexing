<INVENTION-TITLE>
Microdevice for measuring the electromagnetic characteristics of a medium, and utilisation of that microdevice.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01N2772	G01N2772	G01R2720	G01R2720	G01R3316	G01R3316	G01V318	G01V328	G01V330	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
Micro device for measurements of electromagnetic characteristics of a medium, including at least one transmitter coil (31), at least one receiver coil (41, 51), means for generating an AC voltage across the terminals of the transmitter coil or coils, and means for measuring a signal across the terminals of the receiver coil or coils. It furthermore comprises a circuit of soft and electrically insulating magnetic material which serves to support each of the coils and which is open to the medium to be measured.
