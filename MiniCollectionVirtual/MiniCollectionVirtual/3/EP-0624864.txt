<INVENTION-TITLE>
ROTARY JOINT WITH CLAMPING DEVICE.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F16M1102	F16M1106	F16M1120	F16M1122	F16M1138	G10D1300	G10D1300	G10D1302	G10G500	G10G500	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
A rotary joint (4) for an essentially bar-like article (3) such as a stand, a stand leg or a similar support or holder for devices, in particular for percussion musical instruments, with two joint parts (7, 10) which are movable relative to one another and can be fixed by a clamping device, can be used in a more variable manner if the clamping device is designed for simultaneous clamping of the bar-like article (3).
