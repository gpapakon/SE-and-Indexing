<INVENTION-TITLE>
Display hoarding, handling device 
&
 dispensing device for placards or parts thereof and a complete placard changing system using such.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G09F1304	G09F1304	G09F1500	G09F1500	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G09F	G09F	G09F	G09F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G09F13	G09F13	G09F15	G09F15	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A display hoarding, having a rigid flat support (1) for a poster (2) to be mounted thereon, has a transparent film (3) arranged over the support (1) with interposition of the poster (2), which transparent film engages over the sides of the support (1) and is under tensile stress (F), said stress having a component (Fz) directed perpendicular to the support (1) from the surface of the film (3) to the surface of the support (1).
