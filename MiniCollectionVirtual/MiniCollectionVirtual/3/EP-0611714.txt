<INVENTION-TITLE>
Conveyor for transporting printed products fed regularly on a flat support formed by at least a first driven web.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65H502	B65H502	B65H2900	B65H2912	B65H2966	B65H2966	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65H	B65H	B65H	B65H	B65H	B65H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65H5	B65H5	B65H29	B65H29	B65H29	B65H29	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
For transporting flat printed products (6) which rest on a support formed by a first driven belt (4) and are moved forwards by the strand (7) of a second belt (3), arranged above it, in a conveying channel (8), the entry region (11) of the latter (8) is designed to be variable in its height.
