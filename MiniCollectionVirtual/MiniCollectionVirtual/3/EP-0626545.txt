<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for passive solar heating of buildings.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F24J200	F24J204	F24J204	F24J246	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F24J	F24J	F24J	F24J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F24J2	F24J2	F24J2	F24J2	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device for passive solar heating of buildings, consisting of a transparent heat insulation (4), absorber (5), weather protection (3) and a housing (7), the device being designed as a closed module and the absorber (5) lying against the heat insulation (4), an air gap (8) being provided between absorber and wall to be heated, and two adjustable air inlet and outlet openings (1) being provided in the housing (7).
