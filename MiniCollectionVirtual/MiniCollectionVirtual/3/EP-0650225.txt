<INVENTION-TITLE>
Casing part of an electrical connector.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H01R13436	H01R13436	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H01R	H01R	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H01R13	H01R13	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Electrical-connector casing part of the type consisting of a body (1) made of insulating material, having a series of channels (2, 20) each intended to receive a male or female electrical contact member (4, 21), one end of which is intended to interact with a complementary electrical contact member, while the other end is fastened to an electrical conductor (8, 29), the said casing part (1), on the side containing its end through which the electrical conductors (8, 28) extend including a locking member (50, 50a) having an opening for passage of the conductors and emerging onto the channels so as to lock the electrical contact members (4, 21) in the said channels.
