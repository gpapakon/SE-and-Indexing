Sanitary device for disabled persons

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a sanitary facility for disabled persons, which contains, in particular, a toilet bowl, a urinal, a washbasin (3) and a mirror (9), these being arranged on vertically adjustable lifting walls (2) which can be adjusted in height via a lifting device (6). The sanitary facility is intended to aid the disabled person to adapt the individual sanitary facilities in height, operating elements which are known per se and can be reached in an optimum manner being provided for this purpose.

<CLAIMS>
Sanitary installation for disabled persons, comprising
a wash basin (3) and at least one further sanitary item such

as a toilet pan, a urinal (10) or the like, wherein the
sanitary items (3,10) are jointly attached to a vertically

adjustable lifting wall (2) and provided with flexible in
and outlet pipes (4), the vertically adjustable wash basin

(3) being tiltably arranged.
Sanitary installation for disabled persons according to
claim 1, with a toilet pan, characterised in that the toilet

pan is provided with a device for automatic placing and
removing of protective layers of paper.
Sanitary installation for disabled persons according to
claim 1 or 2, characterised in that a mirror (9) is attached

to the lifting wall (2).
Sanitary installation for disabled persons according to
claim 1, characterised in that for the vertically adjustable

lifting wall (2) there is provided a lifting motor (6)
which, via a spindle or pantograph drive, ensures

displacement of the vertically adjustable lifting wall (2)
with respect to a stationary wall.
