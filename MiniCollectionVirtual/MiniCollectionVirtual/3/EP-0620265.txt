<INVENTION-TITLE>
Fuel composition with improved low temperature flow behaviour
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C10L1197	C10L110	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C10L	C10L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
Fuel compsns. comprise a base fuel with a boiling range of 180-382 deg.C and 0.01-1 wt.% of an ethylene/vinyl acetate (EVA) copolymer having a vinyl acetate content of 25-35 wt.%, a wt.-average molecular wt. (Mw) of 5000-15,000 and an asymmetric molecular wt. distribution such that (Mw/Mn) is greaterthan (Mz/Mw). (Mw/Mn) and (Mz/Mw) have values of 3-4 and 1.3-2 respectively, as determined by gel permeation chromatography.
</ABSTRACT>
