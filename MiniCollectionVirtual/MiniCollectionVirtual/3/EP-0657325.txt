<ABSTRACT>
It is characterised in that the bottom (3) of the said loudspeaker mount is provided with cavities (4) that are open at one of their ends (5) and are provided with at least one opening (7) at the other, opposite end (6). Application in particular to improving the acoustics of loudspeakers in motor vehicles.

<CLAIMS>
Preformed sealing panel (1) for automobiles 
incorporating a loudspeaker housing (2) characterised in 

that the bottom (3) of said loudspeaker housing is provided 
with cells (4) open at one end (5) and having at least 

one opening (7) at the opposite end (6), the height of 
the cells being between 3 mm and 25 mm. 
Sealing panel according to claim 1 
characterised in that the axis (8) of each cell (4) is 

inclined to the perpendicular (9) to the bottom (3) of 
said housing. 
Sealing panel according to claim 1 or claim 2 
characterised in that the axis (8) of the cells is 

inclined downwardly at an angle between 40° and 20° to 
the horizontal when the panel is fitted to the vehicle. 
Sealing panel according to any one of claims 1 
to 3 characterised in that the cells (4) are in a 

quincunx arrangement. 
Sealing panel according to any one of claims 1 
to 4 characterised in that each cell (4) is 

frustoconical. 
Sealing panel according to claim 5 
characterised in that the average diameter of each cell 

(4) is between 3 mm and 20 mm. 
Sealing panel according to claim 1 
characterised in that protuberances (10) are provided 

between the cells (4) on the inside face of the bottom (3) 
of the housing (2). 
Sealing panel according to claim 1 
characterised in that a reinforcing rib (11) is formed in 

the bottom (3) of the housing (2). 
Sealing panel according to any one of claims 1 
to 8 characterised in that a film (12) between 5 µm and 

30 µm thick is glued or welded to the inside face of the 
bottom (3) of the loudspeaker housing (2).  

 
Application of a sealing panel according to 
any one of claims 1 to 9 to improving the acoustics of 

loudspeakers in automobile vehicles. 
</CLAIMS>
