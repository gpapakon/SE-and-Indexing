<INVENTION-TITLE>
Standard module for vehicle dashboard
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60Q300	B60Q304	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The module for a dashboard including a front face comprises a motor (1) with which an indicator member (2) is associated. The motor comprises a flange (3) for supporting a source (5) for illuminating the indicator (3). The flange (3) extends between the body of the motor (1) and the indicator (2), the latter being illuminated directly by the source (5). The module has no light guide (conduit).

<CLAIMS>
Module for a vehicle dashboard comprising a front face, 
comprising an indicator needle (2), a motor (1) for driving the 

indicator needle through the intermediary of an output shaft (4) 
of the motor (1) and a support (3) for at least one source (5) 

for illuminating the indicator needle (2), the support (3) 
extending beneath the foot of the indicator needle (2) and the 

latter being illuminated directly by the source (5), 
characterised by the fact that said support is constituted by a 

cheek (3) supporting a bearing for the output shaft (4) of the 
motor (1). 
Module according to Claim 1, in which the cheek (3) 
comprises an illumination alveolus (7) inside which is disposed 

the source (5) for illuminating the indicator member (2). 
Module according to one of Claims 1 and 2, in which there 
is provided on the cheek (3) at least one other source (10) 

intended to illuminate a screen-printed film bearing characters 
associated with the indicator member (2). 
Module according to one of Claims 1 to 3, in which the 
cheek (3) comprises lugs (8) for indexing on the front face. 
Module according to Claim 4, in which electrical 
connection areas (9) are disposed on the indexing logs (8). 
Module according to one of Claims 1 to 5, in which the 
indicator needle (2) is illuminated by a two-coloured source. 
Module according to one of Claims 1 to 5, in which two 
sources (5) for illuminating the indicator needle (2) are 

provided. 
Module according to Claim 7, in which the two sources (5) 
for illuminating the indicator needle (2) are of different  

 
respective colours. 
Module according to Claim 8, in which the two sources (5) 
for illuminating the indicator needle (2) are supplied by signals 

having variable and complementary cyclic ratios. 
Module according to one of Claims 1 to 9, in which the 
means for controlling the drive motor (1) are mounted on the 

cheek (3). 
</CLAIMS>
