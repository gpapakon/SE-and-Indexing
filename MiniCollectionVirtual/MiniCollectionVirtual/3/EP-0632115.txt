<INVENTION-TITLE>
Binder and coating composition, its preparation and its use.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
A binder composition is described which is suitable for coating compositions and contains one or more unsaturated polyesters having an acid number of from 5 to 60 mg of KOH/g, a hydroxyl number of from 50 to 200 mg of KOH/g, a weight-average molecular weight (Mw) of from 500 to 3000 g/mol and a content of from 0.8 to 1.2 ethylenic double bonds per 100 g of polyester, which are obtainable by reacting   A) one or more alpha,beta-ethylenically unsaturated dicarboxylic acids or their anhydrides, B) one or more hydroxy-functional allyl ethers, without the addition of other polyols, and C) one or more ethylenically unsaturated compounds containing an oxirane group in the molecule, in a molar ratio of A) : B) : C) = (2 to 4) : (2 to 4) : (0.5 to 1.8). The composition is particularly suitable for filling compounds (trowelling compositions) and filler compositions which dry rapidly even at low temperatures.
</ABSTRACT>
