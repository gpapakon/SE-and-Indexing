<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for the degradation of harmful substances in a flow of gas.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B01D5332	B01D5332	B01J1908	B01J1908	B01J1912	B01J1912	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B01D	B01D	B01J	B01J	B01J	B01J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B01D53	B01D53	B01J19	B01J19	B01J19	B01J19	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In order, in a device for the decomposition of pollutants contained in a gas stream, which device has a flow duct (1) having at least one wire electrode (2) and at least one counter-electrode (3) interacting with the wire electrode (2) to generate a variable electrical high-voltage field, to ensure a satisfactory decomposition of pollutants, it is proposed that the wire electrode (2) which can be excited to transverse oscillations is supported in an axial direction via at least one tension spring (7) and/or forms at least in sections a tension spring.
