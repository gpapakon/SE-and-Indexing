<INVENTION-TITLE>
ELECTRIC ENERY TRANSMISSION SYSTEM.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H02J300	H02J300	H02J334	H02J334	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H02J	H02J	H02J	H02J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H02J3	H02J3	H02J3	H02J3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A process and device are disclosed for economically transmitting low power electric energy over large distances. The energy is transmitted in a single phase and a load-dependent voltage drop in the transmission line is appropriately compensated. Electric energy may thus be economically supplied to isolated localities.
</ABSTRACT>
