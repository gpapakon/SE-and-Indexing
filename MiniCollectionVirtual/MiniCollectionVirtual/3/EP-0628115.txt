<INVENTION-TITLE>
COMPONENT FOR A PERMANENT FORMWORK.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E04B286	E04B286	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E04B	E04B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>E04B2	E04B2	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A component for a permanent formwork comprises at least two insulating panels (5) which are arranged with a gap between them and can be assembled on site using spacers (20) and reusable ties which run through them.
</ABSTRACT>
