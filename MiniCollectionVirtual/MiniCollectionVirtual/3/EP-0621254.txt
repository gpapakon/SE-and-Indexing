<INVENTION-TITLE>
Procedure for isomerising double bonds of pyrethrinoids.

<ABSTRACT>
The subject of the invention is a process for the isomerisation of double bonds, characterised in that a compound of formula (I): 
<
IMAGE
>
 in which the geometry of the double bond is E or an E + Z mixture, Hal represents a halogen atom, A represents an electron-withdrawing group, Hal preceding A in the Cahn-Ingold-Prelog classification, Z1 and Z2, which are different from one another, represent a hydrogen atom or an organic radical, Z1 preceding Z2 in the Cahn-Ingold-Prelog classification, is subjected to the action of bromine in the presence of light to produce the compound of formula (I) in which the geometry of the double bond is Z. The process of the invention finds its use in the preparation of biologically active products, for example pesticides.
</ABSTRACT>
