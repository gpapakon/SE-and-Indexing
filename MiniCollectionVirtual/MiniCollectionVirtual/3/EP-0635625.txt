<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method and apparatus to burn the soot on ceramic diesel particulate filters.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F01N3023	F01N3028	H05B680	H05B680	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F01N	F01N	H05B	H05B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F01N3	F01N3	H05B6	H05B6	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Method for regenerating soot-loaded ceramic diesel particle filters, characterised in that the ignition temperature of the soot layer deposited on the filter is reached as a result of it selectively absorbing electromagnetic high-frequency radiation (e.g. microwave frequency 2.46 GHz) and the soot burns off in the oxygen excess of the diesel exhaust gas. High local electric field strengths of the magnetic (H10) or electric (E010) fundamental modes are used. The field amplification of the magnetic H10 mode is carried out by means of capacitive coupling in a rectangular hollow conductor (resonator). The soot ignition temperature is lowered by catalytically active substances.
