<INVENTION-TITLE>
Installation for loading wagons of different sizes with bulk material.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65G6702	B65G6706	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65G	B65G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65G67	B65G67	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
An installation for loading wagons of different sizes with bulk material located in a bunker is proposed, having a container which is subdivided into a plurality of chambers and is equipped, on the outlet side, with a plurality of outlet gates for selective loading. The container is designed as a weighing container, the individual chambers being designed to receive a preset quantity of bulk material.
