<INVENTION-TITLE>
Card guide block.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H05K714	H05K714	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H05K	H05K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H05K7	H05K7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Card guide block, for equipment including circuit-carrying cards (2) positioned in parallel by means of guide elements (1), with parallel card-holding sliders (4), which are fixed to one wall, of the board type, each card for this purpose having at least one edge held in a slider of a guide element. The block consists of a plurality of guide elements which, parallel and identical or the like, are identically fastened to one another, from one to the other, by spacers (8) allowing relative displacement between guide elements.
