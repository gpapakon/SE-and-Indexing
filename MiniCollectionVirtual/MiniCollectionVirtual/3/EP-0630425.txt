<ABSTRACT>
In order to make carbon fibre reinforced carbon resistant to oxidation, the proposal is for a process by means of which a bonding layer of amorphous SiNx is applied to the carbon fiber reinforced carbon to which in turn is applied a protective layer which is oxidation resistant at a temperature T, where T 
>
 400  DEG C, consisting preferably of Si3N4.
</ABSTRACT>
