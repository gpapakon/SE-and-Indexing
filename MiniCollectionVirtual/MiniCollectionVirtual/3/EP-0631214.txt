<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for the automatic landing of aircrafts and device for implementing it.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01S500	G01S516	G05D100	G05D106	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01S	G01S	G05D	G05D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01S5	G01S5	G05D1	G05D1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The method and the device for the automatic landing of aircraft (1) on a prescribed flight path (4) of a prescribed landing zone (2) use no active aids connected to the ground, but rather control the automatic landing approach on the basis of a passive optical 3D position measurement carried out from the aircraft (1) and using determination means arranged in the aircraft.
