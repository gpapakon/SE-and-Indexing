<INVENTION-TITLE>
ZSM-48 ZEOLITE SYNTHESIS METHOD.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B01J2900	B01J2970	C01B3700	C01B3702	C01B3900	C01B3904	C01B3946	C01B3948	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B01J29	B01J29	C01B37	C01B37	C01B39	C01B39	C01B39	C01B39	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A method for the synthesis of ZSM-48 type zeolites in the absence of a nitrogenous organic agent by hydrothermally reacting a mixture containing a tetravalent silicon source, hydroxide ions, water, a glycol and optionally a trivalent aluminium source. The resulting zeolites are useful as selective organophilic adsorbents and catalysts.
 
</ABSTRACT>
