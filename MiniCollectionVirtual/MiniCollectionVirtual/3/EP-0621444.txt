<INVENTION-TITLE>
Quick fastener for insertable lighting fixtures.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F16B500	F16B510	F21V1700	F21V1718	F21V2102	F21V2102	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F16B	F16B	F21V	F21V	F21V	F21V	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F16B5	F16B5	F21V17	F21V17	F21V21	F21V21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The quick fastener (1) for releasably securing luminaire elements (3) (luminaire inserts) to a supporting element (2) (supporting rail of a light strip) is mounted in the luminaire element so as to be rotatably movable and has locking means (12, 13, 14) which are automatically pretensioned counter to their locking direction when the luminaire element is fitted on the supporting element, and automatically lock in the built-in position of the luminaire element, when the locking position is released. A multifunctional spring element (12) is provided for securing the quick fastener in the luminaire element and for applying the forces for rotational movements and for securing the locking positions.
