<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for detecting breakage or presence of a tool, using a fluid-jet.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B23Q1709	B23Q1709	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B23Q	B23Q	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B23Q17	B23Q17	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
This device for monitoring the breakage or presence of tools under machining conditions using cutting fluids, by means of the interruption or non-interruption of a jet of fluid is composed of a double piston for creating a fluid pressure, of a nozzle for providing a jet of fluid, of a piezoelectric sensor which provides a signal if the jet strikes the sensor; in the event of the jet of fluid being interrupted the sensor will not supply any signal. This signal can be used to stop the machine if the tool breaks during machining.
