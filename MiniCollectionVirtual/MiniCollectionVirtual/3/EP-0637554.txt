<INVENTION-TITLE>
Gum formed liner with leakage testability.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65D2514	B65D2514	B65D7900	B65D7902	B65D9000	B65D9050	F16L55162	F16L55165	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65D25	B65D25	B65D79	B65D79	B65D90	B65D90	F16L55	F16L55	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A lining made of rubber (2), e.g. a container lining, consists of at least two layers of rubber (5, 6, 7), of which the layer (7) on the product side is designed to be electrically non-conductive and the layer (6) following this layer is designed to be electrically conductive. Damage to the rubber coating is recognised by closing an electric circuit. By subdividing the electrically conductive layer (6) into individual regions or segments, the damage zone can be located.
