<INVENTION-TITLE>
Antireflection coating.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G02B110	G02B111	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an antireflection coating for a spectacle glass made from poly(diethylene glycol bisallyl carbonate), polycarbonate, polymethyl methacrylate, polythiourethane or poly(bisphenol A bisallyl carbonate). The invention is distinguished by the fact that the uppermost layer of the antireflection layer system is a low-refraction layer having a thickness of from 0.5 to 1.5 x  lambda /4 for a reference wavelength conventional in spectacle optics, where this layer comprises, at least partly, at least one metal fluoride having a refractive index of 
<
/= 1.43.
</ABSTRACT>
