<INVENTION-TITLE>
CATHODE FOIL FOR ELECTROLYTIC CAPACITORS
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H01G904	H01G904	H01G9045	C22C2100	H01G9042	C22C2100	H01G9042	C22C2112	C22C2112	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H01G9	H01G9	H01G9	C22C21	H01G9	C22C21	H01G9	C22C21	C22C21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Improved cathode foil of an Al-Mn alloy for electrolytical 
capacitors with which a higher capacitance value 

and, in accordance with a further elaboration, a higher impact 
strength of the foil is obtained. To this end the foil 

has next to 0.2-2 weight % of Mn a Cu-content between 
0.15 and 5.3% and preferably a Ti-content between 0.03 

and 0.5%. 
</ABSTRACT>
