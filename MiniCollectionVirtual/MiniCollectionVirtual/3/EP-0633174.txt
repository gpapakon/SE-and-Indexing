<INVENTION-TITLE>
Magnetic track brake.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B61H700	B61H708	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B61H	B61H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B61H7	B61H7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Magnetic track brake with an exciter coil (1) and an elongated coil core (2) with U-shaped cross-section whose legs are provided on the rail side with one magnetizable brake shoe (12a, 12b) each, a non-magnetizable slip shoe (13) being arranged between the two brake shoes (12a, 12b) and the core (2) having a plurality of magnetizable metal plates (7) which are of U shape and are arranged one behind the other.
