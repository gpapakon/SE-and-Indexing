Method for generating images by means of a CCD sensor and device for carrying out this method

<ABSTRACT>
The method is of the type according to which a sensor is employed, including a charge-coupled device and means for control and processing of image data stored in the charge-coupled device. It is characterised in that the sensor is provided with means allowing it to acquire the images in integration mode in the state where it is disconnected from the control and processing means, the sensor is disconnected from the control and processing means for acquiring an image, and the sensor is reconnected to the said control and processing means for reading, processing and use of the information stored in the sensor in the course of the integration phase. The invention can be used for image acquisition.

<CLAIMS>
Method for producing images from an image information including
radiation, according to which a sensor (1) comprising a charge coupled

device organized for a field transfer and means for the control and the
processing of the image data which are stored in the charge coupled

device, characterized in that the sensor is provided with means
comprising electric circuits (2,3) for the electric energy supply and for

putting the charge coupled device in the integration state, enabling it to
acquire images during the integration mode in a state switched off from

the control and processing means, the sensor is switched off from the
control and processing means for the acquiring of an image and the

sensor is again connected to said control and processing means for the
reading, the processing and using of the informations which are stored

in the charge coupled device during the integration phase.
Method according to claim 1, characterized in that one uses as charge
coupled device a charge coupled device provided for working in a so-called

Multi Pinned Phase (MPP) mode and that the integration phase
takes place during the MPP mode.
Method according to claim 1, characterized in that the charge coupled
device is brought when it is connected to the said control and processing

electronic means to a temperature less than the temperature of the
medium in which the integration phase takes place.
Method according to claim 1, characterized in that the charge transfer
device is connected to a cooling device which maintains the charge

coupled device at a temperature less than the temperature of the medium
in which the integration takes place, during the integration phase.
Method according to any of the foregoing claims, characterized in that
one provides the sensor with an electronic means controlling the 

blanking of the charge coupled device, said electronics insuring the
passage into the integration mode by detection of an external order or a

manual action.
Method according to any of the foregoing claims, characterized in that
the control voltages necessary for the good working of the sensor during

the integration mode when it is disconnected from said control and
processing electronics are produced from an accumulator integrated into

said sensor, said accumulator being automatically recharged when said
sensor is connected to said control and processing electronics.
Arrangement for putting into practice the method according to any of
the claims 1 to 6, of the type comprising a sensor provided with a charge

transfer device organized for a field transfer and means for controlling
and processing image data stored in the charge coupled device, said

sensor being disconnectable from said control and processing electronic
means, characterized in that the sensor is provided with means

comprising electric circuits for the supply with electric power and for
putting the charge coupled device in the integration state, enabling it to

acquire the images during the integration mode in a state disconnected
from the control and processing means and constitutes thus a self

containing image acquiring unit in the integration mode.
