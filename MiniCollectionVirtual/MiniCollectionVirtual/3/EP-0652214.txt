<ABSTRACT>
The compounds of general formula (I): 
<

>
  in which   R1 represents an alkyl group containing from 1 to 6 carbon atoms, a cycloalkyl group containing from 3 to 6 carbon atoms, an alkenyl group containing from 2 to 6 carbon atoms or an alkynyl group containing from 2 to 6 carbon atoms; R2 and R3, with the atoms which carry them, form an optionally substituted carbocyclic or heterocyclic ring system; and R4 represents a hydrogen atom or an alkyl group containing from 1 to 3 carbon atoms; their tautomers and their addition salts with bases have an anti-inflammatory and immunomodulating activity.
