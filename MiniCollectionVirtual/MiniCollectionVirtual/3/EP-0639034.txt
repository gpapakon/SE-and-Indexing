<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for initiating a call-forward process with help of a supporting device for a mobile radio station.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H04B138	H04B138	H04W416	H04W416	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H04B	H04B	H04W	H04W	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H04B1	H04B1	H04W4	H04W4	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a method for call forwarding from a mobile station (MS) of a radio communications network (NW) to another instrument, the call number (N) of which is known to appropriately configured elements of the network (NW), triggering of the call forwarding being performed by means of the mobile station (MS) communicating for this purpose by radio with the network (NW); the mobile station (MS) is furthermore capable of being recharged by the use of a recharging device (C). This method is characterised in that the detection of the presence of the mobile station (MS) in the recharging device (C) automatically triggers the call forwarding (ACTIVATE CF) towards another instrument.
