<INVENTION-TITLE>
Shredder.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B02C1806	B02C1814	B02C1822	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B02C	B02C	B02C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
In a shredder for bulky scrap material which has a shredding roller (5) and a feed shaft, a wall (2) is pivotably arranged laterally next to the shredding roller (5) about an axis (4) parallel to it. The movable wall (2) thereby presses the material against the shredding roller (5) along a base (8) curved in a circular-arc shape.
