<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for applying a pasty product, in particular a cosmetic product such as lipstick
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A45D4000	A45D4012	A45D4000	A45D4006	A45D4006	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A45D	A45D	A45D	A45D	A45D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The device comprises: a tubular element (1) with a slide in which a cup (3) is slidably mounted, the said cup being intended to receive a stick (B) of product and including at least one stud (4) engaged in a slide (2); a cylindrical casing (11) in the wall of which at least one helical groove (13) is provided, this casing being slipped over the tubular element, the stud (4) being engaged in a groove (13); and an outer sleeve (14) in which the casing (11) is locked whilst the tubular element (1) can turn relative to the said casing (11) and sleeve (14). Flexible friction means (F) are provided between the casing (11) and the tubular element (1) and include at least one tongue (15), flexible in the radial direction, provided on one of the pieces and capable of interacting with an inclined bearing surface (20) provided on the other piece. The tongue (15) and the inclined bearing surface (20) are located, in the axial direction, beyond the lower end of the outer sleeve (14).
