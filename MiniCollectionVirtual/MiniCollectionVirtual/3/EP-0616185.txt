<INVENTION-TITLE>
Propulsive charge storage and feeding system for armoured vehicles
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F41A901	F41A920	F41A978	F41A900	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F41A	F41A	F41A	F41A	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F41A9	F41A9	F41A9	F41A9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
System for storing and feeding propulsive charges intended to be inserted in the chamber of the barrel of a medium- or large-calibre weapon on an armoured vehicle, such as a tank, for example, of the type comprising a magazine (1) divided into m stages each split into n rows each containing p elementary charges (CE) of the modular type, and a transfer device (DT) comprising an elevator (11) located opposite one face or front face (1a) of the magazine (1) in order to receive elementary charges (CE) and transport them as far as a first stage of the elevator (11), a first device (12) for inserting the elementary charges (CE) in the elevator (11) from each stage of the magazine (1), and a second device (13) for removing the elementary charges (CE) from the first stage of the elevator (11) and transporting a specific number of said charges as far as a loading trough (litter, barrow) (10).

tank for example, of the type comprising a storage part

device to transfer the charges from the magazine to a
