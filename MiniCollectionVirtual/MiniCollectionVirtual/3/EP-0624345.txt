<INVENTION-TITLE>
Sterile cutting pliers for a more or less rigid wire.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a sterile cutting pliers intended to cut a more-or-less rigid wire (8). This cutting pliers include a first lever (10) and a second lever (12) which are joined by a pin (14). Each lever has a cutting edge (20, 22). According to the invention, the cutting pliers also includes a member (24) for blocking the wire (8), which member is mounted so that it can pivot on the pin (14) or on a pin solidly attached to one of the levers and has an active face (36). During and after cutting, the wire (8) or its cut end (34) is kept blocked between the said active face (36) and the first cutting edge (20).
