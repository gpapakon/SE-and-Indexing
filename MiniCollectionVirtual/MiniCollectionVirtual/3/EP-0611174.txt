<INVENTION-TITLE>
Fastening device.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F16L1902	F16L1902	F16L4700	F16L4704	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F16L	F16L	F16L	F16L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The fastening device (connector) between a first, non-metallic, tubular end fitting (1a) and a second, metallic, tubular end fitting (1b) includes an end fitting (2) attached onto the tubular end fitting (1a), a nut (3) and a piece (4) for connection with the second tubular end fitting (1b). An annular seal is interposed between the collar (6) of the attached end fitting (2) and the connection piece (4).
