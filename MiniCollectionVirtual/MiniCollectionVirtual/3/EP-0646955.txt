<INVENTION-TITLE>
Apparatus for encapsulating a semiconductor element mounted on a heat sink with plastic.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H01L2102	H01L2156	H01L2334	H01L23433	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H01L	H01L	H01L	H01L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H01L21	H01L21	H01L23	H01L23	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In order, when encapsulating a semiconductor component (3) and a heat sink (6) which is connected thereto, which heat sink is intended not to be encapsulated on one outer surface, to prevent the production of a thin thermoplastic film because of the material tolerances and gaps on the outer surface of the heat sink, it is proposed to arrange a sheet (8) between the heat sink (6) and an associated shaped part (1) of the shaping device (1, 2). The sheet can in this case consist of paper or polyester.
