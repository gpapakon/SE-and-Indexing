<INVENTION-TITLE>
Illumination arrangement for light and weathering testing systems with a xenon gas discharge lamp.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
An illumination arrangement for light and weathering testing systems is provided with a pulse-operated xenon gas discharge lamp with an elongate discharge bulb which has an electrode spacing in the range of 10 to 50 cm; a predetermined ratio of ultraviolet to infrared radiation is achieved by setting the pulse duty factor (duty ratio) of the lamp current by means of an electronic control device; for the purpose of high luminance, the lamp bulb is bent in a U-shape.
