<INVENTION-TITLE>
GELLED COMPOSITION
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61K900	A61K900	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61K	A61K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61K9	A61K9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A gelled composition prepared by adding a gelling component to a carrier for adsorption. The composition can significantly improve the taste of the carrier for adsorption, can reduce the dosage, and can be easily administered to a patient.
</ABSTRACT>
