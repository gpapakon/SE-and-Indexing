<INVENTION-TITLE>
Magnetic angular indicator.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01D512	G01D514	G01P342	G01P3487	H02K2114	H02K2114	H02K2906	H02K2908	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
A cylindrical magnet (M) with diametric magnetisation (N/S) is mounted axially on a rotating shaft. At least one magnetic sensor element (S1, S2,...) is positioned in the spatial vicinity of the lateral surface of the magnet (M) in a fixed fashion. A ring (R) made from magnetically conductive material embraces the magnet (M) while forming an air gap (L) which is as uniform as possible and in which the sensor element (S1, S2,...) is positioned in a fixed fashion. The latter emits a sinusoidal measurement signal (MS1, MS2,...) which serves as a measure for the current angular position of the rotating shaft (DW). The ring can be mounted in a fixed fashion or in a fashion capable of rotating with the rotating shaft (DW). The ring has the effect, in particular, of increasing the amplitude of the measurement signal (MS1,...) of the sensor element (S1,...).
