<INVENTION-TITLE>
Structure for aircraft seat and seat with this structure.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a structure for an aircraft seat comprising a triangular base (1) defining a front anchorage (2), a rear anchorage (3), and a raised intermediate vertex (4) and a leg (5) connected by its upper end (6) to the structure of the seat and connected by its lower end (7) to the triangular base (1). According to the invention the leg (5) is connected rigidly to the base (1) by rigid connection means (8) which, in normal use of the seat, positively hold the leg (5) with respect to the base (1), particularly preventing any pivoting of the leg (5) forwards or rearwards.
