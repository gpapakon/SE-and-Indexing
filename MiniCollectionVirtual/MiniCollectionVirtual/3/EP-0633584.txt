<ABSTRACT>
A bidirectional electronic switch 30 driven by a pulse-modulation control circuit 40 is placed in series with a first current path C1 which is active in normal operation and can be shunted by a second current path C2 which is activated transiently upon a cutoff with an arc. Application to modulation of the operation of an electrical load.
