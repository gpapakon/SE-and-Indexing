<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for closing water baths of heating installations
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
For closing water baths of heating installations, for example in the case of natural gas-preheating installations, there is provided according to the invention a flexible sealing element (8) which floats on the open surface of the water bath and sealingly bears on the housing (2,3) of the water bath with its edge region, and which, at least in its edge region bearing on the housing of the water bath, consists of an envelope made of a flexible material (9) which is filled with a medium.

<CLAIMS>
Device for closing water baths of heating installations, characterised by an 
elastic sealing element (8) which floats on the open water bath surface and 

rests tight against the edges of the water bath housing (2, 3), at least the 
edges of said sealing element which rest against the water bath housing 

consisting of a sleeve (9) made of elastic material and filled with a medium. 
Device according to claim 1, characterised in that the sealing element (8) is a 
medium-filled body (9) which completely covers the open water bath surface. 
Device according to claim 1, characterised in that the sealing element (8) is a 
medium-filled frame (9) which runs along the edge of the open water bath 

surface and whose open inner surface is sealed with an additional element 
(10). 
Device according to claim 3, characterised in that the additional element (10) 
is impermeable to oxygen. 
Device according to either claim 3 or 4, characterised in that the additional 
element (10) is a membrane which, together with the frame (9), forms one 

piece. 
Device according to any one of the aforementioned claims, characterised in 
that the edge of the sealing element (8) resting against the water bath housing 

is rounded. 
Device according to any one of the aforementioned claims, characterised in 
that a valve (11) for venting the space under the sealing element (8) is 

arranged on the sealing element. 
Device according to any one of the aforementioned claims, characterised in 
that the sealing element (8) rests on a buoy (12).  

 
Device according to any one of the aforementioned claims, characterised in 
that a ballast element (13) is arranged on the sealing element. 
Device according to any one of the aforementioned claims, characterised in 
that a function control (14) is arranged on the sealing element. 
Device according to any one of the aforementioned claims, characterised in 
that the sealing element is arranged in a part (3) of the water bath housing in 

which the open water bath surface is reduced. 
</CLAIMS>
