<ABSTRACT>
The subject of the invention is a flange strengthening in which a supporting element (8) is an integral part of the flange strengthening. The flange strengthening is fashioned in the shape of a fork and the supporting element (8) is shaped out from the space (7) lying between the forks.

<CLAIMS>
A flange reinforcement for the flange surface of the 
connection pipe of a pump housing (1) constructed of sheet 

metal, comprising support elements (8) arranged between the 
flange reinforcement and the pump housing, characterized in 

that the flange reinforcement is designed in the form of 
bifurcated part (2), one or more support elements (8) being 

formed between two prongs (3 and 4) of the bifurcated part 
(2). 
The flange reinforcement as claimed in claim 1, 
characterized in that the support element or support elements 

(8) are formed on the external and/or internal peripheral 
part of a connecting means (5) of the bifurcate part (2). 
The flange reinforcement as claimed in claim 1 or in 
claim 2, characterized in that the bifurcate part (2) is 

provided with a rim (12) formed thereon. 
The flange reinforcement as claimed in claim 3, 
characterized in that one or more support elements (10) are 

formed on one or both prongs (3 and 4) of the bifurcate part 
(2)
