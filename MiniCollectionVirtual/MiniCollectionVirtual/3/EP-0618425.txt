<ABSTRACT>
A sensor head (1) is described which has a spacer (5b) between a carrier element (2) and the spring-loaded beam (7) which, if appropriate, carries a sensor tip (9) on the free end. Said spacer defines the clearance d between the spring-loaded beam (7) and carrying element (2). The spacer (5b) can consist of a sacrificial layer (5a) which is etched out as far as the spacer (5b) after the construction of an appropriate layer system between the spring-loaded beam (7) and carrying element (2). The carrying element (2) and the spring-loaded beam (7) are each provided with a reflecting layer (4 and 6). In accordance with another embodiment, it is also possible to provide plasmon-active layers.
