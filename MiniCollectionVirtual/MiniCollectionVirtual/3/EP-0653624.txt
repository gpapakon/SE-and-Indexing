<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for on-line analysis and control in industrial synthetic and dye processes using UV/VIS-spectroscopy and multicomponent analysis.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C09B6700	C09B6700	D06P500	D06P500	G01N2131	G01N2133	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C09B	C09B	D06P	D06P	G01N	G01N	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C09B67	C09B67	D06P5	D06P5	G01N21	G01N21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Method for controlling and optimising processes for production of dyestuffs, other colouring substances and their intermediate products or colourings with one or more dyestuffs, by using multicomponent analysis of UV/VIS spectra, optionally after previous preparation of the samples, to determine and optionally control and optimise the actual quantities of the substances involved in the production or colouring.
