<INVENTION-TITLE>
Treating installation, especially for steaming and drying of clothing articles such as jackets and coats hung on clothing hangers.

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an installation for the treatment, especially for the steaming and drying, of articles of clothing 8, especially jackets and overcoats, hanging on clothes-hangers, characterised by at least one turntable 6 rotatable about an essentially vertical axis of rotation X-X and having at least one diametral line, on which a bust Bu1; Bu2 is located on each of the two sides of the axis of rotation X-X, by a loading station B, in which an article of clothing 8, together with the hanger 12, is to be lowered onto a bust Bu1; Bu2 as a result of the lowering of the hanger 12, and by an unloading station E, in which, after the rotation of the turntable 6 through essentially 180 DEG , the article of clothing 8, together with the hanger 12, is to be lifted off from the bust Bu1; Bu2.
