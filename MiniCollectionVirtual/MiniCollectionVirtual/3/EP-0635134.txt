<CLAIMS>
Non-contact device for measuring the rotational speed of a component 
arranged on a shaft, with several circumferential radially aligned magnets in 

whose magnetic fields a stationary pulse-wire sensor is arranged,
 
characterised in that
 
the magnets (4) are arranged at an axial distance to each other and 

alternately with opposite poles so that the superposition of the individual 
magnetic fields produces a large axis-parallel field component which acts on 

the pulse-wire sensor (5) arranged parallel to the rotary axis of the shaft (3). 
Device according to claim 1,
 
characterised in that
 
two bar magnets (4) are used which are aligned parallel to each other. 
Device according to claim 1 or 2,
 
characterised in that
 
the magnets (4) are inserted in the shaft (3) of the component (3a). 
</CLAIMS>
