<INVENTION-TITLE>
Digital time-division multiplex switching system.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H04Q1104	H04Q1104	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H04Q	H04Q	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H04Q11	H04Q11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The communications system has more than two relatively spaced coupling fields (MSM), each comprising a matrix of reception and transmission collector rails, connected via controlled coupling prints. Each coupling field is associated with a respective communications module (M1..Mn), with a coupling field control (MSMC) providing the required connections. The transmission and reception collector rails (MHY) provide the internal connections, auxiliary collector rails (AHY) providing connections to tone transmitters and receivers and further collector rails (1MHY) provide the connections between the coupling fields (MSM), via intermediate adaptors (IMLA). The serial connection lines (1ML) for asynchronous transmission of connection information are coupled to a central module (1MTU) incorporating the intermediate adaptors and a central coupling field (IMSM).
</ABSTRACT>
