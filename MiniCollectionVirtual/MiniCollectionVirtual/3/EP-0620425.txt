<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for increasing the measuring accuracy of a resistance thermometer.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01K716	G01K718	G01K1500	G01K1500	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01K	G01K	G01K	G01K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01K7	G01K7	G01K15	G01K15	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for increasing the measuring accuracy of resistance thermometers. The object of the invention is to configure the measuring method in such a way that the distortion of the temperature by the intrinsic heating of the sensor is compensated. This object is achieved in that the temperature is determined at at least two different electrical loadings dissipated in the sensor by means of the measuring process and is then extrapolated to the value corresponding to a zero electrical loading.
</ABSTRACT>
