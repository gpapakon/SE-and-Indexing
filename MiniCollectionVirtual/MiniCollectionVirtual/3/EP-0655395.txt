Dispenser for cleaner or similar

<ABSTRACT>
The dispenser for a cleaning liquid or the like, particularly for use in a service kitchen, is characterised in that it comprises: an outer casing 2; a container 13 for cleaning liquid under gas pressure, the container being supported by the outer casing and being equipped with a lower seal which is normally closed; a nozzle 19 cooperating with the seal and designed such that it is able to move between a passive position, in which the seal is closed, and an active position, in which it opens the seal in order to allow the liquid under pressure to emerge in a continuous flow from the container 13 and pass through the nozzle 19 so as to be dispensed in the form of a downwardly directed jet 20; and manually operated means 21 designed to bring the nozzle 19 into the active position and situated in the area under the nozzle 19 and in the zone of the jet 20 in order to be activated by the fingers of the user.

<CLAIMS>
Dispenser for cleaning liquid product or 
similar, among others for collectivity kitchen, comprising a 

housing (2) ; a container (13) of cleaning liquid product 
under gas pressure, supported by the housing and provided 

with a normally closed closing device ; a nozzle (19) 
cooperating with the closing device and arranged to be 

movable between an inoperative position for which the closing 
device is closed and an operative position for which it opens 

the closing device to allow the liquid under pressure to exit 
from the container (13) and to flow through the nozzle (19) 

to be dispensed in the form of a jet (20), characterized in 
that the closing device is located at the lower portion of 

the container in order to downwardly dispense the product in 
a continuous manner, and in that it comprises manual means 

(21) arranged to bring the nozzle (19) in operative position 
and located generally under the nozzle (19) and in the zone 

of the jet (20) to be operated by the user's fingers. 
Dispenser according to claim 1, characterized in 
that the nozzle (19) is biased in inoperative position by 

gravity, the closing device and/or the manual means (21). 
Dispenser according to one of claims 1 and 2, 
characterized in that the closing device and the manual means 

(21) are arranged so that the inoperative and operative 
positions of the nozzle (19) are a lower position and an 

upper position, respectively. 
Dispenser according to one of claims 1 to 3, 
characterized in that the manual means (21) comprise a 

resilient action lever. 
Dispenser according to one of claims 1 to 4, 
characterized in that the housing (2) has an upper chamber 

(10) for receiving the container (13) and a dispensing lower 
chamber (11) in which the manual means are located.  

 
Dispenser according to claim 5, characterized in 
that the housing (2) comprises a lateral wall (3) in which is 

provided a front window (12) giving access to the lower 
chamber (11) for the user's hands. 
Dispenser according to one of claims 1 to 6, 
characterized in that the nozzle (19) is a part independant 

from the closing device. 
Dispenser according to one of claims 1 to 6, 
characterized in that the nozzle (19) is a part incorporated 

in the closing device. 
</CLAIMS>
