<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the preparation of a hydrophobic aerogel.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C01B3300	C01B33158	G10K1100	G10K1102	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C01B	C01B	G10K	G10K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C01B33	C01B33	G10K11	G10K11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
For an improved hydrophobic aerogel, it is proposed that a correspondingly hydrophobic alkoxy silane mixture be hydrolysed in a basically catalysed manner under the action of ultrasound and the wet gel obtained be dried supercritically. Aerogels thus obtained have an impedance of about 10
<
5
>
kg/m
<
2
>
s, which corresponds to the optimum value of the impedance matching layer of an ultrasonic transducer.
