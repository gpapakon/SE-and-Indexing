<ABSTRACT>
For replacing human vertebral bodies (25, 26) the invention proposes a slightly bent, approximately cylindrical implant (10) on the dome-shaped ends (14, 15) of which there are fastened in each case one supporting element (17, 18) in an angular position adapted to suit the anatomy of the human spine.
