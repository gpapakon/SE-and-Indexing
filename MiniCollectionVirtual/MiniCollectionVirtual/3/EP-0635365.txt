Rotary printing machine.

<ABSTRACT>
The machine also contains at least one web offsetting device (9). Pref. the impression cylinders (2,3) are independently driven and have different jacket surfaces. The latter may be formed on releasable cylinder sleeves. The sleeve release is easily carried out by oil pressure. The web offsetting drive cavity of two round bars (10), mounted in parallel above, or below the impression cylinders on a housing (4). They are displaceable at an angle to the web direction.
</ABSTRACT>
