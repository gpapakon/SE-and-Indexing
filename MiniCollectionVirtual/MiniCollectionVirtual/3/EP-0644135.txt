<INVENTION-TITLE>
Conveyor-way for bottles, boxes or similar containers.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65G4768	B65G4771	B65G4784	B65G4784	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65G	B65G	B65G	B65G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65G47	B65G47	B65G47	B65G47	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A conveyor stage for bottles, cans or suchlike containers possesses a multi-track conveying region (4), to which the containers (3) are fed on one track (7) and which moves the containers further in a conveying direction (A), a plurality of push-over elements (9) which are provided on at least one auxiliary conveyor (11) and which, in a working stroke, are movable in the conveying direction by the auxiliary conveyor and, controlled individually, are movable transversely to the conveying direction (A) for at least one transverse stroke (Q) or lifting movement, in order to displace containers in a controlled manner from one track (7) on to another track transversely to the conveying direction (A), drivers provided on the push-over elements (9) and with control cams for the drivers for generating the respective lifting movment, and at least one activating device which has at least one electromagnet and which can be activated for the engagement of a driver into the control cam corresponding to the desired lifting movement. The particular feature is that each driver is movable out of an inactivated position into an activated position by means of the activating device.
