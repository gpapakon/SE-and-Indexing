<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the valorisation of sewage sludge.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C02F1112	C02F1112	C10L540	C10L540	C10L1000	C10L1000	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C02F	C02F	C10L	C10L	C10L	C10L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C02F11	C02F11	C10L5	C10L5	C10L10	C10L10	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In the process, the wet sewage sludge originating from a preceding mechanical dewatering is mixed with brown coal, brown coal coke or mixtures thereof in a weight ratio of sewage sludge to brown coal or mixtures with brown coal coke of 1:3 to 3:1 and then compressed to form dimensionally stable mouldings at moderate compressing pressures above 50 bar. The briquetted mouldings can then be supplied for thermal utilization in a power station boiler as an auxiliary fuel or are materially utilized in a pyrolysis or gasification process for producing heating gas or synthesis gas.
</ABSTRACT>
