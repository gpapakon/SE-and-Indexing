<INVENTION-TITLE>
Metering device for non-ferrous metal melts and method to control the release of a melt volume.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B22D	B22D	F27D	F27D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a metering device (10) for non-ferrous metal melts and a method for controlling the release of a melt volume. In order to permit accurate metering of the melt volume, the metering device (10) is provided with a metering chamber (22) separate from a holding trough (20). A riser pipe (48) leads out of the metering chamber (22), which is sealed off from the environment and can be subjected to pressure. The metering chamber (22) is connected to the bottom area (26) of the holding trough (20) by a connecting passage (50), with the result that, when there is a pressure balance between the holding trough (20) and the metering chamber (22), melt can flow across into the metering chamber (22).
