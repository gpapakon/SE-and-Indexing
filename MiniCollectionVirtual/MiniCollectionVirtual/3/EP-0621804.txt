<CLAIMS>
Apparatus for catching shredded material, in particular 
shredded banknotes, comprising a bag (3) for the shredded material, which 

can be fitted into the catch container of a shredding device and which is 
open at the top and which is in the form of a disposable item of paper 

and which at its opening has a holder (4) which can be fixed to a 
mounting means (5) arranged in the region of the discharge opening of the 

shredding device, characterised in that the holder (4) is in the form of 
flat cardboard which is sealingly glued or stitched to the bag and which 

can be inserted with its side edges into guides (5.4) provided at the 
underside of the mounting means (5), wherein both the holder (4) and also 

the mounting means (5) are provided beneath the disposal shaft (7) of the 
shredding device with corresponding passage openings (4.1; 5.1) for the 

shredded material and additionally with aligned openings (4.2; 5.2) for a 
filling level sensor. 
Apparatus according to claim 1 characterised in that disposed 
between the holder (4) and the mounting means (5) is a seal (8) 

surrounding the passage openings (4.1; 5.1) thereof. 
Apparatus according to claim 1 characterised in that the 
holder (4) can he pressed against the mounting means (5) by means of a 

movable frame (9). 
Apparatus according to one of claims 1 to 3 characterised in 
that the passage opening (4.1) of the holders (4) is adapted to be 

closable. 
Apparatus according to claim 4 characterised in that the 
holder (4) is provided with a closure slider (10).  

 
Apparatus according to claim 4 characterised in that in the 
region of its passage opening the holder (4) is provided with closure 

flaps (d, e) which are fixed by elastic bending binges (c) to the edge of 
the passage opening (4.1) of the holder (4).
