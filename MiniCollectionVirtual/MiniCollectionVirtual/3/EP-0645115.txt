<INVENTION-TITLE>
Board for cutting meat products.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47J4700	A47J4700	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47J	A47J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47J47	A47J47	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
This board is of the type consisting of a rectangular or square board (3). Along one of its edges, namely its front edge (3a) it has a front vertical rim (4) pointing downwards and designed to bear against the front edge (5a) of a surface or of a work bench (5) and, along its opposite side, that is to say along its rear edge (3b), has a rear vertical rim (6) pointing upwards and designed to constitute a stop against which the product to be cut up can rest.
