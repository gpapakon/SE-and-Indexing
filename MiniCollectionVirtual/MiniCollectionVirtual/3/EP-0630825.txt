<INVENTION-TITLE>
Bottle carrier.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65D7100	B65D7100	B65D7106	B65D7130	B65D7134	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65D	B65D	B65D	B65D	B65D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65D71	B65D71	B65D71	B65D71	B65D71	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A bottle carrier having a one-piece blank of foldable flat material which has a top wall with at least two rows of receiving holes for bottle necks, side walls linked to the two longitudinal sides of the top wall, and a bottom wall linked to the two other longitudinal sides of the side walls, and which can be placed around a group of bottles in the manner of a sleeve, the top wall having a gripping section between two halves of the top wall each having one row of receiving holes, which gripping section has two gripping halves which are linked laterally in each case to one half of the top wall and can be pivoted about their lateral attachment line in relation to their half of the top wall and can be folded against one another.
