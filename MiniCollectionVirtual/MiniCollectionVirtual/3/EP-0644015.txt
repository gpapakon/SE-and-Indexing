<ABSTRACT>
Connection between two elements of aluminium or aluminium alloys, at least the one element constituting a covering layer as part of a composite material. The connection is formed by a fusion weld between at least one outer covering layer of the one element and the second element. The invention is especially suitable for producing complex, mechanically robust and bearing structures which are of any shape and contain composite materials. The invention also relates to the process for the fusion welding of composite materials, the main importance being attached to the weld preparation.
