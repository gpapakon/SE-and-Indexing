<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method and device for treating workpieces in form of disks with a fluid.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B08B304	B08B304	B08B308	B08B308	H01L2100	H01L2100	H01L2102	H01L21304	H01L21306	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
Method and device for treating workpieces (4) in the form of wafers with a liquid in a chamber (1) which can be sealed in a gas-tight manner, the workpieces stacked in a transport rack (basket) (3) being brought into contact with the supplied liquid in the closed chamber, the liquid being discharged from the chamber and harmful vapours or gases originating from the liquid being washed out and discharged from the chamber in the dissolved state.
