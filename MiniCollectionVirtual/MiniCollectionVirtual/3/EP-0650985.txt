<INVENTION-TITLE>
Cross-linkable binder for powder coatings.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Crosslinkable copolymers having a number average molecular weight of from 1500 to 6000 and a molecular weight distribution corresponding to a polydispersity of from 1 to 4.0, comprising   a) from 50 to 85 mol % of a monomer (a) containing the methacrylic acid structural unit, b) from 15 to 50 mol % of a monomer (b) containing the acrylic acid, the styrene or a styrene derivative structural unit, and c) from 0 to 20 mol % of further free-radical-polymerising comonomers (c), where d) from 5 to 40 mol % of the total amount of the monomers (a) to (c) are monomers (d) having functional groups via which crosslinking by condensation or addition reactions can be effected by means of a crosslinking agent, obtainable by a free-radical bulk or solution polymerisation at a temperature of from 140 to 220 DEG C and a mean residence time of from 2 to 90 min. The crosslinkable copolymers are used, in particular, as binders for powder coatings (paints).
</ABSTRACT>
