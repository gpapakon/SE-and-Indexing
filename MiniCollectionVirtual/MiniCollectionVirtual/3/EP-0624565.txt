<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the preparation of formaldehyde by oxidative dehydrogenation of methanol in the presence of nitrous oxide
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C07C4538	C07C4702	C07C4500	C07C47055	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C07C	C07C	C07C	C07C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C07C45	C07C47	C07C45	C07C47	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Process for the preparation of formaldehyde by oxidative dehydrogenation of methanol in the gas phase on a silver catalyst, or silver-containing catalyst, by means of an oxygen-containing gas containing nitrous oxide (dinitrogen monoxide).
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
A process for the preparation of formaldehyde by oxidative 
dehydrogenation of methanol in the gas phase on a 

silver or silver-containing catalyst by means of an 
oxygen-containing gas which contains from 1 to 50% by 

volume of dinitrogen oxide.
