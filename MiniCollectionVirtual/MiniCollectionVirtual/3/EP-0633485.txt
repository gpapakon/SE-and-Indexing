<INVENTION-TITLE>
Filteroptical multifiber connection assembly and connection elements.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G02B638	G02B638	G02B642	G02B642	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a fibre-optic multichannel connection assembly of the type comprising a plug and socket, including:     - for the plug:     - a first mounting-plate (31) forming the plug body; - plates (21) housed in the first mounting-plate (31) and having first tubular housings (19) for fibre-optic connectors, and having a first locking means (109);     - for the socket:     - a second mounting-plate (32) forming the socket body; - second plates (22) housed in the second mounting-plate (32) and having second tubular housings (19) for fibre-optic connectors (200), and having a second locking means (109).     It also includes:     - ferrule (sleeve) holders (1) mounted in the housings (19); - alignment ferrules (6) arranged in each of the ferrule holders (1).
