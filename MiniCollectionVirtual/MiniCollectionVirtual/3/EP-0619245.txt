<INVENTION-TITLE>
Waste container mounted on a support.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65F114	B65F114	E05B1100	E05B1102	E05B3500	E05B3500	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a waste container, especially a paper basket, which is releasably fixed on a support by means of a lock which can be locked and unlocked by means of a key having a handle, the key, in the unlocked state, being secured on the waste container and/or on the lock fixed to the waste container in a non-removable manner.
