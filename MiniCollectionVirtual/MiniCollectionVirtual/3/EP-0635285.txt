<INVENTION-TITLE>
Net, in particular for volleyball.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A63B6100	A63B6100	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A63B	A63B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
At least one of the ends (5,6) of the tensioning cable (3), protruding out of the tubular container (2) of the net (1) goes round a deflector roller (8) mounted in the housing (7), and is fixed to the container (15). The deflector roller's housing has a tensioning line (9) for attaching to the relevant post (10). At least one end of the tensioning cable is fixed to a ring element (12) held in a looped strip (13) on the net's side edge.
</ABSTRACT>
