<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to magnetoelectric rotors in general and relates, more particularly, to a rotor of a synchronous machine. The rotor of a synchronous machine is characterised by a progressive magnetic gap and by a constant aerodynamic gap.

<CLAIMS>
Synchronous machine rotor having poles (1) and
adapted to be associated with a stator, characterised in that

said poles (1) of said rotor define with the associated stator
a progressive magnetic airgap and a constant aerodynamic

airgap.
Synchronous machine rotor according to claim 1
characterised by at least one amagnetic material progressive

magnetic airgap area.
Synchronous machine rotor according to claim 1 or
claim 2 wherein the poles (1) include a curved surface (2) at

the centre and a plane surface in at least one of said
progressive magnetic airgap areas and at least one sharp

edge (3).
Synchronous machine rotor according to claim 1 or
claim 2 wherein the poles (1) include a curved surface (2) at

the centre and a plane surface in at least one of said
progressive magnetic airgap areas and at least one bevelled

edge (3).
Synchronous machine rotor according to claim 1 or
claim 2 wherein the poles (1) include a curved surface (2) at

the centre and a plane surface in at least one of said
progressive magnetic airgap areas and at least one rounded

edge (3).
Synchronous machine rotor according to claim 1 or
claim 2 wherein the poles (1) include a curved surface (2) at

the centre and in at least one of said progressive magnetic
airgap areas and at least one sharp edge (3).
Synchronous machine rotor according to claim 1 or
claim 2 wherein the poles (1) include a curved surface (2) at

the centre and in at least one of said progressive magnetic
airgap areas and at least one bevelled edge (3).
Synchronous machine rotor according to claim 1 or
claim 2 wherein the poles (1) include a curved surface (2) at

the centre and in at least one of said progressive magnetic 
airgap areas and at least one rounded edge (3).
Synchronous machine rotor according to any one of
claims 1 to 8 wherein the wedge members (4, 6) are

complemented by lateral members (7, 8) to constitute an
electrically conductive amagnetic material damper turn.
Synchronous machine rotor according to any one of
the preceding claims wherein the progressive magnetic airgap

is formed on one side only of at least one of the poles.
</CLAIMS>
