<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for manufacturing articles, particularly granular long-term release fertilizers and fertilizer granules.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The process for manufacturing granular plant fertilisers with long-term (sustained) release action provides mixing a preferably pulverulent fertiliser with a biodegradable plastifiable particulate material, heating the mixture of fertiliser and material, preferably in an extruder, and plastifying and extruding it in the form of a rod which, when cold and hard, is comminuted to give the plant fertiliser granules according to the invention. The material is an NWR-based polymer which decomposes in the moist soil within a period which can be predetermined by selecting the type of the material, and the enclosed fertiliser particles are gradually released. The method is inexpensive.
</ABSTRACT>
