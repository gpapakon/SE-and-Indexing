<INVENTION-TITLE>
Bend as a sliding guide for a travelling, flat carding machine.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>D01G1500	D01G1524	D01G1528	D01G1530	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>D01G	D01G	D01G	D01G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>D01G15	D01G15	D01G15	D01G15	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A flexible bow (70) forms a sliding guide for the bars of the flat in their working position. The bow is designed as a wearing element. If the bow is formed from a plurality of elements, they can be mounted or adjusted jointly in the card.
