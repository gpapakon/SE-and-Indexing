<INVENTION-TITLE>
Seat reservation display.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B61D4100	B61D4104	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B61D	B61D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B61D41	B61D41	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The subject matter of the invention is a system for displaying information on the occupation or non-occupation of reservable seats in passenger transport means (17). On or near to the seats (19...30) display fields (1; 31...42) which can be actuated by electrical signals are respectively provided for displaying alphanumeric data and are each connected to a control unit (7) which has its own address. A transmitting unit outputs at the addresses the data to be displayed relating to the occupation or non-occupation of the respective seat.
