<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the realisation of an active composite.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B01J2028	B01J2028	C04B35536	C04B35536	C09K500	C09K504	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B01J	B01J	C04B	C04B	C09K	C09K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B01J20	B01J20	C04B35	C04B35	C09K5	C09K5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Process for producing an active composite consisting of a support and of an active agent, the support comprising an exfoliated lamella compound, the process comprising the stages of:     - impregnation of the support with the active agent, and - mixing the impregnated support with expanded graphite which has a density of between 0.001 and 0.02.   An active composite prepared by this process is also described.
