<INVENTION-TITLE>
Safety barrier.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E01F1300	E01F1302	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a safety barrier comprising a vertical frame (1) consisting of an upper rail (2) and two uprights (3), and a lower rail (4) connected, on the one hand, by its ends to the uprights (3) of the said frame (1) and, on the other hand, to the upper rail (2) of the said frame (1) via bars (5). The said lower rail (4) is bent at its ends in order to form a central longitudinal member (6) parallel to the upper rail (2) and two lateral crosspieces (7).
