<INVENTION-TITLE>
Baby carrier.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47D1300	A47D1302	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47D	A47D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47D13	A47D13	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The baby carrier is noteworthy in that it comprises a monobloc shaped framework (2) having various successive zones capable of forming a back part (A), a part (B) forming a seat, a part (C) forming a ventral support extending from a first folding line (L.1) via an articulated flap (H) in order to make it easy to fit the baby in, the said flap being arranged with shapes defined by complementary folding lines (L.2-L.3), one of the shapes of which constitutes a cap (D), the said framework also comprising a part forming a lateral band (E) which can be adjusted in order to adapt to the morphology of the baby and to ensure it is held, and surrounding the part forming a central support (C), fastening and linking straps (F) being arranged with respect to certain zones in order to enable the baby carrier to be worn on the front of the body, the said framework being thus arranged in order to enable the baby to be carried in various positions with respect to the wearer (carrier).
