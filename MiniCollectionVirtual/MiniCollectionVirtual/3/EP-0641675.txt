<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a retaining device for loose-leaf filing systems, comprising a plate on which there are arranged bars which are intended for receiving the perforated sheets and of which the ends can be laid against one another in a positively locking manner, the plate being arranged releasably on an adapter plate which is provided with fastening elements for the positively locking connection with the plate.

<CLAIMS>
Holding device for a tilting-lever ring
mechanism, which is detachably connected to the file

sleeve via an adapter plate (4), fastening elements
being provided to connect the adapter plate positively

to the plate (1) of the tilting-levering mechanism,
characterized in that a moveable pin (5), which latches

into an associated opening (10) in the plate (1), is
arranged centrally on the adapter plate (4) and

elevations (5a, 5b), which cooperate with openings in
the plate (1) of the tilting-lever ring mechanism, are

arranged at the ends of the adapter plate.
Holding device according to Claim 1,
characterized in that adapter plate (4) is detachably

connected to the file sleeve.
