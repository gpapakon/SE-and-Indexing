<INVENTION-TITLE>
Particulate graft copolymer.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08F28500	C08F28500	C08L5100	C08L5100	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08F	C08F	C08L	C08L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C08F285	C08F285	C08L51	C08L51	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A particulate graft polymer A comprising   A1: a graft base based on a polydiene rubber A1 comprising, based on A1,   A11: from 70 to 100 % by weight of a polymerisable diene and A12: up to 30 % by weight of a copolymerisable, ethylenically unsaturated monomer,  with a first graft shell A2 comprising, based on A2,   A21: from 75 to 99.8 % by weight of at least one C1-C8-alkyl acrylate A21, A22: from 0.1 to 5 % by weight of at least one polyfunctional, crosslinking monomer A22, A23: from 0.1 to 20 % by weight of at least one monomer A23 containing one or more acidic or basic groups,  and a second graft shell A3 comprising, based on A3,   A31: up to 99.9 % by weight of at least one vinylaromatic monomer or methyl methacrylate (A31), A32: from 0.1 to 20 % by weight of at least one monomer A32 containing one or more basic or acidic groups, and A34: up to 5 % by weight of at least one polyfunctional, crosslinking monomer A33, where A23 and A33 are in each case different groups.%
</ABSTRACT>
