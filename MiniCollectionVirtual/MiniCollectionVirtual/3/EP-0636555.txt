<INVENTION-TITLE>
Box for the packaging and transport of products liable to exude and/or produce liquid.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65D8126	B65D8126	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65D	B65D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65D81	B65D81	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
- The subject of the present invention is a box of parallelepipedal general shape, which comprises a bottom, a top and a lateral surround, and the walls of which are made of a material based on board (cardboard), corrugated board or the like, and which have, on their inner face, a layer of material forming an impermeable coating and, on their outer face, a layer of material forming an impermeable coating and having a low coefficient of friction, the said box being intended for packaging and for transporting products liable to exude and/or to produce liquid, and comprising, in its bottom (2), at least one hole (5) for draining off the liquid. - According to the invention, the said hole (5) is bordered by a ring (6), a first part (7) of which projects from the hole (5) on the outside of the box (1) and is equipped with radial outflow channels (9), each emerging in the central hole (10) of the ring (6).
