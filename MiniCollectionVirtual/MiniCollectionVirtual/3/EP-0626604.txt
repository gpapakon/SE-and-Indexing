<INVENTION-TITLE>
Flexible endoscope tube.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61B1005	A61B1005	G02B2324	G02B2324	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61B	A61B	G02B	G02B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61B1	A61B1	G02B23	G02B23	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A flexible endoscope tube which consists of short, tubular elements (10) which are connected to each other in the longitudinal direction of the tube by swivelling members 14, 16 and can be controlled by means of one or more tension ropes (44) which run along the tube. The swivelling members (14, 16) and tension ropes (44) are in this case arranged in the region of each tubular element (10) inside the wall thickness of the element.
