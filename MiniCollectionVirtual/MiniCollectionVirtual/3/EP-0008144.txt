BLEACH-FIX SHEET
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
A bleach-fix sheet for use in bleaching and fixing an 
exposed and developed retained image color photographic 

element. The sheet comprises a support carrying thereon a 
metallic silver bleaching agent, a silver salt fixing agent 

and a hydrophilic binder having a swelling rate ¹/₂ greater 
than 5 seconds. 
</ABSTRACT>
