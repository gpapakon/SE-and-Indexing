<INVENTION-TITLE>
Cross cutter.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B26D100	B26D100	B26D726	B26D726	F16F136	F16F1366	F16F1504	F16F1504	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
Cross cutter for a web made of flexible material, including a cylindrical rolling body which can be rotated about its centre of gravity and is provided at at least one point on its circumference with a knife (3) which extends essentially parallel to the axis, is accommodated in a metal mount (2) and points radially outwards. The rolling body is composed of a plastic tube (1), the mount (2) being produced separately and being connected to the plastic tube (1).
