<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for influencing liquid present in the soil.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
A device for influencing liquid present in the soil (20) by means of a well shaft (10) which is introduced in the soil and is split up by at least one dividing wall (13) into a plurality of shaft areas (14, 15). To produce a larger filter area for the liquid, a plurality of well pipes (11, 12) provided with a partly liquid-permeable wall (11.1, 12.1) and at least partly with a liquid-delivery device (18) are inserted into the well shaft (10) for producing at least one liquid circuit (23). If they are passed through a plurality of shaft areas (14, 15), the well pipes (11, 12) likewise have a dividing wall (16) at the level of the shaft dividing wall (13).
