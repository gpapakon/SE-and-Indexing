<INVENTION-TITLE>
Receiver for programmes broadcast by a satellite or an MMDS-station
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H03D700	H03D700	H03D716	H04B118	H04B118	H04N544	H04N544	H04N720	H04N720	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H03D	H03D	H03D	H04B	H04B	H04N	H04N	H04N	H04N	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H03D7	H03D7	H03D7	H04B1	H04B1	H04N5	H04N5	H04N7	H04N7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The receiver comprises a low-noise convertor block (LNB) (1) integral with an external antenna. An internal unit (3) is connected to it by coaxial cable (2). The LNB incorporates a polarisation selector (18), a wideband low-noise amplifier-filter (19), a frequency band selector module (4), a down-convertor (36), a second mixer (30) and an output amplifier-filter (36a). The first local oscillator (9) is controlled by a phase-locked loop (12), with a voltage-controlled oscillator delivering a frequency of about 2 GHz. The internal unit may incorporate a television receiver or video tape recorder.
</ABSTRACT>
