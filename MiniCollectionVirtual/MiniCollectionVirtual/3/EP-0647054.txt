<INVENTION-TITLE>
Interface device between a telephone terminal and a modem.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H04M1106	H04M1106	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H04M	H04M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H04M11	H04M11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Interface arrangement SA1 between a telephone terminal FE and a modem M, having at least disconnectable connections Asl1a, Asl1b, Asl2a, Asl2b for the transmission of   - subscriber lines Asl1 which can be connected to a switching device,     - subscriber lines Asl2 which can be switched by the modem,   - a reference potential Ub of the modem M,   - a supply potential Us of the modem M, and - a signal EHC, which can be emitted by the modem M, for effecting a subscriber loop closure in the telephone terminal FE.   In addition, a telephone terminal FE and/or a modem M having an interface connection for such an interface arrangement SA1.
