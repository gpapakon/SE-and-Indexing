<INVENTION-TITLE>
Temperature measurement system.

<ABSTRACT>
The invention describes an arrangement for non-contact temperature measurement on rotors. The measurement is based on the drop in the permeability of ferromagnetic materials in the case of overshooting the Curie temperature. One or more rings (2) of ferromagnetic material are fitted on a rotor (1). With ferromagnetic parts (3) fastened on the stator, these form a magnetic circuit. The magnetic resistance of this circuit is measured and serves as an indicator of the overshooting of a specific temperature of the rotor.
