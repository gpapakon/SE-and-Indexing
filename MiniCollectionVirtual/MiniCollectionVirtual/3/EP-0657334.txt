<INVENTION-TITLE>
Underframe for a railway vehicle for use in transporting containers.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The subject-matter of the invention is an underframe (1) for a rail vehicle which serves for container transport. Supports for foldable container supporting legs (7) in their position of transport are provided on the underframe (1). Each of these supports (8) is a cylinder whose longitudinal axis encloses with the longitudinal axis of the underframe (1) an angle of at least approximately 90 DEG . The cylindrical support (8) can be a tube or a roller and it can be coated with plastics material or consist of plastics material.
