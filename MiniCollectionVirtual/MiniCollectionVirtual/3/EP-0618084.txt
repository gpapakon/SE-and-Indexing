<INVENTION-TITLE>
Identification carrier.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B42D1510	B42D1510	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B42D	B42D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B42D15	B42D15	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Identification carrier, made in particular from paper, board or plastic, having a first identification which is directly visually perceptible in the case of illumination with visible light and a second identification not directly visually perceptible by the human eye without auxiliary means in the case of illumination with visible light, the first and the second identification having coincident geometric elements, the latter essentially forming the outlines or surface elements of the first identification, while they are contained in the second identification in a holographic image.
