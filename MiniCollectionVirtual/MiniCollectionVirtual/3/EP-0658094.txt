<ABSTRACT>
A keratin fibre dyeing composition, in particular for human keratin fibres such as hair, including a medium suitable for use in dyeing and containing at least one oxidation dye precursor selected from 3-methyl para-aminophenol, 2-methyl para-aminophenol and 2-hydroxymethyl para-aminophenol, and acid addition salts thereof; at least one coupler selected from the 2-methyl 5-aminophenols of formula (I), wherein R is hydrogen, methyl, ethyl,  beta -hydroxyethyl or  gamma -hydroxypropyl, and acid addition salts thereof; and at least one ortho-aminophenol selected from ortho-aminophenol and 3-acetylamino 6-aminophenol or an acid addition salt thereof. A dyeing method using an oxidising agent for colour development is also disclosed.
</ABSTRACT>
