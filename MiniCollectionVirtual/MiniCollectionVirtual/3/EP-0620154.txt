<INVENTION-TITLE>
Label with grip strap
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
A label with a grip tab for opening a package in the region of an intended tearing point is characterised in that the grip tab (C) is punched out within the label (1) and adjoins a tear-off region (A) which is bounded by intended tearing lines (3), the grip tab (C) not sticking on both sides, but the label (1) otherwise being provided on one side with an adhesive (6) for permanent bonding.

<CLAIMS>
Label with a grip tab for opening a pack in the 
regiuon of a predetermined breaaking point, characterized 

in that the grip tab (C) is punched out within the label 
(1) and adjoins a tear-open region (A) which is bounded 

by predetermined breaking lines (3), the grip tab (C) 
being non-adhesive on both sides, but the rest of the 

label (1) being provided with an adhesive (6) on one side 
for permanent adhesive bonding. 
Label according to Claim 1 characterized in that 
the grip tab (C) is secured by a spot of adhesive (7). 
Label according to Claim 1, characterized in that 
it has a backing consisting of a plastic film or paper. 
Label according to Claim 1, characterized in that 
the grip tab (C), in the same way as the rest of the 

label (1), is coated with adhesive (6), but the latter is 
covered, or is printed so as to be non-adhesive, in the 

region of said grip tab (C). 
Label according to Claims 1 and 2, characterized 
in that the adhesive (6) and the spot of adhesive (7) are 

applied in a printing operation, in particular gravure 
printing or screen printing, it also being possible for 

the adhesive (6) to comprise individual adhesive segments. 
Use of a label according to one of Claims 1 - 5 
characterized in that a pack with a predetermined 

breaking point has the label with its tear-open region 
adhesively bonded on said predetermined breaking point, 

and the pack can be torn open by pulling at the grip tab 
of the label. 
</CLAIMS>
