Outlet with a vortex flow.

<ABSTRACT>
In an outlet with a vortex flow for insertion into a ceiling (10) and for supplying air into a space, the air comes out of openings (2) in a cover plate (1). In this arrangement, the cover plate (1) is to form with a masking frame (5) an annular air outlet gap (6).
