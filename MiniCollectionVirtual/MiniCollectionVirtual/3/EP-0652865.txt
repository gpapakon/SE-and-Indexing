<ABSTRACT>
Hydrofluorocarbon-containing compounds of formula (I): RF-(CH2)n-X-[C3H5(OH)]-(Y)x)-RH in which C3H5(OH) denotes the structures (Ia) or (Ib) or (Ic), RF is a perfluorinated linear or branched C4-C20 alkyl radical or a mixture of perfluorinated, linear or branched C4-C20 alkyl radicals; RH is a linear or branched C23-C36 alkyl radical or a mixture of linear or branched C23-C36 alkyl radicals; n is 0-4; X is O, S, (a) or (b); x is O or 1; Y is O, S, (a) or (b). The invention also concerns the use of said compounds in cosmetic compositions and cosmetic compositions comprising same.
</ABSTRACT>
