<INVENTION-TITLE>
Accordion.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G10D1100	G10D1100	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G10D	G10D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G10D11	G10D11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Improved accordion, in which one of the end parts constituting the base device (1) includes a box (3) containing the musical system and is equipped with a pull strap (2; 11-12) separated from the end plate or cap (4; 4', 4'') of the said box by a space (7) allowing the passage of the hand, characterised in that said end part is arranged or shaped so that the said space (7) has a constant or approximately constant width (L) over all or almost all of its length; the end plate or cap (4) of the box (3) of the base device (1) having, for example, in the longitudinal direction, a domed central portion (5) located between two concave portions (6).
