<INVENTION-TITLE>
Angular magnetic field detector.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01R33028	G01R33028	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to an angular magnetic field detector comprising deflecting-beam sensors which are orthogonal in pairs. Each of the sensors (12-1...12-4) extends towards the centre of the frame-shaped support (11). The assembly is formed by photolithography of a silicon wafer.
