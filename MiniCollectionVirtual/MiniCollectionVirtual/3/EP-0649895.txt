<INVENTION-TITLE>
Absorbant mat for oily or aqueous fluids
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
An absorbent mat for absorbing oily and aqueous liquids contains a core which is surrounded by a liquid-permeable covering and consists of an absorbent material with storage (reservoir) properties. The core consists of a textile fibre nonwoven (mat) consisting of about 70-80% polypropylene and 30-20% viscose. This fibre nonwoven core is surrounded by a fabric covering consisting of about 60-70% polyester and 40-30% cotton.
