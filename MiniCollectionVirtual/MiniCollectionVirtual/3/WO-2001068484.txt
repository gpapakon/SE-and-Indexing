<INVENTION-TITLE>
AUTOMATIC DISPENSER FOR INJECTION-CONTAINING MEMBERS
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61J700	A61J700	B25J1500	B25J1500	B65B2100	B65B2112	B65B3500	B65B3516	G07F1100	G07F1146	G07F1146	G07F1162	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61J	A61J	B25J	B25J	B65B	B65B	B65B	B65B	G07F	G07F	G07F	G07F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61J7	A61J7	B25J15	B25J15	B65B21	B65B21	B65B35	B65B35	G07F11	G07F11	G07F11	G07F11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
 gripping means and a moving means are installed in a case. And with a packaging box positioned in a predetermined position, the moving means is drive-controlled by a control means on the basis of coordinate data on an injection-containing member prestored in a coordinate data table, whereby the injection- containing member is moved by the gripping means to a location where it can be gripped. Subsequently, the injection-containing member is gripped by the gripping means and then automatically transferred to a desired position.
</ABSTRACT>
