<INVENTION-TITLE>
Platelike apparatus for the conservation of a liquid or powdery product.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B01L314	B01L314	G06K1904	G06K1904	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B01L	B01L	G06K	G06K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B01L3	B01L3	G06K19	G06K19	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Device for preserving a liquid or pulverulent product, including a tube (1) of flexible plastic resistant to lengthwise tension and compression, the said tube containing the liquid or pulverulent product being provided with means for closure (7) at its two ends and with a support (5) for product identification data, characterised in that the support for data (5) is itself also contained in the said tube (1).
