<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for enlarged display of video images.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G06T340	G06T340	G09G536	G09G5391	H04N5262	H04N5262	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G06T	G06T	G09G	G09G	H04N	H04N	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G06T3	G06T3	G09G5	G09G5	H04N5	H04N5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The video frames stored for processing with conventional computing capacities in the form of digitised image data of a reduced image area of a reproduction device can be reproduced with the usual computing and storage capacities on an enlarged, preferably on the full image area of the reproduction device with an improved video resolution if at least one pixel value (X) which is between the values of the adjacent digitised pixel data is in each case inserted between two existing adjacent digitised pixel data (A, B, C, D, E, F).
