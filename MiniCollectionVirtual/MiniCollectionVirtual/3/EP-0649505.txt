SHAFT SEAL

<ABSTRACT>
The invention concerns a seal for a shaft (4) in a dividing wall (1) which separates an oil-containing space (2) from an oil-free space (3) by means of two sealing rings (13, 14). In order to provide a seal which is effective in both directions, the invention proposes that the sealing rings (13, 14) are located immediately next to each other, that their closed sides face each other and that the open side of the ring (14) facing the oil-free space (3) is fitted next to a chamber (15) filled with grease (16).
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
Sealing for a shaft (4) in a partition wall (1), which 
separates an oil-containing area (2) from an oil-free 

area (3), with the aid of two shaft sealing rings (13, 
14), 
characterized in
 that the shaft sealing rings (13, 
14) are disposed directly next to one another in such a 

way that their closed sides lie adjacent to one 
another, and that disposed in front of the open side of 

the sealing ring (14) directed towards the oil-free 
area (3) is a chamber (15) filled with grease (16). 
Shaft seal according to claim 1, 
characterized in
 that 
the shaft sealing rings (13, 14) and the grease chamber 

(15) are situated inside an annular recess (11) in the 
partition wall (1) which surrounds the shaft (4). 
Shaft seal according to claim 2, 
characterized in
 that 
the recess (11) is open towards the oil-free area (3) 

and that a cover (17, 18) is provided for the chamber 
(15), which is directed towards the oil-free area (3) 

and used to receive grease (16). 
Shaft seal according to claim 3, 
characterized in
 that 
the cover comprises a felt washer (17). 
Shaft sealing ring according to claim 4, 
characterized 
in
 that a retaining plate (18) for fixing the fuzz-free 

felt washer (17) in position is provided. 
</CLAIMS>
