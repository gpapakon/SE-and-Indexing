<INVENTION-TITLE>
Polishing and cleaning apparatus.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B24B3100	B24B31027	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B24B	B24B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B24B31	B24B31	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A polishing and cleaning apparatus is described, in particular for tableware and jewellery having at least one rotatably driven polishing container (4) in which the article to be polished is arranged together with balls and soap suds, and which is connected via a rotary carrier connection to a drive unit, said rotary carrier connection being provided with a clearance in the direction of rotation.
