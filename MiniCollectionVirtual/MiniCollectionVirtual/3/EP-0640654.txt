<INVENTION-TITLE>
Thixotropic unsaturated polyester resins.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08J302	C08J309	C08L5100	C08L5108	C08L6700	C08L6706	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08J	C08J	C08L	C08L	C08L	C08L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to thixotropic unsaturated polyester and vinyl ester resins containing inorganic thixotropic agents and, as emulsifiers, a block or graft copolymer of an ethylenically unsaturated monomer and a polyalkylene ether. The mixtures exhibit an extended shelf life.
</ABSTRACT>
