<INVENTION-TITLE>
Apparatus for coating fluid media onto a running paper web.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B05C108	B05C108	B05C900	B05C904	B05C1102	B05C1102	D21H2300	D21H2356	D21H2500	D21H2512	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The appts. has two rollers (1,2), axially parallel to each other, with an applicator (5,6) at each roller mantle to transfer the medium to the paper web (4) passing through the roller gap (3). At least one side of the paper web (4) is in contact with a smoothing unit (10a) in its free path (9) after leaving the roller gap (3).
</ABSTRACT>
