<INVENTION-TITLE>
Instrument panel with single front plate and light boxes.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60K3700	B60K3700	B60Q300	B60Q304	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60K	B60K	B60Q	B60Q	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60K37	B60K37	B60Q3	B60Q3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The dashboard comprises a single front face (1) and light boxes (19-22) for illuminating characters (6) and symbols (5) silk-screen printed on a mask (4) fastened on the front wall (3) of the front face (1). The front face (1) and the light boxes (19-22) are formed in a single piece, the light boxes (19-22) projecting rearwards. The invention is well-suited to dashboards for a motor vehicle.
