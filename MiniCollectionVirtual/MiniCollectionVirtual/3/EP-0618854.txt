<INVENTION-TITLE>
PROCESS FOR MANUFACTURING FIBRE OR PARTICLE BOARDS.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08G1800	C08G1818	C08G1864	C08G1870	C08L9700	C08L9702	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08G	C08G	C08G	C08G	C08L	C08L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C08G18	C08G18	C08G18	C08G18	C08L97	C08L97	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A process is disclosed for manufacturing fibre or particle boards. A polyurethane reaction accelerating catalyst is added to a binder containing NCO groups. Lignocellulose-containing particles or fibres are coated with the binder and then shaped into a mat which is pressed under the influence of heat to form a particle or fibre board.
</ABSTRACT>
