Cleaning composition and process therefor.

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a cleaning composition, in particular for cleaning surfaces of articles, which is characterised by a mixture of the following substances:     a) 35% to 50% of cold-pressed orange peel oil,   b) 35% to 50% of citrus terpenes D-limonene and   c) 5% to 15% of emulsifier for producing separating emulsions.   This produces a low-cost, effective cleaning composition suitable for a large number of applications and of high ecological compatibility.
</ABSTRACT>
