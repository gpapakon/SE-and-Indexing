<CLAIMS>
Process for producing transition metal-fullerene
intercalation compounds,
wherein

the transition metal compounds are reacted in a
solvent with fullerene or fullerene-containing

carbon black.
Process as claimed in claim 1,
wherein

the transition metal compounds are used in the
oxidation state 0.
Process as claimed in claim 1,
wherein

the transition metal carbonyls are used as
transition metal compounds.
Transition metal-fullerene intercalation compounds
obtainable by the process as claimed in one of the

claims 1 to 3.
Compounds as claimed in claim 4,
wherein

the metals are from the 8th side group.
Compounds as claimed in claim 5,
wherein

ligands are additionally bound to the metals. 
Compounds as claimed in claim 6,
wherein

the ligand is a carbonyl ligand.
Compounds as claimed in claim 6,
wherein

the ligand is a dibenzylideneacetone ligand.
Use of compounds as claimed in one of the claims 4
to 8 as catalysts.
</CLAIMS>
