<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to an oscillator with a low harmonic content. It finds an application especially for a remote control signal transmitter. The harmonic oscillator includes a resonant circuit (3), an inductive element of which is used to constitute a low-pass filter (6) which cuts off the frequencies higher than the first harmonic. Application to remote control transmitters.
