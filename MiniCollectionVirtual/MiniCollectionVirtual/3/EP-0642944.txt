<INVENTION-TITLE>
Cooling device for a battery charger and an on-board voltage converter in electric vehicles
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H02J700	H02J700	H05K720	H05K720	B60K104	B60K104	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H02J	H02J	H05K	H05K	B60K	B60K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H02J7	H02J7	H05K7	H05K7	B60K1	B60K1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In a cooling device for a battery charger (BL) inside a vehicle and an on-board voltage converter (BW) in electric vehicles, the battery charger (BL) and the on-board voltage converter (BW) are fixed to a common heat sink plate (KB).  The heat sink plate (KB) is preferably liquid-cooled.

<CLAIMS>
A cooling device for a battery charger (BL) and an on-board transformer 
(BW) inside electric vehicles, characterised in that the battery charger (BL) 

and the on-board transformer (BW) are fastened to a common cooling plate 
(KB). 
A cooling device according to claim 1, characterised in that the cooling plate 
(KB) is liquid-cooled. 
A cooling device according to claim 2, characterised in that a coolant pump 
(KP) is provided for conveying the coolant and can be switched on during 

operation of the battery charger (BL) when the electric vehicle is stationary. 
A cooling device according to claim 3, characterised in that the coolant 
pump (KP) can be switched on in dependence on the temperature. 
A cooling device according to claim 3, characterised in that the coolant 
pump (KP) can be switched on in dependence on time. 
</CLAIMS>
