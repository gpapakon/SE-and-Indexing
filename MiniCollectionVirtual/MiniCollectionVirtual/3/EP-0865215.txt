<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method of least cost telecommunications routing, and apparatus for carrying it out
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H04Q364	H04Q366	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H04Q	H04Q	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H04Q3	H04Q3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The method involves automatically and repeatedly receiving and storing routing data updated and optimised regarding the minimisation of communications tariffs from a service platform (8). The updated and optimised routing data are called up from the service platform by the router (1.1) and stored in the router memory. The interrogation time is determined by adding a randomly generated time interval to a regular time interval. It prevents a number of routing data requests from different routers being incident on the service platform simultaneously.
</ABSTRACT>
