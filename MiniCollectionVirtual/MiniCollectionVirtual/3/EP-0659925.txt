<INVENTION-TITLE>
Washing machine tub with a pressure chamber.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>D06F3908	D06F3908	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>D06F	D06F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>D06F39	D06F39	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a washing-machine tub equipped with a compression chamber. The tub (2) comprises on the inside a compression chamber (21) connected to an air-pressure measuring system (7). The compression chamber is at least partially submerged and is filled in its lower part with water (22) from the tub (2), the pressure of the air contained in its upper part (23) being the image of the water level (1) of the tub. The invention is used for all types of washing machine, the operation of which requires a knowledge of the water level inside the tub.
