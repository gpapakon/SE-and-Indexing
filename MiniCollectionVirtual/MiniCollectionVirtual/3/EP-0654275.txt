Surgical laparoscopic device.

<ABSTRACT>
A surgical laparoscopic device comprises a protective pipe (11) and a handle (12) provided at its rear side. At least one rod-shaped instrument (13) is arranged so as to be axially removable in an axial guide channel (14) provided in the protective pipe (11) and the handle (12). In parallel thereto, a rinsing or suction channel (15) which is guided through the handle (12) to an external rinsing and suction connection (16, 17) extends in the protective pipe and partially in the handle (12). Besides the guide channel (14) the handle comprises a two-position three-way valve (19) which connects the rinsing or suction channel (15) with the rinsing or suction connection (16, 17) in one position and the guide channel (14) with the rinsing or suction connection (16 or 17) in the other position.
