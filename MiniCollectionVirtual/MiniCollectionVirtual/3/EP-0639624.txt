<INVENTION-TITLE>
Phthaloperinone dyestuffs.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08K500	C08K534	C08K53465	C08K541	C09B5700	C09B5712	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08K	C08K	C08K	C08K	C09B	C09B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
Phthaloperinone dyes of the general formula (I) 
<
IMAGE
>
  in which     Z denotes SO2 or CO,   A represents optionally substituted alkyl or aryl, and the other substituents have the meanings given in the description, are prepared by condensation of corresponding phthalic acids and/or functional derivatives thereof and optionally substituted 1,8-napthalenediamines. The dyes according to the invention possess very good fastness properties and are employed in processes for the mass coloration (bulk dyeing) of plastics.
</ABSTRACT>
