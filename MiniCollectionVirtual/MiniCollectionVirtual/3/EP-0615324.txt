<INVENTION-TITLE>
METHOD AND DEVICE FOR OBTAINING AN ELECTRIC DISCHARGE.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H01T100	H01T122	H01T124	H01T200	H01T202	H05H124	H05H148	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H01T	H01T	H01T	H01T	H01T	H05H	H05H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H01T1	H01T1	H01T1	H01T2	H01T2	H05H1	H05H1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The method for producing an electrical discharge, which method is implemented by means of a device for producing an electrical discharge, comprises a layer (4) of the oxide of the cathode material being formed on the surface of a cathode (3) and this layer (4) being accommodated in a vacuum chamber (1), together with an anode (2) which is constructed as a hollow cylinder and surrounds the cathode (3). Equality of the impedances at each point is ensured on the surface of the anode (2) facing the cathode (3). An electrical discharge is initiated in the interspace between the anode (2) and the cathode (3). The movement and extraction of a current of the plasma-forming material from the interspace between the anode (2) and cathode (3) is regulated by means of separating walls (17, 18).
