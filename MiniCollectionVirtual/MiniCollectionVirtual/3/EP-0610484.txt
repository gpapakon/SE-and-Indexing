<INVENTION-TITLE>
DEVICE FOR ENRICHING WATER WITH CO 2? GAS IN ORDER TO GENERATE CARBONATED WATER.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A23L200	A23L200	B01F100	B01F100	B01F304	B01F304	B01F1500	B01F1506	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A23L	A23L	B01F	B01F	B01F	B01F	B01F	B01F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A23L2	A23L2	B01F1	B01F1	B01F3	B01F3	B01F15	B01F15	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A device for enriching water with CO2 gas in order to generate carbonated water has a reservoir which is provided in the area of its walls with cooling pipes of a cooling circuit for cooling its content and for forming an ice envelope (9) in the area of its walls. Carbonated and cooled water taken out of the reservoir is replaced by fresh water. The spraying openings (14) of the water supply pipe arranged in the top space of the reservoir are oriented towards the lateral walls in the area of the generated ice envelope.
</ABSTRACT>
