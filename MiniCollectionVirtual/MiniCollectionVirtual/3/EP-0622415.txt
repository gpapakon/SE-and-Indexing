<INVENTION-TITLE>
THERMOPLASTIC MOULDING COMPOSITION BASED ON POLYALKYLENE TEREPHTHALATE AND ETYLENE-VINYLACETATE COPOLYMERS.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08L3100	C08L3104	C08L6700	C08L6702	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08L	C08L	C08L	C08L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C08L31	C08L31	C08L67	C08L67	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Thermoplastic moulding compositions comprising     A) from 10 to 96 parts by weight of polyalkylene terephthalate, B) from 5 to 60 parts by weight of ethylene-vinyl acetate copolymers, and optionally a flameproofing agent combination comprising C) from 5 to 20 parts by weight of halogen-containing organic compound and D) from 0.5 to 7.5 parts by weight off antimony trioxide or antimony pentoxide, where the sum A + B + C + D is 100.n
</ABSTRACT>
