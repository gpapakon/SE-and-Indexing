<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the preparation of trifluoroethylsulphur compounds of the formula RSCH2CF3 by reaction of 1-chloro-2,2,2-trifluoroethane CH2Cl-CF3 in a polar aprotic solvent with salts of thiols of the formula RSH, where R can be an alkyl or aryl radical or a heterocyclic radical.
