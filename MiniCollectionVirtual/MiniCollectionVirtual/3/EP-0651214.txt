<INVENTION-TITLE>
Cooling or freezing apparatus.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F25D1100	F25D1100	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F25D	F25D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F25D11	F25D11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A cooling or freezing apparatus is provided with a flat or plate-shaped evaporator (5) which is arranged in a housing wall (1) which delimits a cooling space (20) and is provided with a heat insulation (4). In order to be able to cool the cooling space (20) sufficiently even when the power supply of the compressor is temporarily interrupted, a cold store (6) is arranged on the side of the evaporator (5) facing away from the cooling space.
