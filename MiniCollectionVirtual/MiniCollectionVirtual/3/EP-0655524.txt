<INVENTION-TITLE>
Umbrella-type clothes dryer
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Umbrella-type clothes drier with supporting arms consisting of a box profile open on one side, for holding a washing line divided into individual line rings which are held in slides at the corners via clips, and segmental covers with a line passage for closing the supporting arms, the box profile being provided, for the sliding reception of the slides (7), with holding strips (17) and the segmental covers (19, 37, 39) being provided with holding grooves (18) for releasable interlocking.

<CLAIMS>
Rotary clothes drier having carrying arms (4) 
comprising a box profile (15) which is open on one side 

and is intended for securing a clothes line which is 
divided up into individual line rings (5) and is 

retained, via clamps, at the corners in slides, and 
segment coverings (19) with a line through-passage (32) 

for closing off the carrying arms (4), characterized in 
that the box profile (15), for receiving the slides (7) 

in a sliding manner, are provided with retaining strips 
(17) and the segment coverings (19, 37, 39) are provided 

with retaining grooves (18) for mutual releasable 
latching. 
Rotary clothes drier according to Claim 1, 
characterized in that the segment coverings (19, 37, 39) 

are each provided, on both sides, with a grip strip (35) 
such that, by way of these, a force component can be 

exerted on the retaining grooves (18) and retaining 
strips (17). 
Rotary clothes drier according to Claim 1 or 2, 
characterized in that the segment covering (19, 37, 39) 

is of an arcuate design with grip strips (35) adjoining 
at a flat angle. 
Rotary clothes drier according to Claim 2 or 3, 
characterized in that the grip strips (35) form a continuous 

surface with the grip strips (35) of the next 
carrying arm (4). 
Rotary clothes drier according to Claim 3 or 4, 
characterized in that the arcuate region (34) of the 

segment coverings (19, 37, 39) is configured with a wall  
 

thickness which tapers towards the centre (36). 
Rotary clothes drier according to one of Claims 
1 to 5, characterized in that the slide (7) is configured 

to be approximately H-shaped in cross-section with a 

chamber (21) for receiving the line-ring clamp (22) via 
a keyhole (26) in the transverse web (24), and with a 

line chamber (23). 
Rotary clothes drier according to one of Claims 
1 to 6, characterized in that both the segment coverings 

(19) and the slides (7) are divided up in accordance with 
the number of line rings (5). 
Rotary clothes drier according to Claim 7, 
characterized in that the segment covering (19, 37) is 

provided with a recess which is open on one side and 
serves as a line through-passage (32). 
Rotary clothes drier according to one of Claims 
1 to 8, characterized in that the box profile (15) is 

open towards the inside. 
</CLAIMS>
