<CLAIMS>
Fire extinguishing composition containing a thermally
diffusible component, an oxidising agent, an oxygen-containing

binder and conventional auxiliaries, characterised in that the
thermally diffusible component used is an inorganic compound

selected from a group containing chloride, sulphate, sulphide,
phosphate of alkali or alkaline earth metals and ammonium salt

or mixtures thereof and in that, as oxidising agent, it
contains a metal or ammonium nitrate or perchlorate or

mixtures thereof and in that, as binder, it contains a
compound from the group comprising cellulose ester, rubber,

resin, polymers or mixtures thereof, the aforementioned
components being contained in the following ratio in per cent

by weight:

thermally diffusible component
1.0 - 35.0
oxidising agent
50.0 - 94.0
oxygen-containing binder
3.0 - 5.0
conventional auxiliaries
1.0 - 25.0
Composition according to claim 1, characterised in that
it contains K or Na as metal ion of the oxidising agent K or

Na.
Composition according to claim 1 or 2, characterised in
that, as cellulose ester, it contains: ethylene cellulose or

cellulose acetate or cellulose nitrate or a mixture thereof,
as rubber a compound from a group which contains butadiene

acrylonitrile rubber, butyl rubber, urethane rubber, natural
rubber, butadiene styrene rubber,


or mixtures thereof, as polymer a compound selected
from a group which contains chlorinated polyvinyl chloride,

polyvinyl chloride, polyvinyl butyral, polyvinyl acetate or 
mixtures thereof, as resin a compound se
lected from a group
which contains epoxy resin, unsaturated polyester resin,

phenol formaldehyde resin or a mixture thereof.
Composition according to claim 1, characterised in that
the thermally diffusible component used is potassium chloride

in a quantity of 1.0 to 25.0 per cent by weight.
Composition according to claim 2, characterised in that,
as oxidising agent, it contains potassium nitrate and

potassium perchlorate in a quantity of 50.0 to 85.0 per cent
by weight.
Composition according to claim 1 or 3, characterised in
that, as conventional auxiliaries, it contains either a

plasticiser or a combustion modifier or a processing aid or
mixtures thereof.
Composition according to claim 6, characterised in that,
as plasticiser, it contains esters or polyesters from the

series: dibutyl phthalate, dibutyl sebacinate, tributyl
phosphate, glycerine triacetate, tricresyl phosphate or

mixtures thereof; organic nitrates: glycerine nitrate,
dinitrodiethylene glycol, dinitrotriethylene glycol or

mixtures thereof; organic azide, including those in the S-triazide
series such as, for example, 2,4-diazido-6-amino-S-triazine,

2,4-diazido-6-azidoethoxy-S-triazine, 2-azido-4,6-diazidoethoxy-S-triazine
or mixtures thereof; or mixtures of

the aforementioned components.
Composition according to claim 6, characterised in that
the conventional auxiliaries contain the combustion modifier

in a quantity of 1.0 to 15.0 per cent by weight. 
Composition according to claim 8, characterised in that,
as combustion modifier, it contains a compound from the group

comprising carbon, silicon dioxide, aluminium, aluminium
oxide, iron, iron oxide and mixtures thereof.
Composition according to claim 6, characterised in that
the processing aid in it is contained in a quantity of 0.5 to

6.0 per cent by weight.
</CLAIMS>
