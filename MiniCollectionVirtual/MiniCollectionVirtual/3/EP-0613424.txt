MOUNTING FOR CASTORS OR RUNNERS
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The mounting proposed has metal plates (2), each with a mounting pin (21) for one of the castors (3) or runners, injection-moulded into a plastic support frame (1). The support frame (1) is designed to be fixed to the side walls (41) of a cupboard-type container (4) instead of a floor panel.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
Receiving device for rollers (3) and sliding elements, 
said receiving device being so designed as to be capable of 

mounting on the bottom of cabinet-type containers (4), 
characterised in that metal plates (2), each of which is 

provided with a receiving spigot (21) for one of the rollers 
(3) or sliding elements are injection-moulded into a plastic 

carrier (1) capable of being attached, instead of a base 
plate, to side walls (41) of the cabinet-type container (4). 
Receiving device according to Claim 1, characterised in 
that a single plastic carrier (1) with not less than three 

rollers (3) or sliding elements is attached to the side 
walls (41). 
Receiving device according to Claim 1 for cabinet-type 
containers of identical width but different length, 

characterised in that two identical plastic carriers (1) are 
secured to the side walls (41) by way of frontal and rearward 

receiving device. 
Receiving device according to Claim 1 for cabinet-type 
containers of different length and different width, 

characterised in that four identical plastic carriers (1) are 
secured to the bottom of the cabinet-type container (4) in 

each of the four corners. 
Receiving device according to Claim 4, characterised in 
that at least two adjacent plastic carriers (1) are joined to 

one another with the aid of fitted hollow sections. 
Receiving device according to one of Claims 1 to 5, 
characterised in that dowels (12) are laterally moulded to the 

plastic carrier (1), said dowels being capable of insertion 
into the wood or chipboard panels constituting the sidewalls 

(41).  
 
Receiving device according to Claim 6, characterised in 
that the sidewall (41) is, in addition, supported by a web 

(13) and screwed to the latter. 
Receiving device according to one of Claims 1 to 5, 
characterised in that receiving spigots (14) for thin 

sidewalls (42) are laterally moulded to the plastic 
carrier (1). 
Receiving device according to one of Claims 1 to 8, 
characterised in that the spigots (21) for the rollers (3) are 

fitted in such a way that the latter partly protrude into the 
plastic carrier (1). 
Receiving device according to one of Claims 1 to 9, 
characterised in that screw-on lugs (15) for securing a dust 

cover (44) are provided on the plastic carrier (1). 
Receiving device according to one of Claims 1 to 10, 
characterised in that the plastic carrier (1) is provided with 

fasteners (16) for covering strips. 
</CLAIMS>
