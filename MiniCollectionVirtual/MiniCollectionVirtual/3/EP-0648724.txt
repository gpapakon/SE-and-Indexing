<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the preparation of 1-Bromo-3,5-difluorobenzene
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The process is characterised in that 1-bromo-2,4-difluorobenzene or a mixture of 1-bromo-2,4-difluorobenzene and 1-bromo-2,6-difluorobenzene is isomerised on acidic zeolites of the pentasil type.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
A process for preparing 1-bromo-3,5-difluorobenzene, 
which comprises isomerizing 1-bromo-2,4-difluorobenzene 

or a mixture of 1-bromo-2,4-difluorobenzene 
and 1-bromo-2,6-difluorobenzene over acid zeolites 

of the pentasil type. 
The process as claimed in claim 1, wherein the 

zeolite used has the crystal structure of ZSM-5. 
The process as claimed in claims 1 and 2, wherein 
the SiO
2
/Al
2
O
3
 ratio of the zeolite used is between 
22 and 200, in particular between 24 and 100. 
The process as claimed in at least one of claims 1 
to 3, wherein at least 90% of the cations are 

protons or ammonium ions. 
The process as claimed in at least one of claims 1 
to 4, wherein the reaction temperature is between 

250 and 500°C, in particular between 275 and 450°C. 
The process as claimed in at least one of claims 1 
to 5, wherein the reaction is carried out continuously 

in a fixed-bed flow reactor. 
The process as claimed in at least one of claims 1 
to 6, wherein the process is carried out in the 

presence of gases, preferably hydrogen.  
 
The process as claimed in at least one of claims 1 
to 7, wherein the space velocity is 0.1 to 5, in 

particular 0.2 to 2, l of feed per l of catalyst per 
hour. 
The process as claimed in at least one of claims 1 
to 8, wherein the reaction pressure equals 

atmospheric pressure. 
The process as claimed in at least one of claims 1 
to 5, wherein the feed mixture/catalyst weight ratio 

in the batchwise isomerization is from 1:1 to 
1000:1, in particular from 5:1 to 50:1. 
</CLAIMS>
