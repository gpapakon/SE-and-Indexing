<INVENTION-TITLE>
Preformed compressed composite panel for installation as a thermal insulation in cooking and heating devices.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B32B1900	B32B1904	F24C1500	F24C1534	F24H902	F24H902	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B32B	B32B	F24C	F24C	F24H	F24H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B32B19	B32B19	F24C15	F24C15	F24H9	F24H9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention proposes a composite panel which can be produced in one operation, pressed from one or more layers and, based on perlite as heat-insulating material, for installation and for heat insulation, preferably in cooking and heating appliances, is characterized in that the highly heat-insulating basic panel (4) is protected against thermal and/or mechanical overloading by a protective layer (5) which is produced in composite arrangement and in one pressing operation with said basic panel.
