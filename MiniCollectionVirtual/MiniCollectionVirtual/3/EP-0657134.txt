<INVENTION-TITLE>
Dispenser for paper rolls and suitable paper roll therefor
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
A dispenser for toilet-paper rolls is disclosed, having a box-like housing which has laterally vertically running guides (12) for receiving bearing journals attached to the rolls, the guides each being provided with an introduction section (15) pointing to the front side of the housing. An inwardly projecting guiding element (14) is arranged in the introduction section (15) at least in one of the two lateral guides (12), said guiding element fitting into a recess (13), running in the axial direction, of at least one of the bearing journals (10, 11) of a paper roll (3; 4).

<CLAIMS>
Dispenser for paper rolls, in particular toilet paper rolls or the 
like, with a box-like housing which has lateral vertically extending 

guides for receiving bearing pins mounted on the rolls, the guides 
each being provided with an entry portion leading to the front face 

of the housing, characterised in that an inwardly projecting guide 
element (14) is provided in the entry portion (15) of at least one of 

the two lateral guides (12) and matches an axially extending recess 
(13) in at least one of the bearing pins (10, 11) of a paper roll (3; 

4). 
Dispenser according to claim 1, characterised in that the guide 
element (14) is arranged substantially centrally in the associated 

entry portion (15). 
Dispenser according to claim 1 or 2, characterised in that the guide 
element (14) is a web extending parallel to the lower edge of the 

entry portion (15) of the associated guide (12). 
Dispenser according to claim 3, characterised in that the web (14) 
terminates within the entry portion (15) and before the vertical 

guide (12). 
Dispenser according to claim 3 or 4, characterised in that the guide 
element is a guide metal sheet (14). 
Paper roll for a dispenser according to one of claims 1 to 5, with 
plugs arranged on its ends each of which has a bearing pin fitting 

into one of the guides in the housing of the dispenser, characterised  
 

in that the bearing pin (10; 11) of at least one of the two plugs (3 
and 4) contains a recess (13) which can be guided past the guide 

element (14) of the housing (2) and fits it. 
Paper roll according to claim 6, characterised in that the recess 
(13) in the one bearing pin (11) is a groove extending in the 

direction of the axis of the pin (11).
