<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an aqueous composition which is time-stable and which comprises an organic peroxiacid and at least one thickening agent with a concentration such that the viscosity of the composition is higher than 100 mPa.s, as well the use of such composition, particularly as a disinfectant and/or cleaning agent.
