<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for removing a filter cake-base layer.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B04B1100	B04B1108	B04B1500	B04B1506	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B04B	B04B	B04B	B04B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B04B11	B04B11	B04B15	B04B15	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The device incorporates a peeling tool and a jet unit. The jet (10) has a moveable carrier (13) with jet head (14) and jet connector (20), which is fixed relative to the peeling tool. The jet carrier is movable on the jet connector between a retracted rest position, and a working position near the centrifugal drum. The jet carrier is moved into working position to blow off the base layer, and is returned into rest position, when the process is finished. It is locked in this position by the spring drive of a spring (15). This is located between the jet carrier and connector.
</ABSTRACT>
