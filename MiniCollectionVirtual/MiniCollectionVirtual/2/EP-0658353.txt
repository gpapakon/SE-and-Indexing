<INVENTION-TITLE>
Pyruvate containing CAPD- and substitution solutions and pyruvate containing dialysis solutions.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61K31185	A61K3119	A61M128	A61M128	A61M134	A61M134	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61K	A61K	A61M	A61M	A61M	A61M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61K31	A61K31	A61M1	A61M1	A61M1	A61M1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Dialysis solutions for purifying blood by all types of kidney replacement therapy, especially peritoneal dialysis, and replacement solutions for use in haemofiltration and haemodiafiltration which contain sodium pyruvate as buffer substance, a substance which makes adequate correction of metabolic acidosis possible and in use has advantages for the macrophage system and for the cells of the peritoneum are described.
 
</ABSTRACT>
