<INVENTION-TITLE>
Arrangement for protecting against all unauthorized use by deactivating the clutch, a vehicle or a machine equipped with a clutch.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60R2500	B60R2500	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60R	B60R	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60R25	B60R25	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
1. Device for protecting against any unauthorised use, by deactivating the clutch, a vehicle or a machine against theft, of the type comprising means for locking the clutch in the disengaged position which consist of a bolt (2) kinematically connected to the clutch fork (3) cooperating with a stationary part of the engine compartment, the said bolt (2) driven in a relative motion with respect to the stationary part of the engine block being capable of being connected to the latter during disengagement of the clutch by the locking member (1) occupying an active position, so that any return of the clutch fork into the position in which the clutch is engaged is prevented.
