<INVENTION-TITLE>
Stowage compartment for camping vehicle.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60P332	B60P336	B62D4300	B62D4304	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60P	B60P	B62D	B62D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60P3	B60P3	B62D43	B62D43	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The proposal is for a stowage compartment for camping vehicles such as motor homes, caravans or the like, which is arranged below the vehicle floor and permits improved accessibility. This is achieved according to the invention by virtue of the fact that a stowage container (3) is provided, which container has a loading opening (5) on its upper side and is installed displaceably below the vehicle floor of the camping vehicle (1).
