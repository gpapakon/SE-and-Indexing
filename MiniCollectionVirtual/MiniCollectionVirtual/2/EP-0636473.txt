<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a thermoformable multilayer (composite) film which has a polyolefin-containing upper film and at least one lower film. The lower film or films contain(s) certain amounts by weight of a at least one partially crosslinked EPDM, b a propylene homopolymer, copolymer or graft polymer, c at least one polymer and/or ionomer containing reactive groups, and d at least one polymeric crosslinking agent containing reactive groups.
</ABSTRACT>
