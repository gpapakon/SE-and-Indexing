<INVENTION-TITLE>
Polyalkylene terephthalate injection moulding compositions.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B29C4500	B29C4500	B29K6700	C08J504	C08J504	C08K300	C08K300	C08K322	C08K700	C08K714	C08L6700	C08L6700	C08L6702	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
Glass-fibre- and/or filler-reinforced thermoplastic polyalkylene terephthalate moulding compositions and the use of polyolefins in polyalkylene terephthalate moulding compositions, where the moulding composition contains the following components:   A: from 92 to 99.9% by weight, preferably from 95 to 99.9% by weight, in particular from 96 to 99.5% by weight of polyalkylene terephthalate, B: from 0.1 to 8% by weight, preferably from 0.1 to 5% by weight, in particular from 0.5 to 4% by weight of polyolefin and C: from 10 to 60% by weight, preferably from 10 to 50% by weight (based on the sum of components A and B) of glass fibres and/or fillers, and, if desired, additionally flameproofing agents.k
</ABSTRACT>
