Paints on the basis of polyamide powders suitable for the coating of PVC profiles.

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a coating composition for PVC profiles, based on a binder, a crosslinker and a thermoplastic powder. It is particularly useful for coating PVC profiles for windows, shutters and doors. The PVC window/door frames thus coated have exceptional impact, abrasion, friction and weather resistance.
</ABSTRACT>
