<INVENTION-TITLE>
Air inlet duct for a gas turbine power unit.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F02B6300	F02B6304	F02B7711	F02B7713	F02C704	F02C7045	F02C724	F02C724	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
Power system (1) with a gas turbine (6), which is arranged in a sound-insulated housing (2), which has just one common inlet and outlet opening (3) each respectively for the cooling air and the combustion air. The common air inlet passage (19, 20) has double walls (11, 18), the cooling air to the engine compartment (15) being led through the gap (19) between them.
