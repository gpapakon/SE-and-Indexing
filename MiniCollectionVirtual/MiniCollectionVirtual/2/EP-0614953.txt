<INVENTION-TITLE>
UV-flexographic ink, method of producung and use.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47K108	A47K108	A47K1000	A47K1010	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47K	A47K	A47K	A47K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47K1	A47K1	A47K10	A47K10	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
New cationic, UV-curable flexographic-printing dyes contain the following (all w.r.t. total wt.): (a) 1-30% pigments and/or dyes; (b) 1-15% triarylsulphonium salt as cationic photoinitiator; (c) polyhydroxy-functional component comprising 1-10% hydroxy-functional silicone and 0.5-3% cellulose acetobutyrate; (d) 0.1-8% additives chosen from waxes, slip additives, defoaming agent de-airing agent, processing aid, wetting agents, dispersants or other opt. additives; and (e) up to 100% cationic curable components based on 30-70% cycloaliphatic diepoxide, 1-10% glycidyl (meth)acrylate (co)polymer and opt. 5-30% low viscosity epoxy resin reactive thinner. Prepn. of the dyes is also claimed.
</ABSTRACT>
