<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process and device for the dry purification of hot gases.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B01D5306	B01D5308	B01D5350	B01D5350	B01D5368	B01D5368	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B01D	B01D	B01D	B01D	B01D	B01D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B01D53	B01D53	B01D53	B01D53	B01D53	B01D53	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
To remove compounds containing fluorine, chlorine and/or sulphur from hot gases, in particular waste gases, it is proposed in the way of dry purification that the gases controlled to a temperature of below 850 @C be contacted with an absorbent which contains calcium carbonate incompletely converted into calcium oxide. To carry out the process, there is proposed a hot gas absorber having a multi-region absorber housing and an integrated recuperator for achieving process-integrated waste gas purification.
