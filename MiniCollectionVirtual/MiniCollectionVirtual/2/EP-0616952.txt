<INVENTION-TITLE>
Closure cap.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65D5100	B65D5100	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65D	B65D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65D51	B65D51	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A closure cap for use on the mouth of a pharmaceutical bottle (1), comprising a seal (3) which is provided with a pierceable removal point (2), is made of elastomer material, and can be pressed onto the mouth by means of a closure capsule (4) made of plastic in the region of the base. The closure capsule (4) has at least one piercing point (5) which is at least partially congruent with the removal point (2) and can itself be pierced without prior removal of constituents of the closure capsule (4).
