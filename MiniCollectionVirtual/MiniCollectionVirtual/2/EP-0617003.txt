<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the preparation of methyle formiate.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B01J3102	B01J3102	C07B6100	C07B6100	C07C6700	C07C6736	C07C6900	C07C6906	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B01J	B01J	C07B	C07B	C07C	C07C	C07C	C07C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B01J31	B01J31	C07B61	C07B61	C07C67	C07C67	C07C69	C07C69	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Methyl formate is prepared by reaction of carbon monoxide and methanol at elevated pressure and elevated temperature in the presence of an alkali metal methoxide by   A) mixing the starting substances in a mixing zone, partially allowing them to react, saturating the reaction solution with CO and B) carrying the reaction to completion in one or more subsequent reaction zones without addition of further starting compounds.
</ABSTRACT>
