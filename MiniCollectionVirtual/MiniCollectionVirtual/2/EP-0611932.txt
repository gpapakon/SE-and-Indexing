<INVENTION-TITLE>
Drawer-like container.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47B8800	A47B8800	F25D2502	F25D2502	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47B	A47B	F25D	F25D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47B88	A47B88	F25D25	F25D25	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In the case of a drawer-like container, in particular a container intended for cooling or freezing apparatuses and used for the storage of refrigerated or frozen food, the container space of which is enclosed by substantially closed walls, the front wall being fitted, on its outside facing away from the container space, with a handle for moving the container, additional handles for transporting the container are provided on the side walls, besides the handle arranged on the front wall.
