<INVENTION-TITLE>
Clamp for fixing plate elements on a base.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F16B500	F16B500	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F16B	F16B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F16B5	F16B5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In particular for fastening solar modules (SM) over a base (UL), the invention proposes a clamping element which has an approximately planar bearing surface (AF) and exhibits a guide (ES) for an upper part (AT) which can be plugged in and is provided with clamping jaws (KB). Said upper part and the corresponding lower part (UT) with the bearing surface are manufactured from sheet-steel profiles and make it possible to fasten one or two adjacent flat bodies such that the latter do not tilt.
