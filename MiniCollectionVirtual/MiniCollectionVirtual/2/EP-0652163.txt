<INVENTION-TITLE>
Dual container for multiple components products
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65D2102	B65D8132	B65D2102	B65D8132	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65D	B65D	B65D	B65D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65D21	B65D81	B65D21	B65D81	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A dual container for multiple-component products, in particular two-component adhesives, comprising a container (1) with a receiving lid (2) having an undercut (7), and a plug-on container (3) which is provided at its lower fold edge (4, 5) with a bead engaging behind the undercut, and in which the surface of the receiving lid (2) is fitted with a peripheral groove (6), and the groove (6) is fitted at its upper inner edge with a projecting outer bead (8) having an outside diameter corresponding to the inside diameter of the fold edge (4) of the plug-on container (3), and the bottom fold (5) being set inwards, calibrated to an extent which is less than the extent of the outer bead (8).

<CLAIMS>
Double container for multiple component products, particularly a
two-component adhasive, consisting of a container (1) with a receiving lid

(2) having an undercut, and with a push-on container (3), which has
beneath its base a folded edge (4, 5) with a bead engaging behind the

undercut, characterised in that area orf the receiving lid (2) is formed
with a surrounding groove (6), and the groove (8) is provided at its upper

inner edge with a projecting outer rib (8), the diameter of which
corresponds with the inner diameter of the folded edge (4) of the push-on

container (3), and the base fold (5) is inwardly limited to a
circumference less than the circumference of the outer rib (8).
