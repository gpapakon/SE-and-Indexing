<INVENTION-TITLE>
Commercial deep freezer.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47F304	A47F304	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47F	A47F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47F3	A47F3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
This commercial deep freezer has an article space (8), the upper side of which can be covered by a roll top (4). The roll top (4) is connected to a winding-up device (3) fixed to the commercial deep freezer. The winding-up device (3) is arranged on the upper side of the central divider (1) running parallel to the winding-up device (3) and subdividing the article space (8) into two partial spaces.
