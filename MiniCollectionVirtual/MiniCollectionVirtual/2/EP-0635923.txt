Circuit for protection against high energy overvoltages with controlled voltage clipping.

<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a circuit for protection against overvoltages consisting of a voltage-clipping component (Z2) arranged between two of its inputs (E1, E2), which further comprises a current-limiting component (CLD) connected between one (E1) of the inputs and a terminal (T1) for connection to this input.
