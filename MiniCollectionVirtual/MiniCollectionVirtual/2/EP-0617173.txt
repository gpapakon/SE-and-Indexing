<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method and apparatus for cleaning a porous road surface.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E01H100	E01H110	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E01H	E01H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>E01H1	E01H1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The method comprises the following practically simultaneous steps:     a) a liquid is poured onto a first surface portion (4) of the roadway (1) so as to fill the underlying pores and to form a volume of liquid (8) adjacent to the first surface portion, above the latter; b) ultrasonic vibrations are applied to the said volume of liquid, these vibrations preferably having a frequency lying between 20 kHz and 1 MHz; and c) the liquid contained in the pores is sucked through a second surface portion (10) of the roadway which is close to the first surface portion.
