Apparatus for playing lottery games.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
An apparatus for playing games, in particular prize games, in particular lottery games, comprises at least one playing unit (game unit) 10', 12'. The at least one playing unit 10', 12' comprises a first read/write apparatus 18', 20' for reading data from a data medium 22' and for writing data to the data medium 22', a picture reproduction apparatus 14' for reproducing information relevant to the game and data read from the data medium 22', a data storage medium for storing data relevant to the game, a command input apparatus 50' for inputting input commands, in particular playing commands (game commands), and a central computing unit for controlling the playing unit 10', 12'.
