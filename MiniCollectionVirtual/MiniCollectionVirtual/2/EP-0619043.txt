<INVENTION-TITLE>
RECORDING SUBSTRATE WITH A CASING AND CLOSURE THEREFOR.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G11B2303	G11B2303	G11B23087	G11B23087	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G11B	G11B	G11B	G11B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G11B23	G11B23	G11B23	G11B23	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In a recording substrate in a casing with an aperture temporarily covered by a movable closure, the latter is made of polyacetal with under 0.1 % wt lubricant. This closure may be used for any kind of recording substrate, especially magnetic media with a closable casing aperture and cassettes with closable frontal apertures.
</ABSTRACT>
