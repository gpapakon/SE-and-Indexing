<INVENTION-TITLE>
Anti-fraud electronic payment token and manufacturing process.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G06K19077	G06K19077	G07F100	G07F106	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G06K	G06K	G07F	G07F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G06K19	G06K19	G07F1	G07F1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an electronic payment token mainly characterised in that it includes an electronic contactless read circuit (3, 4) and a set of mechanical parts (2, 5, 6) which is composed of materials of different kinds such as to afford mechanical toughness to the token, physical protection of the circuit and contactless reading of the circuit. It also relates to a process for manufacturing the electronic token. Application to electronic payment.
