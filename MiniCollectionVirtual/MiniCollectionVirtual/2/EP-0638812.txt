<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for monitoring electrical faults in a tripping device of an electrical installation.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01R31327	G01R31327	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01R	G01R	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01R31	G01R31	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device and a method for monitoring for electrical faults in tripping circuits of electrical installations, especially of power switches (circuit breakers) in medium-voltage or high-voltage switchgear, the trip coil 2 of which tripping circuits is connected via a supply line 5 to a remotely arranged tripping contact 3. In order to facilitate accurate fault analysis, provision is made according to the invention for the step response of the tripping circuit 5, 1, 2 to a voltage step applied to the line input E to be detected, for the temporal evolution of the step response to be divided into discrete temporal regions and for a comparison to be made, related to the regions, of the evolution of the step response to the step response determined in the case of fault-free operation.
