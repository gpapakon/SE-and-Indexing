<INVENTION-TITLE>
Rucksack, in particular for walking and climbing.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A45F304	A45F304	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A45F	A45F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A45F3	A45F3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Rucksack (1) able to be connected to another piece of equipment such as a climbing harness (4). The climbing harness (4) does not itself contain any additional parts. Instead, connecting means such as two quick-release clips (17A, 17B) on the rucksack (1) and two independent connecting sleeves (19A,19B) are provided for attaching the belt (5) of the harness to the base of the rucksack (1).
