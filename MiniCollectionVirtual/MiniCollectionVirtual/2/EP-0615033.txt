<INVENTION-TITLE>
Beam wall or the like with a sealing
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E04B270	E04B270	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E04B	E04B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>E04B2	E04B2	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The wall comprising bars, planks, boards or the like has a seal which is formed by a sheeting strip (1). Said sheeting strip (1) is laid in the gap (2) which is provided in the case of a groove-and-tongue connection comprising the cutout (3) and the protrusion (4). The sheeting strip bears in an elastic manner on the walls of the gap.
