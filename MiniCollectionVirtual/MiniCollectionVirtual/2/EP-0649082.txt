<INVENTION-TITLE>
Pocket calculator data transmission apparatus.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G06F116	G06F116	G06F1502	G06F1502	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a data transmission device for a pocket computer (briefcase computer, hand-held computer) having a data transmission modem for the wire-free transmission of data, which modem can be electrically connected to a data bus of the pocket computer by means of a data bus plug-in device. For simple and convenient handling of the combination of pocket computer and data transmission device, it is provided that the data transmission device has a housing suitable for accommodating the pocket computer in a detachable manner, the data bus plug-in device being arranged on the housing in such a way that it can be pushed into the data bus opening of the pocket computer.
