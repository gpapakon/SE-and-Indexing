<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for reducing the concentration of pollutants in waste water from industrial processes.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B01D6142	B01D6144	C02F1469	C02F1469	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B01D	B01D	C02F	C02F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B01D61	B01D61	C02F1	C02F1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In a process for reducing the concentration of pollutants in waste waters from industrial processes by means of electrodialysis, the waste waters are separated into a concentrate enriched with pollutants and a diluate depleted in pollutants. To reduce the pollutant concentration, the concentrate is circulated through a container filled with concentrate, a substream bled from the container is subjected to precipitation to form a solid phase of pollutants and the residual water thus formed is recirculated to the container filled with concentrate.
