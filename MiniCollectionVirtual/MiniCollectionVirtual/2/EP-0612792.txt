<CLAIMS>
A process for preparation of bead-form, 
expandable styrene polymers by polymerizing styrene, in 

the presence or absence of further comonomers, in aqueous 
suspension in the presence of suspension stabilizers and 

conventional styrene-soluble polymerization catalysts and 
with addition of a blowing agent and with or without 

addition of conventional additives in effective amounts, 
where the aqueous suspension contains, based on the 

monomers, from 0.005 to 1.5% by weight of a polyethylene 
wax and from 0.005 to 1% by weight of dimeric Î±-methylstyrene, 

wherein the suspension stabilizer used is 
polyvinylpyrrolidone. 
A process as claimed in claim 1, wherein the 
polymerization catalysts employed are a combination of 

dibenzoyl peroxide and dicumyl peroxide. 
</CLAIMS>
