<INVENTION-TITLE>
Apparatus for compacting plastic containers, in particular bottles.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B30B932	B30B932	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B30B	B30B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B30B9	B30B9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an apparatus for compacting plastic containers, in particular bottles. It includes, in combination, a receptacle 9 of substantially cylindrical shape equipped with an inlet orifice 9a at one of its ends and with an outlet orifice 9b at its other end, and a crushing member 10 having a lower part 12 of downward-facing convex shape, capable of being displaced between a first position in which it is located outside the receptacle 9 and a second position in which its lower part 12 is located in the vicinity of the lower part of the receptacle 9, after having crushed a container.
