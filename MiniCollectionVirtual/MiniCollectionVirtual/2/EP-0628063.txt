<ABSTRACT>
Described is a detachable heat-sealing plastic, in particular polystyrene, sheet for closing containers, the sheet consisting of a single biaxially oriented high-impact polystyrene film, in particular a film made of high-impact polystyrene mixed with a styrene/butadiene-two-block copolymer.
</ABSTRACT>
<APPLICANTS>
</APPLICANTS>
<INVENTORS>
</INVENTORS>
<CLAIMS>
Use of a material consisting of impact-resistant polystyrene mixed up with a 
styrene-butadiene-biblock-copolymer as a detachable, heat-sealing, biaxially oriented plastic 

sheet formed from only one layer for the closing of containers, e.g. cups, of plastic-material. 
Use of a material according to claim 1, said material consisting of 20 percent of weight 
to 80 percent of weight of impact-resistant polystyrene and of 2 percent of weight to 80 percent of 

weight of styrene-butadiene-biblock-copolymer. 
</CLAIMS>
