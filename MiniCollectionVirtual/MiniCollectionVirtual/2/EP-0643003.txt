<INVENTION-TITLE>
Dispenser for at least two rolls of foil.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65H3500	B65H3500	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65H	B65H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
A dispenser for at least two foil rolls 2, 13 has a separate housing 1 and 9 for each roll, with a severing device 22 for severing a portion of foil. The housings 1, 9 are arranged one above the other, the first lower housing 1 being designed for mounting on a wall 7, whilst the second upper housing 9 is mounted removably on the first housing 1.
