<INVENTION-TITLE>
CUTTING ROTOR.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B02C1806	B02C1818	B26D726	B26D726	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B02C	B02C	B26D	B26D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B02C18	B02C18	B26D7	B26D7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A cutting rotor has knives (11) clamped in recesses of the rotor body (10) and provided with a flat clamping surface (115) against which lies a wedge-shaped clamping element (12) detachably linked to the rotor body by a screw (13). The knives may thus be secured by means of standard parts.
</ABSTRACT>
