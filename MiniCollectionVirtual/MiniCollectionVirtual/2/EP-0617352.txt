<INVENTION-TITLE>
Control and regulating device.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a control and regulating device for boilers, consisting of a device housing (1) in and on which a timing clock (2) and other switching and operating elements used for control and regulation are arranged. In order to ensure that the running accuracy of the timing clock is always guaranteed and also that switching over the clock is carried out automatically at time changes, the timing clock (2) arranged in the device housing (1) is provided, according to the invention, with a receiver module (3) for the broadcast clock signal DCF-77.
</ABSTRACT>
