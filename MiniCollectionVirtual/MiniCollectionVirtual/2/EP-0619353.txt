<INVENTION-TITLE>
Adhesive for attaching hard material layers on metallic substrates.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08J512	C08J512	C08L6100	C08L6104	C09J10100	C09J10100	C09J10108	C09J10128	C09J16100	C09J16100	C09J16106	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08J	C08J	C08L	C08L	C09J	C09J	C09J	C09J	C09J	C09J	C09J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C08J5	C08J5	C08L61	C08L61	C09J101	C09J101	C09J101	C09J101	C09J161	C09J161	C09J161	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Adhesive for attaching flexible plastics bodies contg. hard substances and/or soldering powder to metallic substrates prior to fusing the soldering powder, comprises 57-94.9 wt.% ethyl acetate, 5-25 wt.% ethyl cellulose, 0.1-10 wt.% phenolic resin, 0-3 wt.% SiO2 and/or B2O3 powder and 0-5 wt.% of a plasticiser.
</ABSTRACT>
