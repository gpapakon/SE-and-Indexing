<INVENTION-TITLE>
Portable telephone directory and telephone dialler comprising a voice recognition device.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H04M127	H04M127	H04M1274	H04M1275	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H04M	H04M	H04M	H04M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H04M1	H04M1	H04M1	H04M1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
This apparatus includes: a memory containing a series of telephone numbers; means of addressing this memory; dialling means, capable of converting the number addressed into a sequence of multi-frequency signals; and a first transducer (5), able to convert this sequence into acoustic signals and suitable for being coupled to a telephone handset microphone. According to the invention: the memory further contains, for each number, at least one associated voice signature; a second transducer (4) is provided, able to pick up a sought name spoken by the user; voice recognition means are provided, capable of analysing the name picked up and converting it into an associated voice signature; and the addressing means comprise associator means, able to find information in the memory corresponding to that supplied by the voice recognition means and address the memory at the corresponding position.
