<CLAIMS>
Multiple-stand rolling train for the longitudinal 
rolling of seamless steel pipes in a continuous rolling 

operation by means of an inner tool using two-roller 
stands offset by 90 degrees relative to each other, 

with correspondingly calibrated rollers radially 
adjustable relative to each other, characterised in 

that at least the rollers of the final roll stand in 
the direction of rolling (n ) are divided in their 

central plane intersecting the roller axis (1) at 
right-angles, and the two halves (2, 3) of the divided 

roller are axially adjustable relative to each other. 
</CLAIMS>
