<CLAIMS>
Ternary elemental compounds, characterised in that the 
elements are aluminium, boron and phosphorus. 
Ternary elemental compounds according to claim 1, 
characterised in that each element is present in the 

range of from 1 to 98 mol%. 
Ternary elemental compounds according to claims 1 or 
2, characterised in that boron is present in a 

quantity of at least 50 mol%. 
Ternary elemental compounds according to one or more 
of claims 1 to 3, characterised in that boron is 

present in a quantity of at least 85 mol%. 
Ternary elemental compounds according to claim 4, 

characterised in that they consist predominantly of 
icosahedral B
12
 units, between which are situated 
aluminium atoms, phosphorus atoms and optionally boron 

atoms. 
Ternary elemental compounds according to claim 4, 
characterised in that they possess a rhombohedral 

crystal symmetry. 
Ternary elemental compounds according to one or more 
of claims 1 to 6, characterised in that the average 

particle size is more than 80 µm. 
Method for the preparation of ternary elemental 
compounds according to one or more of claims 1 to 7, 

characterised in that mixtures of aluminium phosphide 
and boron or aluminium phosphide and boron phosphide  

 
or aluminium phosphide and boron and boron phosphide 

are caused to react in liquid aluminium. 
Method according to claim 8, characterised in that the 
reaction is carried out at temperatures of between 

1100°C and 1800°C. 
Method according to claim 8 and 9, characterised in 
that the cooling rates after ending the reaction are 

between 0.1 and 30 K/min. 
Method according to one or more of claims 8 to 10, 
characterised in that the weight ratio of aluminium 

matrix and educt mixture is greater than four. 
Use of the ternary elemental compounds according to 
one or more of claims 1 to 11 as abrasive grains in 

abrasive materials. 
</CLAIMS>
