A fastener driving tool for driving anchorable fasteners.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
A fastener driving tool for driving anchorable fasteners (1) is proposed, the fastener having a shank and a head (3). To permit insertion of the fasteners even at places of poor accessibility, a centring hole (10) is made in the head (3) of the fastener (1), into which centring hole a centring pin (11) arranged on the driving face (9) of the driving tool (8) engages.
