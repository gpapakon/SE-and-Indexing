<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the accomplishment of gas-solid reactions in which the solid reactant contains a salt which is at least partially hydrated.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B01J1900	B01J1900	C01F500	C01F500	C01F530	C01F534	C09K500	C09K516	F25B1700	F25B1708	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The process makes it possible to shift the position of the equilibrium straight line of the reaction under consideration and to decrease its slope in a Clapeyron diagram so as to cover a broader temperature region for a restricted pressure range.
</ABSTRACT>
