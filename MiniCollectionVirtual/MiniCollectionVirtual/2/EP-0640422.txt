Circular saw blade.

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a metal circular saw blade with a toothed cutting rim and a central clamping hole, characterized by at least one annular damping zone (6) which is arranged between the clamping hole (4) and the toothed rim (7) and by a multiplicity of damping webs (8) separated from one another by separating slots (9), arranged laterally next to one another and extending towards the toothed rim (7).
