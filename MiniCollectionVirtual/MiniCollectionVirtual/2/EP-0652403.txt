<INVENTION-TITLE>
Improvements to incinerators for liquids, having one or more oxy-fuel burners.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F23D1110	F23D1110	F23G544	F23G544	F23G700	F23G700	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F23D	F23D	F23G	F23G	F23G	F23G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F23D11	F23D11	F23G5	F23G5	F23G7	F23G7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Incinerator (1) for liquid materials comprising one or more burners (2) functioning according to the principle of oxy-fuel burners, characterised in that, in the burner (2), the fuel is atomised with the aid of oxygen or air enriched with oxygen which is fed into the cone (41) for atomisation of the fuel and in that the installation comprises at least one lance (4) for injection of the liquid to be incinerated.
