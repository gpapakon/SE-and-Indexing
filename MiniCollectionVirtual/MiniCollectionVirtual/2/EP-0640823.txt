<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device and method for examining of hollow space.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01M328	G01M328	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01M	G01M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01M3	G01M3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The pipeline checking device uses a pair of test heads inserted in the examined conduit or pipeline via a displacement device, each having two test bodies. (1A, 1B; 2A, 2B) coupled together via a coupling of adjustable length. Each test body has a respective sealing device, cooperating with the walls of the conduit or the pipeline to allow a section of the pipeline or conduit to be isolated for leakage testing using a pressurised fluid source. Pref. the coupling between the test bodies uses a hydraulic cylinder attached to one body receiving a sliding piston with a piston rod attached to the other piston.
</ABSTRACT>
