<INVENTION-TITLE>
Safety device for the passenger compartment of a motor vehicle body.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60R2100	B60R2100	B60R2102	B60R2102	B60R2104	B60R21045	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60R	B60R	B60R	B60R	B60R	B60R	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60R21	B60R21	B60R21	B60R21	B60R21	B60R21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A safety device for the passenger compartment of a passenger vehicle comprises a cushion pad (4) which is fitted at knee height in the front part of the vehicle and can be displaced in the direction of the knees of the passenger by means of a drive. A simple embodiment of the drive comprises a restrained leaf spring (11) which is prestressed in the rest state and can be triggered counter to the travelling direction of the vehicle.
