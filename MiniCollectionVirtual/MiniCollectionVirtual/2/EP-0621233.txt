Vessel handling machine.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
In the case of a vessel handling machine having a stand (1) on which there is rotatably mounted a rotor (2) having transport elements (3) and handling elements (4) and to which there is fastened a frame (8) for protective walls (7) and spraying nozzles (6) for a cleaning liquid, the frame (8) at least partly takes the form of a pipe for the cleaning liquid.
