<INVENTION-TITLE>
Surfactant composition characterized by an improved foam height.

<ABSTRACT>
Foamable surface-active composition characterised by an increase in the height of foam, containing as surfactant:     a) a zwitterionic surfactant or   b) a nonionic surfactant based on alkylphenol ethoxylate or c) a mixture of two or a number of nonionic, anionic, cationic and zwitterionic surfactants in a total quantity which is sufficient to produce a foam in water, and d) a polygalactomannan in a sufficient quantity to increase the height of foam of an aqueous solution of the said surfactant by at least 5 mm relative to a surfactant control without polygalactomannan.   The said compositions produce an increase in height of foam with the same content of surfactant or the same height of foam with a lower content of surfactant.
</ABSTRACT>
