Catalyst system for the polymerization of alpha-olefins and process for this polymerization
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Catalyst system usable for the polymerisation of alpha-olefins, including     (a) a catalytic solid based on titanium trichloride (TiCl3), (b) a nonhalogenated cocatalyst including at least one nonhalogenated organoaluminium compound, characterised in that the nonhalogenated cocatalyst additionally contains at least one aminoalane containing no active hydrogen.   Such a catalyst system may additionally contain a third constituent chosen from the organic oxygen compounds of silicon.
</ABSTRACT>
