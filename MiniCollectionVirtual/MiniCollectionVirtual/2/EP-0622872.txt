<INVENTION-TITLE>
Circular connector with filter.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H01R13719	H01R13719	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H01R	H01R	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H01R13	H01R13	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A plug connector, especially a round plug connector, which has the following items: an insulating body, at least one contact which extends through the insulating body, and a sleeve which is used as the earth contact element and into which the insulating body can be inserted, a filter arrangement being arranged between the at least one contact and the sleeve, which filter arrangement consists of at least one filter element and at least one spring element.
</ABSTRACT>
