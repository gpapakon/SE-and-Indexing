<INVENTION-TITLE>
DEVICE FOR CONNECTING ROD- OR PLATE-SHAPED BUILDING COMPONENTS.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
A device for connecting rod- or plate-shaped building components is provided with a gripping unit (1, 2) and an insert (5, 6). The insert (5, 6) is preferably spherical or polyhedral and can be introduced in various positions in an appropriate clamping region of the gripping unit (1, 2). The clamping region is accessible in five different mutually perpendicular or antiparallel spatial directions (7.1, ..., 7.5).
</ABSTRACT>
