<INVENTION-TITLE>
Fire resistant coating for ventilating ducts.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A62C200	A62C206	E04B194	E04B194	F16L502	F16L504	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A62C	A62C	E04B	E04B	F16L	F16L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A62C2	A62C2	E04B1	E04B1	F16L5	F16L5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Method of protecting ventilation ducts or partition walls from fire.   The element which provides the fire resistance is a continuous coating. This coating, by way of its composition and its application, is capable of retarding the effects of fire. A suitable reinforcement contributes to the stability of the coating.   The coating is completely harmless, whatever its temperature. The coating consists of an iron potassium aluminosilicate salt, plaster, water and a reinforcing metal. The commercial name is "PYROLICA".
</ABSTRACT>
