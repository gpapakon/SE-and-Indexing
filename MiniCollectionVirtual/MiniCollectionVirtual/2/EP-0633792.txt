<CLAIMS>
Separator for powder inhalers, characterised by a
chamber (5) which has a connector (4) for receiving the

mouth tube (3) of a powder inhaler, as well as a
mouthpiece (8), and wherein an impact and deflection

means (6) is arranged in the flow path of the aerosol
passing through the separator, wherein an opening (7)

between the impact and deflection means (6) and the
chamber wall provides a connection to the rear part of

the chamber (5) and hence to the mouthpiece (8) and
wherein the impact and deflection means (6) rests with

its lower edge (in the position of use) on the wall of
the chamber, and any separated particles are deposited

in the angle thus formed between the impact means (6)
and the chamber wall.
Separator according to claim 1, characterised in
that the opening (7) is slot-shaped, round, elliptical

or polygonal.
Separator according to claim 1 or 2, characterised
in that the surface of the impact plate (6) is uneven.
Separator according to claim 1, 2 or 3,
characterised in that the unevennesses on the surface of

the impact plate (6) are small depressions, elevations
or transverse grooves or ribs.
Separator according to claim 1, 2, 3 or 4,
characterised in that it is made up of parts which can

be fitted or screwed together or folded.
Separator according to claim 1, 2, 3, 4 or 5, in
which the impact plate (6), in the upper part thereof,

is bent or angled counter to the direction of flow of
the aerosol.
</CLAIMS>
