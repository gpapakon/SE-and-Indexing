<INVENTION-TITLE>
Joint for two cooperating tubes with mirror oriented to follow their bisector.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Joint (articulation) for two tubes (1 and 2) and for a mirror (3) to be oriented along the bisector of the angle which they form. The original transmission is composed of two bevel gears (5, 6) integral with the tubes and meshing with a toothed wheel (8) mounted so as to rotate on the element (3). This joint may be used on systems for guiding light beams where the arms (1 and 2) are tubes and the element (3) a mirror.
