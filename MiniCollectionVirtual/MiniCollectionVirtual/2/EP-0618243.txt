<ABSTRACT>
Thickener preparation for thickening aqueous systems, comprising a mixture of a) a urethane group-containing, water-soluble or -dispersible thickener, b) a nonionic emulsifier, c1) at least one diol containing an acetylenic triple bond and optionally ether groups, and optionally c2) further auxiliaries and additives, and the use of the aqueous solutions or dispersions of these preparations as thickening additives in aqueous systems.
</ABSTRACT>
