<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a foldable workbench, in particular for use in vehicles or on site. The workbench is universally usable, operationally reliable and suitable for different types of vehicle. It has a bearing pedestal (12), which is to be fixed to a support, with a first pivot joint (14) for pivotably mounting a worktop (16) and a second pivot joint (22), arranged on the worktop (16), for pivotably mounting a standing leg (24) on the worktop (16).
