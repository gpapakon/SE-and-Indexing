<INVENTION-TITLE>
Sports shoe with an at least partially elastic lining
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A43B504	A43B714	A43B500	A43B714	A43B500	A43B504	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A43B	A43B	A43B	A43B	A43B	A43B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A43B5	A43B7	A43B5	A43B7	A43B5	A43B5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
This shoe comprises an outsole (outer sole) (2), a counter (stiffener) (4) surrounding the heel, a vamp (8), as well as a lining (11) made from elastic material extending over the top of the front of the foot inside the vamp (8), the vamp and the rear counter being assembled to the outsole (2) in a peripheral assembly zone (3) of the sole. According to the invention, the elastic lining (11) has no bottom and is fastened via its lower peripheral edge to the outsole, in the peripheral assembly zone of this sole, and the lining (11) is fixed at the rear, on either side of the shoe (4a), to the rear counter.

<CLAIMS>
Sport boot comprising an outer sole (2, 102), a stiffener (4, 104) surrounding the heel, a
vamp (8, 108), as well as a lining (11, 110) made of an elastic material extending over the top of the

front-foot inside the vamp (8, 108), the vamp and the rear stiffener being assembled to the outer sole
(2, 102) in a peripheral assembly zone (3, 103) of the sole, characterized in that the elastic lining (11,

110) does not have a base and is fixed by its lower peripheral edge to the outer sole in the peripheral
assembly one of this sole, and in that the lining (11, 110) is fixed at the rear, on each side of the boot

(4a, 104a), to the rear stiffener.
Sport boot according to claim 1, characterized in that the connection (15, 115) between
the lining (11, 110) and the stiffener (4, 104) is located substantially at the level of the flexion fold

line of the boot.
Sport boot according to any of claims 1 or 2, characterised in that it comprises at the level
of the instep, an inner tightening system (5, 105) arranged between the lining (11, 110) and the outer

vamp (8, 108), this inner tightening system (5, 105) being constituted of two lightening quarters (6,
106), each of these quarters (6, 106) being fixed to one end of the sole (2, 102) in the peripheral

assembly zone (3, 103) thereof, and being provided at the other end with tightening means (7, 107)
complementary to those of the other quarter, and in that at least one of these quarters (6, 106) is

furthermore fixed by its rear edge to the stiffener (4, 104) of the boot by the same stitching (15, 115)
as the lining (11, 110).
Sport boot according to claim 3, characterized in that the elastic lining (110) has
substantially the shape of a sock without a base covering the entire foot and ankle.
Sport boot according to claim 4, characterized in that the lining (110) is provided with
a vertical opening (120) at the level of the ankle.
Sport boot according to claim 5, characterized in that the opening (120) extends from the
sole (102) up to the tip of the lining (110).
Sport boot according to any of claims 1-6, characterized in that the lining (10, 110) is
constituted, at least in the portion (11, 111) covering the front-foot, of two layers of elastic fabric

(16, 17; 116, 117) joined together by a peripheral stitching (18, 118) before assembly on the boot and
in that only the outer fabric layer (18, 118) is connected to the stiffener (4, 104). 
Sport boot according to claim 7, characterized in that the assembly to the sole (2, 102) is
obtained by means of the inner elastic fabric layer (17, 117).
Boot according to one of claims 1-8, characterized in that the volume of the lining (11,
110) is designs so as to be slightly smaller than the volume of the foot of the wearer so as to exert

a slight pressure thereupon.
Boot according to one of claims 1-9, characterized in that the volume of the lining (113a)
at the rear is provided so as to exert a slight pressure on the foot, substantially above the zone of the

malleoli.
</CLAIMS>
