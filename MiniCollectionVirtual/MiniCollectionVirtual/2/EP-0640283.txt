<INVENTION-TITLE>
Bath for dog.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A01K1300	A01K1300	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A01K	A01K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A01K13	A01K13	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Bath especially for a dog, produced in the form of an oblong receptacle intended to be filled with a washing liquid, and in which the dog can be in a standing position, characterised in that, in its median vertical longitudinal plane, it has a cross-section in the form of an upturned U, the branches (1, 2) of which constitute regions suitable for accommodating the legs of the dog whereas the mid-part (3) is raised depending on the height of the belly of the dog.
