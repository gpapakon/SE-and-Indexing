<INVENTION-TITLE>
SHOWER CABIN.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47K328	A47K328	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47K	A47K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47K3	A47K3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention concerns a shower cabin with a basin (10) which has at least one corner or only one straight side and has vertical uprights (11, 25, 28), walls (29) and a door (27). By virtue of the special disposition of the uprights (11, 25, 28) and the way in which they are fixed (12) to the basin (10) or fixed by means of a crossbar (15) to the wall of the room in which the shower is located or to a corner- or wall-mounted upright (19), it is not necessary for the shower cabin to have a top frame or for the uprights (11, 19, 25, 28) to be fixed to the ceiling.
</ABSTRACT>
