<INVENTION-TITLE>
METHOD OF JOINING METAL PLATES TOGETHER, AND A DEVICE AND TOOL FOR CARRYING OUT THE METHOD.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B21D3903	B21D3903	F16B1700	F16B1700	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B21D	B21D	F16B	F16B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B21D39	B21D39	F16B17	F16B17	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Described is a method of joining together abutting metal plates (21, 22), as well as a machine tool for carrying out the method in which a tenon (19) on the first plate (22) is inserted into an opening (41) in the second plate (21) and wedged in place by notching the tenon (19) from the end in order to produce the joint.
</ABSTRACT>
