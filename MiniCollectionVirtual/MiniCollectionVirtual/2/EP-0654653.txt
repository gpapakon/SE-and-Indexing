<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for measuring the speed of a fluid.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01F132	G01F132	G01P500	G01P501	G01P502	G01P504	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01F	G01F	G01P	G01P	G01P	G01P	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01F1	G01F1	G01P5	G01P5	G01P5	G01P5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The flow metering device, operating on the Karman principle, has a bending sensor (8) in a cross-piece (7) and thus sheathed with plastic. As a result of the one-piece construction of the moulding (2) forming a part of the housing with the cross-piece (7), corners and dead spaces and sealing problems are avoided.
