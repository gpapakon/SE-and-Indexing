<INVENTION-TITLE>
Dihydropyridines with substituted esters showing cerebral activity.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Dihydropyridines with substituted esters are prepared by esterifying appropriate carboxylic acids, where appropriate via an activated derivative, with appropriate alcohols, or introducing the substituents into the ester group. The dihydropyridines can be used as active ingredients in pharmaceuticals, in particular in compositions with cerebral activity.
</ABSTRACT>
