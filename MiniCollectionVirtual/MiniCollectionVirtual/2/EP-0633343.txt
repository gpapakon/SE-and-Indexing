<INVENTION-TITLE>
Spreading and ironing device for trousers, cuffs and pockets and use of this device.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>D06F7100	D06F7128	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>D06F	D06F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>D06F71	D06F71	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A tensioning and ironing appliance for articles to be ironed (1), such as trouser turn-ups and pocket flaps, with a tensioning device (4) for the articles to be ironed (1) and with press plates (3) which are movable perpendicularly to the ironing surface of the articles to be ironed (1) by means of press drives (6a, b), is characterised, according to the invention, in that the tensioning device (4) is fixed and the press plates (3) are displaceable not only perpendicularly, but also parallel to the ironing surface, so that they can be guided out of a position of rest into a suspended position over the ironing surface.
