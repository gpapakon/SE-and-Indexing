<INVENTION-TITLE>
Multi-jet shower head.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B05B114	B05B116	B05B118	B05B118	B05B130	B05B130	B05B132	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B05B	B05B	B05B	B05B	B05B	B05B	B05B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B05B1	B05B1	B05B1	B05B1	B05B1	B05B1	B05B1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Multi-jet shower-head unit having a body (1) containing a distributor (3) and carrying a head (2) rotationally driving a spray assembly (4) having several series of water-outlet orifices, each corresponding to a jet of defined type, the said shower-head unit making it possible to obtain a toning jet by partially blocking off the orifices (401) forming a rain-type jet, characterised in that the said orifices (401) are partially blocked off by the teeth (70) of a comb (7).
