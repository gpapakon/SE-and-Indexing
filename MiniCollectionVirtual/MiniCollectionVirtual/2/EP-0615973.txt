<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for the preparation of alkylglycosides.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C07H1500	C07H1504	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C07H	C07H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C07H15	C07H15	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A method for the preparation of alkyl glycosides by reacting monosaccharides with fatty alcohols in the presence of an acid catalyst is characterised in that sulpho carboxylic acids and their esters with the exception of sulphosuccinic acid and its dialkyl esters wherein the total number of carbon atoms in the two alkyl groups is at least 10, as well as anhydrides of polyfunctional sulpho carboxylic acids are used as catalyst.
</ABSTRACT>
