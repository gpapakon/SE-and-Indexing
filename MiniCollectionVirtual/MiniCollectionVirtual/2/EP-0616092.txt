<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a rafter and post profile for structures such as Winter gardens, facades or the like, having a glass-receiving profile (1), for example consisting of plastic, and a functional profile (3), for example consisting of steel, which is connected, on one side, to a glass-receiving profile (1) with glass panes (6, 7) and, on the other side, to a functional profile (3) and a clipped-on cladding profile (2).
