<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for providing a composite tube with a socket, and socket so obtained.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method of producing a boss on a fluid-transporting pipe (1) made of composite material. According to the invention, the method comprises the steps consisting in:   - drilling a hole (2) emerging inside the said pipe, of a diameter suitable for receiving one end (6) of a tubular socket (5) to be connected in a sealed manner to the pipe, this socket including a thread, - fastening the said end (6) of the socket to the pipe (1) by adhesive bonding, - applying reinforcing pieces made of resin-impregnated textile material to the external surface of the pipe bordering the said hole and to the socket.
