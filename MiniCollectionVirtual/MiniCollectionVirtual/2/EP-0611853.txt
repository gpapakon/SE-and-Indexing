<INVENTION-TITLE>
Metallic panel for the construction of buildings.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E04C208	E04C208	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E04C	E04C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>E04C2	E04C2	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Metal (metallic) panel intended for the construction of buildings of the sort of those including a corrugated sheet-metal wall and uprights, characterised in that the length of the panel is much greater than its height and in that it is made up by assembling, at the factory, a plurality of elementary panels (1a) joined together by uprights (2) which are made up of simple sections.
