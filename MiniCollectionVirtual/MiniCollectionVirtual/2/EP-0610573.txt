<INVENTION-TITLE>
Grinding method and corresponding plant.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B02C1700	B02C1707	B02C2100	B02C2100	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B02C	B02C	B02C	B02C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B02C17	B02C17	B02C21	B02C21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In order, in the grinding of e.g. cement clinker to cement fineness, in particular in a grinding plant with a mill, upstream of which a material bed comminution rolling press is arranged, still further to reduce the overall energy requirement (kWh/t), it is proposed according to the invention to replace the conventional rotary-tube mill or ball mill by such a mill (13) in which there are arranged about the axis of rotation a number of sectionally arranged grinding chambers which lie parallel to the axis of rotation of the mill (13) and which are passed through parallel by the material to be ground.
