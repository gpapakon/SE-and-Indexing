<INVENTION-TITLE>
Automotive vehicle with two steering- and operating systems.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60N2005	B60N201	B62D102	B62D122	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60N	B60N	B62D	B62D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60N2	B60N2	B62D1	B62D1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a motor vehicle 20 with a steering and operating device 1 arranged in the region of a driver's seat 4 and with companion seats 2. According to the invention, one of the companion seats 2 is assigned a second, independent steering and operating device 1', it being possible to control the motor vehicle by either of the two steering and operating devices 1, 1'.
