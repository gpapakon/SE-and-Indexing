<INVENTION-TITLE>
Posterior vertebral column implant
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61B1760	A61B1760	A61B1770	A61B1770	A61F244	A61F244	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61B	A61B	A61B	A61B	A61F	A61F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61B17	A61B17	A61B17	A61B17	A61F2	A61F2	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A posterior vertebral column implant is useful for a vertebral column supporting device having a supporting rod (1). The implant has an anchoring section (2) designed as a screw or hook, a fastening head (3) provided with a U-shaped slit (6) for the supporting rod (1) which forms two branches (4, 5), and fixing means (7, 8) for fixing the fastening head (3) to the supporting rod. The fixing means (7, 8) are made of two pieces. One cylindrical locking piece (7) may be axially introduced from above into the U-shaped slit (6) and be blocked there and a fixing piece (8) may be brought to bear on the supporting rod (1) through the first locking piece (7).
</ABSTRACT>
