<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for treatment of a glass container for the adhesion of a polymer.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C03C1734	C03C1734	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C03C	C03C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C03C17	C03C17	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Process for the treatment of a glass receptacle, intended for the heat-sealing by means of a film including a polymer, including a stage of deposition of a layer of metal oxide and a stage of deposition of a layer of a soluble zirconium salt, on at least a proportion of the receptacle.
