<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a screen printer operating with a back pressure roller for webs of material, an intermittently operating back pressure cylinder for the screen stencil (1, 11) moving back and forth and its squeegee being provided, and an intermediate gear (5) provided with an oscillating drive being provided between the driving gears for the screen stencil and the gears of the back pressure roller (7), which intermediate gear superimposes the rotary movement of the driving gear (6).
