<INVENTION-TITLE>
Electrical welding source comprising a process control element.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B23K910	B23K910	B23K932	B23K932	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an electrical welding source with operating elements for presetting and a unit for controlling and/or regulating welding parameters such as welding voltage, welding current, wire feed, pulse- and basic-time data and the like, a welding-process monitoring unit for the constant comparison of the desired and actual values of the welding parameters being integrated in the electrical welding source.
