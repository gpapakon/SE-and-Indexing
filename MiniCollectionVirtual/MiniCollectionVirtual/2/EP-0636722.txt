<INVENTION-TITLE>
Woven fabric with jacquard pattern.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Warp and weft fabric making it possible to produce articles of the Jacquard table-linen type, produced on a conventional weaving machine requiring no particular adaptation and using a single warp. It is characterized in that it is based on a combination of weaves designed in such a way that each weave comprises, in its repeat, a number of identical sets of weft threads which is defined by the formula: 1/3 
<
/= number of different sets of weft threads DIVIDED number of wefts to the weave repeat
