Masks for a double-sided exposing device.

<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a pair of masks (1) intended for exposing the upper surface and the lower surface of a silicon wafer. Each mask includes, outside its working surface (5) which corresponds to the surface of the silicon wafer, identical or complementary target patterns (30, 31).
