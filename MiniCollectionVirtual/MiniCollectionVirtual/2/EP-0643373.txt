<INVENTION-TITLE>
Data transfer device with IC-cards.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G07B1502	G07B1502	G07F708	G07F708	G07F710	G07F710	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G07B	G07B	G07F	G07F	G07F	G07F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G07B15	G07B15	G07F7	G07F7	G07F7	G07F7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Source-data chip cards (KK1...KKn) which are in circulation contain source data (QD1...QDn). These can be loaded into an internal accumulating memory (S1...Sz) in distributed source data processing units (DE1...DEz) by means of a reading and writing device (C1...Cn). The accumulated data can be stored on demand on an accumulated-data transfer chip card (SK1...SKn) by means of the reading and writing device of one of the source data processing units. The accumulated-data transfer chip cards (SK1...SKn) can be read by means of at least one central reading and writing device (CL) in a central accumulated-data processing unit (ZR). The source data (QD1...QDn) thus read from individual source-data chip cards (KK1...KKn) can thus be processed further in the accumulated-data processing unit (ZR).
