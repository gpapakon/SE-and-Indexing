<INVENTION-TITLE>
Fixing bath.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G03C742	G03C742	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G03C	G03C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G03C7	G03C7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A fixing bath for colour-photographic materials which contains as fixer, thiosulphate or a mixture of thiosulphate and thiocyanate and in addition at least one other buffer substance which acts in the pH range from 5 to 8, in particular hydrogen carbonate, exhibits significantly reduced formation of leucocyan.
</ABSTRACT>
