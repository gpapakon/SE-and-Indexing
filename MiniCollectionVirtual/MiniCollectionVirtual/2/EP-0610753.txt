<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the coating of tubular sleeves, preferably based on cellulose, with a coating system which comprises a plurality of components, the single application of material leading to a multilayer structure of the coating applied. The coating system is preferably aqueous and free from organic halogen compounds, and leads to an improved barrier effect of the tubular sleeve with respect to water vapour and oxygen.
</ABSTRACT>
