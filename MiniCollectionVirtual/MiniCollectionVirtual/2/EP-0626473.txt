<INVENTION-TITLE>
Interlacing device.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>D02G116	D02G116	D02J100	D02J100	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>D02G	D02G	D02J	D02J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>D02G1	D02G1	D02J1	D02J1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
What is presented is a yarn-interlacing device for the interlacing of multifilament yarns, in which the blowing nozzle (15), a compressed-air piston (12) and a mounting (2) are arranged in an interlacing housing (11) which has an opening and closing flap (7), and in which the holder (2) as well as the opening and closing flap (7) are to be opened and closed by hand, with the result that the compressed-air piston (12) moves automatically into the opening and the closing position respectively.
