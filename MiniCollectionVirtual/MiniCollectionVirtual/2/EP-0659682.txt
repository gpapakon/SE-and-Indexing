<INVENTION-TITLE>
Bottling machine with cleaning containers for C.I.P.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B67C300	B67C300	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B67C	B67C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B67C3	B67C3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A bottling machine with a multiplicity of filling elements (1) which are arranged at intervals around the circumference of the machine, rotate about the axis of rotation of the latter and the container edge seals (4) of which have return gas pipes (2) projecting from them downwards, and with holding elements (7) which are for gripping bottle necks (6), of bottles to be filled and are mounted on lifting elements (9) by means of arms which are arranged radially with respect to the axis of rotation, the said holding elements (7) being capable of being lifted beneath the filling elements over the return gas pipes (2) in sealing engagement with the bottles, the bottling machine also having containers (10) which are movably arranged on the lifting elements, are used for CIP cleaning and can be brought out of a waiting position into the position of the bottles, is characterised in that the arms take the form of slides (11) which are mounted on the lifting elements in a vertically fixed and radially displaceable fashion, the containers (10) being arranged on the said slides (11) outside the holding elements (7) in a radial direction.
