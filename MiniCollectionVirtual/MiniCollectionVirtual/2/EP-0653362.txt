<INVENTION-TITLE>
Foil bag packaging with dispensing head.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B05C17005	B05C17005	B05C1701	B65D3336	B65D3336	B65D8132	B65D8132	B65D8300	B65D8300	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The film bag packaging has two film bags (1, 2) which are provided at the end face with integrally joined-together bottom parts (3, 4). Piercing spatulas (8, 9), extending transversely to the direction of the folds (1c, 2c) at the end face (1a, 2a) of the film bag (1, 2), are provided adjacent to passage channels (5, 6) in the bottom parts (3, 4). This guarantees a sufficient opening of the film bag (1, 2) during the entire emptying operation.
