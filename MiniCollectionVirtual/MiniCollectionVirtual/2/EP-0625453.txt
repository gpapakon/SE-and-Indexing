<INVENTION-TITLE>
Joint for windscreen-wiper yoke.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a windscreen wiper device of the type including a yoke system consisting of a first element (1) having a U-shaped section and a second element (2) having a U-shaped section and which are articulated by means of a pin (15) passing through the bores (11 to 14) provided on the lateral walls of the said elements (1, 2), the articulation having means for preventing the axial movement of the pin (15) relative to the said elements (1, 2), characterised in that the pin consists of two parts (30, 31) including means (18) for locking one to the other.
