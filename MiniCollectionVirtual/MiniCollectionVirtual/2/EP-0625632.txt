<INVENTION-TITLE>
Exhaust gas silencer.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F01N102	F01N104	F01N108	F01N110	F01N124	F01N124	F01N714	F01N714	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F01N	F01N	F01N	F01N	F01N	F01N	F01N	F01N	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F01N1	F01N1	F01N1	F01N1	F01N1	F01N1	F01N7	F01N7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Exhaust gas silencer for motor vehicles, especially as a final silencer, containing a sound-absorbing material, having a plurality of chambers and in each case at least one gas inlet pipe and one gas outlet pipe, characterised in that two spaced chambers, namely an inlet-end chamber (12) and an outlet-end chamber (14) are filled with the sound-absorbing material (20) while leaving at least one reflection chamber (16a, 16b), the gas inlet pipe(s) (22) being passed through the inlet-end chamber (12) and opening into one of the reflection chambers (16a, 16b, ...), and the gas outlet pipe(s) being passed through the outlet-end chamber (14) and being completely or partially surrounded by a jacket tube (26) in the region of at least one of the reflection chambers (16a, 16b, ...) and having perforations (28) in this region.
</ABSTRACT>
