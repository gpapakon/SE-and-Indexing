<INVENTION-TITLE>
THE MANUFACTURE OF A SEMICONDUCTOR DEVICE
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H01L2122	H01L21285	H01L21268	H01L2102	H01L21225	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H01L	H01L	H01L	H01L	H01L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H01L21	H01L21	H01L21	H01L21	H01L21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A semiconductor layer, of polycrystalline silicon, (67) is 
provided on a substrate (61). The layer (67) contains an impur­

ity, for example phosphorus. The layer (67) is irradiated with a 
high energy laser beam, the layer (67) and part of the subs­

trate (61) are melted by the high energy beam and impurity 
diffuses from the layer (67) into the substrate (61) to form 

impurity diffusion regions (69 and 70) where the layer (67) 
contacts the substrate (61). 
The melted semiconductor layer is single crystallised and 
can be used to form contact electrodes of low resistance for 

the impurity diffusion regions (69 and 70). 
</ABSTRACT>
