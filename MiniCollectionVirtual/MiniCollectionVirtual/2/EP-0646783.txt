<INVENTION-TITLE>
Calibration standard and method for producing such a standard.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01N2162	G01N2162	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01N	G01N	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01N21	G01N21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a calibration standard and methods for producing a calibration standard for emission spectroscopy. The constituents of the calibration standard are a base material and at least one alloying element, with the constituents being present in the calibration standard in a fine, uniform distribution having a maximum particle size of 100  mu m. The calibration standard is produced by atomising the melt of the base material and the alloying constituent by means of an atomisation device with feeding-in of an inert gas to give finely divided particles, and solidifying the melt on a collection device moving relative to the atomisation device to give a preform. The calibration standard is made from this preform, if desired after hot and/or cold forming.
</ABSTRACT>
