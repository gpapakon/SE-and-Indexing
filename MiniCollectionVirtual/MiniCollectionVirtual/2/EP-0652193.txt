<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the preparation of oligomers of 1-alkenes in C2 to C12.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C07C200	C07C234	C08F400	C08F402	C08F460	C08F464	C08F4642	C08F46592	C08F468	C08F1000	C08F1000	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
Oligomers of C2- to C12-alk-1-enes are prepared by   a) oligomerising C2- to C12-alk-1-enes in the presence of a catalyst system which, as active constituents, contains     A) a finely divided carrier, B) a complex compound of metals of sub-group IV or V of the Periodic Table and   C) a co-catalyst  and b) then separating the catalyst system mechanically. )
</ABSTRACT>
