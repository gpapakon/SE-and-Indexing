<INVENTION-TITLE>
Optical local area network and optical subscriber switch.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H04B1002	H04B1002	H04B1020	H04B1020	H04Q352	H04Q352	H04Q360	H04Q360	H04Q1100	H04Q1100	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
Prior art: Subscribers are connected to subscriber exchanges at the lowest level of an hierarchical network. Technical problem: Optical switching technology is uneconomic with a small traffic volume. Subscribers have only a very small traffic volume. Basic concept: Connecting the subscribers only via devices which concentrate the traffic volume. Example: Decentralised connection of the subscribers to optical ring lines.
