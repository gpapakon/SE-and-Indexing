<INVENTION-TITLE>
Private branch exchange.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H04M342	H04M342	H04M900	H04M900	H04Q362	H04Q362	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H04M	H04M	H04M	H04M	H04Q	H04Q	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H04M3	H04M3	H04M9	H04M9	H04Q3	H04Q3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A private branch exchange is indicated with a central unit and a plurality of telephone sets, of which at least one has a programmable control unit and a loudspeaker/hands-free device. To allow all incoming calls to be handled as direct calls on a programmable telephone set, the control unit, following the input of specific codes, controls the loudspeaker/hands-free device of its own telephone set in a pre-defined way in the case of incoming calls.
