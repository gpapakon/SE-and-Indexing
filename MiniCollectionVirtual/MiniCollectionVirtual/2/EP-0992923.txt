<INVENTION-TITLE>
System for data acquisition, processing 
&
 control
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G06F1740	G06F1740	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G06F	G06F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G06F17	G06F17	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The system consists of modules (M) which are either interconnected through interfaces (I) to a ground bus (2) or interconnected by radio. The modules which include digital/analogue conversion, calculation and transmission/reception receive data from sensors (C) and may operate devices such as alarms and switches (A). A central unit (5) is used for system configuration and data collection.
</ABSTRACT>
