<INVENTION-TITLE>
Control and driving of embroidering segments.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>D05C1100	D05C1100	D05C1106	D05C1116	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>D05C	D05C	D05C	D05C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>D05C11	D05C11	D05C11	D05C11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Embroidering machine with a large number of embroidering stations arranged at an equal distance from one another in one or more rolls and having the associated tools, namely a needle (2), a borer (3), a thread guide (20) and thread brake (22), the embroidering tools of each embroidering station being couplable to and uncouplable from their respective drive means (13-18, 21), there being arranged for each embroidering station two or more actuators (8, 9, 24) which each couple one or more embroidering tools to or uncouple them from their respective drives, there being provided for each embroidering station only one switching element (12) which jointly switches all the actuators (8, 9, 24) of the embroidering station.
