<INVENTION-TITLE>
Stabiliser for soilless culture pots.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47G700	A47G702	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47G	A47G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47G7	A47G7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Stabiliser (1) for plant pots, especially for growing plants in soil, designed for use with at least two pots (4, 5, 6) arranged side by side but separated from each other. According to the invention, the stabiliser takes the form of a connecting piece (2) located outside the pots (4, 5, 6) and comprising at least two legs (7, 8, 9) which can be pushed into the culture substrate against the walls of said pots (4, 5, 6) in order to keep them in a stable position of separation.
