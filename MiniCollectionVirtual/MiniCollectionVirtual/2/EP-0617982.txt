<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method and apparatus for neuromagnetical stimulation.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61H2302	A61H2302	A61H3900	A61H3900	A61N200	A61N200	A61N202	A61N204	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
In a method and an apparatus for neuromagnetic stimulation of nerve strands, a magnetic field having a magnetic induction B and at least one focussed ultrasound beam are applied simultaneously. The magnetic field is orthogonal relative to the direction of the ultrasound oscillation. A focus having a diameter of about 1 cm is produced. The relatively strong fields required for effective and deliberate stimulation can thus be directed onto a single nerve bundle or a single central nerve region. Unintentional stimulation of adjacent nerve regions is prevented.
