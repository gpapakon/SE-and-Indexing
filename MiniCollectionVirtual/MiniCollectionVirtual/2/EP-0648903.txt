<INVENTION-TITLE>
Roofing panel.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E04D1314	E04D13147	F16L502	F16L502	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E04D	E04D	F16L	F16L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>E04D13	E04D13	F16L5	F16L5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A roofing panel (2) which comprises a basic panel (3) with an opening and also comprises a pivotable ventilation duct (4) which can be inserted into the opening achieves a construction of simple design and permits a standard structure for the pivotable part of the ventilation duct (4) in that the ventilation duct (4) can be pivoted about a pivot pin (6) arranged on the roofing panel (2), and the surface which is penetrated by the ventilation duct (4) in the plane of the basic panel (3) is constant, in terms of position and size, during pivoting.
