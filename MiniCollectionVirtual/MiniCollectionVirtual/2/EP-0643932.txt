<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for holding the end of a neck-tie and attaching the neck-tie to the shirt.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A41D2500	A41D2500	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A41D	A41D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A41D25	A41D25	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The guiding of the narrow end (1.1) of the neck-tie and the attachment of the neck-tie (1) to the shirt are achieved according to the invention in that a device (2) connected to the neck-tie (1) has, on one side, a curved shape for guiding the end (1.1) of the neck-tie between the neck-tie (1) and the device (2) and has, on the other side, a fastener (2.1) for attaching the neck-tie (1) to the button strip of the shirt. The device (2) is preferably designed as a hollow body for receiving and giving off scents.
