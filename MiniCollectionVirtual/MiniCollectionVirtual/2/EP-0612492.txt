<INVENTION-TITLE>
Fixing element to prevent a mat from slipping
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The present fixing element serves to prevent a mat (7) from slipping on an underlay (8) of felt material or the like. It consists of a metal plate (1) which can be anchored on the underside of the mat (7) by means of cramps (4) and is equipped with a multiplicity of claws (2) projecting away from the underside (9) of the plate and bent in all directions, the tips (3) of which claws are bent back by at least 180@.

<CLAIMS>
A fixing element for non-slip fixing of a carpet (7) on an 
underlay (8) of felt material or the like, comprising a metal plate (1) 

which can be anchored to the underside of the carpet (7) by means of clasps 
(4), the metal plate having a plurality of claws projecting from the plate 

underside (9), characterised in that the bent-over tips (3) of the claws (2) 
are directed towards all sides and are bent back through an angle of at 

least 180Â° with respect to the plate underside (9).
