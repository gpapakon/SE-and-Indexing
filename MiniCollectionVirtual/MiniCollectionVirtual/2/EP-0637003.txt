Hand-driven transport cart

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a transport trolley (1) movable by hand, especially a shopping trolley, with at least one device (3) intended for receiving merchandise, luggage and the like and with a pushing device which is arranged between two grip-holding parts (17) and is designed as a grip rod (5) and which is equipped with a coin-operated lock (11) making it possible to hire out the transport trolley on a deposit basis. It is proposed to arrange the coin-operated lock (11) on the grip rod (5) in such a way that an upper region (8) not taken up by the coin-operated lock (11) and extending from one grip-holding part (17) to the other grip-holding part (17) is maintained on the grip rod (5).

<CLAIMS>
A hand-driven transporting trolley (1), in particular a shopping trolley, having at least 
one device (3) for receiving goods, luggage and the like, and having a pushing device 

which is arranged between two handle supports (17), is in the form of a handle bar (5) 
and has an upper region (8) which can be used to receive an advertisement carrier (18) 

and extends from one handle support (17) to the other handle support (17), the handle bar 
(5) supporting a coin-operated lock (11) which is secured against loosening, is provided 

with a coupling mechanism (14) and with a deposit retaining device (16) and enables the 
transporting trolley (1) to be hired out against a deposit, characterised in that the coin-operated 

lock (11) is arranged below the handle bar (5), and in that the portions (13) of 
the coin-operated lock (11) provided to secure the latter on the handle bar (5) are 

arranged outside of the upper region (8) provided for the advertisement carrier (18) and 
partially engage around the handle bar (5) from below. 
A transporting trolley in accordance with Claim 1, characterised in that the coin-operated 
lock (11) is locked with one of the two handle supports (17) supporting the handle bar 

(5). 
A transporting trolley in accordance with either one of Claims 1 or 2, characterised in 
that the two portions (13) of the coin-operated lock (11) engage over the central 

horizontal axis (7) of the handle-bar cross-section. 
A transporting trolley in accordance with any one of Claims 1 to 3, characterised in that 
the coin-operated lock (11) engages around the handle bar (5) over more than 180Â° with 

its two portions (13).
