Slanted seat valve.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a slanted seat valve for pipe conduits, especially for concealed installation, with a housing (12) which is composed of plastic and in which is installed a metallic connection piece (4) which is provided with a thread (13) for screwing in the valve shut-off member for the control of the flow through the valve, the connection piece and the valve seat (3) being two separate components and the valve seat being composed of metal. Provided in the wall on the discharge side of the valve and in front of its closing member is an additional hole (8) which serves as an inspection hole where the valve is used as a non-return valve.
