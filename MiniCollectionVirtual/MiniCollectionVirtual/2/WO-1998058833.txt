<INVENTION-TITLE>
MOTOR DRIVEN POWER STEERING DEVICE
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B62D504	B62D504	H02H708	H02H708	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B62D	B62D	H02H	H02H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B62D5	B62D5	H02H7	H02H7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A motor driven power steering device comprises a motor (1) for assisting a manual steering effort on the basis of a steering torque or the like detected by a torque sensor (7), a motor drive circuit (2) for drivingly controlling the motor (1), a supply voltage detecting circuit (17) for detecting a supply voltage VB of the motor drive circuit (2), and a microcomputer (9) for detecting respective terminal voltages VM+, VM- when the supply voltage detecting circuit (17) judges the supply voltage VB to be at least a predetermined value, judging whether the detected value deviates from set values VTHH, VTHL, and judging trouble of wiring connected to the motor (1) when the respective terminal voltages VM+/- deviate from the set values VTHH, VTHL.
</ABSTRACT>
