Packaging unit for pipette tips.

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a packaging unit for pipette tips which have, at their rear ends, a plug-on sleeve with an annular edge. A flat, plate-like, thin carrier of rectangular contour has, on its surface, a layer of a pressure adhesive. The pipettes are bonded releasably on the carrier by their annular edges in equidistant rows and columns.
