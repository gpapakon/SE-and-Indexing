<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to regenerable separation elements formed from coils (1, 1'), from spiral coils (36), from pleat packages or bellows (28, 28') or from stacked segments (37), which separation elements are for treating liquid and gaseous media, the layers (5, 5') or separation media (7, 7'), separation units or filter units (18) or separation segments or filter segments (40) composed of separation media and/or drainage elements of which separation elements can be separated by changing rolls, rolling up, folding over, turning over, bending or detaching, so that direct interaction with a regeneration medium (21) and/or at least one regeneration unit (2, 2') which is necessary for regeneration is possible. By means of the separation elements according to the invention which may be operated, depending on the variant, statically, in cross-flow or by the dialysis principle, novel separation objectives may be achieved economically and known separation objectives may be achieved more economically and the waste of used separation elements arising may be considerably decreased.
