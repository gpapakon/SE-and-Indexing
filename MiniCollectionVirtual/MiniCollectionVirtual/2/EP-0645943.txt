<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an operating device for electric lamps. The connecting part (3), provided with the electrical connections (4), of the operating device is designed as an insert and is inserted in a receptacle of the housing (1). The operating device can optionally be provided with connecting terminals or with connecting cables (4) by replacing the connecting part (3) without changes to the construction of the housing being required.

<CLAIMS>
Operating unit for electric lamps, comprising a
housing (1), an electric circuit which is arranged in

the interior of the housing (1), and a connecting part
(3, 3') which has electric terminals (4, 4') for

supplying power to the operating unit, as well as
electric terminals (4, 4') for at least one electric

lamp, it being the case that

the connecting part (3, 3') is constructed as an
insert and the housing (1) has a holder into which

the connecting part (3, 3') is inserted, and
the housing (1) has one or more guide rails (7),
and has on the connecting part (3, 3') one or more

webs (6, 6') which engage in the guide rails (7),

characterized in that the operating unit has a stress
relief device (14), fastened detachably on the housing

(1), for the feeder cables led to the supply terminals
(4'), and for the connecting cable (4) led out of the

connecting part (3), the stress relief device (14)
being clamped in the guide rail or rails (7).
Operating unit for electric lamps according to
Claim 1, characterized in that the electric terminals

(4) are constructed as connecting cables (4) led out of
the connecting part (3).
Operating unit for electric lamps according to
Claim 1, characterized in that the electric terminals

(4') of the connecting part (3') are constructed as
supply terminals (4') for connecting feeder cables. 
Operating unit for electric lamps according to
Claim 1, characterized in that the stress relief device

(14) is constructed as a cover for the electric
terminals (4') of the connecting part (3').
Operating unit for electric lamps according to
Claim 1, characterized in that the housing (1) of the

operating unit comprises a plastic hood (1b) with an
essentially cuboid contour, and a metallic base plate

(1a).
Operating unit for electric lamps according to
Claims 1 and 5, characterized in that the holder is

constructed as a depression or recess on an end face
(5) of the plastic hood (1b).
</CLAIMS>
