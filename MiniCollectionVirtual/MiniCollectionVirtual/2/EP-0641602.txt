<ABSTRACT>
It includes a cutting means (2) positioned at the end (10) of a conveying and compacting tunnel (1), narrowing towards the said end (10) and the four walls of the said tunnel consisting of parallel and juxtaposed rollers (40, 50, 60) arranged transversely, the rollers (40) of the vertical walls and (60) of the upper horizontal wall being moved in rotation by a drive device.

<CLAIMS>
Compacting and shearing device for voluminous
highly elastic objects, especially tyres, of the type

including a feeding and compacting tunnel (1) with a
rectangular section and retracting at one of its ends

(10), characterised in that it comprises a cutting element
(2) positioned at the end (10) of the tunnel (1) and in

that the four walls of said tunnel (1) are formed of
parallel and juxtaposed rollers (40, 50, 60) disposed

transversally, at least from the rollers (40) of the
vertical walls and that (60) of the lower horizontal wall

being moved in rotation by a motor device.
Device according to claim 1, characterised in that
the cutting element (2) is a guillotine type element.
Device according to claim 1 or 2, characterised in
that the horizontal rollers (50) of the upper wall are

rotation mounted to freely rotate..
Device according to one of claims 1 to 3,
characterised in that the rollers (40, 50, 60) are smooth.
Device according to one of claims 1 to 3,
characterised in that the rollers (40, 50, 60) are

corrugated.
Device according to one of the preceding claims,
characterised in that the axes of the vertical rollers

(40) and those of the horizontal rollers (50, 60) are
situated inside different vertical planes.
Device according to one of the preceding claims,
characterised in that the motor rollers (40, 60) can

rotate in both directions.
