<INVENTION-TITLE>
Alkylphtalates of polyoxyalkylenophtalyllactame for increasing impact strength and tear resistance of polyether-polycaproamid block copolymers and process for their preparation.

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to acyl derivatives of lactams which are used for the synthesis of polyether-polycaproamide block copolymers by activated anionic polymerisation of caprolactam. The polyether-polycaproamide block copolymers obtained using the proposed alkyl phthalates of polyoxyalkylene phthalyl lactams have high impact strength and tear strength and can be employed as construction materials in various industrial areas.
