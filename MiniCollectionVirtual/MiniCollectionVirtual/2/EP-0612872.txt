<INVENTION-TITLE>
Pulley-protecting shoe for the pulling systems on the heald frames of looms.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>D03C100	D03C114	D03C1300	D03C1300	F16H700	F16H718	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>D03C	D03C	D03C	D03C	F16H	F16H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>D03C1	D03C1	D03C13	D03C13	F16H7	F16H7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The shoe has a sector-shaped general profile which, on its concave profile edge, comprises a projection (14) capable of occupying the groove (11) of the pulley (12) to be protected. Provided in the region of the lateral segments are, on the one hand, a terminal sternpost (13) and, on the other hand, a straight channel (10) whose bottom is tangential to the groove (11) so that the cable (2) returned by the pulley (12) itself prevents fluff (flock) from getting in.
