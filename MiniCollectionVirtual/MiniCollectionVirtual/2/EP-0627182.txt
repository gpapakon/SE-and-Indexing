<INVENTION-TITLE>
Applicator unit for a liquid product, especially a cosmetic product
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A45D4026	A45D3404	A45D4026	A45D3404	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A45D	A45D	A45D	A45D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
Applicator unit (1) for a fluid product, especially a cosmetic product, comprising a bottle (2), a cap (3) fastened on the bottle (2) to close the said bottle (2), an applicator (4) arranged at the end of a wand (5) integral with the cap (3) and a wiper (6) equipped with a lip, fastened to the bottle, the applicator being entirely immersed in the bottle (2) and the wand (5) passing through the wiper when the cap is fastened onto the bottle, and the applicator passing entirely through the wiper (6) when the cap (3) is removed from the bottle (2) when the product is taken out, in which the lip (63) of the wiper (6) is at least partially flocked with the aid of flocking fibres (64).
