Sealing profile having a textile layer and method for producing the same.

<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a sealing strip (profile) (1), especially for a motor vehicle, for example of the sliding-channel, window-hugging or door-seal type, and to its method of production. According to the invention, a method of manufacturing a sealing strip comprises the steps consisting in:     a) extruding a profile, advantageously made of elastomer; b) depositing an adhesive layer (12) on one surface of the profile;    c) applying a fabric (11) on the adhesive layer (12).   The present invention applies mainly to the production of sealing strips, especially for motor vehicles.
