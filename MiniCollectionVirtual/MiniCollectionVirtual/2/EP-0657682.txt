<ABSTRACT>
The invention proposes a fastening device, in particular for fastening a component on an elastomeric pipe. The fastening device is essentially of a concentric form, a terminating flange forming one end of the same and the opposite end being provided with a threaded connection. The fastening device is inserted into an opening of the elastomeric moulding or pipe.

<CLAIMS>
Resilient tube having a securing means for securing a structural
member, wherein the securing means is of substantially concentric

shape, and an end flange forms one end thereof, and the oppositely
situated end is provided with a threaded connection, wherein the

resilient tube has an opening (11), and this opening (11) is
reinforced by an extension member (12), and the securing means is

inserted into the opening of the resilient tube, and wherein the
concentric shape widens conically from the threaded connection to

the end flange.
Securing means according to claim 1, 
characterised in that
 it has a
through-bore.
Securing means according to one of the preceding claims,

characterised in that
 the face, which comes into contact with the
resilient shaped part, is provided with ribs or grooves.
Securing means according to one of the preceding claims,

characterised
 for securing a reduced-pressure indicator on the
resilient shaped part.
