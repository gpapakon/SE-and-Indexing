<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process and apparatus for making multilayered plastic tubes which are diffusion tight.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B29C5300	B29C5356	B29D2300	B29D2300	B32B100	B32B108	F16L1100	F16L1112	F16L1112	F16L1114	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B29C53	B29C53	B29D23	B29D23	B32B1	B32B1	F16L11	F16L11	F16L11	F16L11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In the case of a process and apparatus for making multilayered plastic tubes which are diffusion-tight, in particular for fuel lines, a permeation-tight foil or film, preferably of aluminium or PTFE strip, is wound in an overlapping manner around a tube preferably consisting of PA and subsequently the wound-around tube is sheathed with at least one outer tubular layer.
