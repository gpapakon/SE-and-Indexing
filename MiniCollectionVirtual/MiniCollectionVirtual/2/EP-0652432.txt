<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for detecting foreign material, especially foreign fibres in textile articles in motion.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65H6300	B65H6306	G01N2188	G01N2189	G01N21898	G01N3336	G01N3336	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a detector device (1) for detecting foreign material in a longitudinally moved textile article (13). The detector device (1) essentially consists of a polychromatic light source (6) arranged in an illumination part (3), a sensor device (7) arranged in a detection part (4) and a thread-scanning space (5) having a mirror element (14). The geometrical arrangement, according to the invention, of these components and the use of a wavelength-selective photodetector with photodiodes (16, 17) arranged vertically staggered leads to a foreign-fibre detection device which is insensitive to fluctuations in yarn diameter and changes in yarn structure and independent of the yarn colour of the thread (13) to be tested.
