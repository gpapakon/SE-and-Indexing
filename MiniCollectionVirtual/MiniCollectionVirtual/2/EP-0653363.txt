<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a cigarette pack, in particular a hinged-lid box, for cigarettes (10). A cigarette (10) or a partial cigarette (33) is to be received temporarily in the cigarette pack. For this purpose, the length of the cigarette (10) is smaller than the height or length of the cigarette pack in such a way that, when the cigarette pack is closed, a hollow space (chamber 31) is formed inside the lid (12) above the group (28) or block (30) of cigarettes to receive at least one cigarette (10) or partial cigarette (33) arranged lying transversely.

<CLAIMS>
Cigarette pack with lid (12), in particular 
hinge-lid box, with a box part (11) and a cigarette group 

(28) received therein, characterized in that the length 
of the cigarettes (10) is smaller than the height or 

length of the cigarette pack, such that, when the 
cigarette pack is closed, a cavity (31) for receiving at 

least one transversely arranged cigarette or part-cigarette 

(33) is formed above the cigarette group (28), 
or a cigarette block (30), in the interior of the lid 

(12). 
Cigarette pack according to Claim 1, characterized 
in that the cavity (31) above the cigarette group 

(28) extends over the entire cross-section of the lid 
(12) and, in height, corresponds approximately to the 

thickness of a cigarette or part-cigarette (33). 
Cigarette pack according to Claim 1 or 2, characterized 
in that the dimensioning of the cavity (chamber 

31) in the lid (12), in the longitudinal direction of the 
same, corresponds to the length of a cigarette or part-cigarette 

(33), such that the latter can be clamped in 
transversely or diagonally between lid side walls (24, 

25). 
Cigarette pack according to Claim 1 or one of the 
further claims, characterized in that the cigarettes 

forming the cigarette group (28) or the cigarette block 
(30) can be divided preferably in the centre, and in 

that, in the event of a cigarette (10) being removed and 
divided, one of the part-cigarettes formed can be fitted 

in the chamber (31) of the lid (12).
