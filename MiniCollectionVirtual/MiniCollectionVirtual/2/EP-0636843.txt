<INVENTION-TITLE>
Thermal insulation for electric storage heater.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F16L5902	F16L5902	F16L5906	F16L5906	F16L5908	F16L5908	F24H700	F24H700	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The thermal insulation consists of a plurality of thin-walled, fibre-free reflectors (11...14) arranged one behind the other in the direction (3) of heat flow. On their hot side, the reflectors are provided with reflective surfaces (25) which reflect the heat from one stage to the next. Spacer elements (21, 22, 23) are arranged between adjacent reflectors. The thermal insulation takes up only a small overall space while exhibiting good thermal-insulating properties. Its environmental acceptability is ideal in comparison with known fibre-containing thermal insulations.
