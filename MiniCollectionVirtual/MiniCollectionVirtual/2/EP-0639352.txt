<INVENTION-TITLE>
Bone treating device.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61B1700	A61B1700	A61B1760	A61B1766	A61B1768	A61B1772	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61B	A61B	A61B	A61B	A61B	A61B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61B17	A61B17	A61B17	A61B17	A61B17	A61B17	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In a bone treating device with a nail (12) which can be inserted in the bone and whose central axis is crossed by perforations for the reception of screws, bolts and similar pin-like elements (22, 23, 23p) and which is provided on one end with at least one round hole (18), especially a pair of round holes, a longitudinal slot (20) for reception of at least one screw, bolt or similar pin-like element (23, 23p), which is slideable therein, is arranged at the other end of the nail (12). This pin-like element is connected to the screw(s) or similar pin-like element(s) (22) of the other nail end (16) by an adjustment device (28) of variable length.
