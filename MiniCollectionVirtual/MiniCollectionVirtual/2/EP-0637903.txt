Assembly for card distribution

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an assembly for distributing cards such as phone cards for example. The assembly comprises a box (10) which receives and houses the said cards which are positioned perpendicularly with respect to the longitudinal axis of the box (10). The box (10) according to the invention is also equipped with apertures (34) which allow a visual check of the filling of the box (10). The box (10) is made from a single part in recyclable and non-polluting material. It can easily be adapted to an interface so as to serve as a magazine box in automatic machines for dispensing the said cards.

<CLAIMS>
A system for dispensing cards, the system including a
principally rectangular magazine (10) surrounded by walls (12, 14,

16, 18), the magazine (10) being made in one piece from a material
that can be recycled and that does not cause any environmental

pollution and serving to receive and to package the cards (46) which
are positioned perpendicularly to a longitudinal axis of the magazine

(10), the magazine (10) having a mouth (68) through which the cards
(46) can be extracted from said magazine (10) one after the other and

where the magazine (10) can be closed in order to transport the cards
(46), said magazine being adapted to be fitted and fixed into at

least one automatic card dispenser, said system being characterized
in that it further comprises an adapter interface (50) having

positioning and fixing means (60) for correctly positioning the
magazine and the adapter interface inside the automatic card

dispenser and said adapter interface has a central opening (52)
adapted to receive the magazine (10) and fixing means (64, 62) for

fixing said magazine (10) in this central opening (52).
A system according to claim 1, characterized in that the
adapter interface (50) is made from a light metal, preferably

aluminium, or a strong plastics material.
A system according to claim 1 or claim 2, characterized in that
the magazine (10) when filled and closed and prepared for transport

is secured by means of a strap (44) and by means of at least one
anti-fraud seal (42) which leaves indelible traces in the event of

opening or manipulation of the seal.
A system according to any one of claims 1 to 3, characterized
in that at least one of the longitudinal walls (16) of the magazine

(10) has at least one opening (34) for visually inspecting the

filling of the magazine (10).
A system according to any one of claims 1 to 4, characterized
in that the magazine (10) is made of cardboard. 
A system according to any one of claims 1 to 5, characterized
in that a predetermined number of cards (46) is packaged in a

lightweight plastics material box (70) and a predefined number of
said lightweight boxes is packaged in a magazine (10).
