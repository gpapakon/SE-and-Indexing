<INVENTION-TITLE>
Multi-part casting mould
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B22C900	B22C920	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B22C	B22C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B22C9	B22C9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In order to be able to produce castings in more than one casting plane using casting moulds consisting of moulding blocks arranged in a top and a bottom box, one or more moulding blocks are inserted between the top and the bottom box, these blocks advantageously being accommodated completely within the mould boxes and, with particular advantage, within the bottom and the top mould block.

<CLAIMS>
A casting mould for manufacturing castings, comprising a
moulding block (1, 2) arranged in an upper and a lower box (3, 4),

wherein at least one boxless moulding block (5, 5') is inserted
between the moulding blocks (1, 2) of the upper and lower box (3,

4) and, together with the respective adjacent moulding block (1,
2, 5, 5'), forms at least two mould cavities (6, 7, 19) arranged in

substantially parallel planes, wherein all the mould cavities (6, 7,
19) are fillable by at least one common runner (11, 12),

characterised in that the boxless moulding block or blocks (5, 5')
are surrounded on all sides by the moulding blocks (1, 2) of the

upper and/or lower box (3, 4).
A casting mould according to claim 1, characterised in that the
boxless moulding block or blocks (5, 5') are wholly or partly

enclosed by the upper or lower box (3, 4).
A casting mould according to claim 1 or 2, characterised in that
the runner or runners (11, 12) for the moulds (6, 7, 19) are

formed in the moulding block (1, 2) of the upper and/or lower box
(3, 4). 
A casting mould according to any one of claims 1 to 3,
characterised in that the gates (13, 14) for the moulds (6, 7, 19) are

formed in the boxless moulding block or blocks (5, 5').
A casting mould according to any one of claims 1 to 3,
characterised in that mutually engaging pins (8) and recesses (9)

are formed in the upper and lower moulding blocks (1, 2) and the
boxless moulding blocks (5, 5').
</CLAIMS>
