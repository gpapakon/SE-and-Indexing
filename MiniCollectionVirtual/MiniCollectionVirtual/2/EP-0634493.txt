<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process and apparatus for automatically and continuously preparing a cooked sugar mass.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A23G302	A23G304	C13F100	C13F100	C13G100	C13G106	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A23G	A23G	C13F	C13F	C13G	C13G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A23G3	A23G3	C13F1	C13F1	C13G1	C13G1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Process and apparatus for continuously and automatically preparing a cooked sugar mass, a device for measuring the temperature and the degree of moistness of the sugar mass being arranged at the end of the installation for preparing the cooked sugar mass, the measuring signals emitted by the measuring device being compared with desired values and the steam pressure in the cooking chamber and/or the speed of rotation of the feed pump for feeding the sugar syrup and/or the degree of low pressure inside the low pressure chamber being modified in dependence on a differential signal.
