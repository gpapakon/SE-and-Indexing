<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for connecting wheelable argicultural implements to tractors.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A01B7100	A01B7106	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A01B	A01B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A01B71	A01B71	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A device for coupling movable agricultural mounted machines having a power pick-off shaft drive to a tractor comprises a coupling part (14,15) which can be connected to the power pick-off shaft of the tractor and which is mounted in a coupling housing (12), arranged on the linkage (1,1a,1b) located on the power-lift system of the tractor, and is displaceable between the tractor and the mounted machine via a lever linkage (5,9,11,13) in dependence on the position of a blocking device (4).
