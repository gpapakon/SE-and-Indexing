<INVENTION-TITLE>
METHOD FOR INHIBITING THE PERCEPTION OF SMELLS.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61L901	A61L901	A61L914	A61L914	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61L	A61L	A61L	A61L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61L9	A61L9	A61L9	A61L9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a process for inhibiting the perception of a smell prevailing in an atmosphere and due to a first substance, the process being characterized in that an efficient amount of a second substance is introduced in said atmosphere, said second substance being selected among those which have a perception threshold lower than or equal to 3.10
<
-8
>
 voluminal fraction and a curve slope giving the olfaction logarithm as a function of the concentration logarithm, said slope being equal to at least 2.5 times that of the first substance.
</ABSTRACT>
