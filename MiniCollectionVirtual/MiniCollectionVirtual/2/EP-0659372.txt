<CLAIMS>
A thermally insulating filler (3) for a bedcover, a
sleeping bag, clothing or the like, comprising a first

outer fabric layer (4), a second outer fabric layer (5)
opposite thereto and a puffy filling layer (7), the

puffy filling layer (7) being arranged in chambers (9)
formed by walls arranged between the two outer layers,

characterised by punched cuts (10) passing through the
first outer fabric layer (3), the puffy filling layer

(7) and the second outer fabric layer (5) from one outer
fabric layer (4, 5) to the other outer fabric layer (5,

4).
A filler according to claim 1, characterised in that the

punched cuts (10) are elongate in shape.
A filler according to claim 1, characterised in that the
punched cuts are punctiform in shape.
A filler according to any one of the preceding claims,
characterised in that the punched cuts pass in a

straight line through the bedding (3).
A filler according to claim 2, characterised in that the
elongate punched cuts (10) are arranged at right angles

to each other.
A filler according to any one of the preceding claims,
characterised in that the punched cuts are oriented

perpendicularly to the surface of the filler (3). 
A filler according to any one of the preceding claims,
characterised by a covering (2) protecting the filler

(3) on both sides.
A filler according to claim 7, characterised in that the
covering (2) is connected with the filler (3) in the

edge area by sewing, quilting or the like.
A filler according to claim 7, characterised by a cover
(1) enclosing the filler (3) and the covering (2) on all

sides.
