<INVENTION-TITLE>
Graft polymer in particles and thermoplastic moulding composition obtained therefrom.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08F400	C08F440	C08F29100	C08F29100	C08F29102	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08F	C08F	C08F	C08F	C08F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C08F4	C08F4	C08F291	C08F291	C08F291	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a process for the preparation of elastomeric, particulate graft polymers by polymerisation of one or more monomers in the presence of a preformed graftable elastomeric polymer (graft base or graft core) with formation of a graft sheath (shell), where the polymerisation initiator used is a redox system comprising alkali metal persulphate, a divalent iron salt and alkali metal formaldehyde sulphoxylate.
</ABSTRACT>
