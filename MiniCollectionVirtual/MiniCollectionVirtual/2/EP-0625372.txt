<INVENTION-TITLE>
Apparatus for discharging a processed plasticized material, in particular a ceramic mass.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B02C1500	B02C1514	B65G1500	B65G1502	B65G2120	B65G2120	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B02C	B02C	B65G	B65G	B65G	B65G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B02C15	B02C15	B65G15	B65G15	B65G21	B65G21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Apparatus for discharging processed plasticised material, in particular a ceramic mass, such as brick clay, having an at least partially annular collecting path for receiving and discharging the plasticised material, which falls onto the collecting path from a processing device which is arranged above the collecting path and comminutes the plasticised material in circulation about a central shaft, in which arrangement the collecting path has at least one endlessly circulating collecting belt which extends at least around part of the circumference of the central shaft.
