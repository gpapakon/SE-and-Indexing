<INVENTION-TITLE>
Charcoal lighter.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C10L1100	C10L1104	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C10L	C10L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C10L11	C10L11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Lighter for charcoal and firewood, comprising dry corn cob matter, with the kernels removed, as support material which is impregnated with paraffin and/or ceresine. The corn cob matter is used either in the form of pieces having a length of about 5 cm or as a powder obtained by grinding which is compressed after impregnation to form pieces.
</ABSTRACT>
