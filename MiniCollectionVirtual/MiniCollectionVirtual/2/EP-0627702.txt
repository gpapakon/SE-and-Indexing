<ABSTRACT>
Method and device for obtaining 3D images of an object (4) using a plane array (1) of detectors (2) whose focusing points are situated on a circle (Ce). The advantage obtained is a wider field of detection than with focusing on a single point. Rules are given for using the algorithms for inverting the measurements characteristic of conical focusing (on a single point). Application to medicine and non-destructive testing in industry.
