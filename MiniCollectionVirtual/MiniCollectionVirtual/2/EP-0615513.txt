<INVENTION-TITLE>
FLEXIBLE CONTAINER MADE OF TEXTILE FABRIC
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65D8816	B65D8800	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65D	B65D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
Described is a transport container (1) made of textile fabric, the container walls (7, 8, 9, 10) being made from at least one strip of fabric (7, 8, 9, 10) and having at least one longitudinal seam (11, 12). The container is characterized in that the single longitudinal seam (21) or the several longitudinal seams (11, 12) are flush-sewn. The flush-sewn seams (11, 12, 21) are preferably produced using chain stitches.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
Transport container made of textile fabric, which 
has a casing (1) made of at least one web of fabric and 

provided with at least one longitudinal seam (11, 21), 
the longitudinal seam or the longitudinal seams (11) 

being sewn flat, characterized in that the flat seams 
(11, 21) are formed in their entire length by one or more 

chain-stitch seams. 
Transport container according to Claim 1, characterized 
in that the holding loops (12) are sewn onto the 

flat seams (11) in a manner known per se or are sewn into 
the flat seams at the top of the casing. 
Transport container according to one of the 
preceding Claims 1 or 2, characterized in that the bottom 

(15) and the lid (13) are connected to the casing (1) by 
an edge seam in a manner known per se. 
Transport container according to one of the 
preceding Claims 1 to 3, characterized in that the casing 

(1) has four flat seams (11), distributed over the 
periphery of the casing, and four holding loops (12) 

attached thereto. 
Transport container according to Claim 1 or 3, 
characterized in that it has a casing (1) which is formed 

from a web of fabric which is sewn by a flat seam (21). 
Transport container according to Claim 5, characterized 
in that it is constructed as a one-point bag. 
</CLAIMS>
