<INVENTION-TITLE>
Card for refunding lock.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a deposit box for articles to be fastened releasably to one another, such as shopping trolleys and luggage trolleys, with a lock case 1 which is fastened to the first article and which possesses an orifice 1b for a deposit and an orifice 1a for a coupling member which is fastened to the second article and which is kept detained by a locking bolt until the deposit is inserted, the deposit being a card which, during insertion, moves a control part 3 which moves the locking bolt 4 from the blocking position into the unblocked position, the card having at least one first region which can be bent out of the card plane relative to the remaining second card region by means of a lock projection 14 projecting into the card-insertion path, in the bent-out state this first region butting against a stop 3d of the control part directly or via an intermediate part, in order to move the control part.
