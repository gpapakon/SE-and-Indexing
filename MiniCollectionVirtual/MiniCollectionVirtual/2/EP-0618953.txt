<CLAIMS>
The use of partly dehydrated castor oils with hydroxyl 
values of 110 to 130 mg KOH/g as lubricants. 
The use claimed in claim 1, characterized in that the 
partly dehydrated castor oils are used in combination with 

known lubricants in engine oil, transmission oil, hydraulic 
and/or cooling lubricant fluids, 
The use claimed in claim 1 or 2, characterized in that 
the partly dehydrated castor oils are used on their own as 

a base oil in engine oil fluids, preferably in racing 
engine oil fluids. 
The use claimed in any of claims 1 to 3, characterized 
in that the partly dehydrated castor oils have been produced 

by reaction of castor oils having hydroxyl values of 

160 to 170 mg KOH/g with acidic catalysts at temperatures 
of 200 to 300Â°C with removal of water.
