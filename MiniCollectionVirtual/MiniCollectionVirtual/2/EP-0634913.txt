<INVENTION-TITLE>
HOOK FOR THE TREATMENT OF SPINAL DEFORMITIES.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61B1770	A61B1770	A61B1900	A61B1900	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61B	A61B	A61B	A61B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61B17	A61B17	A61B19	A61B19	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
This hook for the treatment of spinal deformities consists of a shank section 2) having a longitudinal axis (1), transversely to which can be secured a longitudinal support (5), and a hook unit (3) adjacent the shank section (2) and curved away from the longitudinal axis (1). It also has a stabilising extension (4) running substantially in the opposite direction to the curved hook unit (3). The hook can be firmly and stably secured to the vertebra independently of the longitudinal support bearing it.
</ABSTRACT>
