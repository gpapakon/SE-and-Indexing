<INVENTION-TITLE>
SALTS OF POLYVALENT CATIONS OF BLOCK COPOLYMERS OF VINYL-AROMATIC MONOMERS AND CONJUGATED DIENES
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08F842	C08F29700	C08F29704	C08F800	C08F844	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08F	C08F	C08F	C08F	C08F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C08F8	C08F297	C08F297	C08F8	C08F8	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
1. A block polymer of vinylaromatics and conjugated dienes which possesses acidic terminal groups, the said polymer being in the form of a salt of the formula [R-Ya -X (cation)]n [M]
**n (anion) where M is polyvalent metal cation or a metal cation complex, X is a group of the formula   see diagramm : EP0220612,P10,F1   or   see diagramm : EP0220612,P10,F2   in which R' is a divalent organic radical, Y is an alkylene oxide unit, R is a block polymer of a vinylaromatic and a conjugated diene, n is from 2 to 10 and a is from 1 to 10.
</ABSTRACT>
