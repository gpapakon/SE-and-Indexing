<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a heterogeneous composition with a water content of more than 82%, based on meat or fish containing 5 to 15% of solid flakes having dimensions of between 2 and 10 mm and a water content of more than 95% and containing at least one gelling agent.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
Heterogeneous meat- or fish-based composition with a 
water content above 82 %, characterized in that it contains 5 

to 15 % of solid pieces between 2 to 10 mm in size with a 
water content greater than 95 % containing at least one 

gelling agent. 
Heterogeneous composition according to claim 1, 
characterized in that it contains approximately 10 % of solid 

pieces. 
Heterogeneous composition according to claim 1 or 2, 
characterized in that it has a water content of between 82 % 

and 85 %. 
Composition according to one of claims 1 to 3, 
characterized in that the solid pieces have a water content 

above 97 %. 
Composition according to one of claims 1 to 4, 
characterized in that the solid pieces contain as a gelling 

agent a compound selected from K-carrageenin on its own, 
K-carrageenin mixed with a galactomannan, gellane and 

alginate. 
Composition according to claim 5, characterized in that 
the galactomannan is selected from tara gum, carob and cassia 

flour. 
Process for obtaining a composition according to any one 
of claims 1 to 6, characterized in that water is mixed with 

the gelling agent at a temperature of between 15 and 95째C, the 
mixture is treated for a period of between 5 and 15 min at a 

temperature of between 25 and 95째C and is then cooled to a 
temperature of between 4 and 25째C, the gel obtained is diced,  

 
the cubes are mixed with the meat or fish base and the whole 

is sterilized. 
Process according to claim 7, characterized in that water 
is mixed with 0.25 to 3 % of gelling agent. 
Process according to claim 7 or 8, characterized in that 
the gel is cut up to obtain cubes from 2 to 10 mm in size. 
Process according to one of claims 7 to 9, characterized 
in that 85 to 95 % of meat or fish base are mixed with 5 to 

15 % of cubes. 
Process according to one of claims 7 to 10, characterized 
in that sterilization is carried out at a temperature of 

between 110 and 140째C.
