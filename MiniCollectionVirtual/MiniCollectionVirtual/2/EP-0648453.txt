<INVENTION-TITLE>
A device for handling the bedding of a wall bed.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47C1700	A47C1738	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47C	A47C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47C17	A47C17	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a mechanism for operating the base and bedding of a wall bed that pivots on a first fixed pivot (2) between the down position and the up position, said bed having a housing (15) in which the base and bedding (21, 22) can be installed. This mechanism makes it possible to fit part of the base and bedding (21, 22) in the interior of the housing (15) when the wall bed is in the up position and to move it out of the housing when the bed is in the down position.
