<INVENTION-TITLE>
Current generator for therapeutic treatment in field of biophysics and physiology.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61N132	A61N132	A61N136	A61N136	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61N	A61N	A61N	A61N	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a current generator apparatus for powering at least two probes intended to be applied onto a part of the human body with a view to a therapeutic treatment in the field of biophysics and physiology. This apparatus comprises an autonomous electrical supply source (2) capable of delivering a constant voltage, means (18) for generating a constant current, frequency generator means (10, 19) capable of converting the constant current so as to deliver a current having the shape of biphase square-wave (rectangular) pulses with zero mean and steep edges, the total duration T in  mu s of which lies substantially between 200  mu s and 800  mu s, and means (12, 14, 15) for timing the sending of the pulse starts, which means can trigger a pulse every k x 15 ms, with k = 1 or 4.
