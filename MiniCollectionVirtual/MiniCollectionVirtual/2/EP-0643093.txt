<INVENTION-TITLE>
Sheet-like preform from recycled glass mat reinforced thermoplastics.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B29B1702	B29B1702	C08J1100	C08J1106	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B29B	B29B	C08J	C08J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B29B17	B29B17	C08J11	C08J11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a sheet-like semifinished product made from glass matt-reinforced thermoplastic (GMT) predominantly comprising recycled GMT, and in which more than 50% by weight of the glass fibres have a length of greater than 2 mm, and to a process for the production of this semifinished product in which recycled GMT beads are extruded in such a way that essentially no comminution of the glass fibres takes place.
</ABSTRACT>
