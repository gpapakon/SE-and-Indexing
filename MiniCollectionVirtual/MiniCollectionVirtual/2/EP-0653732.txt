<INVENTION-TITLE>
Administration system for hired cars.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G07B1502	G07B1504	G07F700	G07F700	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G07B	G07B	G07F	G07F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G07B15	G07B15	G07F7	G07F7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Administration system for vehicles (V) hired out to customers, characterised in that it comprises at least one parking area (C) comprising a given number of identifiable spaces (C1 ... C20) for vehicles, the vehicles each containing means (12) for transmitting information relating to the state (7 to 10) of certain of its facilities as well as door locking and unlocking control means (11) actuated by an external signal, the said system moreover comprising a central facility (A) for administration and invoicing of hire, each parking area (C) including at least one beacon (D) furnished with means for exchanging information on the one hand with the said means of transmission of the said vehicles, and on the other hand, directly or indirectly with the said central facility (A) and finally with the said customers (H).
