<ABSTRACT>
The post (1) comprises a base (2) for bearing on the ground and an upright (4). The upright (4) comprises a web (5) and at least one plate (7) attached against a peripheral part of the web (5), so that the upright (4) has a cross-section having substantially the shape of an I.

<CLAIMS>
Railing post (1) for road or motorway safety barriers of 
the type comprising a stand (2) resting on the ground and an 

upright (4) comprising a web (5) and at least one sole (7) 
attached against a peripheral part of the web (5), so that the 

upright (4) has a cross-section substantially in the shape of an 
I, characterised in that, if x is the distance in a longitudinal 

direction of the upright (4) of any cross-section of the web (5) 
from a longitudinal end of the upright (4) constituting the top 

of the post (1) and y is the width of the web (5) at the level 
of said section in a transverse direction, y is proportional to 

the square-root of x. 
Post according to Claim 1, characterised in that it 
comprises a single sole (7, 9) which surrounds the web (5) over 

at least part of its periphery. 
Post according to one of the preceding Claims, 
characterised in that it also comprises a reinforcing member (8) 

at the level of a connection area between the stand (2) and at 
least one sole (7, 9). 
Post according to Claim 3, characterised in that the 

elements: stand (2), web (5), sole (7, 9) and reinforcement (8) 
of the post (1) are constituted at least partly by pieces cut 

from a steel sheet and assembled by welding. 
Post according to Claim 4, characterised in that the steel 
sheet from which each of the pieces is cut is a weldable steel 

sheet having a high limit of elasticity, whereof the limit of 
elasticity is greater than 600 MPa.
