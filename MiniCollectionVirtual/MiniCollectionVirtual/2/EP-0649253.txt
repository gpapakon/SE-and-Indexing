<INVENTION-TITLE>
Television receiver with built-in loudspeakers.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H04N564	H04N564	H04R102	H04R102	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H04N	H04N	H04R	H04R	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H04N5	H04N5	H04R1	H04R1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Television receiver equipped with loudspeakers arranged in a particular way. This television receiver (1) includes at least one loudspeaker (9) situated in the region of the back (8) of a cavity (6, 7) external to the casing (3) of the said receiver (1) and formed in at least one of the side faces of the said casing, the said bottom (8) and the said loudspeaker (9) being oriented so as to radiate the sound from the said loudspeaker (9) at least partially towards the front of the said receiver (1) and the said back of the said cavity (6, 7) being arranged close to the rear face of the said casing (3).
