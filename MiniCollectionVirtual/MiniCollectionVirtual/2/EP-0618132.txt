<INVENTION-TITLE>
Low air pressure alarm system for scuba diving.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an alarm system for the event of low air pressure, for scuba divers (10). A manometric contact and an electromagnetic transmitter are arranged on the compressed air bottle (tank) (12) of the scuba diver (10), or in proximity thereto, and a corresponding electromagnetic receiver (24) is arranged on the mask (18) of the diver, the said receiver (24) comprising a pair of light-emitting diodes having two different colours which flash at frequencies which are also different, depending on the state of the manometric contact (20), this state depending on the quantity of air contained in the air bottle (12) of the diver. Application to the practice of undersea diving.
</ABSTRACT>
