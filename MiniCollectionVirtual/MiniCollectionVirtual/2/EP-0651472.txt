<INVENTION-TITLE>
Plastic hammer type brush holder assembly
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H01R3939	H01R3900	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H01R	H01R	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H01R39	H01R39	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A noise-insulating and/or noise-damping sheath (9) is provided around the free outer surfaces of a brush supporting arm (5) in order to reduce the operating noise of a plastic hammer-type brush holder arrangement, which interacts with a rotating commutator (1) and has a brush receptacle (56) at one end and a rotatable bearing (51) at the other end of said brush supporting arm (5).

<CLAIMS>
Plastic hammer-type brush holder arrangement 
having a brush receptacle (56) at one end of a brush 

supporting arm (5) and having a bearing (51), which can 
rotate, at the other end of said brush supporting arm 

(5), characterized by a silencing and/or noise-damping 
sheath (9) of free outer surfaces of the brush supporting 

arm (5). 
Plastic hammer-type brush holder arrangement 
according to Claim 1, characterized by a sheath (9) 

composed of a rubber material. 
Plastic hammer-type brush holder arrangement 
according to Claim 1, characterized by a sheath (9) 

composed of a thermoplastic elastomer. 
Plastic hammer-type brush holder arrangement 
according to Claim 1 or 2, characterized by a sheath (9) 

which is clamped on and is preformed as a profiled part. 
Plastic hammer-type brush holder arrangement 
according to Claim 1 or 2, characterized by a sprayed-on 

sheath (9). 
Plastic hammer-type brush holder arrangement 
according to one of Claims 1 - 5, characterized by a 

sheath (9) formed by all the plastic outer surfaces of 
the brush supporting arm (5). 
Use of a plastic hammer-type brush holder 
arrangement according to one of Claims 1 - 6; 

characterized by installation in a permanent-magnet-excited 
DC motor for driving a motor vehicle fan drive. 
</CLAIMS>
