<INVENTION-TITLE>
DEVICE FOR HOLDING UP TROUSERS, SKIRTS, SHORTS OR SIMILAR
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A41F500	A41F500	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A41F	A41F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A41F5	A41F5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A device for holding up trousers (1), skirts, shorts or similar by attaching this item of clothing to a second item of clothing such as a shirt (2), blouse, top or similar comprises a rigid base piece (3) less than fifteen centimetres in length and having a clip (4) at one end, one leg of said clip being secured to or integral with the base piece (3). The rigid base piece (3) is provided with an attachment means (5) at its end opposite the clip (4), the mobile leg (6) of said clip (4) being oriented, when the clip (4) is closed, towards the above mentioned end fitted with the attachment means (5) in such a way that, when the clip (4) is attached to the edge (12) of the first item of clothing (1), the attachment means (5) at the opposite end can be positioned inside said item of clothing (1), the attachment means (5) being designed for attachment to the second item of clothing (2) lying inside said first item.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
Device for holding up trousers (1), a skirt, shorts 
or similar, that are worn, by fixing this first garment 

to a second garment, formed by a shirt (2), a blouse, a 
body or similar, this device comprising a rigid base 

element (3), of a length lesser than fifteen 
centimetres, provided at one extremity with an 

attachment means (5) constructed to be fixed to a second 
garment (2) inside the first garment (1), characterised 

in that the rigid base element (3) is provided, at its 
extremity opposed to the attachment means (5), with a 

clip (4), constructed to be fixed to the waist-stripe 
(12) of the first garment (1), one leg of this clip (4) 

forming one piece with or being an integral part of the 
base element (3), the other mobile leg (6) of the clip 

(4) being, in the locked position of the clip (4), 
directed towards the aforementioned extremity carrying 

the attachment means (5), in such way that, when the 
clip (4) is fixed to the waist-stripe of the 

aforementioned first garment (1), the attachment means 
(5) on the opposite extremity, can be situated inside 

this garment (1) and can be fixed to the second garment 
(2), inside the first one. 
Device according to the preceding claim, 
characterized in that the attachment means (5) is also a 

clip. 
Device according to the preceding claim, 
characterized in that one of the legs (6) of the clip, 

constituting the attachment means (5), forms one piece  
 

with or is an integral part of the rigid base element 
(3). 
Device according to the preceding claim, 
characterized in that the legs (6) of the clips (4 and 

5) are directed towards one same side in the locked 
position. 
Device according to any one of the claims 3 and 4, 
characterized in that the mobile leg (6) of the clips (4 

and 5) is located along one same face of the rigid base 
element (3). 
Device according to any one of the claims 2 to 5, 
characterized in that the clips (4 and 5) comprise a 

control lever (11), the lever (11) of one of the clips 
(4) having an ornamental design or being provided with 

an ornament. 
Device according to any one of the preceding 
claims, characterized in that the rigid base element (3) 

is made of metal. 
Device according to any one of the preceding claims, 
characterized in that the base elemnt (3) is slightly 

bent or curved, the clip (4) being located at the hollow 
side. 
</CLAIMS>
