<INVENTION-TITLE>
FULLY DIGITAL VOLTAGE TRANSFORMER
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H02M304	H02M3155	H02M3157	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H02M	H02M	H02M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H02M3	H02M3	H02M3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a switching DC voltage transformer, the regulation circuit of which functions digitally, apart from the digitisation element. During the off-phase of the switching cycle the output voltage is measured, which results in a characteristic sloped voltage curve. Control of the output voltage is achieved in such a way that said sloped voltage curve is divided about the given set voltage such that a particular fraction of the digitised voltage values lies above the set voltage and the remainder below the set voltage.
</ABSTRACT>
