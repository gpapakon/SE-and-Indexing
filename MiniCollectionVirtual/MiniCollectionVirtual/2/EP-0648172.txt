<INVENTION-TITLE>
WATER-TIGHT SELF-PROPELLED ELECTRIC MINI-CART.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60P306	B60P310	B62D5100	B62D5100	B62D5106	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60P	B60P	B62D	B62D	B62D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60P3	B60P3	B62D51	B62D51	B62D51	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a self propelled cart (1) characterized in that it is water-tight when immersed, and is comprised of three associated sub-assemblies which may be mounted and dismounted by one person; a water-tight box (6) which acts as a frame, and  contains a motor (4) mounted either directly on the box or on a structure (3) contained in said box (6); two off-road wheels (2a, 2b) which are power driven, dismountable, and fixed to an axle (45) extending through the box (6) by means of two water-tight bearings (14a, 14b); a beam (8) connected to the box (6) by means of a hooking part (86) comprised of two angles and of a cylindrical adaptor containing a connector (88) and assembled so that the beam (8) may be dismounted from the box, and having a handle (82) provided at its extremity allowing through a push button switch (83) to start, accelerate and stop the water-tight self-propelled cart (1). Application to launching of light boats and to taking them out of the water.
</ABSTRACT>
