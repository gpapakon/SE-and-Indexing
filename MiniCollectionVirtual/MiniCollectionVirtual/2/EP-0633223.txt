<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to manganese(III)-containing nickel(II) hydroxide, a process for its preparation and also its use as electrode material for secondary batteries.
</ABSTRACT>

Nickel(II) hydroxide powders containing manganese,

Nickel(II) hydroxide powders according to one or more

of claims 1 to 5, characterised in that it contains 

one or more of the elements from the group Cd, Co, Zn, 
Ca and Mg in a maximum total quantity of 8 mol-%, 

referred to the total quantity of nickel and 
manganese.
