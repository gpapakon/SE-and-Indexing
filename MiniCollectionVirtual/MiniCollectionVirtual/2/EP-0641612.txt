<INVENTION-TITLE>
Industrial washer for the automatic washing of tools and equipment used in food laboratones.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47L1500	A47L1524	B08B302	B08B302	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47L	A47L	B08B	B08B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47L15	A47L15	B08B3	B08B3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The washer comprises a tunnel (1) inside which circulate, in two stages, carriages capable of receiving individual tool-holder frames. During their vertical transfer inside end towers (2, 3), these frames, advancing jerkily, receive the action of spray nozzles for the washing, rinsing and sterilisation of the tools they carry.
