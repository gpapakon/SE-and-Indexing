<INVENTION-TITLE>
DENTAL FIXING ELEMENT, IN PARTICULAR BRACKET
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61K602	A61C700	A61K604	A61C714	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
A metallic dental fixing element, in particular bracket, is made of an aluminium alloy with a hard anodized surface.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
Bracket of metal,
 
characterised in that the metal is an aluminium 

alloy which has a hard anodised surface. 
Bracket according to claim 1,
 
characterised in that the aluminium alloy is 

Al Mg Si O 5. 
Bracket according to claim 1,
 
characterised in that the aluminium alloy is 

Al Mg 3. 
Bracket according to claim 1,
 
characterised in that the aluminium alloy is 

Al Mg 5. 
Bracket according to claim 1,
 
characterised in that the aluminium alloy is 

Al Mg 1. 
Bracket according to claim 1,
 
characterised in that the aluminium alloy is 

Al Mg 2 Mn O 3. 
Bracket according to claim 1,
 
characterised in that the aluminium alloy is 

Al Mg 2 Mn O 8. 
Bracket according to claim 1,
 
characterised in that the aluminium alloy is 

Al Mg Si 1. 
Bracket according to one or more of the claims 1-8,
 
characterised in that the surface is hard anodised 

by means of the Ematal process.
