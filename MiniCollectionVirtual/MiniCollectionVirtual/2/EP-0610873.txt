<INVENTION-TITLE>
ARTIFICAL LEATHER AS A BORDER REINFORCEMENT FOR TEXTILE CONSTRUCTIONS.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B32B2712	B32B2712	D06N300	D06N300	D06N306	D06N700	D06N700	E04H1532	E04H1554	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The material (I) comprises a textile strip (3) coated on one side (A) with a fire-retardant flexible PVC material, opt. contg. pigments, active and inactive fillers and stabilisers, (2,6) and on the other side (B) with a fire-retardant thermoplastic or crosslinked elastomer contg. low flammability additives (4).
</ABSTRACT>
