Method of processing electronic or electrical components.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
In a method for conditioning electronic or electrotechnical components, for example transformers, coil windings, electric motors or the like, the components are to be split or cut. This is performed by means of a device in which there is arranged above a counterplate (heel plate) (11) for supporting the component a splitting knife (10) which executes a stroke (x) with respect to the counterplate (11).
