<INVENTION-TITLE>
CABLE END CAP.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H02G1502	H02G1504	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H02G	H02G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H02G15	H02G15	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The cable end cap described comprises a tubular sleeve (3), made of thermoplastic material and closed at one end, and an elastomeric sealing element (4) with sealing lips (41). The sleeve (3) and the sealing element (4) are connected to each other hermetically by means of an assembly ring (10). This enables cut-off cable ends to be sealed off reliably and rapidly.
</ABSTRACT>
