<INVENTION-TITLE>
Stop valve with actuator.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F16F300	F16F304	F16K712	F16K716	F16K717	F16K31126	F16K31126	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F16F	F16F	F16K	F16K	F16K	F16K	F16K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F16F3	F16F3	F16K7	F16K7	F16K7	F16K31	F16K31	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The actuator (2) of a stop (shut-off) valve (1) has spring assemblies (12) which are arranged in a circumferentially uniformly distributed manner in the top part (8) of the housing. A plurality of springs (13) of a spring assembly (12) are inserted into the top part (8) of the housing, being subjected to a preload between two supporting parts (15) by means of a tightening screw (14), and are compressed further to the operating preload when the top part (8) of the housing is fastened to the bottom part (6) by means of screws (9).
