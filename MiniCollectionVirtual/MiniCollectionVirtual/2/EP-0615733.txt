<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a joint for total joint replacement on the knee, the hips, the shoulder or other joints, comprising the two joint members (2, 3) and a friction-reducing insert located between these. According to the invention, the insert comprises a ball cage with balls (6) located therein.
