<INVENTION-TITLE>
Crosslinking agent for polymer adhesives on the basis of reaction products of 2,4,6-triisocyanato-toluene and polyols.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Crosslinking agents for polymer adhesives having a viscosity of from 3 to 4000 mPas/ 25@C, based on the products of the reaction of 2,4,6-triisocyanatotoluene and polyols having an OH number of from 56 to 1867 and mean functionality of from 1.2 to 2.2 and selected from the group consisting of polyether-polyols, polyester-polyols, alkanepolyols and polycarbonate-polyols, where the residual content of 2,4,6-triisocyanatotoluene in the reaction product is less than 1 % by weight, are suitable for the preparation of polymer adhesives based on natural and synthetic rubbers and hydroxyl-containing polyurethanes.
</ABSTRACT>
