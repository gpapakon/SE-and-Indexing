<INVENTION-TITLE>
Water sterilising device especially for use by dentists and physicians.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61C100	A61C100	C02F128	C02F128	C02F132	C02F132	C02F172	C02F172	C02F900	C02F900	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
An apparatus for sterilising water in the continuous flow process, in particular for medical or dental use, includes a unit for feeding hydrogen peroxide into the water and a downstream UV unit. The UV unit comprises a single low pressure mercury lamp. As a result it is possible to produce the apparatus inexpensively and to design it so as to be spatially small, so that integration into the medical treatment units is easily possible.
