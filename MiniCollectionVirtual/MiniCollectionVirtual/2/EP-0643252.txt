<INVENTION-TITLE>
Suspension arm for control apparatus or control panel.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F16M1102	F16M1106	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F16M	F16M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F16M11	F16M11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The suspension arm for a control apparatus (1) or a control panel (1) has a coupling (4) which is arranged on a vertical supporting tube (2) and supports the control apparatus (1) or the control panel (1) such that it can be adjustably rotated about the longitudinal axis of the supporting tube. The coupling (4) exhibits a top coupling part (5) and a bottom coupling part (6), connected to the top coupling part by a horizontal pivot pin (7), and articulated between the two coupling parts (5, 6) is a manually actuable adjustment member (8) which can steplessly adjust the two coupling parts (5, 6) in a limited pivot-angle ( alpha ) with respect to one another.
