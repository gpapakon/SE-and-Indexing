<INVENTION-TITLE>
Blank, method for cutting the blank, and tray made from cardboard.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a blank (1) made from corrugated cardboard sheet for the construction of a tray (16) with cut corners, a method for cutting out such a blank and a tray constructed from such a blank. The blank includes a set of nine panels connected together by first fold lines (2' to 9'), namely four pairwise identical main panels (3, 5, 7, 9), three intermediate panels (4, 6, 8) separating the said pairwise main panels, and two end panels (2, 10), one (10) forming a fastening tab. The first fold lines (2' to 9') are convergent, the main panels (3, 5, 7, 9) forming isosceles trapeziums.
