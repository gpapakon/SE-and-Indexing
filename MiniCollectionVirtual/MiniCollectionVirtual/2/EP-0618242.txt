<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to self-curing systems based on polymerisable and hydrolytically condensable or condensed silicon compounds. The systems according to the invention comprise one or more components containing initiator and/or activator systems, where at least one component of the system contains a silicon compound of the formula I and/or precondensates derived therefrom and/or condensates derived therefrom.
