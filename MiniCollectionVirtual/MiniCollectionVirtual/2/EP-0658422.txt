<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to moulded articles with a supporting core part of a first thermoplastic polymer and a protective layer, surrounding the core part, of a second thermoplastic polymer based on nylon 11, nylon 12 or nylon 1212, it being possible, depending on the mould and process, for the protective layer to have a thickness from 0.2 to 5 mm and to have specifically provided functional openings.
</ABSTRACT>
