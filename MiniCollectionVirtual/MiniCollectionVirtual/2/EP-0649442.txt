<INVENTION-TITLE>
POLYESTER RESIN FOR THE PRODUCTION OF HIGH-TRANSPARENCY, NEUTRAL-COLOURED ARTICLES.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08G6300	C08G63199	C08G6382	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08G	C08G	C08G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C08G63	C08G63	C08G63	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention concerns a polyester resin for the production of thick-walled bottles of high transparency and neutral colour. This polyester resin, which contains at least one antimony compound, one germanium compound and one phosphorous compound, is produced by reacting a dicarboxylic acid with polyhydric alcohols. It also contains cyclohexanedimethanol. As the polyhydric alcohols, a mixture of ethylene glycol and at least 0.5 % of cyclohexanedimethanol is used. This gives, in particular, a low crystallization rate, permitting the manufacture of thick-walled hollow articles.
</ABSTRACT>
