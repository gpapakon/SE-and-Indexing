<INVENTION-TITLE>
TWO-PIECE CONTAINER AS LARGE PACKING DRUM FOR PASTE-LIKE SUBSTANCES.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B05B1100	B05B1100	B65D8800	B65D8862	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B05B	B05B	B65D	B65D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B05B11	B05B11	B65D88	B65D88	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A two-piece container is subdivided by a flexible membrane (5) stretched between both pieces (1, 2) and securedly but removably attached to their periphery. A pressure medium supply (7) is arranged on one piece (2) of the container and at least one opening (6) is arranged in the other piece (1) of the container. This container is useful as a large packing drum for transporting paste-like substances, in particular adhesives and sealing substances.
 
</ABSTRACT>
