<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method, apparatus for high speed resistance welding of thin sheets.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B23K1124	B23K1124	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B23K	B23K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B23K11	B23K11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A power supply and power recovery module and a conventional servo module (1) are used for supplying a welding transformer (T) with a predetermined current waveform. The power supply and power recovery module provides the connection to the mains and creates a regulated 600 V intermediate circuit. The servo module (1) is programmed as a single-phase current source and is connected to the active outputs of the welding transformer (T). A special required-value source supplies the required current level to the servo module (1) and is controlled by a controller with a programmable memory.
