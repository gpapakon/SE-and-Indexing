DOUBLE CHECK-OUT COUNTER FOR SUPERMARKETS.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to recording systems used in supermarkets and commonly called check-outs. The invention is a device enabling two consumers to record, themselves, their products purchased with the aid of an optical reader and to stash them in bagging devices, under the surveillance of a cashier. The device includes a central station 1 intended for the cashier, enabling the latter manually to record the products and check them through, and two symmetrical gathering stations 2 and 3, for the consumers, each fitted with a reader 11 and with one or two bagging devices 12. The invention is intended to be used in supermarket type stores.
