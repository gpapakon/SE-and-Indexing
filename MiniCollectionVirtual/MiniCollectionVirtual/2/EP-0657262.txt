<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for reducing the number of bacterial germs in plastic dispersions, trough microwave radiation.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61L208	A61L212	B29B1300	B29B1308	C08J328	C08J328	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61L	A61L	B29B	B29B	C08J	C08J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61L2	A61L2	B29B13	B29B13	C08J3	C08J3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A method of reducing the microorganism count in microbially contaminated aqueous multiphase systems containing synthetic resins, such as polymer dispersions and preparations containing polymer binders, by dielectric warming, preferably directly before packaging of the multiphase systems.
</ABSTRACT>
