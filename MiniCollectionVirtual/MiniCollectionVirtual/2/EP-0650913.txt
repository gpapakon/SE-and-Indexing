<INVENTION-TITLE>
Dispenser for at least two rolls of foil
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
A dispenser for at least two rolls of foil/film 2, 13 has a separate housing 1 and 9 respectively for each roll with a separating device 22 for cutting off a section of foil/film. The housings 1, 9 are arranged one above the other, the first, lower housing 1 being designed for attachment to a wall 7, whereas the second, upper housing 9 is mounted on the first housing 1 so as to be removable.

<CLAIMS>
Dispenser for at least two rolls of film or foil 
which are each arranged in their own housing (1 or 9) 

with a severing device (22) for severing a film or foil 
section, a first housing (1) being designed for fitting 

on a wall (7), while a second housing (9) is fitted on 
the first housing (1), characterized in that the second 

housing (9) is fitted on the first housing (1) such that 
it can be removed easily. 
Dispenser according to Claim 1, characterized in 
that each housing (1, 9) has, at the top or bottom, at 

least one projecting continuation (14, 39, 43, 46, 49) 
and, at the bottom or top, a receiving means (16, 42, 44, 

47, 50) for each continuation of an adjacent housing. 
Dispenser according to Claim 2, each housing 
comprising a base (32), a front wall (33), two side walls 

(34), a rear wall (35) and a swing-open cover (11), 
characterized in that the cover (11) is fastened pivotably 

at a distance from the rear side of the rear wall 
(35), and the rear wall is extended downwards beyond the 

base (32) or upwards beyond the cover (11) and terminates, 
towards the top or bottom, at such a distance from 

the upper side (41) of the cover (11) or from the underside 
(40) of the base (32), respectively, that a space 

(42) is provided for receiving the extension (39) of the 
rear wall (35) of an adjacent housing. 
Dispenser according to Claim 2, each housing 
comprising a base, a front wall, two side walls, a rear 

wall and a swing-open cover, characterized in that the 
continuations (46, 49) are provided on the side walls  

 
(45, 48) and the receiving means (47, 50) are provided in 

the side walls (45, 48). 
Dispenser according to Claim 2, each housing 

comprising a base (32), a front wall (33), two side walls 
(34), a rear wall (35) and a swing-open cover (11), 

characterized in that the receiving means (44) or the 
continuation (43) is respectively provided in or on the 

cover (11) and the continuation (43) or the receiving 
means (44) is provided on or in the base (32). 
Dispenser according to Claim 5, characterized in 
that the continuation is formed by one or more pins (43) 

and the receiving means is formed by one or more holes 
(44). 
Dispenser according to Claim 6, characterized in 
that the continuation and the receiving means are formed 

by a releasable latching connection (14, 16). 
Dispenser according to Claim 7, characterized in 
that the latching connection is formed by vertical, 

radially deformable plugs (14) on one housing (1) and by 
openings (16) which receive these plugs and are located 

in the other housing (9). 
Dispenser according to Claim 8, characterized in 
that the plugs (14) are arranged on the upper side (28) 

of the first, bottom housing (1) and the openings (16) 
are arranged on the underside (29) of the second, top 

housing (9). 
Dispenser according to Claim 1, characterized in 
that, as seen in cross-section, the upper side (28) of 

the first, bottom housing (1) runs downwards in the form 
of an arc and the underside (29) of the second, top 

housing (9) runs upwards in the form of an arc. 
Dispenser according to Claim 1 or 10, characterized 
in that the second housing (9) is retained on the 

first housing (1) by magnetic attraction. 
Dispenser according to one of Claims 2 to 11, 
characterized in that the front wall (33) of each housing 

terminates at a distance from the cover (11) and 
delimits, with said cover, a gap (38) for the passage of 

the film or foil, and in that the severing device (22) is  
 

fitted displaceably on the cover (11) and engages, by way 
of a cutting slide (22), in a longitudinal groove (24) in 

the outside of the front wall (33). 
Dispenser according to Claim 12, characterized in 
that the severing device (22) has a cutting slide which 

engages, by way of guide parts (23), in an undercut guide 
groove (21) which extends along the bottom edge (20) of 

the cover (4 or 11). 
Dispenser according to Claim 13, characterized in 
that the guide parts (23) and the guide groove (21) have 

a circular cross-section. 
Dispenser according to Claim 12, characterized in 
that the cutting channel (24) is arranged beneath the top 

edge (25) of the bottom part (3 or 10), and parallel to 
the same, and in that, when the cover (4 or 11) is 

closed, the bottom edge (20) of the cover engages over 
the top edge (25) of the bottom part (3 or 10), respectively. 
Dispenser according to Claim 15, characterized in 
that, in relation to the cutting channel (24), the top 

edge (25) of the bottom part (3 or 10) is offset towards 
the inside of the bottom part. 
Dispenser according to Claim 16, characterized in 
that the top edge (25) of the bottom part (3 or 10) is 

supported by transverse ribs (27) which run along the 
inner surface of the bottom part. 
Dispenser according to one of the preceding 
claims, characterized in that means (30) for securing a 

kitchen roll (31) are fitted on the first, bottom 
housing (1). 
Dispenser according to one of Claims 3 to 18, 
characterized in that the base (32), the side walls (34) 

and the rear wall (35) are produced in one piece from 
plastic and the cover (11) and the front wall (33) are 

produced from wood. 
</CLAIMS>
