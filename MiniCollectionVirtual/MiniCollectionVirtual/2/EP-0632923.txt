<ABSTRACT>
Substantially spherical particles made of lithium silicates have improved mechanical properties and surface quality and are particularly suitable as breeding material for tritium. These particles are characterized by containing added tellurium or tellurium compounds.
</ABSTRACT>

having improved mechanical properties and surface quality, 

characterised in that they contain additions of up to 5 wt-% 
tellurium and/or tellurium compounds.

they contain additionally up to 5 wt-% aluminium and/or 

aluminium compounds and/or silicon and/or silicon compounds.
