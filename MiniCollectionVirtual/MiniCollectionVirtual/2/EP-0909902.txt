<INVENTION-TITLE>
Failsafe clutch control for a vehicle with an automatic clutch
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F16D4800	F16D4806	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F16D	F16D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F16D48	F16D48	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The automobile has an automatic clutch inserted between the engine and the gearbox, operated by a clutch control (1) which is linked to the ignition switch (4). A further control device (13-16) provides a replacement signal for the clutch control upon failure of the connection between the clutch and the ignition switch, allowing the function of the clutch control to be maintained.
</ABSTRACT>
