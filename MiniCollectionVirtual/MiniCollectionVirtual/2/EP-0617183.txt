<CLAIMS>
A key (1) for a motor vehicle with an electrical power supply (7) and 
switchable electrical power consuming devices, characterised in that the key 

contains a voice storage device (6), a microphone (4) and a voice 
reproduction unit (5) as such devices, and that the voice storage device (6) 

can be connected to the microphone (4) in recording mode (4, 6) and to the 
voice reproduction unit (5) in reproduction mode (5, 6). 
A key according to claim 1, characterised in that the operating mode of the 
voice storage device (6) can be selected by entering a switching command. 
A key according to claim 1 or 2, characterised in that the operating mode 
of the voice storage device (6) can be selected by entering a spoken 

command.
