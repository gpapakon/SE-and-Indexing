<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method and apparatus for taking and preparing samples for capillary electrophoresis.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01N27447	G01N27447	G01N3508	G01N3508	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01N	G01N	G01N	G01N	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01N27	G01N27	G01N35	G01N35	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A rapid sample prepurification and a reproducible and automatic formation of derivatives of test substances and also a reproducible introduction of the sample into the separation capillary are to be achieved. For this purpose, the test substance to be analysed is fed to a flow-through reactor and the substances reacted there are fed to the separation capillary, in both cases with a preselected concentration-time profile. The feeding of the reacted substances to the separation capillary is carried out via a defined volume flow, e.g. via a thin flowing liquid layer. The method of the invention and the apparatus make possible the periodic metering-in of on-line samples, by means of which capillary electrophoresis can also be used in process control.
