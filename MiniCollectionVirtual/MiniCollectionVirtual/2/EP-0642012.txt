<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device and method for densitometric and/or colourmetric measurements.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B41F3300	B41F3300	G01N2159	G01N2159	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B41F	B41F	G01N	G01N	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device, consisting of a support surface (1) for the material (7) to be measured, of a device (3, 4, 5, 6) for positioning according to three mutually perpendicular axes (x, y, z), of an interchangeable measurement head (9) and/or a video camera and/or a light-spot indicator and of a computer (8) having a keyboard (8b) and a display screen (8a), which makes it possible to program the measuring positions for the measurement heads (9) and to analyse, evaluate and compare and graphically represent the measurement values collected.
