<INVENTION-TITLE>
Utility vehicle for loading and unloading containers and transfer system.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60P144	B60P144	B66C110	B66C110	B66C122	B66C122	B66F9065	B66F9065	B66F918	B66F918	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60P	B60P	B66C	B66C	B66C	B66C	B66F	B66F	B66F	B66F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60P1	B60P1	B66C1	B66C1	B66C1	B66C1	B66F9	B66F9	B66F9	B66F9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A utility vehicle for loading and unloading containers which can be displaced on the loading platform by means of rolling tracks or sliding tracks, can be locked in the transport position and can be raised or lowered by means of a rear lifting device, the lifting device being a tailgate (4) which can be pivoted and vertically adjusted between a vertical transport position and a horizontal transfer position in which it is flush with the loading platform (1) and a loading position in which it rests on the ground, a cable winch (6) with lengths of cable (13, 14) which extend to the side of the utility vehicle and, as they engage on the container (33), can be deflected about rollers (38) at the free end of the tailgate (4).
