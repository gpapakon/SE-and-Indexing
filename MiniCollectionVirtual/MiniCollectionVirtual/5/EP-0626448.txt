<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a process for preparing recombinant IFN- alpha . Expression in E. coli takes place under the control of a phoA promoter. Linkage of the IFN- alpha  gene to the STII signal sequence achieves secretion of the protein into the periplasmic space and a correctly processed N terminus. The protein is purified by adsorption chromatography on silica gel, hydrophobic interaction chromatography, cation and anion exchange chromatography.
</ABSTRACT>
