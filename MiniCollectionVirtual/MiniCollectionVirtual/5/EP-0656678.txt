<INVENTION-TITLE>
Detector device for homopolar faults on an electric energy distribution network.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H02H338	H02H338	H02H726	H02H726	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H02H	H02H	H02H	H02H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H02H3	H02H3	H02H7	H02H7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a device for detecting homopolar faults on an electrical energy distribution network, characterised in that it comprises:   - a detector (112) of zero-crossing by the homopolar voltage (V0) and   - a phase demodulator (122) of the homopolar current (I0), driven by a signal originating from the zero-crossing detector (112).
