<INVENTION-TITLE>
PRESS-THROUGH PACK, SEAL SHEET FOR PRESS-THROUGH PACK AND METHOD OF PREPARING TABLETS
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61J100	A61J103	A61J310	A61J310	A61J700	A61J700	B65D7528	B65D7532	B65D7534	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61J	A61J	A61J	A61J	A61J	A61J	B65D	B65D	B65D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61J1	A61J1	A61J3	A61J3	A61J7	A61J7	B65D75	B65D75	B65D75	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A press-through pack comprising an accommodation main body (2) having a plurality of accommodation recesses (2a) connected with one another by a sheet-like portion (2b), a substrate (3), and a seal sheet (5) having an adhesive layer (4) disposed on one of the surfaces of the substrate (3), wherein the adhesive layer (4) of the seal sheet (5) and the sheet-like portion (2b) of the accommodation main body (2) are bonded under the state where a solid matter (T) is accommodated in each of a plurality of recesses (2a) of the accommodation main body (2), the accommodation main body (2) is sealed by the seal sheet (5), the seal sheet (5) is bonded to the sheet-like portion (2b) of the accommodation main body (2) so that the solid matter (T) can be taken out when a protuberance portion (2c) as the outer surface of the accommodation recess (2a) accommodating the solid matter is pushed by a finger and the portion of the seal sheet (5) sealing the accommodation recess (2a) is ruptured, without causing float and peel at the bond portion between the adhesive layer (4) of the seal sheet (5) and the sheet-like portion (2b) of the accommodation main body (2), and can be easily peeled off from the accommodation main body (2); and the seal sheet for this press-through pack.
</ABSTRACT>
