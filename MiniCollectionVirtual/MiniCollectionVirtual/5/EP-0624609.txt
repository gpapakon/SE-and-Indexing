<INVENTION-TITLE>
Low emission unsaturated polyester resins.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08F29000	C08F29006	C08L6700	C08L6707	C09D406	C09D406	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08F	C08F	C08L	C08L	C09D	C09D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C08F290	C08F290	C08L67	C08L67	C09D4	C09D4	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a low-emission, styrene-free, unsaturated polyester resin made from a polyester containing acrylate or methacrylate terminal groups and a high-boiling acrylate or methacrylate comonomer. The resin is suitable for the production of flexible coatings and seals (gaskets).
</ABSTRACT>
