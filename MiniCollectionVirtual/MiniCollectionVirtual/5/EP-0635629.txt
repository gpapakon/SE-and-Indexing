<INVENTION-TITLE>
Exhaust gas recycling system diagnose method for a combustion engine.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F02D2100	F02D2108	F02D4122	F02D4122	F02M2507	F02M2507	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F02D	F02D	F02D	F02D	F02M	F02M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F02D21	F02D21	F02D41	F02D41	F02M25	F02M25	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
During stable idling operation the exhaust gas recirculation valve is actuated for a certain diagnostic period at a diagnostic duty factor whilst at the same time the reaction on the engine speed as an engine operating parameter is analysed.
