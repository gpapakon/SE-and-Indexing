<INVENTION-TITLE>
Screening machine.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B07B146	B07B146	B07B148	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B07B	B07B	B07B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B07B1	B07B1	B07B1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A screening machine with a machine stand (1) has longitudinal members (11), on which are releasably mounted exchangeably supports (22, 22'; 40, 40', 40''; 41), on which plug-system screen linings (13) or cross-tentered screen linings (14) are supported. Provided for fastening these are tentering devices (42) which are standard for the screen linings described and which, on the one hand, bear on the respective sidewall (2, 3) of the stand (1) and, on the other hand, fix the adjacent edge region of the screen lining (13, 14).
