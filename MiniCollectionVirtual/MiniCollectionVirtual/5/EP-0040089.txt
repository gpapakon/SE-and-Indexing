<INVENTION-TITLE>
FACE SEAL FOR RELATIVELY ROTATABLE MEMBERS
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F16J1532	F16J1538	F16J1538	F16J1534	F16J1534	F16J1536	F16J1536	F16J1532	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
A face seal, such as for a water pump in the cooling 
system of an internal combustion engine, comprises a rigid 

sealing ring (1) carried by the free end (14) of a moulded 
elastomeric sleeve (2) having a mounting cup (3). The sleeve 

is geometrically equivalent to an axially opposed pair of 
frusto-conical parts (17,19) forming a toggle system which 

under the pressure of sealed fluid exerts a balancing axial 
bearing load on the sealing ring. 
</ABSTRACT>
