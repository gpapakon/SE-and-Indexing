<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a sanitary unit which comprises a three-dimensional, essentially vertically running urinal wall (1), a toilet bowl (3) which can be pivoted into the urinal area (2), and a washbasin (4) which terminates the urinal area (2) to the top.

<CLAIMS>
Sanitary unit, characterized by a three-dimensional,
essentially vertically running urinal

wall (1), a toilet bowl (3), which can be swung
into the urinal compartment (2) bounded by the

urinal wall (1), and a wash-basin (4), which
closes off the urinal compartment (2) at the top.
Sanitary unit according to Claim 1, 
characterized
in that
 the unit is integrated in a wall (5) of a
closet.
Sanitary unit according to Claim 1 or 2,

characterized in that
, in the swung-in state, the
toilet bowl (3) closes off the urinal compartment

(2) in an odour-tight and moisture-tight manner.
Sanitary unit according to one of Claims 1 to 3,

characterized in that
 cleaning nozzles (8) which
are directed towards the toilet bowl (3) are

arranged in the urinal wall (1).
Sanitary unit according to Claim 4, 
characterized
in that
 the cleaning nozzles (8) are designed as
high-pressure nozzles.
Sanitary unit according to one of Claims 1 to 5,

characterized in that
 the unit are [sic] designed

in the manner of a column (9).
Sanitary unit according to one of Claims 1 to 5,

characterized in that
 the unit is designed in the
manner of a half-column and projects from the wall

(5).
Sanitary unit according to one of Claims 1 to 5,

characterized in that
 the unit is designed in the 
manner of a half-column and, in the closed state,

terminates with the wall (5).
Sanitary unit according to one of Claims 1 to 8,

characterized in that
 the urinal compartment (2)
is of arcuate configuration.
Sanitary unit according to one of Claims 1 to 9,

characterized in that
 the unit and its essential
constituent parts consist of high-grade steel.
Sanitary unit according to one of Claims 1 to 10,

characterized in that
 the column (9) contains the
water supply to the cleaning nozzles (8) and to

the wash-basin (4) and is connected to a waste
tank, compost container or sewage-system

connection.
Sanitary unit according to one of Claims 1 to 11,

characterized in that
 a swing-open bidet (14) is
arranged on the underside of the toilet bowl (3).
Sanitary unit according to one of Claims 1 to 12,

characterized in that
 the wash-basin (4), urinal
compartment (2), toilet bowl (3) and bidet (14)

have a common outflow arranged in the column (9).
Sanitary unit according to one of Claims 1 to 13,

characterized in that
 the water flowing out of the
wash-basin (4) can be fed to the cleaning nozzles

(8) via a collecting container.
</CLAIMS>
