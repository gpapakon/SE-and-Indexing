<INVENTION-TITLE>
Genetic selection of proteins able to bind a ligand by signal-transduction in a microorganism
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C07K14195	C12N121	C12N121	C07K1428	C12N1509	C07K1644	C12N1509	C12R119	C07K1644	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for the genetic selection of proteins able to bind a ligand in microorganisms, characterised in that a protein able to bind a ligand is presented extracytoplasmically, and the signal of ligand binding is passed on by signal transduction to the biosynthetic machinery of the microorganism for expression of a detectable and/or selectable function. Also disclosed are microorganisms and replicas suitable for this method and processes for the preparation thereof.
</ABSTRACT>
