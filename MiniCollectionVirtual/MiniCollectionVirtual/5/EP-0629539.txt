<INVENTION-TITLE>
Baby carriage.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B62B900	B62B910	B62B914	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B62B	B62B	B62B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B62B9	B62B9	B62B9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Vehicle for carrying an object or an individual, comprising an underframe (1) with rolling means (2, 3, 4, 5) and a cradle (6) supported by this underframe, the cradle consisting of at least one frame (7) as well as a flexible covering (8) designed for the developed surface of the frame, covering and fixed to the said frame, characterised in that the said frame (7) comprises a central chassis (9) which has the overall shape of a trough, and is formed of two flanks (10) on either side of a base (11), a flat bottom (13), the middle part (13a) of which is designed in terms of shape and dimensions so that it can be entrapped between the two flanks (10) of the central chassis (9), resting flat on its base (11), and a hoop (14), the shape of which is matched to the contour of the flat bottom, and the two middle parts (14a) of which are separated from one another by a distance which is compatible with the separation of the two flanks (10) of the central chassis (9).
