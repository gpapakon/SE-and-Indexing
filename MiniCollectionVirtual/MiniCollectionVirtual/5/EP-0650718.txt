<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a composition for the oxidative dyeing of hair, which is obtained by mixing a high-viscosity, creamy dye-carrier composition (A) with a composition (B) which is in liquid or emulsion form and contains an oxidising agent, where the composition (B) containing the oxidising agent contains a nonionic polymer of the formula (I
>
 R-(O-CH(CH3)-CH2)x-(O-CH2-CH2)y-OH (I),  where     R = C1-C25-alkyl, C2-C25-hydroxyalkyl or C3-C25-dihydroxyalkyl;   x = an integer from 0 to 200;   y = an integer from 0 to 200 and   x + y 
>
/= 20, and to a process for the oxidative dyeing of hair using this composition.
