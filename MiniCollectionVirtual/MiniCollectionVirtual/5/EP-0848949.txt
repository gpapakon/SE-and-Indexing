<ABSTRACT>
Treatment of the mucous membrane of the respiratory tract, comprises applying a preparation in the form of an aerosol, for use on the mucous membrane, which contains: (a) an active agent selected from retinoids and carotinoids; (b) a carrier gas, which may be fluorinated but chlorine free; (c) a saturated hydrocarbon carrier gas; and (d) optionally an antioxidant and/or other suitable additives.
</ABSTRACT>
