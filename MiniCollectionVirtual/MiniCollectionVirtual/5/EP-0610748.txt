<CLAIMS>
Process for purifying industrial anhydrous hydrogen 
fluoride, characterised in that oxygen difluoride is 

added to the hydrogen fluoride. 
Process according to Claim 1, characterised in that 
oxygen difluoride is added undiluted or in mixture with 

inert gases or inert liquids. 
Process according to Claim 1 or Claim 2, characterised 
in that purification takes place in the gas phase at 

temperatures of between 20 and 200 Â°C. 
Process according to one of Claims 1 to 3, 
characterised in that purification takes place at 

elevated pressure.
