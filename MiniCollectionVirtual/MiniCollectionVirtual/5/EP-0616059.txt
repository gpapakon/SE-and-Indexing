<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method and apparatus for drawing, using false twisting, textile fibre tows.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>D02G104	D02G106	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>D02G	D02G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>D02G1	D02G1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The line of material in tow (roving) form, lying between the feed system (1) and the simultaneous drawing (stretching)/false-twisting unit (3), is subjected to rotational driving; consequently the material describes a balloon-type surface of revolution (T) with immediate and opportune consequence, action of the false-twisting in the thinnest regions, even the regions immediately after feeding, and thus even with the possibility of increasing the drawing.
