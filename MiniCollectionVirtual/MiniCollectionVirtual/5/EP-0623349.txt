<INVENTION-TITLE>
Solvent for a sparingly soluble gentamicin salt.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61K4722	A61K4722	A61L2700	A61L2754	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61K	A61K	A61L	A61L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61K47	A61K47	A61L27	A61L27	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a solvent for the salt of gentamicin with 3-p-methoxybenzylidene-6-hydroxy-4'-methoxyflavanone 6-phosphate, which consists of a mixture of 6 to 9.75 parts by volume of tetrahydrofuran and 4 to 0.25 part by volume of water. The solubility of the active substance in these solvents is up to about 50% by weight. A solution of this type is particularly suitable for loading pharmaceutical carriers with this active substance.
</ABSTRACT>
