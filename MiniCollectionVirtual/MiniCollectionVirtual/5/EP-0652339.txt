<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for fixing wall coverings and means performing the same.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the area of furnishing. The aim of the invention is a device which makes it possible to stretch a flexible covering on the walls and on the ceilings characterised in that it is constituted by a composite profile (11) made of semi-rigid plastic material of quadrangular prismatic section, formed by a lip (2) made of deformable flexible material, by an elastic rod (5) bearing the flexible covering by rolling on and allowing its introduction into a front cavity (3) which is closed partially by two projections (4)(4'). Application to decoration.
