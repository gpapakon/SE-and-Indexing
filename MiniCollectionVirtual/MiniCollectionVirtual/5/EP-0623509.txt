<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for making a package and package obtained by the method.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65B510	B65B512	B65D8560	B65D8560	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65B	B65B	B65D	B65D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65B5	B65B5	B65D85	B65D85	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
This device includes a movable punch (20) having a specific profile (20a) and a die (21) having a profile (21a) which complements that of the punch. The method consists in removably fastening individual small dishes (22) on a base support (23) by producing a three-dimensional impression by means of simultaneous stamping of each small dish and of the support using the punch and the die.
