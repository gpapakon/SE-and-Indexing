<INVENTION-TITLE>
Optical means for clock recovery of a coded signal.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G02F300	G02F300	H04B1000	H04B1000	H04L700	H04L700	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an optical device for recovering the clock rate of a signal coded (Si) by a digital code such as the NRZ, CMI, biphase, etc. code. This device includes   . a signal (1) for shaping the coded signal which converts the coded signal to the form of a series of pulses with duration equal to N x T/2 and with recurrence equal to N x T, . a circuit (19) for recovering the clock rate, able to determine the clock signal sought.   Application to digital transmission by optical fibres.
