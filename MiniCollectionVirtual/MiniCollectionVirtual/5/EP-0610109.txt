<INVENTION-TITLE>
Audio-visual installation for conference rooms.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Audiovisual installation for a conference speaker, allowing the latter to control the distribution of audiovisual information simultaneously with his presentation. The installation mainly comprises a reader of information representing images (12), means of viewing these images (24), a central unit (11) and a control monitor (14) on which the text of the conference, stored within the central unit, is displayed, and including means for addressing, to this same central unit, control signals able to drive the reader (12).
