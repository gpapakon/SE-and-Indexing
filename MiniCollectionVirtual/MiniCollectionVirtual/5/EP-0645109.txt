<INVENTION-TITLE>
Convertible sitting and sleeping furniture, particularly a bed couch.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47C1700	A47C1713	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47C	A47C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47C17	A47C17	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The convertible sitting and sleeping furniture, particularly bed couch, has a plurality of upholstered parts (head, middle and foot part 1, 2, 3) movable relative to one another for sitting and sleeping use, a bedding box (5) and a back (4). The head part (1) has a spring interior (6) and, in the sitting position with middle and foot part (2, 3) lying one on top of the other, is retracted into the back (4) and, in the sleeping position, is extended from the back (4) so that it lies in an elongated plane with middle and foot part (2, 3).
