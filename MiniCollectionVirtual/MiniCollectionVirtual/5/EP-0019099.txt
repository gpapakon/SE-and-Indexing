<INVENTION-TITLE>
Hook bolt for coke oven door.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C10B2500	C10B2516	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
In a hook bolt (6) for a coke oven door having a circumferential sealing edge (2), which hook bolt passes through the side walls (4) of the door body and, with its head, retains the sealing edge (2) which comes to bear on the frame located at the head of the oven, the hook bolt (6) possesses a slotted sliding tab (7) and a T-shaped peg (8) which is attached by its central peg to the side walls (4) of the door body and whose head retains the sealing edge (2) by one of its ends, by means of a frictional fit, and at the other end receives a set- screw (9) which presses the sliding tab (7) against the back of the sealing edge (2). For the purposes of simple adjustment and precise setting of the sealing edge (2) in the event of deformations of the oven head or of the coke oven door during a coking operation, the hook bolt (6) is equipped with a simple adjusting element, the set-screw (9). For automatic setting of the sealing edge (2), a compression spring (11) is arranged between the head of the set-screw (9) and the sliding tab (7).
