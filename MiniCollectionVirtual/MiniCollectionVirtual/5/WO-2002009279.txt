<INVENTION-TITLE>
SURFACE ACOUSTIC WAVE FILTER DEVICE
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H03H900	H03H964	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H03H	H03H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H03H9	H03H9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A surface acoustic wave filter device, comprising a surface acoustic wave element having first and second two-port surface acoustic wave filters (100, 200) and a one-port surface acoustic wave filter (300) with a single inter-digital converter connected, by a face down bonding system, to an electrode formed on an envelope (500) through a bump, the envelope (500) further comprising an opening part for a terminal electrode for earthing provided at the portion thereof opposed to the one-port surface acoustic wave filter (300).
</ABSTRACT>
