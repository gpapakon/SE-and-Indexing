<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method and arrangement for forming, levelling and transporting slivers between a carding and a spinning machine.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>D01G2100	D01G2100	D01G2300	D01G2300	D01H900	D01H918	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>D01G	D01G	D01G	D01G	D01H	D01H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>D01G21	D01G21	D01G23	D01G23	D01H9	D01H9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method and an arrangement for forming, levelling and transporting fibre slivers between a card (1) and a spinning machine (5). With the aim of reducing the outlay in terms of the transport of the fibre slivers (70,71), two card slivers (70,71) levelled off on the card are guided directly into a drawframe (16) arranged near to, preferably at the card. The sliver (72) of the drawframe (16), drawn in a regulated manner, is deposited in portions into spinning cans (21,22,23) presentable on the spinning machine and is fed to the spinning stations of an open-end spinning machine (5) via a can changer (41) and a can-transport carriage (4). The two card slivers (70,71) are preferably produced on a two-system card (1) with an integrated drawframe (16) for the two card slivers.
