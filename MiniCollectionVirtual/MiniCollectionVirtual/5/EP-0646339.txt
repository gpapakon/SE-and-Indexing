<INVENTION-TITLE>
Range of products for making up a face, in particular the eyes.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A45D4400	A45D4400	G01J346	G01J346	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A45D	A45D	G01J	G01J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A45D44	A45D44	G01J3	G01J3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a range of products for making up the face, in particular the eyes, comprising, in particular, eye (eyelid) shadows or paints (F), mascaras (M), pencils (C) such as kohl pencils or eye-liner pencils, blushers (B) and lipsticks (R), characterised in that the shades of the said range are chosen with respect to a pallet of colours of limited number, the said colours corresponding to individual colours existing in the iris of the eye, comprising at least the colour or colours of the sparkle of the iris or the colour of the iris contour and, if appropriate, the impression colour of the iris.
