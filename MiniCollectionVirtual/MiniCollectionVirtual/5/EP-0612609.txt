<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for producing a cruved veneered covering element from a metal/wood laminate, particularly for vehicle interiors.

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for producing a curved veneered covering element from a wood/metal laminate, particularly for vehicle interiors, in which the successively performed method steps are carried out in the sequence mentioned; a planar metal sheet is adhesively bonded to a genuine wood veneer to form a semifinished laminate, the genuine wood veneer of the semifinished laminate is superficially smoothed and finish-machined on the visible side, planar pieces are cut out from the treated semifinished laminate as blanks of the covering elements and the blanks of the covering elements are shaped into the form required for installation of the covering elements.
