<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process and inoculant for the production of cast iron.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C21C100	C21C108	C22C3300	C22C3308	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C21C	C21C	C22C	C22C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a process for the production of cast iron, in which a cast iron melt has an inoculant added to it, the inoculant employed being microcrystalline natural graphite.
</ABSTRACT>
