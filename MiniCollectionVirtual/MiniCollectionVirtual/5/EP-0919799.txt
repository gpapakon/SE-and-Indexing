<INVENTION-TITLE>
Deep-drawing test device, in particular for welded sheet metal
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01N300	G01N300	G01N328	G01N328	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01N	G01N	G01N	G01N	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01N3	G01N3	G01N3	G01N3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The test device comprises a sheet holder (1), a sizing die (2), and a piston (3). The sheet (4) under test is held by the clamping action of the sheet holder and the die, which are jointly acted upon by positioning mechanisms (8a,8b). The die has two adjustable clamp pieces (2a,2b), which are acted upon by essentially equal clamping forces.
</ABSTRACT>
