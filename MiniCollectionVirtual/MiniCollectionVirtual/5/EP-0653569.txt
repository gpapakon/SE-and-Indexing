<INVENTION-TITLE>
Mounting for connecting abutting structural members.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F16B1200	F16B1214	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F16B	F16B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F16B12	F16B12	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In order to provide a fitting for connecting mutually abutting structural elements, in particular panel-like or bar-like elements for furniture, having at least one base 3 and at least one housing 2, the base 3 being connected to one element and the housing 2 being connected to the other element, and it being possible for the base 3 and the housing 2 to be latched to one another, which fitting permits a stable connection of the corresponding furniture elements and can be produced with a low degree of outlay, the base 3 exhibits at least one retaining part 4 and at least one latching part 5 which can be connected thereto, and the base 3 is connected to the associated element by means of the retaining part 4 and can be latched to the housing 2 by means of the latching part 5.
