<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method and device to secure a motor vehicle against theft.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60R2500	B60R2500	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60R	B60R	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
In order to secure a motor vehicle against theft, a locking rod (28) is inserted from the outside of the vehicle into the pivot path of a pedal (2, 4, 6) and locked there.
