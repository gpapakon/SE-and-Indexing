<INVENTION-TITLE>
Control and/or display unit for operating at least a security device and a car telephone.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60K3500	B60K3500	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60K	B60K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60K35	B60K35	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A control and/or display unit 17 is arranged in an ashtray 18 so that, with the ashtray 18 open, one or more security devices and a car telephone in a motor vehicle can be operated by way of the control and/or display unit 17. If the control and/or display unit 17 is arranged in the ashtray 18 the control and/or display unit 17 is not visible from outside when the ashtray 18 is closed. By combining the control and/or display unit 17 with one or more security devices and a car telephone it is possible for signals to be automatically delivered by way of the car telephone to any fixed telephones in the event of misoperation of the security devices.
