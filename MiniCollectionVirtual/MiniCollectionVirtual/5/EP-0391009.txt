<INVENTION-TITLE>
VIDEO RECORDER WITH A HEADDRUM UNIT
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G11B1512	G11B1514	G11B15473	G11B15473	G11B2710	G11B2710	H04N1706	H04N1706	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The production tolerances of a head-drum unit (1) are compensated by a data value (Tv8) of a delay time (Tv), which is stored as an operating value in a non-volatile data memory (6) for setting a delay of the position signal (I(LG)) of a position encoder (9) of the head-drum unit (1). This operating value (Tv8) can be determined in a test circuit arrangement (5) from the vertical synchronisation signals (19) sensed in the helical track of a reference video tape (14) and the switching edge of the change-over signal (SK) generated by a head-drum control circuit (2) for changing over the video heads (11, 12), and can be stored in the non-volatile data memory (6).
