<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the recovery of valuable products in the workup of polyvinyl chloride.

<ABSTRACT>
Heating of polyvinyl chloride, in particular plasticised polyvinyl chloride, to a temperature of from 250 to 500 DEG C in the absence of oxygen gives a low-chlorine, carbon-containing residue, plasticiser and hydrogen chloride.
