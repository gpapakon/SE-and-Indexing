<INVENTION-TITLE>
Battery with incorporated voltage converter.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H01M1042	H01M1044	H01M1046	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H01M	H01M	H01M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H01M10	H01M10	H01M10	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Batteries are known in which a voltage converter is connected in order to maintain a constant output voltage when the battery voltage drops. In order to make it as easy as possible to handle the battery and the voltage converter, the invention proposes that the battery and the voltage converter be combined in one mechanical unit.
