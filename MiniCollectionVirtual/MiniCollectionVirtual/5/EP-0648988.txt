<INVENTION-TITLE>
Refrigerator with slidable shelves
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F25D2502	F25D2502	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F25D	F25D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F25D25	F25D25	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In a refrigerator, having horizontal guide rails arranged on opposite side walls of an inner container (2), shelves of different lengths are provided. In order to provide a simple storage possibility for a shortened shelf with a simple construction, at least one guide rail (12) extends from the rear wall (6) merely over a partial length of other non-shortened guide rails (5).

<CLAIMS>
A refrigerator with a vertical inner container (2) to be
cooled, which comprises guide rails (5) for guiding of slidable

storage shelves, said guide rails running horizontally into the
depth of the housing on opposite side walls, whereby the guide

walls extend from a rear wall (6) of the container to a frontal
access opening (3) that is closable by means of a door (4) and

storage shelves of different lengths are provided, characterised
in that at least one guide rail (12) extends away from the rear

wall (6) over only a part of the length of another unshortened
guide rail (5).
A refrigerator according to claim 1, characterised in that
a correspondingly shortened storage shelf (11) is supportable on

the shortened guide rail (12).
A refrigerator according to claim 1 or 2, characterised in
that only one shortened guide rail (12) is arranged above the

usual unshortened guide rails (5).
A refrigerator according to claim 1 or any following claim,
characterised in that a shortened guide rail (12) is provided

above a control housing (13) arranged on a side wall for
receiving control and/or lighting elements. 
A refrigerator according to claim 1 or one of the following
claims, characterised in that a shortened guide rail (12) is

provided between the upper wall (8) of the inner container and
the control housing (13).
A refrigerator according to claim 5, characterised in that
the shortened guide rail (12) is arranged at a spacing of up to

10 cm from the upper wall (8).
</CLAIMS>
