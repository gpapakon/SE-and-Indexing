<INVENTION-TITLE>
Mixing and distribution device for cattle food.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A01F2516	A01F2520	B28C500	B28C542	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A01F	A01F	B28C	B28C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A01F25	A01F25	B28C5	B28C5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The mixing and distribution device for cattle feed is characterised in that the container is a closed drum (2) with a drum casing and with two drum end walls (22, 22'), the drum (2) being rotatable about a horizontal or inclined axis of rotation (20) passing through the drum end walls (22, 22'), the drum (2) having at least one closeable orifice for feeding and emptying the drum (2).
