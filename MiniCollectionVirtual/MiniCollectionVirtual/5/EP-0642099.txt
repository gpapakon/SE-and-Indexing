<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device and process of counting articles streaming on a conveyor.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G06M100	G06M110	G06M700	G06M710	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G06M	G06M	G06M	G06M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G06M1	G06M1	G06M7	G06M7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a device for counting articles (1) streaming on a conveyor belt (2), especially a device for counting notebooks on a conveyor, of the type including a collimated light source (3) and a photodetector (4), as well as an electronic circuit for processing the signal delivered by the photoelectric cell and for generating a signal for counting the number of articles (1) detected, the light source (3) includes means for generating a two-dimensional light beam (10), the plane of the said beam (10) being substantially perpendicular to the axis of streaming of the articles (1) to be counted, and in that the photodetector (4) consists of a linear sensor, the light source and the photovoltaic cell being arranged on either side of the conveyor belt (2).
