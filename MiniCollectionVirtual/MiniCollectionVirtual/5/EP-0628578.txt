<INVENTION-TITLE>
2,6-Dimethylphenylphenymaleimide as comonomer in the copolymerisation of styrene or vinyl chlorid.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08F2200	C08F2236	C08F21200	C08F21200	C08F21208	C08F21400	C08F21406	C08F22200	C08F22240	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The use of 2,6-dimethylphenylmaleimide as comonomer for the preparation of styrene copolymers and vinyl chloride copolymers having a higher glass transition temperature and lower inherent colouration than the prior art.
</ABSTRACT>
