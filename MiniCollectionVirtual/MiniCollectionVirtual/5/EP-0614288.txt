<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method and apparatus for equalising a radio signal using a fast least squares algorithm.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H04B7005	H04B7005	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H04B	H04B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H04B7	H04B7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The method consists in approximating a sampled signal Xn, by a digital filter (1) which is matched (2) in order to minimise an error signal. It consists in searching for a unitary square matrix which, applied directly to the matrix XN(n) of the samples Xn converts the latter into a triangular matrix RN(n). Applications are to the production of radiocommunication receiver sets.
