<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method and device for influencing liquid in ground.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B09C100	B09C100	B09C102	E21B4300	E21B4330	E21B4334	E21B4340	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
In the method and the device for influencing liquids found in the ground (11), auxiliary liquid circulations (27) are generated by means of auxiliary boreholes (22) in the region of influence of a shaft (10) in which a groundwater circulation (20) through the ground (11) is caused, in order to achieve mixing of liquid phases of different specific gravities for transporting them into the main shaft (10).
