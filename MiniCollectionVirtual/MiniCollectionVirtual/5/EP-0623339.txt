<ABSTRACT>
Use as active compounds of resorcin derivatives for the preparation of a cosmetic or dermatological composition having a depigmenting action. The said derivatives correspond to the following formula: 
<

>
  in which:   R1 represents a halogen atom or a radical -CnH2n-CO2R4, n being an integer between 1 and 5, and R4 represents a hydrogen atom or an alkyl radical having from 1 to 6 carbon atoms, R2 represents a hydrogen atom, an alkyl radical having from 1 to 12 carbon atoms, a hydroxyalkyl radical having from 1 to 4 carbon atoms, a formyl or carboxyl radical, and R3 represents a hydrogen atom, an alkyl radical having from 1 to 12 carbon atoms, a hydroxyalkyl radical having from 1 to 4 carbon atoms or a carboxyl radical, with the proviso that if R2 is other than a hydrogen atom, R3 then represents a hydrogen atom and that if R3 is other than a hydrogen atom, R2 then represents a hydrogen atom.
