<INVENTION-TITLE>
Analog-digital conversion device for signals of different levels.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H03M118	H03M118	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H03M	H03M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H03M1	H03M1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The input signal passes through an adjustable analogue amplifier (2) to the first digitiser (1). The n+1 output data bits are applied to a logic unit (31) producing a signal which denotes whether the output is at a max. or a predeterminable threshold level. A fixed-gain amplifier (3) forwards this signal to an integrator (4) for setting the adjustable gain. The setting (S) is weighted as necessary and supplied via a second digitiser (5) for multiplication (6) of the digitised data, cancelling errors due to the variation of gain.
</ABSTRACT>
