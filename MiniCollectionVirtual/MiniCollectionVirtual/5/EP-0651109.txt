<INVENTION-TITLE>
Reconstruction system for stairs.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E04F1102	E04F1116	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E04F	E04F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>E04F11	E04F11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Device permitting worn-out stairs to be renovated and lined, characterised in that it consists of an aluminium section (profile) comprising a vertical plane region (1) intended to be applied against the old riser support, a longitudinal striated region (2) forming a non-slip part, a part (3) intended to cover the step, a part (4) on the upper end of the section, serving to hold a step nosing, and a cavity (5) making it possible to introduce the step nosing and, on the lower end, a guide rail (slide) (7) formed from a projection for the housing of the step nosing.
