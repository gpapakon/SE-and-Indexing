<INVENTION-TITLE>
Drill support.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B23B4500	B23B4514	B23Q1104	B23Q1104	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B23B	B23B	B23Q	B23Q	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B23B45	B23B45	B23Q11	B23Q11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Drill stand (support) including a pillar (3) along which a drill-carrying slider (4) can slide under the action of a sliding motor (5) and of means for controlling the motor. The sliding motor (5) has a fixed speed, the means for controlling the motor are designed to be controlled by a current sensor and drill-carrying means (10) are mounted on the slide (4) so that they are free to slide from a position of rest to a position in which the motor is operating, against the action of return means (12, 13).
