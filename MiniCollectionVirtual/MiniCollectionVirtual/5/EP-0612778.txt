<INVENTION-TITLE>
Epoxy resin composition
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08G5900	C08G5914	C08G5922	C08G5930	C08G5938	C08G5968	H05K103	H05K103	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
Halogen-free, low-flammability epoxy resin systems which are particularly suitable for the production of circuit board base material contain   a. at least one nitrogen-containing epoxide compound containing at least two epoxide groups, b. at least one phosphorus compound containing at least one, preferably at least two, epoxide groups, c. at least one metal complex compound as curing agent, and optionally d. other fillers and additives.  s
</ABSTRACT>
