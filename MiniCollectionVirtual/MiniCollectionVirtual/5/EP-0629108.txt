<CLAIMS>
Flat bus-bar for electrical power distribution, 
characterised by the fact that it comprises a printed circuit (1) 

on a board (1, 2). 
Bus-bar according to Claim 1, in which the printed circuit 
(1, 2) is a multi-layer circuit. 
Bus-bar according to Claim 2, in which the multi-layer 
circuit (1, 2) is arranged to distribute several supplies. 
Support for electrical components (3) on which is mounted 
the bus-bar (1, 2) according to one of Claims 1 to 3, for 

supplying components through the intermediary of power supply 
distribution strips (4) connected to the support (3) and to the 

bus-bar (1, 2). 
Power supply distribution strip (4) for connecting the 
component support (3) of Claim 4 to the bus-bar (1, 2) of one of 

Claims 1 to 3, characterised by the fact that it comprises two 
different edges (5, 6) for connection to the support (3) and to 

the bus-bar (1, 2). 
Strip according to Claim 5, in which the two connecting 
edges (5, 6) are perpendicular to each other. 
</CLAIMS>
