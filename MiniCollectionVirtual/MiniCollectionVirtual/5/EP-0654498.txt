<INVENTION-TITLE>
Preparation of rubber chemicals.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08K700	C08K700	C08K716	C08L2100	C08L2100	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08K	C08K	C08K	C08L	C08L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C08K7	C08K7	C08K7	C08L21	C08L21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A rubber chemical preparation comprising or consisting of rubber chemicals in finely divided form and a carrier liquid, in which the rubber chemical particles have a mean diameter (weight average d50) of from 2  mu m to 20  mu m, preferably 
<
 10  mu m, and a particle diameter distribution corresponding to  delta d10-d90 
<
 20, preferably 
<
 10  mu m.
</ABSTRACT>
