<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device and method for detecting measured date specific to the body.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61B50432	A61B50432	G06F1740	G06F1740	G06F1900	G06F1900	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61B	A61B	G06F	G06F	G06F	G06F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61B5	A61B5	G06F17	G06F17	G06F19	G06F19	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a portable device and a method for detecting body-specific measurement data, in particular ECG and/or blood-pressure measuring devices, with a measurement receiver delivering the measurement data, a hard disk which receives the measurement data and is insensitive to vibrations, a unit for outputting and/or displaying the detected measurement data and a control unit which controls the detection.
</ABSTRACT>
