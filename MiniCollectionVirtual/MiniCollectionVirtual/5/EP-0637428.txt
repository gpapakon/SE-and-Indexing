<INVENTION-TITLE>
Hanging device for pieces, in particular for elongated heavy pieces
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47F500	A47F500	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47F	A47F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47F5	A47F5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Member for hanging articles, especially elongate articles having a certain weight, including a perforated base (2) fixed perpendicularly to a tab (1) of substantially equal width and of substantially rectangular shape equipped at the top with an opening (12). It includes a cutout (11) of a certain height made in the tab (1) facing the opening (21) formed centrally in the base (2) for the purpose of allowing a threaded rod fixed to the end of an elongate article to be inserted.

<CLAIMS>
Device for hanging elements, especially elongated
elements (3) having a certain weight, and comprising a

perforated base (2) fixed perpendicularly to a bracket (1)
with an approximately equal width and having an approximately

rectangular shape and provided at its upper portion with an
opening (12) for its suspension, characterised in that the

bracket (1) is rendered integral with the base (2) at its
middle along a median line of said base (2), and in that a

cut (11) with a certain height is made in the bracket (1)
opposite an opening (21) fitted at the centre of said base

(2) so as to enable a threaded rod (31) to be introduced and
fixed at the end of an elongated element (3).
