<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a vibratory conveyor (2), the drive taking place by means of two pairs of unbalanced shafts (5, 6, 7, 8) rotating in opposition, so that conveyance on the principle of the vibrating shute is achieved, but no appreciable vertical forces to be absorbed by the foundation occur.
