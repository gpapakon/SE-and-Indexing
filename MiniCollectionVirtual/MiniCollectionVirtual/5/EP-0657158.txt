<INVENTION-TITLE>
Hair dye composition.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A45D700	A45D704	A61K800	A61K800	A61K830	A61K833	A61K841	A61K849	A61Q510	A61Q510	D06P100	D06P132	D06P304	D06P308	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A45D	A45D	A61K	A61K	A61K	A61K	A61K	A61K	A61Q	A61Q	D06P	D06P	D06P	D06P	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A45D7	A45D7	A61K8	A61K8	A61K8	A61K8	A61K8	A61K8	A61Q5	A61Q5	D06P1	D06P1	D06P3	D06P3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The lightfastness and intensity of hair dyeings are considerably improved when hair dye compositions which contain at least one hair dye precursor from the group 1,4-diaminobenzene, 1-methyl-2,5-diaminobenzene, a tetraaminopyrimidine and/or a triaminohydroxypyrimidine have added to them 1,4-diamino-2-chlorobenzene in combination with at least one other coupler substance selected from the group  alpha -naphthol, 1-methoxy-2-amino-4-( beta -hydroxyethylamino)benzene, 2,6-diaminopyridine, 3-amino-2-methylamino-6-methoxypyridine, 1-hydroxy-3-amino-2,6-dichlorobenzene, 1,5-dihydroxynaphthalene, 1,7-dihydroxynaphthalene, 1-ethoxy-2,4-diaminobenzene and/or 1,3-bis(2,4-diaminophenoxy)propane.
</ABSTRACT>
