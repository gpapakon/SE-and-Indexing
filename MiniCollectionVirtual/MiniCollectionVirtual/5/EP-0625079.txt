<ABSTRACT>
In order to simplify and to make more accurate the manufacture of tubular parts with at least a branching tubular section, the whole branching part is shaped out of two flat metal sheets (10) whose shape corresponds to that of an unwound half-shell of the branching part and which are cold-formed into corresponding half-shells (12, 13). Both half-shells (12, 13) are then laser soldered to each other at their parting plane (14, 15).
</ABSTRACT>
