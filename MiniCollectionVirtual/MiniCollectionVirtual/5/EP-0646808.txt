<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for monitoring the nitrogen monoxide content in a medium by electron paramagnetic resonance in lithium phthalocyanine.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61K4906	A61K4920	G01R3360	G01R3360	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61K	A61K	G01R	G01R	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61K49	A61K49	G01R33	G01R33	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The method is applicable to a liquid and/or gaseous medium. It consists in measuring the EPR signal of crystals of free-radical lithium phthalocyanine in contact with the medium to be monitored. It is possible to determine NO concentrations of 0 to 16  mu mol/l in this way by determining the line width in an electron paramagnetic resonance spectrometer.
