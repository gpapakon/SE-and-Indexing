<INVENTION-TITLE>
Base plate and flange pivotable upon it.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a flap (40) which can be placed on a baseplate (10) assigned to it and is pivotable between an upright and a horizontal position, in particular a document stop in a document delivery tray of a document printer. The flap (40) can only be released from the baseplate (10) in a position located outside its operational pivoting range and can be placed on the baseplate (10) with one hand even where it can only be reached from one side.
