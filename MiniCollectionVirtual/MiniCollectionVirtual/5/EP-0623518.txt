<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for applying at least partially labels on articles.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65C100	B65C104	B65C900	B65C900	B65C904	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65C	B65C	B65C	B65C	B65C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65C1	B65C1	B65C9	B65C9	B65C9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Device for at least partially applying labels (1) to articles (2), characterised by a linear conveyor (4), on which are located rotatable carriers (3) for the articles (2), on which the articles (2) are to be retained non-rotatably and which, in at least one segment of the conveying travel of the linear conveyor (4), are rotated for cooperation with at least one pressing member (7), pretensioned in the direction of the articles (2), for pressing the labels (1) onto the articles (2), preferably for pressing edge regions (5) of the labels (1) projecting from the articles (2) in the direction of the conveying travel, and/or for cooperation with at least one label dispenser (18) reaching near to the articles (2).
