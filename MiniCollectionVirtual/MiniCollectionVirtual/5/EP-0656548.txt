<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to edge-illuminated waveguide elements comprising a matrix plastic A and scattering particles, distributed homogeneously therein, of a crosslinked polymer B. The invention is characterised in that the matrix plastic A is incompatible with the polymer, the matrix plastic A is highly transparent, the proportion of scattering particles is 0.01 to 1% by weight, based on A, the refractive index nD(B) of the polymer B is at least 0.01 unit greater than the refractive index nD(A) of the matrix plastic A, and the polymer B is preferably built up from comonomers containing aromatic substituents.
</ABSTRACT>
