<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for producing noble metal huminates.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61K3502	A61K3510	C08H500	C08H500	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61K	A61K	C08H	C08H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61K35	A61K35	C08H5	C08H5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In the novel process for the preparation of nobel metal huminates, a polyhydric phenolic compound is oxidised electrochemically or plasma-chemically in alkaline aqueous solution, a solution or a hydrosol of an appropriate nobel metal salt being added to the alkaline solution of polyhydric phenolic compound in the reaction chamber after commencement of the oxidation reaction. When the oxidation reaction is complete, the reaction mixture is adjusted to a pH in the range from 4 to 6.
</ABSTRACT>
