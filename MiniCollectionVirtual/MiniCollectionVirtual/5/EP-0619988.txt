<INVENTION-TITLE>
Attachment for dentures and method of making.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61C508	A61C509	A61C13225	A61C13265	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61C	A61C	A61C	A61C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61C5	A61C5	A61C13	A61C13	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention specifies an attachment for dentures which comprises a first attachment member (1) that can be fixed on a retention element (5) and a second attachment member (3) that can be pushed thereon, and guide elements (7, 9) which extend from the occlusal side to the cervical side and engage each other between the attachment members, as well as a method of making it, in which at least one of the guide elements is releasably fastened on an attachment member in order to facilitate activation.
