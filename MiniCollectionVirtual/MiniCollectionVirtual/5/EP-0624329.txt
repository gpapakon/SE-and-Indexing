<INVENTION-TITLE>
Guide tube for length adjustable column of height adjustable furniture.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47C320	A47C330	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47C	A47C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47C3	A47C3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Guide tube designed as part of a length adjustable column fitted between the underframe and the top part of an item of height adjustable furniture, this tube comprising a tubular body (3) and an end piece (10) which supports the end of the piston rod (9) of a gas spring (8) placed inside the column. Around the perimeter of this end piece are springy tongues (23) that are supported on the inside surface of the tubular body (3) and ensure that the end piece is centred until fixed in place by swaging.
