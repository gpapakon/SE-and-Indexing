<INVENTION-TITLE>
Molecular weight-increasing of polyamides
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08K53415	C08G6928	C08K500	C08G6948	C08K55333	C08G6900	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08K	C08G	C08K	C08G	C08K	C08G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
By heating above the melting point or glass transition temperature and adding a bismaleimide and a sterically hindered hydroxyphenylalkylphosphonic acid ester or monoester, an increase in the molecular weight of fresh polyamide (nylon) and polyamide recyclates can be achieved.
</ABSTRACT>

case of crystalline polyamide types, or glass transition point, in the case of amorphous 

polyamide types, of the polyamide.
