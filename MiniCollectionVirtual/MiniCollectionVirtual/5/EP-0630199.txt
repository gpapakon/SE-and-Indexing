<CLAIMS>
Device of an appararus with rotary supports for shelves 
characterized in that it comprises two vertical members 1 and 2 disposed one 

in front of the other, a rotary mechanism fixed on said rear vertical 
member 1; said front vertical member 2 serving as a bearing surface for the 

cantileverd shelves and whereby the rear surface of said shelves always lies in 
a common plane which is substantially parallel to said vertical member 2. 
Device according to claim 1 characterized in that it 
comprises two distinct and symetrical slideways 4 and 5, forming closed 

loops, disposed side by side, both on a common vertical plane. 
Devise according to claim 1 or claim 2, characterized in 
that it comprises supporting cast metal plates wich surface present a 

combination of ribs forming the arcuate part of said slideways 4 and 5; as 
well as a projection 6 positioned in the midst of of the four downstrokes of 

said two slideways. 
Device according to any of the preceding claims 
characterized in that rollers 7 are fixed on the surface of the carrying 

arms 12 of the supporting mechanism for shelves 14; said rollers 7 leaning 
against said vertical member 2 to keep said supporting mechanism for 

shelves 14 on their fixed rotary plane. 
</CLAIMS>
