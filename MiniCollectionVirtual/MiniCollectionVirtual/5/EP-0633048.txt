<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for conducting chemical reactions in distillation column reactors.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B01D300	B01D300	B01J2100	B01J2106	C07C2700	C07C2702	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B01D	B01D	B01J	B01J	C07C	C07C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B01D3	B01D3	B01J21	B01J21	C07C27	C07C27	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A process is claimed for carrying out chemical reactions in distillation column reactors on acid catalysts. The improvement is that the reaction is carried out on an inorganic catalyst in the form of packings which are arranged above the column bottom.
</ABSTRACT>
