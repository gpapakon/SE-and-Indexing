<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for sensing the underflow of a hydrocyclone
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B04C500	B04C514	B04C1100	B04C1100	G01P502	G01P502	G01P1300	G01P1300	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B04C	B04C	B04C	B04C	G01P	G01P	G01P	G01P	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B04C5	B04C5	B04C11	B04C11	G01P5	G01P5	G01P13	G01P13	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The arrangement has a measurement probe (5) in a housing (6) with a flexible membrane (9) on its side presented to the stream (3) from the lower outlet (2) of the hydro-cyclone (1) or closed by such a membrane. The membrane is deformed by the incident stream and the sensor inside the housing detects the deformation of the membrane.
</ABSTRACT>
