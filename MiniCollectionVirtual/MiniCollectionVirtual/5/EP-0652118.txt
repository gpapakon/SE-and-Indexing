<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a passenger car tyre of significantly reduced weight and with good comfort properties. The belt width is between 60 and 80% of the width of the tyre in the ground contact surface, the side wall thickness 
<
/= 2 mm and the density of the cords in the belt layers is selected to be between 30 and 70. The core profiles in the bead areas have a low Shore A hardness and a high rebound elasticity.
