<INVENTION-TITLE>
Three pick terry fabric, method and loom for its manufacture.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>D03D2700	D03D2708	D03D3900	D03D3922	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>D03D	D03D	D03D	D03D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>D03D27	D03D27	D03D39	D03D39	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
By the use of two misspicks (5, 6) during the pile change for three-pick pile fabrics, the same tie-off as in a pile change for four-pick pile fabric is achieved. A three-pick terry fabric thus has a fabric pattern which is identical to the fabric pattern of a four-pick terry fabric.
