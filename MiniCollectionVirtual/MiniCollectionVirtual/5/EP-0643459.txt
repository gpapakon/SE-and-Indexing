<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method of transferring optical modulation simultaneously over a multitude of wavelengths.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H01S500	H01S506	H01S540	H04B10152	H04B10155	H04J1402	H04J1402	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H01S	H01S	H01S	H04B	H04B	H04J	H04J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H01S5	H01S5	H01S5	H04B10	H04B10	H04J14	H04J14	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Use is made of a semiconductor laser which can oscillate freely over a plurality of longitudinal modes, this laser being capable of locking onto an incident optical beam. The modulation is transposed over the modes ( lambda 21,  lambda 22,  lambda 23,...) which are not at the wavelength ( lambda 1) of the incident beam. Application in optical telecommunications.
