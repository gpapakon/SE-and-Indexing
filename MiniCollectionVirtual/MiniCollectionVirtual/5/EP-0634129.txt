<INVENTION-TITLE>
Bath tub support.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47K316	A47K316	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47K	A47K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47K3	A47K3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A bath tub support made of moulded foam in the form of a block (10) having a bath tub recess adapted to the external form of the bath tub to be received and open at the top comprises a mat (16) of insulating materials arranged in the bath tub recess (12), it being possible additionally to introduce plastics foam (22) between bath tub recess (12) and mat (16).
