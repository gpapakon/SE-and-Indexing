<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for connecting a metal fitting to a plastic pipe.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F16L3700	F16L37248	F16L4700	F16L4724	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F16L	F16L	F16L	F16L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F16L37	F16L37	F16L47	F16L47	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
This connection apparatus comprises:   - first fastening means (10) secured to the metal pipeline accessory (1); - a linking end-piece (3) made of plastic including a bell socket (12) designed to fit on to the plane end (8); - means (4, 14) providing sealing between the bell socket and the plane end; and - a sleeve (5) designed to cover the bell socket, this sleeve comprising on the one hand an internal collar (15) for retaining the end-piece (3), interacting with the end shoulder (13) of the bell socket and, on the other hand, second fastening means (16) designed to interact with the first fastening means.   Application to the insertion of a cast iron valve body into a pipeline made of high density polyethylene.
