<INVENTION-TITLE>
Floor lamp with integrated projection screen.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F21V3300	F21V3300	G03B2156	G03B2158	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F21V	F21V	G03B	G03B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F21V33	F21V33	G03B21	G03B21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A floor lamp is described with a stand (2, 4) and at least one luminaire (5) mounted on the stand, there being mounted on the stand (2) a projection screen drum (4) which is intended to accommodate a projection screen (43) for projecting (showing) films, slides (transparencies) and the like. The projection screen drum (4) forms an integral part of the stand (2).
