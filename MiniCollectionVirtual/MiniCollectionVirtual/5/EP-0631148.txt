<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for contactless testing of soldering.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01R3102	G01R3106	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01R	G01R	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01R31	G01R31	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for contactless testing of soldered junctions of coils (SP), especially for quality analysis of coil-wire ends automatically wound and soldered onto coil terminal pins. A colour-image camera (FBK) and a monochrome-image camera (SWK) scan the coil terminal pins in the region of an annular light (RL) surrounding the latter, with regard to colour and contour of the soldered junction. A method of this type is used for optical testing of soldered junctions.
