<INVENTION-TITLE>
Element for rubber spacing railway wheels and rubber spring railway wheel.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60B900	B60B916	B60B1700	B60B1700	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60B	B60B	B60B	B60B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60B9	B60B9	B60B17	B60B17	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A rubber element 1 for rubber-elastic railway wheels, which in the non-tensioned state has an oval opening 2 for receiving a flexible current bridge 6. When the rubber element 1 is installed in the rail wheel this opening assumes an approximately circular cross-section under the pretensioning.
