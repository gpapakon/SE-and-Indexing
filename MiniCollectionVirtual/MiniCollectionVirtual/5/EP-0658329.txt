<INVENTION-TITLE>
Wall, window or door decoration.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47H2300	A47H2300	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47H	A47H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47H23	A47H23	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A dressing (decoration) for a wall, window or door comprises a curtain (10) woven at least in part with elastic fibres such as 5 to 15 per cent of fibres marketed under the brand name Lycra or Latex. Two opposite edges of the curtain (10) each pass in the form of a wide loop around two rods (20, 22) situated facing each other, and between which this curtain (10) is held in tension. The curtain (10) may also be formed of a single loop passing, under tension, around the two rods (20, 22).
