<INVENTION-TITLE>
Assembly with a waterproof seal and a speed sensor.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01P342	G01P344	G01P3487	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01P	G01P	G01P	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01P3	G01P3	G01P3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device for detecting the speed of a shaft.   The device comprises a seal 16 and a magneto-sensitive sensor 18 is also connected to a support 14 for detecting the polarities of a magnet carried by a rotating shaft 22; the sensor 18 produces an output signal in a conductor 20, the voltage of which signal corresponds to or is proportional to the rotational speed of the shaft; depending on the type of sensor used, the voltage of the voltage signal has a periodically varying amplitude (for example: digital or sinusoidal signal) or a given amplitude, for which the signal cycles from zero to the given amplitude and back to zero, or alternatively the amplitude of the voltage of the output signal, of sinusoidal shape, increases as the speed increases, or alternatively the width of the pulses decreases as a function of the increase in speed. Application to detecting the speed of vehicle wheel shafts (axles).
