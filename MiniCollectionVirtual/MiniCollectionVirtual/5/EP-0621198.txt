<INVENTION-TITLE>
CLOSING DEVICE FOR A CONTAINER WITH A NECK OF ANY SHAPE.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65D	B65D	B65D	B65D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device for closing a container (1) or pot which has a neck (2), of the sort comprising a stopper (3) mounted on the external periphery of the said neck (2) and a member (8) for locking the said stopper (3). According to the invention, the locking member (3) is mounted so that it can pivot about the external periphery of the said stopper (3) and has locking catches (10) each interacting with an elastically deformable zone (6) of the internal lateral wall of the stopper (3). Application to containers for cosmetic products.
