<INVENTION-TITLE>
Pipe- or socket wrench with holding means for the manipulating building part.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B25B1300	B25B1302	B25B1306	B25B2302	B25B2310	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B25B	B25B	B25B	B25B	B25B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B25B13	B25B13	B25B13	B25B23	B25B23	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Pipe or socket wrench (1) or the like, with means for holding the component to be manipulated, wherein, in correspondence with the polygonal recess of the tool for receiving a screw head (7) or a threaded nut, the tool body has a groove (8) on its outside and a through-hole (10) extends from the base of this groove as far as the polygonal recess of the tool, and the circumferential groove receives a resilient means (9) having a radially bent-off tongue (11) which penetrates through the through-hole (10), and the end (12) of the bent-off tongue reaches into the inner space of the polygonal recess of the tool.
