<ABSTRACT>
Installation for coating by spraying powder, having a booth (2), having a powder recovery device (4), which has a filter belt (10) which forms a floor of the booth and passes on powder falling onto it to a take-over device (14), and having a suction removal device (6), which sucks air from the booth through the filter belt by means of a suction fan (52) and sends it through an exhaust-air filter (50) into the ambient air outside the booth. The exhaust-air filter (50) is a filter which can be cleaned without disassembly and is provided with a device (56) by which the filter is automatically cleaned.
