<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to sprayable, solvent-free compositions based on bitumen, polymers and/or wax, containing fillers whose particle size is not more than 200  mu m and which contain, as fillers, aluminium or zinc powders and graphite or glimmer powders or graphite and glimmer powders together, if desired, with other fillers.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
At temperatures under 140°C sprayable, solvent-free masses having, as a base, bitumen,
polymers and/or wax, characterised in that they contain

fillers, the particle size of which is not greater than 200 µm,
and in that they contain, as fillers, aluminium powder or zinc

powder and also graphite powder or mica powder or
graphite powder and mica powder and, optionally, other

fillers.
Sprayable masses according to claim 1, characterised in
that they contain, as fillers, aluminium powder or zinc

powder, graphite and chalk and, optionally, other fillers.
Sprayable masses according to claim 1, characterised in
that they contain, as fillers, aluminium powder or zinc

powder and mica and, optionally, other fillers.
Sprayable masses according to any one of claims 1 to 3,

characterised in that they contain 1 to 8 wt.% aluminium
powder or zinc powder and 10 to 50 wt.% graphite and,

optionally, other fillers.
Sprayable masses according to any one of claims 1 to 4,

characterised by the following composition:

Bitumen
30 to 50 wt.%
Polymer(s)
2 to 22 wt.%
Wax(es)
0 to 10 wt.%
Aluminium powder or zinc powder
1 to 8 wt.%
Graphite
0 to 50 wt.%
Mica
0 to 25 wt.%
Other fillers
0 to 50 wt.%
The use of the masses according to claims 1 to 5 as a
soundproofing coating for vehicles and household

appliances.
</CLAIMS>
