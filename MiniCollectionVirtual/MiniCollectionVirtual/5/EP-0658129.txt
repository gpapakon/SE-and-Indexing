<INVENTION-TITLE>
PROCESS FOR PRODUCING GRANULATES FROM MOLTEN PRODUCTS.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
When granulates are produced from molten materials, vapours result which must be removed from the outgoing air. The single pieces produced, in particular tablets, are directly used to form a fixed bed bulk body (8) placed into the flow of outgoing air for crystallising the sulphur content of the vapours. The invention is suitable for producing sulphur tablets.
</ABSTRACT>
