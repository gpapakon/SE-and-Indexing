<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for connecting and joining electric conductors.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H01R1353	H01R1353	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H01R	H01R	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H01R13	H01R13	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The device is used for connecting and joining electrical cables having a connection (GAT) of an electrical system. In addition to the connection of a cable (LTG1), which is intended to transport power, by means of a first plug connector (CONN1a), the device makes it possible to extend the connection options which are required, for example, for instrument transformers or overvoltage suppressors, by means of a second plug connector (CONN1b) which is connected to the plug connector (CONN1a) via a connecting bolt (VB).
