<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for inert gas shielded arc welding.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B23K9173	B23K9173	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B23K	B23K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B23K9	B23K9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device for gas-shielded arc welding, with a gas supply, a welding-wire feed device (17) and a current source (18), and recommends that a sensor (9) be arranged in the gas supply and that the welding-wire feed device (17) and/or the current source (18) by switched off by the sensor if the pressure and/or the flow of the gas falls below a threshold value.
