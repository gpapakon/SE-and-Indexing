<INVENTION-TITLE>
Container provided with a one-piece catch.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A45C1310	A45C1310	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A45C	A45C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A45C13	A45C13	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Container, made up of two parts which are hinged together, comprising a one-piece catch comprising a body provided with retention means, elastic return means, actuating means which are accessible from the outside and locking means. The body (7) of the catch (1) is made so that it can be inserted into the first part of the container (3) by sliding it through an opening (4) and, once it has been slid into the opening, the retention means (15) engage by snap-fastening with stop means (19) provided on the first part of the container (3) so as to enable the locking means (12) to engage with fastening means on the second part of the container (6). Application: in particular on make-up containers.
