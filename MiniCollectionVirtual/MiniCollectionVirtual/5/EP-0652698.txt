Device for transporting printed circuit boards.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device for transporting printed circuit boards (7), such as printed equipment cards or substrates, while they are being fitted with components, on a transportation path guide between and/or in the various processing stations, the circuit boards being moved into a defined position, having   - a transportation chain (3) on which holding devices (3d) for individual circuit boards (7) are arranged spaced apart by a plurality of chain links (3c), - having transportation strips (7d), which are provided on the circuit boards (7), are inactive in circuitary terms and can be cut off, of the plate material, which can be gripped by the holding devices (3d) for transportation using the moving transportation chain (3).
