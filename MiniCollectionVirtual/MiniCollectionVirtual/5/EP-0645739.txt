<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for franking objects and automated franking system for this method.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G07B1700	G07B1700	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G07B	G07B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G07B17	G07B17	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
According to the method, a batch (10) of objects is placed on the scales (1), all of them capable of passing into the franking machine (2), and a manipulator (3) is used to transport the objects to the franking machine, the weight of each object being calculated by subtractive weighing. As well as the scales (1), the franking machine (2) and the manipulator (3), the system includes a checking means (4).
