<INVENTION-TITLE>
A material for acoustic protection, notably of sonar antennas.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G10K1100	G10K1116	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G10K	G10K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G10K11	G10K11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an acoustic protection material consisting of a matrix made of viscoelastic material containing gas inclusions and heavy inclusions of a material whose density is greater than 10. The gas inclusions may be air inclusions and the heavy inclusions lead inclusions. Application in particular to sonar antennas.
 
</ABSTRACT>
