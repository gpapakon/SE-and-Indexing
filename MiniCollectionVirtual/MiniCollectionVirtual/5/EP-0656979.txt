PANEL ELEMENT.

<ABSTRACT>
The invention concerns a panel element (4) made, in particular, of steel and consisting of two parallel sheets (1, 2) firmly joined to each other by interposed web plates (3), each web plate (3) being in the form of a strip whose longitudinal edges are butt-welded, in particular laser butt-welded, to the inside surfaces of the sheets (1, 2), each weld seam extending through the sheet (1, 2) and into the abutting longitudinal edge of the web plate (3). The sheets (1, 2) used are cut rectangular and the web plates (3) are welded parallel to the longer side of the rectangle.
</ABSTRACT>
