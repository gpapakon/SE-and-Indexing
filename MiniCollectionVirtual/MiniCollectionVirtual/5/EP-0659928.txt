<INVENTION-TITLE>
Powdery mixtures and their use for sizing stable fiber yarns.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>D06M1100	D06M1150	D06M1182	D06M1501	D06M1511	D06M1521	D06M15263	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>D06M	D06M	D06M	D06M	D06M	D06M	D06M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>D06M11	D06M11	D06M11	D06M15	D06M15	D06M15	D06M15	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Pulverulent mixtures of     a) 5 to 94.5% by weight of native starch, b) 5 to 94.5% by weight of a water-soluble polyacrylate size, of which up to 80% by weight may be replaced with other sizes, c) 0.5 to 20% by weight of alkali metal perborate and/or alkali metal percarbonate, and optionally   d) up to 10% by weight of customary additives, and use of the mixtures in the form of aqueous solutions for sizing staple fibre yarns.
</ABSTRACT>
