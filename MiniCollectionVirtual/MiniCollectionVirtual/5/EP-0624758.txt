Steam oven with a system for preventing water droplets

<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to the area of steam ovens and its aim is to equip these ovens with a system which is capable of preventing water droplets which are present on the internal walls of the oven during functioning. To do this, a steam oven according to the invention is equipped with a system (4, 5) which carries out heating of the internal walls of the oven during functioning so as to bring them to such a temperature that the water droplets are eliminated.

<CLAIMS>
Steam cooking oven, characterized in that it 
includes a device (4, 5) for heating the internal walls 

of the oven while the latter is operating so as to bring 
the walls to a temperature such that the water droplets 

generated by the steam on these walls are prevented. 
Steam cooking oven according to Claim 1, characterized 
in that the device consists of a heating electrical 

wire (4) placed on the external part of the walls and 
of means (5) for supplying voltage to the said electrical 

wire. 
Steam cooking oven according to any one of the 
preceding claims, characterized in that the said temperature 

is substantially greater than or equal to 110Â° 
Celsius. 
Steam cooking oven according to any one of the 
preceding claims, characterized in that the device is 

automatically triggered as soon as the oven operates. 
Cooking oven according to any one of Claims 1 
to 3, characterized in that the device is automatically 

triggered after a predetermined cooking time.
