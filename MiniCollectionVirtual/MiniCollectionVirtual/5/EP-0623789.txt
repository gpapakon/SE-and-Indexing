<INVENTION-TITLE>
Ducting system for a room air-conditioner or the like.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F24F1200	F24F1200	F24F1302	F24F1302	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F24F	F24F	F24F	F24F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F24F12	F24F12	F24F13	F24F13	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A first and a second pipe (12, 14) for the transport of the air-conditioning blowing gas and of the air-conditioning recovery gas are accommodated side by side in an external duct (16), with which they delimit a free volume (20). The first and second pipes (12, 14) are protected and thermally insulated thereby and their installation is simplified thereby.
