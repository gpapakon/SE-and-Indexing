Punch set for a tabletting machine

<ABSTRACT>
Punch set (10a, 10b) for a preforming press, in the case of which the identically shaped punches have a head (12a, 12b) which interacts with at least one pressing surface; a shank (16a, 16b) and a pressing section (18a, 18b) on the end opposite the head (12a, 12b), at least one punch (10a, 10b) having a recess on the shaft (16a, 16b), which recess accommodates a data carrier (22a, 22b).

<CLAIMS>
A punch set for a tabletting machine wherein the punches (10) 
which are of the same shape comprise a head (12) cooperating with at least one 

punch surface, a shank (16), and a punch portion (18) at the end remote from the 
head, characterized in that at least one punch (10, 10a, 10b, 10c) includes at the 

shank (16a, 16b) a recess (26) for receiving a data carrier (22a, 22b). 
A set according to claim 1, characterized in that the recess (26) 
is adjacent to the head (12a, 12b). 
A set as defined in claim 1 or claim 2, characterized in that the 
data carrier (22a, 22b, 22c) is an IC member. 
A reading apparatus for a coded punch according to any of 
claims 1 to 3, characterized in that it includes an opening ((32) for the punch (10c), 

in which opening the punch (10c) may be positioned in proper orientation with 
respect to a reading head (34). 
A reading apparatus according to claim 4, characterized in that 
the reading head (34) is biased by a spring (42) and is mounted so as to be movable 

against the data carrier (22c). 
A reading apparatus according to claim 4 or claim 5, characterized 
in that it is mounted to the tabletting machine or outside thereof to a suitable 

support. 
</CLAIMS>
