<INVENTION-TITLE>
Exhaust device for internal combustion engines.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F01N320	F01N320	F01N322	F01N322	F01N1100	F01N1100	F16K1736	F16K1738	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F01N3	F01N3	F01N3	F01N3	F01N11	F01N11	F16K17	F16K17	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In order to obtain a safety device (4), of simple construction and triggering a signal, for an exhaust system of internal combustion engines with an exhaust emission control device (3) arranged in one section of the exhaust pipe (2), it is proposed that the exhaust pipe (2), upstream of the exhaust emission control device (3), have a wall opening (5) with a cover (6), discardable as a function of the temperature, as safety device (4). The cover (6) is held non-positively in the wall opening (5) or in a threaded ring (17) by means of a material melting between approx. 820 DEG C and 910 DEG C.
