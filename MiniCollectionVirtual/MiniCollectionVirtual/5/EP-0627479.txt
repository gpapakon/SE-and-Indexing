<INVENTION-TITLE>
Cleaning agent.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C09G100	C09G104	C09K332	C09K332	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C09G	C09G	C09K	C09K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
A sweeping assistant is proposed for the removal of various kinds of contamination, especially that caused by mineral oils, from solid surfaces. The principal constituents of the sweeping assistant are comminuted, crosslinked and hydrogel-forming polymers, so-called superabsorbers, surface-active substances and/or solvents and substances having a large surface structure and/or a capillary and/or fibrous composition. These constituents are mixed homogeneously to give a moist, flowable composition which can be scattered and swept. The production of different sweeping-assistant compositions, and the use of the sweeping assistant, are demonstrated by way of examples.
</ABSTRACT>
