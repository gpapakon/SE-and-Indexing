<INVENTION-TITLE>
Aromatized roasted and ground coffee and process of preparing aromatized roasted coffee.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A23F500	A23F512	A23F514	A23F546	A23F546	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A23F	A23F	A23F	A23F	A23F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A23F5	A23F5	A23F5	A23F5	A23F5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In order to provide an aromatised roasted coffee product whose aroma can be maintained for a relatively long period even without vacuum packing and to provide a process for producing the roasted coffee product, the individual aromatised roasted coffee particles are provided with an aroma-protecting coat of a coffee extract made from roasted coffee, forming roasted coffee granules. The roasted coffee product according to the invention is produced by coating roasted coffee containing added flavouring with a liquid coffee extract made from roasted coffee in an aroma-protecting manner.
</ABSTRACT>
