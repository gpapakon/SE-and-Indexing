<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a process and an apparatus for manufacturing a powder mixture of oxide ceramic, which is to be used, in particular, for manufacturing of pot-shaped bodies. For this purpose, starting materials are milled and compacted, the compacted materials are broken up and subsequently calcined in a rotary tube furnace (10).
