Process and catalyst for purifying carbon dioxide.

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a catalyst and a process for purifying carbon dioxide as it is obtained in the purification stage of synthesis gas production from heavy petroleum residues, for use thereof in the food industry. The catalyst of the invention comprises  alpha -aluminium oxide having a specific surface area 
<
 50 m
<
2
>
/g and containing platinum in an amount of 
<
 0.2% by mass and optionally further metal compounds of subgroup 8 or copper in an amount of 
<
 0.1% by mass, in a layer thickness of the catalyst of 100  mu m enriched peripherally to 
>
 0.5% by mass. The process for purifying carbon dioxide is carried out at temperatures between 450 and 540@C and space velocities of 5000 v/vh over the catalyst of the invention.
