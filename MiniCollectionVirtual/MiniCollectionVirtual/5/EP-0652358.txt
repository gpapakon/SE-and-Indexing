<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for examination of the functioning capacity of lambda probes.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for testing the dynamic functioning capacity of lambda probes by measuring the dwell times in which the lambda probe signal indicates a rich or lean mixture and comparing the said times with stored limits varying as a function of the operating point.
