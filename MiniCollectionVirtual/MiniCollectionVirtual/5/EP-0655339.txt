<INVENTION-TITLE>
Thermal transfer recording apparatus.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Apparatus for recording prints made by a thermal transfer printer, comprising a transfer film (6) coming from a roll (7) and picked up by a drum (8), the roll (7) and the drum (8) being arranged within a cassette (9), a sidewall (10A) of which comprises an offset (11) defining a printing zone, in which apparatus the cassette (9) comprises a reading window (12) different from the printing zone (11), guides (13, 14, 15, 16, 17, 18) intended for imparting to the transfer film (6) a route such that the transfer film (6) passes between the roll (7) and the drum (8), and a support (20) for a contrast surface.
