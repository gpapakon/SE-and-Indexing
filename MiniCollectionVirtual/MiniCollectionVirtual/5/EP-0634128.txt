<INVENTION-TITLE>
Insertion aids for filter holder of espresso coffee machine
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47J3134	A47J3124	A47J3106	A47J3144	A47J3106	A47J3144	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47J	A47J	A47J	A47J	A47J	A47J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47J31	A47J31	A47J31	A47J31	A47J31	A47J31	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
There is described an espresso coffee machine (10) having a brewing head (1) which is located on the underside thereof and into which a filter holder (3) having a handle (5) and a filter (9) can be inserted from below, it being possible to lock and unlock again the filter holder (3) on the brewing head (1) by a pivoting movement of the filter holder (3) about the axis of the brewing head (1) (projections 7, counter-slot 8), characterised in that there is rotatably fixed on the brewing head (1) a positioning ring (2) which has a cutout (6), open towards its underside, for the handle (5) of the filter holder (3), and in that there is provided an indicating means (4) for the starting position of the positioning ring (2), in which the filter holder (3) can be inserted into the brewing head (1). As a result, a constructionally simple and easy-to-use aid for insertion of the filter holder into the brewing head of the espresso coffee machine is provided.

<CLAIMS>
An espresso machine (10) having on its underside a group head 
(1) into which a filter holder (3) having a handle (5) and a 

filter (9) can be inserted from below, the filter holder (3) 

being lockable to and unlockable from the group head (1) by a 
pivoting movement of the filter holder (3) around the axis of the 

group head (1), characterized in that rotatably attached to the 
group head (1) is a positioning ring (2) which is formed for the 

handle (5) of the filter holder (3) with a cutout (6) open in the 
direction of the ring underside, an indicator (4) being provided 

for the starting position of the positioning ring (2), in which 
the filter holder (3) can be inserted into the group head (1). 
An espresso machine according to claim 1, characterized in 
that the indicator (4) takes the form of a catch. 
An espresso machine according to claim 1, characterized in 
that the indicator (4) has a return spring which tends to rotate 

the filter holder (3) towards a stop marking the starting 
position. 
An espresso machine according to claim 1, characterized in 
that the indicator takes the form of a marking (6, 11).  

 
An espresso machine according to claim 4, characterized in 
that in the starting position the marking (11) on the group head 

(1) or the espresso machine (10) is situated opposite the 
cutout (6). 
</CLAIMS>
