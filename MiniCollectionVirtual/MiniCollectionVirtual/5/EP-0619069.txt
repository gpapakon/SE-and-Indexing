<INVENTION-TITLE>
Guiding system for Amphibia, method for its installation and walkway plate for a guiding system for Amphibia.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A01K300	A01K300	A01K2900	A01K2900	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A01K	A01K	A01K	A01K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A01K3	A01K3	A01K29	A01K29	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a guiding system for Amphibia having a passage (18) for crossing under a road, path or the like, at least one walkway plate (24) being provided which at its edges (38) lies essentially tightly against inside walls of the passage (18) transversely to the passage direction. Underneath the walkway plate (24) a flow channel (20) for carrying off water is provided. Above the walkway plate a sufficient height must be provided for the Amphibia to be able to pass through the passage.
