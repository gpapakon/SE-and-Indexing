<ABSTRACT>
Diaphragm (7) for a pump, especially a twin-diaphragm pump for delivering highly pure products, having circumferentially disposed, radially offset annular beads (9, 10), one of which is fashioned as a grooved bead (10), and a clamping ring (11) inserted in the groove, in which diaphragm the groove of the grooved bead communicates with a pressure medium.
