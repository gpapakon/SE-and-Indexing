<INVENTION-TITLE>
Sleeve for accomodating locations of branching or jointing of fibre optic or electrical cables.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G02B624	G02B624	G02B644	G02B644	H02G330	H02G330	H02G1500	H02G15013	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G02B	G02B	G02B	G02B	H02G	H02G	H02G	H02G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G02B6	G02B6	G02B6	G02B6	H02G3	H02G3	H02G15	H02G15	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A sleeve is described for accommodating junction points or connection points of optical or electrical cables, which sleeve consists of an essentially cylindrical robust hollow body (8) which accommodates the junction points or connecting points and has at least one open end, in the case of which a sleeve head (1) is in each case inserted into the open end or ends, and the sleeve head or heads (1) have passage openings (2) in the form of radial slots (2) for the entering or emerging cables (3, 4). Sealing bodies (11) are provided in the slots (2), which sealing bodies (11) can be unfolded, surround the entering or emerging cables (3, 4) and are inserted into the slots (2) to produce a seal.
