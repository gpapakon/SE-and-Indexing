COLLAPSIBLE REFRIGERATED CABINETS

<ABSTRACT>
Cabinet for displaying food products or similar in a shop. A refrigerated cabinet for displaying products comprises at least two shelves (1) lying over one another, at least one casing and means for guiding and circulating fresh air to the food display levels. The casing is made from a thin, light, cardboard-based material, and is removably mounted on a base (8) comprising means for guiding and circulating fresh air. The technical field of the invention is the manufacture of refrigerated cabinets.

<CLAIMS>
A refrigerated cabinet for displaying products on a plurality of
planes (1) or shelves for displaying said products, the cabinet including

means for guiding and circulating cool air and means for distributing cool air
towards said planes, with said cabinet comprising a cover which is

removably mounted on a base (8) that includes a portion of said means for
guiding and circulating cool air, the cabinet being characterized in that it

includes at least one cover panel made of a cardboard-based material, and
in which said cardboard-based panel(s) is(are) lightweight, of a density that

is less than or equal to 400 kilograms per cubic meter and is(are) thin, has
(have) a thickness that is less than or equal to 30 millimeters, and includes

at least two substantially superposed planes (1).
The cabinet according to claim 1 which includes a zone for
granting access to said products via an opening protected by a curtain of

air.
The cabinet according to any one of claims 1 or 2 which includes
an internal panel (7) for distributing cool air which is made from a

cardboard-based material.
The cabinet according to any one of claims 1 to 3, in which said
display planes or shelves are made from a cardboard-based material.
The cabinet according to any one of claims 1 to 4 in which said
cardboard-based panel(s) mainly consist(s) of two or three layers of

corrugated cardboard.
The cabinet according to any one of claims 1 to 5 which
includes :


said base (8) which includes at least one fan (29) for circulating cool
air, and
a superstructure that is removably mounted on said base, with said
superstructure comprising :


said display planes or shelves for said products, and
means for distributing cool air towards said planes, and
said cover which mainly consists of panels made from a
cardboard-based material.
The cabinet according to claim 6 in which said superstructure
mainly consists of cardboard-based panels. 
The cabinet according to any one of claims 1 to 7 which is
generally cylindrical, pyramidal or parallelepipedic in shape.
The cabinet according to any one of claims 1 to 8 in which said
base includes at least one finned heat exchanger (28) for cooling air

circulating in said cabinet, which heat exchanger is constituted by an

evaporator or else by a cooling battery through which a refrigerating fluid
circulates that may be delivered to said cabinet by a refrigerator unit

external to said cabinet, and said base includes at least one fan (29) for
circulating said air cooled by said heat exchanger (28).
The cabinet according to any one of claims 1 to 9 in which at
least one of said cover panels is provided with an opening (40) suitable for

coinciding with an opening (41) formed in a side wall (42) of said base,
which openings (40, 41) allow a fluid to pass.
The cabinet according to any one of claims 1 to 10 which
includes legs (40) or longitudinal beams (19) delimiting at least one empty

space (14) enabling the forks (20) of a handling device to be engaged
beneath said base.
The cabinet according to any one of claims 1 to 11 which
includes air sealing means (35) between said base and said cover.
The cabinet according to any one of claims 1 to 12 in which said
cardboard-based panel(s) for said cover include(s) a printed or silk-screened

outer covering.
The cabinet according to any one of claims 1 to 13 in which said
base includes a top portion (15) which includes bones (12) that are

substantially rounded, and said base includes support means (13) for at
least one portion of the lower edge (21) of said cover.
</CLAIMS>
