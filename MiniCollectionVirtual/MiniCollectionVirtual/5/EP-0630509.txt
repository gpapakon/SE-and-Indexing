<ABSTRACT>
A till has a quadrilateral housing (10) with an open front wall into which can be slid a drawer (20) with a lower part (22) and an insert (24, 26) subdivided into compartments. The insert has a first removable section (34) next to the front wall (32) of the drawer (20) and a second rear section (26) movable on the lower part (22) of the drawer in a direction parallel to the sliding direction of the drawer (20) between a first position, in which it covers the bottom the lower part (22) of the drawer, and a second position, in which it gives free access to the rear bottom zone of the lower part (22) of the drawer.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
Till, comprising a quadrilateral housing (10) 
which is open at an end wall and a drawer (20), which is 

displaceable in said housing, having a drawer bottom part 
(22) and a compartmental insert (24, 26), the height of 

which is less than the height of the drawer (20) as a 
whole, the compartmental insert comprising an insert 

section (26) which is displaceable on the drawer bottom 
part (22), parallel to the sliding direction of the 

drawer (20), between a first setting covering the base 
(30) of the drawer bottom part (22) and a second setting 

in which it affords access to the base region of the 
drawer bottom part (22), characterized in that the 

compartmental insert comprises a removable, first insert 
section (24), which is close to the front wall (32) of 

the drawer (20), and in that the displaceable insert 
section forms a second, rear insert section (26) which, 

in its displacement settings, covers or alternatively 
affords access to the rear base region of the drawer 

bottom part (22). 
Till according to Claim 1, characterized in that 
on the housing (10) or on the second insert section (26) 

there is provided a resiliently deflectable stop (68), 
which interacts with the other, in each case, of the two 

parts (26, 10) or with a dog disposed thereon, with the 
intention that the second insert section (26) shall be 

displaced into its second setting when the drawer (20) is 
pulled out. 
Till according to Claim 1 or 2, characterized in 
that the drawer bottom part (22) is mounted onto the 

pull-out rail parts (18) of telescopic rail guides (14),  
 

the fixed rail parts (16) of which are fastened to the 
housing (10). 
Till according to one of Claims 1 to 3, characterized 
in that the second insert section (26) has on its 

side walls guide claws (62), which respectively enclasp 
a guide rail (64), parallel to the sliding direction, on 

the drawer bottom part (22). 
Till according to one of Claims 1 to 4, characterized 
in that the drawer bottom part (22) is divided by 

partitions (52) into a plurality of base compartments. 
Till according to one of Claims 1 to 5, characterized 
in that there is disposed between the two insert 

sections (24, 26) at least one compartment (46) extending 
over the entire height of the drawer (20). 
Till according to one of Claims 1 to 6, characterized 
in that in the front wall (32) of the drawer (20) 

there is configured a feed slot (54), leading to a base 
compartment of the drawer bottom part (22), for checks 

and the like. 
Till according to one of Claims 1 to 7, characterized 
in that the insert sections (24, 26) extend over 

the entire width of the drawer (20). 
Till according to one of Claims 1 to 8, characterized 
in that the second insert section (26) has a 

plurality of compartments, which are separated by walls 
(58) parallel to the sliding direction and are provided, 

in a posterior part-region adjoining the rear wall of the 
second insert section (26), with a cover (60). 
</CLAIMS>
