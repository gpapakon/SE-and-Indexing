<INVENTION-TITLE>
Plate heat exchanger.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F28F308	F28F308	F28F2100	F28F2102	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F28F	F28F	F28F	F28F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F28F3	F28F3	F28F21	F28F21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
This plate heat exchanger with parallel and countercurrent flow of the heat-exchange fluids consists of a stack of a defined number of ribbed plates (4) of the same size, clamped against one another between two flanges (1, 2), the said plates having openings (5, 6, 7, 8) in their corners, defining, within the stack, ducts for respectively supplying and discharging the heat-exchange fluids. The plates are made of machined bulk graphite, previously impregnated with a waterproofing material, and especially a resin.
