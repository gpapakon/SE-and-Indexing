<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device and procedure for delivering flat products.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65H114	B65H114	B65H308	B65H308	G07B300	G07B304	G07F1104	G07F1114	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65H	B65H	B65H	B65H	G07B	G07B	G07F	G07F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65H1	B65H1	B65H3	B65H3	G07B3	G07B3	G07F11	G07F11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Automatic dispensing device and procedure for delivering flat products individually, including a reserve adapted for receiving the said products in the form of a vertical stack, the reserve having a bottom (31) which can be displaced vertically between a fixed low rest position and an instantaneous variable height position, in which the product which is placed at the top of the stack comes into contact with a gripping member (8) which can be displaced horizontally between a standby position above the reserve and a delivering position outside the said reserve, the gripping member (8) already being activated when it comes into contact with the product placed at the top of the stack and deactivated when it arrives in line with its delivering position so as to drop the product outside the reserve.
