<INVENTION-TITLE>
Shredder for data carriers and cicuit arrangement for the same
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B02C1800	B02C1800	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B02C	B02C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B02C18	B02C18	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The device has a cutting mechanism driven by an electric drive, an input chute for material to be shredded, a signal transmitter near the chute and a receiver in the radiation range of the transmitter. An evaluation unit switches the drive on if the signal path between the transmitter and receiver is interrupted. A signal transmitter drive circuit causes the transmitter to emit pulsed signals. Independent claims are also included for the following: a circuit with a light barrier.
</ABSTRACT>
