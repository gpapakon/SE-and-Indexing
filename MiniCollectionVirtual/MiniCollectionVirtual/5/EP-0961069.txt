<INVENTION-TITLE>
Pipe clamp
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F16L310	F16L310	F16L312	F16L312	F16L5502	F16L55035	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F16L	F16L	F16L	F16L	F16L	F16L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F16L3	F16L3	F16L3	F16L3	F16L55	F16L55	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The circlip, to secure a hose to a connection, has a web section to link the legs of the laying unit (4) to support the head (13) of the clamping bolt, near the entry opening of the slit where the clamping bolt head (13) to pass through and place the shaft (12) of the clamping bolt (11) between the legs of the laying surface (4). The web section has a U-shape, as an extension of the laying surface (4) legs.
</ABSTRACT>
