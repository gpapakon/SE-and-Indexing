<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for making hydrophilic membranes.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61M116	A61M118	B01D6700	B01D6700	C08J518	C08J518	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61M	A61M	B01D	B01D	C08J	C08J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61M1	A61M1	B01D67	B01D67	C08J5	C08J5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A process for producing hydrophilic membranes using at least one hydrophobic polymer and polyvinylpyrrolidone as a hydrophilic polymer, membrane-forming shaping of the polymers and immobilisation of the polyvinylpyrrolidone is characterised in that the polyvinylpyrrolidone is immobilised on and/or in the membrane by hot treatment with an aqueous peroxodisulphate solution. The membranes are used for haemodialysis, haemodiafiltration or haemofiltration of blood.
</ABSTRACT>
