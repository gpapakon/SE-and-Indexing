<INVENTION-TITLE>
MOVING DEVICE, IN PARTICULAR FOR BABY CARRIAGES.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47D900	A47D904	B62B900	B62B922	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47D	A47D	B62B	B62B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47D9	A47D9	B62B9	B62B9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In a moving device, in particular for baby carriages, a continuous closed frame (1) has two rotary mounted pairs of adjacent rollers (2, 3) with parallel spindles as supporting members for the wheels (6) of the baby carriage (7). In order to imitate rolling and rocking motions, at least one roller  (2', 3') of each pair of rollers (2, 3) has irregularities (4, 5) arranged on and/or in its circumferential surface and a motor (11) mounted on the frame (1) drives at least one roller (2', 3') of each pair of rollers (2, 3).
</ABSTRACT>
