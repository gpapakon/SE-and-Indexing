<INVENTION-TITLE>
Welding element for films.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B29C6500	B29C6500	B29C6518	B29C6522	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a welding element (3) for film-welding machines. The welding element has an elongate electric heating conductor (7) and a carrier (6) supporting the latter. In this arrangement, the heating conductor (7) is firmly connected to the carrier (6) by its full surface area over its entire length. The heating conductor can be fastened together with the carrier easily exchangeably on welding jaws (1).
</ABSTRACT>
