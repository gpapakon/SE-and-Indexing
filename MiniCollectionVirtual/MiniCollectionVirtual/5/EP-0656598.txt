<INVENTION-TITLE>
Contact frame and connector for card.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G06K700	G06K700	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G06K	G06K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G06K7	G06K7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a contact frame for an electronic card, including an insulating body in which are arranged a first and a second row of electrical contact elements exhibiting a face for contact with the card and defining pairs of contact elements situated opposite one another as well as a first and a second detection contact blade, characterised in that the first detection blade (80) exhibits a bearing region for the card cooperating mechanically with the card in such a way that, when the card is set in place relative to the contact frame, the first (80) and the second (90) contact blades come into electrical contact, and in that it includes an electrically insulating region (110) arranged on an upper face of the first blade, at least in the said bearing region.
