<INVENTION-TITLE>
Hand-operated toy.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A63B6710	A63B6710	A63H100	A63H130	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A63B	A63B	A63H	A63H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A63B67	A63B67	A63H1	A63H1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Toy including a main body (1) of revolution consisting of two lateral parts (4, 5) on either side of an annular groove (7), a ball (2) being capable of interacting, by means of nesting, with at least one of the two lateral parts (4, 5), a flexible linking thread (3) connecting the ball and the groove (7), the toy being capable of being used in the manner of a cup-and-ball game as well as in the manner of a yoyo.
