<INVENTION-TITLE>
System fop radio or television broadcasting with decoupling of local programmes.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H04H100	H04H2010	H04H6006	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H04H	H04H	H04H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H04H1	H04H20	H04H60	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
System for radio or television broadcasting with decoupling of local programmes, including, in a central control centre (1), a broadcasting robot (2) managing the transmissions, according to a main predetermined time-based list (6), from a central network head (5) for transmitting to a local network head (31) for retransmitting, in which system the central control centre (1) is configured to receive the main list (6) and to reset it in time on the basis of the actual broadcasting timetables and, from a decoupling list (11), to control the decoupling of the local automatic unit (32) with a defined warning time with respect to the end of a programme currently running.
