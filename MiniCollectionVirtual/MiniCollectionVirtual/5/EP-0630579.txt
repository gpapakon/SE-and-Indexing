<INVENTION-TITLE>
Ruminant feed supplement formulation.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A23K116	A23K116	C07C5141	C07C5141	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A23K	A23K	C07C	C07C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A23K1	A23K1	C07C51	C07C51	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an animal feed supplement formulation in solid form. It comprises a binary mixture of sodium butyrate and ethyl butyrate, the amount of ethyl butyrate in the mixture lying between 1 and 25% weight/weight of the total mixture.
</ABSTRACT>
