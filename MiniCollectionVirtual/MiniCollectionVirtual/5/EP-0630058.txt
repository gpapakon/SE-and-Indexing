<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for forming an arrangement of pyrodetectors by electrochemically etching a silicon substrate.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
In order to produce a pyrodetector arrangement, hole structures (24) are produced in a first main surface (22) of a substrate (21) made of n-doped monocrystalline silicon by electrochemical etching, so that a structured region (26) is produced in the substrate (21). Above the structured region (26) at least one pyrodetector element (28) is arranged on the first main surface (22).
