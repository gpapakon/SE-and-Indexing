<INVENTION-TITLE>
Aqueous polymer dispersions.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C04B2400	C04B2426	C04B2800	C04B2802	C08F212	C08F222	C08F22000	C08F22012	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C04B	C04B	C04B	C04B	C08F	C08F	C08F	C08F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C04B24	C04B24	C04B28	C04B28	C08F2	C08F2	C08F220	C08F220	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Aqueous polymer dispersions of polymers having an increased glass transition temperature and a low mean molecular weight and a specific amount of copolymerised methacrylic acid are suitable, in liquid or dry form, as flow-improving additive in cement-containing mineral binder building materials.
</ABSTRACT>
