<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a low-viscosity offset printing ink which essentially comprises a binder, additives and 5-40 % by weight of pearlescent (pearl lustre) pigments and has an enhanced transfer behaviour for pearlescent pigments.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
Offset printing ink essentially comprising
binders, pigments and additives, 
characterized in that

it contains mineral oil, 10-30% by weight of pearlescent
pigments having a mean particle size D
50
 in the range
from 4 to 100 Mm, the pearlescent pigment : non-volatile

printing ink component ratio is 
<
 0.40, and it has a
viscosity of 
<
 12 Pas, with the proviso that the offset
printing ink has an anhydrous construction.
Offset printing ink according to Claim 1,

characterized in that
 the pearlescent pigment comprises
TiO
2
- and/or Fe
2
O
3
-coated mica platelets, SiO
2
 flakes,
glass flakes, ceramic flakes or synthetic flakes.
Offset printing ink according to Claim 1,

characterized in that
 the ink contains 40-95 % by weight
of binder.
Offset printing ink according to Claim 1,

characterized in that
 the proportion of additives is

<
 10 % by weight.
Offset printing ink according to Claim 1,

characterized in that
 the binder comprises 50-90 % by
weight of hard resin, 5-50 % by weight of alkyd resin

and 0-50 % by weight of drying oils.
Offset printing ink according to Claim 5,

characterized in that
 the hard resin is a hydrocarbon 
resin or a modified hydrocarbon resin having an acid

number of 
<
 50 mg of KOH/g of substance.
Offset printing ink according to Claim 1,

characterized in that
 the pigment component comprises
50-100 % by weight of pearlescent pigments and 0-50 % by

weight of carbon black or metal effect, fluorescent
and/or coloured pigments.
Offset printing ink according to Claim 1,

characterized in that
 it contains 0.1-3 % by weight of a
structure former.
Offset printing ink according to Claim 1,

characterized in that
 it contains 1-10 % by weight of
mineral oil.
