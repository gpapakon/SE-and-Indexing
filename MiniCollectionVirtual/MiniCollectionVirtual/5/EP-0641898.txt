<INVENTION-TITLE>
Manholecover.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E02D2914	E02D2914	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention describes a manhole cover which consists of a manhole lid and a frame. The frame has a continuously encircling bearing surface for the manhole lid and below this bearing surface a bearing plane for accommodating a dirt trap. A continuously encircling groove for accommodating a damping insert and/or a continuously encircling recess for accommodating a sealing ring is put into the bearing surface for the manhole lid.
