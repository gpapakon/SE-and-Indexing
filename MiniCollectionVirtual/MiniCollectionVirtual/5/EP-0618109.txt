<INVENTION-TITLE>
Signal device for vehicles, particularly for passenger vehicles.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a signalling device for a vehicle, especially a passenger transport vehicle. It includes, at the rear of the vehicle, a luminous signalling panel (10) and means for activating the said panel, the said activation means comprising an electrical power source (12), means (14) which can be controlled in order to connect the said power source to the said panel and means (16) for constructing a control signal for the connection means, in response to the request for opening at least one of the doors of the said vehicle.
