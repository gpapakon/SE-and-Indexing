<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the formation of metallic or metalloid coatings by micro-wave assisted plasma CVD.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C23C1634	C23C1634	C23C16448	C23C16452	C23C1650	C23C16511	H01L2102	H01L21205	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C23C	C23C	C23C	C23C	C23C	C23C	H01L	H01L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C23C16	C23C16	C23C16	C23C16	C23C16	C23C16	H01L21	H01L21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A part, containing at least hydrogen, of the reaction gases is, physically separately from the deposition reaction, excited by microwave energy and subsequently fed to a reactor in which, with additional separate introduction of non-excited reaction gases, a chemical deposition reaction is carried out with intact metal-bearing gas molecules.
</ABSTRACT>
