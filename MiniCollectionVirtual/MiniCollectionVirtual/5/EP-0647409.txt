<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for dividing a rectangular block of frozen food into portions.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A23L131	A23L131	A23L1325	A23L1325	A23P110	A23P110	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A23L	A23L	A23L	A23L	A23P	A23P	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A23L1	A23L1	A23L1	A23L1	A23P1	A23P1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In order, when dividing a rectangular block of deep-frozen food into portions, to achieve a higher yield, a combination of multi-stepped parts is required which divides and models the block into units of the same format but which are of an increasingly smaller size.
