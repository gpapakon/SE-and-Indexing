<INVENTION-TITLE>
Building element for making concrete floors.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E04C501	E04C506	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E04C	E04C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>E04C5	E04C5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a building element (1) for making reinforced-concrete floors or reinforced-concrete walls, having a base slab (2), a reinforcement (3) which is assigned to the base slab (2), and a spacer (4) which connects the reinforcement (3) to the base slab (2) and is fixed on the base slab (2). In order to achieve rapid and simple mounting of the reinforcement on the base slab, said building element is configured such that the spacer (4) is an integral part of the reinforcement (3).
