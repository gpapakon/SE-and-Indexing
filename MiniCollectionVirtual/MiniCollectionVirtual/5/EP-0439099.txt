<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the preparation of cyanamide.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B01J2900	B01J2984	C01C300	C01C316	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B01J	B01J	C01C	C01C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B01J29	B01J29	C01C3	C01C3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In order to prepare cyanamide from urea or from its thermal decomposition products by means of a microporous catalyst, the catalyst is a crystalline aluminophosphate, in particular VPI-5, MCM-9, AlPO4-8 or AlPO4-54 and/or a zeolite, in which preferably the strongly acidic centres on the entire (i.e. outer and inner) surface have been made inert, used in non-acidic or only weakly acidic form having a pore width of 0.3 to 1.5 nm.
</ABSTRACT>
