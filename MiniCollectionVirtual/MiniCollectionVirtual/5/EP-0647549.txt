<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method and means of preventing the theft of motor vehicles fitted with hydraulic brakes.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60R2500	B60R2500	B60R2508	B60R2508	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60R	B60R	B60R	B60R	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60R25	B60R25	B60R25	B60R25	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Means of preventing the theft of motor vehicles fitted with a hydraulic or hydropneumatic braking system (5,6,7) and a steering lock (20), multiple operating pressure being exerted at each main braking cylinder (1) when the described mechanism has been initiated and therefore the hydromechanical blocking of all brakes and vehicle doors (8) and the time-dependent initiation of numbing gas cartridges (10,11) being brought about in the interior of the vehicle.
