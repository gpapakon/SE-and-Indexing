<INVENTION-TITLE>
OXIDATION OF OLEFINS TO OXIRANE COMPOUNDS WITH PERIODATE COMPOUNDS
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C01B1122	C07D30100	C07D30112	C01B1100	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C01B	C07D	C07D	C01B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C01B11	C07D301	C07D301	C01B11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Process for preparing oxirane compounds by reacting a 
corresponding olefinic compound with a periodate having the 

formula MIO₄, MH₄IO₆ or M₂H₃IO₆, or the respective hydrates, 
and process for preparing an alkali me
tal monobasic or 
dibasic periodate, or mixture thereof, by reacting an alkali 

metal tribasic, tetrabasic or pentabasic periodate, or mixture 
thereof, with a substance having relative acidic value, pKa, of 

3 to 16 at a temperature of 0°C to 350°C and a pressure of 1 to 
100 atmospheres, the molar ratio of periodate to said substance 

being 1:1 to 1:1000. 
</ABSTRACT>
