<INVENTION-TITLE>
Goalkeeper glove with thumb provided with a latex covering
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A63B7114	A41D1902	A63B7108	A41D1902	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A63B	A41D	A63B	A41D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A63B71	A41D19	A63B71	A41D19	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The leather thumb piece on the side of the back of the hand and the latex layer on the same side (5) lie flatly on top of each other in the thumb tip area.  The leather thumb piece and the latex attachment layer (5) are joined together along the free edge (6) of the layer by means of a seam.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
Goalkeeper glove with thumb latex covering (5),

a) in which a thumb forms a ball-side longitudinal region (11) and a gusset-side longitudinal region (13), which merge into one another at a tip region (12),
b) in which an inner-hand thumb part (4) is covered on the outer side by a latex layer, which merges into an upper hand-side latex covering (5) bent around the gusset-side longitudinal region (13),
c) in which an upper hand-side thumb leather piece (9) is joined to the latex covering (5) along its free edge (6) by means of a seam and is joined to the inner-hand thumb part (4) along the ball-side longitudinal region (11) by means of a seam,
d) wherein the upper hand-side thumb leather piece (9) and the upper hand-side latex covering (5) are placed one on another flat on flat along the stitched (16) free edge (6), even in the tip region (12), and
e) wherein the latex covering (5) extends from the bend-around (14) on the gusset-side longitudinal region (13) in the direction of the ball-side longitudinal region (11) over the greater part of the length of the free edge (6) above the centre (19) of the width of the upper-hand side of the thumb.
Goalkeeper glove according to claim 1, in which the latex covering (5) forms a projecting region (18) with respect to a straight line (17) from the point of the latex covering (5) situated on the tip region (12) to the point of the latex covering (5) situated on the gusset-side longitudinal region (13), the free edge (6) of the projecting region (18) running in a curved line.
Goalkeeper glove according to claim 1 or 2, in which the upper hand-side thumb leather piece (9) is sewn on an intermediate
 Strip (20) between an upper-hand part and an inner-hand part of the goalkeeper glove.
Goalkeeper glove according to claim 3, in which the intermediate strip (20) joins the upper-hand part and the inner-hand part of the goalkeeper glove on the side of the index finger edge (15).
Goalkeeper glove according to one of the preceding Claims, in which the latex layer of the inner-hand thumb part (4) and the latex covering (5) situated thereon are integral with an inner-hand part latex layer (1), which forms an outer index finger edge (15), which merges into the free edge (6) of the latex covering (5) via a transition (7), wherein the transition (7) defines a recess (8), which is arranged only at a slight angle with respect to the outer index finger edge (15), is narrow with respect to its length and is short in its extension into the inner-hand part latex layer (1) at right-angles to the index finger edge (15).
Goalkeeper glove according to claim 5, in which the recess is U-shaped.
</CLAIMS>
