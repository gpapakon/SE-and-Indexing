<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for manufacturing self-sharpening knife edges and self-sharpening knife edge.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method of manufacturing self-sharpening knife edges. Provision is made for the knife edge (1) to be provided on one side with a thin coating of mechanically resistant material, in particular a carbide coating (2).
