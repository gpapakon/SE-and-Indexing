Storage frame.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
In the case of a storage frame (R) for receiving workpieces (5) having stacking columns (2) which are arranged at a distance from one another and have supports (4) for holding the workpieces (5) the stacking columns (2) are to be arranged so as to be rotatable about their longitudinal axes (A). Furthermore, the supports (4) are arranged on each stacking column (2) so as to be offset with respect to one another in a vertical and horizontal direction.
