<INVENTION-TITLE>
Coating for clear-coat top layers and its use in processes for preparing multi-layer coatings.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
A description is given of coating compositions and their use in the production of transparent top layers (topcoats) of multilayer coatings. They contain binders based on hydroxy-functional (meth)acrylic copolymers, blocked polyisocyanates, amino resins and, if desired, hydroxy-functional polyester resins, as well as conventional paint additives and solvents, and are characterised in that the binders contain:   A) 25 - 90 % by weight of one or more hydroxy-functional (meth)acrylic copolymers obtainable from   a1) 10 - 30 % by weight of styrene and/or one or more styrene derivatives a2) 20 - 40 % by weight of one or more glycidyl esters and/or vinyl esters of alpha,alpha'-dialkyl-substituted, branched aliphatic monocarboxylic acids a3) 5 - 15 % by weight of one or more hydroxyalkyl esters of (meth)acrylic acid   a4) 1 - 15 % by weight of (meth)acrylic acid   a5) 15 - 35 % by weight of one or more (meth)acrylic esters   B) 0 - 40 % by weight of one or more hydroxy-functional (meth)acrylic copolymers obtainable from   b1) 5 - 20 % by weight of styrene and/or one or more styrene derivatives   b2) 5 - 20 % by weight of caprolactone b3) 10 - 30 % by weight of one or more hydroxyalkyl esters of (meth)acrylic acid   b4) 1 - 3 % by weight of (meth)acrylic acid   b5) 30 - 50 % by weight of one or more (meth)acrylic esters     C) 0 - 40 % by weight of one or more hydroxy-functional polyesters   D) 5 - 35 % by weight of one or more blocked polyisocyanates   E) 5 - 30 % by weight of one or more amino resins the sum of components a1) to a5), b1) to b5) and A) to E) each adding up to give 100.
</ABSTRACT>
