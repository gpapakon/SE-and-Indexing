<INVENTION-TITLE>
Handset for a subscriber set.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H04M103	H04M103	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H04M	H04M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H04M1	H04M1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A handset (1) for a subscriber set, comprising an upper and a lower casing (2, 3), in which an earpiece and microphone capsule are disposed which establish contact with the lines (7, 8) of the handset connection cord via insulation displacement contacts (10, 11) provided on them. The object of the invention is to design the microphone capsule in particular in such a way that it meets in optimum fashion the requirements imposed upon it in terms of acoustics, ease of assembly and adaptability to different designs. This object is achieved by forming the microphone capsule by means of an electret microphone (5) fitted to a separate bracket (6), the bracket (6) being designed in such a way that it can be inserted in different handset types and can be adapted to the respective required antechamber volume of the handset (1).
