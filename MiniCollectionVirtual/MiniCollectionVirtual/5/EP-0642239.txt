<INVENTION-TITLE>
Substitution of synchronisation bits in a transmission frame.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H04J312	H04J312	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H04J	H04J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H04J3	H04J3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Transmission frame comprising a synchronisation flag, synchronisation bits (S1 to S8) and information bits (B1 to B63), in which a control bit (C1) is substituted for one of the synchronisation bits (S1) if any sequence of bits with a length equal to that of the synchronisation flag including this synchronisation bit (S1) differs from this synchronisation flag by at least one bit in addition to this synchronisation bit (S1). Transmitter and receiver suitable for this frame.
