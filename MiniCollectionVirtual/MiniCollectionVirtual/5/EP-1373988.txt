<INVENTION-TITLE>
DEVICE FOR ADJUSTING A TIME INDICATOR
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G04B1900	G04B1925	G04B19253	G04B2700	G04B2704	G04F300	G04F302	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G04B	G04B	G04B	G04B	G04B	G04F	G04F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device for adjusting a time indicator comprising a gear train (1), the kinematic chain of which comprises a sliding pinion (2) which is driven by a driving mobile (3).  The sliding pinion drives a driven mobile (4) when the driving mobile turns in a first direction (A) and disconnects from said driven mobile when the driving mobile (9) turns in a second opposing direciton (B).  The shaft (6) that forms the sliding pinion (2) is engaged in a groove (7) having first (9) and second (12) end sections, of which at least the first end section (9) is directed radially (R) to the driven mobile (4).
</ABSTRACT>
