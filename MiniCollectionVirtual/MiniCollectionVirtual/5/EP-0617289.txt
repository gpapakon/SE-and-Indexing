<INVENTION-TITLE>
LYOPHILISED CONTROL OR REFERENCE SERUM OR PLASMA FOR DIAGNOSTIC PURPOSES.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a lyophilised control or reference serum or plasma for diagnostic purposes which contains lipoproteins, in particular apo-B-containing lipoproteins, has a water content between 1 and 10 %, preferably between 1.5 and 5 %, and can be reconstituted to a clear solution with a turbidity not exceeding 70 LSU. The invention furthermore relates to a process for the production of a lyophilised control or reference serum or plasma and to a process for the production of a control or reference serum or plasma.
</ABSTRACT>
