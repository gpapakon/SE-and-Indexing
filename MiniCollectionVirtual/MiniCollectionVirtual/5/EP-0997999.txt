<INVENTION-TITLE>
Holding clamp for cables
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H02G302	H02G306	H02G308	H02G308	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H02G	H02G	H02G	H02G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H02G3	H02G3	H02G3	H02G3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The fixing cuff has an insert (9) clamped around the outside of the hose, pipeline, or cable via cooperating clamp plates, provided by a slit sleeve (10) of a flexible material, e.g. an elastomer, having integral lamella profiles (37) extending longitudinally along its inside surface, providing bearing surfaces for the hose, pipeline, or cable at their ends. The profiles have a height to base width ratio of 2 : 1 or more.
</ABSTRACT>
