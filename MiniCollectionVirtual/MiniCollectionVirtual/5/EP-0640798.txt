<INVENTION-TITLE>
Extraction system.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F24C1520	F24C1520	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F24C	F24C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F24C15	F24C15	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
An apparatus 10;40 for extraction and cleaning of grilling, simmering and boiling vapours and also for blowing out cleaned air comprises a housing 15, a filter 27, 28, 43, an electric extraction fan 20, by means of which the vapours in question can be extracted through the filter 27, 28, 43 for cleaning, and a sound-damping chamber 23 which is arranged downstream of the extraction fan 20 and from which the clean air can be blown out, at least the extraction fan 20 and the sound-damping chamber 23 with the insulating material 30 being arranged encapsulated in the housing 15. The apparatus 10 is preferably formed from a cylindrical cartridge which can be inserted, sound-damped, into the leg section 13.
