<INVENTION-TITLE>
Acupressure device.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61H3904	A61H3904	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61H	A61H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61H39	A61H39	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Acupressure device comprising a substrate (10) made of a flexible foil as carrier for pressure bodies (12), preferably connected thereto in a movable manner, with integral pyramid-shaped acupressure elements (4). The pressure bodies may have a hexagonal or rotationally symmetrical design and the substrate may have any desired design.
