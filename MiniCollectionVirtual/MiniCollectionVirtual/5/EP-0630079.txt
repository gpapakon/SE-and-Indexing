<INVENTION-TITLE>
Subscriber termination device
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H01R448	H01R1370	H01R13703	H01R448	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H01R	H01R	H01R	H01R	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H01R4	H01R13	H01R13	H01R4	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The subscriber termination device comprises contacts for connecting an input line to at least one output line, these contacts being carried by an equipment support. It is characterised in that the said line connection contacts comprise first and second connection contacts (27, 28) having first ends under elastic compression in a housing (21) of a female connector (22) connected to the input line and carried by the said equipment support (4) and in that the said female connector can be selectively fitted (adapted) to male plugs of different types.

<CLAIMS>
A subscriber-end line termination device for connecting the 
conductors of at least one input line (25) to the conductors of at 

least one output line (28) corresponding to each input line (25), 
said device including: 


an outer protective structure (2) referred to as the "housing", 
having a "front" opening (3) in a front wall; 
an equipment support (4) inside the housing; 
first connection contacts for each input line and second 
connection contacts for each corresponding output line, the contacts 

being carried by said equipment support (4), being connected to the 
conductors of said lines to be interconnected and having first ends 

that press resiliently into the rear portion of said housing (2), 
facing said front opening (3); and 
a female connector (22) assigned to each input line (25), which 
female connector is carried by said equipment support (4), has a 

recess (21) open over a front portion of the connector facing said 
opening, and is provided with first access contacts (27) which are 

connected to the conductors of the input line and which are 
accessible via the front portion of said connector and via the front 

opening (3) in said housing (1-2); 
said first ends of the connection contacts for connecting the 
input line to each corresponding output line (28) being received in a 

rear portion of said recess (21) in said connector (22) assigned to 
the input line (25); and 
said access contacts comprising said first connection contacts 
(27) further extending substantially from the rear portion to the 

front portion of said recess (21) of the connector, and being 
retractably mounted so as to retract towards a first edge (21G) of 

said recess; 
said device being characterized in that: 
removable adapter means (5, 7) for adapting said female 
connector (22) to a first male plug from among various standard plugs 

are mounted on at least the front portion of the connector and 
received in said front opening (3). 
A device according to claim 1, characterized in that the second 
connection contacts (28) lead into the rear portion of said recess 

(21) substantially transversely to a second edge (21D) that is 
opposite to the first edge (21G) of said recess. 
A device according to claim 1 or 2, characterized in that each of 
the first connection contacts (27) has a projection (27B) that 

projects in a "middle" portion of said recess (21). 
A device according to any one of claims 1 to 3, characterized in 
that said female connector (22) divides up said equipment support (4) 

into a first side portion and a second side portion, the first side 
portion receiving the input line, and the second side portion 

receiving each corresponding output line, and in that the device 
further includes a first connection module (30) assigned to each 

input line, carried by said first side portion of the equipment 
support, and connected to the first connection contacts for the input 

line (27), and at least one second connection module (35, 36) 
assigned to each output line corresponding to the input line in 

question, which second connection module is carried by the second 
side portion of the equipment support, and is connected to the second 

connection contacts (28) for each corresponding output line. 
A device according to claim 4, characterized in that each of the 
second connection contacts (28) has at least one insulation-displacement 

catch (28B) provided on an end portion that is 
"opposite" from the first end of the second connection contact, which 

catch is received directly in the respective second connection module 
(35, 36) assigned to each output line corresponding to the input line 

in question. 
A device according to claim 5, characterized in that at least two 
output lines are connected in parallel to the same input line. 
A device according to claim 5 or 6, characterized in that the 
first connection module is provided with third connection contacts 

(46), each of which has an insulation-displacement first end inside  
 

said first connection module and connected to the input line in 
question, and in that the device further includes a link terminal 

(48) carried by the first side portion of the equipment support, and 
receiving and connecting those end portions (27D, 27E, 46A) which are 

opposite from the first ends of the first and third connection 
contacts (27, 46) for said input line. 
A device according to claim 7, characterized in that said link 
terminal (48) is provided with link wells (48A), each of which 

projects into the front portion of the housing, in which link wells 
the end portions of the first and third connection contacts are 

connected respectively. 
A device according to claim 7, characterized in that it further 
includes a filtering module (31) assigned to each input line and 

mounted on said equipment support, the filtering module having each 
of its outputs (31A, 31B) connected to one of said first and third 

connection contacts (27, 46) for the input line. 
A device according to claim 4 or 9, characterized in that it 
further includes firstly a "lightning-arresting" protective module 

(32) assigned to each input line and mounted on the first side 
portion of said equipment support, which protective module has a 

ground tab (32B) and line-protection tabs (32A) each of which is 
connected to one of said first and third connection contacts (27, 46) 

for the input line in question, and secondly a ground terminal (33) 
carried by the first side portion of said equipment support (4) and 

to which said ground tab (32B) is connected. 
A device according to any one of claims 4, 9, and 10, 
characterized in that it further includes at least one protective 

plate (37) extending in the front portion of the housing (1-2) and 
covering at least the first side portion of the equipment support. 
A device according to any one of claims 1 to 11, characterized in 
that the right section of said recess (21) has a narrow portion 

(21A), the width of which portion matches the width of the first male  
 

plug (62) of the telephone jack type, and in which portion the first 
connection contacts (27) extend and the first ends of the first and 

second connection contacts (27, 28) are connected together, and a 
wide portion (21B) adjoining and axially centered on the narrow 

portion, and in that said front opening (3) has larger dimensions 
than those required for plugging in the various male plugs. 
A device according to claim 12, characterized in that said 
adapter means are constituted by an adaptor plate (5) mounted on the 

front portion of said connector, and having a rear shoulder (5B) 
received in said wide portion (21B) of the recess, a front shoulder 

(5C) received in said front opening (3) in the housing, and a through 
pluggable access (6) for plugging in said first male plug, said 

pluggable access (6) substantially surrounding said narrow portion of 
the recess and limiting the dimensions of said front opening and of 

the wide portion of the recess to the corresponding dimensions of 
said first male plug. 
A device according to claim 12, characterized in that said 
adapter means are constituted by a matching female socket (7) for 

plugging in a second male plug (80) of the RJ45 type, said matching 
female socket being mounted in said narrow portion (21A) and in said 

wide portion (21B) of the recess, and having a narrow rear shoulder 
(70A) received in the narrow portion (21A) of the recess, a wide rear 

shoulder (70B) received in the wide portion (21B) of the recess, a 
front shoulder (70C) received in said front opening (3) in the 

housing, and a through pluggable access (8) matching said second male 
plug, provided through said front shoulder and said wide rear 

shoulder, and divided into a front access (8A) for said second male 
plug (80) and a rear access (8B), said matching female socket itself 

being provided with second access contacts (77) and with associated 
additional contacts (78), said second access contacts firstly 

projecting relative to a first side face of said narrow shoulder 
(70A) and being received under pressure against the first connection 

contacts in the recess (21), and secondly being accessible in said 
front pluggable access (8A) and extended in said rear access (8B), 

said associated additional contacts (78) firstly projecting relative  
 

to a rear end edge of the second side face of said narrow shoulder 
(70A), which face is opposite from the first side face thereof, and 

received under pressure against the first ends of the second 
connection contacts (28) in the recess (21), and secondly extending 

in said rear access (8B) and coming resiliently under pressure 
against the second access contacts in said rear access (8B) so that 

they are separated when said second male plug (80) is plugged into 
said front access (8A). 
A device according to claim 14, characterized in that said 
matching female socket (7) includes a spacer (71) in said rear access 

(8B), which spacer forms an end-of-stroke abutment for said second 
plug (80) plugged into said front access (8A). 
A device according to any one of claims 1 to 15, characterized in 
that said equipment support (4) is mounted on a base (1) that is part 

of said housing (1-2) and that forms a rear wall of said housing. 
A device according to claim 16, characterized in that said 
equipment support (4) and said base (1) are provided with 

complementary means (18, 42, 43) for removably retaining said 
equipment support on said base. 
A device according to claim 1 or 17, characterized in that, on at 
least one of their facing faces, said equipment support (4) and said 

base (1) are provided with ribs (40A) for keeping said facing faces 
apart, which ribs correspond to cutouts (11B) in said housing (1-2), 

so as to create a layer of air between the base (1) and said 
equipment support (4). 
A device according to any one of claims 16 to 18, characterized 
in that said base (1) is provided with a set of through holes (12A) 

so that it can be screwed to a wall, and with sets of lugs (13) 
having end fastening hooks (13A), which lugs project relative to the 

rear face of the base, which face is opposite from the face that 
faces said equipment support (4), so that the base can also be fixed 

on a rail.  
 
A termination device according to any one of claims 1 to 19, 
receiving a plurality of input lines, said device being characterized 

in that it includes a series of female connectors (22) aligned with 
and individually assigned to the various input lines, said input 

lines being received on one of the side portions defined on said 
equipment support (4) by the series of female connectors, and being 

connected to output lines received on the other one of the side 
portions of the equipment support.
