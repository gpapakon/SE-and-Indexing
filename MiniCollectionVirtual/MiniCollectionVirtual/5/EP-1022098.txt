<INVENTION-TITLE>
Tool for temporary modifying the mechanical tention of a belt and method using such a tool
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B25B2500	B25B2500	F16H700	F16H724	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B25B	B25B	F16H	F16H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B25B25	B25B25	F16H7	F16H7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Tool has beam (42) with roller (39) supporting first face (3.1) of belt (3) and other roller (41) supporting second face (3.2) of belt. Rollers ensure compression of belt idler by rotation of tool relative to belt. Square cutout (49) can receive square of ratchet spanner to attach tool to shaft (not represented).  An Independent claim is also included for the method to lay belt in kinematic circuit.
</ABSTRACT>
