<INVENTION-TITLE>
Painting kit.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B44D318	B44D318	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B44D	B44D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B44D3	B44D3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A painting kit (1) for children is described, which is suitable both for painting and, subsequently, for playing. The painting kit is designed as a three-dimensional toy figure (1) with an outer cover (2) which can be at least partially painted or written upon and an elastically resilient filling.
