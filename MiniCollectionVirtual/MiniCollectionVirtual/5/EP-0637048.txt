<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for the disposal of cathode ray or electroluminescent tubes.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B24C300	B24C332	B24C1100	B24C1100	H01J900	H01J952	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B24C	B24C	B24C	B24C	H01J	H01J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B24C3	B24C3	B24C11	B24C11	H01J9	H01J9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The method for the disposal of cathode ray or electroluminescent tubes provides for the tubes to be opened and comminuted, a liquid jet cutting method being used therefor. Separation is carried out along specified separation planes, and the coatings on the inside of the tubes are washed out by means of the same devices by converting the shape of the jet to a soft jet and/or by the reduction in an abrasive additive.
</ABSTRACT>
