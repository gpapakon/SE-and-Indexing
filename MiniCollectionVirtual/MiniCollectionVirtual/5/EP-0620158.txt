<INVENTION-TITLE>
Stacking container.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
A stacking container having an essentially rectangular housing and an outwardly inclined front which leaves an opening to the top free and a flap which closes the opening. The technical problem is to provide a stacking container which can be cleaned easily and completely. Additionally, the interior should be easily accessible. The flap (3) is designed as a flat plate with webs (14) on the longitudinal edges, and one web (14) can be hooked into a slot (13) in the lid (2).
