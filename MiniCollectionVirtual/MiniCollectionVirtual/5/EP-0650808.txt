<INVENTION-TITLE>
Active filler for abrasive tool, its use and the abrasive tool containing the same.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B24D320	B24D328	B24D334	B24D334	C08J514	C08J514	C09K314	C09K314	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B24D	B24D	B24D	B24D	C08J	C08J	C09K	C09K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B24D3	B24D3	B24D3	B24D3	C08J5	C08J5	C09K3	C09K3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Grinding tools usually comprise an abrasive filler (8). It is proposed that, as the abrasive filler (8), sodium chloride be used which is at least very largely free of magnesium and which is non-hygroscopic, the sodium chloride (9) preferably being provided with a coating (10) made of synthetic resin to protect it against being etched by water.
