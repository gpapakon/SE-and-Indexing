<INVENTION-TITLE>
Collapsible packaging box
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65D1902	B65D1916	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
Collapsible packaging box intended for packaging manufactured or bulk products, having a base (2) associated with a pallet (8), a lid (7) and four side panels (3-6) folded by means of flexible hinges arranged at the edges of the parallelepiped. According to the invention, it is characterised in that it comprises:     - two fold lines (11, 12) arranged on two opposite side panels (5, 6), - a lid (7) in two parts (16, 17) each capable of being positioned perpendicular to the pallet when the box is completely folded up,  in order to produce a box in all possible formats. Application in industries using wood as a raw material for manufacturing packagings.

<CLAIMS>
Foldable conditioning box (1) for the packaging of manufactured products 
or loose goods, having a bottom (2) associated to a pallet (8), a cover (7), 

four side panels (3 to 6) folded by means of flexible hinges arranged at the 
level of the edges of the parallelepiped, capable of being folded on a pallet 

without exceeding the surface of same when the height of the side panels does 
not exceed the width of the surface of the bottom (2) associated to the pallet, 

having two folding lines (11, 12) arranged on two opposite side panels (5, 6) 
and a flexible hinge (13) provided for on the bottom (2), with a height allowing 

the folding of the panels, characterized in that it includes a cover (7) 
made of two parts (16, 17) the respective widths of which are adapted for both 

parts (16, 17) to be capable of being each positioned in front of the pallet when 
the box is in fully folded position, in order to manufacture a foldable box 

which does not exceed the surface of the pallet in fully folded position when 
the height of the side panels does not exceed the width of the surface of the 

bottom (2) associated to the pallet. 
Foldable conditioning box for the packaging of manufactured products 
or loose goods according to claim 1, characterized in that both parts (16, 17) of 

the cover (7) are identical. 
Foldable conditioning box for the packaging of manufactured products 
or loose goods according to claim 1, characterized in that both parts (16, 17) of 

the cover (7) are different. 
Foldable conditioning box for the packaging of manufactured products 
or loose goods according to claim 1, characterized in that the bottom (2) has 

sizes, length and width, smaller than the corresponding sizes of the pallet (8). 
Foldable conditioning box for the packaging of manufactured products 
or loose goods according to claim
 1, characterized in that both parts (16, 17) of 
the cover (7) are folded towards the same side. 
Foldable conditioning box for the packaging of manufactured products 
or loose goods according to claim 1, characterized in that both parts (16, 17) of 

the cover (7) are folded according to two opposite sides. 
</CLAIMS>
