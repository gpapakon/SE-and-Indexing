<INVENTION-TITLE>
OVER MOLDING OF LIGHTENED AMIDE BLOCK POLYETHER ON THERMOPLASTIC ELASTOMER.

<ABSTRACT>
Bilayer object comprising a lightened thermoplastic elastomer of the polyether amide type adhering by itself on a non lightened thermoplastic selected from polyether amides, polyether esters, polyurethanes. The bilayer product obtained by molding the lightened thermoplastic over the compact thermoplastic may be used in the fabrication of shoe soles.
</ABSTRACT>
