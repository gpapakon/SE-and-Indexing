<INVENTION-TITLE>
Security device for automatic clutch.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60K2600	B60K2602	G05G130	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60K	B60K	G05G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60K26	B60K26	G05G1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Safety device for a clutch controlled in response to the displacement of the accelerator pedal (1) and cable (3) of a motor vehicle, characterised in that it includes a first clutch-control element (10) connected to the accelerator pedal (1), and a second clutch-control element (12, 15) connected to the accelerator cable (3) so as to keep the first element (10) in the declutched position when the pedal (1) is at rest.
