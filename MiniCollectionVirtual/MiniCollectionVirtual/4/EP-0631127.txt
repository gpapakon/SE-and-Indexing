<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method of detection and/or identification of chemical or biological species using a waveguide of the sol-gel type.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01N2155	G01N2155	G01N2177	G01N2177	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01N	G01N	G01N	G01N	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01N21	G01N21	G01N21	G01N21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a method and a device for detecting and/or identifying a chemical or biological species, called a ligand, in a liquid or gaseous medium, which device comprising, in a known manner, a radiation source (1) producing a collimated monochromatic beam (2), a cell receiving the beam (2) so as to produce a total internal reflection and comprising in particular a waveguide (16), and a radiation detector (3), and characterised in that the said cell (10) comprises a substrate which is transparent to the beam (2) and is coated on one of its faces with a first layer (15) composed of a metallic film and with a second layer (16) composed of a polymer which constitutes a waveguide and on which suitable receptors are bound.
