<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for vertically positioning of can bodies.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65G4724	B65G47252	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65G	B65G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65G47	B65G47	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The device for setting cans (2) upright has two deflection belts (5, 15) arranged in parallel. The device is assigned a transfer device (18) for transferring every other can (2) arriving to the second deflection belt (15).
