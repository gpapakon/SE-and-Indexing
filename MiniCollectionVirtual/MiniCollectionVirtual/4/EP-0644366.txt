<INVENTION-TITLE>
FLANGE CONNECTION WITH A FIXED AND A LOOSE FLANGE.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E01D1900	E01D1908	E03F504	E03F504	E04D1304	E04D1304	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E01D	E01D	E03F	E03F	E04D	E04D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>E01D19	E01D19	E03F5	E03F5	E04D13	E04D13	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Flange connection with a fixed and a loose flange and a screw-arrangement (4) fastened on one of the two flanges (1), at least the flange (1) which contains this screw-arrangement (4) consisting of a deformable flat material, characterised in that the flange (1) exhibits a downwardly directed dome-shaped convexity (5) at the location where a threaded pin (4) is fastened.
