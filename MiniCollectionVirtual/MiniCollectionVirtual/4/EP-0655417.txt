<INVENTION-TITLE>
Water sterilising reactor.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C02F132	C02F132	C02F134	C02F136	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C02F	C02F	C02F	C02F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C02F1	C02F1	C02F1	C02F1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A reactor (1) is provided for sterilising water, having a reactor shell having an inlet (9) at one end and an outlet (16) at the other end. In order that, on the one hand good digestion of zooplankton and other organisms serving as hosts for bacteria is ensured and on the other hand good sterilisation is possible, a UV lamp (12) is provided in the interior of the reactor (1) and an ultrasonic oscillator (5) is provided at the inlet side.
