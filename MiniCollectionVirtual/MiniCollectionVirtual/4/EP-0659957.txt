<ABSTRACT>
The box-like garage unit exhibits two side walls 1 which are connected with the aid of a cover part 3 and of a foot panel or tray 4. At least one side is provided, adjoining the cover part 3, with a foot part 5 and backrest 6, the seat part 5 being adjoined by a door part 7, and the parts 5, 6, 7 being arranged on the cover part 3 in a hinge-like manner. The interior formed by the garage unit is suitable for receiving a bicycle 29.

<CLAIMS>
A box-shaped parking enclosure having side walls (1), a rear 
wall (2), a lid part (3) and where appropriate having a floor 

plate (4) and having a door (7) which closes off the parking 
enclosure, characterized in that at least one side of the lid 

part (3) is constructed as a seat part (5) and back rest (6), and 
the seat part (5), together with the back rest (6) and the door 

part (7), is articulated, connected in the manner of a hinge, to 
the lid part (3) at a hinge (8). 
A box-shaped parking enclosure according to Claim 1, 
characterized in that the side walls (1), together with the rear 

wall (2) and the door part (7), form a trapezium shape which 
becomes narrower towards the rear wall (2). 
A box-shaped parking enclosure according to Claims 1 and 2, 
characterized in that, in accordance with the modular principle, 

it comprises individual single parts (1, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7) which are 
screwed to one another. 
A box-shaped parking enclosure according to Claims 1 to 3, 
characterized in that it comprises half-shells (36) arranged next 

to one another in rows, the one side wall (1) being formed by the 
adjacent half-shell (36'). 
A box-shaped parking enclosure according to Claims 1 to 4, 
characterized in that a seat part (5) with a back rest (6) and 

a door part (7) is provided on either side of the lid part (3). 
A box-shaped parking enclosure according to Claims 1 to 5, 
characterized in that the floor plate (4) is provided with a 

guide (10) running in the longitudinal direction of the 
enclosure.  

 
A box-shaped parking enclosure according to Claims 1 to 6, 
characterized in that a lever arm (11) is secured to the unit 

comprising the seat part (5) and the back rest (6), and a cable 
pull (13) with a counter-weight (14) acts on the end point (12) 

of this lever arm (11). 
A box-shaped parking enclosure according to Claim 7, 
characterized in that a gas spring (34) supported laterally 

against the parking enclosure acts on the lever arm (32). 
A box-shaped parking enclosure according to one of Claims 
1 to 8, characterized in that the seat part (5) is held against 

the side wall (1) by means of a spring-open fitting. 
A box-shaped parking enclosure according to Claims 1 to 9, 
characterized in that the floor plate (4) is secured to 

foundations (15) sunk into the ground (16). 
A box-shaped parking enclosure according to Claims 1 to 10, 
characterized in that individual enclosures (17, 18) are 

connected to one another to form groups. 
A box-shaped parking enclosure according to one of Claims 
1 to 11, characterized in that roofing elements (19, 20), 

advertising boards (21), an advertising column (22), lighting 
elements (23), telephone units (24) and the like are mounted on 

the enclosures (17, 18) which are joined together to form groups.
