<INVENTION-TITLE>
Apparatus and method for filling a container with articles.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65B100	B65B122	B65B506	B65B506	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65B	B65B	B65B	B65B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65B1	B65B1	B65B5	B65B5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Apparatus and method for filling a container (2) with articles (4). The articles (4) are brought into position above a feed orifice on the top side of a container (2) by means of a manipulator (86) and are introduced into the container along an essentially vertical path. The container (2) is shaken during the filling operation or thereafter, so that the articles (4) change position in the container. For this purpose, the lower wall of the container (2) is connected to at least two vibrating plates (20, 22).
