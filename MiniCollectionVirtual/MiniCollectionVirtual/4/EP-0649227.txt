<INVENTION-TITLE>
Handheld or vehicle mounted radio communication apparatus with steerable directional antenna.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H01Q2120	H01Q2120	H04B138	H04B138	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
State of the art: handheld radio communication apparatus with phase-controlled antenna array. Technical problem: easy-to-handle design of the handheld radio communication apparatus and antenna. Fundamental principle: circular arrangement of the individual antenna components. Example: antenna in cylindrical housing formed on the upper end of the handheld radio communication apparatus.
