<INVENTION-TITLE>
Drug compositions containing coated, poorly watersoluble drugs for use in pharmaceutical inhalation forms and method to produce them.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Drug compositions composed of micronised particles of poorly water-soluble drugs are characterised in that they are coated with a natural, physiologically tolerated ampho-surfactant which forms a micellar colloidal solution in water. Processes for producing these drug compositions are described. These compositions are suitable for inhalation when introduced into an appropriate final container and after addition of a chlorine-free, partially fluorinated, pressure-liquefiable propellant gas.
</ABSTRACT>
