<INVENTION-TITLE>
Test strip to determine the UV intensity and the risk of getting a sunburn.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01J100	G01J102	G01J102	G01J150	G01N3122	G01N3122	G01W100	G01W112	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01J	G01J	G01J	G01J	G01N	G01N	G01W	G01W	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01J1	G01J1	G01J1	G01J1	G01N31	G01N31	G01W1	G01W1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the use of test strips with a flat, preferably elongate sheet which carries a matrix with a photoactive chromogenic substance for prior determination of the possible period of exposure to the sun without sunburn, to a test system for this determination containing test strips and a test strip pack, to a test strip pack and to a method for the prior determination of the maximum possible period of exposure to the sun without sunburn using the test system.
</ABSTRACT>
