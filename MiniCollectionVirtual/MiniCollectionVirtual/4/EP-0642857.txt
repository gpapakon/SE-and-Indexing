<INVENTION-TITLE>
Continuous casting machine.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B22D11124	B22D11124	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B22D	B22D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B22D11	B22D11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In a continuous casting machine, the strand-guiding segments (1) of which are provided with spray elements, in particular spray bars (7 and 8a, 8b, 8c), distributed over the adjustable width of the strand, divided, if required, into cooling zones (4, 5, 6) in the direction (2) of movement of the strand and connected to transverse distributor pipes (9), the cooling system is improved if the outer spray bars (8a, 8b, 8c), which are arranged so as to go beyond the minimum strand width, can be connected up or disconnected depending on the respective strand width.
