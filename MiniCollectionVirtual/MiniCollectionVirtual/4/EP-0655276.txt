<INVENTION-TITLE>
Mixing device.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B01F1300	B01F1300	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B01F	B01F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B01F13	B01F13	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
It is provided that a conveyor (2) setting the material to be mixed and/or the material components to be mixed into a translatory conveying motion is assigned to the mixer (10). Furthermore, it is provided that a metering device (8) which can be adjusted to different conveyor output rates is arranged upstream of the mixer (10) in direction of conveyance.
