Pulse generator for vehicles.

<ABSTRACT>
A pulse generator (10) for the direct scanning of gearwheels is proposed, which has a sensor (11), for example a Hall-effect sensor, and a sensor electronic system (12) for conditioning the signal level supplied by the sensor (11). It is possible in a simple way to use a pulse divider (13) which is downstream of the sensor electronic system and has an adjustable division ratio to carry out matching to a multiplicity of different vehicle types.
