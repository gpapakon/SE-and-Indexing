<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to novel preceramic oligo- or polyborosilazane compounds which contain the structural feature Si-N-B and to ceramic material based on SiN and BN, to processes for their respective preparation and to the use of the polyborosilazanes and the ceramic materials.

<CLAIMS>
Monomeric, oligomeric or polymeric borosilazane
compounds which have the structural feature Si-N-B,

characterized in that the first co-ordination sphere of
each boron atom consists only of nitrogen, the first

co-ordination sphere of the silicon atoms consists of
at least two and less than four nitrogen atoms and the

remaining co-ordination sites are occupied by hydrogen
and/or carbon.
