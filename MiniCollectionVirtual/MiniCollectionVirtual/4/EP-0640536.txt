<INVENTION-TITLE>
Disposable container for used material.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65F100	B65F100	B65F104	B65F108	B65F116	B65F116	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65F	B65F	B65F	B65F	B65F	B65F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65F1	B65F1	B65F1	B65F1	B65F1	B65F1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A disposable container for used material is assembled from three components. The first component is a trap (1) for placing things in the container. The second component is an open-topped inner box (60) with leakproof walls (62). The third component is an outer box (20) opened and closed by the trap (1) and containing said inner box (60). Application to the removal and incineration of contaminated products.
