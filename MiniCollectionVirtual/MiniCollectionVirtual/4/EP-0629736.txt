<INVENTION-TITLE>
Iron, allowing the use of a laundry treatment liquid which does not bear high temperatures
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>D06F7514	D06F7508	D06F7522	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>D06F	D06F	D06F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The steam iron (1) comprises a tank (2) of ironing liquid, which communicates with an evaporation chamber (4), and comprises a sprayer (8) for spreading the liquid directly on the laundry; the sprayer (8) is associated with the second tank (9) independent of the first tank (2).

<CLAIMS>
A steam iron (1) including a reservoir (2) of ironing liquid which 
communicates with a steam-generating chamber (4), and including a 

spray (8) for spreading the liquid directly onto the ironing, the 
iron being characterized in that the spray (8) is associated with a 

second reservoir (9) independent from the first reservoir (2) and 
located inside the body of the iron. 
An iron according to claim 1, characterized in that at least one 
of the reservoirs is associated with at least one cassette through 

which the liquid entering or leaving the reservoir passes and 
containing a substance for treating the liquid or the ironing. 
An iron according to claim 1 or 2, characterized in that at least 
one of the two reservoirs (2, 9) is removable. 
</CLAIMS>
