<INVENTION-TITLE>
Roller for shower screens.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47H1500	A47H1502	A47K328	A47K334	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47H	A47H	A47K	A47K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47H15	A47H15	A47K3	A47K3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
There is provided a roller for sliding parts in arcuate regions, especially on angular or round shower screens, whose bearing arrangement is constructed on its axis as a ball-and-socket joint. A roller which guarantees perfect guidance, even in tight curves, with a minimum of effort is thus produced.
