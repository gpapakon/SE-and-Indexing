<INVENTION-TITLE>
Rotary printing machine for producing newspapers.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B41F900	B41F900	B41F901	B41F2712	B41F2712	B41F3108	B41F3108	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B41F9	B41F9	B41F9	B41F27	B41F27	B41F31	B41F31	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In order to print a newspaper by the intaglio method in an improved quality using conventional paper for newspapers, the printing unit (1) has an intaglio form cylinder (2), a transfer cylinder (3) and a backpressure cylinder (4), and a chamber-type doctor (16), operating with water-soluble ink is arranged on the intaglio form cylinder (2).
