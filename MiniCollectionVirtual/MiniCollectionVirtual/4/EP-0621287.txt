<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a complex of cyclodextrin and an organic biocide, where the organic biocide has a maximum solubility in water of 0.15% (weight/volume) at a temperature of 25@C, to a process for preparing the complex and to the use of the complex for preserving aqueous solutions. The invention furthermore relates to a diagnostic assay solution which contains at least one complex according to the invention, and a preserved diagnostic assay kit comprising assay reagents and at least one complex according to the invention.
</ABSTRACT>
