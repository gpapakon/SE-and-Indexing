<INVENTION-TITLE>
Storage device for compact discs and similar.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G11B3304	G11B3304	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G11B	G11B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G11B33	G11B33	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A description is given of a storage device (1) for compact discs (3), which are packed in cases (2) which are similar to acrylic glass (flexiglass) and are in card index box form. In this arrangement, these cases (2) can be fitted into holding elements (4), which comprise a frame (5) and an inner slide-in unit (6), and the holding elements (4) themselves can be displaced via swivel pins (11) in the cavity (14) of a fixed T-shaped profile (13).
