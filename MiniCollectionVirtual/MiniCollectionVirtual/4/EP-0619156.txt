<INVENTION-TITLE>
Wire-mesh web and method for its production.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B21F2700	B21F2700	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B21F	B21F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B21F27	B21F27	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Wire-mesh web consisting of at least one decorative-wire strap (3), which consists of two essentially wave-shaped wires (10, 10') which are bent over one another with an offset of half a wave length in each case and are welded together like tresses, and further comprising mesh-web strips (1, 14) extending on both sides of the decorative-wire strap and consisting of longitudinal and transverse wires which cross one another at right angles and are welded together, the transverse wires (6, 6') of the mesh-web strips extending on both sides of each decorative-wire strap in each case extending only as far as the facing wave crests of each decorative-wire strap and being welded to these, allowing the wire-mesh web (38) to be stretched in the transverse direction in the region of the decorative-wire straps (3).
