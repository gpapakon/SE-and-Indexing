<INVENTION-TITLE>
Cover device for blocking the access to storage drives of a data processing unit
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G11B3300	G11B3302	G11B3300	G11B3302	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G11B	G11B	G11B	G11B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G11B33	G11B33	G11B33	G11B33	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The cover device consists of a screen which covers the opening for insertion into the storage drives, can be moved horizontally, consists of at least two wall elements (9, 10) which can be pushed into each other like a gate, and can be locked in the closed state using a pivoted lever lock which can be operated by a key.

<CLAIMS>
Cover device for blocking access to storage
drives having portable data carriers which can be

supplied or removed via the front panel (1) of the
associated data processing unit, by making use of a

sliding cover which covers the insertion opening for the
storage drives, can be moved horizontally and can be

locked in the closed state by means of a key-operated
catch lever lock (13, 14), characterized in that the

sliding cover comprises at least two wall elements (9,
10) which can be slid into one another in the manner of

a slotted link.
Cover device according to Claim 1, characterized
in that the wall elements (9, 10) in each case have

interengaging rail-like guide elements.
Cover device according to Claim 1 or 2, characterized
in that interengaging stop elements (12) or

driver hooks (11) are provided on the upper and/or lower
edge of the wall elements (9,10).
Cover device according to one of the preceding
claims, characterized in that the sliding cover is

arranged behind a front cover (5) covering the front
panel (1) of the data processing unit with the exception

of the insertion openings for the storage drives.
Cover device according to one of the preceding
claims, characterized in that a gripping element is

provided on the outer wall element (9).
Cover device according to Claim 5, characterized
in that the gripping element is constructed as a gripping

strip (12) extending perpendicular to the plane of the 
wall element (9).
Cover device according to one of the preceding
claims, characterized in that the outer wall element (9)

has an inwardly directed locking hook (13) which engages
in a locking element (14) of the catch lever lock when

the sliding cover is closed.
</CLAIMS>
