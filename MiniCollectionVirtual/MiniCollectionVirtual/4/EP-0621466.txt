<INVENTION-TITLE>
System for capacitive level measurement and its use.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01F2300	G01F2300	G01F2322	G01F2326	G01N2722	G01N2722	G01R2726	G01R2726	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The system for capacitive level measurement of electrically conductive filling materials has a measuring apparatus which determines, via a supply lead, a capacitance between a measuring electrode and a counter electrode formed by the filling material as a measure of the level to be determined. The supply lead and the measuring electrode are respectively formed in this case from a coaxial cable.
