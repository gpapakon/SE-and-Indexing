<ABSTRACT>
The composition is characterised in that it contains an effective amount of at least one aqueous-glycolic extract of Chlorella, Scenedesmus and Spiruline algae and at least one extract of green coffee. The invention also relates to the use, for the preparation of cosmetic or pharmaceutical compositions intended for treatment of the skin or of the hair system and for anti-inflammatory treatments, of an aqueous-glycolic extract of Chlorella, Scenedesmus and Spiruline algae in combination with an extract of green coffee.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
A cosmetic or pharmaceutical composition for topic
application, characterised in that it contains an effective

quantity of at least one hydroglycolic extract of chlorella,
scenedesmus and spiruline algae and at least one extract of

unroasted coffee.
A cosmetic or pharmaceutical composition according to Claim
1, characterised in that the ratio by weight of the algae

extract to the unroasted coffee extract is comprised between
1:1 and 30:1, preferably between 2:1 and 20:1 and even more

preferably between 3:1 and 15:1.
A composition according to one or other of Claims 1 and 2,
characterised in that it comprises from 0.01% to 8%,

preferably from 0.02 to 5% and even more preferably from
0.05 to 5% by weight of unroasted coffee extract.
A composition according to any one of Claims 1 to 3,
characterised in that it contains from 0.1 to 15%,

preferably from 0.2 to 12% and even more preferably from 0.5
to 8% by weight of algae extract.
A composition according to any one of Claims 1 to 4,
characterised in that the extract of algae is obtained by

hydroglycolic extraction from a mixture comprising at least
5% and preferably at least 10% of each of the chlorella,

scenedesmus and spiruline algae.
A composition according to any one of Claims 1 to 5,
characterised in that the green coffee extract has a

caffeine concentration in excess of 40%, preferably greater
than 45% and even more preferably greater than 50% in

relation to the dry matter of the extract. 
A composition according to any one of Claims 1 to 6,
characterised in that it further contains from 0.01% to 6%

by weight tocopherols, preferably constituted by tocopherol
linoleate.
A composition according to any one of Claims 1 to 7,
characterised in that it further contains from 0.001% to

0.1% of a complexing agent, preferably constituted by sodic
EDTA.
A composition according to any one of Claims 1 to 8,
characterised in that it takes the form of a lotion, an

emulsion, a milk, microspheres, microgranules, a cream, a
gel, a balm or an aerosol bomb, liposomes or vesicles of the

ionic or non-ionic type.
Use of a hydroglycolic extract of chlorella, scenedesmus and
spiruline algae in association with an unroasted coffee

extract for the preparation of cosmetic or pharmaceutical
compositions intended for treating the skin or capillary

system and for anti-inflammatory treatments.
A method for the cosmetic treatment of the skin and
capillary system, characterised in that an effective

quantity of a hydroglycolic extract of chlorella,
scenedesmus and spiruline algae in association with an

unroasted coffee extract is applied to the skin or the
capillary system.
</CLAIMS>
