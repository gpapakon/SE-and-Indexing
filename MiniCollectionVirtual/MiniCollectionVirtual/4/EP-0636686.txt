<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a washing composition containing, in an aqueous medium suitable for washing keratinous fibres, at least one anionic surface-active agent containing one or a number of sulphonate groups, at least one surface-active agent from the betaine family and at least one polymer derived from chitosan, the anionic surface-active agent/betaine surface-active agent ratio being greater than 0.7.
</ABSTRACT>
