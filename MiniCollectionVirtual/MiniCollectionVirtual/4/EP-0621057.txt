<INVENTION-TITLE>
Emulsifier for portable fire extinguishers
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A62D100	A62D102	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A62D	A62D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A62D1	A62D1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
An emulsifier according to the invention consists of an aqueous solution containing by weight:     (a) 1 to 15% of at least one fluorinated betaine   (b) 1 to 20% of at least one fluorinated amine oxide   (c) 2 to 8% of at least one alkyl glucoside (d) 2 to 10% of at least one other anionic, non-ionic or amphoteric hydrocarbon surface-active agent (e) 20 to 50% of at least one mono-or diethylene (or propylene) glycol monoalkyl ether, the content of fluorinated compounds (a) and (b) being between 5 and 25% and the weight ratio (a + b)/(c + d) being between 1 and 2.
</ABSTRACT>
