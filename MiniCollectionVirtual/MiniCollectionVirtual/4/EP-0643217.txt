<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for fuel supply to an internal combustion engine and engine for carrying out the method.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F02D4100	F02D4100	F02D4106	F02D4106	F02D4112	F02D4112	F02M2508	F02M2508	F02M3300	F02M3304	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F02D41	F02D41	F02D41	F02D41	F02D41	F02D41	F02M25	F02M25	F02M33	F02M33	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Method for supplying with fuel an internal combustion engine (1) of the multi-cylinder type with controlled fuel injection and including a controlled system for recovering fuel vapours (11), characterised in that during predetermined operating phases of the engine, fuel injection is stopped for a given period of time while the fuel vapours trapped in the said recovery system (11) are recycled into the inlet circuit (2) of the engine (1).
