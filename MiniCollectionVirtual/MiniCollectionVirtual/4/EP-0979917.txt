<INVENTION-TITLE>
Hinge
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E05D704	E05D704	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E05D	E05D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>E05D7	E05D7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The position of the hinge arm (4) relative to the intermediate member is adjustable relative to the depth and width of the furniture without tools through displacement devices which each have a handle for adjusting the position. The hinge arm and/or further intermediate member are held with positive locking by the displacement devices. The handle is formed by a wheel (5, 6) mounted inside the hinge arm and projecting through an opening in one web of the arm.
</ABSTRACT>
