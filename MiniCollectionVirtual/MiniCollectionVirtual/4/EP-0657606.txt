<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an anti-panic lock which can be operated by a key and/or by a handle, with a split follower (5, 6), change mechanism, tumbler, latch (4) and latch bolt, which is to be opened by means of an electrical or electronic device, if appropriate by means of a magnetic or chip card. A mechanical device ensures that the outer handle, when loaded in the locking direction of the lock, does not strike (block) lock furniture parts, but runs against a fixed stop (18), on which it cannot destroy, damage or wear anything which could impair the functioning of the lock.
