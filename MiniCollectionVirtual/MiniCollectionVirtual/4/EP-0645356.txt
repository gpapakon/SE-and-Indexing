<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the preparation of cyclohexene by partial hydrogenation of benzene.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C07C500	C07C511	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C07C	C07C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C07C5	C07C5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The process is carried out with hydrogen in the presence of water and ruthenium catalysts at elevated temperature in the liquid phase by employing ZnO as the catalyst support and adding a base to the aqueous phase.
</ABSTRACT>
