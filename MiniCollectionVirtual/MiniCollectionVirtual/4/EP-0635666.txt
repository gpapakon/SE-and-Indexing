<INVENTION-TITLE>
Thermally insulated wall transition for a pipe or similar.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F16L500	F16L500	F16L502	F16L510	F16L5900	F16L5916	F24F1300	F24F1320	F24H912	F24H912	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The heating-element pipe (radiator pipe) (3) is led through an opening (2) in the wall (1) of a heating appliance, the opening enclosing the heating-element pipe and leaving a spacing around it. The pipe bears a sleeve (4) which is pressed into the opening and, at one end, is connected in a frictionally locking manner to the pipe via a pinch-type connection (9). Furthermore, the sleeve bears, at its free end, an outwardly directed flange (6) which fits into a depression (7) in the heat-insulating wall (1).
