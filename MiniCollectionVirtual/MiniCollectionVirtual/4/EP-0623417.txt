<INVENTION-TITLE>
Stamping die, method of manufacture and tool for use in carrying out said process.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B23P900	B23P902	B23P1540	B23P1540	B24B1900	B24B1920	B26F138	B26F144	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B23P	B23P	B23P	B23P	B24B	B24B	B26F	B26F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B23P9	B23P9	B23P15	B23P15	B24B19	B24B19	B26F1	B26F1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A stamping die (1) of sheet steel has at least one web (6) projecting from the front side (5) and having in each case a stamping edge (7), in which arrangement the normal distance of the stamping edge (7) from the rear side (4) of the stamping die (1) varies by a desired size (h) with a tolerance of at most 0.04 mm. At both flanks (30) of the stamping edge (7), one recess (31) or bulged portion (32) each, produced by pressing, is arranged directly on this stamping edge (7). If need be, the rear side (4) of the stamping die (1), in at least one area of the webs (6), can have material removed from it or can be filled with material.
