<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device and method for determining the suitable clamping torque of bolt connections.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device and a method for determining suitable clamping torques of screw connections having self-tapping screws. While known devices make possible the determination, display and storage of maximum values by means of a torque pick-up and a computer connected thereto, besides the maximum value which represents the over-torque MÃœ, a relative maximum value ME lying before the latter, which must be applied by a screwdriver for cutting the thread grooves, is also recorded in the invention. The device and the method according to the invention determine the difference between these two torque values and permit, in an advantageous refinement, both a statistical mean-value formation from a series of multiple measurements and also the automatic calculation of a recommended clamping moment for a screwdriver.
</ABSTRACT>
