<INVENTION-TITLE>
Foudry sand binder.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B22C116	B22C118	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B22C	B22C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B22C1	B22C1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a foundry sand binder based on alkali-activated alkaline earth metal smectites; the binder is characterised in that the alkaline earth metal smectite has been activated by an inorganic ion exchanger in the alkali form. The activation is preferably carried out using a zeolite or a phyllosilicate in the alkali form.
</ABSTRACT>
