<INVENTION-TITLE>
METHOD FOR BENDING AN ASSEMBLY OF METAL SECTIONS AND THIN METAL SHEETS.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B21D3900	B21D3900	F16B1700	F16B1700	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B21D	B21D	F16B	F16B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B21D39	B21D39	F16B17	F16B17	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for bending an assembly of at least one metal section (2) and at least one thin metal sheet (1), comprising the following steps: assembling in flat conditions the thin metal sheet (1) with the section (2) along a longitudinal axis (10) of the assembly, so that the section (2) is free to slide with respect to the thin metal sheet (1) along the longitudinal axis of the assembly (10) while being blocked with respect to the thin metal sheet (1) in any other direction; and bending the sliding flat assembly (3) about a bending axis which is tranverse to the longitudinal axis of the assembly (10).
</ABSTRACT>
