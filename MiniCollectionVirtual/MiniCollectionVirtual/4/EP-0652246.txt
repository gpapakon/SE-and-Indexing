<INVENTION-TITLE>
Base-catalysed preparation of an hydrogen containing polyorganosiloxane
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
In the process, a mixture of the components     A) Organosilicon compounds selected from     A1) organosilanes and   A2) organosiloxanes with the proviso that component A contains at least 0.01-4 mol of alkoxy groups per mol of silicon atoms and that at least some of the organosilicon compounds of component A contain Si-bonded hydrogen B) at least 0.5 mol of water per mol of alkoxy groups in component A, optionally   C) a water-miscible solvent, in the presence of D) ammonia or primary or secondary C1-C4-alkylamine or a compound which liberates ammonia or primary C1-C4-alkylamine with water.
</ABSTRACT>
