<INVENTION-TITLE>
Substituted mono- and bipyridylmethyl derivatives as angiotensin II antagonist.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The substituted mono- and bipyridyl derivatives are prepared by reaction of substituted pyridine halides with phenylboronic acids. They are suitable as active ingredients in medicaments for the treatment of arterial hypertension and atherosclerosis.
</ABSTRACT>
