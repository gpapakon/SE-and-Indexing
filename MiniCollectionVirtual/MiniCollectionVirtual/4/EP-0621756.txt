<INVENTION-TITLE>
DUAL FUNCTION TOOTHBRUSH.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A46B1500	A46B1500	B65D3524	B65D3528	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The toothbrush of the invention enables remaining toothpaste to be gathered in the end of a deformable tube. The toothbrush has a slot (1) for receiving the flattened end (2) of a tube, the two sides (3) of which are then flattened.
</ABSTRACT>
