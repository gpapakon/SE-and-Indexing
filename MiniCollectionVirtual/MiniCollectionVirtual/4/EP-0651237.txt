<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for determining the temperature of an object.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01K1500	G01K1500	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01K	G01K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01K15	G01K15	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In a method for determining the temperature of an object using a temperature sensor, in particular in the case of motor vehicles, a further temperature sensor is assigned to the same or to another object. The output signal of the further temperature sensor is taken into account if the temperatures to be expected are at least approximately equal for both temperature sensors and the actual temperature of the object or of both objects is derived by forming the average from the temperatures determined by the two sensors.
