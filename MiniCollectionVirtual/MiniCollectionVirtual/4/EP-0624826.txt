<ABSTRACT>
A process for the production of relief images comprising the following steps:   (A) coating of a substrate with a photopolymerisable composition containing a polymer (a) and a photoinitiator (b), (B) imagewise exposure of the coated substrate with UV radiation in the i-line region (about 360-370 nm),   (C) removal of the unexposed areas with the aid of a solvent, and   (D) conditioning of the exposed and developed material, characterised in that the polymer (a) used is a polyimide precursor containing recurring structural units of the formula (I),
