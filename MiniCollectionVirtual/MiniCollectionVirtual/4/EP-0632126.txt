<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for working up used break and hydraulic fluids.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C10M17500	C10M17500	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C10M	C10M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C10M175	C10M175	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In a process for treating used brake fluids and hydraulic fluids based on glycol ethers and glycol ether borates by distillation, the fluids are first dehydrated in vacuo if necessary, then if appropriate the low boilers comprising glycols and glycol ethers and a middle fraction comprising long-chain glycol ethers are separated off by thin-layer distillation from a residue containing glycol ether borates and then the residue containing the glycol ether borates is distilled by short-path distillation to separate off the glycol ether borates.
</ABSTRACT>
