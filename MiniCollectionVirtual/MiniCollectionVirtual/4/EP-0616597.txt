<INVENTION-TITLE>
FEEDER FOR A SHEET-PAPER PROCESSING MACHINE.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65H308	B65H308	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65H	B65H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65H3	B65H3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A feeder for a sheet-paper processing machine with vacuum lifters (5) to lift the sheets of paper from the stack, means for transferring the lifted sheet and taking it from the raised position above the stack to the processing unit, with potential power permanently acting downwards on the vacuum lifters towards the stack, means (3) for controllably raising the lifters cyclically against the potential power, magnetic means to hold the lifters (5) in the raised position, and means for controlling the magnetic holding force of the magnetic means for holding the vacuum lifter (5).
</ABSTRACT>
