<INVENTION-TITLE>
Apparatus and process for thermal projection of thermosetting polymer material.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B05B702	B05B708	B05B716	B05B720	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B05B	B05B	B05B	B05B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B05B7	B05B7	B05B7	B05B7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The device includes, in front of the end (3) of the spray nozzle (1), at least one pair of little nozzles (12) facing each other and projecting a flux (18) of gas onto the spray flux plume (15-17), thus making it possible to achieve a high spray rate of thermosetting material.
