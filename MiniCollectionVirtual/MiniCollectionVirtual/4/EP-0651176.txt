Hydraulically-damped support.

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a hydraulically damped support, especially an engine support (mount) for motor vehicles, with a fluid-filled working chamber (4) surrounded by a rubber-elastic wall (1) and a plunger plate (6) which is fixed on the engine-support plate (2) within the working chamber (1). To reduce the dynamic stiffness of this support over a wide frequency range, the invention proposes that the plunger plate (6) should be bordered by a flexible rubber cushion (10) which extends radially outwards and that the resonant frequency of the rubber-cushion system should lie above that of the plunger system.
