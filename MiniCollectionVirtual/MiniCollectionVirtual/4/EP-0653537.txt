<INVENTION-TITLE>
All-glass partition wall element.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E05D1506	E05D1506	E06B302	E06B302	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E05D	E05D	E06B	E06B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>E05D15	E05D15	E06B3	E06B3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a full-glass partition wall element for a displaceable partition wall, having a glass panel (4) and at least one fastening profile (11; 12; 19; 20) which is fitted on a border (6; 7; 8; 10) of the glass panel (4) and is intended for securing at least one functional element (13; 14; 21), for example a load-bearing member (13; 14) for the displaceable mounting of the partition wall element (2) in a guide rail (18) or a securing element (21) for arresting the partition wall element (2). In the case of said partition wall element, the fastening profile (11; 12; 19; 20) extends over only part of the border (6; 7; 8; 10) of the glass panel (4).
