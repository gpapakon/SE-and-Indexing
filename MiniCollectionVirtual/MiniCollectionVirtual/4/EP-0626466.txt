<INVENTION-TITLE>
Cup-tappets, method for coating of cup-tappets and use of plasma spraying for coating of cup-tappets.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C23C406	C23C406	C23C412	C23C412	F01L114	F01L114	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C23C	C23C	C23C	C23C	F01L	F01L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C23C4	C23C4	C23C4	C23C4	F01L1	F01L1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In a process for coating cup tappets, a powder mixture is applied as coating (25) by the plasma-spraying process to a surface (26) of the cup tappet consisting essentially of an aluminium alloy. The powder mixture contains molybdenum (Mo) and molybdenum trioxide (MoO3), the oxygen content of the mixture being between 2% and 8%.
