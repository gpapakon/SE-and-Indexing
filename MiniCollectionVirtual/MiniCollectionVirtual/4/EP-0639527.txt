<INVENTION-TITLE>
Loading frame with movable coupling-elements, particularly twist-locks.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B66C162	B66C166	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B66C	B66C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B66C1	B66C1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A superstructure frame (11) with telescopically adjustable supporting columns (12) is described in which, via a control shaft (13) arranged in the supporting columns (12), the coupling elements (20) between superstructure frame (11) and container (21) - open-top container or flat - can also be actuated without an additional energy source solely via the spreader pivot (19) (Fig. 3).
