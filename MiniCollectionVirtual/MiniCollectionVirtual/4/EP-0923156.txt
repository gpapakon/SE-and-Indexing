<CLAIMS>
A microstrip antenna including:

a dielectric substrate (2) having a bottom surface (S1), a top surface
(S2) and an edge surface (S3),
a conductive ground plane (4) on said bottom surface,
a conductive patch (6) on said top surface,
two short-circuit conductors (C2, C12) on said edge surface and
connecting said patch to said conductive ground (4), and
connecting conductors for transmitting a signal between said antenna
and a signal processing unit; said antenna being 
characterized in that
 the
connecting conductors include a coplanar line having a first section (F4, C1,

F14) on the top face of the substrate and a second section (F5, C3, F15) on
the edge surface and extending the first section with no significant impedance

discontinuity, and including two ground conductors constituted by the two
short-circuit conductors (C2, C12).
An antenna according to claim 1, including a resonant structure including:

said dielectric substrate (2), said substrate having two mutually
opposed main surfaces extending in directions defined in said antenna and

constituting horizontal directions (DL and DT), said two surfaces respectively
constituting said bottom surface (S1) and said top surface (S2), another

direction being further defined in said antenna at an angle to each of said
horizontal directions, said other direction constituting a vertical direction (DV),

said edge surface (S3) containing said vertical direction,
a conductive bottom layer on said bottom surface and constituting said
ground (4) of said antenna,
a conductive top layer on an area of said top surface above said
ground to constitute said patch (6), said patch having a configuration, edges,

a length and a width, said length and said width extending in two of said
horizontal directions constituting a longitudinal direction (DL) and a transverse

direction (DT), respectively, said edge surface further containing an edge (10)
of said patch, said edge extending in said transverse direction (DT), and
said short-circuit conductors (C2, C12), said conductor extending in
said vertical direction (DV) and imposing at least approximately on said

resonant structure a quarter-wave type resonance,

   said antenna further including a coupling line adapted to couple a
traveling wave propagating in said line and said resonance of the resonant 

structure, said line including:

a main conductor connected to said patch (6) at an internal connecting
point (18), and
said ground conductors parallel to and alongside said main conductor,

   said antenna being 
characterized in that
 said main conductor of the
coupling line includes a vertical section (C3) alongside said short-circuit

conductor (C2, C12) and constituting said second section, said ground
conductors of said line including a vertical section consisting of said short-circuit

conductors to enable said resonant structure to be connected to said
signal processing unit (8) by means of a vertical line including said vertical

sections of said conductors forming part of said coupling line.
An antenna according to claim 2, 
characterized in that
 said main conductor
of the coupling line further includes a horizontal coupling strip (C1) formed in

said top conductive layer and extending in said longitudinal direction (DL) to
connect said vertical section (C3) of said conductor to said internal

connecting point (18), said horizontal coupling strip being separated from said
patch by two longitudinal lat
eral slots (F4, F14) on respective edges of said
strip, said ground conductors of said line further including a horizontal section

consisting of said patch on either side of said coupling strip, said horizontal
coupling strip and said horizontal section of the main conductor constituting a

horizontal coplanar line,

   said antenna including a vertical conductive layer on areas of said edge
surface (S3), said short-circuit being a composite short-circuit including two of

said short-circuit conductors (C2, C12), said two short-circuit conductors
comprising two vertical short-circuit strips forming part of said vertical

conductive layer on respective opposite sides of said vertical section (C3) of
the main conductor of the coupling line which comprises a vertical coupling

strip which is also part of said vertical conductive layer and is separated from
said two short-circuit conductors by respective vertical lateral slots (F5, F15)

so that said vertical line section constitutes a vertical coplanar line connected
to said horizontal coplanar line with no significant impedance discontinuity.
An antenna according to claim 3, 
characterized in that
 said vertical
coplanar line (C2, F5, C3, F15, C12) is formed over only a fraction of said

width of the patch (6). 
An antenna according to any one of claims 2 to 4, 
characterized in that
 it is
symmetrical about a plane passing through an axis (A) of symmetry of said

patch (6) in said vertical direction (DV).
A radio communication device including:

an antenna according to any one of claims 1 to 5, and
a signal processing unit (8) connected to said antenna by said
connecting conductors (C3, C2, C12).
</CLAIMS>
