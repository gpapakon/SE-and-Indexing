<INVENTION-TITLE>
Cleaning mitten.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47L1316	A47L1318	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47L	A47L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47L13	A47L13	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A cleaning mitten is shown, having two pieces of textile material (2, 2') attached to one another, from each of which pile threads (4) or pile fibres protrude towards a surface to be cleaned, the pieces of textile material being joined to one another at all boundary edges, with the exception of an opening region (6), and the pieces of textile material (2, 2') tapering in width in a boundary region (8) lying opposite the opening region (6).
