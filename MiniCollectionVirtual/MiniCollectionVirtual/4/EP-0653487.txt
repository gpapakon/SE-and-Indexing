<INVENTION-TITLE>
Serum and protein-free growing cells.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C12N500	C12N500	C12N502	C12N502	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C12N	C12N	C12N	C12N	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C12N5	C12N5	C12N5	C12N5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Substrate-, serum- and protein-independent eukaryotic cells which are specifically suitable as host cells for genetic engineering and biotechnology and which have never been exposed to a specifically mutagenic substance are described. A process for the preparation thereof is also described.
</ABSTRACT>
