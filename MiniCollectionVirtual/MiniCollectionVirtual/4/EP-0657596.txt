<INVENTION-TITLE>
Self-supporting panel.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E04B286	E04B286	E04C238	E04C238	E04C240	E04C240	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E04B	E04B	E04C	E04C	E04C	E04C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>E04B2	E04B2	E04C2	E04C2	E04C2	E04C2	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Improved self-supporting panel (1) including two sub-panels (2) provided with means for joining them together, giving it its self-supporting nature, each of these sub-panels (2) having a metal meshwork (4) and a sheet of thermo-acoustic insulant (5), the space between the sub-panels being filled with reinforced concrete (11), and the assembly being floated (rendered) on at least one of its faces. Capable of application in the building trade.
