<INVENTION-TITLE>
TOOTHED WHEEL WITH A VARIABLE NUMBER OF TEETH.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F16H5532	F16H5554	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F16H	F16H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F16H55	F16H55	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention concerns a toothed wheel designed for use in physically interlocking traction means with a variable number of teeth, the wheel rim being made up of tooth elements (3, 4, 5, 8, 9). These tooth elements are in turn made up of insert elements (3, 4, 5) and switch elements (8, 9). The teeth (6) on the insert elements (3, 4, 5) lie permanently within the contact diameter (7), whereas the teeth (19, 21) on the switch elements (8, 9) can be switched between a stable position within the contact diameter (7) and a stable position outside the contact diameter (7). The toothed wheel is simple to manufacture.
 
</ABSTRACT>
