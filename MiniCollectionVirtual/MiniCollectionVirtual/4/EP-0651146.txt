<INVENTION-TITLE>
Exhaust brake with gap position.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F02D900	F02D906	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F02D	F02D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F02D9	F02D9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The brake has a pivoting throttle flap (2) located in a section of exhaust pipe and mounted on a shaft (3). In the throttle open position the shaft has a locked position via a lever arm (15) on the shaft contacting a striker (20). The striker adjusts between locked and free positions via an additional piston-cylinder unit (18), which has an additional air pressure connection.
</ABSTRACT>
