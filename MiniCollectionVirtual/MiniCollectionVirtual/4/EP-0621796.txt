<INVENTION-TITLE>
FOAM-SPRAYING DEVICE.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A62C500	A62C502	B05B700	B05B700	B05B724	B05B724	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A62C	A62C	B05B	B05B	B05B	B05B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A62C5	A62C5	B05B7	B05B7	B05B7	B05B7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Proposed is a foam-spraying device (10) in which the air/liquid mixture to be foamed is passed, before it reaches a sieve (52), through an element containing perforations in order to ensure homogeneous distribution of the air trapped in the foam produced.
</ABSTRACT>
