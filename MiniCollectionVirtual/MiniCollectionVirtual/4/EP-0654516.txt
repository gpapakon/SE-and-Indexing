<ABSTRACT>
Bentonite liquid media are provided for borehole flushing and cementation of seismic horizontal bores, which media contain not only bentonite and cements but, in addition, selected polysaccharides.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
The use of liquid media containing water and bentonite 
as well as cements comprising xanthane (XC) 

and/or hydroxyethylcellulose (HEC) as a drilling 
fluid and back-filling medium for sinking seismic 

flat boreholes. 
The use of liquid media according to claim 1, wherein 
the XC content is from 0.5 g/l to 6 g/l, particularly 

from 1 g/l to 4 g/l of liquid medium. 
The use of liquid media according to claim 1, wherein 
the HEC content is from 1 g/l to 8 g/l, particularly 

from 2 g/l to 6 g/l of liquid medium. 
The use of liquid media according to any one of the 
preceding claims, wherein one or more additional 

polymeric saccharide(s), particularly carboxymethylhydroxyethylcellulose 
(CMHEC) and carboxymethylcellulose 

(CMC), is (are) added. 
The use of liquid media according to any one of the 
preceding claims, wherein the liquid media comprise 

XC plus CMHEC, HEC and/or CMC. 
</CLAIMS>
