<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process and apparatus for titrating
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01N2742	G01N2744	G01N3116	G01N3116	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01N	G01N	G01N	G01N	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01N27	G01N27	G01N31	G01N31	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A differential component is obtained on the basis of the progress of the addition of the titrate agent incrementally. In the process for titration of substances by step-wise addition of each individual amount or increment of titrate agent at specific time periods (t4, t5, t6), obtaining of the end- product (EP) is monitored during the course of the reaction and the addition of the titrate agent is controlled, so that the titration speed represents determined parameters of individual samples and time periods (t4, t5, t6). An Independent claim is included for an apparatus for carrying out the process.
</ABSTRACT>
