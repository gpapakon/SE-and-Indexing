<INVENTION-TITLE>
Unsupported self-adhesive tapes with different adhesiveness on either side and method for their manufacture.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08F26500	C08F26506	C08F29900	C08F29900	C09J406	C09J406	C09J700	C09J700	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08F	C08F	C08F	C08F	C09J	C09J	C09J	C09J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C08F265	C08F265	C08F299	C08F299	C09J4	C09J4	C09J7	C09J7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Adhesive tape with different adhesion on each side comprises unsupported tape produced by UV irradiation from a mixt. of solvent-free COOH gp. contg. UV-crosslinkable polymers (I) with polyfunctional radiation-activated (meth)acrylates or acrylate oligomers (II) and a photoinitiator, spread out as a sheet. Also claimed is a process for the prodn. of such adhesive tape, by mixing the above components, spreading the mixt. as a sheet and irradiating with UV. The irradiated side is pref. more adhesive than the other. (I) is a copolymer of 40-90 wt/% (4-12C alkyl) (meth)acrylate(s), pref. butyl, pentyl, hexyl, heptyl, octyl, 2-ethylhexyl, iso-octyl, 2-methyl-heptyl, nonyl, decyl and/or dodecyl (meth)acrylate, 5-40 w.% (1-3C alkyl) (meth)acrylate(s), pref. methyl, ethyl and/or propyl (meth)acrylate, 1-30 wt/% hydroxy (meth)acrylate(s), pref. 2-hydroxyethyl, 2- or 3-hydroxypropyl and/or 4-hydroxybutyl (meth)acrylate, 0.05-25 wt.% vinylcarboxylic acid, pref. (meth)acrylic,~b-acryloyloxypropionic, vinylacetic, trichloroacrylic, dimethylacrylic, crotonic, fumaric, aconitic and/or itaconic acid and 0.01-5 wt.% photoinitiator(s), pref. of the acetophenone and/or benzophenone type. (II) are polyfunctional acrylates, urethane-acrylates, polyester-acrylates and/or polyether-acrylates, in amts. of 5-25 wt.% w.r.t. polymer (I).
</ABSTRACT>
