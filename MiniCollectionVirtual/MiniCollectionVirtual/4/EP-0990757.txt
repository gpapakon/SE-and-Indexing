<INVENTION-TITLE>
Motor vehicles device for user identification
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60R2500	B60R2500	G07C900	G07C900	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60R	B60R	G07C	G07C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60R25	B60R25	G07C9	G07C9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The user identification device uses a hand-held transponder (6) communicating with an onboard device for transfer of user identification signals (7), with an ultrasonic transceiver (4), positioned within the automobile passenger space, cooperating with the transponder for identifying its position within or outside the automobile, e.g. for releasing a vehicle disabling device only when the transponder is within the automobile.
</ABSTRACT>
