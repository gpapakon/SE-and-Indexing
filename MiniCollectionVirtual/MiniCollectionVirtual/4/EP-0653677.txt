Colour photographic material containing a coupler.

<ABSTRACT>
A colour-photographic recording material having at least one silver-halide emulsion layer which has been sensitised for the red spectral region, and a cyan coupler of the formula I below. The cyan coupler of the formula I is distinguished by steep gradation, a high maximum colour density and a high colour yield.
