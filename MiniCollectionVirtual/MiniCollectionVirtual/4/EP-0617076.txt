<INVENTION-TITLE>
Flame protected PBT/PET-moulding compositions.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08K500	C08K500	C08K700	C08K714	C08L6700	C08L6702	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08K	C08K	C08K	C08K	C08L	C08L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C08K5	C08K5	C08K7	C08K7	C08L67	C08L67	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to flameproofed polyester moulding compositions based on polybutylene terephthalate (PBT) and polyethylene terephthalate (PET) which contain a halogenated flameproofing agent and antimony pentoxide as flameproofing synergists.
</ABSTRACT>
