<INVENTION-TITLE>
SEMICONDUCTOR LASER DEVICE
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H01S500	H01S500	H01S5028	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H01S	H01S	H01S	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H01S5	H01S5	H01S5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In a semiconductor laser device of the type which 
includes at least one laminate of a first dielectric layer (2) and 

a second dielectric layer (3) on at least one of the two facets 
of a resonator (11) and in which the refractive index of the 

first dielectric layer is lower than that of the second dielectric 
layer, the improvement wherein the second dielectric layer 

consists of an amorphous material containing silicon and 
hydrogen at its essential constituent elements. 
</ABSTRACT>
