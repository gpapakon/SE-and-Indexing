<INVENTION-TITLE>
Use of trimetazidine for the preparation of medicaments for the treatment of troubles due to the nephrotoxicity of cyclosporin A
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61K31495	A61P1312	A61K31495	A61P1300	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61K	A61P	A61K	A61P	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
Use, for the preparation of medicaments intended for the treatment of troubles due to the therapeutic use of immunosuppressants, of trimetazidine or of one of the physiologically tolerable salts thereof.
