<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for centering of steerable axles of a bogie.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B61F500	B61F538	B61F544	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B61F	B61F	B61F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B61F5	B61F5	B61F5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a method for centring the steerable axles of a bogie, characterised in that, at the entrance of a track curve, at least one of the axles (5,6) is steered towards the centre of the curve, and in that each wheel is positioned transversely relative to the track, in such a way that each of the wheels is at the same distance from its rail (7,8).
