<INVENTION-TITLE>
Portable and compact radiotelephone terminal
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H04M102	H04M102	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H04M	H04M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H04M1	H04M1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The terminal includes a body (1) holding an earpiece (2) at one end and a tray (3) at the other end incorporating a microphone or constituting a means for reflecting the sound towards a microphone. The tray can be folded between a position for receiving the sound of the voice and a folded position intended to reduce the size of the appliance when the terminal is out of use or on standby. The power supply unit (14) is inserted into a cavity (16) of a plinth (8) of the tray (3) in such a way as to form an integral part of the tray in the fitted state. Used to reduce the size in terms of thickness and to enhance ease of handling.

<CLAIMS>
Portable mobile telephone comprising a body (1), a removable battery (14), and
a flap (3) hinged to the body (1) to move between an operative position in which

it is located in front of the mouth of the user and a position folded on top of the
body (1), characterized in that said flap consists of a reflector designed to

redirect the sound towards a microphone and in that the battery (14) constitutes
said flap (3) itself.
Telephone according to claim 1, characterized in that the flap (3) includes a
short mount hinged to the body.
Telephone according to claim 1, characterized in that said battery includes
articulation means which snap onto complementary means carried by the body

(1), this articulation incorporating a system of rotary contacts.
Telephone according to any of claims 1 to 3, characterized in that the battery
(14) is chosen from nickel-metal-hydrogen type batteries and rechargeable

lithium type batteries.
</CLAIMS>
