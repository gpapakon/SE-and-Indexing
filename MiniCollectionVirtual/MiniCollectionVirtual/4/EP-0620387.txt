<INVENTION-TITLE>
Actuator for vehicle gear box.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F16H6128	F16H6128	F16H6130	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F16H	F16H	F16H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F16H61	F16H61	F16H61	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The actuator has a housing (1) in which an actuating element (2) is mounted. It can be displaced into three positions and can be coupled to a selector shaft. The housing (1a) is provided with valve-controlled connections (A,B,M) via which a pressure medium is supplied, allowing the actuating element (2) to be acted upon on two different sides. By displacing the actuating element (2a), the respective gear is selected via the selector shaft. The actuator makes it possible to automate motor-vehicle gearboxes designed for conventional manual gear changes.
