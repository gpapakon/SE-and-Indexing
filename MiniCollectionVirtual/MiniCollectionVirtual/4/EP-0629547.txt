<INVENTION-TITLE>
Partition device.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B64C100	B64C114	B64D1100	B64D1100	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B64C	B64C	B64D	B64D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B64C1	B64C1	B64D11	B64D11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The device is used for partitioning a cabin of a commercial aircraft into a working area for personnel on the flight and a payload area. The device is essentially formed from a non-transparent wall part and at least one window part. The window part is formed from two transparent covering layers arranged essentially parallel to one another. A liquid-crystal film is arranged between the covering layers and is connected to a control device to provide a control voltage for at least one transparent operating mode and at least one milky opaque operating mode.
