<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for the detection of the angular position of an internal combustion engine camshaft.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Device for detecting the angular position of a camshaft (2) of an internal combustion engine, comprising a sensor (4) of Hall effect type, intended to be mounted in a corresponding housing (5) of the engine, in order to detect the passage of a target (3) of magnetic material which is secured to the camshaft (2), characterised in that the sensor (4) cooperates with elastic thrust means (6) intended to press the sensor against the target (3) when the said sensor (4) is fitted in its housing (5).
