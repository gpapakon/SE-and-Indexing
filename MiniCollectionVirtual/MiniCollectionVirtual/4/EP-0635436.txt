<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a closure (1) having a pressure compensating device. In order always to guarantee a complete pressure compensation between the container and the surroundings with a simple construction and an operationally reliable mode of functioning, provision is made according to the invention for the pressure compensating device to have a microporous aeration and venting membrane (3) with a liquid-repellent surface.
