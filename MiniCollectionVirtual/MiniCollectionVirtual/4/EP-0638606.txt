<INVENTION-TITLE>
Aqueous emulsions containing polyvinylbutyral.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Aqueous emulsions which contain polyvinylbutyral and are prepared by mixing a nonionic emulsifier with polyvinylbutyral and optionally a plasticizing resin and optionally one or more solvents and addition of water under the action of shear forces.
</ABSTRACT>
