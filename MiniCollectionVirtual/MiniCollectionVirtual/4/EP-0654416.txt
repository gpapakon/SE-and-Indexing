<INVENTION-TITLE>
Elastic latch for container with hinged lid.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Latch for container with hinged lid, characterised in that it consists of a one-piece component which, made from an elastically deformable synthetic material, comprises a curved base (1), one of whose edges is integral with a central hook-shaped lug (3) and, on either side of this lug, with two curved arms (4) by way of supports for the flange (15) of the lid (11) when the latter is closed onto the casing (10) of the container.
