Container for cut flowers

<ABSTRACT>
A transport vase for cut flowers and bouquets of flowers consists of a water-tight wrapper (1) shaped as a pouch provided on its inner side with a liquid-storing, absorbent material (3). This type of transport vase may be produced in all sizes, shapes and colours in an extremely economical manner. The transport vase can be re-used many times and recycled.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
A storage container for cut flowers comprising a flexible sleeve 
(1) of water-impermeable sheet and a liquid reservoir, characterised 

in that the flexible sleeve is shaped like a pocket and is internally 
partly coated with absorbent material (3), which forms the liquid 

reservoir impregnatable with water and/or nutrient liquid, the upper 
rim of the sleeve (1) being drawn downwards on the inside and 

enveloping the absorbent material (3) in this area. 
A container according to claim 1, characterised in that the 
absorbent material (3) on the inside of the container is covered by 

a porous or perforated sheet (11). 
A container according to claim 1, characterised in that to the 
outside of the sleeve (1) is fitted at least one resealable adhesive 

tape (6), so that the sleeve (3) is contractable around the flower 
stalks and can be fixed with the adhesive tape (6). 
A container according to any one of claims 1 to 3, characterised 
in that a wetness indicator (7) is fitted at a suitable point on the 

outside of the sleeve (1). 
A container according to any one of claims 1 to 4, characterised 
in that to the outside of the sleeve (1) is fitted at least one pocket, 

in which can be inserted keep-fresh bags or greeting cards. 
A container according to claim 1, characterised in that the 
absorbent material is cellulose. 
A container according to claim 1, characterised in that the 
absorbent material (3) is a foam. 
</CLAIMS>
