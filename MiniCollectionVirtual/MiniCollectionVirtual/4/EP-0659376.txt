<INVENTION-TITLE>
Shoe hange.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47G2500	A47G2500	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47G	A47G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47G25	A47G25	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A shoe hanger has a carrying part (11), a connecting element (12) adjoining the latter towards the bottom, and two shoe-holding projections (13) which are attached to said connecting element via a connecting bow (17) and onto each of which a shoe can be slipped. Mounted beneath the connecting element (12) is a coupling part (15) which can be brought into carrying engagement with the carrying part (11) of a shoe hanger of the same design. The connecting element (12), the connecting bow (17) and a rectilinear part (13a) of the shoe-holding projections (13) consist of a U-shaped plastic profile.
