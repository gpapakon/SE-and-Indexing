<INVENTION-TITLE>
Binder with lever.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B42F1300	B42F1300	B42F1324	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B42F	B42F	B42F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a binder with a lever in which the lever mechanism is detachable. In accordance with the invention, the cover (10) of the binder has, on the inside face of a lateral portion (12), a track (50) formed by two parallel rails (51), into which track (50) the base plate (21) of the lever mechanism (20) is inserted, so that this mechanism can be fitted removably to the cover, and at least one projecting stud (54, 55) for locking this base plate in position once said mechanism has been fitted; the cover (10) with its track (50) and its stud or studs (54, 55) is in addition made of a single integral piece of plastic, preferably injected polypropylene.
