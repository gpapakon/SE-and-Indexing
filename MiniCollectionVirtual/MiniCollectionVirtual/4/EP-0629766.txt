<INVENTION-TITLE>
3-Piece metal interior door frame
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E06B3988	E06B396	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E06B	E06B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>E06B3	E06B3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Three-piece shaped metal door frame for interior doors, this frame being composed of two uprights (1) and of a lintel (3), inside the uprights (1) and lintel (3) and against their ends to be attached to each other, there are provided locking tenons (4) and slots (6) which fit into one another when the frame is being assembled, the upper edges (7) of the lintel (3) are provided with cutouts (8) hidden from view to allow the passage of a tool for folding back the said locking tenons (4) and thus invisibly attaching the uprights (1) to the lintel (3).

<CLAIMS>
Three part frame made of metal section for interior doors 
comprising two posts (1) and a lintel (3), characterized by the fact 

that inside the posts (1) and the lintel (3) and at their ends where 
the one is attached to the other, they are provided with locking lugs 

(4) and slots (6) which fit into one another when the frame is 
assembled, the upper edges (7) of the lintel (3) being provided with 

openings (8) concealed from view for the purpose of allowing the 
passage of a tool for bending said locking lugs (4) and thus invisibly 

joining the posts (1) to the lintel (3). 
Three part door frame as claimed in claim 1, characterized by the 
fact that the locking lugs (4) are prolongations of the posts (1) and 

are provided along the two sides of the jamb (2) of the posts (1), and 
that the slots (6) are provided in the extension strips (5) with which 

the lintel (3) is equipped. 
Three part door frame as claimed in claim 1, characterized by the 
fact that the openings (8) provided in the upper edge (7) of the 

lintel (3) are rhombic in shape. 
Three part door frame as claimed in claim 1, characterized by the 
fact that the posts (1) are extended by projecting jambs (9) which fit 

next to the ends of the lintel (3). 
</CLAIMS>
