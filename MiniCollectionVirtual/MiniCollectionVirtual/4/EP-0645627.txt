<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a measuring device, in particular for measuring the glucose level in the blood, intended to be associated with a removable sensor comprising at least one active zone. In particular, the invention relates to an ejection system (138, 142, 192) used for ejecting the removable sensor (108) from the casing (104) of the measuring device. This ejection is carried out during a final advance of the removable sensor, carried out by means of an advancing device (110, 112, 124, 130).
