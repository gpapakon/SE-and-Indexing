<INVENTION-TITLE>
Chronographic watch with two push buttons and without any crown-stem.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G04C300	G04C314	G04C2100	G04C2116	G04F800	G04F800	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G04C	G04C	G04C	G04C	G04F	G04F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G04C3	G04C3	G04C21	G04C21	G04F8	G04F8	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The chronographic watch includes two hands (1, 2) making it possible to display on request, either the time of day, or a chronometrically-determined time. Control of the functions for setting the time to the origin, for setting the watch to time and for passing from the time of day to the chronometrically-determined time is carried out by two push buttons (A, B) without any crown-stem. A second embodiment includes an alarm function which can be set to the alarm time, enabled or disabled by means of the same two push buttons.
