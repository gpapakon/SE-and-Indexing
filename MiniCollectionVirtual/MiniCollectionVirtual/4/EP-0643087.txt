<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for producing isocyanurate and uretdione groups containing polyisocyanate mixtures with low colour values
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a process for the preparation of isocyanurate and uretdione group-containing polyisocyanates of reduced colour number by catalytic oligomerisation of aliphatic and/or cycloaliphatic diisocyanates and removal of the unreacted monomeric diisocyanates, where the oligomerisation of the diisocyanates is carried out in accordance with the invention in the presence of up to 6000 ppm of carbon dioxide.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
A process for the preparation of isocyanurate-
and uretdione-containing polyisocyanate mixtures having

a reduced color number, wherein, in a first process
stage, aliphatic and/or cycloaliphatic diisocyanates are

prepared by thermal cleavage of the corresponding carbamates
and, in a second process stage, these diisocyanates,

with a carbon dioxide content of from 20 to 500 ppm are
converted into isocyanurate- and uretdione-containing

polyisocyanate mixtures by catalytic oligomerization.
A process as claimed in claim 1, wherein the
aliphatic and/or cycloaliphatic diisocyanate is

1, 6-diisocyanatohexane.
</CLAIMS>
