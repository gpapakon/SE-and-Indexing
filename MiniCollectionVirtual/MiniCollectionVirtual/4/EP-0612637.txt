<INVENTION-TITLE>
Sliding door for driving cab of a truck.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60J506	B60J506	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60J	B60J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60J5	B60J5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a sliding door (1) for a driver's cab (2) of a lorry. In order to achieve the object of developing a sliding door in such a way that it can be easily produced by retrofitting a series-produced lorry door, the door is mounted and guided by means of two vehicle-side guide rails (4, 10) and door-side rolling and sliding elements which run therein and exclusively guided by means of one guide rail (3) arranged on the outside of the vehicle.
