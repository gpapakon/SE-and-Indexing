<INVENTION-TITLE>
Setting of a jewellery stone.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A44C1700	A44C1704	C25D100	C25D100	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A44C	A44C	C25D	C25D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A44C17	A44C17	C25D1	C25D1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A jewellery article and a method to produce the jewellery article, in particular a ring, which has been produced by the galvanoforming method, consists of a basic body 2 and has at least one additional jewellery item 4. The additional jewellery item 4 is seated in a recess 5 and has an edge 6 at least partially on its circumference. The cross-section of the jewellery item 4 is at least partially reduced adjoining the edge 6 remote from the base of the recess 5. The jewellery item 4 is seated at such a depth in the recess that the reduced cross-section is located at least partially within the recess 5. The jewellery item 4 is held by an additional layer 3 which is applied by a galvanic method and reduces the cross-section of the recess 5 adjoining the edge 6 remote from the base of the recess 5.
