<INVENTION-TITLE>
Block copolyamides.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08G6900	C08G6904	C08G6926	C08G6928	C08G6936	C08G8100	C08G8100	C08L7700	C08L7700	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C08G69	C08G69	C08G69	C08G69	C08G69	C08G81	C08G81	C08L77	C08L77	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Block copolymers obtainable by mixing   A) a melt of from 15 to 85 % by weight of an amorphous polyamide (nylon) prepolymer having a viscosity number of from 40 to 100 ml/g with B) a melt of from 15 to 85 % by weight of a partially crystalline polyamide prepolymer having a viscosity number of from 40 to 100 ml/g, and C) from 0 to 70 % by weight of conventional additives and processing auxiliaries in effective amounts, followed by post-condensation in the solid phase, where the percentages by weight of components A) to C) add up to 100 %.
 
</ABSTRACT>
