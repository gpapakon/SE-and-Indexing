<INVENTION-TITLE>
Camouflage device.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F41H300	F41H302	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F41H	F41H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F41H3	F41H3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a device for camouflaging an object, a vehicle in particular, of the type comprising a multi-layer camouflage cover, effective in the visible, infra-red and radar wave bands, characterised in that the camouflage cover comprises, successively superimposed starting from the camouflaged object: at least a first insulating later (1), at least a second metallised layer (2) for reflecting, on the one hand, infra-red waves towards the object and, on the other hand, radar waves towards the exterior, at least a third layer made of plastic material (3) and at least a fourth outer layer (4) formed by a leaf net. Application to the camouflaging of military vehicles.
