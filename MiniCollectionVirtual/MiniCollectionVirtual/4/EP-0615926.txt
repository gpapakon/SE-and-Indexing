<INVENTION-TITLE>
Automatic storage installation of objects in cases and dispensing of these objects.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47F300	A47F3026	B65G1137	B65G1137	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47F	A47F	B65G	B65G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47F3	A47F3	B65G1	B65G1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Automatic installation for storing objects, bearing an identification mark, in cases and for dispensing these objects at the request of a user, characterised in that it comprises   - an assembly (3) of cases each bearing an identification mark, each case being intended to receive only a single object, the assembly being housed in a normally closed chamber (1), - at least one station (5, 6) for access to the said installation by a user, the said station being equipped with a dialogue interface (9, 11) and with a detector for identification marks, - a means (R) which is movable in the closed chamber and transfers objects between the access station (5, 6) and the cases (3), - a means (30) for managing the displacement of the said transfer means (R), for identifying the objects, for storing them in a specific case in the assembly of cases, for identifying the specific case and for access to the said installation as a function of information supplied by the marks identifying objects and cases and the dialogue interface.
