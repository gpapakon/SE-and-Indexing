<INVENTION-TITLE>
Control for evaluating sensor signals of domestic appliances.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G05D2320	G05D2324	H05B368	H05B374	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G05D	G05D	H05B	H05B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G05D23	G05D23	H05B3	H05B3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a control for domestic appliances for evaluating sensor signals, in particular for radiant heaters in glass ceramic hobs, the control decoding direct and indirect temperature measurements, made by means of sensors, of the respective hot plate (cooking plate) of a glass ceramic hob and comparing them with typical temperature curves T = f(t), so that the control achieves a pot recognition and that the control switches off the radiant heater in the case of a hot plate being idle.
