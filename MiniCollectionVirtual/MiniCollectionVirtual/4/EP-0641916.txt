<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method and apparatus for drawing gas and/or liquid samples from different layers.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E21B3312	E21B33124	E21B4300	E21B4312	E21B4312	E21B4314	E21B4900	E21B4908	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E21B	E21B	E21B	E21B	E21B	E21B	E21B	E21B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>E21B33	E21B33	E21B43	E21B43	E21B43	E21B43	E21B49	E21B49	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In the drawing of gas or liquid samples from different layers, for example soil layers, via a common measuring pipe (10), the measuring pipe is provided with passage openings (23) at the individual sampling points only over a limited peripheral area facing the direction of flow of the medium, and the drawing pumps are controlled in such a way that no more medium than flows normally into the measuring pipe at the sampling point is drawn as sample. This ensures that only medium which originates from the layer in which the sampling point is located is drawn as sample.
