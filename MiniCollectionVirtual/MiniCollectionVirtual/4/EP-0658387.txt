<INVENTION-TITLE>
Mould for continuous casting steel strip.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B22D1104	B22D1104	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B22D	B22D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B22D11	B22D11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In the case of a mould for the continuous casting of steel strip, with broad side walls (2, 102, 202) and narrow side walls (3), the broad side walls having a funnel-shaped entry region (4) which is reduced towards the narrow sides and in the casting direction to the dimensions of the cast strip, and the narrow side walls, which are arranged adjustably between the broad side walls, lying parallel opposite to one another, the thickness of the strand can be changed with the mould closed, and the strand withdrawn from the mould without deforming the narrow-side area if the broad side walls in the funnel-shaped entry region are designed as convex circular arcs (6) and, to the side of the funnel-shaped entry region, have oblique surfaces (7) which approach one another towards the outside in the direction of the narrow sides.
