<INVENTION-TITLE>
Detector for combustible gases, particularly methane.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01J300	G01J310	G01N2131	G01N2135	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01J	G01J	G01N	G01N	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01J3	G01J3	G01N21	G01N21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention describes an apparatus and a method with which detectors for recognising the presence of dangerous concentrations of, for example, methane can be produced. The method ensures selective detection and is based on the property of polyatomic gases that they absorb light quantas having energies in the infrared range, the signal evaluation, especially the distribution of the signal on the time axis, being employed for recognising dangerous concentrations.
