<ABSTRACT>
The invention proposes a vessel, in particular for receiving paints or similar materials, having a container which consists of paperboard or cardboard and into which there is inserted a plastic bag (7) which is connected to a retaining ring (5). The retaining ring (5) is arranged on the open border of the container (2, 3), and a plastic multifunctional part (8) with an encircling sealing ring (9) is attached on the retaining ring (5) and contains implements (14) associated with the intended usage of the vessel, a removable covering sheet (17) being arranged between the contents of the vessel (1) and the multifunctional part (8).

<CLAIMS>
Container for receiving paints or similar
substances, having a receptacle which is made of

paperboard or cardboard and in which there is
positioned a plastic bag (7) connected to a retaining

ring (5), the retaining ring (5) being arranged at the
open border of the receptacle (2, 3), characterized in

that a plastic element (8), which receives at least one
painter's tool (14), with a peripheral sealing ring (9)

is positioned on the retaining ring (5), and a
removable covering sheet (17) is arranged between the

contents of the container (1) and the plastic element
(8).
Container according to Claim 1, characterized
in that the covering sheet (17) is fastened on the

retaining ring (5) or on the plastic element (8).
Container according to Claim 1 or Claim 2,
characterized in that at least one opening lug (13) is

arranged on the sealing ring (9) of the plastic element
(8).
Container according to one of Claims 1 to 3,
characterized in that the base of the receptacle (2, 3)

has a projecting border (4), and the plastic element
(8) has at least two elevations (18) or a peripheral

protrusion, which engage behind the border (4) and form
an arresting means for stacking purposes.
Container according to one of Claims 1 to 4,
characterized in that the plastic element (8) has a

wiping grid (14), which forms a unit with the sealing
ring (9). 
Container according to one of Claims 1 to 5,
characterized in that the plastic element (8) is

provided with a suspension device (16), which can be
fitted in over the border or the retaining ring (5) of

the receptacle (2, 3).
Container according to Claim 6, characterized
in that the suspension device has two hooks (16), which

are arranged at the sides of the wiping grid (14).
Container according to Claim 6 or Claim 7,
characterized in that the suspension device or the

hooks (16) is/are designed with swing action.
Container according to one of Claims 1 to 8,
characterized in that provided alongside the wiping

grid (14), in the region or in the vicinity of the
suspension device (16), is a continuous surface area

(15) which serves as a grip.
Container according to one of Claims 1 to 9,
characterized in that the plastic element (8) comprises

at least one spatula and/or at least one stirring
element, these being removable.
Container according to one of Claims 1 to 10,
characterized in that the sealing ring (9) has a

protrusion (11), which engages in the retaining ring,
and a border (10, 12), which engages around the

retaining ring.
Container according to one of Claims 1 to 11,
characterized in that provided on the receptacle (2, 3)

is a handle which consists of a paper and/or cardboard
composite or of jute.
Container according to one of Claims 1 to 12,
characterized in that the receptacle (2, 3) is designed

with four sides and rounded corners.
</CLAIMS>
