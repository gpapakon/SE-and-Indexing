<INVENTION-TITLE>
Sulfurized hemicryptates and their use for the extraction of metals especially rhodium.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C07C32100	C07C32114	G21C1942	G21C1946	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C07C	C07C	G21C	G21C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C07C321	C07C321	G21C19	G21C19	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to new sulphur-containing hemisepulcrands (hemicryptands) and to their use for extracting metals, in particular rhodium. These sulphur-containing hemisepulcrands correspond to the formula: 
<
IMAGE
>
  in which R represents a group chosen from     -(CH2)pH   -(CH2)m-S-(CH2)pH   -(CH2)m-S-(CH2)n-S-(CH2)pH with m and n, which can be identical or different, representing an integer equal to 2, 3, 4 or 5 and p being an integer ranging from 1 to 18. They can be used for separating rhodium from solutions from the reprocessing of irradiated nuclear fuels by bringing the aqueous solution into contact with an immiscible organic phase containing the sulphur-containing hemisepulcrand, in order to extract the rhodium into the organic phase.
</ABSTRACT>
