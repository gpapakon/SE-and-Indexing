<INVENTION-TITLE>
Flowable concentrate formulations of polyalkoxylated, phosphated styrylphenol derivatives, in particular for use in agrochemistry.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Fluid composition stable in time and at temperatures, consisting of at least one phosphated polyalkoxylated tristyrylphenol derivative and a diluent, characterised in that the said diluent is glycerine and in that the concentration of the phosphated polyalkoxylated tristyrylphenol in the said diluent is at least 40 % by weight. It can be employed for the stabilisation of agrochemical active substances in formulations of the concentrated suspensions, emulsions and suspoemulsions type.
</ABSTRACT>
