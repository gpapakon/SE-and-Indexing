<INVENTION-TITLE>
Dispenser for blades or similar.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65D8308	B65D8310	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65D	B65D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65D83	B65D83	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
There is disclosed a dispenser (1) for blades which has a boxlike housing (2) containing at least one slotlike opening for removing and/or inserting blades. The housing has a wall folded from paperboard, cardboard, corrugated cardboard or the like and two head pieces (3, 4) made of another material which can be put onto the two ends of the folded wall and keep the wall in the folded shape, or two head pieces which can be put into the housing, folded and bonded together in this form, it being possible for the housing and head pieces to be easily separated from one another and economically disposed of after use of the dispenser.
