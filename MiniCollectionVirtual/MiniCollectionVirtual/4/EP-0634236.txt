<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method of making rotors for excentric spiral pumps.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B21D900	B21D912	B21D1100	B21D1114	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B21D	B21D	B21D	B21D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B21D9	B21D9	B21D11	B21D11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method of making rotors for eccentric spiral pumps, the method being intended to eliminate machining operations for the formation of the spiral shape of the rotor. To achieve this object, a strand or bar (10) with a cross-section corresponding to that of the rotor is, according to the invention, permanently deformed by twisting and/or conversion to a helical form, and the strand or bar is thus imparted the form of a coarse-pitch thread.
