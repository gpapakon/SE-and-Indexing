<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the preparation of fluorobenzonitriles
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C07C25300	C07C25330	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C07C	C07C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C07C253	C07C253	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Fluorobenzonitriles and chlorofluorobenzonitriles are prepared advantageously from the corresponding chlorobenzonitriles and an alkali metal fluoride by a chlorine/fluorine exchange reaction, the reaction being catalysed with a quaternary ammonium compound which contains at least one alkoxypolyoxyalkyl radical.
</ABSTRACT>
