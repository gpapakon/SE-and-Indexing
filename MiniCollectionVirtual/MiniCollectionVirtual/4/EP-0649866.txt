<INVENTION-TITLE>
Lacquer polyisocyanates and their use.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C07D25100	C07D25134	C08G1800	C08G1878	C08G1879	C09D17500	C09D17500	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C07D	C07D	C08G	C08G	C08G	C09D	C09D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C07D251	C07D251	C08G18	C08G18	C08G18	C09D175	C09D175	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Paint (surface coating, laquer, enamel, varnish) polyisocyanates having an NCO content of from 8 to 20% by weight, a mean NCO functionality of less than 3, a content of isocyanurate groups of from 3.5 to 24% by weight, a content of allophanate groups of from 2.5 to 23% by weight and a content of monomeric diisocyanates of less than 2% by weight obtained by trimerisation of some of the isocyanate groups of cycloaliphatic diisocyanates or of mixtures of cycloaliphatic diisocyanates and aliphatic diisocyanates with prior, simultaneous and/or subsequent reaction of some more of the isocyanate groups with a C1-C5-alkanol with allophanate formation, and the use of the paint polyisocyanates, optionally in blocked form, as the isocyanate component in one- or two-component coating systems, in particular two-component polyurethane paints.
</ABSTRACT>
