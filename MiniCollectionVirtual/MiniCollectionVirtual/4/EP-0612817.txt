<INVENTION-TITLE>
Blue mixtures of reactive dyes with an improved metamerism.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C09B6700	C09B6722	C09B6724	D06P138	D06P138	D06P382	D06P387	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C09B	C09B	C09B	D06P	D06P	D06P	D06P	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C09B67	C09B67	C09B67	D06P1	D06P1	D06P3	D06P3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A blue mixture of reactive dyes which shows improved metamerism comprises at least one vinylsulphonylphthalocyanine dye, at least one fluoro- and/or chloro-triazinylformazan dye and optionally a vinylsulphonyl disazo dye.
</ABSTRACT>
