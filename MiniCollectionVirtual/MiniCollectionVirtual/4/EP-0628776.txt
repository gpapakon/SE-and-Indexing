<INVENTION-TITLE>
(Low temperature) refrigerated cabinet.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47F304	A47F304	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47F	A47F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47F3	A47F3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
(Low temperature) refrigerated cabinet (1) which is open towards the front or can be closed with (glass) doors and if appropriate has goods display shelves (2) and/or a chest-shaped lower goods space,   a) a fan (4) being provided in the lower region (3) of the rear space of the (low temperature) refrigerated cabinet, which compresses the cold air flowing back via a common return-air duct (5) on the bottom of the (low temperature) refrigerated cabinet (1), b) the region of the rear space arranged above the region (3) described under a), and the top region of the (low temperature) refrigerated cabinet (1) being divided into at least two incoming-air ducts and c) an evaporator (8) being arranged in one of these incoming-air ducts.
