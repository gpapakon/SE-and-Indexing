<INVENTION-TITLE>
Burial crypt.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E04H1300	E04H1300	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E04H	E04H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a burial installation (10), comprising a chamber element (14) which encloses a burial chamber (12), is installed in the ground and exhibits at least three side walls (20, 22) and a cover (34), at least two mutually opposite side walls (20) and the cover (34) being designed as a prefabricated compound unit (16). In order to improve said burial installation such that the burial chamber (12) is more easily accessible, it is proposed that the cover (34) close the burial chamber (12) in an inaccessible manner, that the burial chamber (12) have a lateral access opening, that the access opening (26) open into a distributor chamber (36) located beside the burial chamber (12), and that the distributor chamber (36) be installed in the ground and be able to be covered by a distributor covering (50) such that it cannot be seen from above.
