<CLAIMS>
Press device of a paper machine comprising at least 

1.1 a cylinder (6), 
1.2 a first press roller (4) with smooth surface, 
1.3 a press unit (5), 
1.4 a second press roller (3) provided as a suction or groove 
roller, and 
1.5 a felt (1), and with 
1.6 a first press gap provided between the first press roller (4) 
and the second press roller (3), 
1.7 a second press gap provided behind the first press gap, as 
seen in the movement direction of the paper web (2), 

between the first press roller (4) and the press unit (5), and 
1.8 following on in the movement direction of the paper web 
(2) is provided a third press gap without felt between the 

first press roller (4) and the cylinder (6), 
characterised in that
1.9 the paper machine produces tissue, 
1.10 the cylinder (6) is a drying cylinder, 
1.11 the press unit (5) is a long-shoe press unit, 
1.12 the second press roller (3) is associated with a water 
collecting trough (9), and 
1.13 the felt (1) is together with the paper web (2) guided 
through the first press gap and the second press gap and 

after the second press gap leaves the periphery of the first 
press roller (4). 
Press device according to Claim 1, 
characterised in that
 the 
drying cylinder (6) is at the same time provided as a crepe 

cylinder.  
 
Press device according to Claim or 2, 
characterised in that
 
the second press roller (3) is designed as a suction roller and 

comprises a suction zone in such a manner that the felt (1) is 
dehydrated prior to entry into the first press gap. 
</CLAIMS>
