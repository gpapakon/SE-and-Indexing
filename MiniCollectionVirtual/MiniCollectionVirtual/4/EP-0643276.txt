<INVENTION-TITLE>
Energy selector switch, especially for absorber refrigerators.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F25B4900	F25B4904	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F25B	F25B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F25B49	F25B49	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an energy selector switch (1), in particular for absorber refrigerators, for selecting different types of energy such as electric power or gas. It is provided that when selecting a specific type of energy the types of energy not selected are simultaneously blocked, with the result that malfunctioning by simultaneously switching on a plurality of types of energy is excluded.
