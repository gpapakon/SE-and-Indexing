<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method with multiple access by orthogenal frequencies, corresponding central station, remote station, system and their use
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method of multiple access to a central station by a set of at least two remote stations, comprising the following steps:   - allocation (1001) to each of the said remote stations of at least one carrier frequency selected from among a plurality of orthogonal carrier frequencies; - in each of the said remote stations, generation (1020), modulation (1031) and transmission (1038), in a synchronized way, of each of the said carrier frequencies which is allocated to it by a succession of digital modulation elements representative of a source signal; - reception (1040), in the said central station, of a single signal (21; 31) corresponding to the multiplex of orthogonal frequencies which has been formed by all the carrier frequencies which are modulated and transmitted by the said set of remote stations (SR1 to SR12); - demodulation (1040) of the said single signal comprising at least one mathematical time-frequency conversion.
