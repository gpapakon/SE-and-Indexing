<INVENTION-TITLE>
Laser beam device for vehicles.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01S1700	G01S1793	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01S	G01S	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01S17	G01S17	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In the case of a laser beam device for motor vehicles, having at least one transmitter, at least one receiver and having resolution optics for laser beams, via which an area which is located in front of the motor vehicle can be scanned if split up into a plurality of investigation areas, edge areas are connected on both sides to a central area, the investigation areas of which edge regions overlap with that of the central area.
