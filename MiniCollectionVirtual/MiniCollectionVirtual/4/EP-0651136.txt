<INVENTION-TITLE>
Rotary piston engine.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F01C100	F01C1356	F01C144	F02B5300	F02B5300	F02B7502	F02B7502	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F01C	F01C	F01C	F02B	F02B	F02B	F02B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F01C1	F01C1	F01C1	F02B53	F02B53	F02B75	F02B75	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In a rotary piston engine with a hollow-cylindrical casing, a rotor which is arranged centrally and rotatably in the casing, an annular chamber, shut-off members, an inlet for fresh gases and an outlet for combustion gases, and a piston connected to the rotor, the piston is mounted in the rotor in a manner which allows it to pivot about a pivoting axis between a closed position and an open position, and the pivoting axis of the piston is aligned parallel to the axis of rotation of the rotor.
