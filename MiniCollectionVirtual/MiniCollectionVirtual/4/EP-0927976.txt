<INVENTION-TITLE>
Beverage dispenser
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47F100	A47F112	G07F1146	G07F1158	G07F1310	G07F1310	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47F	A47F	G07F	G07F	G07F	G07F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47F1	A47F1	G07F11	G07F11	G07F13	G07F13	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The machine provides and outputs beverages (1) packed in containers. It has a storage space (2) and a dispensation compartment (11,12). The drinks are held in drinks boxes (1) arranged next to each other or one behind another on horizontal conveyors (4). There are at least two such conveyors arranged one on top of another. The machine has a device (5) which horizontally moves the boxes. It also has a second device (6) which vertically moves the conveyors. The conveyors are preferably conveyor belts with a drive shaft having a drive wheel.
</ABSTRACT>
