<INVENTION-TITLE>
Ballet shoe.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A43B500	A43B512	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A43B	A43B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A43B5	A43B5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Ballet shoe (pump) comprising a footwear part made from fabric covered with an external material (21), generally in satin, an outsole (12) and an insole (14) made from biocompatible material, as well as a toe (10), at the point of the shoe, characterised in that the insole (14) comprises a reinforcement (42) made from an elastically deformable material.
