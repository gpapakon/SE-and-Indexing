<INVENTION-TITLE>
Two-component coating composition for the preparation of PUR-elastomers.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08G1800	C08G1810	C08G1816	C09D17500	C09D17500	C09D17504	C09D17504	C09K310	C09K310	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
A two-component, solvent-free or low-solvent coating compsn. for prodn. of polyurethane (PUR) elastomers contains (A) a (cyclo)aliphatic polyisocyanate prepolymer with an NCO content of 1-15 wt.%, (B) a hardener component and known additives etc. (B) comprises (B1) a diketimine of formula (I) and (B2) an aromatic or aliphatic carboxylic or sulphonic acid; R1=2-20C alkylene, 4-100C alkylene with in-chain O and/or N-alkyl, or 5-20C cycloalkylene; R2, R3=1-8C alkyl, or R2+R3+C=5- or 6-membered ring.
</ABSTRACT>
