Removable fixation means for a goal on a sportsfield

<ABSTRACT>
a) Removable device for fastening a goal on a sports field. b) Device comprising a fastening plate (16) intended to be fastened to a part (5, 6, 7) of the movable goal (1) and carrying a guide tube (19) which is inclined with respect to the plate (16), an anchoring rod (20) equipped with a threaded part (22) and with a bearing collar (24) with a gripping piece (25), a tool (26) which is fastened removably on the gripping piece (25) in order to screw the anchoring rod (20).

<CLAIMS>
Removable securing device for a goal on a sports ground by the aid of parts in the form of 
screws fixed in the ground, characterised in that it comprises: 


A: A securing plate (16) designed to be affixed to a part (5,6,7) of the movable goal 
(1), to which plate (16) is affixed a guide tube (19) inclined in relation to the plate 

(16) to which it is affixed, 
B: An anchoring rod (20) fitted at one end with a screw-threaded part (22) with a 
wide thread, the other end terminating in a supporting collar (24) and a contact part 

(25), 
C: A tool (26) detachably affixed to the contact part (25) introduced into the tube (19) 
for the purpose of screwing the anchoring rod (20) into the ground or unscrewing 

it therefrom. 
Removable securing device in accordance with Claim 1, characterised in that it comprises a 
protective cap (28) which is placed on the tube (19). 
Removable securing device in accordance with Claim 1, characterised in that the anchoring 
rod (20) includes a smooth part (23) between the screw-threaded part (21) and the contact part 

(25), the length of the said smooth part (23) being substantially equal to the length of the guide 
tube (19). 
Securing device in accordance with Claim 1, characterised in that the contact part (23) is a 
hexagonal head or a cruciform or hexagonal impression and that the tool (26) includes a 

supplementary shaped piece (27). 
Securing device in accordance with Claim 3, characterised in that the tool (26) is a crank. 
</CLAIMS>
