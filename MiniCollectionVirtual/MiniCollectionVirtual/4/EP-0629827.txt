<ABSTRACT>
In a refrigerator and/or freezer apparatus, a single fan motor (11) with two blades (14, 15) on one and the same shaft (12) is used in the outer space for the air circulation in the inner space (13) and for ventilating the condenser (18), an extended shaft end (12a) passing through a wall (10).
