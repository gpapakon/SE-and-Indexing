<INVENTION-TITLE>
INFUSION-BAG PACKAGING METHOD.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65D8100	B65D8100	B65D85804	B65D85808	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65D	B65D	B65D	B65D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention concerns an infusion-bag packaging method calling for different distribution of the contents (4) of the package before or during the packaging process and various dispositions of the infusion-bags (1) in the package (2) which are adapted to the distribution of the contents mentioned above. This method allows the realization of a package conforming with the requirements of  
&
sect
&
 1, para. 2, No.1, concerning the avoidance of packaging waste and  
&
sect
&
 7, para. 2, concerning the interdiction of deceptive packaging by the law on weights and mesures, the package also ensuring that the aroma is retained longer.
</ABSTRACT>
