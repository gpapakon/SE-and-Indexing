<CLAIMS>
Method for controlling a roving frame which includes leading 
wings, whereby 


the bobbin rotary frequency is predetermined, 
the supply speed is calculated and adjusted based upon 
the bobbin rotary frequency and 
the wing rotary frequency is controlled in such a way, 
that the bobbin rotary frequency is not increasing during the 

winding-on of a tube. 
</CLAIMS>
