<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a screw-connection between two plate-like elements, in particular a plastic plate and a sheet-metal plate, having a plastic dowel 1 which can be introduced into a coaxial opening of the two plates. The shank 3 of the plastic dowel 1 comprises a cylindrical zone 10 and an adjoining frustoconical zone 11 with a closed-off bottom end 12 and exhibits, at least over a certain length, a region B1 of low wall thickness W. The region B1 of low wall thickness is adjoined by a region B2 of relatively small diameter of a cutout 4 which is provided with engagement elements 13 for thread turns of the screw shank.
