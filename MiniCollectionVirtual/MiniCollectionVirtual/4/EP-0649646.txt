<INVENTION-TITLE>
Depilatory composition.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a depilatory preparation, to a process for producing such a preparation and to a depilatory process using the said depilatory preparation. According to the invention, the depilatory preparation, which is intended to be applied cold to the body for the removal of hairs, is in the form of a cooked mixture of any sugar and of any form of vinegar. More precisely, in an advantageous embodiment, two measures, by volume, of white sugar powder and one measure of white alcohol vinegar are mixed together, and the mixture is then brought to boiling and is blended until a paste which is ready to use is obtained. The invention will find application in the field of body care.
</ABSTRACT>
