<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for supporting a radio listener at the setting of a transmission.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H03J100	H03J100	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H03J	H03J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H03J1	H03J1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The present invention specifies methods for assisting a radio listener in setting a programme at a broadcast receiver, in which the broadcast receiver automatically selects a programme on the basis of a comparison between data which were obtained from setting actions of the broadcast listener and data which were received by the broadcast receiver as data accompanying broadcast programmes.
