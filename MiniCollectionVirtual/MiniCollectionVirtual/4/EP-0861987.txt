<INVENTION-TITLE>
Piece of sheet metal
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F16B3700	F16B3702	F16B3900	F16B39284	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The screw fastening is positioned in at least one flat part (A) of the inner thread (5). The flat parts are positioned inside the normal shape of the inner thread. The flat-angle over which the flat part runs at a tangent to the round outline of the thread, amounts to at least 7 degrees. The transition angle amounts to at least 25 degrees. The height of the flat part amounts at least to the axial extension of two thread pitches of the inner thread.
</ABSTRACT>
