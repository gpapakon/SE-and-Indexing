<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for producing a thermoplastic polymeric foam.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08J900	C08J900	C08J912	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08J	C08J	C08J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C08J9	C08J9	C08J9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Thermoplastic foams are produced by mixing the plastic, such as polymethyl methacrylate or a polymethacrylalkylimide polymer, with magnesium hydroxide and/or aluminium hydroxide or silicic acid as blowing agent in the melt and heating the mixture until it expands and cooling the foam to below the softening point. If necessary, the mixture is heated under pressure and expanded by decompression.
</ABSTRACT>
