<ABSTRACT>
The process is characterised in that the final annealing is performed by heating one of the sides of the product in the direction (D) to a temperature T and the other side to a temperature of t 
<
 T for a period (d). This treatment can be performed in a double-chamber furnace. The invention finds its application especially in the manufacture of spars for aircraft aerofoils.
