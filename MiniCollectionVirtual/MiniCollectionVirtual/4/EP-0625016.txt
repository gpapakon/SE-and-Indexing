<CLAIMS>
Applicator tube for liquid or semi-liquid 
cosmetic products, in particular for mascara, 

comprising a container (2) for the cosmetic 
product, an external tubular body (1), one end of 

which comprises a narrowing (10) acting as a wiper 
for an applicator (31) disposed at the end of a 

rod (30) and in the interior of the external body 
(1), below the narrowing (10), characterised in 

that the applicator (31) is disposed above a 
tubular piece (41), this tubular piece coming to 

bear on a deformable piece (5) arranged at the 
upper end of the container (2) provided at the 

lower part of the tubular body (1), which 
container (2) is movable in relation to the 

tubular piece (41), such that the deformable piece 
(5) is provided with connection apertures between 

this container (2) and this cylindrical space (42) 
for the passage of the cosmetic product during the 

relative displacement of the container (2) and the 
tubular piece (41). 
Applicator tube according to Claim 1, 
characterised in that the tubular piece (41) is 

integral with the lower end of a piece (4,4',) of 
which one piece (40,40') surrounds the applicator 

(31).  
 
Applicator tube according to any one of 
the preceding Claims, characterised in that the 

tubular body (1) is open at its lower end for the 
introduction of the container (2). 
Applicator tube according to any one of 
Claims 1 to 3, characterised in that the 

applicator (31) consists of a spiral brush or of a 
small sponge. 
Applicator tube according to any one of 
the preceding Claims, characterised in that the 

piece (4,4') comprises longitudinal slots (44). 
Applicator tube according to any one of 
Claims 1 to 5, characterised in that a capsule (3) 

integral with the rod (30), the end of which is 
provided with an applicator (31), is fitted to the 

upper end of the external tubular body (1) and in 
that the piece (4) is longitudinally movable in 

relation to the container. 
Applicator tube according to any one of 
Claims 1, 3, 4 and 5, characterised in that the 

rod (30), provided at one of its ends with the 
applicator (31), is integral, by means of its 

other end, with the tubular part (41) of the piece  
 

(4'), and in that the container (2) is 
longitudinally movable in relation to the piece 

(4). 
</CLAIMS>
