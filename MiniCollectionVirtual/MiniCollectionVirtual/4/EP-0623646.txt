<INVENTION-TITLE>
Packaging material for manufacture of folded boxes.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65D6538	B65D6538	C08K300	C08K300	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65D	B65D	C08K	C08K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65D65	B65D65	C08K3	C08K3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a plastic packaging material as replacement for board or cardboard in the production of collapsible (folded, folding) boxes, where the material comprises a polymer matrix containing a particulate filler, where the filler is present in a proportion of from 20 to 60% by weight - based on the filled matrix - and where the cohesion forces between filler and matrix are smaller than the cohesion forces in the matrix itself.
</ABSTRACT>
