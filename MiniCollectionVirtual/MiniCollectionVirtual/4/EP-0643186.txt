<INVENTION-TITLE>
Sliding door of large dimensions.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E05D1510	E05D1510	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E05D	E05D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>E05D15	E05D15	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Sliding door of large dimensions, guided and held using curved rails, characterised by at least one additional holding point and at least one connection piece (10), articulated in rotation on a spindle (11) joined to the frame, which connection piece (10) comprises a connection branch (15) which is inclined with respect to the closure plane and the end of which is provided with a relief (14) which can slide freely in a groove (12, 13) made in the leaf (1).
