<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process and apparatus for the continuous preparation of peroxycarboxylic acids.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
A process for the continuous preparation of peroxycarboxylic acids by reaction of a carboxylic acid with hydrogen peroxide in an aqueous medium in the presence of a catalyst at a reaction temperature in the range from 40 to 60 DEG C, and an apparatus for carrying out this process, are described. The apparatus comprises a residence reactor 1 for receiving the reaction medium, which is provided with heating 10, two feed pipes 2, 2 provided with check valves 9, 9, each of which is provided with a metering pump 3, 3 for feeding the reaction components from the storage containers 11, 12 into the feed pipes 2, 2.
