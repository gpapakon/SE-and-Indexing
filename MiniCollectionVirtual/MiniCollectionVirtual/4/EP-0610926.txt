<INVENTION-TITLE>
Topical compositions for protection against stinging animals.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Topical formulations containing     a) inorganic micropigments and   b) where appropriate additional organic UV filter substances,   c) where the formulations are aqueous dispersions, d) which consist of an internal lipid phase and an external aqueous phase, and   e) which are essentially free of emulsifiers, and f) where the inorganic micropigments are preferably incorporated in the lipid phase of the aqueous dispersions, for protection against stinging animals.
</ABSTRACT>
