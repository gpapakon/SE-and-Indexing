<INVENTION-TITLE>
Improvements relating to material containers.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65D8800	B65D8816	B65D8822	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65D	B65D	B65D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65D88	B65D88	B65D88	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Containers in the form of bags for carrying one tonne or 
more of powdered or other material are disclosed, the bags 

being parallel-sided and comprising a base (1), four side walls 
(2-5) extending upwardly in full width from the base to the top 

of the bag and four corner seams (7-10) each defining the 
juncture of two adjacent walls and extending the full height of 

the bag. Lifting loops (12) are provided at the top of the bag, 
each loop having a leg (14) which extends down a major part 

of the height of the bag and which is secured to the bag fabric 
solely by the stitching used to form a respective one of the 

corner seams. 
</ABSTRACT>
