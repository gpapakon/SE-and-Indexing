<INVENTION-TITLE>
Lifting platform with several arms.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B66F700	B66F702	B66F728	B66F1700	B66F1700	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B66F	B66F	B66F	B66F	B66F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B66F7	B66F7	B66F7	B66F17	B66F17	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In lifting platforms (1) with several arms (3), sensors, switches or the like are arranged on at least one of the arms (3) in order to enable the mounting plates (6) located at the free ends of the arms (3) to be placed exactly under the specified points of the vehicle to be lifted. The sensors, switches or the like respond to the weight loading of the arm (3) or to loading on one side and are connected to an indicating/monitoring device or the like.
