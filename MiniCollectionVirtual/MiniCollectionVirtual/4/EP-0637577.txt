<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process and material for decreasing gas emission in manure.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A01C300	A01C300	C05C900	C05C900	C05F300	C05F300	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A01C	A01C	C05C	C05C	C05F	C05F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A01C3	A01C3	C05C9	C05C9	C05F3	C05F3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Process for decreasing emission of gas from liquid manure by adding an active amount of a bactericide and of one or more salts of nitric acid and a composition for decreasing the emission of gas from liquid manure, which composition contains these additives in an active amount.
</ABSTRACT>
