<CLAIMS>
Target for darts comprising

a frame with spokes (1) and bands (2) between which are located a number of

fields (3) in each of which is located a spring-loaded segment (4) capable of
axial movement,
a contact plate (9) with contacts for the display of points scored in each
segment (4),
a rubber mat (10) arranged between the contact plate (9) and the rear face of
the segments (4), and
a further plate (12) arranged between the rubber mat (10) and the rear face of
the segments (4)
characterised by

the design of the further plate as an adapter plate (12) which exhibits recesses
or holes (13) through which pins (11, 20) can pass and in which pins (11, 20)

are located.
Target for darts according to Claim 1, characterised in that the adapter plate
(12) exhibits lateral protrusions (14) with which it is supported at the spokes

(1) and/or bands (2) of the frame.
Target for darts according to Claim 1 or 2, characterised in that the adapter
plate (12) is at least partially arranged at a distance (b) from the rubber mat

(10), preferably characterised by at least one wire stirrup (15) arranged at the
rear of the adapter plate (12) and running parallel to it, said wire stirrup (15)

passing through an oblong hole (16) of pin (11), and further preferably 
characterised by hooks (17) provided at the adapter plate (12) which support

the wire stirrup (15).
Target for darts according to one of the preceding Claims, characterised in that
the pins (11) are integrated as a single piece construction with the segments (4)

and are preferably integrally injection-moulded with these.
Target for darts according to one of the preceding Claims, characterised in that
the pins (20) are not integral with the segments (4) and are located with their

forward end (21) against the rear face (22) of said segments (4).
</CLAIMS>
