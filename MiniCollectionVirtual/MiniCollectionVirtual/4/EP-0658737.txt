<ABSTRACT>
Heat exchanger block, produced from a monoblock made from ceramic material, having at least two channel systems for media flowing through the block, characterised in that at least one of the channel systems consists of slots which have an elongated cross section and whose walls are formed in cross section by overlapping circular arcs.
