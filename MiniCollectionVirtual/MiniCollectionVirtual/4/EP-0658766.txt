<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for the determination of alkylpolyglycosides.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01N3100	G01N3100	G01N3122	G01N3122	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01N	G01N	G01N	G01N	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01N31	G01N31	G01N31	G01N31	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Detergent alkyl polyglycosides with long-chain alkyl radicals are determined in aqueous solution according to the invention by adding a colour reagent based on an aromatic ketone and then determining the intensity of the coloration.
</ABSTRACT>
