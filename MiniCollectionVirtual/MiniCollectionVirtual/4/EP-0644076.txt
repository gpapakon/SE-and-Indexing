<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for holding an energy storage unit in a cover for a motor vehicle.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60K100	B60K100	B60K104	B60K104	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60K	B60K	B60K	B60K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device for holding an energy storage unit (19) in a pan (8) for a motor vehicle. The pan (8) is of modular design and serves for holding the energy storage unit (19) and/or a drive unit (24) and can be introduced into the motor vehicle from the underside and detachably fixed to the said motor vehicle.
