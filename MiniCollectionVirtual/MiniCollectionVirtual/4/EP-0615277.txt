<INVENTION-TITLE>
HIGH POWER UV TUBE.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61N506	A61N506	F25B2102	F25B2102	H01J6102	H01J6152	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61N	A61N	F25B	F25B	H01J	H01J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61N5	A61N5	F25B21	F25B21	H01J61	H01J61	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
So that the emission rate of UV tubes can be increased which are used for cosmetic purposes, for example, a limited, partial cooling of the tube is proposed. Despite higher current input, the gas pressure inside the tube is reduced as a result. In the case of such tubes, the coating with UV luminescent pigments can also vary over their lengths so that a higher UV radiation is emitted for example in the face region of a tanning bench (solarium).
</ABSTRACT>
