<INVENTION-TITLE>
Use of a copper-zinc alloy for making nickel-free consumer articles
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Copper-zinc alloy contains in wt.%: 67-72 (68-70) Cu; 6-8 (7-8) Mn; 4-8 (6-8) Al; 1-2.5 (1-1.5) Si; 1-1.5 Fe; 0.5-1.5 Pb; balance Zn plus usual impurities.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
Use of a copper-zinc alloy which consists of 

67 to 72 % copper, 
6.0 to 8.5 % manganese, 
4.0 to 8.0 % aluminium, 
1.0 to 2.5 % silicon, 
1.0 to 1.5 % iron, 
0.5 to 1.5 % lead, 
the rest zinc and usual impurities 
 
for producing nickel-free consumer articles which do not 

trigger any allergic reactions. 
Use of a copper alloy as claimed in Claim 1 with 68 to 
70 % copper for the purpose as claimed in Claim 1. 
Use of a copper alloy as claimed in Claim 1 or 2 with 
7.0 to 8.0 % manganese for the purpose as claimed in Claim 1. 
Use of a copper alloy as claimed in one or more of 
Claims 1 to 3 with 6.0 to 8.0 % aluminium for the purpose as 

claimed in Claim 1. 
Use of a copper alloy as claimed in one or more of 
Claims 1 to 4 with 1.0 to 1.5 % silicon for the purpose as 

claimed in Claim 1. 
Use of a copper alloy as claimed in one or more of 
Claims 1 to 5 for producing spectacle parts, pieces of 

jewellery, cutlery and haberdashery. 
</CLAIMS>
