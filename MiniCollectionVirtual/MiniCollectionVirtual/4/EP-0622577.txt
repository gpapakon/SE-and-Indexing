<INVENTION-TITLE>
Pipe connection with air cushion sealing ring.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F16L2102	F16L2102	F16L2103	F16L2104	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F16L	F16L	F16L	F16L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F16L21	F16L21	F16L21	F16L21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a pipe connection with a sealing ring (4) which is designed as an air-cushion ring and is inserted into an encircling groove of the pipe socket (3), there being additionally provided a plurality of encircling chambers (8, 9, 10, 16) which are arranged between the external diameter of the spigot (1) and the inner contour of the pipe socket and, in the active position of the pipe connection, are in partially asymetrical and partially symmetrical shapes of different volumes.
