Spreading apparatus.

<ABSTRACT>
A spreading vehicle having a spreading-material reservoir and a feeding device via which spreading material can be fed to a throwing plate is characterized in that the feeding device (30) is constructed as a conveying worm (34) and the throwing plate (32) can be removed, especially pivoted away, from a spreading material-delivering station (20) in the manner of a drum spreader.
