New multiplex communication system and vehicle protection system so obtained.

<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to the field of electronics and more particularly to a multiplex communication system. The invention relates in particular to a central control unit connected to a succession of transmitter/receiver relays arranged connected to control units with different functions. The control system is driven by a microprocessor which communicates directly with the satellite relays by an encoded language. Application to the construction of alarm and anti-theft devices for vehicles and, in particular on motor vehicles.
</ABSTRACT>
