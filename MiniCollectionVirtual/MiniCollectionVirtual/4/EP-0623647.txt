Flame-retardant thermoplastic copolyester moulding compositions
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to thermoplastic moulding compositions containing, as essential components,   A) 10 - 80% by weight of a copolyester based on polyalkylene terephthalates which contains, as cocomponent, from 5 to 25% by weight, based on A), of a dimeric acid having 7 to 52 carbon atoms, B) 20 - 70% by weight of mixtures of metal carbonates of metals from main group 2 of the Periodic Table.
</ABSTRACT>
