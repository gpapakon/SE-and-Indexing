<ABSTRACT>
Specific ester products are described which are obtainable by esterification of a selected polyethylene glycol with a dicarboxylic acid in a molar ratio of 1 to 0.25 to 1. The novel ester compounds are good and readily biodegradable fibre finishing agents.
</ABSTRACT>
