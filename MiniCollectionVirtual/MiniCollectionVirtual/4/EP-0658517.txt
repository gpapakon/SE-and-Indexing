<INVENTION-TITLE>
Homogeneous mixtures of silicate and carbonate of alcali metal.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C02F500	C02F500	C11D702	C11D712	C11D714	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C02F	C02F	C11D	C11D	C11D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C02F5	C02F5	C11D7	C11D7	C11D7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Homogeneous anhydrous or low-water amorphous solid alkali metal silicate/alkali metal carbonate mixtures of the composition Me2SixO5.y Na2CO3.z H2O, where Me denotes sodium or potassium and x is a number from 1.5 to 3, y is a number from 0.025 to 1 and z is a number from 0 to 1. These mixtures can be used to soften water.
</ABSTRACT>
