<INVENTION-TITLE>
PISTON AND PISTON RING ASSEMBLIES
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F02F700	F02F700	F02F500	F02F300	F16J910	F02F300	F16J928	F16J900	F16J926	F16J900	F02F500	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F02F7	F02F7	F02F5	F02F3	F16J9	F02F3	F16J9	F16J9	F16J9	F16J9	F02F5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A two ring piston and piston ring assembly has an upper 
thin steel compression ring (15) and a lower ring (17) which 

is thicker than the compression ring and which acts both as a 
compression ring and an oil control ring. The lower piston 

ring (17) is made of a material which is more conformable 
than steel, for example a plastics material, which may be 

reinforced. In this way, effective sealing is provided using 

only two piston rings, while the frictional forces between the 
piston (10) and an associated cylinder (18) are reduced. 
</ABSTRACT>
