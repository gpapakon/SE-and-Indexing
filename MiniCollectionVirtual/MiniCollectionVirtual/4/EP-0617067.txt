<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for producing polyurethane-foams.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B32B520	B32B520	C08G1800	C08G1816	C08G1822	C08G1838	C08G1848	C08G10100	C08J900	C08J902	C08J914	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B32B	B32B	C08G	C08G	C08G	C08G	C08G	C08G	C08J	C08J	C08J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B32B5	B32B5	C08G18	C08G18	C08G18	C08G18	C08G18	C08G101	C08J9	C08J9	C08J9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A process for producing polyurethane foams, in particular for producing composite elements by foam-backing a plastic film as top layer by reaction of a polyisocyanate component with a polyol component containing no salt groups in the presence of at least one blowing agent, alkali metal carboxylates as catalysts and further auxiliaries and additives, where selected, highly polar, organic compounds having a dipole moment of greater than 2.0 Debye are present in an amount of 0.01 to 30% by weight, based on the polyol component, as compatiblisers for the alkali metal carboxylates.
</ABSTRACT>
