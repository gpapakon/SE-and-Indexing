<INVENTION-TITLE>
Medical instrument with a central working rod and with a handle.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61B1814	A61B1814	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61B	A61B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61B18	A61B18	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A medical radiofrequency surgical instrument comprises a central working rod whose front end is provided with one or more working tools 35. The rear end of the working rod 1 is provided with a handle 2. The working rod 1 can be rotated about its longitudinal axis relative to the handle 2 and can be fixed in predetermined rotary positions. Radially inside the outer circumference of the working rod, engagement means 30 are present between the handle 2 and the working rod 1, and these engagement means 30 engage automatically in predetermined rotated positions of the working rod 1 relative to the handle 2 and oppose a rotating force executed in the sense of a further rotation of the working rod 1 relative to the handle 2 with a predetermined retaining force.
