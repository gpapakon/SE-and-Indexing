<INVENTION-TITLE>
PROTECTION DEVICE AGAINST RADIATION.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61N100	A61N116	H01Q1700	H01Q1700	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61N	A61N	H01Q	H01Q	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61N1	A61N1	H01Q17	H01Q17	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In a protection device against radiation an improved radiation protection is achieved by at least one electromagnetically vibrating structure. The invention is particularly advantageous in connection with a radiation protection blanket consisting of at least two basic components each having at least one vibrating structure.
</ABSTRACT>
