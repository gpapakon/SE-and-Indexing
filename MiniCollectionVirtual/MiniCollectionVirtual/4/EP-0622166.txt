<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for dispensing individual labels and for delivering them to the mould.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a process for dispensing individual labels (4) from a series of labels and for delivering the individual labels to mould parts of moulds in which articles are moulded - in particular by blowmoulding, injection moulding, casting or thermoforming - and simultaneously labelled, characterised in that a backing strip (2), on which the labels (4) are attached by adhesion at intervals from one another in a row, is drawn from a roll (30) of this backing strip (2) around a detaching edge (18) for the successive detachment of the labels located on it and in that the individual labels (4) are then fed to the mould parts assigned to them.
