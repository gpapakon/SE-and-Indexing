<INVENTION-TITLE>
CONTRACEPTIVE SHEATH WITH ORIENTATION AND PROTECTION CAP
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Protection and undeceitful rigid patch for contraceptive sheath, having on its contour a flat flange (1) intended to be engaged under the winding bead (B) of the contraceptive sheath (P), said patch being arranged on the extremity and on the external side of the sheath. The flange (1) surrounds a portion of said patch which presents externally an overthickness with respect to the plane of the flange. Said overthickness is preferably comprised of a lateral bearing skirt (2) surrounding a convex bottom (3) which may be spherical. Application to all contraceptive sheathes.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
Contraceptive device also giving protection
against sexually transmissible diseases comprising, in

combination:

a) a rolled sheath (P) with a rolling bead (B)
and
b) a rigid protective orientating disc positioned
underneath the rolling bead (B) and on the outside of the

sheath, i.e. on the opposite side from that intended to be
in contact with the user's member, the said disc

comprising :
a lateral bearing skirt (2) comprising a peripheral
surface against which the bead (B) of the said rolled

sheath (P) is to bear,
a flat collar (1) that extends radially outwards from
the base of the said skirt (2), its perimeter being engaged

underneath the rolling bead (B) of the sheath, and
a convex hood (3) situated inside the said skirt (2)
and joined to the base of the said skirt (2).
Device according to Claim 1, characterized in that
the height of the said skirt (2) is approximately 4 to 5 mm

and its external diameter is between approximately 26 and
30 mm.
Device according to Claim 1 or 2, characterized in
that the height of the said convex hood is approximately

equal to or slightly greater than that of the said skirt
(2).
Device according to any one of Claims 1 to 3,
characterized in that the width of the said collar (1) is

approximately 1.2 to 2.5 mm.
Device according to any one of Claims 1 to 4,
characterized in that the said collar (1) is circular and

has a diameter of about 30 to 35 mm. 
Device according to any one of Claims 1 to 4,
characterized in that the said collar (1) comprises a

series of peripheral nubs (4).
Device according to any one of Claims 1 to 6,
characterized in that the said convex hood (3) is in the

shape of a spherical dome with a radius of curvature of
about 20 to 25 mm.
Device according to any one of Claims 1 to 7,
characterized in that the said disc is of food-grade

polyethylene.
</CLAIMS>
