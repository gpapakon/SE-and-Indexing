<INVENTION-TITLE>
WATER-CONTINUOUS SYSTEM, POLYMERISATION METHOD AND USE OF SAID WATER-CONTINUOUS SYSTEM
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08G6100	C08G6112	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a water-continuous system, a polymerisation method and the use of said water-continuous system. The inventive water-continuous system comprises a polymerisable aromatic compound consisting of oil, water and a non-ionic tenside. Said polymerisable aromatic compound is preferably a component from the following group: aniline, thiophene, pyrrol, furane and 3,4-ethylenedioxythiophene. The non-ionic tenside is an alkylpolyethoxylate and/or an alkylpolyglucoside. The inventive water-continuous system can be used for electropolymerising the polymerisable aromatic compound when an electrolyte is added. These systems also comprise liquid crystals, which enables e.g. surfaces to be coated with a liquid crystal layer with a defined thickness before polymerisation and thereby makes it possible to control the layer thickness of the polymer.
</ABSTRACT>
