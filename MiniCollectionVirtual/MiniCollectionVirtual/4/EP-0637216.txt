<INVENTION-TITLE>
COSMETIC SKIN-PEELING DEVICE.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A46B500	A46B504	A47K702	A47K702	A61B1754	A61B1754	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A46B	A46B	A47K	A47K	A61B	A61B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A46B5	A46B5	A47K7	A47K7	A61B17	A61B17	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A cosmetic skin-peeling device uses a woven or knitted textile fabric (4) with a rough surface. The surface may preferably contain a linen weave (L 1/1), a weft sateen weave or a crepe weave. The threads or yarns for the fabric are twisted and/or textured and consist of continuous filaments. Silk, polyester or the like may be used as fibrous material.
</ABSTRACT>
