<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method and devices for treating magnetic tape cassettes.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B03B900	B03B906	B09B500	B09B500	B29B1702	B29B1702	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B03B	B03B	B09B	B09B	B29B	B29B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B03B9	B03B9	B09B5	B09B5	B29B17	B29B17	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Method and devices for treating magnetic tape cassettes (5) for reusing the individual parts comprise opening the housing (3), separating the roll(s) of tape (21) and metal parts and separately treating the plastic parts, the magnetic tape and the metal parts. Special opening devices (11) effect simple opening operations of the housing (3). Material-identifying devices (7) can control the treatment appropriately for the material concerned. All magnetic tape cassettes (5) having at least one reel can be treated.
