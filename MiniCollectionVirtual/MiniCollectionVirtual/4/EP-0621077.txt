<INVENTION-TITLE>
Hand-held shower
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E03C104	B05B118	E03C102	E03C104	B05B118	E03C108	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E03C	B05B	E03C	E03C	B05B	E03C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
A hand-held shower which contains a shower head (2), a handle (1) and a connection (4) for a shower hose exhibits, in the interior of its handle, an insert part (11) which, in the region of one end, is connected to a line (8) leading to the shower head and, in the region of its other end, exhibits the connection for the shower hose. The insert part may serve as a securing means for various functional parts or be part of various functional parts.

<CLAIMS>
Hand shower having

1.1 a handle (1)
1.2 a shower head (2) located at one end of the handle (1),
1.3 a passage (8) for the water passing through the handle (1),
1.4 a connection (4) for connecting the hand shower to a hose and
1.5 an insert part (9), which

1.5.1 is located in the handle (1),
1.5.2 forms a passage for the water,
1.5.3 being connected on its one side with the passage (8)
leading to the shower head (2) and
1.5.4 on its other side to the connection (4),

characterized in that the insert part (9) is constructed for receiving and
holding a complete functional element and/or forms a complete functional

element with a part inserted therein.
Hand shower according to claim 1, wherein the insert part (9) is located
in the end region of the handle (1) associated with the hose.
Hand shower according to claim 1 or 2, wherein the insert part (9) has
a standardized shape.
Hand shower according to one of the preceding claims, wherein the insert
part (9) has standardized dimensions.
Hand shower according to one of the preceding claims, wherein the insert
part (9) is constructed for holding different functional elements.
Hand shower according to one of the preceding claims, wherein the functional

element is a screen.
Hand shower according to one of the preceding claims, wherein the functional
element is a preferably adjustable restrictor.
Hand shower according to one of the preceding claims, wherein the 
functional element has a cartridge with an additive.
Hand shower according to one of the preceding claims, wherein the
functional element has a ventilator or a return flow preventer.
Hand shower according to one of the preceding claims, wherein the
functional element has a connection possibility for the connecting line to

an additive container.
Hand shower according to one of the preceding claims, wherein the insert
part (29) can be inserted into the handle (1) of the shower and is locked

there by locking means.
Hand shower according to claim 11, wherein the locking means is
constituted by a bayonet catch.
