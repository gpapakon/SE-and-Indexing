<INVENTION-TITLE>
Aqueuos epoxy resin dispersion.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Aqueous epoxy resin dispersions containing   (A) an epoxy resin which is a product of the condensation of one or more epoxide compounds, an aromatic polyol and optionally a modifying compound containing at least two expoxide-reactive groups, (B) a dispersant in the form of a product of the condensation of an aliphatic polyol and an epoxide compound, where the ratio between the number of equivalents of OH groups and epoxide groups is from 1:0.8 to 1:3.5,   (C) a curing agent for the epoxy resin and   (D) optionally conventional additives, as a one- or two-component system, the preparation thereof, and the use thereof as a coating composition.
</ABSTRACT>
