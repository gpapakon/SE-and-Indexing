<INVENTION-TITLE>
Light emitting garments.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A41D1301	A41D1301	G08B500	G08B500	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A41D	A41D	G08B	G08B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A41D13	A41D13	G08B5	G08B5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A light-emitting garment comprises at least one item of clothing (1) which is placed at least partially against a part of the body and on which illuminating elements (14, 15, 16), are attached, preferably removably, which consist of electroluminescence lamps and which receive energy from an energy supply device (20), preferably attached to a strap (2) of the item of clothing (Fig. 1).
