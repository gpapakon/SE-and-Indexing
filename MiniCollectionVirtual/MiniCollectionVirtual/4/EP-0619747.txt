<INVENTION-TITLE>
ENDOTRACHEAL TUBE.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61M1604	A61M1604	A61M2500	A61M2500	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61M	A61M	A61M	A61M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61M16	A61M16	A61M25	A61M25	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A catheter (1) for insertion by its distal end (20) into the human body. The catheter consists of a tube (3) with a central lumen (2) extending lengthwise therein. The tube (3) includes first (31) and second (30) sections in end-to-end abutment. The first section (31) has a longitudinal channel connected to a supply of a substance to be administered, and leading to an opening in the central lumen of the tube near the abutment area (32) between the first and second sections.
</ABSTRACT>
