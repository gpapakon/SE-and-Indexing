<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a gas cooker. This apparatus comprises, associated with at least one gas feed control button (1), a means (7) of indicating the power setting constituted by a small bar of electroluminescent diodes connected in series. The control button (1) comprises a slider (14) which is capable of being brought into contact with one of the studs separating two diodes to light a number of diodes according to the gas feed power. Application mainly to gas cooking tops and to gas cookers.
