Diving jacket

<ABSTRACT>
In a diving jacket with a back part (1) which has at least one chamber which can be filled by a gaseous medium, a head support (20) is to be allocated to the back part (1) if need be with a shoulder portion (7) in between.

<CLAIMS>
Diving jacket having a back section (1) and a shoulder portion (7),
which include at least one chamber which can be filled by a gaseous

medium, characterised in that a head support (20), which can be filled by a
similar gaseous medium, is associated with the shoulder portion (7), said

head support being disposable around the neck of a user.
Diving jacket according to claim 1, characterised in that the head
support (20) forms at least one chin support, preferably two chin supports

(21, 22), in the position of use.
Diving jacket according to claim 2, characterised in that the head
support (20) comprises a neck portion (25), which communicates with the

chin supports (21, 22) at each side.
Diving jacket according to one of claims 1 to 3, characterised in that
the head support (20) is connected to the back section (1) or to the

shoulder portion (7) by a zip fastener, a seam (23) or the like.
Diving jacket according to at least one of claims 1 to 4,
characterised in that a valve (16) is associated with the head support (20).
Diving jacket according to at least one of claims 1 to 5,
characterised in that a CO
2
 or low-pressure connection (24) is associated
with the head support (20).
