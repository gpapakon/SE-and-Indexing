<INVENTION-TITLE>
Bicycle stand with anti-theft device.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B62H300	B62H300	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B62H	B62H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B62H3	B62H3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a bicycle stand with an anti-theft device with a post (1) which is upright when in use and the base of which can be secured on or in the ground; with a bearing surface provided on the post for placing the bicycle against in its parked state. The invention is characterised by the following features: Two hoops (4, 5) are attached in an articulated manner to the post, which hoops are each coupled by one end to the post (1), can be pivoted, for the purpose of securing the bicycle, about a hinge point and in doing so can be passed through the front wheel or the rear wheel, respectively, of the bicycle in order to form a complete, closed loop; a lock is provided for locking the two hoops in their closed position.
