<INVENTION-TITLE>
Retaining device for articles to be stocked in a storing compartment.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F25D2304	F25D2304	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F25D	F25D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F25D23	F25D23	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In order to stop bottles and the like which are stocked in a storing compartment (2) of a refrigerator door (1) and the like which is opened and closed from falling out, slipping, toppling over and the like, dividers (11) and an elastic band (20) cooperating with one another are fitted into the storing compartment (2). The dividers 11 are slidingly displaceable on the wall (5) and the band runs through openings (23) in the same. In use, the dividers (11) are restrained in self-locking fashion from sliding away unintentionally.
