<INVENTION-TITLE>
Filling head for filling machines for filling bottles or similar containers with a liquid.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B67C302	B67C310	B67C326	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B67C	B67C	B67C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B67C3	B67C3	B67C3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a filling head (3), preferably to a filling-tube-less filling head or a filling head with a short filling tube for filling machines for filling bottles (2) or similar containers with a liquid. The filling head (3) has a functional element (14), which can be moved between an upper and a lower stroke position. The movement takes place by means of the pressure of at least one operating medium to which the containers (2) are also subjected in the filling phase and/or in at least one treatment phase preceding or subsequent to the latter.
