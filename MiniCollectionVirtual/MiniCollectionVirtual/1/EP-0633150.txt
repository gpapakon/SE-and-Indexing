<INVENTION-TITLE>
Sealing device for spoked wheels und wheel with such a device.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60B2100	B60B2112	B60C2900	B60C2902	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60B	B60B	B60C	B60C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60B21	B60B21	B60C29	B60C29	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Sealing apparatus for a wheel with spokes and a tubeless tyre, comprising an annular strip (F) which is initially sized so that after it has been fitted onto the rim (J) with which it is intended to interact, it forms two circumferential marginal zones (z1, z2) which overlap the lateral cheeks (j1, j2) of the said rim (J) and which includes two circumferential ribs (N) for holding the beads of the tyre.
