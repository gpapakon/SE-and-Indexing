<ABSTRACT>
Use of an effective quantity of a single substance having both emollient and lipase-inhibiting properties, or alternatively of the combination of an effective quantity of an emollient substance not having activity with respect to lipase action and of a non-emollient substance displaying lipase-inhibiting activity, in a suitable cosmetic base, for the treatment of combination skin.
</ABSTRACT>

</APPLICANTS>

</INVENTORS>
<CLAIMS>
Usage, for the aesthetic treatment of mixed skin, of a cosmetic
composition comprising an effective quantity of a substance chosen

from the oil of Limnanthes and the oil of Jessenia Bataua.
Usage, according to claim 1, characterised in that the substance
is the oil of Limnanthes Alba.
Usage according to claim 2, characterised in that the composition
contains from 0.5 to 15% of the oil of Limnanthes Alba.
Usage according to claim 1, characterised in that the substance
is the oil of Jessenia Bataua.
Usage according to claim 4, characterised in that, the
composition contains from 0.5 to 15% of the oil of Jessenia Bataua.
Usage according to any one of the claims 1 to 5, characterised in
that the composition is the form of a cream, of a serum, or of a

lotion.
</CLAIMS>
