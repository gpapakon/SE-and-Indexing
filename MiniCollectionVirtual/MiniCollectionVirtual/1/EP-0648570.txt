<INVENTION-TITLE>
Welding apparatus.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B23K9133	B23K9133	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B23K	B23K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B23K9	B23K9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A welding apparatus for the fusion welding of light-alloy workpieces under atmospheric conditions by means of a welding filler material in the form of a welding wire (2) made of a material readily reacting with constituents of the atmosphere has a cleaning device (5) for the continuous, mechanical removal of a thin surface layer of the welding wire (2) directly before the welding. This prevents impurities, in particular hydrogen, from being able to reach the weld melt with the welding wire (2).
