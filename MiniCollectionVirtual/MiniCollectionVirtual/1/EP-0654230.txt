<INVENTION-TITLE>
Key-ring.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A45C100	A45C100	A45C1500	A45C1500	F23Q200	F23Q232	F23Q236	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
A keyring attachment is disclosed, which has a keyring (16), an attachment body (1) and a chain (15) which connects the keyring to the attachment body. The attachment body is provided with a holder (18) for a coin (21) in each case. The holder for a coin (21) is arranged at least on the one outer side (17) of the attachment body (1) and has an open end (25) for pushing in and pushing out coins (21) parallel to the outer surface of the attachment body.
