<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a process for fractionating and separating off low-molecular-weight liquid substances contained in process streams, e.g. separating water and higher-molecular-weight substances or condensable components from gases, which is characterised by the following process steps:   a) the process stream is passed via an absorption unit containing a water-insoluble hydrophilic gel which can be collapsed by the action of physicochemical variables, or the absorption unit is passed through the process stream, the gel becoming charged with the low-molecular-weight liquid substance, b) the gel containing the low-molecular-weight constituents being separated off after charging, and c) the gel is then made to collapse by the action of physicochemical variables and the low-molecular-weight constituents separated off are then removed from the absorption unit.
