Electrical power steering apparatus with locking of the steering column.

<ABSTRACT>
Power-assisted steering device with steering lock. The invention relates to the grouping-together of the steering column theft-prevention means and power-assisted steering function. According to the invention, a mechanical locking means makes it possible to immobilise the steering column. In a particular embodiment, the locking member acts via a mechanical locking member (38) on the peripheral teeth (42) of a toothed wheel (34) serving as member for coupling to the power-assisted steering actuator (30-33).
