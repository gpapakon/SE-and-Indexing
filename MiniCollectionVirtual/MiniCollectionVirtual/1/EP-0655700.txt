<INVENTION-TITLE>
Marking of a metal container.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G06K100	G06K112	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G06K	G06K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G06K1	G06K1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The marking, in particular a bar code, is applied directly to the container wall by means of a laser beam. The container preferably consists of stainless steel. In this case the surface remains absolutely smooth and the marking is resistant to aggressive cleaning agents. As a result, logistics can be considerably rationalised using such containers.
</ABSTRACT>
