CLEANING TOOL AND ELECTRICALLY DRIVEN TOOTH-CLEANING INSTRUMENT USING SUCH A TOOL

<ABSTRACT>
The cleaning tool (32) proposed is fitted on an electrically driven tooth-cleaning instrument (1) in such a way that the cleaning tool (32) can be driven to rotate about its longitudinal axis. The cleaning tool (32) is designed as a one-piece component and has a long, thin cleaning shaft. Said shaft has at least one longitudinal edge and a multiplicity of teeth.

drive shaft (16) and set in rotation about its longitudinal

axis,
