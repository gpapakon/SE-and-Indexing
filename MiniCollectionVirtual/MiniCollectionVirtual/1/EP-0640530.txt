<INVENTION-TITLE>
Folding box.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65D550	B65D550	B65D564	B65D566	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65D	B65D	B65D	B65D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65D5	B65D5	B65D5	B65D5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A folding box made of cardboard or similar foldable material, having a bottom section (2) and wall sections linked thereto, and having a lid for closing the folding box. Linked to two mutually opposite boundary edges of the bottom section (2) are a front wall (4) and a rear wall (10), a covering wall strip (6) being linked to the front wall while the rear wall (10) bears an intermediate bottom (14) by means of an additional rear wall strip (12). Linked to the front wall (4) and to the rear wall (10) are lateral connecting flaps (7, 8; 15, 16) which are folded onto the inner side of the two side walls (17, 18) and fixed thereon when the folding box is set up.
