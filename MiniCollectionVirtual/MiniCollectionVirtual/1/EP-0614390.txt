<INVENTION-TITLE>
FIRE EXTINGUISHING DEVICE.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A62C	A62C	A62C	A62C	A62C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention concerns the fire-fighting field and in particular fire extinguishing devices generating extinguishing gas aerosol mixtures and may be used for extinguishing indoor fires. According to the present invention, in a fire extinguishing device having a casing with apertures, a charge therein which, on being burnt, generates a fire extinguishing gas aerosol mixture and an ignition system, use is made as a charge of a substance which, on burning, forms a slag which is contained in one or more cups arranged inside the casing with a space between them and the side walls and directed with their base surfaces towards the apertures in the casing. There are perforations in the side walls of the cups and the total area of the cup bases exceeds the total area of apertures in the casing.
</ABSTRACT>
