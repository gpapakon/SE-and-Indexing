<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for annealing products of high chrome manganese steel.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C21D126	C21D132	C21D176	C21D176	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C21D	C21D	C21D	C21D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C21D1	C21D1	C21D1	C21D1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Process for annealing products of steel manufacture made of carbon steel whose chromium content is between 0.15 and 1.5 % by weight and whose manganese content is between 0.15 and 1.5 % by weight, according to which the said products are allowed to stay in an annealing furnace under nitrogen atmosphere to bring about spheroidising of the perlite, characterised in that oxygen is added to the said atmosphere in a content higher than or equal to 0.5 % by volume.
</ABSTRACT>
