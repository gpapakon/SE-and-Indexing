<INVENTION-TITLE>
Transport container having at least two swinging walls.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65D2106	B65D2106	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65D	B65D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65D21	B65D21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a transport container, in particular made of plastic, having at least two pivotable walls. The invention consists in that the size of the area of the container bottom (3, 4, 35 to 38) can be changed, and in that the lower edge at least of the pivotable container walls (1, 2, 18, 12) is connected to the edge of the container bottom and follows the latter when the bottom area is changed so that the wall assumes an oblique position when the bottom area is reduced and a vertical position when the bottom area is enlarged.
