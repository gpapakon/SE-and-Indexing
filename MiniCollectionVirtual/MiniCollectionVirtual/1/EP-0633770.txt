<INVENTION-TITLE>
CAPSULE FILLED WITH A MIXTURE OF ACTIVE INGREDIENT IN POWDER FORM AND HIGHLY DISPERSED SILICON DIOXIDE.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61K948	A61K948	A61K31185	A61K3119	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61K	A61K	A61K	A61K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61K9	A61K9	A61K31	A61K31	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention pertains to a pharmaceutical preparation in the form of a quick-release capsule that is filled with a mixture of active ingredient in powder form and highly dispersed silicon dioxide as inactive ingredient, and to a process for producing it.
