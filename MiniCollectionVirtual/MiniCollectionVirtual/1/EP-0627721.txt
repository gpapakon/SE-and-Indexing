<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for the sales presentation of a rotary brush
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G09F100	G09F2300	G09F2300	G09F110	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The device (10) comprises a plate (11) bearing information elements (14) relating to the brush (1), and a threaded rod (12) integral with the lower edge (13) of the plate. The rod (12) is screwed into the threaded hole (8) provided for the link with the threaded shaft of an apparatus for driving the brush, in such a way that the plate (11) rises upwards when the brush (1) is placed on its face (5) defined by the free ends of the tufts (4).

<CLAIMS>
Device (10) for the display, at places of sale, 
of a rotary tool (1) having a working face (5), which may 

also serve as a rest face by which the tool rests on a 
horizontal receiving surface (6), and a hole (8) for 

fixing it to a rotational driving apparatus, which hole 
is located opposite the working face and the axis (3) of 

which is perpendicular to the latter, the device being 
characterized in that it comprises means (12) of connection 

with the fixing hole so as to extend upwards from 
the tool when the latter is laid on its working face. 
Device according to Claim 1, characterized in 
that the connection means comprise a threaded rod (12) 

for interacting with a threaded fixing hole (8). 
Device according to one of Claims 1 and 2, 
characterized in that it comprises a vertical sheet (11) 

bearing an informative message (14) about the tool. 
Device according to Claim 3, related to Claim 2, 
characterized in that the threaded rod is arranged so as 

to project over the lower edge (13) of the sheet. 
Device according to one of the preceding claims, 
characterized in that it comprises means (15) for hooking 

onto a hook or a hanging rod. 
Assembly comprising a rotary tool (1) having a 
working face (5), which may also serve as a rest face by 

which the tool rests on a horizontal receiving surface 
(6), and a hole (8) for fixing it to a rotational driving 

apparatus, which hole is located opposite the working 
face and the axis (3) of which is perpendicular to the 

latter, and a device (10) according to one of the preceding 
claims, the connection means (12) of which interact  

 
with the hole for fixing the tool. 
</CLAIMS>
