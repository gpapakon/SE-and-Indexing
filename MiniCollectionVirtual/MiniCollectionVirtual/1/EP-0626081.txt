<INVENTION-TITLE>
FUZZY-LOGIC CONTROLLER.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G05B1302	G05B1302	G06F944	G06F944	G06N700	G06N702	G06N704	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G05B	G05B	G06F	G06F	G06N	G06N	G06N	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G05B13	G05B13	G06F9	G06F9	G06N7	G06N7	G06N7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Described is a fuzzy-logic controller which determines a linear relationship between output parameters and input parameters within a hyperquader of the input space exclusively by means of fuzzy-logic operators such as min or max and fuzzy-logic operations such as the calculation of the centre of gravity of associative functions.
</ABSTRACT>
