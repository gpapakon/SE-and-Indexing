<INVENTION-TITLE>
Saw blade with toothing having different pitches
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B23D6100	B23D6102	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B23D	B23D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B23D61	B23D61	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The saw teeth (22a) have a different division in the region of the first section (12a) of the blade than in the second section (14a). The blade can be curved in a circular arc whose centre of curvature coincides with the fixing socket (26a). The saw blade can have an oblong basic shape with the fixing socket at one end and the cutter at the other end.
</ABSTRACT>
