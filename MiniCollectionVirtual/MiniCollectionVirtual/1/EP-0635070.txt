<INVENTION-TITLE>
PROCESS FOR RECYCLING DUST COLLECTED DURING THE PRODUCTION OF STEEL IN AN ELECTRIC ARC FURNACE.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C21C500	C21C528	C21C538	C21C552	C22B702	C22B702	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C21C	C21C	C21C	C21C	C22B	C22B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C21C5	C21C5	C21C5	C21C5	C22B7	C22B7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The process of the invention concerns recycling dust collected during steel manufacture in an electric arc furnace. The colected dust is mixed with fine carbonaceous material in the slag or at the slag and metal bath interface in the melting vessel. The process of the invention enhances the slag's degree of foaming efficiency while improving steel production profitability.
</ABSTRACT>
