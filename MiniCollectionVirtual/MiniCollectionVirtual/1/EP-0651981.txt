<INVENTION-TITLE>
Opthalmoscopic illumination probe.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The illumination probe is provided, in particular, for lateral insertion into the eyeball in opthalmoscopy. It contains an optical fibre (14) whose exit end is suitable together with a device (15, 12) for enlarging the solid angle of the emitted light, and thus the illuminated area. Examples of embodiments of this device are an element (15) resembling a Fresnel lens which is arranged at the fibre end or worked into the fibre end, or a concave recess (12) worked into the fibre end and symmetrical relative to the fibre axis. The illumination probe can be combined with an image conductor.
