<INVENTION-TITLE>
Smoking article with a wrapper producing aroma upon smoking
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A24D102	A24B1528	A24D100	A24B1500	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A24D	A24B	A24D	A24B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A24D1	A24B15	A24D1	A24B15	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Smoking article (cigar or cigarette) containing at least one smoking material chosen from among natural tobacco, reconstituted tobacco and tobacco substitutes comprising natural or synthetic non-tobacco products intended completely or partially to replace tobacco in a smoking article. For this purpose, the wrapper of the article has added to it a mixture of at least one reducing sugar which is non-volatile at room temperature and of at least one nitrogen-containing compound which releases ammonia at the temperature of and throughout the burning of the product.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
Product to be smoked, of the cigar or cigarette type, containing at least one
material to be smoked, selected from natural tobacco, reconstituted tobacco, tobacco

substitutes which include natural or synthetic non-tobacco products proposed for
replacing tobacco totally or partially in a product to be smoked, the product wrapper

of which contains a mixture of at least one reducing sugar, not volatile at ambient
temperature, and of at least one nitrogenous compound releasing ammonia at the

combustion temperatures of the product and during the combustion of same, the
compounds being mixed so as to obtain their combination during the combustion of

the product to be smoked.
Product according to claim 1, wherein the reducing sugars and the nitrogenous
compounds have been mixed in such proportions that nitrogenous heterocycles are

formed by pyrolysis and combination.
Product according to one of claims 1 or 2, wherein the reducing sugars have
been chosen from glucose, fructose and starch hydrolysates.
Product according to one of claims 1 or 2, wherein the nitrogenous
compounds are ammonium salts.
Product according to claim 4, wherein the ammonium salts are chosen from
phosphates, carbonates, citrates and acetates.
Products according to one of claims 1 or 2, wherein the nitrogenous
compounds are amino acids.
Product according to claim 6, wherein the amino acids are chosen from
proline, asparagine, glutamine and phenylalanine. 
Product according to one of claims 1 to 7, wherein the mixture is introduced
into the press, the size press for example, of the machine for manufacturing the paper

of the wrapper.
Product according to one of claims 1 to 7, wherein the mixture is added to the
wrapper as an ingredient of the composition of the paper.
Product according to one of claims 1 to 7, wherein the mixture is added as a
surface product, by induction.
Product according to one of claims 1 to 7, wherein the mixture is directly
added onto the wrapper on the machine for producing cigars or cigarettes.
Product according to one of claims 1 to 7, wherein the mixture is incorporated
in the formulation of the adhesive used in the seam of the wrapper.
Method of manufacturing a product to be smoked according to one of claims 1
to 12, wherein there is added to the wrapper of the product a mixture of at least one

reducing sugar, not volatile at ambient temperature, and of at least one nitrogenous
compound releasing ammonia at the combustion temperatures of the product and

during the combustion of same, the compounds being mixed so as to obtain their
combination during the combustion of the product to be smoked.
Method according to claim 13, wherein the mixture of compounds is added in
solution.
Method according to claim 13, wherein the mixture of compounds is added in
suspension.
</CLAIMS>
