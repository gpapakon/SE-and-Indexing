<INVENTION-TITLE>
Ventilating apparatus for building roofs.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E04D1303	E04D13035	F24F702	F24F702	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E04D	E04D	F24F	F24F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>E04D13	E04D13	F24F7	F24F7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a ventilating apparatus for roof buildings, having at least one ventilating flap (6, 7) which is articulated on one side on a frame (2, 3) via a pivot bearing (4, 5) and can be actuated by a pivot drive (8, 9), and having wind-directing fins (10, 11) which extend perpendicularly to the frame plane in the region of the frame sides adjoining the pivot-bearing side. In order, when the ventilating flap (6, 7) is closed, to avoid dynamic loading of the wind-directing fins (10, 11) by wind and associated development of noise, the wind-directing fins (10, 11) are intended to be connected fixedly on one side to the ventilating flap (6, 7) and, when the ventilating flap (6, 7) is closed, to penetrate into the frame (2, 3).
