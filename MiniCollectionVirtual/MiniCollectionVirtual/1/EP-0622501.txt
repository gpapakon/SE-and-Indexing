<ABSTRACT>
In order to improve the cladding of facades with respect to the capacity for using different functional elements, the invention specifies a device for cladding of the facade, in the case of which a sheet-like facade element (3) which, arranged on a facade substructure (2), forms a facade outer side can be fastened, by means of retaining elements (8), on the facade substructure (2), such that it can move relative thereto, and can be adjusted relative to the facade substructure by at least one actuating member (7).
