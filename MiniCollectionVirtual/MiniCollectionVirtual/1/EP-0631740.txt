Adhesive stick.

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an adhesive stick, having a piston which supports the stick substance and a sleeve which surrounds the stick substance, in which the drive device for moving the stick substance upwards and downwards and also serving as an actuating element is designed, together with the sleeve and the piston, as a uniform injection moulding. This drive and actuating element (2) is of curved design. Two such drive and actuating elements (2A, 2B) can also be provided, located opposite one another and arranged in each case with their one end at the edge of the sleeve (1).
