<INVENTION-TITLE>
THERMOPLASTIC MOULDING COMPOSITIONS BASED ON POLYCARBONATES AND STYRENE COPOLYMERS.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08L5100	C08L5104	C08L6900	C08L6900	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08L	C08L	C08L	C08L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C08L51	C08L51	C08L69	C08L69	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Thermoplastic moulding compositions containing, as essential components,     A) from 40 to 85% by weight of an aromatic polycarbonate and   B) from 15 to 60% by weight of a styrene copolymer built up from     B1) a hard matrix comprising   b1) from 10 to 90% by weight of at least one vinylaromatic monomer and   b2) from 10 to 90% by weight of a C1- to C8-alkyl (meth)acrylate,  and   B2) a soft phase,   where the styrene copolymer is obtained by bulk, solution or suspension polymerisation of the components b1) and b2) building up the hard matrix in the presence of a dissolved, elastomeric graft base.
</ABSTRACT>
