<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to an improved process for the preparation of 1,3-dimethyl-4,5-diaminouracil by catalytic reduction of 1,3-dimethyl-4-amino-5-nitrosouracil in the presence of a palladium-carbon catalyst.
