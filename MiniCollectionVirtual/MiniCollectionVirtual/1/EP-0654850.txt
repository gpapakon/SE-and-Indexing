<INVENTION-TITLE>
Terminal for a circuit board.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H01R1200	H01R1216	H02B1015	H02B104	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H01R	H01R	H02B	H02B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a connecting terminal (2), consisting of a terminal mounting (4) and a clamping point (6), which has a terminal body, a pressure transmission part and a clamping screw (8), and to a multipole connecting terminal (2). According to the invention, the pressure transmitting part of a terminal point (6) is electrically conductively connected to a busbar, this busbar being routed in a base part (16) of the terminal mount (4) and its free end projecting sideways out of this base part (16). A connecting terminal (2), or a multipole connecting terminal (2), is thus obtained for whose use no more additional installation parts and live parts are required.
