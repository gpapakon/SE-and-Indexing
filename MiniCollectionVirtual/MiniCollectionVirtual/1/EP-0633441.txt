Smelting furnace for the thermal treatment of special wastes containing heavy metals and/or dioxine.

<ABSTRACT>
In a melting furnace for the thermal treatment of heavy-metal-containing and/or dioxine-containing special wastes having a main furnace vessel which is provided with a melting trough for receiving a melt (8), at least one feed apparatus (7) for feeding the material to be treated and a discharge chamber (15) spatially separated from the feed apparatus, the discharge chamber is connected to the melting trough in a gas-tight manner via a siphon (9). In the main furnace vessel (1) and in the discharge chamber (15), third heating elements are provided in the form of bath electrodes (19, 20), by means of which an electric current can be passed through the melt (8) and the siphon (9) for additional heating of the melt. In this manner, blockages of the siphon can be reliably avoided.
