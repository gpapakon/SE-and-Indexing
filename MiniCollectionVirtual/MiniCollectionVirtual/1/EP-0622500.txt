<INVENTION-TITLE>
Aeration device for roofs.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E04D1300	E04D1317	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E04D	E04D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>E04D13	E04D13	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Device for ventilating roofs, in the case of which there is provided, on the lower ends of the roof rafters (1), an eaves board which runs transversely with respect to the roof and over which there is guided a plastic sheet (2) located beneath the roof tiles (23). The eaves board is formed by a cross-sectionally essentially trapezoidally designed screen grid (3) which can be fastened on the roof rafters (1), between roof rafters (1) and sheet (2).
