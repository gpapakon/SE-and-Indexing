<INVENTION-TITLE>
FUNGICIDAL 2-IMIDAZOLINE-5-ONE AND 2-IMIDAZOLINE-5-THIONE DERIVATIVES.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A01N4348	A01N4350	A01N4352	C07D23300	C07D23370	C07D23384	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A01N	A01N	A01N	C07D	C07D	C07D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A01N43	A01N43	A01N43	C07D233	C07D233	C07D233	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
2-imidazoline-5-one or 2-imidazoline-5-thione derivatives have general formula (I) in which W is the sulphur or oxygen atom, or the group S=O, n is 0 or 1, A is N(R5) or O or S or C(R5)(R6) or SO2 or C=O, R1 to R4 are hydrogen or a hydrocarbon radical, optionally substituted by halogen atoms in particular. Used as fungicides in agriculture.
</ABSTRACT>
