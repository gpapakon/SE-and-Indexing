<INVENTION-TITLE>
PROCESS FOR THE PRODUCTION OF 2,2,6,6,-TETRAALKYL 4 PIPERIDYLAMINES
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
2,2,6,6-tetraalkyl-4-piperidylamines are produced by 
reacting an amine with a 2,2,6,6-tetraalkyl-4-piperidone in 

the presence of a palladium catalyst.
