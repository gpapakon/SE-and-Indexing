<INVENTION-TITLE>
CIRCUIT ARRANGEMENT FOR TELECOMMUNICATION.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H04J304	H04J304	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H04J	H04J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H04J3	H04J3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A circuit arrangement for telecommunication by means of a multiplexer (1), with the aid of which output signals of a transmitting device (3, 4) can be inserted into a signal frame. In order to avoid unchecked insertion of interference signals during an adjustment routine, a data signal and/or insertion control signal input of the multiplexer is preceded by isolating stages which can be inhibited. The device can be used, in particular, in digital signal transmission devices.
