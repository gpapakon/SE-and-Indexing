Method of transmitting safe information on a bus.

<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a method of transmitting information on a bus (6) allocated to an electrical device (1), the bus connecting one main unit (2) to at least one subsidiary unit (3 to 5). According to the invention, the transmission reliability of information via the bus (6) can be increased if information transmissions are tested for errors, a repetition of the information transmission is carried out in the event of determining a faulty information transmission, a fault report only being carried out, however, when a faulty information transmission has been determined continuously for a prescribed number of successive information transmissions.
