<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a rail block module, which can be cast in the ground, for a current supply system of an electric floor conveyor with two current rails in an insulating block, one of which is provided on the floor of a guide duct for the engagement of the steering system of an industrial vehicle.

Rail block module which can be cast in the floor
and is intended for a power supply system of an electric

industrial-conveyor track, having two busbar units (12,

block, the first busbar (18) being situated at the bottom

guidance system of an industrial-conveyor vehicle.
