<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the production of polyphenylene ethers.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08G6500	C08G6544	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08G	C08G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C08G65	C08G65	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A process for the preparation of polyphenylene ethers from monohydric phenols which contain alkyl substituents in both ortho-positions or additionally in one meta-position, but not in the para-position, by oxidative coupling with oxygen-containing gases in the presence of an oxidation catalyst and in the presence of a compound which regulates the molecular weight, in organic solvents, characterised in that the molecular weight regulator used is an aliphatic, aromatic or cycloaliphatic mono- or dicarboxylic acid having 1 to 24 carbon atoms.
</ABSTRACT>
