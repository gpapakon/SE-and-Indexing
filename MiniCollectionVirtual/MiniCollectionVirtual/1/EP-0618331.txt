<INVENTION-TITLE>
Curbs, method of fabrication and device for realizing curbs.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E01C1900	E01C1948	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E01C	E01C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>E01C19	E01C19	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device for producing curbs (kerbs) (1) made of coated mixes. This device comprises:     - a hopper (4),   - a moulding tunnel (5),   - a vibrator-compactor (vibrocompactor) (6),   The hopper (4) supplying the moulding tunnel (5) with coated mixes, and the vibrator/compactor (6) being connected to the moulding tunnel which it vibrates and advances. The invention also relates to curbs and their method of production.
