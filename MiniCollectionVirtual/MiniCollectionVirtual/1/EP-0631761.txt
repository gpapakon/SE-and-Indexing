Bandage for limbs.

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a bandage (14) for limbs, in particular a fetlock bandage for hooved animals. It comprises an essentially rectangular multi-layered wrap-around cushioning (20) on the outside of which there is fitted an elastic band (22). The bandage (14) can thus be applied quickly and is substantially secured against slipping.
