<INVENTION-TITLE>
Dispenser for cartridges
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B05C17005	B05C17015	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B05C	B05C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B05C17	B05C17	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The output device has a thrust device with a cylinder (4) and piston arrangement. The piston is displaced by pressure on one end. A pressure chamber is at least partly formed in the cylinder on the drive side of the piston. A valve regulates a connection line between a pressurized gas bottle (7) and the pressure chamber. A locking device can hold the piston in any position. An Independent claim is also included for a pressure reduction valve for the output device.
</ABSTRACT>
