<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an adhesive label as well as to its method of manufacture. The adhesive label (1) consists of a frontal (2) adhesive (3) material and of an anti-adhesion protector (4). Moreover, it includes at least one peel-off or tear-off element (5) arranged between the frontal material (2) and the anti-adhesion protector (4), at least partially secured to the frontal material (2).
