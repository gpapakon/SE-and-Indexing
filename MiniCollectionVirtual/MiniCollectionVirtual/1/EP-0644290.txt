<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for rinsing and spinning the laundry in a washing machine.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>D06F3500	D06F3500	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>D06F	D06F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>D06F35	D06F35	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Method for rinsing and spinning laundry in a programme-controlled washing machine having a washing drum (2) which is mounted in a tub (1) and in which the water is conveyed via an injection device (15) into the laundry ring (16) bearing on the inside of the washing-drum casing and the rinsing stage is divided into a plurality of rinsing cycles, each of the rinsing cycles consisting of a water-injection phase and of a subsequent spinning phase. In order to ensure a uniform dampening of the laundry for each of the individual rinsing cycles, a redistribution of the laundry is carried out for each of the rinsing cycles before the spinning phase.
