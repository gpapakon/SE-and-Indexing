<ABSTRACT>
A process for concentrating the anabolic p-hydroxyphenylpyruvate dioxygenases from vegetable tissues, and a method with which the enzymatic activity of the enzyme can be easily measured without complete purification of the enzyme being necessary, are described. The invention additionally describes a test system for identifying inhibitors of p-hydroxyphenylpyruvate dioxygenase from plants, in which an enriched HPPD from plants is incubated with a test substrate to be investigated, and the enzymatic activity of the enzyme is measured and compared with the activity of the uninhibited enzyme. The invention additionally describes the herbicidal agents of the general formula (I) which have been found by the test method according to the invention.
