<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to grease removing compositions comprising a) at least one product selected amongst the hydrochlorofluorocarbons, the hydrofluorocarbons or the perfluorocarbons; (b) at least one alcochol; (c) at least one ether having an OH function; d) optionally at least one product selected amongst water and esters.
