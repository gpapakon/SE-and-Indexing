<INVENTION-TITLE>
Capacitor device for a semiconductor circuit arrangement and method for making the same
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H01L2102	H01L2102	H01L21334	H01L2170	H01L218242	H01L27108	H01L27108	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H01L	H01L	H01L	H01L	H01L	H01L	H01L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H01L21	H01L21	H01L21	H01L21	H01L21	H01L27	H01L27	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Capacitor arrangement comprises a first electrode region (44) and a dielectric region (46) formed in a semiconductor substrate (20, 21), a passivating region, an insulating region and/or a surface region (20a, 21a). The first electrode region is partially made from a metallic material, a metal nitride or similar material. An Independent claim is also included for a process for the production of a capacitor arrangement. Preferred Features: The dielectric region is partially made from silicon nitride having an increased dielectric constant. A second electrode region (48) is formed close to the dielectric region and is partially made from a metallic material, a metal nitride or similar material. The electrode regions are partially made from aluminum (Al), tungsten (W), tungsten nitride (WN), tantalum (Ta), tantalum nitride (TaN), titanium (Ti), titanium nitride (TiN), hafnium (Hf), hafnium nitride (HfN), zirconium (Zr), zirconium nitride (ZrN), molybdenum (Mo), molybdenum nitride (MoN), yttrium (Y), yttrium nitride (YN), lanthanum (La), lanthanum nitride (LaN), cerium (Ce) and/or cerium nitride (CeN). The dielectric region is made from alumina (Al2O3), titania (TiO2), tantalum oxide (Ta2O5), hafnia (HfO2), zirconia (ZrO2), tungsten trioxide (WO3), molybdenum oxide (MoO2), yttrium oxide (Y2O3), lanthanum oxide (La2O3), ceria (CeO2) and/or magnesia (MgO).
</ABSTRACT>
