<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an agent and method for the immunological determination of diphenhydramine in body fluids, and to the tracers, immunogens and antibodies necessary for a determination and to their preparation. The tracers and immunogens employed are compounds of the general formula (I)

  in which     U = H or W,   V = X-(CH2)n-Y or X-W,   A = [(CH2)k-X-(Q)m-Z]m,   W = (CH2)n-(Q)m-Z,   X = O, S or NR,   Y = COOR or NR2,   Q = crosslinker or spacer,   Z = marker or carrier,   R = H or alkyl having 1-8 C atoms,   n = 1-10,   m = 0 or 1,   k = 0-4, and the substituent A is only present if U = H and V = X-(CH2)n-Y.
