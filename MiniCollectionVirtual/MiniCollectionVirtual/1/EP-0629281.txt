<ABSTRACT>
This drier (1) with a spiral guide has an initial warm outer region (12) and an adjoining inner region (13) at lower temperature. The inner region (13) takes the form of a climatic region with a predetermined air humidity. The dimensional variations and changes occurring in the drying process with warmer air in the warmer outer region (12) are compensated in the inner region (13) by the addition of chemical-free moisture.
</ABSTRACT>
