<INVENTION-TITLE>
Rotor for drum chopper respectively shredder.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B02C1806	B02C1818	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B02C	B02C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B02C18	B02C18	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The rotor (2) for a drum chopper respectively shredder (20), with radially projecting cutter bars (1) extending over the entire length of the rotor, is characterised by guide pipes (3) which serve for the in each case insertable reception of a cutter bar (1), are longitudinally slotted on the circumference of the rotor (2) and are welded on the rotor. As a result of these measures, rapid exchange of the cutter bars is possible.
