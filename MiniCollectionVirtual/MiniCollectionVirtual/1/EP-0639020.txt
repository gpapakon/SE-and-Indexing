<INVENTION-TITLE>
Terminal and access modules assembly for telecommunications installation.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H04M1108	H04M1108	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H04M	H04M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H04M11	H04M11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The terminal (PA) contains means (DIPA) for corresponding with an access module and means (ME) for establishing intercommunications with at least one telecommunications network (RT). Each access module comprises authentication means (AUMA1, AUMA2) and means (DIMA) for corresponding with the terminal. The application-specific functions are transferred into the access modules, the terminal fulfilling only basic functions common to all the applications. Application in telecommunications.
