<INVENTION-TITLE>
Package for goods.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65D7300	B65D7300	B65D8542	B65D8542	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65D	B65D	B65D	B65D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65D73	B65D73	B65D85	B65D85	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A package for goods, in particular for fluorescent tubes, having a punch-out to receive a goods carrier, which package has an outer region (A) which can be folded together to form a box which surrounds the goods to be packaged over at least part of their length, a top region (B) which can be folded back on to itself along a transverse fold (2), and two punch-outs (1, 1'), arranged in mirror image relative to the transverse folder (2), to receive a goods carrier, and an inner region (C) which adjoins the top region (B), can be folded back with the top region (B), is arranged in the finished package inside the box formed by the outer region (A) and can be connected in a supporting manner to the goods to be packaged.
