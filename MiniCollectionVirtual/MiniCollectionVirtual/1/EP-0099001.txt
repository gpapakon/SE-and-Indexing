<INVENTION-TITLE>
METHOD AND APPARATUS FOR MEASURING THE WHEEL LOADS OF FAST MOVING RAILROAD CARS
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01G1902	G01G1904	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01G	G01G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01G19	G01G19	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The apparatus has at least one measuring point and at least one strain gauge mounted on the web of a rail at the level of the neutral fibre. A reliable determination of the wheel loads becomes possible if the main direction of action of the strain gauge is arranged orthogonally to the neutral fibre (vertically) and if, to determine the wheel loads when a wheel passes over a measuring point, its measured signal is only detected above a certain threshold value, the detected measured signal being approximated by a regression polynomial and the wheel load then being determined from the threshold value and the distance from the threshold value and the maximum of the regression polynomial.
