<INVENTION-TITLE>
Resinous matters partially crosslinked with metal compounds.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08F800	C08F842	C08K500	C08K509	C08K5098	C08L2300	C08L2300	C08L2310	C08L2314	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08F	C08F	C08K	C08K	C08K	C08L	C08L	C08L	C08L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C08F8	C08F8	C08K5	C08K5	C08K5	C08L23	C08L23	C08L23	C08L23	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Partially crosslinked plastic compositions having a melt flow index of from 0.1 to 100 g/10 min, at 230 DEG C and under a weight of 2.16 kg, comprising   a1) a polymer comprising from 25 to 97% by weight of a propylene copolymer (I) which contains from 0.1 to 15% by weight of copolymerised C2-C10-alk-1-enes, and from 3 to 75% by weight of a further propylene copolymer (II) which contains from 15 to 80% by weight of copolymerised C2-C10-alk-1-enes, or a2) a polymer comprising from 25 to 97% by weight of a propylene homopolymer and from 3 to 75% by weight of a propylene copolymer containing copolymerised C2-C10-alk-1-enes, where the comonomer content is from 15 to 80% by weight, based on the latter component, or a3) a propylene homopolymer having a melt flow index of from 0.01 to 100 g/10 min, at 230 DEG C and under a weight of 2.16 kg, or a4) a random propylene copolymer containing copolymerised C2-C10-alk-1-enes, where the comonomer content is less than 15% by weight and   b) a metal compound of one of the following formulae (Ia) and (Ib) 
<

>
  where     R is hydrogen or a C1-C4-alkyl group, M1 is a metal from main group II of the Periodic Table or zinc, and    M2 is boron or aluminium,   obtainable by reacting the polymer a) with the metal compound b) at temperatures of from 180 to 280 DEG C, pressures of from 1 to 100 bar and mean residence times of the reaction mixture of from 0.2 to 10 minutes.
</ABSTRACT>
