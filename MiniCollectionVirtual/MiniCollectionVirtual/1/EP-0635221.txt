<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to an article of footwear characterised in that it is provided with an insulating device (5) arranged under the insole (3). The invention applies more particularly to the field of shoe manufacture, particularly shoes with an injected wear sole.

<CLAIMS>
Article of footwear, characterised in that it is provided
with an insulating device (5) disposed beneath the insole (3)

and consisting of an elongate element (6) disposed at least
beneath the support zone for the sole of the user's foot and

delimiting, beneath the insole (3), at least one air space,
the elongate element (6) having the form of a false sole fixed

by its periphery (7) by welding, adhesion or sewing to the
internal face of the insole (3) and equipped with spacer means

(8), preferably in the form of ribs, delimiting air spaces
with said insole (3).
Article of footwear according to claim 1, characterised
in that the spacer means (8) extend transversely to the

longitudinal axis of the elongate element (6).
Article of footwear according to claim 1, characterised
in that the spacer means (8) extend parallel to the

longitudinal axis of the elongate element (6).
Article of footwear according to any one of claims 1 to
3, characterised in that the spacer means (8) in the form of

ribs are provided with at least one interruption in the form
of a cut-out allowing communication with the adjoining air

space delimited by the following or preceding means (8) or
rib.
Article of footwear according to claim 1, characterised
in that the insole (3) is provided with perforations (9) over

all or a proportion of its surface extending above the air
space or spaces delimited by the means (8) or ribs provided on

the elongate element (6). 
Article of footwear according to any one of claims 1 to
5, characterised in that the space or spaces delimited between

the insole (3) and the elongate element (6) are partially
filled with a solid material with a deodorising and/or

desiccating action having long-lasting effects.
Article of footwear according to any one of claims 1 to
6, characterised in that the elongate element (6) is provided,

on the one hand, with an upper closure part delimiting, with
the lower part, a flat pouch and, on the other hand, with an

aspiration means equipped with a valve and with a reversal
means also equipped with a valve, said aspiration means

opening at the exterior of the article of footwear or at the
interior thereof and said reversal means in the form of

nozzles opening beneath the sole of the foot and/or the toes,
and optionally close to the heel.
Article of footwear according to any one of claims 1 to
7, characterised in that the insulating device (5) is combined

with an air circulating pump disposed in the heel portion of
the sole and connected by a conduit to the air space delimited

by the elongate element (6), said pump aspirating air to the
exterior of the article of footwear or to the interior

thereof.
