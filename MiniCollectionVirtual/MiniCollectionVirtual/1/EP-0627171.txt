<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process and apparatus for casting chocolate mass in mold.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A23G104	A23G120	A23G302	A23G302	A23G320	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A23G	A23G	A23G	A23G	A23G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A23G1	A23G1	A23G3	A23G3	A23G3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Process and apparatus for casting chocolate compound in moulds, the chocolate compound being inserted in different quantities along the axis of the slab of chocolate to be produced, and, to achieve this, the driving means of the pump for delivering the chocolate compound and the driving means for moving the casting device towards the mould taking the form of controllable motors which are operatively connected to an NC controller.
