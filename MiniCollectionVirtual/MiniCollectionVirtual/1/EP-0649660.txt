<INVENTION-TITLE>
Pharmaceutical composition for parenteral, enteral and dermal administration of essentially insoluble drugs and process for their preparation.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
A pharmaceutical composition containing a drug which is virtually insoluble in water and lipophilic media and containing one or more physiologically tolerated amphosurfactant(s) which form solutions or micellar colloidal solutions in water and which are present in a form dissolved in one or more physiologically tolerated, anhydrous and water-miscible solvent(s) is described. This pharmaceutical composition is used dermally, buccally or enterally or, after addition of water or water-containing solutions, parenterally, enterally or buccally.
</ABSTRACT>
