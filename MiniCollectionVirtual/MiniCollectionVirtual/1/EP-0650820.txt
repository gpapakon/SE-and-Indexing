<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for making automatically spectacle frames in plastic, and device for its execution.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B29C6544	B29C6544	B29D1200	B29D1202	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B29C	B29C	B29D	B29D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B29C65	B29C65	B29D12	B29D12	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The device comprises a conveyor (1) provided with a clamp (10) for gripping a strip (sheet) of plastic (2), on which conveyor are arranged, in succession, a heat generator (3), a station (4) for putting metal reinforcements (40) into place, a means (5) for movably gripping the end (20) of the said strip (2), a transverse sawing means and a milling profile-copying (master-control) template.
