<INVENTION-TITLE>
Biodegradable absorbent product for industrial use based on flax fibres and a binding agent.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A01K1015	A01K1015	B01J2022	B01J2024	C09K332	C09K332	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
Biodegradable absorbent product for industrial use. This absorbent product is in the form of crumbs, of density between 0.3 and 0.4 g/cm
<
3
>
, and consists of flax chaff and of a binding agent in the form of a mixture of at least one animal fat and of a lignosulphonate. This product makes it possible to obtain excellent absorption, and extended use and ease of recovery.
</ABSTRACT>
