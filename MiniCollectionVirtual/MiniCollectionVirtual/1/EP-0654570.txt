Strut.

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a strut (support), in particular a heavy-load strut, which exhibits head and foot parts (9, 10) which are of identical shapes and dimensions and can be displaced axially via a strut centre part (1). The head and foot parts (9, 10) exhibit strut end parts (16) which are fastened pivotably on a spindle (15) in each case. Furthermore, provision is made, on the head and foot parts (9, 10), for coupling parts (11) for connection to the strut centre part.
