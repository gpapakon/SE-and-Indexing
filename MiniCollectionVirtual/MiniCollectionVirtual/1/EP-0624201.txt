<INVENTION-TITLE>
METHOD FOR PRODUCING SOLUBLE T-RECEPTORS BY CO-TRANSFECTION, AND USE OF RESULTING PRODUCTS.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
A method for producing soluble T-receptors, wherein DNA sequences coding for each of the T-receptor subunits deleted from the T-receptor transmembrane portion are co-transfected in a host cell. The resulting products and their diagnostic and therapeutical uses are also disclosed.
</ABSTRACT>
