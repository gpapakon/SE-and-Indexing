<INVENTION-TITLE>
CONTAINER ENGAGING AND LIFTING DEVICE.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60P164	B60P164	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60P	B60P	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60P1	B60P1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A device for engaging and lifting a container (15) set on the ground, by means of a trailer (10-14) which has a horizontal U-shaped frame (11, 12) for gripping the container along both its longitudinal sides and a transverse side, as well as a draw-bar (14) for coupling to a shunting vehicle. Lifting arrangements (20) for lifting the container (15) are arranged at the front and at the rear of both branches (12) of the U-shaped frame (11, 12).
</ABSTRACT>
