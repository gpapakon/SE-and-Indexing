<INVENTION-TITLE>
Door control unit.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
A door control unit, in particular for elevator doors and/or other sliding doors which meets a high safety standard and improves the operating ease by an increased degree of automation. The door control unit comprises a programmable microcontroller (1), to which programmable control units (9) and operating units (4), which are at a higher level than the sensor elements (2), can be connected. The microcontroller (1) is effectively connected to the drive motor (6) of the door (22) or the doors and exhibits a switching device (5) with the selection of at least two operating modes.
