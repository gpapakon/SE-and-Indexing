<INVENTION-TITLE>
Crushing device.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B02C100	B02C102	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B02C	B02C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B02C1	B02C1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In order, in the case of a crushing device, preferably for the selective comminution of collected glass, to reduce the amount of fine fragments, it is proposed that there be arranged, within a fixed frame (7), a fixed plate (1), connected rigidly to the frame (7), and a plate (1) connected movably to the frame (7), said plates being arranged at an acute, upwardly open angle (15) with respect to one another, thus forming a gap (10b) defined by a minimum distance of the lower end regions (1b, 2b) of the plates (1, 2) with respect to one another, and that there are provided means for the powered movement of the movable plate (2) relative to the fixed plate (2).
