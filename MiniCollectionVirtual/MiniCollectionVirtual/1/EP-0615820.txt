<INVENTION-TITLE>
Wet razor.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B26B2100	B26B2152	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B26B	B26B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B26B21	B26B21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a wet razor having a handle (1) on one end of which there is formed a razor head (2). A razor blade unit (3) is arranged on the said razor head (2). In order to improve the ergonomic properties of a handle in the case of a wet razor of the generic type, to ensure that the wet razor is guided more evenly and uniformly, it is proposed that the handle (1) be curved in the direction pointing towards the surface to be shaved in the operating position.
