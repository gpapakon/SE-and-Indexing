<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for recycling waste elastomeric materials based on silane-crosslinked polyethylene or ethylene copolymers.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08J1100	C08J1128	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08J	C08J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C08J11	C08J11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Process for recycling scrap elastomeric materials based on polyethylene or on copolymers of ethylene and of another olefinic monomer, which are crosslinked by silane, with a view to the manufacture of regenerated elastomeric materials, especially for sheathing electrical cables. Dicyclohexylcarbodiimide or a polydicyclohexylcarbodiimide, in a weight proportion of 0.5 % to 5 % relative to the weight of the elastomeric material, is added to the scrap before it is kneaded with heating for reuse.
