<INVENTION-TITLE>
Alkali citrates and their use as medicaments, especially against arterosclerosis.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61K31185	A61K3119	C07C5900	C07C59265	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61K	A61K	C07C	C07C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the use of alkali metal hydrogen citrate compositions or compounds for the prophylaxis and dissolving of calcareous deposits in blood vessels, in particular associated with arteriosclerosis.
</ABSTRACT>
