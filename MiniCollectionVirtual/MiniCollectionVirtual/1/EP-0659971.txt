<INVENTION-TITLE>
Clamping element for fixing a glass pane in a frame or in a cutout of a door panel.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E06B354	E06B354	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E06B	E06B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>E06B3	E06B3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Clamping element for fixing a glass pane in a frame or in a cutout of a door panel, the clamping element (1) being of an elastically compliant and angular design, the two legs (2,3) each exhibiting a tongue (2a, 3a) which is angled off with respect to the leg (2,3), and the tongues (2a, 3a) resting against the glass pane (4).
