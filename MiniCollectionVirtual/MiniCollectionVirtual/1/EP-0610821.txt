<INVENTION-TITLE>
Holder for a wet razor
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A45D2722	A45D2700	A45D2729	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A45D	A45D	A45D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A45D27	A45D27	A45D27	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In order to be able to produce a holder for a wet razor in a more economical and stable fashion, it is proposed that the holding device for the wet razor be composed of a shell-like recess (3) and of an upwardly directed support (4) which is arranged on one edge of the recess.

<CLAIMS>
A holder a for wet razor having a base provided with a holding device 
for holding the wet razor in an essentially upright position, wherein, the 

holding device comprises a recess (3), characterised in that 
a supporting device (4) is provided at an edge of the recess (3). 
A holder according to claim 1, characterised in that the supporting 
device (4) comprises an upwardly extending support (4) arranged on 

an edge of the recess (3). 
A holder according to claim 2, characterised in that the recess (3) 
comprises an essentially semi circular upper edge with a cross-wise 

diametrical edge (6), wherein the support (4) is arranged on the 
diametrical edge (6). 
A holder according to claim 3, characterised in that the diametrical 
edge (6) is formed from a larger radius than the remaining semi 

circular upper edge (5). 
A holder according to any one of claims 3 or 4, characterised in that 
the form of the recess (3) is complimentary to the form of the lower 

edge (8) of the handle of a wet razor (7), and that an essentially 
vertical surface is formed in the region of the diametrical edge (6). 
A holder according to any one of claims 3 to 5, characterised in that 
the recess (3) is bevelled to the semi circular upper edge (5). 
A holder according to any one of claims 3 to 5, characterised in that 
the support (4) is a support wall arranged on a vertical axis. 
A holder according to any one of claims 2 to 7, characterised in that 
the support height (11) is located below the centre of gravity level of 

the inserted wet razor.  
 
A holder according to any of the preceding claims, characterised in that 
a slot-like recess is arranged in the base portion (2) for the box-shaped 

dispensers for razor blade units. 
A holder according to any of the preceding claims, characterised in that 
the base portion (2) comprises feet (13) on its underside. 
A holder according to any of the preceding claims, characterised in that 
the holder (1) is a one-piece plastic shape. 
A holder according to any of the preceding claims, characterised in that 
the recess (3) is formed as a through opening in the base portion. 
A holder according to claim 12, characterised in that in the underneath 
part of the through opening an enlarged free space is formed. 
</CLAIMS>
