<INVENTION-TITLE>
Table-top element and table-top combination for office desks.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47B1308	A47B1310	A47B8700	A47B8700	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47B	A47B	A47B	A47B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47B13	A47B13	A47B87	A47B87	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a table-top element (A,B,C,D,E) for office desks having four lateral edges, of which all or at least two are curved with the same radius of curvature, as well as to a table-top combination for office desks of at least two table-tops which each have at least one lateral edge which are curved with the same radius of curvature but in the opposite direction.
