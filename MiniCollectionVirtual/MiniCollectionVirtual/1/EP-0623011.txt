<INVENTION-TITLE>
DEVICE FOR SPRAYING THERAPEUTICAL SUBSTANCES
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
A device for spraying therapeutical substance(s) in veterinary medicine. The device comprises a muzzle (1) connected to a compressed air source (3) via a pneumatic duct (2) and provided with a spray-forming chamber (4) having at least one spray nozzle (5).
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
Nebulizing device for therapeutical 
substance(s), comprising a face mask (1) connected through 

a pneumatic duct (2) with a pressed air source (3), 
characterized in that a nebulizing chamber (4) is arranged 

in the mask (1) and comprises at least one nebulizer (5) 
connected with the pneumatic duct, the connection between 

the pneumatic duct (2) and the nebulizer(s) (5) consists in 
a cover (6) closing the nebulizing chamber (4) and 

comprising at least a tip (7) connected through a seal (8) 
with the nebulizer(s) (5) so that, by closing either the 

cover (6) or the nebulizing chamber (4), each nebulizer (5) 
is connected with a tip (7). 
Device according to claim 1, characterized in 
that the seal (8) consists in a rubber membrane (9) 

arranged between the nebulizing chamber (4) and its housing 
(6). 
Device according to claim 1, characterized in 
that 
the seal (8) consists in a nipple (10) secured on the 
tip end (7), preferably by screwing. 
Device according to any of the preceding 
claims, characterized in that the seal (8) is maintained on 

the tip (7) and/or on the lower end (12) of the nebulizer 
through a securing O-ring (11). 
Device according to any of the preceding 
claims, characterized in that the face mask (1) comprises 

a deformation (15) for receiving an exchangeable gasket  
 

(20) and adapted for the animal head and securing straps 
(16). 
Device according to any of the preceding 
claims, characterized in that the pneumatic duct comprises 

a flow rate regulator (18) arranged between the face mask 
(1) and the pressed air source (3). 
Device according to any of the preceding 
claims, characterized in that the face mask (1) comprises 

one or more openings (20, 21) to allow for the breathing 
through the nose and/or the mouth of the animal and/or for 

the flowing of the animal saliva. 
Device according to any of the preceding 
claims, characterized in that the nebulizing chamber (4) 

comprises at least two nebulizers (5). 
Device according to any of the preceding 
claims, characterized in that the therapeutical substances 

are nebulized as particles the median mass aerodynamical 
diameter of which is comprised between 100 and 0.01 Âµm. 
Device according to any of the preceding 
claims, characterized in that the pressed air source is a 

transportable dry air compressor. 
</CLAIMS>
