<INVENTION-TITLE>
ADVANCED, EVOLVING DENTAL IMPLANT SYSTEM.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61C800	A61C800	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61C	A61C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61C8	A61C8	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Improved dental implant system for fitting into the jaws. The implant system is characterized in that the implant-peg assembly is formed by multiple indexing, the implant including an xn-sided female portion and the peg an n-sided male portion. According to a second embodiment of the invention, the implant comprises an n-sided male portion and the peg an xn-sided female portion.
</ABSTRACT>
