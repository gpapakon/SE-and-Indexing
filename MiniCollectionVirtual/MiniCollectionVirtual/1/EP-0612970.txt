<INVENTION-TITLE>
Cartridge belt made of flexible material
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F42B3900	F42B3908	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F42B	F42B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F42B39	F42B39	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The new cartridge belt (1), which is made of a flexible synthetic material and has chambers (2) for cartridges (7) (which chambers are stamped in at a preselected distance from one another), is constructed as a narrow strip in which the chambers (2) are woven in as closed rings or eyes (3) which can be displaced axially over the cartridges (7) as far as a stop (9) at the approximate centre of gravity of the cartridges (7).

<CLAIMS>
Cartridge belt (1) of a flexible plastic or a strip 
of fabric of natural fibres with chambers or compartments 

impressed therein at a preselected distance from one 

another and with cartridges (7) which are inserted in 
said receiving chambers (2) and which are provided 

optionally with or without cases, the cartridge belt (1) 
being a narrow band in which the receiving chambers (2) 

are formed as closed rings or eyes (3), characterised by 
the fact that the receiving chambers (2) are pushed over 

the cartridges (7) as far as a stop (9) approximately at 
the centre of mass of the cartridge (7), the stop (9) 

being constructed as an annular groove (10) or embossing 
continuing annularly around the case (11) of the 

cartridge (7) and having a depth of at least twice the 
thickness of the cartridge belt band (4). 
Cartridge belt in accordance with Claim 1, 
characterised by the fact that the cartridge belt (1) is 

made of a material capable of being separated and/or 
closed. 
Cartridge belt in accordance with Claim 1 or 2, 
characterised by the fact that the beginning (5) and/or  

 
end (6) of the belt are bevelled in the zone of a 

receiving chamber (2). 
Cartridge belt in accordance with Claim 1 or 2, 
characterised by the fact that the cartridge belt (1) 

consists of a fabric strip of synthetic or natural fibre 
in which receiving chambers (2) are provided in the form 

of rings or eyes, while the parts of the fabric strip 
which are between the receiving chambers (2) are 

permanently interwoven. 
Cartridge belt in accordance with any one of the 
preceding claims, characterised by the fact that the 

cartridge belt (1) is impregnated with resin. 
</CLAIMS>
