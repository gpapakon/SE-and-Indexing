<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for determining the end of a filtration operation.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B01D2901	B01D2901	B01D3700	B01D3704	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B01D	B01D	B01D	B01D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B01D29	B01D29	B01D37	B01D37	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In a filtration operation, liquid is pressed through a filter medium using pressure acting on the liquid surface. This pressure is continuously determined. If at the end of the filtration operation a pressure change occurs because of the gas then flowing instead of the liquid through the filter medium, the end of the filtration operation is concluded therefrom.
</ABSTRACT>
