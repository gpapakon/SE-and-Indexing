<INVENTION-TITLE>
Leak control of a gas or liquid gas container.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method and a device for leak control of a gas container or liquid gas container. For simple and reliable leak control, it is provided that points of the container (1) in danger of leaking be connected to a measuring space (2) which is at least partly evacuated, and a change of the pressure in the measuring space (2) be used as a measure of the tightness of the container (1).
