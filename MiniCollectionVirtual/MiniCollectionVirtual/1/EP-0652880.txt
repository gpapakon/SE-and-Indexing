<INVENTION-TITLE>
IMPROVED PROCESS FOR PREPARING XANTHINE DERIVATES, IN PARTICULAR 1,3-DIPROPYL-8-(3-OXOCYCLOPENTYL)-XANTHINE.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C07D31900	C07D31908	C07D40500	C07D40512	C07D47300	C07D47306	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C07D	C07D	C07D	C07D	C07D	C07D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C07D319	C07D319	C07D405	C07D405	C07D473	C07D473	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
An improved process is disclosed for preparing xanthines substituted at position 8 by an alkaline ring closure reaction of the corresponding uracils in the presence of lithium hydroxide.
