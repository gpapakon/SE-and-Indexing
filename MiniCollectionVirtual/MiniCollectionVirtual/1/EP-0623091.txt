<CLAIMS>
Lap carrier (1, 21) for holding yarns, with a rotationally 
symmetrical body (9), of which the external face forms a yarn-carrying 

surface (7) with a band (4) projecting axially at one 
end with a yarn reserve groove (6) and a holder (2) located at 

the other end for the band (4), characterized in that the lap 
carrier (1, 21) has, at the end comprising the holder (2), an 

additional band (3) which is offset inwardly radially to the 
holder (2) and, in its external dimensions, corresponds to the 

internal dimensions of the band (4) located at the other end, the 
additional band (3) extending axially roughly to the end face of 

the holder (2) or beyond. 
Lap carrier according to Claim 1, characterized in that each 
lap carrier has a stop shoulder (5, 5') for a band (3, 4) of an 

axially adjacent lap carrier (1). 
Lap carrier according to Claim 2, characterized in that the 
stop shoulder (5) is formed on the holder (2). 
Lap carrier according to one of Claims 1 to 3, characterized 
in that the surface (7) carrying the yarn has means (8), in at 

least one end region, for the axial guidance of the yarn. 
Lap carrier according to one of Claims 1 to 4, characterized 
in that the outer envelope of the body (9) of the lap 

carrier (1, 21) is conically shaped, at least in some regions. 
Lap carrier according to one of Claims 1 to 5, characterized 
in that the band (4) and/or the additional band (3) is formed on 

a respective insert (12, 11) having a respective insert band (13,14) 
and being inserted with it into a corresponding internal face of 

a lap carrier (15, 16).  
 
Lap carrier according to one of Claims 1 to 6, characterized 
in that the additional band (3) is formed by ribs (17, 18) of 

which the radially external faces (19, 20) form a band surface. 
Lap carrier according to Claim 7, characterized in that the 
ribs (17, 18) are radially orientated. 
Method of applying the lap carrier according to one of the 
preceding claims, characterized in that the lap carrier (1) is 

wound over its entire remaining peripheral face and over the yarn 
reserve groove (6). 
</CLAIMS>
