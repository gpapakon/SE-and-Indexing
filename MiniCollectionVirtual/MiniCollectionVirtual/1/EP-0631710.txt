<INVENTION-TITLE>
ADAPTOR DEVICE FOR INDUCTION HEATING GENERATOR.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H05B606	H05B606	H05B608	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H05B	H05B	H05B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H05B6	H05B6	H05B6	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The adaptor device for an induction heating generator is characterized by two oscillating circuits tuned on neighbour frequencies and by using a saturable inductance controlled by a direct current. The impedance variation of the saturable inductance provides for a precise and continuous adaptation of the induction heating generator to its load.
</ABSTRACT>
