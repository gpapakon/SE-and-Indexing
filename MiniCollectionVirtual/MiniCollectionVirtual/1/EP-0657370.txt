<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for waste disposal, especially for use in railway vehicles.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65F104	B65F106	B65F114	B65F114	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65F	B65F	B65F	B65F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65F1	B65F1	B65F1	B65F1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A device for receiving waste, especially for use in rail-bound vehicles, has a container (1) which is mounted, for example, on a wall of the vehicle in a removable fashion and the open top of which is covered by a flap (2). The said container (1) has, beneath the flap (2) a peripheral bead (1a) which is positively enclosed by the open end section of a refuse sack (3) which is arranged inside the container (1).
