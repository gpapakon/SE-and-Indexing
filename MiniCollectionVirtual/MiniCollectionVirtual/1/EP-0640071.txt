<CLAIMS>
The use of esters - liquid at room temperature - of C
6-22
 fatty acids with
monohydric alcohols as solvents for isocyanates and/or isocyanurates.
The use claimed in claim 1, characterized in that methyl esters of C
6-22

fatty acids are used.
The use claimed in claim 1 or 2, characterized in that the isocyanates
are di-, tri- and/or tetraisocyanates and/or prepolymers thereof containing 2

to 4 isocyanate groups.
The use claimed in claim 3, characterized in that the isocyanates are
aromatic diisocyanates.
The use claimed in any of claims 1 to 4, characterized in that the
esters are used in a ratio by weight to the isocyanates and/or isocyanurates

of 5:95 to 95:5.
</CLAIMS>
