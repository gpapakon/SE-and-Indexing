<INVENTION-TITLE>
Escalator or moving walkway with lighting device
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B66B2322	B66B2322	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B66B	B66B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B66B23	B66B23	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The escalator or moving walkway consists of a carrying body, an escalator belt or a pallet belt, a balustrade with a hand rail and an illumination unit for the illumination of the escalator belt or the pallet belt, arranged in a balustrade support. An illumination unit (9) is provided, which illuminates with priority, a danger region of the escalator belt (1.1) or the pallet belt and a support edge region. The illumination unit is arranged in a cavity of the support edge region. The cavity is formed by a first limb, a second limb of a metal sheet cover of the support (8), and a transparent cover.
</ABSTRACT>
