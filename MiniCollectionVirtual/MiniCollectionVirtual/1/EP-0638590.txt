<CLAIMS>
Rubbers prepared by grafting 99.5 to 90 wt.% of
syndiotactic 1,2-polybutadiene with 0.5 to 10 wt% of

maleic anhydride in the melt, wherein the data
referring to weight adds up to 100 %.
A process for preparing rubbers according to Claim 1,

characterized in that 99.5 to 90 wt% of syndiotactic
1,2-polybutadiene is reacted in the melt with 0.5 to

10 wt% of maleic anhydride at temperatures of 160 to
200 Â°C in the melt for 20 sec to 5 min in extruders or

compounders, wherein the data referring to weight adds
up to 100 %.
Use of the rubbers according to Claim 1 as a polymer
additive.
