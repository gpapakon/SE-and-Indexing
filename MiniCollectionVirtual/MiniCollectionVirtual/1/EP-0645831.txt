<ABSTRACT>
The sensor is constructed using material having a crystal structure similar to perovskite and an increased magnetoresistive effect. The material is based on the composition (A1)1-x(A2)xMnOz with A1 (trivalent) comprising Y, La or a lanthanide, with A2 (divalent) comprising an alkaline-earth metal or Pb and with 0.1

According to the invention, the sensor contains a layer system (2) having at least two layers (3, 4) with different materials, but in each case within the framework of the mentioned composition, which is selected such that the temperature-dependence of the electrical resistance is comparatively low. The two layers in the layer system may also be combined to give a single layer structure having a concentration gradient.
