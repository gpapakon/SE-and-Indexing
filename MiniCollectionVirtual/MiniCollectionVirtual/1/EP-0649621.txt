<INVENTION-TITLE>
Tree stand, in particular Christmas tree stand
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Tree stand (1), in particular Christmas tree stand, stand (1) having a base (4) for receiving the tree trunk (6), the base (4) possessing a pattern of holes (9) for receiving a plurality of rods (5) which project, around the trunk (6), into the gaps formed by the branches of the tree (7).

<CLAIMS>
Tree stand, in particular Christmas tree stand, designed as a
container, whereas the container has a bottom (4) for receiving the

tree-trunk, the bottom (4) being provided with a pattern of holes (9)
for the reception of several rods projecting around the trunk (6) into

the spaces between the branches of the tree (7),
characterized in that
 the length of the rods (5) may
be varied by division.
Tree stand according to claim 1,
characterized in that
 the pattern of holes (9) is such
that the rods (5) may be arranged in various circular diameters.
Tree stand according to claim 2,
characterized in that
 the stand (1) designed as a
container has got two chambers (2, 3), whereas the lower chamber

(3) may be filled with heavy material in order to improve its stability
and whereas the other, upper chamber (2) may be filled with water.
Tree stand according to claim 2,
characterized in that
 the rods (5) are telescopic.
Tree stand according to claim 1,
characterized in that
 the bottom (4) has cone shaped
holes (9a), whereas the rods (5) are correspondingly also provided

with a cone (5a). 
Tree stand according to claim 1,
characterized in that
 the rods (5) may be screwn (10)
into the holes (9a) in the bottom (4).
Tree stand according to one or several of the claims 1 - 6,
characterized in that
 the rods (5) may be received by
the holes (9a) in the bottom (4) so that the rods are standing

slantways.
Tree stand according to one or several of the claims 1 - 7,
characterized in that
 each rod (5) may be received
eccentrically and rotatably by one hole (9a) of the pattern of holes

(9).
Tree stand according to claim 8,
characterized in that
 each rod (5) is provided on its
end facing the stand (1) with an eccentric member (11), whereas the

eccentric member (11) has a foot (12) assigned for its reception in a
hole (9a) of the pattern of holes (9).
Tree stand according to claim 9,
characterized in that
 a web (13) is provided for a
reinforced connection between rod (5) and eccentric member (11).
Tree stand according to claim 9,
characterized in that
 the foot (12) may be fastened
in the hole (9a).
</CLAIMS>
