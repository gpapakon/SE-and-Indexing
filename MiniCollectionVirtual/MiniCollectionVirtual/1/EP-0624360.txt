<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an yttrium oxide stabilised zirconium oxide which is predominantly in tetragonal form and has the composition ZrO2 92.1% to 93.5%, Y2O3 4.5% - 5.5%, HfO2 1.8% to 2.2%, impurities not more than 0.2%, for the production of dense-sintered semifinished articles such as plates, discs or the like as starting material for prostheses.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
An yttrium-stabilized zirconium oxide in predominantly
tetragonal form is of the composition ZrO2 92.1% to

93.5%, Y203 4.5% to 5.5%, HfO2 1.8% to 2.2% impurities
at most 0.2% for the production of a densely sintered

semi-finished article like plates and rounds or the
like as starting material for a prothesis.
