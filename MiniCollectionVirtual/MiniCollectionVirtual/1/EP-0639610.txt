<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for painting of plastics, painted plastics and use of suitable adhesion agents.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
A process for painting plastics using a coating composition which contains, in addition to a one- or two-component binder, at least one chemically modified, chlorine-containing polyolefin as adhesion promoter a) and, as further auxiliary b), OH-functional, hydrophobic esters made from (i) polyhydric alcohols and (ii) aliphatic C8-C22-monocarboxylic acids, the plastics painted by this process, and the use of combinations of the abovementioned adhesion promoters a) with the abovementioned auxiliaries b) as additives which improve the adhesion promotion and shelf life in polymer paints.
</ABSTRACT>
