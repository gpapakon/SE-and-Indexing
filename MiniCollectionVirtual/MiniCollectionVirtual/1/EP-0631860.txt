<INVENTION-TITLE>
Fluid absorbent textile composite laminate.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B32B522	B32B526	D04B102	D04B104	D04B2100	D04B2100	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B32B	B32B	D04B	D04B	D04B	D04B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B32B5	B32B5	D04B1	D04B1	D04B21	D04B21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A fluid-absorbent textile composite laminate having a plurality of absorbent textile material webs has   1. at least one fluid-absorbing double-layered outer web, which distributes the absorbed fluid in all directions and carries it away from the upper face to the lower face,   2. at least one adjoining spacing web, which passes fluid on, and 3. at least one accumulating web, absorbing fluid to a greater extent than the outer web and spacing web.
</ABSTRACT>
