<ABSTRACT>
The invention concerns a hair fixer characterized in that it contains lignin obtained without sulphur and/or at least one sulphur-free lignin derivative plus the new sulphur-free lignin derivative dihydroxypropyl lignin. The agent proposed has excellent fixing properties, gives the hair good feel and shine and is also easy to rinse or brush out.
</ABSTRACT>
