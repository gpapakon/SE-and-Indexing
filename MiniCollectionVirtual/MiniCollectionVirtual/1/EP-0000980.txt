<INVENTION-TITLE>
Carbon current collecting contactor and pantograph assembly.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60L518	B60L520	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60L	B60L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60L5	B60L5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A carbon current collecting contactor (1) which is 
resistant to breakage due to sudden impacts in service. It 

is particularly useful when applied to pantographs (10) for 
electric locomotives or trains, and comprises a carbon 

current contactor (1) to which is affixed a resilient pad (2). 
</ABSTRACT>
