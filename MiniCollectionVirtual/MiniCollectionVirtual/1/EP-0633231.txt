according to claim 1 or 4.

there is attached to one or more faces of the material a

heat reflecting component consisting of a very fine layer
of mica or of metallic glass flakes placed on the face or

faces of the material and held by a glass mat impregnated
with an unfilled resin such as is described in claim 1 and

hardened to achieve the bond.
