<INVENTION-TITLE>
Mobile snow plant.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F25C300	F25C304	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a tank which can be transported by an optionally tracked vehicle, and containing an autonomous system for manufacturing artificial snow. This tank receives water in a warm container (A) either via a pump drawing it from a river (G) or from a distribution network (K). The water is sucked (B) into a cooling tower (C) and then falls into a cold container (D). It is then forced by pumping (E/F) to the snow cannon or cannons of the low-pressure type.
</ABSTRACT>
