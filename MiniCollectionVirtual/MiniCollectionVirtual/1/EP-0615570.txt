<INVENTION-TITLE>
ANCHORAGE FOR REINFORCED CONCRETE.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E04G2114	E04G2114	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E04G	E04G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>E04G21	E04G21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Anchorage and anchor for concrete elements such as thin panels and pipes. The anchorage comprises an anchor having a split lower portion (3) which can be fixed to a reinforcing bar (B), and means for attaching the anchor to at least one reinforcing bar. Anchor points can thus be suitably positioned in a thin concrete element.
</ABSTRACT>
