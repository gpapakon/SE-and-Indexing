<INVENTION-TITLE>
Crane, particularly mobile jib type crane.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B66C1700	B66C1706	B66C2300	B66C2318	B66C2336	B66C2378	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B66C	B66C	B66C	B66C	B66C	B66C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
A crane, in particular a mobile jib-type crane, is provided with a crane underframe (1) and a crane upper frame (2) which is rotatably arranged on the crane underframe (1) and has a first main jib (3) of lightweight construction and furthermore additional devices (7) increasing the permissible load. In order to obtain a further increase in the permissible load beyond the increase provided by the devices (7) and in order to thus dispense with the use of a second complete crane, it is proposed that a second main jib (8) be linked to the first main jib (3) via an essentially horizontally arranged supporting framework (30). The second main jib (8) is supported on a platform (10).
