<ABSTRACT>
An electroconductive transparent pressure-sensitive adhesive foil having an electroconductivity above 10
<
-4
>
 useful for producing biomedical electrodes consists of 100 parts by weight of a carboxyl group-containing acrylate-based copolymer; 50 to 150 parts by weight of a water-soluble amine; 50 to 250 parts by weight of a polyoxyalkylene having a molecular weight below 1000, and/or a polyol and/or its derivates; 50 to 200 parts by weight of an electrolyte solution constituted by a solution of an alkali metal sulfite; and 0,1 to 6 parts by weight of a cross-linking agent.
