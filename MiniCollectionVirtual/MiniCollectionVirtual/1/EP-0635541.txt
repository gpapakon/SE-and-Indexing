<INVENTION-TITLE>
Bonding agents for ester-curable resin binders for the foundry industry.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B22C116	B22C122	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B22C	B22C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B22C1	B22C1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Organofunctional silanes are proposed as adhesion promoters (coupling agents, primers) for ester-curing resin binders for the foundry industry. Epoxy-functional silanes or mixtures of epoxy-functional silanes and alkyl silanes are employed.
</ABSTRACT>
