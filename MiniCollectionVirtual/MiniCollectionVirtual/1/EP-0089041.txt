USE OF A NEGATIVE ACTING PHOTOPOLYMERIZABLE ELEMENT AS A SOLDER MASK

<ABSTRACT>
Improved release proposities are obtained between a 
cover sheet and a supported negative acting radiation 

sensitive photopolymerizable composition by introduction 
into the composition a compound of the formula A-B, 

wherein
 

A is substituted or unsubstituted alkyl or alkenyl of 6 to 
24 carbon atoms, and

 
B is a 5 or 6 atom aromatic or nonaromatic heterocyclic 

radical containing 2 or 3 heteroatoms taken from N, O, 
S or combination thereof, said radical optionally 

containing a six carbon fused aromatic ring,
 

an alkali metal salt of α β amino carboxylic acid or a bis 
alkali metal salt of a β, β′ amino dicarboxylic acid, 

wherein said release compound or mixture is present 
in said negative acting photopolymerizable composi­

tion in an effective amount sufficient to lower peel 
force to a value not greater tnan 75 grams. 
</ABSTRACT>
