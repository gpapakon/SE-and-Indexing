<INVENTION-TITLE>
Pillar-shaped indicator lamp.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F21S800	F21S800	G09F1304	G09F1304	G09F1500	G09F1500	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F21S	F21S	G09F	G09F	G09F	G09F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F21S8	F21S8	G09F13	G09F13	G09F15	G09F15	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A pillar-shaped indicator lamp (1) is described with lamp sections arranged one on top of the other, which consist of transparent housing sections (4, 5) with inserted parts (7, 8), and in which the inserted parts have electrical lines (10, 10') which are in connection with the lines of the inserted part respectively arranged therebelow or of the base (3), and in which there are provided furthermore in the inserted parts the receiving devices for the indicator lamps which are in connection with the conductor tracks. In this process, the inserted parts (7, 8) are interconnected and form a unit. The housing parts (4, 5) are likewise interconnected and form a unit. The insert formed by the inserted parts can be removed from the housing upwards.
