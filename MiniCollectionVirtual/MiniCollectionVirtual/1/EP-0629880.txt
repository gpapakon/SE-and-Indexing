<INVENTION-TITLE>
Rangefinder.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01S1700	G01S1736	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01S	G01S	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01S17	G01S17	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Range finder, comprising a light source such as a laser diode associated with collimation optics (12) for sending out a light beam (14) towards a target (16), means (18) for modulating the intensity of the beam (14) sent out at two different frequencies, the ratio of which is a power of 2, means (20, 22, 24) for receiving the luminous flux backscattered by the target (16), and means (18) for simultaneous measurement of the phase shifts between the beam sent out and the flux received at the two abovementioned frequencies, and for calculating the distance of the target from these two phase shifts.
