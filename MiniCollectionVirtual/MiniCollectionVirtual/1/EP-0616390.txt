<INVENTION-TITLE>
Electrical coupling housing.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H01R13502	H01R13502	H01R13508	H01R13516	H01R13516	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
A plug connector housing (11) is composed of two housing parts (31, 33) which can be fixed to one another by means of at least one spring clip (13). Each spring clip (13) is provided with a threaded bolt holder (29) in which a threaded bolt (15) is held such that it can move but is captive.
