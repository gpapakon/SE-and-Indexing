<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method of banding the faces of two ceramic bodies.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C04B3700	C04B3700	H01M1036	H01M1039	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C04B	C04B	H01M	H01M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C04B37	C04B37	H01M10	H01M10	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A process is provided for the vacuum-tight sealing of a ss-Al2O3 ceramic part to an  alpha -Al2O3 ceramic part, in which the bonding glass in powder form is applied by means of an adhesive liquid to the ss-Al2O3 ceramic part and the glass sealing is then carried out at elevated temperatures.
