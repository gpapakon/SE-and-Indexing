<INVENTION-TITLE>
GRAVITY-COMPENSATING ACCELEROMETER AND METHOD FOR MAKING SAME
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B81B300	B81B300	B81C100	B81C100	G01P1502	G01P1502	G01P1508	G01P1508	G01P2100	G01P2100	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B81B	B81B	B81C	B81C	G01P	G01P	G01P	G01P	G01P	G01P	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B81B3	B81B3	B81C1	B81C1	G01P15	G01P15	G01P15	G01P15	G01P21	G01P21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
An accelerometer comprising a seismic mass (1) capable of being acted upon by a force induced by an acceleration to be measured. The seismic mass (1) is connected to a support (2) by a mechanical linkage (3) that can be bent by said force, and sensing means are provided for determining the acceleration from the force induced in the seismic mass. Compensation means are provided for compensating for the force exerted on the seismic mass by gravity. The mechanical linkage comprises, as the compensation means, at least one portion (4, 5) that mechanically biases the linkage against the force exerted on the seismic mass by gravity. A method for making said accelerometer is also disclosed.
</ABSTRACT>
