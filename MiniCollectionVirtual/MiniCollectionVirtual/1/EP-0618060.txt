<INVENTION-TITLE>
Plasticising machine for unidirectionally fibre-reinforced plastic granulate.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B29B1100	B29B1110	B29B1300	B29B1302	B29C4738	B29C4740	B29C4744	B29K10506	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
In the case of a plasticising machine for thermoplastic granules reinforced with long fibres, a plasticising screw is arranged in the conical feed section and cylindrical discharge section of the plasticising tube. The discharge section (5) is provided with a moulding, closeable nozzle (8) and the plasticising screw (3) has a device (6) for axially displacing the screw (3) with respect to the plasticising tube.
