<INVENTION-TITLE>
Procedure and device for endurance testing of electrical systems of a vehicle
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01M1900	G01M1900	G01R3100	G01R3100	H01H1100	H01H1100	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01M	G01M	G01R	G01R	H01H	H01H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01M19	G01M19	G01R31	G01R31	H01H11	H01H11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The method involves actuating the control elements of the system by at least one automatic actuation device for test purposes. The electrical systems are tested in the installed state in the vehicle (3) and the vehicle is subjected to a simulation of real ambient conditions during the testing, including an ambient temperature that is preferably varied between -40 and + 85 deg. C. An Independent claim is also included for an arrangement for load testing of motor vehicle electrical systems.
</ABSTRACT>
