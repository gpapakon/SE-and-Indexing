<INVENTION-TITLE>
Slide-in dispenser for sanitary articles and similar.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47K1000	A47K1018	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47K	A47K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47K10	A47K10	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Disclosed is a dispenser for sanitary articles, such as bags capable of being folded up, paper handkerchiefs or the like, having a box-like housing and a container, made of flexible materials such as cardboard, which is arranged exchangeably in the housing and contains the sanitary articles, the housing of which contains at least one projection reaching into its interior and the container of which is provided in the region of the projections with predetermined breaking points for piercing by the projections.
