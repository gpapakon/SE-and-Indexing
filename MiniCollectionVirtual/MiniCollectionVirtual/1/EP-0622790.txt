<INVENTION-TITLE>
Selectively modifiable optical storage medium and production method.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G11B724	G11B724	G11B726	G11B726	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G11B	G11B	G11B	G11B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G11B7	G11B7	G11B7	G11B7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The optical data memory includes two discs (38,39), of which at least one is transparent, and a storage medium consisting of solid particles with a liquid crystal phase located between the two discs. A pitted layer (37) carrying pits (34) is arranged between the discs with their size matched in plan view to the source of electromagnetic radiation or to the achievable focussing (resolution) of the electromagnetic radiation. The data pits are distributed uniformly over the pitted layer, in concentric, spiral-shaped or parallel paths (tracks). The data pits can be distributed non-uniformly over the pitted layer.
</ABSTRACT>
