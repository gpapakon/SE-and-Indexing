<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for sealing plastic bags.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device for sealing plastic bags, with a welding bar which has an electrically heated welding wire (3). It is essential that the welding wire be surrounded by a plastic hose (8), which replaces both the previous base of the welding wire and its covering relative to the plastic bag.
