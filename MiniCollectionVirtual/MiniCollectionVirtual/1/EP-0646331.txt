<INVENTION-TITLE>
Connecting device for a waterproof collar.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A41D300	A41D304	A41D2700	A41D2700	A41D2702	A41D2704	A41D2718	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A41D	A41D	A41D	A41D	A41D	A41D	A41D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A41D3	A41D3	A41D27	A41D27	A41D27	A41D27	A41D27	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A waterproof collar connecting device for retrospective fitting of an item of clothing with a waterproof, water vapour-permeable functional layer of clothing (49). This is releasably attached by means of the collar connecting device (13) between an inner layer (15) of the collar and an outer layer (17) of the collar of the item of clothing by means of three releasable fasteners (23, 39, 45) between the layers (15, 17) of the collar on the one hand and the functional layer of clothing (49) and the lining (41) on the other hand.
