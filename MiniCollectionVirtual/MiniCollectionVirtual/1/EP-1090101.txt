<INVENTION-TITLE>
METHOD FOR CLEANING THE PAINT LINES OF A PAINTING INSTALLATION
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C09D900	C09D900	C11D337	C11D337	C11D1100	C11D1100	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C09D	C09D	C11D	C11D	C11D	C11D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C09D9	C09D9	C11D3	C11D3	C11D11	C11D11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A method for cleaning paint feeding parts of a painting installation, especially the paint lines, is described, wherein the paint feeding parts are rinsed with a rinsing liquid containing an organic solvent. In order to obtain particularly advantageous ratios, polyaspartic acid is admixed with the rinsing liquid.
</ABSTRACT>
