<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process and device for fluid pressure generation and/or control using Peltier effect modules.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F25B2102	F25B2104	G05D1620	G05D1620	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F25B	F25B	G05D	G05D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F25B21	F25B21	G05D16	G05D16	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The generator and/or controller of the pressure of a fluid in a sealed enclosure (1), comprises at least one Peltier effect module (2) in a relationship of heat exchange between the enclosure (1) and the ambient medium (7) via an exchanger (5). Depending on the polarity of the DC current supplying the module (2), the latter absorbs or dissipates heat in the enclosure (1), so that it respectively decreases or increases the temperature, and hence the pressure of the pressurized fluid contained in the enclosure (1). Applications to the control, around a set pressure, of the pressure of a pressurized fluid contained in a sealed enclosure.
