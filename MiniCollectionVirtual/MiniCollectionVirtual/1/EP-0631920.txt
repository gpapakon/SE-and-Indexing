<INVENTION-TITLE>
Control device for at least two electrical consumers arranged in a railway vehicle.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B61L1500	B61L1500	G08C1500	G08C1500	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B61L	B61L	G08C	G08C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B61L15	B61L15	G08C15	G08C15	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Control device for at least two electrical loads (61, 62, 63, ..., 610) which are arranged in a rail vehicle and are connected to a power supply line (7) via one actuator (51, 52, 53, ..., 510) each, each actuator (51, 52, 53, ..., 510) being capable of being driven by a central control unit (1) and a multiplex transmission system (3) being assigned to the central control unit (1) in order to drive the at least two loads (61, 62, 63, ..., 610), which multiplex transmission system (3) is connected via a multiplex connection (4) to in each case one multiplex receiver for each actuator (51, 52, 53, ..., 510) which is to be driven and is spatially adjacent to the associated load. The multiplex connection can be for example an electric line or an optical line or a spark gap.
