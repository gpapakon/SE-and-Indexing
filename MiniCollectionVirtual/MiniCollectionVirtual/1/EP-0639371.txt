<ABSTRACT>
Cosmetic composition or cosmetic composition carrier comprising an aqueous microdispersion of particles containing at least one wax, the said particles being solid up to a temperature at least equal to 30 DEG C, the said particles containing, in addition, an organic lipophilic hydrofluorocarbon compound such that the ratio of the number of fluorine atoms to the total number of fluorine and hydrogen atoms linked to the carbon atoms, in the molecule of the said compound, is at least equal to 10% and is not greater than 90%. Application of the said composition especially for making up the eyelashes or for hair care.
</ABSTRACT>
