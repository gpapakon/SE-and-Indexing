<INVENTION-TITLE>
Docking station for small computers.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G06F116	G06F116	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G06F	G06F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G06F1	G06F1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a docking station for the insertion of a small computer for the purpose of connecting additional hardware units. For the communication with a small computer, it is provided that the docking station is constructed with a first external interface for releasable connection of the small computer, at least one second external interface for releasable connection of a personal computer or of a peripheral device, and an internal interface, which can be changed over and closes in a controllable manner, and so as to be disconnectable, data lines for the data transmission between the first external interface for the small computer and a built-in functional element or the at least one second external interface.
