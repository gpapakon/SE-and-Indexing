<ABSTRACT>
An aqueous amino acid solution with a ratio  Nessential to Nnonessential of 1:1 to 1:3 is described for parenteral nutrition, which contains as the amino acid component only free amino acids in a concentration of 15 to 23 % by weight, and this is composed per 100 g of L-isoleucine                        2.6 - 3.2 g L-leucine 6.0 - 7.0 g L-lysine                            6.5 - 8.0 g L-methionine                        2.6 - 3.2 g L-phenylalanine 4.0 - 4.5 g L-threonine                         3.0 - 5.0 g L-tryptophan                        0.8 - 1.2 g L-valine 5.0 - 6.0 g L-arginine                         10.0 - 12.5 g L-histidine                         1.8 - 3.0 g L-alanine 10.0 - 16.0 g L-aspartic acid                     5.0 - 6.5 g L-glutamic acid                     8.5 - 12.0 g glycine 11.5 - 14.0 g L-proline                           5.5 - 8.0 g L-serine                            3.5 - 5.0 g L-tyrosine 0.2 - 0.4 g L-ornithine                         0.1 - 3.0 g
