<INVENTION-TITLE>
Barrier film free from metal and chlorine for gases and flavouring agents.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
A multilayer composite film comprising a gas barrier layer A, a heat-sealable layer C and optionally an adhesive or bonding layer B, where the individual layers in the composite film are arranged as follows:     A a metal- and chlorine-free gas barrier layer,   B an adhesive or bonding layer, C a heat-sealable layer of amorphous polyester or copolyester or of an amorphous, impact-modified polyester or copolyester.
</ABSTRACT>
