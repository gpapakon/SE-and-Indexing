<INVENTION-TITLE>
Oven to bake bread.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A21B100	A21B140	A21B300	A21B304	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A21B	A21B	A21B	A21B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A21B1	A21B1	A21B3	A21B3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an oven for baking bread having at least one baking chamber (98) which is characterised in that at least one temperature sensor (100, 102, 104, 108) and one humidity sensor (106) are situated in the baking chamber (98) to control the temperature in the baking chamber (98) and the humidity concentration in the baking chamber (98).
