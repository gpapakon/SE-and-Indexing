<INVENTION-TITLE>
Container for boroniding metallic articles
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C23C800	C23C868	C23C870	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C23C	C23C	C23C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C23C8	C23C8	C23C8	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Metal parts are borided with a boron powder or boron powder granulate using a container whose parts which come into contact with the boriding agent granulate are made of copper. This prolongs the active life of the boriding agent.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
Container for boronising metal parts with boronising 
powders and boronising granular powders, characterised 

in that at least the parts of the container (3) and of 
the crucible lid (4) coming into contact with the 

boronising powder or the boronising granular powder 
(6) consist of copper or of a layer of low-alloy 

copper alloy. 
Container for boronising according to claim 1, 
characterised in that the container (3) and the lid 

(4) consist of iron or iron alloys and at least the 
inner surfaces, which come into contact with the 

boronising granular powder (6), bear a layer of 
copper or a layer of low-alloy copper alloy. 
Container for boronising according to claim 1, 
characterised in that the container (3) and the lid 

(4) consist of copper or of a layer of low-alloy 
copper alloy. 
Container for boronising according to claim 1, 
characterised in that the container (3) consists of 

copper or of a layer of low-alloy copper alloy and is 
fitted into an outer container (1) made of iron or 

iron alloy. 
</CLAIMS>
