<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for making a mattress of natural fibres or a futon support avoiding the formation of depressions.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47C2700	A47C2700	A47C2712	A47C2712	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47C	A47C	A47C	A47C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47C27	A47C27	A47C27	A47C27	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a mattress of natural fibres which consists in each case of a single large ring tube, the size of which is adapted to the finished mattress dimension/futon support. The finished mattress or futon support is produced by simply folding this tube ring.
