<INVENTION-TITLE>
SYSTEM FOR ANALYSING THE CONTENTS OF LIQUID SAMPLES.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01N3122	G01N3122	G01N3500	G01N3500	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01N	G01N	G01N	G01N	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a system for the analysis of sample liquids by means of dry reagents, which is particularly suitable for the determination of clinical parameters. An analysis device, in which test elements for carrying out an analytical test are located, is part of the system. The form and condition of the test elements is matched to the use in the analysis system according to the invention. The system is particularly suitable for carrying out analyses for which the test elements available have a short storage life in contact with the environment.
