<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method of joining wooden elements, abutment plates, upper and lower booms, cross-beams, struts or the like
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B27F700	B27F715	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B27F	B27F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B27F7	B27F7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The connecting process involves using a pressing tool (4) to press a connecting element such as a nail plate into the wooden elements (10) at a connecting point (9). Numerous pressing tools are used, directing them onto at least one connecting point. The wooden elements, to which the connecting elements have been fitted, are connected to each other by simultaneous pressing.
</ABSTRACT>
