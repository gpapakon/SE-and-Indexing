<INVENTION-TITLE>
CONTINUOUSLY BURNING CANDLE.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F21S1300	F21S1312	F21V100	F21V110	F21V3500	F21V3500	F21V3700	F21V3702	F23D300	F23D302	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F21S13	F21S13	F21V1	F21V1	F21V35	F21V35	F21V37	F21V37	F23D3	F23D3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In a continuously burning candle, paraffin-like fuel (8) is laterally enclosed by an envelope (M), at the center of which is arranged a non-combustible wick (5) which slightly projects above the top edge (R) of the envelope in a constant manner while the fuel (8) is consumed. A venting lid (D) with a central opening which is slightly larger than the diameter of the flame is arranged on the edge (R) of the envelope. A cylinder (Z), in particular a glass cylinder, is arranged thereon, concentrically to the wick (5). Air inlets (70) are distributed all around the venting lid (D) next to the lower end of the cylinder (Z) and/or between the venting lid (D) and the cylinder (Z).
</ABSTRACT>
