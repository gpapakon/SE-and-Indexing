<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for laser welding of aluminium under a shielding gas
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B23K3522	B23K2612	B23K3538	B23K2612	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B23K	B23K	B23K	B23K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B23K35	B23K26	B23K35	B23K26	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Argon, helium and their mixtures are used as shielding gas in the laser welding of aluminium. To improve the welding behaviour, 80 vpm to 250 vpm, preferably 120 vpm to 180 vpm, of nitrogen, nitrous oxide or nitrogen/nitrous-oxide mixture is added to the shielding gas.
</ABSTRACT>
