<INVENTION-TITLE>
Power supply installation.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G05F110	G05F159	H02J110	H02J110	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G05F	G05F	H02J	H02J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G05F1	G05F1	H02J1	H02J1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A power supply arrangement has at least two interdependently controlled power supplies, each of which has a control unit. Furthermore, a bus is provided, via which the control units are interconnected for the purpose of exchanging digital signals.
