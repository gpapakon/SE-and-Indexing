<INVENTION-TITLE>
Steering column unit, especially of a motor vehicle.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B62D116	B62D116	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B62D	B62D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B62D1	B62D1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
This steering column unit, particularly for a motor vehicle, of the type including, at least at one of its ends, means for screw-fastening the rest of the structure of the vehicle, is characterised in that it includes means (5) for centring and for attaching to the rest of the structure of the vehicle, to make it easier to fit the screw means and the assembly to the rest of the structure of the vehicle.
