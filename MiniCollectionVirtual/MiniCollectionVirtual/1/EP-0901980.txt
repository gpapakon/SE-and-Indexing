<ABSTRACT>
Drive pulley (4) is driven by a permanent magnet-excited synchronous motor (2) via a planetary gear unit (3). Preferably, a rotor (7) fitted with permanent magnets is fixed to a drive shaft (13) of the gear unit, the two being on common bearing in the gear unit housing.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
Claims for the following Contracting States : AT, DE, FR, GB, IT
A drive for elevators, having a driving pulley (4)
driven by an electric synchronous motor (2), equipped

with permanent magnets, the drive being by way of a
planetary gear (3)
characterized in that

a unit formed by a synchronous motor (2) and planetary
gear (3) is detachably mounted in a frame (1) that

radially surrounds this unit and that in the frame (1)
the stationary parts of brakes (5) whose pivotable

jaws (21) cooperate with a brake disk (20) are
mounted.
The drive according to Claim 1,
characterized in that

the end face of the rotor (7) facing away from the
planetary gear (3) is connected to a pot-shaped ring

brake disk (20) whose ring wall radially surrounds the
stator housing (9).
The drive according to Claim 1 or 2,
characterized in that

the electric coils (10) of the stator (11) are
arranged in a stator housing (9) made of aluminum with

optimum heat dissipation properties.
The drive according to one of the preceding claims,
characterized in that

the motor-gear unit is flange-connected axially to the
frame (1) on the gear end and a conical clamping

// tension ring (23) which is under axial tension and has
transverse slots on the circumference clamps

// secures the stator housing (9) in a radially form-fitting
manner within the frame (1) to permit good

dissipation of heat.
The drive according to one of the preceding claims,
characterized in that

the frame (1) is equipped with several mounting
flanges (24) for different fastening options for the

frame (1) on a receptacle body of the elevator system,
and the driving pulley (4) is designed in a pot shape

and radially surrounds the planetary gear (3) with its
ring wall which carries the elevator cable.
The drive according to one of the preceding claims,
characterized in that

a resolver (16) of the synchronous motor (2) is
situated at the free center of the ring-shaped brake

disk (20), whereby its rotating part (15) is fixedly
connected to the rotor (7) and its stationary part

(17) is fixedly connected to the frame (1), whereby
the fixed connection in the frame (1) may be

accomplished in any rotational position of the fixable
rotating disk (18) that can be secured.
Claims for the following Contracting States : BE, FI, LU, NL
A drive for elevators having a driving pulley driven
by an electric motor via a planetary gear (3),
characterized by
 the features

the electric motor drive is designed as a synchronous
motor (2) energized by permanent magnets,
a unit formed by a synchronous motor (2) and planetary
gear (3) is detachably mounted in a frame (1) which

surrounds this unit radially, whereby stationary parts
of a shoe brake (5) are mounted in this frame (1),

their pivotable shoes (21) cooperating with a brake
disk (20).
The drive according to.Claim 1,
characterized in that

a rotor (7) of the synchronous motor (2), said rotor
being equipped with permanent magnets, is arranged

coaxially with a drive shaft (13) of the planetary
gear (3) and is fixedly connected to it.
The drive according to Claim 1 or 2,
characterized in that

the rotor (7) and the gear drive shaft (13) are
mounted jointly.
The drive according to Claim 3,
characterized in that

the joint bearing is provided only in the housing of
the planetary gear (3).
The drive according to one of the preceding claims,
characterized in that

the rotor (7) is equipped with permanent magnets on
its outside circumference and rotates within a ring

equipped with electric coils (10) as the stator (11).
The drive according to one of the preceding claims,
characterized in that

the stator housing (9) is fixedly connected to the
housing of the planetary gear (3).
The drive according to one of the preceding claims,
characterized in that

the electric coils (10) of the stator (11) are
arranged in a stator housing (9) that is made of

aluminum and provides optimum heat dissipation.
The drive according to one of the preceding claims,
characterized in that

the end face of the rotor (7) facing away from the
planetary gear (3) is connected to a pot-shaped ring

brake disk (20) whose ring wall surrounds the stator
housing (9) radially. 
The drive according to one of the preceding claims,
characterized in that

a resolver (16) of the synchronous motor (2) is
situated at the free center of the ring-shaped brake

disk (20), whereby its rotating part (15) is fixedly
connected to the rotor (7) and its stationary part

(17) is fixedly connected to the frame (1), whereby
the fixed connection in the frame (1) may be

accomplished in any rotational position of the
rotating disk (18) which can be secured.
The drive according to one of the preceding claims,
characterized in that

the motor-gear unit is flange-connected axially to the
frame (1) on the gear side and a conical axially

braced clamping ring (23) having transverse slots on
the circumference radially encloses in a form-fitting

manner the stator housing (9) within the frame (1) to
permit good dissipation of heat.
The drive according to one of the preceding claims,
characterized in that

the frame (1) is provided with a plurality of
fastening flanges (24) for different fastening

possibilities for the frame (1) on a receptacle body
of the elevator system, and the driving pulley (4) is

designed in a pot shape and can extend radially around
the planetary gear (3) with its ring wall which

carries the elevator cable. 
The drive according to one of the preceding claims,
characterized in that

the shoe brake (5) is provided with an inspection
device for determining the brake lining wear and/or

can be unlocked by remote control.
