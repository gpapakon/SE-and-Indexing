Labelling device.

<ABSTRACT>
The aforesaid invention relates to a labelling device for packaging means, especially order wallets for photographic work, with a printer (12) for applying coded or uncoded information to the labels (2) for the purpose of identifying the pack and the article to be packaged, and with a device for detaching the labels from the stock roll and for applying them to the order wallet, there being provided an approximately horizontal positioned bearing plate (1) which is located downstream of the printer (12) and on which the printed label (2) detached from the stock roll comes to rest with its adhesive face directed towards the bearing plate (1), there being arranged above the bearing plate (1) gripping means (5) which are suitable for receiving the labels by separating these from the bearing plate and which are made pivotable and extendable, in order to transfer the label out of the horizontal position into a vertical position, in which it can be applied to the order wallets erected approximately vertically in front of the bearing plate and the gripping means.
