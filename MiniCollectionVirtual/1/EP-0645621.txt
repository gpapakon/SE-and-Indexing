<ABSTRACT>
A sensor arrangement comprises a substrate (21) of doped silicon which has channels (23) in a main surface (22), a selective device for detecting a material which covers the main surface (22) without filling up the channels (23), and a measuring device for picking up a physical quantity dependent on the action of a material. In particular, a catalytic layer (24) is used as the selective device and a temperature sensor (25) is used as the measuring device. Alternatively, the sensor arrangement is designed as a capacitor with a porous opposite electrode. The channels are preferably produced by electrochemical etching.
