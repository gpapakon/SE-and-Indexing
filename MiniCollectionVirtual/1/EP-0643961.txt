<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a cosmetic composition containing, in a cosmetically acceptable aqueous medium, at least one nonionic surfactant of the alkylpolyglycoside family and/or one polyglycerolated nonionic surfactant and at least one polydimethylsiloxane/polyoxyalkylene block copolymer, as well as to a cosmetic treatment process which consists in applying a cosmetically effective amount of this composition to keratinous substances.
</ABSTRACT>

The composition of any one of Claims 1 to 11, characterized 
in that it contains 0.5 to 5% by weight of the total weight of the 

composition of non-ionic surfactants of the alkylpolyglycoside and/or 
polyglycerol family. 
The composition of any one of Claims 1 to 11, characterized 
in that it contains 1 to 30% by weight, and preferably 5 to 20% by 

weight of the total weight of the composition, of non-ionic surfactants 
of the alkylpolyglycoside and/or polyglycerol family. 
The composition of any one of Claims 1 to 13, characterized 
in that the cosmetically acceptable aqueous medium consists exclusively 

of water or of a mixture of water and a cosmetically acceptable 
solvent.  

 
The composition of any one of Claims 1 to 14, characterized 
in that it is present in the form of a liquid of varying consistency, 

gel, emulsion, aqueous or hydro-alcoholic lotion, dispersion, or 
aerosol foam. 
The composition of any one of Claims 1 to 15, characterized 
in that it contains anionic, cationic, amphoteric or zwitterionic 

surfactants, non-ionic surfactants other than those described 
previously, acidizing or alkalinizing agents, preservatives, thickeners, 

suspending agents, softeners, sun filters, perfumes, biocides, anti- oxidants, 
pigments, dyes, pearl lustering agents, biocides, repellants, oxidants, 

reducing agents, moisturizers, vitamins, preservatives, foam 
reinforcers, electrolytes, ceramides, UV filters, anti-bacterial agents, 

anti- dandruff agents, anti-seborrhoeic agents, pesticides, or other additives 
routinely used in cosmetics. 
Use of the composition as defined in any one of Claims 1 

to 16, for the treatment and/or washing of keratinic materials 
consisting of the hair, the skin, the eyelashes or the eyebrows. 
A process of cosmetic treatment, characterized in that it 
consists in applying to the skin or the hair a cosmetically effective 

quantity of a composition according to any one of Claims 1 to 17. 
A process of washing and cosmetic treatment of the hair, 
characterized in that it consists in applying to the wet or dry hair an 

effective quantity to wash it, of the composition as described in any 
one of Claims 1 to 11 and 13 to 16, and further characterized in that 

this is followed by rinsing with water.
