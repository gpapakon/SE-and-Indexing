<INVENTION-TITLE>
Harness collar.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B68B300	B68B304	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B68B	B68B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B68B3	B68B3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a horse-collar (1) for harnessing a horse up to a vehicle, the collar comprising an annular cushion (1) to be laid around the horse's neck and harnessing devices arranged thereon, in particular a plurality of draw clips (5) for receiving breast straps and a breast ring (6) for receiving the breech etc. The horse-collar is arranged in such a manner that the harnessing devices (5, 6) engage on a special supporting and stabilising frame (7; 10) which is embedded in the interior of the collar cushion (2) in order to conduct the tensile and compression or restraint forces acting on the collar (1) into the interior of the collar (1).
