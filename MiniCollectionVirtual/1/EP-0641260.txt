<INVENTION-TITLE>
PROCESS AND DEVICE FOR PRODUCING AN ELECTRICALLY CONDUCTIVE WALLING MATERIAL FROM A FABRIC AND FOIL MATERIAL.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a process for producing an electrically conductive walling material (3) having an insulating coating from a fabric or foil, especially for producing a fabric web having an insulating coating and electrically conductive strips. In order in particular to create a process by which an electrically conductive walling material with an electrically insulating coating can be produced in which the risk of brush discharges is effectively reduced, the coating (2) is perforated after the walling material (3) has been coated.
</ABSTRACT>
