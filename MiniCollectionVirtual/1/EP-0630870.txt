Cathodic element not containing asbestos fibres.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Electrically conductive microporous material including an asbestos-free fibrous sheet, characterised in that the said sheet includes carbon or graphite fibres, polytetrafluoroethylene fibres, inert inorganic fibres, at least one fluoropolymer binding the fibres and, if appropriate, at least one thickener. Cathodic element including the said material and combination of this cathodic element with a diaphragm or a membrane. Use of this combination in a cell for electrolysis of aqueous solutions of alkali metal halides.
</ABSTRACT>
