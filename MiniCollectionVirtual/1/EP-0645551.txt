<INVENTION-TITLE>
Floating caliper disc brake.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
This is a brake of the sort including a disc (11) mounted so that it can rotate, two brake pads (12A, 12B) which are located on each side of this disc (11), a calliper (14) which, engaged over the assembly, is mounted so that it can slide on at least one support pin (15) and which, for action on one of the brake pads (12A, 12B) includes an operating unit (16) in line with this pad. According to the invention, centring means (40) act axially between the calliper (14) and the support fin (15). Application especially to power-off brakes, particularly for industry.
