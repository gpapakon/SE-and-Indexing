<CLAIMS>
Antenna (3) for radio apparatus, a first portion (10) of which is resiliently
deformable and a second portion (7, 8, 9) of which is formed by a rigid conductor

which is adjustable in length and electrically connected to the first portion (10) of the
antenna.
Antenna according to claim 1, wherein the first resiliently deformable portion
(10) extends as far as the free end of the antenna.
Antenna according to one of claims 1 and 2, wherein said first resiliently
deformable portion (10) comprises a resiliently deformable conductor (10).
Antenna according to claim 3, wherein said resiliently deformable conductor
(10) is coated with a supple protective material (11).
Antenna according to one of claims 1 to 4, wherein the second antenna
portion (7, 8, 9) of adjustable length comprises tubular telescopic elements.
</CLAIMS>
