<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the preparation of dialkyle carbonates.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B01J3112	B01J3112	C07B6100	C07B6100	C07C6800	C07C6800	C07C6900	C07C6996	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B01J	B01J	C07B	C07B	C07C	C07C	C07C	C07C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B01J31	B01J31	C07B61	C07B61	C07C68	C07C68	C07C69	C07C69	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
C1-C4-dialkyl carbonates can be obtained from the C1-C4-alkanols on which they are based, carbon monoxide and oxygen by reacting these substances in the presence of copper salts, the water of reaction in the reaction mixture being limited to less than 10 % by weight by removing at least some of it.
</ABSTRACT>
