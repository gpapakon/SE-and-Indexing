<INVENTION-TITLE>
Low temperature impact-resistant composition of semicrystalline propylene homopolymers
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Propylene polymers containing   A) a partially crystalline propylene homopolymer having a melting point in the range from 125 to 165 DEG C and a glass transition temperature above -10 DEG C, and B) a partially crystalline propylene homopolymer having a melting point of below 130 DEG C and a glass transition temperature of less than or equal to -10 DEG C or a non-crystallising propylene homopolymer having a glass transition temperature less than or equal to -10 DEG C.
</ABSTRACT>
