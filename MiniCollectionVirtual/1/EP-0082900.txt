<INVENTION-TITLE>
Adjustable supports for bicycle saddles.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B62J100	B62J108	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B62J	B62J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B62J1	B62J1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The subject of the invention is an adjustable support or "saddle bar" for fixing a saddle onto the frame of a bicycle, permitting modification of the orientation and of the position of the saddle. On the crosspiece (10) of the saddle tube (1) there slides a carriage (2), the impressions (22) of which interact with corresponding impressions (31) of a head (3) which is joined to the said crosspiece by a single screw (4) which traverses an elongated slot (11) in the crosspiece and screws into a tapped hole (32) in the head.
