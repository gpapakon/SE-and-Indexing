<INVENTION-TITLE>
Tool for the formation of an internal thread.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B23G500	B23G506	B28D114	B28D114	F16B2500	F16B2500	F16B3900	F16B3930	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B23G	B23G	B28D	B28D	F16B	F16B	F16B	F16B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B23G5	B23G5	B28D1	B28D1	F16B25	F16B25	F16B39	F16B39	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A tool for forming an internal thread in concrete, rock or the like has a clamping part (1), a shank part (2), a guide part (3) with a thread (4) arranged on it, and a thread run-out part (5) which is formed with a slight taper and extends over the rest of the tool length and via which the thread (4) continues. The ratio of the outside diameter (da) to the core diameter (dk) of the guide part is about 1.25 to 1.5, and the ratio of the outside diameter (da) to the pitch (p) of the thread (4) is about 1.5 to 1.6. The thread (4) is provided with cutting teeth.
