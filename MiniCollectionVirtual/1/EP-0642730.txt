<INVENTION-TITLE>
Mower with a housing open to the ground.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A01D3400	A01D3400	A01D3463	A01D3482	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A01D	A01D	A01D	A01D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A01D34	A01D34	A01D34	A01D34	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In a mower with a housing (10) which is open to the ground and has an essentially vertically extending side wall (14) and in which at least one cutter (26) drivable by a vertical spindle (18) rotates, there rotating between the cutter (26) and the top side (12) of the housing (10) a disc part (36) with a radial outer portion (50) having a vertical clearance relative to the cutter (26), the disc part (36) has a further portion (52) which closes a space between the cutter top side, the spindle (18) and the inside of the radially outer portion (50) of the disc part (36).
