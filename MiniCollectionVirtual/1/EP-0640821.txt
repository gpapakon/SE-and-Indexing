<INVENTION-TITLE>
Apparatus for testing hollow bodies.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01M300	G01M300	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01M	G01M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01M3	G01M3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The device for testing hollow spaces for tightness has a testing head which can be displaced in the hollow space and is provided with an advance device and at least two sealing arrangements arranged at a distance from each other. Between the sealing arrangements, at least one satellite testing head is provided, which can be moved by means of its own advance device at an angle to the longitudinal axis of the testing head and is rotatably supported with reference to the longitudinal axis of the testing head.
