<INVENTION-TITLE>
Make-up case with upper and lower parts hinged to each other.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A45D4000	A45D4022	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A45D	A45D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A45D40	A45D40	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a make-up case of the type comprising two parts, an upper part (2) and a lower part (3), respectively, which are hinged on each other. According to the invention, the two parts of the case are formed with a base made from solid glass (5, 9) including projections (12, 13) on which the mounting decorative frames (6, 10) are locked with interposition of intermediate pieces (7, 11) for taking up the play. The invention relates to a make-up case of improved appearance and quality.
