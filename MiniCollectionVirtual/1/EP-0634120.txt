<INVENTION-TITLE>
Desk for supporting flat, information carrying objects.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47B1900	A47B1900	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47B	A47B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47B19	A47B19	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The stand (desk) for supporting flat information-carrying objects comprises a support structure (4) receiving the object and holding it wedged under the effect of its own weight, it being composed of a support strip (16) which, in its stretched-out position, constitutes a support surface for the flat object, means for holding its upper transverse end (20) in its stretched-out position on or by means of a holding support (23), and a casing (17) in which the support strip (16) is housed with elastic return, in its retracted, rest, position.   This invention is particularly of interest to the manufacturers of music stands.
