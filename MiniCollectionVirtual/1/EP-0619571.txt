<INVENTION-TITLE>
Color matrix display.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G02F113	G02F11335	G09F933	G09F933	G09G320	G09G320	G09G336	G09G336	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G02F	G02F	G09F	G09F	G09G	G09G	G09G	G09G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G02F1	G02F1	G09F9	G09F9	G09G3	G09G3	G09G3	G09G3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The display is intended to provide an image in the form of pixels following a regular array, including at least three sets of respectively green, red and blue pixels, the pixels of each set being distributed according to a matrix including rows and columns, all the pixels of the same row being addressed simultaneously for writing by respective column conductors. Each of the column conductors allocated to red or blue pixels writes over a surface area approximately double that in which a column conductor allocated to a green pixel writes, and the time or spatial period for writing green pixels is half of the period for writing red and blue pixels.
