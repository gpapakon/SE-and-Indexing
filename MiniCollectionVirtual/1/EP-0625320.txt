<INVENTION-TITLE>
Proofshoe
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
It is proposed to provide at least part of the upper (2) of a sample shoe for making a custom-made orthopaedic shoe with a window portion (3) consisting of a transparent, flexible plastic material. This window portion is preferably arranged in the region of the upper front part of the sample shoe. It makes it possible reliably to pinpoint pressure points and cavities when trying the shoe on.

<CLAIMS>
Proofshoe for determing the dimensions of the foot for the
manufacturing of a precisely fitting orthopedic shoe, comprising

a sole member (1) and an adjacent shoe upper (2) consisting of
leather, said upper having a windowed portion (3) only on the

top side of its front half and said windowed portion consisting
of a transparent and flexible foil of plastic material.
Proofshoe according to claim 1, characterized by said
windowed portion (3) consisting of styrol or polystyrol.
