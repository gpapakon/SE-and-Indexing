<INVENTION-TITLE>
Electrical apparatus with a configurable interface.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G05B1905	G05B1905	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G05B	G05B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G05B19	G05B19	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to an electrical apparatus, especially a memory-programmable controller, having a signal-processing intelligence (2), eg. a microprocessor (2), and at least one interface for transmitting and/or receiving digital signals having interface connections (17, 17') for the connection of external loads, eg. a bus connection. According to the invention, a configurable circuit (9, 9') is arranged between the intelligence (2) and the interface connections (17, 17'). By this means, the interface is considerably more universally usable than previously.
