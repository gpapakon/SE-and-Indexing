<INVENTION-TITLE>
SEAL BETWEEN TWO PARTS INSERTABLE INTO EACH OTHER.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F16L1700	F16L17035	F16L2102	F16L2103	F16L21035	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F16L	F16L	F16L	F16L	F16L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F16L17	F16L17	F16L21	F16L21	F16L21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A seal between two parts (concrete tubes) insertable into each other has the form of a seal composed of layers (13). It thus becomes easier to axially insert the parts into each other, while the radial supporting forces between the parts remain strong.
</ABSTRACT>
