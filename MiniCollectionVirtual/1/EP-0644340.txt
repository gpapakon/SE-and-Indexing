<INVENTION-TITLE>
Snap-hook device, particularly for mooring boats.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B63B2100	B63B2154	F16B4500	F16B4502	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B63B	B63B	F16B	F16B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B63B21	B63B21	F16B45	F16B45	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
This fastening device with a snap hook (snapshackle) includes a hook (12) which is closed from inside by a shutter (13) pressed by a spring (14) against the internal end of the hook (12). The hook (12) is articulated about a pin (8) between two support flanges (6,7) and is integral with a lever (12a) applied against a limit stop (10) integral with the flanges (6,7) by a bolt (15) mounted so it can slide longitudinally against a return spring (29) and controlled remotely by a flexible link (20).
