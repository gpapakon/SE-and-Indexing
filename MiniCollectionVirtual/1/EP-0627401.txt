<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for preparing acetic acid by oxidation of ethane.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a process for the preparation of acetic acid by controlled oxidation of ethane. The process according to the invention consists in reacting ethane with an oxygen source, the reaction being carried out in the presence of a catalyst whose active phase is based on vanadium(V), titanium and oxygen.
</ABSTRACT>
