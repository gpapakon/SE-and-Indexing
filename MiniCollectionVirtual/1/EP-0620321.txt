<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process of manufacturing a road traffic sensor and sensor for installing a cross the road.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E01F1100	E01F1100	G08G102	G08G102	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E01F	E01F	G08G	G08G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>E01F11	E01F11	G08G1	G08G1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The subject of the present invention is a road traffic detector comprising an elongated sensitive element to be placed across a roadway (2). This method consists in enclosing the said sensor over its entire periphery by means of filaments (8) made of inorganic or organic material, arranged in unidirectional layers, each extending over the entire length of the sensitive element of the sensor and being intended to be embedded completely in a mass of surfacing mix based on a binder which is cured by polymerisation. It is thus possible to obtain a detector in the form of a self-supporting bar (7).
