<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device and method for automatic recognition of electrical signal sequences, transmitted from a transmitter station to a receiving station.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H04H2028	H04H6037	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H04H	H04H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H04H20	H04H60	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A method for the automatic recognition of an electrical signal sequence transmitted from a transmitting station (3) to a receiving station (7) is described. For its automatic recognition, the signal sequence is marked by an identification signal before or during the transmission over its entire period of transmission. The identification signal is recognised in the receiving station (7) during the entire period of transmission.
