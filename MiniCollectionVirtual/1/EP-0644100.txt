<INVENTION-TITLE>
Baby-pushchair convertible into a walker.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47D1300	A47D1304	B62B700	B62B712	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47D	A47D	B62B	B62B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47D13	A47D13	B62B7	B62B7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The pushchair which can be converted into a walker for a baby includes:   - a seat (1) in which an opening (2) has been made, closed by a retractable seat part (3) so that the baby can be dropped through the said opening and put in the walking position, - a support (5) immobilised under the fixed part of the seat, and on which the baby can sit, his feet resting on the ground, or stand up to walk, holding onto the seat or onto a bar which there is on some pushchairs.
