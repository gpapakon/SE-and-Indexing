<INVENTION-TITLE>
Compact soil cover based on straw, bark or any other cellulose by-product, to be used instead of the usual soil covers, its production and its use.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A01G1302	A01G1302	C09K1700	C09K1752	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A01G	A01G	C09K	C09K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A01G13	A01G13	C09K17	C09K17	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Mulching or soil covering is a traditional technique which consists in covering the soil with straw, broken up to a greater or lesser extent, husks, leaves, etc. Untreated tree bark is also used for this purpose. The present invention consists of a compact soil covering consisting of various cellulosic materials (straw, cereal husk, cocoa-bean shells, paper, etc.) and optionally various additives (organic, basifying, acidifying, disinfecting, compression-assisting, oligo-elements or colorants), its method and manufacture and its use on cultivated or uncultivated soils.
</ABSTRACT>
