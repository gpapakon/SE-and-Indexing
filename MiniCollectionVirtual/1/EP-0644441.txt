<INVENTION-TITLE>
Integrated optical 2 x 2 coupler.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G02B6125	G02B6125	G02B628	G02B628	G02B634	G02B634	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G02B	G02B	G02B	G02B	G02B	G02B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G02B6	G02B6	G02B6	G02B6	G02B6	G02B6	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Integrated optical 2 x 2 coupler with straight incoming (input) waveguides (21, 22), running towards each other at an angle of  PHI , of different propagation constants or widths (w1, w2), minimum web width S (approximately 4  mu m) and obtusely connecting outgoing (output) waveguides (23, 24) of identical widths (w0) in circular arcs. Both directions are equivalent. Damping (attenuation) at the interstice is avoided during cross-over. Arcs in narrow waveguides (21) are avoided. Use for example in couplers (branching points), in particular 2 x N-couplers, and in Mach-Zehnder duplexers.
