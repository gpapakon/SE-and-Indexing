<INVENTION-TITLE>
Therapeutic kits against proteinacious mediators, method of preparation and pharmaceutical compositions containing them.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61K3800	A61K3800	A61K39395	A61K39395	A61P3700	A61P3700	C07K1618	C07K1624	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61K	A61K	A61K	A61K	A61P	A61P	C07K	C07K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61K38	A61K38	A61K39	A61K39	A61P37	A61P37	C07K16	C07K16	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Therapeutic kits for counteracting short-lived protein mediators of sufficient size to have at least three antibody sites available, which kits contain at least three antibodies which recognise three separate epitopes of the aforementioned protein mediator, at least one of the three antibodies blocking the biological activity of the protein mediator.
</ABSTRACT>
