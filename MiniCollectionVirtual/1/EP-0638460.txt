<INVENTION-TITLE>
Connecting means.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60P332	B60P332	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60P	B60P	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60P3	B60P3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A device for connecting plate-shaped elements, in particular prefabricated and precoated floor, wall and roof panels 11, 12 for a camper in an angled coverable manner consists of a side wall edge profile 13 and a floor/roof edge profile 36 and a profile 34, 43, which is assigned respectively to the latter in order to mount them by gripping them at the edges, at least one contact and attachment section 14, 17, 18, 27; 37, 40 being attached in each case to the side wall edge profile 13 and to the floor/roof edge profile 36 for the purpose of mounting with screws and/or bonding agents and at least one anchoring element 20, 29 for the engagement of covering profiles 23, 31 being provided.
