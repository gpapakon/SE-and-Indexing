<INVENTION-TITLE>
Dishwasher arrangement to prevent dripping on the washed contents.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47L1514	A47L1523	A47L1542	A47L1542	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47L	A47L	A47L	A47L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47L15	A47L15	A47L15	A47L15	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Dishwasher having internal surfaces which are usually arranged horizontally, these surfaces (2) being arranged at such an inclination that washing suds adhering to them can no longer drip on the washed dishes (7).
