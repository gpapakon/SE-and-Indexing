<ABSTRACT>
Multi-purpose gown whose rear side (3, 4) is continuously open in the longitudinal direction approximately in the region of the spine. According to the invention, one back part (3) is provided with an overlapping back part (6) which covers the other back part (4) completely or partially in the transverse direction of the gown and can be fastened thereon by one or more closures (8, 8a; 9, 9a; 13).
