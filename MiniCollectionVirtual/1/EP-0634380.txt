Liquid composition for plant treatment and process of manufacturing thereof.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The subject of the invention is a liquid composition for plant treatment by foliar (leaf) application, as well as a process for the manufacture of such a liquid composition. The invention is characterised in that is comprises extracts of marine algae (seaweed) and particles with a high calcium content, the mean size of which is less than 30 micrometres. The invention applies to the treatment of any plant which carries leaves and advantageously to plants carrying fruits.
