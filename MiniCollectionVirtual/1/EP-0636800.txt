<INVENTION-TITLE>
Bodyshell with caged nuts being kept by welded joints.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B62D2100	B62D2109	B62D2700	B62D2706	F16B3704	F16B3704	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B62D	B62D	B62D	B62D	F16B	F16B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B62D21	B62D21	B62D27	B62D27	F16B37	F16B37	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A caged nut (5) comprises a cage (9) which is shaped like a clip and into which a base (25) of a nut (10) can be inserted from one side, which nut, when inserted, projects with a cylindrical part (23), which has an internal thread (24), out of the cage (9). Prior to the insertion of the nut (10) the cage (9) is welded to a motor vehicle body shell.
