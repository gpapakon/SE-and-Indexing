<INVENTION-TITLE>
Insulating and illuminating device for roofings reflecting infrared rays.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device comprising white strips for solving the problems of overheating beneath verandas, and for insulating roofing in winter with excellent light transmission. It consists of a rigid envelope (4) made of transparent plastic with insulating translucent white strips (1) placed either above, below, or alternately. The device according to the invention is particularly intended for solar occultation in summer and for its light transmission in winter for roofing in general.
