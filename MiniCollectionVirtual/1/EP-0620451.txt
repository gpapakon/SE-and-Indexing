<INVENTION-TITLE>
Particle dosimeter.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01T102	G01T102	G01T300	G01T308	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01T	G01T	G01T	G01T	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01T1	G01T1	G01T3	G01T3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A dosimeter comprising a diode (1) as the radiation-sensitive element and an electrical measurement circuit divided into two branches (9) which are provided with different frequency filters (10), one of which measures the contribution of protons and neutrons and the other of which measures the contribution of electrons and photons, which particles deliver pulses of different shape on reaching the diode. The dose actually received can be determined with good precision. Application to portable dosimeters.
