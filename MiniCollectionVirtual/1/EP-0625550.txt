Dye mixtures, 1:2 cobalt complex formazan dyes and their use

<ABSTRACT>
Dye mixtures characterised in that they contain at least one 1:2 cobalt complex dye of the formazan compound of the formula 
<
IMAGE
>
  where   X is substituted or unsubstituted C1-C8-alkyl, phenyl, naphthyl, furyl or thienyl, R1 and R2 are independently of each other hydrogen or substituted or unsubstituted C1-C8-alkyl, phenyl or naphthyl,   m is from 1 to 4, and   the rings I, II, III and IV may optionally be further substituted, together with at least one anthraquinone dye of the formula (2) indicated in claim 1 or together with at least one 1:2 chromo or 1:2 cobalt complex dye of two identical or different azo compounds selected from the group consisting of the compounds of the formulae (3), (4), (5) and (6) indicated in claim 1;  and also novel 1:2 cobalt complex formazan dyes. The dye mixtures are suitable in particular for dyeing or printing natural or synthetic polyamide fibre materials, giving dyeings and prints having good fastness properties.
