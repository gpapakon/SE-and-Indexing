<INVENTION-TITLE>
Removable grill plate.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47J3706	A47J3706	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47J	A47J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47J37	A47J37	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
1.1. The invention relates to a removable grill plate having a smooth or structured upper side as a grilling area with a gravy channel running all the way round and an underside, as well as lateral carrying handles. 1.2. The object of the invention consists in simplifying the movable grill plate of the said type and making it independent of electrical connections. 2. The removable grill plate of the said type is designed such that the underside of the grill plate is designed as a heat transmission contact surface for heating from an external source, which, for the purpose of heating up, can be placed upon the hob, in particular Ceran ceramic hob, of an electric cooker, and that the grill plate consists of a material of high heat storage capacity.
