<INVENTION-TITLE>
Binder for instructions relating to the use of organizers.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B42D500	B42D504	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B42D	B42D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B42D5	B42D5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The manual is provided with a ring mechanism (20) which receives not only descriptive pages (30) but also original documents (40) of an organiser for writing upon. The writing pages (30) are selected to be substantially larger than the original documents (40), so that a projecting edge is available on which text blocks (32) can describe individual features of the organiser. For better labelling, indicator lines (34, 44) are provided which point from the text page to the feature to be identified. By way of deviation from the customary adaptation of the ring mechanism to the format of the manual, a ring mechanism is used which corresponds to the size of the original documents.
