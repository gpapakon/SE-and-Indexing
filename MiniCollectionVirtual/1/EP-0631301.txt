<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for fabrication of semiconductor power device for high commutation steepness.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H01L2102	H01L21304	H01L21329	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H01L	H01L	H01L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H01L21	H01L21	H01L21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The non-destructive off-commutation gradient of power semiconductor components can be considerably increased if the blocking pn junction is produced from a polished surface of the semiconductor component. The pn junction then becomes homogeneous enough for local overloads to be avoided, by virtue of which the off-commutation gradient can be increased.
