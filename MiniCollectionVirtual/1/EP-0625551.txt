<INVENTION-TITLE>
Mixtures of dyes, process for their preparation and the use thereof
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Dye mixtures characterised in that they contain at least two reactive dyes of the formula 
<
IMAGE
>
  where   A1 and A2 are each the radical of a monoazo, polyazo, metal complex azo, anthraquinone, phthalocyanine, formazan, azomethine, dioxazine, phenazine, stilbene, triphenylmethane, xanthene, thioxanthone, nitroaryl, naphthoquinone, pyrenequinone or perylenetetracarbimide dye, R1, R2, R3 and R4 are each independently of the others hydrogen or substituted or unsubstituted C1-C4-alkyl,   B is an aliphatic bridge member, and Y1 and Y2 are independently of each other halogen or carboxypyridinium, and in at least one of the reactive dyes of the formula (1) the radicals A1 and A2 have the same meaning, are suitable in particular for dyeing or printing hydroxyl- or nitrogen-containing fibre materials. The dyeings are obtained in a high yield with good fastness properties.
