<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method and apparatus for manufacturing a transport package.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65B1106	B65B1110	B65B1318	B65B1332	B65B2708	B65B2708	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65B	B65B	B65B	B65B	B65B	B65B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65B11	B65B11	B65B13	B65B13	B65B27	B65B27	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A method for manufacturing a transport package consisting of a container (1,2) receiving the product (3) and of a fastening strip (4) looping round the container and the product (3) provides for introducing the product into the container and for looping the fastening strip around the container together with the product. The fastening strip (4) is drawn off from a single roll (19), laid around the unit consisting of the container and of the product in a loop-like manner, cut to length and put under tensile stress. Subsequently, with the tensile stress being maintained, one end (6) of the fastening strip (4) is coated with glue and is pressed onto the other end (5) until the glue sets.
