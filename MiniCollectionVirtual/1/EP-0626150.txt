<INVENTION-TITLE>
Dust bag support for a vacuum cleaner
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47L914	A47L914	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47L	A47L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a dust-bag support (1) placed inside a vacuum cleaner and comprising a handle (2) intended for grasping the said dust-bag support (1), an opening (3) intended for supporting the lateral edges (4 and 4') of a collar (5) of a dust bag (6) and attachment means (7) capable of engaging with the said collar (5). According to the invention, the said dust-bag support (1) also includes a means (9) for unlocking the attachment means (7) which is integral with a manoeuvring member (10) carried by the said support (1) and controlling ejection of the used dust bag (6).

<CLAIMS>
Bag support (1) designed to be placed inside a vacuum 
cleaner, the said support (1) comprising a handle (2) designed 

to grip the said support (1) in order to withdraw the said 

support (1), an opening (3) designed to support the periphery 
of a collar (5) of a dust bag (6) and attachment means (7) 

suitable for engaging with the said collar (5),
characterised in that
 the bag support (1) also has a means (9) 
for unlocking the attachment means (7) which is fixed to a 

handling device (10) carried by the said support (1) and 
controlling the ejection of the used dust bag (6). 
Bag support according to Claim 1,
characterised in that
 the attachment means (7) comprise two 
locks, at least one of which is, firstly, movably mounted on 

the bag support (1) counter to an elastic return means (11) in 
the attachment position and, secondly, connected to the 

unlocking means (9). 
Bag support according to Claim 2,
characterised in that
 the locking means (9) comprises an arm 
(14) which is mounted so as to pivot about an axis (15) and 

one end of which, on the one hand, constitutes the handling 
device (10) and the other end of which, on the other hand, is 

fixed to the movable lock. 
Bag support according to Claim 2 or 3,
characterised in that
 the two locks (7) are situated opposite 
each other on each side of the opening (3) of the bag support 

(1). 
Bag support according to any one of the preceding claims,
characterised in that
 the unlocking means (9) and the movable 
lock (7) are formed of a single piece (7-9) pivotally mounted 

on the bag support (1) about the axis (15) parallel to a  
 

lateral edge (13) of the opening (3) in the bag support (1). 
Bag support according to Claim 5,
characterised in that
 the pivotally mounted piece (7-9) has a 
notch (18) in which the periphery of the collar (5) of the 

dust bag (6) is able to engage, two pivots (19 and 20) able to 
engage in recesses formed in the support (1) and a free end 

constituting the handling device (10) and emerging on the 
outside of the bag support (1) through a hole (30) produced 

near the lateral edge (13) of the opening (3) in the bag 
support (1).
