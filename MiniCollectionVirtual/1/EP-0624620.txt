<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the production of fibres containing polypropylene as main component.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08J1100	C08J1106	D01F604	D01F606	D04H154	D04H154	D04H164	D04H164	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C08J11	C08J11	D01F6	D01F6	D04H1	D04H1	D04H1	D04H1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A process for the production of fibres containing polypropylene as main component by the method of melt spinning comprises melting a binder-consolidated nonwoven and spinning the melt to make new fibres.
</ABSTRACT>
