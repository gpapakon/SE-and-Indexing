<INVENTION-TITLE>
Semiconductor device with a passivation layer.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C23C1626	C23C1626	H01L2102	H01L2131	H01L21314	H01L2328	H01L2329	H01L2331	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C23C	C23C	H01L	H01L	H01L	H01L	H01L	H01L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C23C16	C23C16	H01L21	H01L21	H01L21	H01L23	H01L23	H01L23	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Passivation layers consisting of amorphous, hydrogen-containing carbon satisfy many requirements. Pure a-C:H layers are, however, not optimal for p-doped substrates since an invertion layer can be formed. This can be prevented by doping with boron by between 0.1 per 1000  and 4%.
</ABSTRACT>
