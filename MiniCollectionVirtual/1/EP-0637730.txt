<INVENTION-TITLE>
Angular encoder, angular position detector and rotational pulse generator.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01D526	G01D534	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01D	G01D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01D5	G01D5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a disc with periodic patterns to be scanned by a light cone. For each position or, respectively, for each bit, one pattern field is provided, the structure of which is finer than the beam diameter of the reading lightbeam. The pattern fields are in each case found in one of two possible stripe textures for the logic states 1 and 0, these patterns differing clearly from one another in their characteristics with respect to interference and/or diffraction so that angular positions can also be detected when the disc is at rest.
