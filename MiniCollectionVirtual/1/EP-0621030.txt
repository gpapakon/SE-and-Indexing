<CLAIMS>
Use, as active compounds, of benzomorpholine
derivatives in the preparation of a cosmetic or

dermatological composition having a depigmenting action,
characterized in that the said compounds correspond to

the following formula:


in which:

R
1
 represents a hydrogen atom or an alkyl radical having
from 1 to 4 carbon atoms, and
R
2
 represents a hydroxyl radical in the 6- or 7-position.
Use according to Claim 1, characterized in that
the said compounds of formula (I) are preferably chosen

from:

6-hydroxybenzomorpholine,
7-hydroxybenzomorpholine,
3-methyl-7-hydroxybenzomorpholine.
Use according to either of the preceding claims,
characterized in that the concentration of compounds of

formula (I) in the composition is between 0.01% and 10%
by weight with respect to the total weight of the composition.
Use according to any one of the preceding claims,
characterized in that the concentration of compounds of

formula (I) in the composition is preferably between 0.5%
and 5% by weight with respect to the total weight of the 

composition.
Use according to any one of the preceding claims,
characterized in that the cosmetic or dermatological

composition is provided in the form of a lotion, of a
cream, of a milk, of a gel, of a mask, of microspheres or

nanospheres or of vesicular dispersions.
Use according to any one of the preceding claims,
characterized in that the cosmetic or dermatological

composition additionally contains a humectant, a surface
agent, a keratolytic agent, an anti-inflammatory agent,

a complexing agent, an anti-oxidizing agent, a preserving
agent, a fragrance or a sunscreen.
</CLAIMS>
