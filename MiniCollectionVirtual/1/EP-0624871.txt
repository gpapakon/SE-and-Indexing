<INVENTION-TITLE>
Magnetic head for magneto-optical recording.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G11B502	G11B502	G11B1100	G11B1110	G11B11105	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G11B	G11B	G11B	G11B	G11B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G11B5	G11B5	G11B11	G11B11	G11B11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The head comprises a silicon substrate (21) in which is buried a conductive winding (22). The winding (22) is set back with respect to the plane of travel (28) of the head. A magnetic circuit (24) can complete the head. Application to magneto-optical recording.
