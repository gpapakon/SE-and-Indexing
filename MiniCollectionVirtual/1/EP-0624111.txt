<INVENTION-TITLE>
GOLFING INSTALLATION.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A63B6936	A63B6936	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A63B	A63B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A63B69	A63B69	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Described is a golfing installation with several tees (2.1, ..., 2.18) disposed round a common green (1). The tees (2.1, ..., 2.18) differ sufficiently from each other for each to present different teeing-off conditions for a golfer playing on to the green (1). The green is preferably in the shape of a banked part-conical surface so that incoming balls roll towards a single collection point.
</ABSTRACT>
