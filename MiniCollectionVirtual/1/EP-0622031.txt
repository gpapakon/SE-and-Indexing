<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for humidifying a sun shade.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A45B300	A45B300	A45B2300	A45B2300	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A45B	A45B	A45B	A45B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A45B3	A45B3	A45B23	A45B23	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a sun shade with a material covering. Despite the shade provided by it, it can still become very hot under such a sun shade, which means that there is a requirement to make further cooling possible here. For this purpose, the invention specifies equipping the sun shade with a liquid reservoir (5) from which at least one liquid-conducting line extends, which distributes liquid over the material covering (3) where it evaporates and thus brings about cooling under the shade due to the evaporating cooling.
