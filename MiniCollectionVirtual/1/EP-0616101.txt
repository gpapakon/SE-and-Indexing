<INVENTION-TITLE>
Vehicle locking system.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60R2504	B60R2504	G07C900	G07C900	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60R	B60R	G07C	G07C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60R25	B60R25	G07C9	G07C9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In a vehicle-locking system, in addition to triggering the indication that the energy supply of the energy source of the transmitter is low, a recording device temporarily cancels the moving-off stop until the energy source has been recharged again or renewed, even when the locks of the vehicle continue to be actuated during the cancellation of the moving-off stop.
</ABSTRACT>
