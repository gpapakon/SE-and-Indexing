<INVENTION-TITLE>
Precipitation and flocculation agent.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C02F152	C02F152	C02F154	C02F154	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C02F	C02F	C02F	C02F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C02F1	C02F1	C02F1	C02F1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Precipitant and flocculant having the composition     zeolite A 20 to 80 % by weight   kieselguhr 5 to 40 % by weight   aluminium sulphate 5 to 50 % by weight   bentonite 0 to 30 % by weight   solid polymer 0 to 10 % by weight.   It can be produced by mixing the individual components together and, if appropriate compressing them. It is used for purifying waste waters (effluents) of the surface treatment (machining) industry and for removing heavy metal ions.
