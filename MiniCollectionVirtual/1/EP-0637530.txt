<INVENTION-TITLE>
Windscreen wiper with linking piece inserted between two side walls provided on the mounting part.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60S132	B60S134	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60S	B60S	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60S1	B60S1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a windscreen wiper device comprising a drive head (110) designed to be driven in rotation about a drive axis (E), a casing (100) articulated, at a first end, to this drive head about an axis of deflection (D) extending transversely to said drive axis and equipped, at its second end, with a wiper holder rod supporting a wiper designed to come into contact with a surface to be wiped, characterised in that the drive head (110) includes two flanges (112a, 112b) between which there extends a journal (121) along said axis of deflection (D), the first end of the casing extending between these two flanges in order to be articulated on the said journal.
