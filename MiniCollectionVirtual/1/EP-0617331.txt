<INVENTION-TITLE>
Light-patternable multilayer element, process for its fabrication and printing form obtained therefrom.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G03F700	G03F700	G03F709	G03F709	G03F711	G03F711	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G03F	G03F	G03F	G03F	G03F	G03F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G03F7	G03F7	G03F7	G03F7	G03F7	G03F7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a photostructurable, multilayer element for the production of relief plates, comprising at least one solid photostructurable layer and a non-stick (abhesive) or top layer applied thereto, where the photostructurable layer has optionally been applied to a dimensionally stable support by means of an adhesion layer, and the non-stick or top layer comprises a transparent, micro disperse, multiphase layer of one or more finely divided, hydrophobic, water-insoluble polymers having a primary particle diameter of from 0.01 to 10  mu m which are coated by at least one ionic or nonionic surfactant or a polymeric binder which is water-soluble or water-swellable, or by a mixture of such a surfactant and such a polymeric binder. The photostructurable, multilayer elements according to the invention are suitable for the production of printing plates and relief stamping plates.
</ABSTRACT>
