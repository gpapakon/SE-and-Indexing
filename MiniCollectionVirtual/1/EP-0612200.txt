<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for indicating residual heat.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H05B102	H05B102	H05B368	H05B374	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H05B	H05B	H05B	H05B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H05B1	H05B1	H05B3	H05B3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In particular in cooking zones within glass ceramic cooking hobs, the residual heat is signalled by means of a display; according to the invention, this display is integrated into the setting display for the power supply.
</ABSTRACT>
