<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to an interface circuit (50) intended to be connected on the one hand to a bifilar bus (58, 60) and on the other hand to an application circuit (66). The interface circuit (50) comprises a full-wave rectifier bridge (D1, D2, D3, D4) serving to supply the application circuit (66) jointly with a power-supply capacitor (68). According to a preferred embodiment of the invention, the interface circuit furthermore comprises a first switch (70) and a second switch (72) serving to define a constantly specified voltage between a reference point (54) outside and a reference point (62) inside the application circuit.
