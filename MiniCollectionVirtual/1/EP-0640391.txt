<ABSTRACT>
The invention is intended to create a catalyst for the oxidative detoxification even of traces of the abovementioned group of substances which does not exhibit the disadvantages of known catalysts containing metals of the platinum group with respect to price, control method required and sensitivity to abrasive dusts. This object is achieved in a honeycomb-shaped ceramic catalyst by the combination of the following features:     - support component made of sheet silicates, - uniformly admixed ternary oxides as catalytically active component,     - specific surface area
