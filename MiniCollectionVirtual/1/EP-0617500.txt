<INVENTION-TITLE>
Wireless data transmission device.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device for wire-free data transmission by means of soundwaves, lightwaves or such spherical waves, having at least one transmitter and one receiver which is tuned to the transmitter. A solar cell is used to supply power to the transmitter. In addition, an energy store and a microprocessor for functional control are provided. The transmitter itself has an infrared transmitting element or ultrasound transmitting element or a transmitting antenna and a glass-breakage signalling device so that the device can preferably be used as an intrusion protection device.
