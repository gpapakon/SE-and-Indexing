<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method and plant for the mechanical treatment of waste mixtures.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B03B900	B03B906	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B03B	B03B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B03B9	B03B9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In the mechanical treatment of waste materials containing metallic and non-metallic parts, especially of appliance scrap with electrical and/or electronic components, with the aim of recovering utilisable products by means of dry comminuting and sorting stages, a high selectivity of the separated products is achieved if, according to the invention, the dry pretreatment is followed by wet sorting, fine material occurring during dry pretreatment being fed to a vibrating table (17) and coarse material occurring being fed to a wet-jigging machine (16).
