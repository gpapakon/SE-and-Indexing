<INVENTION-TITLE>
Heater connection device and inserting cartridge for the same.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F16K1102	F16K11085	F24H912	F24H912	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F16K	F16K	F24H	F24H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F16K11	F16K11	F24H9	F24H9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a connection device for connecting a body, preferably a heater (radiator), to a pipe system, preferably a heating pipe system, of a heating installation which is operated with a flow medium, preferably a heat transfer medium, having a housing (10) which comprises connecting portions for coupling to the body and the pipe system, as well as at least one regulating device (28,40) for influencing the flow medium in a through-flow channel (12,16 and 14,18) through the housing (10). Formed in the housing (10) is a receiving channel or bore (20) which is open on at least one side, intersects at least one through-flow channel (12,16 and 14,18) and in which a functional device including the at least one regulating member (28,40) is seated.
