<CLAIMS>
A process for preparing halomethylbenzoyl cyanides of the 
general formula I 


Ph-CO-CN
 
   where Ph is a phenyl radical which is substituted by chloromethyl 

or bromomethyl and which, if desired, can additionally 
carry 1 to 4 further radicals which are inert to the reaction,
 
   which comprises reacting a halomethylbenzoyl chloride of the 

general formula II 

Ph-CO-Cl
 
   with an alkali metal cyanide or transition metal cyanide. 
A process as claimed in claim 1, wherein the reaction is carried 
out in an organic diluent. 
A process as claimed in claim 1, wherein the reaction is carried 
out in an organic diluent in the presence of a catalyst. 
A process as claimed in claim 1, wherein the compounds II are 
reacted with an aqueous sodium cyanide or potassium cyanide 

solution in an organic diluent in the presence of a phasetransfer 
catalyst.
