<INVENTION-TITLE>
APPARATUS FOR MEASURING SEMICONDUCTOR SURFACE POTENTIAL AND IMPURITY CONCENTRATION
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01N2700	H01L2166	G01N2704	G01R3126	G01N2700	H01L2166	G01N2704	G01R3126	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01N	H01L	G01N	G01R	G01N	H01L	G01N	G01R	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01N27	H01L21	G01N27	G01R31	G01N27	H01L21	G01N27	G01R31	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The apparatus measures the surface potential and 
impurity concentration in a semiconductor body (10) by 

monitoring the current flowing in said semiconductor body 
when it is biased with a ramp voltage (14) above its flat 

band voltage, and summing (20) the monitored current 
with the ramp voltage biasing the body. The apparatus 

provides direct measurement of surface potential and 
impurity concentration in a semiconductor structure and 

is especially useful in metal-insulator-semiconductor (MIS) 
structures. 
</ABSTRACT>
