<INVENTION-TITLE>
Lock-slide, especially for motor vehicle seats.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60N212	B60N212	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60N	B60N	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60N2	B60N2	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The slide comprises, in particular, a stationary element (10), a moving element (20), a lock (30) with a lockplate (31) and a lock bolt (32), a lock barring device (40) and an operating device (50). The lock barring device (40) interacts with the stationary element (10) and the lock bolt (32) in order to immobilise the lock bolt when it is locked onto the lockplate and coupled to the moving element. Application to slideways for landbased motor vehicle seats where the moving element acts as a reminder to automatically place the seat back in its forwards adjustment position.
