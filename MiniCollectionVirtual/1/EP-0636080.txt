<INVENTION-TITLE>
TRANSHIPPING FRAME
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60P164	B60P164	B61D4700	B61D4700	B65D9012	B65D9014	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60P	B60P	B61D	B61D	B65D	B65D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60P1	B60P1	B61D47	B61D47	B65D90	B65D90	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Lifting frame (1) for raising and lowering heavy loads, e.g. containers, interchangeable bodies, swap bodies, etc. with corner fittings (8) to ISO and other standards, or other attachment elements, with retractable support legs (7) and lifting devices (14), e.g. air cushions which can operate upwards or downwards, and allow a limited and controlled lateral movement of the container, and guide rails (41) for precise positioning of the lifting frame (1) for transshipment. In addition, individual operations can be partly automated if desired.
</ABSTRACT>
