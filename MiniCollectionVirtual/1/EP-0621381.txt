<INVENTION-TITLE>
Prestressed reinforcement element.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E04C500	E04C507	E04C507	E04C508	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E04C	E04C	E04C	E04C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a prestressed reinforcement element (8) having centrically arranged stressing members (14) which extend in at least one direction and consist of steel, glass or aramide or carbon in a concrete or mortar matrix, and having outer surfaces which are designed for bonding with the surrounding concrete (20). The concrete or mortar matrix contains flexible fibres made of plastic, glass, carbon or metal or contains rigid reinforcement wires or strips, made of metal, in a quantity which is effective for evening out the crack distribution.
