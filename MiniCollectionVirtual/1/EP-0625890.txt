<INVENTION-TITLE>
DEVICE FOR CORRECTING SLIGHT URINARY INCONTINENCE IN WOMEN.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61F200	A61F200	A61F204	A61F204	A61F537	A61F537	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61F	A61F	A61F	A61F	A61F	A61F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61F2	A61F2	A61F2	A61F2	A61F5	A61F5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A device for correcting slight urinary incontinence in women. The device is designed to be inserted into the vaginal canal (VA) and held under the bladder (VE) in order to support the latter along with the urethra (UR) through the anterior vaginal wall, and counter ptosis of the bladder neck. The device consists of a removable body (1) with rounded edges made of a non-adhesive, non-allergenic, and chemically and biologically inert material. Said body (1) does not prevent micturition and can easily be inserted in and withdrawn from the vaginal canal (VA) as required by the user.
</ABSTRACT>
