<INVENTION-TITLE>
Apparatus for automatic filling and demoulding of food products with moulds and at least products receiving device.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A23G302	A23G302	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A23G	A23G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A23G3	A23G3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an apparatus with moulds and at least one product-receiving device for automatically feeding and automatically removing products of the foodstuff industry, especially for icecream. A way of automatically demoulding complicated shapes in the case of undercut moulds is also disclosed according to the invention. The apparatus according to the invention is especially well suited for automatically filling and emptying undercut moulds in the icecream industry.
</ABSTRACT>
