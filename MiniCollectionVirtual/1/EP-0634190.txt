<INVENTION-TITLE>
Connector for medical cavity tube.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a connector for the releasable coupling and decoupling of individual components, especially of medical lines. There is a connector which brings about axial displacement with respect to one another of the line parts to be connected to one another, brings about the connecting or decoupling and simultaneously ensures a gas-tight connection.
