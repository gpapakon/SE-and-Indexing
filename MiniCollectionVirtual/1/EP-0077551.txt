<ABSTRACT>
1,4-Naphthoquinones of formula (I), methods for their 
preparation, pharmaceutical and veterinary formulations 

thereof and the use thereof in human and animal therapy are 
disclosed. 


Particularly preferred compounds of formula (I) are 
2-(4′-t-butylcyclohexyl)-3-hydroxy-1,4-naphthoquinone and 

2-hydroxy-3-(4′-t-pentylcyclohexyl)-1,4-naphthoquinone. 
The compounds are of value as antiprotozoal agents, in 

particular antimalarial and anticoccidial agents. 
</ABSTRACT>
