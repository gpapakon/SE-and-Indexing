<INVENTION-TITLE>
Reclosable carton
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65D554	B65D554	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65D	B65D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65D5	B65D5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
It includes a flap (2) glued over a part (7) of the front face, delimited by a score line (8), a slot (9) for the introduction of a tab (3) characterised in that the front face (5) also comprises an access opening (10) delimited by at least one score line (14), the said access opening being situated at least partially opposite the said tab (3) when the carton is closed.

<CLAIMS>
Box made of card, produced from a single blank (1) 
folded along prefolding lines to form a flap (2) lying over 

the front face (5) of the box, the flap (2) being stuck to 
a part (7) of the front face, defined by a tear line (8) 

which is split when the box is opened for the first time, 
characterised in that the flap (2) comprises a tab (3) able 

to be introduced into a slit (9) provided on the said front 
face between the tear line (8) and the fold line (11) of 

the said front face to close the box, the front face (5) 
also comprising an access part (10) defined by at least one 

tear line (14) able to form an access opening, the said 
access part being located at least partially opposite the 

said tab (3) when the box is in the closed state. 
Box according to claim 1, characterised in that the 
access opening (10) is located close to the slit (9) and 

separated therefrom by a strip of card (12). 
Box according to one of claims 1 or 2, characterised in 
that the tear line (14) is produced as a semi-circle. 
</CLAIMS>
