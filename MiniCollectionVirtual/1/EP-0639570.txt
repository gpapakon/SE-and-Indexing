<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the preparation of aromatic carboxylic acids.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B01J2316	B01J2322	B01J2702	B01J2702	C07B4100	C07B4108	C07B6100	C07B6100	C07C5116	C07C51275	C07C6300	C07C6300	C07D21500	C07D21548	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
Aromatic carboxylic acids of the formula I  AR(COOH)n    I (where AR is iso- or heteroaromatic radical and n is 1 to 4) are prepared by oxidising a compound II  Ar(CH3)n    II with an oxidising nitrogen/oxygen compound (III) in the presence of sulphuric acid and of a vanadium compound (IV) at 120 to 180 DEG C in the liquid phase, 20 to 100 mol % of the vanadium compound (IV) being available per mol of II for the oxidation in the reaction mixture.
</ABSTRACT>
