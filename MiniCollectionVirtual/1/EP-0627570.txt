<INVENTION-TITLE>
Control cable end fitting.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F16C100	F16C126	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F16C	F16C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an end fitting (1) for a control cable with a sleeve (2) for the accommodation of an actuating jacket of the control cable. The sleeve (2) is connected to a guide tube (3) by a joint (5, 6, 7) and is furthermore surrounded by a connecting bush (11) for fixing the control cable, this connecting bush being connected positively to the sleeve (2). According to the invention, the joint is designed as a ball joint with a ball-joint head (7) and a ball-joint socket (6) as ball-joint components (5), and the ball-joint head (7) and/or the ball-joint socket (6) are composed of a plastic and designed so as to be elastic in such a way that they can be snapped into one another.
