<CLAIMS>
Detachable sabot (10) for a sub-calibre projectile, comprising a 
sabot casing (11), a sabot tail (12) and a guide band (14), at least 

partially provided with longitudinal grooves (13) and therewith cooperating 
nominal breaking points (16), 
characterized in that
 the 
sabot (10) is made in one piece and of plastic material, and the 

longitudinal grooves (13) are arranged continuously from the front 
to the gas-sealing area (14a) on the guide band (14) with 

expansions (15) cross-sectionally in the base area of the grooves. 
Detachable sabot (10) as in Claim 1, 
characterized by
 additional 
grooves (21) on the sabot tail (12). 
Detachable sabot (10) according to Claim 1 or 2, 
characterized in 
that
 the sabot (10) is made of fibre-reinforced plastic material, 
such as glass, carbon and/or armide fibres. 
</CLAIMS>
