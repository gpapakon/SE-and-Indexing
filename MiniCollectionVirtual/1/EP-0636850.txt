<INVENTION-TITLE>
Mine-clearing system, in particular for pressure-plate type land mines.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F41H1100	F41H1116	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F41H	F41H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F41H11	F41H11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Mine-clearing system, particularly for land mines (2) of the pressure-plate type, comprising land-clearing means pushed by a motorised vehicle (3). The mine-clearing means consist of a deformable elongate surface (5) having a master unit length suitable to apply a sufficient pressure force to the mine (2) for a sufficient period of time to cause the mine to explode as the said surface (5) passes over it. This surface consists, for example, of articulated metal shoes (plates) (6) forming a caterpillar track (8).
