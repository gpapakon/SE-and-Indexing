Directional resonance magnetometer.

<ABSTRACT>
The differential magnetometer of the invention comprises a reference assembly (ENSr) and at least one measuring assembly (ENSm). The bias current generated by the first is the same as the bias current used in the second. The feedback signal produced by the second directly gives the difference between the fields. Application in magnetometry.
