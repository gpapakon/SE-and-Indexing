<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for separating the remainder from the crap discharged from the threshing drum.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A01F1218	A01F1218	A01F1228	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A01F	A01F	A01F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A01F12	A01F12	A01F12	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A device for separating residual material from threshed crop leaving a threshing unit consists of a first and a second separating drum (24, 26), of adjustable separating baskets (28, 30) assigned to these and of an adjusting device (35) which engages on the respective mutually confronting end faces of the separating baskets (28, 30) and by means of which these are adjustable between a position located radially near the separating drums (24, 26) and a position radially remote from these.
