<INVENTION-TITLE>
Cylindrical grinding machine.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B23Q100	B23Q101	B24B500	B24B504	B24B4100	B24B4102	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B23Q	B23Q	B24B	B24B	B24B	B24B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B23Q1	B23Q1	B24B5	B24B5	B24B41	B24B41	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A cylindrical grinding machine has a machine bed, a driven workpiece headstock (3) with a feed drive, and a driven grinding headstock (5) with a feed drive. The machine bed is constructed in the form of a fixed slab (1) which may be oriented horizontally or vertically. A tailstock (9) is equipped with a feed drive which can be controlled independently of the other feed drives.
