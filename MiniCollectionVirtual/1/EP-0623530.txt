<INVENTION-TITLE>
Overhead conveyor system with a set of profiling strips for mounting.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65G2100	B65G2102	B65G2120	B65G2122	E01B2500	E01B2524	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65G	B65G	B65G	B65G	E01B	E01B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65G21	B65G21	B65G21	B65G21	E01B25	E01B25	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
An overhead conveyor system (19) which can be mounted simply and in a short time is described. For this purpose, there is provided a set of mounting profiles (1, 13) for mounting a rail system (2), which contains at least one first profile element (1) provided with a plurality of depressions (6) and at least one second profile element (13) provided with a plurality of elevations (14) engaging into the depression (6), the width (B) of each elevation (14) being equal, as seen in a top view, in two directions angled relative to one another, and corresponding to the width (B) of the depression (6), as seen in the top view.
