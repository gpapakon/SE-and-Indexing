<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for bidirectional transmission of analog and/or digital signals.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H04B138	H04B138	H04M172	H04M1737	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H04B	H04B	H04M	H04M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H04B1	H04B1	H04M1	H04M1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A device for bidirectional transmission of analog and/or digital signals between a base station and a distant station, the signals being transmitted between the base station and the distant station, which are designed as transmitting and receiving stations, by wire-free means with the aid of infrared waves.
