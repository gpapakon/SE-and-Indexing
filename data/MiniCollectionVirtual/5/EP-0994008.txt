<INVENTION-TITLE>
Body element
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B62D2900	B62D2900	B62D2904	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B62D	B62D	B62D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
A bodywork component (1), comprising a shape-stable contour-defining body (2) with a smooth-surfaced plastic or composite material cladding (3), is new. A bodywork component (1) has a shape-stable contour-defining body (2) surrounded by a plastic or composite material cladding (3) with a level surface (4) onto which the bodywork skin (5a, b) is applied. An Independent claim is also included for production of the above bodywork component.
</ABSTRACT>
