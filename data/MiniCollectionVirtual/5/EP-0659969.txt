<INVENTION-TITLE>
Pivoting door for passage way for persons.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E05F1512	E05F1512	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E05F	E05F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>E05F15	E05F15	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a pivoting door (1) for a passageway for persons, having a door leaf (5) which is arranged on a housing (2) and can be pivoted about a vertical axis (8) by an electric drive and by a drive mechanism, having control means which control the pivoting operations of the door leaf (5), the rotary movement of the electric drive being transmitted, via a drive pinion (15) arranged on the electric drive, to a drive ring (19) which is connected in a rotationally fixed manner to the door leaf (5) and is provided with inner toothing. The essence of the invention consists in that the electric drive is formed by a stepping motor (11), and in that provision is made, between the drive pinion (15) and the drive ring (19), for at least one intermediate wheel (18) which is in engagement with the drive pinion (15) and with the drive ring (19).
