<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method and device for testing blood vessels.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61B5024	A61B5024	A61B5026	A61B5026	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61B	A61B	A61B	A61B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61B5	A61B5	A61B5	A61B5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
For examining a blood vessel (G) which can be positioned in the groove (20) of a guide block (12), an optical pattern (26) is used, which is generated through the ends (22) of several fibre optic guides (24) arranged in a row in the groove wall.
