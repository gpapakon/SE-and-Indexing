<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a multilayered packaging tray having layers bonded to one another with good adhesion for receiving foods secreting fluid, having a liquid-impermeable base layer of plastic, forming the outer side of the packaging tray, a liquid-permeably designed outer layer of plastic, forming the inner side of the packaging tray, and an inlay formed between the base layer and the outer layer, with a layer of hot-melt adhesive applied on the base layer and a layer of moisture-absorbing polymers applied hereupon and a covering layer of a nonwoven, and also relates to a method for producing such packaging trays.
