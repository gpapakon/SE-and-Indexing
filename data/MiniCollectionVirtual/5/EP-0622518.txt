<INVENTION-TITLE>
Rotation mechanism.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E06B1100	E06B1108	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
Provided as a blocking member for turnstiles and/or rotary barriers with a blocking element which can rotate about a spindle, the invention provides a wrap-spring free-wheel which acts on the rotary spindle (3) of the blocking element. The wrap-spring free-wheel exhibits a helical resilient strip (9) which is wound around the rotary spindle (3) of the blocking element. Preferably, the blocking action of the wrap-spring free-wheel can be eliminated by means of an actuating member, preferably a magnet-operated switch.
