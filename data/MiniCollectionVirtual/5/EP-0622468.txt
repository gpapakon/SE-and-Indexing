<INVENTION-TITLE>
Use of a copper-aluminium alloy for the production of nickelfree articles for everyday use
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C22C901	C22C901	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C22C	C22C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C22C9	C22C9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Copper-aluminium alloy contains in wt.%: 4-9 (6.5-8) Al; 2-5 (2-3.5) Mn; 1-3 (2-2.5) Fe; balance Cu plus usual impurities. The alloy may additionally contain 0.5-1.5 wt.% Pb.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
Use of a copper-aluminium alloy with manganese and iron 
as further components, consisting of (in % by weight) 


4 to 9 % aluminium, 
2 to 5 % manganese, 
1 to 3 % iron, 
as required 0.5 to 1.5 % lead, 
rest copper and usual impurities, 
 
for producing nickel-free consumer articles, particularly 

spectacle parts, pieces of jewellery, cutlery, haberdashery, 
etc., which do not trigger any allergic reactions. 
Use of a copper alloy as claimed in Claim 1 with 6.5 to 
8 % aluminium for the purpose according to Claim 1. 
Use of a copper alloy as claimed in Claim 1 or 2 with 2 
to 3.5 % manganese for the purpose according to Claim 1. 
Use of a copper alloy as claimed in one or more of 
Claims 1 to 3 with 2 to 2.5 % iron for the purpose according 

to Claim 1. 
</CLAIMS>
