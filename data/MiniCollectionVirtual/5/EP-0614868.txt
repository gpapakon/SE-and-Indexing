<ABSTRACT>
Process for the preparation of  alpha , beta -monoethylenic unsaturated aldehydes, carboxylic acids or nitriles containing 3 to 5 C atoms by catalytic gas phase oxidation or ammoxidation of a reduced form of the desired compound in a multitube fixed-bed reactor charged with catalytically active multimetal oxides, in which the volume-specific activity of the catalytic charge decreases abruptly, successively, stepwise and/or continuously along the contact tubes in the flow direction.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
A process for the preparation of α,β-monoethylenically 
unsaturated aldehydes, carboxylic acids or 

nitriles having 3 to 5 carbon atoms by catalytic gas-phase 
oxidation or ammonoxidation of a reduced form of 

the desired α,β-monoethylenically unsaturated compound in 
a tube-bundle reactor charged with catalytically active 

multimetal oxides at elevated temperature and at a 
conversion of starting compounds for a single pass of 
>
 
90 mol%, wherein the volume-specific activity of the 

catalytic charge drops abruptly, successively and/or 
continuously along the contact tubes in the flow 

direction. 
</CLAIMS>
