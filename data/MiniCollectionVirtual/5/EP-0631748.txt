<INVENTION-TITLE>
Foldable seat for skiers.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47C900	A47C910	A63C1100	A63C1100	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47C	A47C	A63C	A63C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47C9	A47C9	A63C11	A63C11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a foldable seat for skiers, in which the ski 10 are inserted into the snow, one ski stick 2 is attached to the upper region of the skis 10 and the second ski stick 3 is attached to the binding, and a material web 1 is fastened to the upper and lower ski stick 2, 3. It is particularly advantageous that the height of the upper ski stick 2 and the length of the material web 1 are continuously adjustable. The fastening of the upper ski stick 2 is preferably effected using a mounting 4, 41 or 42 which is firmly clamped to the ski 10, while the length adjustment of the material web 1 is preferably effected by a Velcro closer 8. The material web 1 itself may at the same time also be designed as a bag for the mountings 4, 41.
