<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the thermoplastic processing of polyurethanes.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Polyurethanes having a melt viscosity which is substantially independent of the NCO:active H ratio which are built up essentially from   A) one or more polydiols having a mean molecular weight of from 550 to 10,000, B) one or more essentially difunctional chain extenders having a molecular weight of less than 500 and containing amino and/or hydroxyl groups as functional groups, and   C) one or more diisocyanates, are obtained if the ratio between the NCO groups of component C) and the Zerewitinoff-active groups of components A and B is at least 1:10, and the polyurethanes are subjected to heat treatment.
</ABSTRACT>
