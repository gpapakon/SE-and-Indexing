<INVENTION-TITLE>
Fail-safe analog interface.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G05B1905	G05B1905	G05F110	G05F110	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G05B	G05B	G05F	G05F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G05B19	G05B19	G05F1	G05F1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The interface includes a normal channel (11), a back-up channel (12) and a control channel (13). Dynamic comparators (20, 21) compare the consistency of the output signals (Sn) from the normal channel and of the output signals (Sc, Ss) from the back-up and control channels. In the absence of a fault, the dynamic comparators provide oscillatory signals (B1, B2). If one of the dynamic comparators detects an inconsistency or is itself defective, its output signal is continuous. The simultaneous presence of a continuous output signal from the two comparators leads to the switching of the workload over to the back-up channel.
