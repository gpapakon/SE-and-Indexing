<INVENTION-TITLE>
Multiaxial resonance magnetometer.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The magnetometer of the invention comprises means (40, 50, 60, Wx, Wy, Wz) for sequential application of polarisation fields of different directions. It then delivers (70) the components (Hmx, Hmy, Hmz) of the ambient field along these directions. Application in magnetometry.
