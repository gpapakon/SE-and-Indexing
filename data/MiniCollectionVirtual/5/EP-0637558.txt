<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method of handling products by means of oscillatory impulses and oscillating machine therefor.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65G2710	B65G2728	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65G	B65G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65G27	B65G27	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In a method for the treatment of products by exciting the product carrier (1) to vibrate, to achieve the object of excluding the negative influence of an elastic bending of a vibrating machine in the longitudinal processing direction and transversely thereto on the quality of the products to be processed, it is proposed, in the case of a product carrier (1) which is long in the treatment direction, and transversely thereto, to cause vibration exciters to act on this and to vary the vibration phase and/or vibration frequency of the individual vibration exciters (2, 7, 8) independently of one another. Furthermore, a vibrating machine for carrying out the method, with at least one vibration exciter for influencing products located on the vibrating machine, is proposed.
