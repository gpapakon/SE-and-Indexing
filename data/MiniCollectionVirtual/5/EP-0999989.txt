<INVENTION-TITLE>
INSTALLATION FOR CONVEYING OBJECTS BY AIR FLOW SUCH AS, IN PARTICULAR BOTTLES AND/OR FLASKS
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65G5100	B65G5103	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65G	B65G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65G51	B65G51	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention concerns an installation for conveying (1) objects (2) by air flow such as, in particular bottles and/or flasks, having at least one line for transferring (3) said objects (2), means (4) for loading said transfer line (3) and blowing means, for projecting an air flow capable of driving said objects (2), consisting of first blowing means (5), provided at least partially at said loading means (4). The invention is characterised in that the first blowing means (5) are removable relative to said loading means (4), independently of the transfer line (3).
</ABSTRACT>
