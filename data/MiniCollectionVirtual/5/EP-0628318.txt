<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for deodorizing animal excrement, deodorized animal excrement and their use.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a process for deodorising animal excrement, according to which the said excrement is brought into contact with an aqueous composition comprising hydrogen peroxide, at least one organic peroxyacid and at least one inorganic acid; deodorised animal excrement and its use.
</ABSTRACT>
