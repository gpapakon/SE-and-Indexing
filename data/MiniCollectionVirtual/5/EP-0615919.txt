<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a reclosable, cuboid container made of plastic film, in particular for receiving paper handkerchiefs, having a front, back, narrow sides, top and bottom, the top adjoining the back integrally and being pivotable in the direction of the front to close the package, and the top being connected to the sides in each case by side flaps which can be folded in. According to the invention, provision is made for the side flaps (18) to have in each case at least one predetermined or prefolded fold line (23, 25, 27) starting in each case from a corner point (20) between the back, top (16) and side.

<CLAIMS>
Reclosable pack (10) made from plastic foil, 
especially for receiving paper handkerchiefs, with a 

front wall (11), a rear wall (12) and with foldable side 
tabs (18, 19) which, for opening and closing, can be 

folded, the side tabs (18, 19) being subdivided into tab 
parts (29, 31; 30, 32) by folding along predetermined 

folding lines (27, 28), characterized in that strip-shaped 
regions of the tab parts (29, 31; 30, 32) on both 

sides of the folding lines (27, 28) rest with the same 
side of the plastic foil against one another and are 

connected permanently to one another by heat sealing or 
adhesive bonding. 
Pack according to Claim 1, characterized in that, 
to form the folding line (27, 28), strip-like regions of 

the plastic foil which are adjacent to the latter, with 
a width of up to 1 mm, preferably of approximately 

0.5 mm, are connected to one another. 
Pack according to Claim 1 or 2, characterized in 
that there is a cuboid shape with narrow side walls (13, 

14), top wall (16) and bottom wall (15), the top wall 
(16) adjoining the rear wall (12) in one piece and being 

foldable in the direction of the front wall (11) in order 
to close the pack (10), and, furthermore, the top wall 

(16) being connected to the side walls (13, 14) by means 
of the respective foldable side tabs (18, 19). 
Pack according to Claim 3, characterized in that 
the folding line (27, 28) divides the side tab (18, 19) 

into a tab part (29, 30) connected to the top wall (16) and 
a tab part (31, 32) connected to the side wall (13, 14).  

 
Pack according to Claim 3 or 4, characterized in 
that a predetermined folding line (23, 24) is at the same 

time a connecting line between the side wall (13, 14) and 
side tabs (18, 19). 
Pack according to Claim 3 or 4, characterized in 
that a predetermined folding line (25, 26) is at the same 

time a connecting line between the top wall (16) and side 
tabs (18, 19).
