<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a circular saw with a saw unit travelling under the table, a material-supporting table (10), and a mitre stop (20) which can be put into an operational position at the material-supporting table (10). In order to move the mitre stop (20) simply and quickly into an operational position from an inoperative position which does not disturb the normal working sequence, provision is made for the mitre stop (20) located in the area of a recess (18) in the material-supporting table (10) to be liftable and lowerable in this recess (18).
