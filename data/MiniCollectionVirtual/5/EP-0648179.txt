<INVENTION-TITLE>
BLANK AND PALLETIZABLE STRUCTURE FOR HOLDING AND GRIPPING CONTAINER LAYERS AND METHOD AND DEVICE FOR PACKAGING USING SUCH A BLANK.

<ABSTRACT>
Blank or sheet blank of cardboard or corrugated cardboard for packaging superimposed container layers, package obtained and method and device for packaging such a blank. The package (35) comprises two lateral T-shaped faces (41, 42) of a height substantially equal to the height of the layers, the lateral edges of the legs (11, 12) of the two lateral faces facing one another being mutually connected by two transversal walls (52, 53) perpendicular to the plane of the central legs.
</ABSTRACT>
