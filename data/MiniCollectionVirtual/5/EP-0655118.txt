<INVENTION-TITLE>
MANIPULATOR FOR THE INSPECTION OF DIFFICULTLY ACCESSIBLE LOCATIONS IN A STEAM GENERATOR.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F22B3700	F22B3700	G21C17017	G21C17017	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F22B	F22B	G21C	G21C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F22B37	F22B37	G21C17	G21C17	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Described is a manipulator with an inspection device (8) for inspecting the clamping strips (4) in the tube-turn region (6) of a steam generator (2). The manipulator (10), which is made up of several elements (12, 14, 16), has a pivoting arm (16) which is curved to match the curvature of the tubes in the tube-turn zone (6). The inspection device (8) is mounted at the free end of the arm (16) so that it can be extended in the longitidunal direction.
</ABSTRACT>
