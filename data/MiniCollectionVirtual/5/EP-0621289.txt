<INVENTION-TITLE>
Use of aqueous dispersions of vinyl ester copolymers as a glue.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08F212	C08F224	C08F21800	C08F21804	C09J508	C09J508	C09J13100	C09J13102	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08F	C08F	C08F	C08F	C09J	C09J	C09J	C09J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C08F2	C08F2	C08F218	C08F218	C09J5	C09J5	C09J131	C09J131	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the use of aqueous vinyl ester copolymer dispersions as adhesives which can easily be washed off during processing, but form water-resistant adhesive bonds, where the vinyl ester copolymer dispersions are prepared by the emulsion polymerisation method in the presence of from 1 to 10% by weight, based on the total weight of the comonomers, of thermally crosslinkable comonomers and from 1 to 15% by weight, based on the total weight of the comonomers, of water-soluble protective colloid.
</ABSTRACT>
