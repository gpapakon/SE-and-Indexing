<INVENTION-TITLE>
Use of synthetic huminates for the manufacture of a medicament for the treatment of disease of the mucosa.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Synthetic humates prepared by oxidation of polyhydric phenolic compounds are used for the manufacture of compositions with activity in the mucosal region. These compositions are used in human and veterinary medicine, in particular preferably in the region of the gastrointestinal mucosa, in the respiratory tract and in the genital region.
</ABSTRACT>
