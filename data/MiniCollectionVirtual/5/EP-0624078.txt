<INVENTION-TITLE>
DENTAL HANDPIECE.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61C100	A61C105	A61C108	A61C108	A61C118	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61C	A61C	A61C	A61C	A61C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61C1	A61C1	A61C1	A61C1	A61C1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In order to give rotational speed of preferably 180,000 to 200,000 rpm, the invention proposes that the air-powered turbine (5), which in prior art dental handpieces is fitted in the head (4) of the handpiece, be mounted in the handle (3) and a reduction gear (2, 2') used to enable the turbine to be run at lower speeds.
</ABSTRACT>
