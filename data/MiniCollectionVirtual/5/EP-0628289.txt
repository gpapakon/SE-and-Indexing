<INVENTION-TITLE>
Developable modular exernal fixator for immobilisation of a fracture site.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61B1760	A61B1764	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61B	A61B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61B17	A61B17	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The fixator comprises:   - standard jaws (3) designed to be fastened coaxially in various angular positions with a body (1, 20) and/or other jaws, - intermediate jaws (7) designed to be fastened coaxially in various angular positions with other jaws or a jaw and a body, - jaws (8) designed to be fastened with the body and/or other jaws in an orthogonal plane, - jaws (9), a part of which can be oriented for angular positioning of the anchoring pins,   - an extension body (20) which can be mounted between two jaws,   - an alignment module (23).
