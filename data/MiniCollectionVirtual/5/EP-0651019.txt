<INVENTION-TITLE>
Curable siloxane compositions and release coatings
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08L8300	C09J18307	C09J18300	C09J18305	C08L8305	C08L8307	C09J18300	C09D18304	C08L8304	C09D18304	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
Compositions comprising   (A) a polymeric organosilicon compound containing 1 to 6 Si-bonded hydrogen atoms per molecule, (B) a polymeric organosilicon compound containing at least two alkylene groups per molecule and having a maximum viscosity of 100 mm
<
2
>
/s, (C) a polymeric organosilicon compound containing on average at least 10 Si-bonded hydrogen atoms per molecule,   (D) a catalyst, and optionally   (E) an inhibitor,  with the proviso that   - the molar ratio between the total number of Si-bonded hydrogen atoms and the total number of SiC-bonded alkenyl groups in the composition is on average less than 1, - the composition contains (B) in such an amount that the composition contains on average at least 1.1 alkenyl radicals from (B) per Si-bonded hydrogen atom from (A), - and a proportion of Si-bonded hydrogen atoms from (C) is from 5 to 50 %, based on the total number of Si-bonded hydrogen atoms present.
</ABSTRACT>
