Security device with a locking mechanism for electrical sockets.

<ABSTRACT>
This standardised socket meets, from all points of view, the desired security requirements. It is constituted by a base 1, of a conventional internal system supplying the live, neutral and earth, with the special feature that when the plug is disconnected, a mask 3, under the effect of a torsion spring, pivots at a certain angle and is locked by studs 3a in housings provided for this effect in the mask, thus blocking off the 2 or more input holes for the current. Application in electrical equipment for domestic, industrial or related use.
</ABSTRACT>
