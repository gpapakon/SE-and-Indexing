<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for the immobilisation of nucleic acids.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
A method for the immobilisation of nucleic acids by hybridisation of the nucleic acid with a trapping probe, where the trapping probe is protected against enzymatic extension and/or enzymatic degradation of the hybrid formed.
</ABSTRACT>
