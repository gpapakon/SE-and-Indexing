Handcarts especially shopping carts

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a handcart (1), especially a shopping trolley, which can have a child's seat facility (21) and the underframe (2) of which, which is equipped with front and rear castors (3, 4), is formed from components, which can be put together, and supports at least one container (19) and/or at least one rack for holding goods, the underframe (2) having two connection joints (10) which are intended for receiving struts (6, 8) and are equipped with connecting points (13) for the struts (6, 8), and a respective strut (6) leading from each of the connecting points (13) to one of the front castors (3) and a respective strut (8) leading upwards to the container (19). The essence of the invention consists in the fact that a further connecting point (13) is provided on each connection joint (10) and that these connecting points (13) are intended for receiving a respective strut (7), the struts leading to the rear castors (4).

<CLAIMS>
A hand-operated trolley (1), in particular a shopping trolley, which 
can be provided with a child seat arrangement (21) and which 

has a wheel frame (2) provided with front and rear casters (3, 4) 
and formed from plug-in components and carrying at least one 

receptacle (19) and/or at least one shelf for holding goods, 
wherein the wheel frame (2) has two connecting nodes (10) for 

receiving front and upper longitudinal members (6, 8) and 
provided with connecting points (13) for the front and upper 

longitudinal members (6, 8) and wherein, from each connecting 
point (13), a front longitudinal member (6) leads to one of the front 

casters (3) and an upper longitudinal member (8) leads upwards 
to the receptacle (19), characterised in that a further connecting 

point (13) is provided on each connecting node (10) and these 
connecting points (13) are each intended to receive a rear 

longitudinal member (7) leading to the rear casters (4). 
A trolley according to claim 1, characterised in that the rear 
longitudinal members (7) end in two caster holders (18) carrying 

the rear casters (4). 
A trolley according to claim 1 or 2, characterised in that the front 
longitudinal members (6) end in two caster holders (11) carrying 

the front casters (3).  
 
A trolley according to any one of claims 1 to 3, characterised in 
that the front longitudinal members (6) are connected to form a U-shaped 

support. 
A trolley according to any one of claims 1 to 3, characterised in 
that the caster holders (11) are connected by a plug-in transverse 

connecting member (23). 
A trolley according to any one of claims 1 to 5, characterised in 
that the connecting points (13) on the connecting nodes (10) are 

formed in the manner of plugs. 
A trolley according to any one of claims 1 to 6, characterised in 
that the connecting points (13) on the connecting nodes (10) are 

formed by recesses. 
A trolley according to any one of claims 1 to 7, characterised in 
that the connecting points (13) are arranged either in a common 

plane (17) or in part in a common plane (17) or in different planes. 
A trolley according to any one of claims 1 to 8, characterised in 
that each connecting node (10) comprises two halves (10', 10'') 

and each half (10', 10'') has teeth (25) and, when the halves (10', 
10'') are assembled, the surfaces (24) thereof rest against one 

another and the teeth (25) mesh. 
A trolley according to any one of claims 1 to 9, characterised in 
that the connecting points (13) for the front and upper longitudinal 

members (6 and 8) are provided on one half (10' or 10'') and the  
 

connecting point (13) for a rear longitudinal member (7) is 
provided on the other half (10' or 10''). 
A trolley according to any one of claims 1 to 10, characterised in 
that the connecting nodes (10) have a recess (15) for receiving a 

transverse connecting member (22). 
A trolley according to any one of claims 1 to 11, characterised in 
that the connecting nodes (10) are connected by the transverse 

connecting member (22) so as to be secure against torsion.
