<INVENTION-TITLE>
Shopping or luggage trolley with lock.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B62B300	B62B300	B62B314	B62B314	B62B500	B62B500	G07F700	G07F706	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B62B	B62B	B62B	B62B	B62B	B62B	G07F	G07F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B62B3	B62B3	B62B3	B62B3	B62B5	B62B5	G07F7	G07F7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Shopping or luggage trolley with a lock which is fastened on the trolley and by means of which a trolley can be coupled to an adjacent trolley. The lock prevents the trolley from being detached from the other one for as long as the lock is in operation, it being possible to operate the lock, to unlock it and release the trolley, by means of a key which can be inserted into the lock.
</ABSTRACT>
