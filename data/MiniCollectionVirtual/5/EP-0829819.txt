<INVENTION-TITLE>
Multilayer substrate IC-card for contactless communication
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B29C4514	B29C4514	G06K19077	G06K19077	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B29C	B29C	G06K	G06K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B29C45	B29C45	G06K19	G06K19	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A process is claimed for manufacturing a multi-layer plastic chip card comprising a coil carrier layer(10A) with a coil and connections to a chip module sandwiched between a front cover layer(10B) with a cut-out for the chip module and a rear side stabilising layer. A prefabricated product(10) comprising the coil carrying layer(10A), coil and connectors and the front cover layer(10B) are placed in a cavity(33) of an injection mould(3) whose dimensions correspond to those of the card body(1) with the front cover layer(10B) lying flat against one cavity wall(31A) leaving the remaining volume of the cavity to be filled by injection of plastic melt to form the rear side layer.
 
</ABSTRACT>
