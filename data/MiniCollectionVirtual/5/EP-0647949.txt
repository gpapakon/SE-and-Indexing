<INVENTION-TITLE>
Remote control for a car radio.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H04B120	H04B120	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H04B	H04B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H04B1	H04B1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The installation options for a car radio in a motor vehicle do not always ensure unimpeded operation (control) of the unit. In some cases, it is therefore desirable to be able to control the most important unit functions via a wire-free remote control system whose remote control transmitter is fitted in the immediate area which the vehicle driver can access. The present invention discloses a car radio which also makes it possible to equip the unit with a remote controller retrospectively, with the aid of a remote control receiver which is installed in a removable control element, without the basic unit having to be provided with any additional circuit complexity.
