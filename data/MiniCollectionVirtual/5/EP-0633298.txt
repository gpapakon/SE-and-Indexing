<INVENTION-TITLE>
Powder mixtures for metallising substrates.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Novel spray powder formulations are described which, after application in the form of a thin coat to substrate surfaces by processes based on electrostatic attraction and baking of this coat by means of heat or high-energy radiation, enable the deposition of firmly adhering metal coats by currentless, wet-chemical metallisation. The essential components of such a formulation are a nonconducting material in powder form and (semi-)precious-metal compounds.
</ABSTRACT>
