<INVENTION-TITLE>
Vehicles for use both on rail and on road.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60F100	B60F100	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60F	B60F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60F1	B60F1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Road/rail vehicle (MF) for travelling on roads and rails by means of pneumatic tyres (R1-R4) to which guide devices (FE) which are mounted so as to be capable of being moved out of a position of rest into a working position are assigned, which guide devices (FE) have guide rollers (42, 43) which provide guidance when the vehicle is travelling on rails, are provided with rims, are constructed in the manner of a bogey and are mounted on the associated wheel axle (22) so as to be rotatable in each case about an axis (Z) which is approximately perpendicular to the rail line (SW) and penetrate the centre of the associated wheel axle (22).
