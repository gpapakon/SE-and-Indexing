<INVENTION-TITLE>
Liquid disinfectant composition and its uses.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A01N3716	A01N3716	A01N5900	A01N5900	A61K31185	A61K3119	A61L216	A61L216	A61L1100	A61L1100	A61P1700	A61P1700	C02F172	C02F172	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
A disinfectant liquid composition containing at least one disinfectant peroxy acid and at least one colouring agent, the said composition being stable for more than fifteen days. This composition can be used for disinfecting and colouring waters, an article or a surface.
 
</ABSTRACT>
