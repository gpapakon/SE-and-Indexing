<INVENTION-TITLE>
Instrument for length-measuring by visible laser beams without physical contact.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01B1102	G01B1102	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01B	G01B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01B11	G01B11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The instrument comprises an extensible device (1) which includes, at its upper end (2), an emitter of visible laser beams oriented perpendicular to the axis of the device, so that it is possible to measure, remotely, the height above the ground of an object (3) without it being necessary to have any physical contact with the latter.
