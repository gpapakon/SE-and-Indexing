<INVENTION-TITLE>
Motor car for the transport of passengers.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B61C500	B61C500	B61C1700	B61C1704	B61D300	B61D310	B61D1300	B61D1300	B61F300	B61F300	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
A motor car for the transport of passengers has at least one wagon body (1), a driver's cab (2), a drive assembly (3) and two sets of running gear (4, 5). In order to be able to join together many kinds of motor car with the smallest possible number of standard elements, the driver's cab (2), the drive assembly (3) and a drivable set of running gear (4) form an independent drive unit (A) which can be coupled to the wagon body (1).
