<INVENTION-TITLE>
Combined installation of a metal production unit and a separation unit of gases from air
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The combined installation comprises at least one metal production unit (II), comprising at least one, typically a series of, metal production or treatment devices (1-6), and at least one unit (III) for separating gas from air, comprising at least one outlet (14-18) for at least one gas in air, the units being fed with compressed air at low water vapour content by a common compressed-air production unit (I), at least one of the gas outlets (14-18) of the separation unit (III) being connected to at least one of the devices (1-6) of the production unit for supplying the latter with gas.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
Plant combining at least one unit for producing at 
least one metal (II) comprising at least one metal 

production or treatment device (1-6) having at least one 
air inlet and at least one inlet for gas from the air, at 

least one unit for separating gases from the air (III) 
comprising at least one air inlet (12) and at least one 

outlet (14-18) for at least one gas from the air, able to 
be connected to the said inlet of the device for gas from 

the air, and of a unit for producing compressed air (I) 
having a first outlet (21) connected to the air inlet of 

the separation unit (III) and at least one second outlet 
(22) connected to the air inlet of the device (1-6) for 

supplying the separation unit (III) of the said device (1-6) 

with air. 
Plant according to claim 1, characterized in that the 
unit for producing compressed air (I) has at least one 

downstream unit (20) for drying the compressed air. 
Plant according to claim 1 or claim 2, characterized in 
that the first compressed air outlet (21) is an outlet at a 

first pressure, the second compressed air outlet (22) being 
an outlet at a second pressure different from the first 

pressure. 
Plant according to one of claims 1 to 3, characterized 
in that the device is a device for sorting metals (7). 
Plant according to one of claims 1 to 3, characterized 
in that the device is a metal smelting furnace (1). 
Plant according to one of claims 1 to 3, characterized 
in that the device is a device for treating molten metal 

(2).  
 
Plant according to one of claims 1 to 3, characterized 
in that the device is a roll mill (5). 
Plant according to claim 7, characterized in that the 
device is a device for supplying the roll mill (5) with 

metal (3;4). 
Plant according to one of the preceding claims, 
characterized in that the device is a device for the 

reduction or pre-reduction of minerals (6). 
Plant according to one of claims 3,5,6,8,9, 
characterized in that the gas supplied to the device 

(1;2;4;6) is oxygen. 
Plant according to one of claims 3 and 6 to 8, 
characterized in that the gas supplied to the device (2-5) 

is nitrogen. 
Plant according to one of claims 3 and 6 to 8, 
characterized in that the gas supplied to the device 

(2,3,5) is argon. 
Plant according to one of the preceding claims 
characterized in that the device (1,6) and/or the unit for 

producing compressed air (I) comprises at least one cooling 
circuit (26,A;B;C) of which at least a part is associated 

functionally (27;13) with at least one fluid circuit 
(170;12) of the separation unit (III). 
Plant according to claim 13, characterized in that the 
cooling circuit (A;C) comprises at least one upstream part 

(26) in a heat exchange relationship (27) with a pipe for 
the gas (170) produced by the separation unit (III). 
Plant according to one of the preceding claims, 
characterized in that the separation unit (III) is a  

 
cryogenic unit (9) connected to the unit for producing 

compressed air (I) via a purification unit using an 
adsorbent (13). 
Plant according to one of claims 13 to 15, 
characterized in that the cooling circuit (A;B;C;) 

comprises a downstream part connected to the purification 
unit (13) for regenerating its adsorbent. 
Plant according to one of the preceding claims, 
characterized in that the metal is steel. 
Plant according to one of claims 1 to 16, 
characterized in that the metal is a non-ferrous metal. 
Plant according to claim 15, characterized in that the 
separation unit (III) is of the cryogenic type (9) with a 

medium pressure column (10) partially supplied with air 
boosted in pressure, expanded in a turbine (30). 
Plant according to claim 19, characterized in that it 
comprises a medium pressure compressed air line (34) 

leaving downstream from the turbine (30) to supply a user 
station. 
Plant according to one of the preceding claims, 
characterized in that the unit for producing compressed air 

(I) comprises a set of compressors (19). 
Plant according to claim 21, characterized in that at 
least a part of the set of compressors (19) is driven by a 

steam-actuated drive device. 
Plant according to claim 22, characterized in that it 
comprises a steam supply system (E) at least part of which 

is in a heat exchange relationship with the devices (1-6). 
</CLAIMS>
