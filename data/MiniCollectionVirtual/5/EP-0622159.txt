<INVENTION-TITLE>
Cutting device, especially for meat.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B26B2500	B26B2500	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B26B	B26B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B26B25	B26B25	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Cutting device, especially for meat, such as kebabs or the like, having a knife (10) and a handle (2), a disc-shaped knife (10) being provided which is fastened at its end face in a removable fashion to a rotatably mounted and driven shaft (3) and is surrounded by a protective casing (15, 16), a segment of the circle formed by the knife (10) projecting from the protective casing.
