<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for removing raised weld bead
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B21C3708	B21C3708	B23D7900	B23D7902	B23K3708	B23K3708	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B21C	B21C	B23D	B23D	B23K	B23K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B21C37	B21C37	B23D79	B23D79	B23K37	B23K37	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The apparatus serves for removal of a weld seam protrusion by means of a cutting tool while the workpiece (3) is being fed in the course of its manufacturing process. The apparatus is characterised by the presence of at least two cutting tools (4, 4a) which can be alternately brought into their respective working positions during the workpiece feed motion.
</ABSTRACT>
