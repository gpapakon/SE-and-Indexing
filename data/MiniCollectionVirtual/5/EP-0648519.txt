<INVENTION-TITLE>
Evaporator flask for a rotary evaporator.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B01D300	B01D308	B01L700	B01L702	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B01D	B01D	B01L	B01L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B01D3	B01D3	B01L7	B01L7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
An evaporator flask (5) for a rotary evaporator (1) comprises a cover part (5b) and a beaker part (5a). A connection element (12) is provided for their connection. A device (16, 17, 18) for mechanically influencing the material charged into the beaker part (5a) is arranged in the beaker part (5a).
