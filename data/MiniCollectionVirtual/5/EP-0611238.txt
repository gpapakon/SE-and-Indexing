<INVENTION-TITLE>
Automatic supplying device in particular for stackable cassettes or cartridges with interlocking means as well as a loading/unloading assembly incorporating such a device.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The subject of the present invention is an automatic supplying device, in particular for stackable cassettes or cartridges with interlocking and a loading/unloading assembly incorporating such a device. Device characterised in that it consists principally, on the one hand, of a magazine (2) for holding a sizeable stack of cassettes (3) interlocked in a pile, and on the other hand, of a mechanism (4) for extracting at least one cassette (3) around the lower part of the stack and, lastly, a means (6) for transporting the cassette(s) (3) extracted from the stack to the entrance of a processing machine (7), the said extraction mechanism (4) and transporting means (6) being actuated by drive means operated by a control module.
