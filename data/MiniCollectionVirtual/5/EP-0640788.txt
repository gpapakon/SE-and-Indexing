<INVENTION-TITLE>
Tracing cooling hose for beverage retailing conduits.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B67D100	B67D108	F16L5900	F16L59153	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B67D	B67D	F16L	F16L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B67D1	B67D1	F16L59	F16L59	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A tracing cooling hose (1) for draught beverage lines, comprising an internal hose bundle (2) and thermal insulation (5) of closed-pore cellular rubber (foam rubber) enclosing the hose bundle (2) and a plastic sheath (8) surrounding the thermal insulation (5). The thermal insulation (5) comprises two layers (6, 7) of cellular rubber tapes (strips) one on top of the other.
