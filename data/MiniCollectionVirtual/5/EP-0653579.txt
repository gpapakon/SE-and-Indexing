Torsional damper for crankshafts.

<ABSTRACT>
In previous torsional dampers (1) tuned at low frequency on crankshafts, acute durability problems occur at the elastomeric damper webs (12) when a reciprocating engine is started or stopped. Overloading of the damper webs (12) is avoided by the damper mass (second flywheel rim 6) being connected via a slip clutch (friction pair 15) to a rubber spring device (5) having damper webs (12).
