<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process and prepation of peroxydisulfuric and peroxymonosulfuric acids.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C25B100	C25B128	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C25B	C25B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C25B1	C25B1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Process for the preparation of peroxosulphuric acids from aqueous sulphuric acids in an electrolytic cell which is divided into two by a cationic exchanger membrane, by oxidation of the aqueous sulphuric acid on a smooth platinum anode in the presence of thiocyanates and/or rhodanic acid (thiocyanic acid, hydrogen thiocyanate) and/or dirhodan (cyanogen disulphide) and thioamides and/or thioaldehydes and/or thiocarboxylic acids and/or thiocarboxylic acid esters and/or thioalkanols and/or thioketals and/or dithiocompounds and/or nitrogen-containing heteroaromatic compounds, the peroxosulphuric acids being obtained with high current yields.
</ABSTRACT>
