<INVENTION-TITLE>
Exhaust gas recirculation device for an internal combustion engine.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F02M2507	F02M2507	F16L2700	F16L27108	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F02M	F02M	F16L	F16L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F02M25	F02M25	F16L27	F16L27	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Device for recirculating the exhaust gases of an internal combustion engine, of the type comprising a pipe (2) for recirculating the exhaust gases from the exhaust manifold to the inlet manifold (1), characterised in that the end adaptor part (12) of the recirculation pipe (2) is connected to the inlet manifold (1) via a flexible sleeve tube (3) made of a material with a low coefficient of thermal conduction.
