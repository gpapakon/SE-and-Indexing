<INVENTION-TITLE>
Electrical devices holder for bicycle.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B62J600	B62J606	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B62J	B62J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B62J6	B62J6	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a holder for the attachment of an electrical device, such as a headlamp, a dynamo, a battery or a rear light, to a two-wheeler, it being possible to attach releasably to the holder, one, two or more electrical devices, in each case by means of a positive latching connection.
