<ABSTRACT>
The invention concerns the use of cyclic metallic-organic bonds as components in co-ordination catalyst systems, corresponding co-ordination catalyst systems and a process for producing polymers by co-ordination polymerisation and unsaturated hydrocarbons by the catalysed metathesis of alkenes and alkines using such co-ordination catalyst systems.
</ABSTRACT>
