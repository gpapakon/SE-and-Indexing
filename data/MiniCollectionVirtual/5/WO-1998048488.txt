ELECTRIC CONNECTOR FOR HIGH FREQUENCIES

<ABSTRACT>
The invention concerns an electric connector for high frequencies comprising two elements (100, 200) adapted to be engaged by translation, each element (100, 200) comprising a body (110, 210) in electrically insulating material provided with an electromagnetic screen (130 230) bearing several electric contacts (120-127; 220-227). The invention is characterised in that each element screens (130, 230) comprise a cage-like portion (132, 232) provided with an internal spider (135, 235) defining receptacles each housing a contact pair (120-127; 220-227) and said screens (130, 230) forming in assembled position an electromagnetic joint face generally transverse to the engaging direction of the connector elements (100, 200).
</ABSTRACT>
