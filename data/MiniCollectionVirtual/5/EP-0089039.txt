<INVENTION-TITLE>
PROCESS FOR PRODUCING D-BETA-HYDROXYALKANOIC ACID
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C12P740	C12P742	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C12P	C12P	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C12P7	C12P7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A process for producing D-β-hydroxyalkanoic acids by 
utilizing microorganisms having an ability of stereospecific­

ally converting alkanoic acids, 2-alkenoic acids or alcohols 
into D-β-hydroxyalkanoic acids. 
</ABSTRACT>
