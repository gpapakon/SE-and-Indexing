Method and apparatus for determining cumulative pitch error of a gearwheel.

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for determining the cumulative pitch error of a gearwheel in a machine tool, in particular in a gear-cutting/honing machine. The cumulative pitch error of the workpiece must be determined before the honing operation and then applied as the influencing quantity in the rotary coupling of the workpiece axis and tool axis. According to the invention, a measuring operation internal to the machine is carried out before the honing treatment. In this case, via a rotation of the gearwheel to be processed pulse generators on the tool side and workpiece side supply pulses to the machine controller, from which the cumulative pitch error is determined. The latter is then not only applied as influencing quantity to the NC controller, but is also used to detect rejects and the end of the processing cycle.
