<ABSTRACT>
1. A ventilation grille for apparatus housings, comprising a plurality of parallel ribs (12) connected to each other, vent slots (22) being formed between the ribs, wherein said ribs (12) project from one side of a plate (10) which id integral with said ribs, characterized in that strips (26) of said plate (10) project from between the ribs (12) away from the side of the plate opposite to said ribs (12), thus forming said vent slots (22), and that said ventilation grille has a metallic surface on at least one side.
