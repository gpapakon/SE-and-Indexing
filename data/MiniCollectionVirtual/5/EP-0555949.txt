<INVENTION-TITLE>
Fastening device for mobile apparatus connector.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H01R13629	H01R13629	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H01R	H01R	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H01R13	H01R13	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
An attachment device is proposed for a plug connection, which has a plug and socket, for apparatuses for mobile use, the plug or socket being fixed elastically in an opening in a receptacle (20) by means of a bracket which has a centre plate and spring legs bent away from these [sic]. Each spring leg (5) has a barb (8) at its end and lugs arranged at a distance therefrom. Perforations (16) are provided in the receptacle (20), the number of which perforations (16) corresponds to the number of spring legs and the respective longitudinal axis (17) of which perforations is at a predetermined angle with respect to the longitudinal axis, seen in cross-section, of the barb (8).
