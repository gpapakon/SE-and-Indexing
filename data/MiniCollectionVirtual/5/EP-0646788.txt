<INVENTION-TITLE>
Electronic apparatus for detecting inflammable gases.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01N2714	G01N2716	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01N	G01N	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01N27	G01N27	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Apparatus for detecting inflammable gas, comprising a sensor (3) of the coil type, made using a platinum alloy wire, in which the said sensor (3) is connected to a constant-current supply (1) via a current regulator (2); the said regulator (2) being slaved to a unit (4) for controlling the excitation current of the sensor (3) and for D/A conversion of a correction signal generated by a microprocessor (6), which is connected to this unit and to which the output signal of the sensor (3) is transmitted after analog/digital conversion of this signal by means of an A/D converter (7).
