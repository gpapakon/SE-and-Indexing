<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device especially for working on channels.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B08B902	B08B904	E03F700	E03F712	F16L5526	F16L5526	F16L5528	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B08B9	B08B9	E03F7	E03F7	F16L55	F16L55	F16L55	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Device, in particular, for channel work, having a main appliance which can be moved in a main channel, and having a secondary appliance which can be introduced into a secondary channel, the main appliance (1) exhibiting a deflecting unit (8) which can be set in terms of its curvature in at least one plane and through which, or along which, the secondary appliance (4) can be introduced from the main appliance (1) into the secondary channel (3).
