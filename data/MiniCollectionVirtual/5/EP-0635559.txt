<INVENTION-TITLE>
Siloxane lubricating composition without hydrogen emissions.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60C100	B60C100	B60C500	B60C500	C10M10700	C10M10750	C10M17302	C10M17302	C10N3000	C10N4000	C10N5002	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60C	B60C	B60C	B60C	C10M	C10M	C10M	C10M	C10N	C10N	C10N	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60C1	B60C1	B60C5	B60C5	C10M107	C10M107	C10M173	C10M173	C10N30	C10N40	C10N50	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A siloxane-based lubricating composition contains a mixture of unreactive and reactive polydimethylsiloxanes, of crosslinker and of sufficient quantities of surfactant and of water to obtain an emulsion. The composition does not contain any methylhydrosiloxane or dimethylhydrosilane and/or polymethylhydrosiloxane and consequently does not release hydrogen while it is being stored or used.
</ABSTRACT>
