<INVENTION-TITLE>
Flame retardant pillow.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47C2700	A47C2700	A47G900	A47G900	A47G910	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47C	A47C	A47G	A47G	A47G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47C27	A47C27	A47G9	A47G9	A47G9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Described is a low flammability (flame retardant) pillow (1) comprising a cushioning core (2) and a cover (5) separable therefrom, wherein the cushioning core (2) comprises a binder-consolidated nonwoven-block from 5 to 15 cm in thickness and from 700 to 3000 g/m
<
2
>
 in basis weight comprising fibres or filaments having a linear density from 3 to 30 dtex and the cover (5) is a pocket-shaped, closable casing comprising a textile sheet material comprising or consisting of woven or knitted fabric, the fibres and filaments of the cushioning core (2) and of the casing (5) comprising flame resistant polyester.
