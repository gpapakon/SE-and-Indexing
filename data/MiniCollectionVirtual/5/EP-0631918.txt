<INVENTION-TITLE>
Body for railway goods wagon.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B61D300	B61D304	B61D320	B61D3900	B61D3900	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B61D	B61D	B61D	B61D	B61D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B61D3	B61D3	B61D3	B61D39	B61D39	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Body (11) for railway goods waggon, particularly suitable for transporting loading vessels or loading units (10) for high speed services, whose openable roof (4 and 5) which preferably has a plurality of roof segments (4, 5) accompanying the division of the waggon body (11) into sub-loading spaces (2, 3) is divided in its upper apex. The halves (4a; 5a) of the roof or roof segment are attached in a folding-down manner laterally by means of the bearing points (12) which can be coupled to the connection profiles (14) of the side wall (13). In the closed state, the halves (4a; 5a) of the roof or roof segment are connected in a positively engaging fashion to the side walls (13) and to one another. The waggon body (11) has vehicle-internal loading and unloading aids (6, 7).
</ABSTRACT>
