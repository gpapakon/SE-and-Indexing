<INVENTION-TITLE>
Probe for electrical measuring instrument, in particular a voltage tester.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01R1067	G01R1067	H01R1315	H01R1317	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01R	G01R	H01R	H01R	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01R1	G01R1	H01R13	H01R13	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The test probe for electrical measuring devices, in particular a voltmeter, is characterised by means for self-holding of the test probe in a plug socket receptacle of any desired kind, which means comprise a contact spring (2) partly pressing on a contact pin (1) and bulging out on both sides towards the tip of the contact spring. This produces a universally usable test probe which holds itself in any form of receptacle of the most varied of plug sockets, with protection against electric shock being guaranteed at all times.
