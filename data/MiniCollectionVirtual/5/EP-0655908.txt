<ABSTRACT>
Cosmetic filtering compositions containing at least one hydrophilic agent for filtering ultraviolet radiation, said agent comprising at least one sulphonic acid radical -SO3H and at least one aminofunctional silicone derivative. The compositions are useful for shielding skin and hair from ultraviolet radiation.
