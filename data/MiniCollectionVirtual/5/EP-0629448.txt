<INVENTION-TITLE>
Installation and method for milling and sifting brittle material.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B02C2100	B02C2100	B02C2300	B02C2314	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B02C	B02C	B02C	B02C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B02C21	B02C21	B02C23	B02C23	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a milling installation and method for milling and sifting brittle material, two sifters being used. The finer fraction of the first sifter is fed to a second sifter which separates it into coarse material and fine material. The coarse material passes into a fine mill and from there is conveyed back to the second sifter via a mechanically transporting conveyor device.
</ABSTRACT>
