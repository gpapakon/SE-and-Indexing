<INVENTION-TITLE>
Stabilised liquid mixtures for the labelling of nucleic acids.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to stable compositions in liquid form for enzymatic radioactive and non-radioactive labelling of nucleic acids and oligonucleotides. The mixtures preferably contain at least 30% (v/v) glycerol. The mixtures show no loss of activity on storage at a temperature between about -20 DEG C and +4 DEG C for at least 12 months.
</ABSTRACT>
