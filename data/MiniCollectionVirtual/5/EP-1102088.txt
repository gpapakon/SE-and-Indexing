<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method and device for determining the distance to an object using a pulsed electromagnetic signal
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01S728	G01S7282	G01S7285	G01S7288	G01S740	G01S740	G01S1300	G01S1310	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01S	G01S	G01S	G01S	G01S	G01S	G01S	G01S	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01S7	G01S7	G01S7	G01S7	G01S7	G01S7	G01S13	G01S13	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The method involves determining the signal transition time to the object and back by a comparison technique whereby the received signal is mixed with the delayed transmitted signal in a homodyne mixer. The transmitted and/or mixer output signals are modulated so the mixer output signal is raised to a higher frequency plane not equal to null. The mixer output can be mixed with a defined frequency signal and raised to the higher frequency plane. Independent claims are also included for the following: an arrangement for determining the distance to an object using a pulsed electromagnetic signal.
</ABSTRACT>
