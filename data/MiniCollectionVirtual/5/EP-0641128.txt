<INVENTION-TITLE>
Communication system for transmitting picture and sound signals.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H04N714	H04N714	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H04N	H04N	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H04N7	H04N7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A low-cost and flexible use of a communication system (CS) for the transmission of picture and sound signals is to be made possible. For this purpose, a number of access units (TAE1, ..., TAE8, QAE, KAE, CAE) is connected to a broadband cable (K) by means of direction-independent coupling means (AD1, ..., AD8) both for injecting and extracting picture and sound signals. Thus, a bidirectional picture and sound signal transmission can take place on the broadband cable (K).
