<INVENTION-TITLE>
Land mine clearing apparatus.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F41H1100	F41H1116	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F41H	F41H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F41H11	F41H11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In order to clear land mines on ground surfaces, it is provided for clearance units to be arranged with a vertical rotation axis. In this case, clearance elements are arranged over a hub such that they move in the underground level, approximately at right angles to the rotation axis.
