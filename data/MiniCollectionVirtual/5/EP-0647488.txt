<ABSTRACT>
To avoid space problems when using a blank loader in the insertion area of a press due to the movements of the transfer rails (2) in three axes, the length resulting from the length of the transfer movement (4) of the transfer rails (2) and the required length of the guide means on said transfer rails is included in the length of the press-side guides (13), on which those regions (18) of the transfer rails (2) which are remote from the driving region for the transfer movement (4) are mounted in the direction (4) of the transfer movement.
