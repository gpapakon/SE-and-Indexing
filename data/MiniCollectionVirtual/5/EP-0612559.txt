<CLAIMS>
Extrusion machine comprising a machine frame (1), 
an extrusion press (2) with a material inlet opening 

(21) and a material outlet opening (22), a drive (23) 
for the extrusion press (2) as well as a die (30) adjacent 

the material outlet opening (22) and a cutting 
tool (32) associated with said die (30),
characterized
 in
 
that a changer mechanism (3) is provided, which has a 

support member (34) for at least two changeable dies 
(30, 31) and/or cutting tools (32, 33), and 

that the the support member (34) is arranged for movement 
in a plane substantially parallel to the plane of 

the material outlet opening (22) relative to the extrusion 
press (2). 
Extrusion machine according to claim 1,
characterized
 in
 
that the support member (34) is circular and is 

mounted for rotation in a changer casing (35) fitted 
eccentrically to the extrusion press (2) adjacent its 

material outlet opening (22).  
 
Extrusion machine according to claim 1,
characterized
 in
 
that the support member (34') has a pitch circular 

construction and is pivotably mounted in a changer 
casing (35') eccentrically fitted to the extrusion 

press (2) adjacent its material outlet opening (22). 
Extrusion machine according to claim 2 or 3,
characterized
 in
 
that the dies (30, 31) and/or the cutting tools (32, 

33) have the same radial spacing from the rotation or 
pivot axis (36, 36') of the support member (34, 34'). 
Extrusion machine according to claim 1,
characterized
 in
 
that the support member (34'') is mounted for a substantially 

translatory displacement in a changer 
casing (35'') fitted to the extrusion press (2) adjacent 

its material outlet opening (22) and the respective 
arrangements (30', 31') formed by the die and/or 

cutting tool being juxtaposed in the translation 
direction (Z). 
Extrusion machine according to one of the preceding 
claims,
characterized
 in
 
that there are more than two replaceable dies (30, 31) 

and/or cutting tools (32, 33). 
Extrusion machine according to one of the preceding 
claims,
characterized
 in
 
that the drive for the movement of the support member 

(34; 34'; 34'') is formed by an electric motor (38).  
 
Extrusion machine according to one of the claims 1 
to 6,
characterized
 in
 
that the drive for the movement of the support member 

(34; 34'; 34'') is formed by a hydraulic member. 
Extrusion machine according to claim 7 or 8,
characterized
 in
 
that a drive chain (38') is provided between the motor 

(38) and the support member (34; 34'; 34''). 
Extrusion machine according to claim 7 or 8,
characterized
 in
 
that a worm wheel drive is provided between the motor 

(38) and the support member (34; 34'; 34''). 
Extrusion machine according to claim 7 or 8,
characterized
 in
 
that a gear drive is provided between the motor (38) 

and the support member (34; 34'). 
Extrusion machine according to claim 7 or 8,
characterized
 in
 
that a rack drive is provided between the motor (38) 

and the support member (34''). 
Extrusion machine according to one of the preceding 
claims,
characterized
 in
 
that the cutting tool (32, 33) is formed by a knife 

arrangement mounted for rotation on a side of the die 
(30, 31) remote from the extrusion press (2) and which 

is subject to the action of a cutting drive (37).  
 
Extrusion machine according to claim 13,
characterized
 in
 
that there is at least one deflection gear (37') in 

the drive line between the cutting drive (37) and the 
cutting tool (32, 33). 
Extrusion machine according to one of the preceding 
claims,
characterized
 in
 
that the cutting drive (37) is formed by an electric 

motor. 
Extrusion machine accroding to one of the claims 
1 to 4,
characterized
 in
 
that the cutting drive (37) is formed by a hydraulic 

motor. 
Extrusion machine according to one of the claims 
1 to 14,
characterized
 in
 
that the cutting drive (37) is formed by a pneumatic 

motor. 
Extrusion machine according to one of the preceding 
claims,
characterized
 in
 
that there is at least one differential shaft in the 

drive line between the cutting drive (37) and the 
cutting tool (32, 33).  

 
Extrusion machine according to one of the preceding 
claims,
characterized
 in
 
that the changer mechanism (3) is hingedly tiltable 

attached to the extrusion press (2). 
Extrusion machine according to one of the claims 1 
to 18,
characterized
 in
 
that the changer mechanism (3) is securely attached to 

the extrusion press (2). 
Extrusion machine according to one of the preceding 
claims,
characterized
 in
 
that heating and/or cooling arrangements are provided 

in the changer mechanism (3).
