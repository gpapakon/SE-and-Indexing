<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a current supply connector for planar fuel cells, which connector contains a cell-side ceramic plate, a joint layer made of a glass which is viscous and conducts electricity under operating conditions, and of a metallic current-collector plate, all three junction components exhibiting thermal expansion behaviour compatible with one another.
