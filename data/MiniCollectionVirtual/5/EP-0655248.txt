<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the use of 1-{4-[4-aryl(or heteroaryl)-1-piperazinyl]butyl}-1H-azole derivatives as well as their physiologically acceptable salts for the manufacture of medicaments intended for the treatment of disorders associated with gastric secretion.
