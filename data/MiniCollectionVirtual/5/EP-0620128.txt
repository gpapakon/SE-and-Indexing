<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a tray (1, 1') for art paper, paint pots, cosmetic containers, food, etc., on the underside of which an anti-slip covering is provided, a rotation/adhesion point (6, 6') projecting above the supporting surface of the tray (1, 1') being provided on the underside of the plate. As a result of this design, the tray (1, 1') can be rotated easily but displaced only with difficulty.
</ABSTRACT>
