The hoist has a support element in which is placed a linkage (12) on which an insertion pin (11) vertically flaps, with a stop (17) restricting the depth to which the pin is inserted. The stop is part of an intermediate layer near the linkage between the insertion-pin and support element in the form of a support arm (2). The stop is in the form of a sleeve (18) enclosing the pin.
