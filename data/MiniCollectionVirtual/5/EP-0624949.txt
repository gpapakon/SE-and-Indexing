<INVENTION-TITLE>
Apparatus comprising a circuit with a transfer function which is controllable by a command signal and approximating method
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Apparatus (20) comprising a circuit (26) which delivers an output signal (OUT) in response to an input signal (IN), means (C) for changing values (R) of the said response by at least one control signal (V), calculating means (24) for determining second response value/control signal value pairs (RA, VA), from an approximation of first response values/control signal values pairs (R1, V1) (R6, V6) originating from the circuit (26). The apparatus comprises:   - coding means (22) for determining at least one linear regression function (D) forming the approximation of a series of the said first pairs (R1, V1) (R6, V6) and for determining codes specific to the said function, - the calculating means (24) operating, on the basis of the said specific codes, a transcoding of predetermined response values (REQ) into control signal values coming under the said second pairs.   The coding means (22) can also be arranged outside the apparatus (20). Application to adjustments of appliances such as television, radio receiver, radio recorder or others. Application: television, radio receiver, radio recorder.
