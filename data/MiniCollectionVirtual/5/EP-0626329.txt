<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for separating containers having peripheral regions from a stack and for turning them onto a support surface.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65G3300	B65G3306	B65G5900	B65G5910	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65G	B65G	B65G	B65G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65G33	B65G33	B65G59	B65G59	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Device for separating containers having edge regions from a stack of containers and for overturning the containers onto a supporting-surface region, characterised in that the stack (1) is fed to the supporting-surface region with an essentially horizontal stack axis, the edge regions (13) of the containers (5) in front, by means of at least two oppositely rotating separating worms (2a, 2b) arranged on both sides of the stack and having a pitch engaging the edge regions of the containers and increasing in the direction of the supporting-surface region (7) and is overturned onto the supporting-surface region.
