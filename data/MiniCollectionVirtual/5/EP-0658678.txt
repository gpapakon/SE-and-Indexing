<INVENTION-TITLE>
Elements of protective windows.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The protective window element is arranged in a frame profile (5). A composite assembly (10, 20) with at least two glass panes (11, 12) is provided with a metal layer (3, 12) which extends over the entire surface area of the glass assembly. Provided on the outer border of the glass assembly are contacting elements (4, 24) which can be connected to contacting profile strips (7, 34).
