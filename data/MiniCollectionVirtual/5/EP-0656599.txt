<INVENTION-TITLE>
Transponder and data communication system.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01S1300	G01S1375	G01S1378	G06K700	G06K700	G06K1700	G06K1700	G06K1907	G06K1907	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01S	G01S	G01S	G06K	G06K	G06K	G06K	G06K	G06K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01S13	G01S13	G01S13	G06K7	G06K7	G06K17	G06K17	G06K19	G06K19	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A description is given of a transponder and of a data communication system having such a transponder, with which system an identification and/or individual addressability of the transponders which are present at the same time is ensured in a simple manner, even in cases of the simultaneous presence of a plurality of transponders within the field range of the reading device.
</ABSTRACT>
