<INVENTION-TITLE>
Security sheet containing protected security elements, and process for making the same.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>D21H2140	D21H2142	G07F708	G07F708	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>D21H	D21H	G07F	G07F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>D21H21	D21H21	G07F7	G07F7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Security sheet comprising at least one security element situated at a point of the surface (S) of the sheet, the security element (4) being coated with a protective layer (5). The security element 4 may comprise at least one metal surface reflecting or diffracting light, for example a hologram. Application to paper for banknotes.
