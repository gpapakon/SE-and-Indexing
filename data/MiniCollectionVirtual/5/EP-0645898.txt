System for contactless transmission of data or power.

<ABSTRACT>
The system according to the invention for the contactless transmission of data or power between two stations in each case with a transmission coil located in one station has a coil system, in which the coil is disposed around a cylindrical body, and an inner and an outer screen are provided which are connected to one another and to a terminal of the coil.
