<INVENTION-TITLE>
Protective device for vehicle transport wagon.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B61D300	B61D318	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B61D	B61D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B61D3	B61D3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a protective device for motor-vehicle transport rail wagons, the said wagons comprising a lower floor and an upper floor which are equipped with side members forming between them a longitudinal aperture with an upper pillar and a lower flat edge. The device according to the invention comprises an upper horizontal profile (3) fastened to the corresponding upper side member (1) and a lower horizontal profile (5) fastened at a distance to the flat edge (2) of the wagon, a plurality of translucid cellular panels (11) arranged end to end between the said upper (3) and lower (5) profiles, with the cells vertical, and vertical round iron bars (12) arranged in cells at least in the vicinity of the vertical ends of each panel (11), the ends of the said round iron bars (12) being retained in the said upper (3) and lower (5) profiles. The invention is used for motor-vehicle transport wagons.
