Process for the continuous production of a solid food matrix.

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a process for the continuous preparation of a solid food matrix having a moisture content between 35 and 75%, starting with a liquid mixture based on blood, lipid and water, in which the said mixture is homogenised and is heat-treated, and a source of proteins and/or of hydrocolloids is added thereto before or after the homogenization.
</ABSTRACT>
