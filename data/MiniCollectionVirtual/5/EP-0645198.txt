<INVENTION-TITLE>
APPARATUS AND METHOD FOR CLEANING KNIVES OR CUTLERY.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47L1514	A47L1516	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47L	A47L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device for cleaning cutlery, having a cleaning chamber and a rinsing device which acts in the cleaning chamber, the cleaning chamber being removable and being arranged on the cleaning device. Furthermore, the invention relates to a method for cleaning and disinfecting cutlery, both disinfection and cleaning taking place successively or in the reverse order in the same cleaning chamber.
