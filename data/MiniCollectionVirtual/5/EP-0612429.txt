<INVENTION-TITLE>
METHOD AND INSTALLATION FOR DIFFUSING MESSAGES ON A VIDEO SCREEN NETWORK.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G09F2700	G09F2700	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G09F	G09F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G09F27	G09F27	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
According to the method, the messages are distributed in each item, a number of screens is assigned to each item in accordance with the number of messages of said item with reference to a number of screens and to a total number of messages, and series of messages each containing a number of messages equal to the number of screens assigned to this item are sequentially displayed.
</ABSTRACT>
