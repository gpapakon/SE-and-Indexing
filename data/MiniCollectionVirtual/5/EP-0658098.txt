<ABSTRACT>
Use of hydrofluorocarbon-containing compounds of the formula (I) in cosmetic compositions: RF-(CH2)n-X-[C3H5(OH)]-Y-RH in which C3H5(OH) denotes the structures (Ia), (Ib), (Ic); RF is a perfluorinated C4-C20 alkyl radical or a mixture of perfluorinated, C4-C20 alkyl radicals; RH is a straight or branched C1-C22 alkyl radical or a mixture of straight or branched C1-C22 alkyl radicals or an aryl or aralkyl radical; n is 0-4; X and Y are S, (a) or (b). The invention also concerns several sulphoxide and sulphone-type compounds, and cosmetic compositions including the compounds of the invention.
</ABSTRACT>
