<INVENTION-TITLE>
Water-ski.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B63B3573	B63B3583	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
A water-ski is described which is formed by a skid (1) which is provided with a location means for the foot of the user. According to the invention, the skid (1), at its bottom water planing surface, is provided with at least one holding means (7) directed on one side and having a planing surface (11) for the propulsion of the skid (1) in one of the longitudinal directions and with a hollow space (8) in the other longitudinal direction.
