<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the preparation of polyisobutene succinic anhydrides.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C07C23100	C07C23102	C08F800	C08F846	C10L110	C10L12383	C10M13300	C10M13356	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C07C	C07C	C08F	C08F	C10L	C10L	C10M	C10M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C07C231	C07C231	C08F8	C08F8	C10L1	C10L1	C10M133	C10M133	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Preparation of polyisobutylsuccinic anhydrides from polyisobutene and maleic anhydride by reacting polyisobutene having a mean molecular weight MN of 600 - 5000 and a content of terminal double bonds of at least 50% with maleic anhydride in a purity of at least 99% in a maleic anhydride:polyisobutene molar ratio of 1.1:1 to 6:1 at 140 to 200 DEG C.
</ABSTRACT>
