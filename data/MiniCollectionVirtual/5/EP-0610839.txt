<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for supporting the body in an upside down position.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61H102	A61H102	A63B2100	A63B2116	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61H	A61H	A63B	A63B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61H1	A61H1	A63B21	A63B21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
2.1. The invention describes a device by which an individual is held in a position in which the shoulders are directed downwards and the legs are directed upwards. 2.2 The device is essentially formed by an annular strap which is preferably made of an elastic textile material. 2.3 "Upside down positions" have a vitalising effect on the entire body and are useful for stretching of the spine.
