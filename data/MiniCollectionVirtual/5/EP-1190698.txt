<ABSTRACT>
Hair washing composition comprises in a cosmetically acceptable medium, (1) particles essentially of aluminum oxide of number average size below 200nm; (2) one or more soluble or insoluble conditioners; and (3) at least one detergent. The composition does not simultaneously contain an anionic surfactant and an amphoteric or non-ionic surfactant Hair washing composition comprises in a cosmetically acceptable medium, (1) particles essentially of aluminum oxide of number average size below 200nm; (2) one or more soluble or insoluble conditioners selected from cationic surfactants, cationic polymers, silicones, vegetable oils, ceramides, anionic and/or amphoteric polymers; and (3) at least one detergent. The composition does not simultaneously contain an anionic surfactant and an amphoteric or non-ionic surfactant
</ABSTRACT>
