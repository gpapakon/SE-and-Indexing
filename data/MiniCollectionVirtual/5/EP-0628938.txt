<INVENTION-TITLE>
Telealarm system.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G08B2501	G08B2501	G08B2510	G08B2510	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G08B	G08B	G08B	G08B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G08B25	G08B25	G08B25	G08B25	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Remote alarm system, characterised in that it comprises individual short-range portable transmitters (1), which can be actuated manually so as to transmit a predetermined warning signal, and a multiplicity of fixed receiver devices arranged at appropriate locations in a site to be protected, these receiver devices being able to receive the warning signal transmitted by any one of the portable devices (1) within range, and to retransmit the warning thus detected to personnel for intervention. This system is more particularly intended for users of public transport and sites, such as the underground railway for example.
