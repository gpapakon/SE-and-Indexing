<INVENTION-TITLE>
Self-service sales cupboards.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
A self-service display arrangement comprising a perforated plate with individual receptacles carried by the latter, the individual receptacles expediently being made of transparent plastic and having a filling flap and a removing flap. The receptacles are equipped with hook attachments in the rear wall, by means of which they can be hung into the available holes. Also present is an upwardly extended piece having a transverse slot by means of which the receptacles can be suspended from conventional support rails or hangers. Receptacles are filled with sales units consisting, for example, of objects which are sealed in plastic bags.
