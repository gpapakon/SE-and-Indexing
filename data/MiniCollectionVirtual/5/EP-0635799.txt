<INVENTION-TITLE>
Laser printer.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B41J2435	B41J2465	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
For a laser printer using masks, there is proposed a storage device which is switchable in terms of rotational angle for a plurality of transmission or reflection masks. The storage device transports the masks exactly into the beam path between laser and printing substrate.
</ABSTRACT>
