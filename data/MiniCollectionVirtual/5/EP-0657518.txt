<INVENTION-TITLE>
Pressure-sensitive tear strip and its production process.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C09J702	C09J702	C09J704	C09J704	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C09J	C09J	C09J	C09J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C09J7	C09J7	C09J7	C09J7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a self-adhesive tear-off strip of a biaxially oriented support film or laminates made thereof, which are provided first in one operation with colour, a release system and layer of adhesive, which are applied in particular from an aqueous medium, which operation is followed by further operations such as cutting and/or winding into rolls.
</ABSTRACT>
