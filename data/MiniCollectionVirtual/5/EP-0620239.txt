<INVENTION-TITLE>
Epoxy modified alkyd resins, preparation process thereof and use thereof as water-thinnable binders.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08G1800	C08G1842	C08G6300	C08G6349	C08G6391	C09D16300	C09D16300	C09D16708	C09D16708	C09D17506	C09D17506	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The present application relates to water-emulsifiable alkyd resins which are obtainable by reacting an alkyd resin with an epoxide and which are optionally subsequently neutralised. The reaction with the epoxide is preferably carried out in the presence of a fatty acid.
</ABSTRACT>
