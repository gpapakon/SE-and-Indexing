<INVENTION-TITLE>
Ratio meter.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01R700	G01R706	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01R	G01R	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01R7	G01R7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Ratio meter including two coils (1, 2) for producing two magnetic fields respectively, in different directions (10, 11) and with different strengths depending on a signal to be displayed (22), and for making a display deflect as a function of the said signal (22), in which the coils (1, 2) are arranged in such a way that the said directions (10, 11) of the magnetic fields are inclined to one another. - Application to the speed display in a motor car.
