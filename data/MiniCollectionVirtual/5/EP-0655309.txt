<INVENTION-TITLE>
Mould closing unit for a plastic injection moulding machine.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B22D1726	B22D1726	B29C3322	B29C3324	B29C4567	B29C4567	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
In the case of a mould-closing unit for a plastics injection-moulding machine, a stationary platen 11 and a movable platen 12 are provided. A pressure cylinder 14 with pressure piston 14a serves for building up the locking force and at least one piston cylinder unit 15 serves for moving the movable platen 11. Drive piston or drive cylinder 15b and pressure cylinder 14 are connected to one another. The pressure cylinder forms, with the movable platen 12, a movement unit b, which is displaceable with respect to the stationary pressure piston. This makes possible a compact construction of the mould-closing unit which can nevertheless meet the requirements demanded of the movable platen (Fig. 2).
