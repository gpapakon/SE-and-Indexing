<INVENTION-TITLE>
Two strobe engine.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F01N320	F01N320	F02B3302	F02B3304	F02B7502	F02B7502	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F01N	F01N	F02B	F02B	F02B	F02B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F01N3	F01N3	F02B33	F02B33	F02B75	F02B75	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The two stroke engine (1) has a cylinder (3), which acts as the combustion chamber. The cylinder has an inlet port (4) and an exhaust port (7), for the evacuation of the burnt gas. The exhaust port is connected to the exhaust system by means of two separate exhaust channels (8 and 9). A distributor valve (10) controls and regulates the flow of exhaust gas through the two exhaust channels, according to the requirements of one or more of the engine operating parameters. The distributor is such that it is designed to direct the flow of exhaust gas towards one or other of the two channels.
</ABSTRACT>
