<ABSTRACT>
Preparation of multi-ring aromatic compounds comprises cross-coupling of aromatic boron compounds with aromatic halogen compounds or perfluoroalkyl sulphonates. The catalyst used is metallic palladium. At least one water-soluble complex ligand is added, and the reaction mixture contains enough water to form an aqueous phase.
