<INVENTION-TITLE>
Protective work garment.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A41D1301	A41D1301	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a protective work garment, namely a shirt or jacket, whose synthetic fabric is produced with a means which fulfils a particular protective function or is produced from fibres which fulfil the protective function (in particular whose synthetic fabric fibres are coloured with a brightly illuminating warning colour). The inner collar part (11) and/or the collar (12) on the outside and/or the inner cuffs (14) are produced from cotton fabric or a cotton blend fabric.
