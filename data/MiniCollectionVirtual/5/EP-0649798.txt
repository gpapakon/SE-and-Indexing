<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a flexible container, having at least one lifting loop, the casing of which container is gathered at two sections in the region of the upper edge of the container and these are joined together forming the lifting loop, characterised in that a loop part (7) is provided between the gathered sections (5, 6).
