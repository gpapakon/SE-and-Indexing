<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the production of fatty acid methyl esters from a natural oil or fat, fatty acid methyl esters so obtained, and their use.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C10L100	C10L102	C11C300	C11C304	C11C310	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C10L	C10L	C11C	C11C	C11C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C10L1	C10L1	C11C3	C11C3	C11C3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Process for the production of fatty acid methyl esters from a natural oil or fat by transesterification with methanol in the presence of a catalyst, in which a catalyst based on an organotitanate, in particular tetrabutyl orthotitanate, is used as catalyst, methyl esters so obtained and their use as fuel.
</ABSTRACT>
