<INVENTION-TITLE>
Elastic sliding-contact bearing.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F16F138	F16F138	F16L316	F16L318	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F16F	F16F	F16L	F16L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F16F1	F16F1	F16L3	F16L3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
An elastic sliding-contact bearing (1) has a central passage opening (6) for receiving a bearing pin (2) of circular cross-section. The inner surface of the passage opening (6) is formed from a sleeve (7) embedded in a spring element (5) of elastic material. The central passage opening (6) has a polygonal or star-shaped cross-section. The sleeve (7) is formed from a flexible material.
