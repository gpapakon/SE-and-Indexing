<INVENTION-TITLE>
Box for contaminated medical articles.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61B1900	A61B1902	A61M532	A61M532	B65F116	B65F116	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61B	A61B	A61M	A61M	B65F	B65F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61B19	A61B19	A61M5	A61M5	B65F1	B65F1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Box for small articles, in particular medical articles which may be contaminated, such as needles (81) or syringes (82), the said box consisting of a casing (20) and of a lid (10) which in each case comprise on their upper faces a partial opening (11, 21), the lid (10) being mounted rotatably on the casing (20) without changing level so that, as and when necessary, it can be moved alternately from an open position to a closed position either by arranging or not arranging the two openings (11, 21) in line with each other, and such that a partial opening (11) is equipped with a protective shaft (12) having a substantially vertical wall and with a tongue (13) having a slot (14) for facilitating the protected removal of the needle (81) from the syringe (82).
