<INVENTION-TITLE>
Composition for fire-proofing of fibrous materials.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Compositions comprising urea or a salt of urea, a guanidine phosphate, an ammonium polyphosphate and an alkali metal salt of an inorganic acid are suitable for the treatment of fibre materials. By applying such compositions in the form of aqueous solutions or dispersions to fibre materials such as, for example, fabrics, it is possible to bring about good properties of flame-resistance in the fibre materials. The flame-resistant properties are retained well after washing processes. The fibre materials give off little smoke when subjected to thermal stress.
</ABSTRACT>
