<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a preparation for destroying mites, characterised in that it contains at least one acaricidal active substance and can be used during washing operations.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
Preparation for killing mites during washing
operations by hand or in a washing machine,

characterized in that it is an acaricidal light-duty
detergent having the following composition:

water, dist. 10-70% by weight, fatty acids 5-25% by
weight, potassium hydroxide solution (45% strength)

1-20% by weight, fatty alcohols 1-20% by weight,
alkylbenzenesulfonates 5-25% by weight, ethanol 1-20%

by weight, ethylene diglycol 0.1-10% by weight, and
acaricidal active ingredient 1-20% by weight.
Preparation for killing mites during washing
operations by hand or in a washing machine,

characterized in that it is an acaricidal light-duty
detergent having the following composition:

water, dist. 20-70% by weight, fatty acids 1-20% by
weight, alkylbenzenesulfonates 2-30% by weight, ethanol

0-15% by weight, ethylene diglycol 0-10% by weight,
1,2-propylene glycol 0-10% by weight, acaricidal active

ingredient 1-20% by weight and optionally 0-30% by
weight of emulsifiers and 0-1% by weight of fatty acid

mono-, di- and polyalkylolamides.
Preparation for killing mites during washing
operations by hand or in a washing machine,

characterized in that it is a detergent concentrate
having the following composition:

water, dist. 0-20% by weight, emulsifiers 10-60% by
weight, lauryl ether sulfates or lauryl ether sulfate, 

Mg salt 10-50% by weight, and acaricidal active
ingredient 10-50% by weight.
Preparations according to one or more of the
preceding claims, characterized in that the acaricidal

active ingredients are benzyl alcohol and/or benzyl
benzoate.
Preparations according to one or more of the
preceding claims, characterized in that they are in the

form of a mixture with a commercially available
pulverulent or liquid detergent.
Use of the preparations according to Claims 1-4
for killing mites in washable materials during a

washing operation by hand or in a washing machine at
concentrations of the acaricidal active ingredient of

between 0.04 and 0.01%, based on the total volume of
the wash solution.
Use according to Claim 6, characterized in that
the washing operation can take place at temperatures

below 55Â°C.
Use of preparations according to Claim 5,
characterized in that the mites in textiles or bedlinen

and in articles with fillings, such as pillows or
duvets, are killed.
Use of preparations according to Claims 1-4,
characterized in that the mites are killed both in

synthetic and natural fibres during washing.
</CLAIMS>
