<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a gripping device, in particular for lift trucks with a vertically adjustable slide, for transporting barrels. The device has a gripper (16) which is displaceably arranged on a guide bar (18) pivotable about an axis (17). The gripper (16) can be locked for the transport of the barrels.
</ABSTRACT>
