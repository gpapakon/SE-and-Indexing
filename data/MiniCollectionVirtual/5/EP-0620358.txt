<ABSTRACT>
A hydraulic unit, especially a hydraulic motor or hydraulic pump, with a fixed housing, rotor and stator and bearing for the rotor opposite the stator is proposed, the components being rotatable about a geometric axis and fixed to one another in this axial direction. At the same time the hydraulic separating forces between cylinder block and control element (20) can be hydraulically compensated for. Hydraulically pressurised, annular surfaces (23a, 23b), arranged on that side facing the geometric axis, are provided on the control element, which in an axial direction define annular chambers which are formed between the control element and an adjacent counterpart (25). The said counterpart (25), held against the action of the separating forces, is thereby fixed on the rotor and rotates with the latter.
