<INVENTION-TITLE>
Catalytic fluorination of halogenated hydrocarbons in the vapour phase.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B01J2316	B01J2326	C07B6100	C07B6100	C07C1700	C07C1720	C07C1721	C07C1900	C07C1908	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the catalytic fluorination of halogenated hydrocarbons in the gas phase by means of hydrofluoric acid. Use is made of a mixed catalyst based on chromium and vanadium derivatives, these two elements being mainly in the III oxidation state.
</ABSTRACT>
