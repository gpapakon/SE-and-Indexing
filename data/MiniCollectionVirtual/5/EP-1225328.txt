<INVENTION-TITLE>
High pressure fuel system for combustion engines
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F02M5500	F02M5500	F02M6100	F02M6116	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F02M	F02M	F02M	F02M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The high pressure fuel system, for an internal combustion motor, has at least two high pressure bodies (1,3) pressed together at their facing surfaces (10,12). A high pressure fuel channel (17) at the bodies, passes between their facing surfaces. One surface carries a ring step (5) around the fuel passage, with a sealing surface (15) at the end side at the other surface.
