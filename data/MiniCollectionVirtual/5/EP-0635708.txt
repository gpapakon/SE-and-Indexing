<INVENTION-TITLE>
Apparatus for ultrasonic-controlled tightening of a piece and signal sampling.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01L524	G01L524	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01L	G01L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01L5	G01L5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
This apparatus for inspecting the tightening of a part using ultrasound, comprises an emitter (2) and a receiver (3) of ultrasound signals and means (14) for analysing the signals sampled by a parallel digitiser (12) in order to act as a basis for calculating the tightening value of the part; a delay generator (7) is arranged between the emission assembly (4), for introducing a time shift for each repetition of the sampling.
