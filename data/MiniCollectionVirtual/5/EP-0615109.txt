<INVENTION-TITLE>
Armoured vehicle with a movable protection roof.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F41H500	F41H516	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F41H	F41H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F41H5	F41H5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
An armoured combat vehicle, especially a battle tank, having a protection roof (11), which is arranged on the top of the vehicle and covers a predetermined subsection of the vehicle surface, for intercepting approaching shaped-charge missiles. The protection roof (11) can be moved from a locked rest position in which it rests on a first predetermined subsection of the vehicle surface, for example the rear of the turret, of a battle tank, into a lockable protection position in which it is arranged over a second predetermined subsection of the vehicle surface to be protected, for example the hatch region, and at a predetermined distance from this region.
