<INVENTION-TITLE>
Garden awning with canvas with two slopes to be rolled up.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E04H1532	E04H1558	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E04H	E04H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>E04H15	E04H15	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The lightweight shelter (awning) is of the type comprising a fixed structure and moving elements so that it exhibits two divergent and inclined canvas panels. It is characterised in that two arms (11-13 and 10-12) of one and the same pair are articulated to a component (31-32) which is mounted so that it can slide longitudinally relative to a mast (1-2), mechanical means (51 to 53, 61 to 66) connected kinematically to the moving elements being provided in order to move the sliding components (31 and 32) relative to the masts (poles) (1 and 2) in order to alter the inclination of the arms (11-13 and 10-12) and the deployed length of the canvas panels (6 and 7).
