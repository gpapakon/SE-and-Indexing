Cutting press
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Cutting press has a device for moving a strip across the direction of rotation. Cutting press has a press table (1) with a strip (2) rotated by two rollers (3, 4) arranged on both sides of the press. The foil (5) lies on the strip to feed sections by means of a cutting tool in the region of the table. The press has a device for moving strip across the direction of rotation.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
Blanking press for cutting workpieces out of a sheet (5), with a press bed (1) with a
belt (2) which is led across the press bed (1) and led in circulation around two return

pulleys (3, 4) arranged one on either side of the press bed (1) and on which the sheet
(5) is laid for the production of the cutouts by means of a blanking die (9) in the region of

the press bed (1), the blanking press having means for shifting the belt (2) at right
angles to its direction of circulation, 
characterized in that
 the means for shifting the belt
(2) are formed by a vertical positioner (12) of one bearing (11) of one return pulley (4).
Blanking press according to Claim 1, 
characterized in that
 the vertical positioner
(12) has a wedge (13) which is positionable by a spindle (14) and on which a bearing

(11) of the return pulley (4) is supported.
Blanking press according to either of the preceding claims, 
characterized in that
 the
belt (2) is made of steel.
Blanking press according to any one of the preceding claims, 
characterized in that

the belt (2) is provided with a belt chilling device (16) to cool it to below the dew point of
the ambient air.
Blanking press according to Claim 4, 
characterized in that
 the belt chilling device
(60) is provided inside one of the return pulleys (3, 4).
</CLAIMS>
