<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for preparing polymers of esters of vinyl phosphonic acid.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08F204	C08F210	C08F400	C08F430	C08F3000	C08F3002	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08F	C08F	C08F	C08F	C08F	C08F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C08F2	C08F2	C08F4	C08F4	C08F30	C08F30	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A monomer mixture principally comprising vinyl phosphonic acid esters of methanol and/or ethanol is polymerised by means of free radicals in aqueous solution at a polymerisation temperature of from 80 to 150 DEG C using a salt of peroxodisulphuric acid as polymerisation initiator.
</ABSTRACT>
