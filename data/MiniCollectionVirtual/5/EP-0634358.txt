<ABSTRACT>
Intermetallic cpd. has compsn. of Zr1-yTiyCo (where Y = 0.1-1). Pref. Y = 0.2-0.3. Prepn., the cpd. is prepd. by coarsely mixing the powdered components and fusing in a vacuum or in noble gas, To improve homogenisation, the solidified intermetallic cpd. is crushed and fused again. This process is opt. repeated many times.
