<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method and apparatus for methanol production.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C01B300	C01B312	C01B348	C07C2900	C07C29151	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C01B	C01B	C01B	C07C	C07C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C01B3	C01B3	C01B3	C07C29	C07C29	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a method of producing methanol by obtaining synthesis gas in a gasifier, preferably in a slag bath gasifier or a cyclone gasifier, wherein the starting material used in the gasifier is preferably wastes or materials containing wastes, reaction of the synthesis gas with steam in a high-temperature conversion, removal of the sulphur compounds and CO2, preferably in separate purification steps, and production of methanol from the purified synthesis gas in a methanol plant with intermediate takeoff of methanol.
