<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method and apparatus for changing into sounds the vibrations of strings of a musical instrument.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G10H300	G10H318	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G10H	G10H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G10H3	G10H3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The method consists in picking up, by use of sensors (CA), the vibrations on the strings of a musical instrument, for example a piano, or on an element (CE) driven in vibration by these strings by mechanical contact with them, and in generating sound signals from the vibrations thus picked up with the aid of loudspeakers (HP).
