<INVENTION-TITLE>
Injection grout for the consolidation and sealing of soils.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C04B2800	C04B2826	C09K1702	C09K1702	C09K1708	C09K1712	C09K10300	C09K10700	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C04B	C04B	C09K	C09K	C09K	C09K	C09K	C09K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C04B28	C04B28	C09K17	C09K17	C09K17	C09K17	C09K103	C09K107	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Grout, characterised in that it is capable of being obtained by bringing into contact a solution of alkali metal silicate, a solution of alkali metal polyphosphate and a solution of alkali metal aluminate. Process for the preparation of this grout, it being possible for this process to be used for sealing and/or consolidating grounds and/or materials of construction, by injection.
</ABSTRACT>
