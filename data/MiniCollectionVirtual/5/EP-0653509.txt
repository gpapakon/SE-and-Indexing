<INVENTION-TITLE>
Dancer for controlling rotational speed.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65H2304	B65H2304	D06B300	D06B334	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65H	B65H	D06B	D06B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65H23	B65H23	D06B3	D06B3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
2.1 To control the rotational speed of a plurality of deflecting rollers, arranged at a distance one behind and one above the other, for the guidance of a textile sliver running in the manner of a loop over the deflecting rollers, and to control the rotational speed of a downstream pair of pinching rollers for the textile sliver, spring-loaded dancers are used for compensating the change in length of the sliver during treatment at the inlet. 2.2 In order to increase the run-through capacity, it is proposed to arrange the dancer for controlling rotational speed approximately in the middle between the deflecting rollers in the run-through direction of the sliver.
