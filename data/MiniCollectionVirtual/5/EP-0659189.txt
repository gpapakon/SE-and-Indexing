<INVENTION-TITLE>
PHARMACEUTICAL COMPOSITIONS CONTAINING ASSOCIATIONS OF METALS AND TANNINS OR TANNIN MIXTURES.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C07F900	C07F900	C07F1900	C07F1900	C07H1300	C07H1308	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C07F	C07F	C07F	C07F	C07H	C07H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C07F9	C07F9	C07F19	C07F19	C07H13	C07H13	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Novel pharmaceutical compositions contain as active principle an association of a tannin or tannin mixture and a metal derivative having a degree of oxidation of at least 3, said metal being selected from: V, Nb, Cr, Mo, W, Mn, Co, Ru, Pd, Pt, Au, Ga, Ge, Sn, Sb and Se, said active principle being incorporated in a pharmaceutically acceptable excipient vehicle or support. Specifically disclosed are compositions having hypoglycemic activity where the metal is vanadium. A method for increasing the bioavailability of the above metals is also disclosed.
</ABSTRACT>
