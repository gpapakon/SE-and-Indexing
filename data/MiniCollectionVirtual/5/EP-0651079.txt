<INVENTION-TITLE>
Spinning or twisting machine.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>D01H100	D01H116	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>D01H	D01H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>D01H1	D01H1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a spinning or twisting machine, particularly a ring-spinning machine, consisting of identical machine portions provided with work stations and each covering some of the length of the machine, machine portions being limited by stand walls (3, 4) supporting longitudinal structural parts (5) and extending transversely relative to the longitudinal direction of the machine. The machine portions (1) consist alternately of complete full sections (2) provided with two end stand walls (3, 4) and of intermediate sections (6) having only the longitudinal structural parts (5) and mounted in the adjacent stand walls (3, 4) of the complete full sections (2).
