<INVENTION-TITLE>
BRAKE DISC.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F16D6512	F16D6512	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F16D	F16D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F16D65	F16D65	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a brake disc, especially a brake disc for rail vehicles, with a hub section (1) and a disc section (2) releasably secured to the hub section (1), in which the hub section (1) and the disc section (2) are a positive fit together, there is a slight clearance between the two sections (1, 2), at least in the radial direction, and between the hub section (1) and the disc section (2) there are several at least radially operative pressure spring components (8) distributed over the periphery. This ensures that the disc section (2) can expand radially under the effect of temperature independently of the hub section (1).
</ABSTRACT>
