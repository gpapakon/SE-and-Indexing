<INVENTION-TITLE>
Plate cylinder for a printing machine
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B41F1308	B41C100	B41N600	B41N600	B41F1310	B41C118	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B41F	B41C	B41N	B41N	B41F	B41C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
In a plate cylinder for a printing machine having a printing plate which can be mounted on its outer surface, the surface of the plate cylinder and/or the underside of the printing plate is coated with a sliding material which forms a friction-reducing layer between the printing plate and the plate cylinder.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
Plate cylinder for a printing press with a printing 
plate which can be tensioned on its cover surface, 

which has a layer arranged on its rear side, 
characterized in that the surface of the plate 

cylinder and the underside of the printing plate are 
coated with a lubricant material. 
Plate cylinder according to Claim 1, characterised 
in that the layer thickness of the lubricant 

material amounts to ca. 10 Âµm. 
Plate cylinder according to one of Claims 1 or 2, 
characterized in that the lubricant material can be 

baked in to the surface of the plate cylinder or the 
underside of the printing plate. 
Plate cylinder according to one of Claims 1 to 3, 
characterized in that the lubricant material is 

connected via a carrier layer with the plate 
cylinder or the printing plate. 
</CLAIMS>
