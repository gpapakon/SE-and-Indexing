<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to an enclosure formed by cellular panels, the method of manufacture and the use of such an enclosure. The enclosure formed by cellular panels (1, 4), two ends of which are welded together, is characterised in that the weld line (9) is parallel to the direction of the braces (spacers) (12, 22, 32, 42) and at least four folding lines (5, 6, 7, 8) forming a hinge are provided in order to constitute a deformable parallelogram.
