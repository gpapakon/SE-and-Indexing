<INVENTION-TITLE>
Improvements to damping means of cross coil type indicating instrument.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01D1100	G01D1112	G01R700	G01R706	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01D	G01D	G01R	G01R	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01D11	G01D11	G01R7	G01R7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a ratio meter (crossed-coil instrument), especially for the dashboard of a motor vehicle, of the type comprising a shaft (16) which is guided in rotation, a magnet (18) solidly attached to the shaft, a chamber (13) containing a damping liquid, which also accommodates the magnet (18), characterised in that the damping liquid is formed by ferro-fluid (20).
