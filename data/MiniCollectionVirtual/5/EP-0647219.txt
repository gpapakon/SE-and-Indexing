<ABSTRACT>
The invention concerns vitamin D derivatives, modified at the 20 position, of general formula (I), in which L, X, Y, Z, R1, R2 and R3 are as defined in the description, a method of preparing them and their use as agents in the treatment of hyperproliferative skin diseases.
