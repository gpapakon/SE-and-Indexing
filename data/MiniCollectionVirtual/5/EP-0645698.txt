<INVENTION-TITLE>
Circuitry for generating a quotient.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The division circuit according to the invention is particularly suitable for binary values, and includes a multiplier, an adder, a subtractor and an integrator, which form a closed signal loop. The input signals are fed to a second input of the multiplier and the adder respectively, and the output signal is derived at the output of the integrator. Such a circuit can be implemented particularly favourably using a digital signal processor, and has a good transient response for specified wide value ranges of the input signal, so that it is also suitable for time-variable input signals.
