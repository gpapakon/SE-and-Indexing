<INVENTION-TITLE>
Exhaust device for a barrel vault.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E04D1303	E04D1303	E04D13035	F24F702	F24F702	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E04D	E04D	E04D	F24F	F24F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>E04D13	E04D13	E04D13	F24F7	F24F7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The exhaust (vent) device (2) for a barrel vault (1) includes a support (4) whose profile has a radius of curvature substantially equal to the radius of curvature of the barrel vault (1). The support contains two openings (5, 6) and two flaps (leaves, wings) (10) are fixed to the support (4) and are designed to close off the openings (5, 6) when shut. Used in particular for polyester barrel vaults.
