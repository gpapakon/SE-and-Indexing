<INVENTION-TITLE>
Mortar spreader.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B05C1702	B05C17035	E04G2120	E04G2120	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B05C	B05C	E04G	E04G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
A mortar spreader comprises a supply container (10) which is provided with an outlet opening (16). Arranged beneath the outlet opening (16) is a roller (18) on which spacer elements (22) are provided. Furthermore, the spreader exhibits a grip part (17) and an actuating lever (26) for opening the outlet opening (16).
