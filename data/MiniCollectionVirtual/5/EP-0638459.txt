<INVENTION-TITLE>
Armrest for automotive vehicles
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60R712	B60R700	B60N246	B60N246	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60R	B60R	B60N	B60N	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60R7	B60R7	B60N2	B60N2	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
An elongated armrest for a seat in the passenger compartment of a motor vehicle, which armrest is mounted with its free end side pointing in the direction of travel, is equipped with an inner reinforcement element which extends in the longitudinal direction and is covered with upholstery material and at whose, with respect to the direction of travel, rear end the mounting means engage is characterised in that an elongated receptacle container (5) is provided for a collapsed pocket umbrella (11) which is accommodated in the interior in the longitudinal direction, is covered with upholstery material and to which access can be gained through an opening (7), provided in the end side (6), for the umbrella.

<CLAIMS>
Elongate armrest (80), for a seat in the interior 
of a motor vehicle, 


which has a front, free end side (84) and 
is equipped with an inner stiffening body (81) which 
extends in the longitudinal direction of the armrest 

(80), is encased by padding material and has the 
installation means acting at its rear end, 
there being provided an elongate receiving container 
(82) tor a collapsed telescopic umbrella (83) with a 

handle (87), said receiving container being accommodated 
in the longitudinal direction of the armrest (80), in the 

interior thereof, being encased by the padding material 
and being accessible through in each case one opening 

(85), provided in the front end side (84), for the 
telescopic umbrella (83),
 
characterized in that 
the opening (85) has an internal thread (90) into which 
it is possible to screw an external thread (91) of the 

handle (87) of the telescopic umbrella (83), said handle 
being designed as a closure. 
Armrest according to Claim 1, characterized in 
that the receiving container (82) is arranged beside the 

stiffening body (81). 
Armrest according to Claim 2, characterized in 
that the receiving container (82) is connected to the 

stiffening body (81) and is stiffened by the latter. 
Armrest according to Claim 1, characterized in 
that the receiving container (82) is formed by the 

stiffening body (81). 
Armrest according to one of the preceding claims, 
characterized in that there is provided, for the opening 

(85), a closure which is arranged in the end side (84) 
and can be opened and closed by hand. 
</CLAIMS>
