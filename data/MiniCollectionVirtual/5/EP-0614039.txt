<INVENTION-TITLE>
Procedure and installation to deliver gas into a customer unit on a customer place.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F17C1300	F17C1312	F17D100	F17D102	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F17C	F17C	F17D	F17D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F17C13	F17C13	F17D1	F17D1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Away from the building (A) containing the user station (Pi), a safety building (B) is made which contains at least one source (2; 7; 10) of active gas, for example an apparatus (2) for in situ production of this active gas, advantageously associated with a purification apparatus (4), for supplying the active gas, optionally mixed in a controlled manner (at 6) with a carrier gas coming from a pure source (1) via a high-purity safety pipeline (C), to the user station (Pi). Application in particular to the fabrication of electronic components.
</ABSTRACT>
