<INVENTION-TITLE>
Vehicle bodies.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B61D1704	B61D1704	B62D2700	B62D2706	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B61D	B61D	B62D	B62D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B61D17	B61D17	B62D27	B62D27	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Vehicle bodies for rail vehicles and road vehicles, containing frame components and external panelling components over or between frame components, the external panelling components being connected to one another and/or to the frame components using detachable fastening means and the detachable fastening means constituting touch-and-close fasteners.
</ABSTRACT>
