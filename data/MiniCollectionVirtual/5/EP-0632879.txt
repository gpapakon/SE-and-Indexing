<ABSTRACT>
The device is used to control the flight behaviour of a vehicle rocket having both a drive and a payload. The vehicle rocket has at least one control and at least one steering component to alter a flight path transversely to the rocket longitudinal axis. The steering component takes the form of a pivoted guide wing arranged in a side region of the vehicle rocket. Connected to the guide wing is an adjuster which is set by the control in such a way that measurements made by sensors of the side-wind effect are converted into positions of the guide wing to offset transverse forces.
</ABSTRACT>
