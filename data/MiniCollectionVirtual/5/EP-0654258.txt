<INVENTION-TITLE>
Container with a thermometer.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61J900	A61J902	G01K500	G01K564	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61J	A61J	G01K	G01K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61J9	A61J9	G01K5	G01K5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In a container equipped with an analog thermometer with a bimetallic or multi-metal strip, one end (3) of the spiral (4) is integral with a base (2) held in close thermal contact with the outer wall (1) of the container or its contents, while the other end has a pointer. A casing (2, 6) has at least one transparent zone and encloses this spiral (4).
