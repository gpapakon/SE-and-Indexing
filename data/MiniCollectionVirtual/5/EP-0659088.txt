<INVENTION-TITLE>
WOUND SEALING CHITOSANE FOIL AND PROCESS FOR PRODUCING THE SAME.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61L1516	A61L1528	A61L1544	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61L	A61L	A61L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61L15	A61L15	A61L15	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A wound sealing chitosane foil contains glycerine as softening additive and has a gas exchange promoting perforated surface. A process for producing the chitosane foil is also disclosed.
