<INVENTION-TITLE>
Sulphuric acid-urea composition resulting from the reaction of urea with a sulphuric acid sludge of petroleum origin, method of preparation and use as a fertiliser source.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C05C900	C05C900	C07C27300	C07C27302	C07C27500	C07C27502	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C05C	C05C	C07C	C07C	C07C	C07C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C05C9	C05C9	C07C273	C07C273	C07C275	C07C275	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Sulphuric acid/urea composition formed by the product resulting from the action of urea on a sludge of petroleum origin containing, by weight, from 50 to 99% of sulphuric acid and from 1 to 20% of organic residues. The acidic sludge can contain water and often arises from an alkylation reaction. This composition can be used as a fertiliser source or as a component of a fertilising formulation.
