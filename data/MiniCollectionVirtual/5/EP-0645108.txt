<INVENTION-TITLE>
Sitting and/or sleeping furniture.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47C1700	A47C1702	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47C	A47C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47C17	A47C17	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The sitting and/or sleeping furniture, in particular sofa and upholstered bed, has a back (3) which is attached to the underframe (2) and which comprises a substructure of upright wooden or laminate strips (5) held at a distance one beside the other. The strips (5) are fastened by way of their lower end region to the underframe (1, 2) by nails or staples or by plug-in connection (7, 8). The strips (5) are interconnected in the upper strip region by at least one tension webbing (6) made of flexible and/or elastic material, such as textile, rubber- or plastics-impregnated textile, plastics, or the like.
