<ABSTRACT>
In a deodorising filter for insertion into a sewer shaft, a filter housing (1) has a filter space (19) in which is arranged a filter material (16). Through the filter housing (1) passes a descender pipe (13) which serves to drain water which is trapped by a collecting funnel (4) beneath a sand trap (6) or the like. In the filter housing (1) or in the filter space (19) is arranged a support framework for the filter material (16).
