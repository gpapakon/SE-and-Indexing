<INVENTION-TITLE>
Sprayable road binder consisting of gelled bitumen emulsion.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08L9500	C08L9500	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08L	C08L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C08L95	C08L95	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Bitumen emulsion for spraying, comprising a continuous aqueous phase, a bitumen dispersion and an emulsifying agent, which is capable of being converted by breaking, so as to produce a road binder. According to the invention it includes a gelling agent and exhibits a flow threshold. This emulsion is both simple to spray and stable after spraying.
</ABSTRACT>
