<INVENTION-TITLE>
Polyarylensulfide molding composition.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
A moulding composition comprising     (A) from 20 to 80% by weight of at least one polyarylene sulphide,   (B) from 1 to 60% by weight of at least one mineral filler, (C) from 1 to 65% by weight of at least one surface-modified mineral filler and (D) from 0.01 to 0.4% by weight of at least one saturated, long-chain carboxylic acid ester (carboxylate), can be converted by injection moulding or extrusion into mouldings which have a high surface quality and isotropic properties. Metallisation of the mouldings proves to be durable, which favours use for lamps, in particular reflectors, and electronic functional parts.
</ABSTRACT>
