<INVENTION-TITLE>
Installation channel.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H02G304	H02G304	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H02G	H02G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H02G3	H02G3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
At least one equipment holder 9 for installation apparatuses 10 such as plug sockets, switches or the like is provided in an installation channel for supply lines 23, which channel can be fastened to the wall of a building, and has a rear wall 1, an upper wall 2, a base wall 3 and a front wall 4. The front wall 4 is designed integrally with the upper wall 2 and covers only the upper region of the installation channel. The equipment holder 9 is arranged on the inside of the front wall 4 and on the rear wall 1, inclined with respect to the upper wall 2.
