<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the preparation of a catalyst carrier, supported catalyst for the polymerization of olefins and process for the polymerization of olefins with this catalyst.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B01J3700	B01J3702	B01J3708	C08F400	C08F406	C08F460	C08F478	C08F1000	C08F1000	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B01J	B01J	B01J	C08F	C08F	C08F	C08F	C08F	C08F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B01J37	B01J37	B01J37	C08F4	C08F4	C08F4	C08F4	C08F10	C08F10	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Process for the preparation of a catalyst support according to which an inorganic powder including silica is impregnated with a solution, in an alcohol, of an alcoholate of a transition metal chosen from groups IVB and VB of the Periodic Table, the hydrocarbon radical of the alcohol being identical with at least one hydrocarbon radical of the alcoholate, at the end of the impregnation a suspension is collected from which the liquid phase is evaporated until a solid is obtained, and then the solid thus obtained is dried and the dried solid is calcined. Catalyst for olefin polymerisation, containing chromium deposited on such a catalyst support.
</ABSTRACT>
