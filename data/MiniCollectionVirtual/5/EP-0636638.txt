<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the preparation of polytetrahydrofuran or tetrahydrofuran-alkyleneoxide-copolymers
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08G6500	C08G6506	C08G6526	C08G6502	C08G6520	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08G	C08G	C08G	C08G	C08G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C08G65	C08G65	C08G65	C08G65	C08G65	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A process for the preparation of polytetrahydrofuran or C2- to C10-alkylene oxide-tetrahydrofuran copolymers having a mean molecular weight of from 250 to 20000 daltons by cationic polymerisation of tetrahydrofuran or tetrahydrofuran and C2- to C10-alkylene oxides in the presence of a promoter, in which process the catalyst used is a mixture of one or more C2- to C6-di- or tricarboxylic acids with boric acid.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
A process for the preparation of a polytetrahydrofuran
or a C
2
-C
10
-alkylene oxide/tetrahydrofuran
copolymer having an average molecular weight of from 250

to 20,000 Dalton by the cationic polymerization of tetrahydrofuran
or tetrahydrofuran and C
2
-C
10
-alkylene oxides
in the presence of a promoter, wherein the catalyst used

is a mixture of one or more C
2
-C
6
-di- or tricarboxylic
acids with boric acid.
A process as claimed in claim 1, wherein the
dicarboxylic acid used is oxalic acid.
A process as claimed in claim 1, wherein a
catalyst is used in which the boric acid/C
2
-C
6
-di- or
tricarboxylic acid molar ratio is from 1 : 10 to 10 : 1.
A process as claimed in claim 1, wherein the
catalyst is used in an amount of from 0.01 to 10% by

weight, based on the amount of tetrahydrofuran used.
A process as claimed in claim 1, wherein the
promoter used is a carboxylic anhydride.
A process as claimed in claim 1, wherein the
promoter used is a C
2
-C
10
-alkylene oxide.
