<INVENTION-TITLE>
Arrangement for connecting several telecommunication cables
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H04M172	H04M172	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H04M	H04M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H04M1	H04M1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The system includes several connection devices (10-18,21-27) which are fixedly arranged for each cable to be connected. The connection devices can be connected via connection elements (61-65). At least some of the connection devices include a plug device. The connection elements are pref. flexible plug-in leads and are provided at one end with a plug which matches the plug device included in the connection devices. Pref. the connection elements are switches. The connection devices may be arranged in two groups. A first group is for telecommunication cables which lead to individual terminals. The second group is for an exchange device and/or external cables.
</ABSTRACT>
