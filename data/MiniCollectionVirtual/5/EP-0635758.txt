<INVENTION-TITLE>
Color photographic silver halide material.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G03C1035	G03C1035	G03C700	G03C700	G03C720	G03C720	G03C730	G03C730	G03C7305	G03C7305	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G03C1	G03C1	G03C7	G03C7	G03C7	G03C7	G03C7	G03C7	G03C7	G03C7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A colour-photographic silver-halide material having at least two of each of blue-sensitive, yellow-coupling, green-sensitive, magenta-coupling and red-sensitive, cyan-coupling silver-halide emulsion layers of different photographic sensitivity, in which the silver-halide emulsions of the most sensitive blue-sensitive layers and of the most sensitive green-sensitive layers have an iodide content of 
<
/= 4 mol % and a mean particle size of 
>
/= 0.65  mu m, and the silver-halide emulsions of the least sensitive blue-sensitive layers and the least sensitive green-sensitive layers have an iodide content of 
>
/= 8 mol % and a mean particle size of 
<
/= 0.3  mu m, and contains an effective amount of at least one DIR compound whose inhibitor has a diffusibility of 
>
/= 0.4, is distinguished by excellent sharpness and very good colour separation.
</ABSTRACT>
