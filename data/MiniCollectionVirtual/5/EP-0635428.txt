<INVENTION-TITLE>
Plant for the packaging of food products in trays and tray used therefore.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65B716	B65B716	B65B4100	B65B4114	B65B4342	B65B4344	B65D134	B65D134	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65B7	B65B7	B65B41	B65B41	B65B43	B65B43	B65D1	B65D1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
This plant (installation) comprises, between a station (A) for individual dispensing of food products and a station (B) for weighing:   - on the one hand, a motorised conveyor (3) carrying fingers (14) projecting out from its strands (belts) and capable of interacting with the stop faces (15a, 15b) provided in the sides (2a) of each tray (2) in order to ensure translational linking between tray and conveyor, - and, on the other hand, a station (C) for storing and individually dispensing trays (2) and a station (D) for placing a heat-shrinkable film (46) over each tray (2).
