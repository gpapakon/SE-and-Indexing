Packing case in the form of a display

<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a cardboard display packing case of the type composed of two separable elements, one inserted into the other, of which one forms a display tray, each element being made from a cardboard sheet (1, 2) cut and scored so as to produce panels (11, 12, 13, 14, 21, 22, 23, 24) defining the side walls of the case which are edged with flaps (15, 16, 17, 18, 25, 26, 27, 28) respectively constituting the top and bottom end walls, the two elements being connected by means enabling them to be separated, is characterised in that the said means consist of at least one panel portion (111, 131) precut from one of the said panels of the tray-forming element some distance from its top edge and joined with the corresponding wall of the other element.

<CLAIMS>
Display packing case made of cardboard, of the type 
composed of two separable elements inserted one into the 

other, one of which forms a display container, each element 
being produced from a sheet of cardboard (1, 2) cut and 

grooved so as to arrange panels (11, 12, 13, 14, 21, 22, 
23, 24) defining the lateral walls of the case, bordered by 

flaps (15, 16, 17, 18, 25, 26, 27, 28) respectively 
constituting the top and bottom walls, the two elements 

being joined by means which permit their separation, the 
said means being constituted by at least one detachable 

panel portion (111, 131) pre-cut in one of the said panels 
of the element forming the container and integral with the 

corresponding wall of the other element, characterized in 
that the said panel portion is pre-cut according to a 

closed line at a distance from the top edge of the said 
panel. 
Case according to Claim 1, characterized in that an 
aperture (211, 231) is cut in the corresponding wall of the 

other element above the said panel portion (111, 131), and 
extends above the top edge of the element forming the 

container. 
Case according to one of the preceding Claims, 
characterized in that two of the said means are provided, 

one on each of the panels forming longitudinal wall of the 
container. 
Case according to Claim 3, characterized in that an 
additional tongue (141) is pre-cut on the top edge of a 

third panel (14) of the container to form a third fixing 
point. 
Case according to one of the preceding Claims, 
characterized in that the bonding tab (241) of the belt of  

 
the element forming the container comes to be inserted in a 

corresponding cut-out of the adjacent panel of the other 
element. 
Case according to one of the preceding Claims, 
characterized in that the first sheet comprises top edges 

(112, 132) in bevel shape. 
Case according to Claim 1, characterized in that the 
cut-outs (111, 131) of the first sheet (1) are shaped to 

form handles after removal of the internal part of the cut-out 
of the first sheet (1).
