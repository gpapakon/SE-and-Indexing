<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method and device for the forming of stacks of individual articles, especially biscuits.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65B2300	B65B2314	B65B3550	B65B3550	B65B5700	B65B5720	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
In a method for the forming of stacks of product articles lying flat, especially biscuits, from rows conveyed in a conveying direction 14, the product articles 2 are conveyed individually at a distance from one another and, without continuous stacking, come into contact with one another only in the stack having a fixed number. The product articles 2 are counted while being conveyed at a distance from one another, are separated according to the predetermined number in the stack, in each case into a row stack 22, 23 consisting of product articles 2 conveyed in succession at a distance from one another, and the product articles of each conveyed row stack are collected in a magazine 25, 26 in a manner lying flat one above the other in the conveying direction 14.
