<INVENTION-TITLE>
Gas burner.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F23D1446	F23D1464	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F23D	F23D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F23D14	F23D14	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In the case of a gas burner having a device for setting the necessary primary-air admixture in the region of the gas injector using the gas injector nozzle (9) at the lower end of the burner support (2) which is provided with an inner bore (15), a solution is to be provided with which, in the case of all-gas burners on stoves used is the household and catering establishments, adjustment to the appropriate gas type is made possible without the need for dismounting any elements of the stoves. This is achieved in that a mixing sleeve (7) acting upon the primary-air supply opening (10) and guided on the burner support (2) is provided, the mixing sleeve being adjustable by means of a tool from the flame side of the burner through the inner bore (15) of the burner support (2)
