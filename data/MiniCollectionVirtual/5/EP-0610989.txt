RADIO SYSTEM WITH SPACE DIVERSITY TRANSMITTER/RECEIVER.

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a radio system with a transmitting/receiving device having at least two antennas (1, 2, 5, 6). Detectors (3) for measuring time offsets between antenna signals which have been received separately from one another are provided. Signals are sent out via different antennas (5, 6). Furthermore, means (7) for setting time offsets of the transmitting signals which correspond to the measured time offsets are provided.
