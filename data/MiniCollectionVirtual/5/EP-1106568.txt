<INVENTION-TITLE>
Automated refuelling system
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B67D501	B67D504	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B67D	B67D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B67D5	B67D5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
An automatic fuel filling installation is provided, with a refuelling robot (1) which has an arm (2) with at least three axes to the free end (3) of which is fitted a filling nozzle (4), a fuel distributor (5) for feeding the filling nozzle (4) with a single fuel type selected from a number thereof, a hose (9) with an end (8) coupled to a fuel distributor (5) and at least two feed pumps (6) which are coupled to at least two fuel storage tanks (7). The hose (9) has at least two chambers (21, 22, 23, 24, 25, 26) where the fuel distributor (5) from which the individual lines (10) lead to the filling nozzle (4), is arranged between the filling nozzle (4) and the hose (9). The other end (11) of the hose (9) is coupled to a connection box (13).
</ABSTRACT>
