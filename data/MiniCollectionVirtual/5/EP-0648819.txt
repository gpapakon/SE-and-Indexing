Conductive coating composition.

<ABSTRACT>
Conductive coating formulation comprising     (i) 5-25% by weight of a binder consisting of a mixture of   a) from 40 to 60% by weight of an acrylic resin based on methyl methacrylate and   b) from 60 to 40% by weight modified PVC,   (ii) 60-85% by weight of a solvent mixture consisting of esters, aromatic compounds and ketones in a ratio of from 0.9 : 2.5 : 1.2 to 1.1 : 2.7 : 1.4, and (iii) 5-25% by weight of an electrically conducting pigment.
</ABSTRACT>
