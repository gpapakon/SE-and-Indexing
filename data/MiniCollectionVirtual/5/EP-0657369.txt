<INVENTION-TITLE>
Partition wall for refuse containers.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65D2504	B65D2506	B65F100	B65F100	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65D	B65D	B65F	B65F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65D25	B65D25	B65F1	B65F1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Partition wall 2 for a refuse container, the partition wall being capable of being fastened by at least one edge to the inside of the container wall 1 and forming at least one angular corner region 7 at the fastening point, in such a way that a flexible surface structure is fastened to the edge of the partition wall 2 so as to form an intermediate piece 3 which bridges the corner region 7.
