<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a deformable material containing silicon carbide powder, polycarbosilane, organic solvent, optionally boron compounds and/or other auxiliaries, preferably pore-forming agents, and to the use thereof for the production of mould pieces and semifinished products by plastic shaping.
</ABSTRACT>
