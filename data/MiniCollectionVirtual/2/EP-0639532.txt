<INVENTION-TITLE>
ELECTRONIC DESCALING DEVICE.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C02F148	C02F148	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C02F	C02F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C02F1	C02F1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A software-controlled electronic water softening device affects the water which contains hardness-producing substances by electrical pulses which are transmitted to the water flowing through by means of electrodes. The electronic water softening device makes it possible for a throughflow- and hardness-dependent or alternatively -independent treatment of the water to be carried out within a wide scope with freely selectable parameters.
