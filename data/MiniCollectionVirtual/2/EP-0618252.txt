<INVENTION-TITLE>
Formable compositions based wholly aromatic polyamides, foamed articles and laminates.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08G6900	C08G6932	C08G6942	C08G7700	C08G77455	C08J900	C08J902	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08G	C08G	C08G	C08G	C08G	C08J	C08J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to foamable compositions based on fully aromatic polyamides, where they essentially contain 4 - 20 mol % of at least difunctional oligosiloxanes and 80 - 96 mol % of aromatic starting components, to foamed mouldings and layered (laminated) materials produced therefrom, and to a process for the production of these foamed mouldings. The novel foamable compositions and foams based on fully aromatic polyamides (nylons) are distinguished by flame resistance and low smoke densities in contact with flames. They are particularly suitable for the production of materials and mouldings for the passenger transport sector. The thickness of the mouldings can be varied over a broad range. It is essentially dependent on the mould used and the amount of powdered foamable polyamide employed.
</ABSTRACT>
