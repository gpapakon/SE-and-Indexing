<INVENTION-TITLE>
Steering column frame for an automotive vehicle
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F16B1700	F16L314	F16L314	F16B1700	B62D116	B62D116	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F16B	F16L	F16L	F16B	B62D	B62D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
This steering column body (frame) for a motor vehicle, to which is fixed at least one connection flange (2, 3) for connecting this body to the rest of the structure of the vehicle, is characterised in that the or each connection flange includes a collar (2a, 3a) fixed to the body (1) by crimping between radial outward deformations (4, 5) of the body.

<CLAIMS>
Steering column body for a motor vehicle, on which is 
fixed at least one flange (2, 3) for connecting this body to 

the rest of the structure of the vehicle, through the 
intermediary of a collar (2a, 3a) of the flange, surrounding 

the body (1), characterised in that the collar (2a, 3a) of the 
or each flange is fixed on the body (1) by crimping between 

radial deformations towards the exterior (4, 5) of the body. 
Body according to Claim 1, characterised in that means 
(6) are provided for locking in rotation of the or each flange 

and the body with respect to one another. 
Body according to Claim 2, characterised in that the 
locking means comprise complementary projecting and recessed 

parts (6a, 6b) of the body and of the or each flange. 
</CLAIMS>
