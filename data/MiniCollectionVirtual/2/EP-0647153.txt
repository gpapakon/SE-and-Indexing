<INVENTION-TITLE>
ICE-SKATING SHOE.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A63C100	A63C126	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A63C	A63C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A63C1	A63C1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The ice-skating shoe proposed includes a main body (1), joined to the blade, and a vertical edge (3) extending along the longitudinal axis of the upper surface of the main body (1). The ice-skating shoe is made of composite material and the blade may be made of a metallic, metallo-ceramic or other material, the blade being fixed in place by means of a detachable connection.
</ABSTRACT>
