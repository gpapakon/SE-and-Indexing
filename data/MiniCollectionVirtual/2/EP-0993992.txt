<INVENTION-TITLE>
B-pillar roof unit
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60N302	B60N302	B60R1302	B60R1302	B62D2504	B62D2504	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60N	B60N	B60R	B60R	B62D	B62D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60N3	B60N3	B60R13	B60R13	B62D25	B62D25	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The roof module has an elongated basic body (2) that extends from the roof end of a B-column (6) transversely wrt. the B-column to a roof frame (3) when the module is installed. The interior vehicle fittings (5) are functionally integrated into the B-column roof module. The cross-section of the roof module matches the shape of an indentation in the vehicle roof. An Independent claim is also included for a motor vehicle with a roof with a roof module and a vehicle canopy between the roof module and the roof.
</ABSTRACT>
