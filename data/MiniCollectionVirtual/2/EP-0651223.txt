<INVENTION-TITLE>
Army vehicle
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F41A2300	F41A2320	F41A2334	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The military vehicle, in particular an armoured fighting vehicle (armoured combat vehicle) has on its top an opening which can be closed by a hatch, as well as a telescopic mast which can be extended through this opening. An opening is likewise provided on the underneath of the fighting vehicle, which opening is aligned in the vertical direction with the opening at the top and whose diameter is somewhat larger than the diameter of the lower part of the telescopic mast.

<CLAIMS>
Military vehicle (1), especially an armoured combat 
vehicle, provided with an opening (11) in its top surface 

closable by a hatch, and having a telescopic mast (13, 2) 
extendable through that opening, characterised in that it is 

provided with an opening (8) in its bottom surface (12) 
vertically aligned with the opening in the top surface and 

whose diameter is slightly larger than the diameter of the 
lower end (16) of the telescopic mast. 
Vehicle according to claim 1, characterised in that it has 
a plurality of hydraulic gripping and positioning 

systems (9, 10) for the telescopic mast, which are so arranged 
that the lower end of the telescopic mast can be lowered to the 

ground through the opening in the bottom surface. 
Vehicle according to claims 1 and 2, characterised in that 
the telescopic mast is provided at its lower end (16) with a 

ground spike (if appropriate, screwable into the ground). 
Vehicle according to one of claims 1 to 3, characterised in 
that the upper end of the mast is provided with a gyroscopic 

and/or weapons platform (14). 
</CLAIMS>
