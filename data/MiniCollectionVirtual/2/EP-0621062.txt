<ABSTRACT>
1. Arrangement of a game coating for the releasable attachment of at least one toy brick (2, 3). 2.1. In the case of the known toy bricks, only limited use in building is possible, especially with ranges of toys from different manufacturers. 2.2. According to the invention, a game coating arrangement (1) is provided whose surface (4) facing the toy brick (2, 3) has at least one first mechanical connecting means (5) for interaction with at least one second mechanical connecting means (6) provided on the or each toy brick. The surface of the game sheet (1) remote from each toy brick (2, 3) has an adhesive layer (7) for the releasable adhesion of the game coating arrangement (1) to a wall (8). Moreover, the game coating arrangement (1) is produced from a material which permits adaptation of the game coating arrangement to a surface (9) of the wall (8) of any desired curvature. 2.3. The game coating arrangement according to the invention can be used to connect a very wide variety of toy bricks.
