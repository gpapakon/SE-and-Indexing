Manganese-zinc ferrite
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
A MnZn ferrite having low power loss for high-frequency applications has the following composition:     53.0 to 55.0 mol % Fe2O3,   35.0 to 41.0 mol % MnO,   7.0 to 13.0 mol % ZnO,   0.03 to 0.12 % by weight CaO,   0.01 to 0.1 % by weight V2O5,   0.1 to 1.0 % by weight TiO2,   0.005 to 0.015 % by weight SiO2,   0.01 to 0.05 % by weight Nb2O5.
