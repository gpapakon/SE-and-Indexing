<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for reducing scrap during extrusion of metal profiles in particular aluminium profiles.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B21C3100	B21C3100	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B21C	B21C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B21C31	B21C31	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Method for reducing scrap during extrusion of metal profiles, especially aluminium profiles, in which a so-called initial extrusion billet is in general pressed before the first production billet, the calculation of the first production billet being performed taking into account the actual weight per metre of the bars of raw material.
</ABSTRACT>
