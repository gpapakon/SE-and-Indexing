<INVENTION-TITLE>
Electric appliance to be fixed on a support.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Rapid-fixing electrical appliance. According to the invention, the appliance includes a plinth (11) and a cap (12) respectively equipped with catches (30,32) imbricating side-by-side with the mounting in order to allow a locking piece (40) to be put in place.
