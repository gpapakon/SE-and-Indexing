Coupling converting system.

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device (hitch conversion system) for towing vehicles, which permits optional use, with easy operation, as a ball-type and mouth-type hitch (4), the hitch ball (3) being permanently connected to the mount on the towing vehicle and the mouth-type hitch being capable of being fitted onto the hitch ball by means of an adapter.
