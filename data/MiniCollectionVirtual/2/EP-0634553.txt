<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a door handle with locking by electromagnetic suction, characterised in that it takes the form of a one-piece shell (1) comprising   - at least one cylindrical cell (3) (4) open towards the rear of the shell (1) and dimensioned to receive a standard electromagnetic suction device, - a receptacle (9) forming a housing open towards the front of the shell, in order to contain electrical components, the said receptacle being closed off by means of a cover,   - a lateral extension (2) for gripping.
