<INVENTION-TITLE>
Change-over system for different media.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B05B1200	B05B1214	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B05B	B05B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B05B12	B05B12	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a change-over system unit for different media, in particular a paint changer (10, 30) for paint-spraying installations with separate feeds (12, 13, 14, 15, 32, 33, 34, 35) for each paint and for cleaning fluid and/or for compressed/scavenging air, which feeds (12, 13, 14, 15, 32, 33, 34, 35) have blocking members (20, 36) and can be connected to a common outlet (19, 39), the feeds (12, 13, 14, 15, 32, 33, 34, 35) being designed as modules (16, 18) which are arranged in cascade form.
