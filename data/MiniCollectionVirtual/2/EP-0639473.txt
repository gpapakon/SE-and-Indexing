<INVENTION-TITLE>
Air spring for supporting a vehicle body.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60G1500	B60G1510	F16F710	F16F7108	F16F902	F16F905	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60G	B60G	F16F	F16F	F16F	F16F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60G15	B60G15	F16F7	F16F7	F16F9	F16F9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
An air spring supports a vehicle body on another, oscillating vehicle structural component by means of an air spring bellows to which compressed air can be admitted. The ends of the air spring bellows are each arranged in an air-tight manner on a connecting piece, the one connecting piece being fitted on the vehicle body and the other on the vehicle structural component. In order to reduce oscillations of the vehicle structural component in the resonant range, the connecting piece (4) fitted on the vehicle structural component has an elastically connected oscillation damper (18).
