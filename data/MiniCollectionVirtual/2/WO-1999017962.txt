<INVENTION-TITLE>
DEVICE FOR PRODUCING A SIGNAL TO INITIATE ACTUATION OF A SECURITY SYSTEM IN A VEHICLE MAKING AN OVERTURN
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60R2100	B60R2100	B60R2101	B60R2101	B60R21013	B60R21013	B60R210132	B60R210132	B60R2113	B60R2113	B60R2116	B60R2116	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60R	B60R	B60R	B60R	B60R	B60R	B60R	B60R	B60R	B60R	B60R	B60R	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60R21	B60R21	B60R21	B60R21	B60R21	B60R21	B60R21	B60R21	B60R21	B60R21	B60R21	B60R21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In order for the inventive device to reliably determine whether a security system has to be actuated, a first series of elements (1) is provided to derive from a rotation rate ( omega x) measured around the longitudinal axis (x) of the vehicle and/or from accelerations (ay, az) measured at least in the direction of the transverse axis (y) and the vertical axis (z) of the vehicle a first assessment criterion for triggering actuation of a security system (2). A second series of elements (5, 6) is provided to feature irrespectively of the first series (12) a second assessment criterion (e2). When the two assessment criteria (e1, e2) are met, a signal for actuating the security system (2) is produced by a circuit (7).
</ABSTRACT>
