<ABSTRACT>
Apparatus for uniformly applying a suspension to a collagen substrate for the preparation of a material for sealing and healing of wounds, which comprises a container in which the suspension is introduced. The bottom of this container comprises a basic frame (1) and a bottom perforated plate (2), a movable perforated plate (3) being arranged directly above the bottom perforated plate (2).
