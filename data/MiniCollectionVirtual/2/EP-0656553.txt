<INVENTION-TITLE>
X-Y-drive for microscope specimen holder.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G02B2124	G02B2126	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G02B	G02B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G02B21	G02B21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Microscope having an object stage (microscope stage) which defines an x-y plane and is provided with a specimen holder (object holder, object slide) which is adjustable in the x-y plane by means of x-y adjusting devices. This device has adjusting drives with shafts (9, 21), which can be rotated about respective drive axes (5, 5'), in order to adjust the specimen holder along the x-direction or the y-direction. The drive axes (5, 5') run parallel to the x-y plane, in the exemplary embodiment both axes are identical.
