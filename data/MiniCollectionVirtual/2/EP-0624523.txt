<INVENTION-TITLE>
Two-parts container.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65D600	B65D600	B65D2102	B65D2102	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
A vessle is proposed, consisting of a pot-shaped bottom part (5) and a pot-like or lid-like top part (9), in which the outer walls have an opposite conicity, and the edge regions of the parts intermesh and rest on one another in a linear manner, engaging in a positive-locking manner in one another.
