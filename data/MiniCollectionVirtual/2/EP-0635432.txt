<INVENTION-TITLE>
Foldable box, in particular carton
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65D570	B65D570	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65D	B65D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65D5	B65D5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In a foldable box having a bottom wall (6), four upright side walls (2, 3, 4, 5) and a lid wall (7), said lid wall (7) being folded from four closure flaps (26, 27, 28, 29) which are connected to the associated side wall in each case at a bending line (22, 23, 24, 25) and extend in each case essentially over the entire length of the associated side wall (2, 3, 4, 5), and the foldable box having an opening (61) which can be torn open at prefabricated weakening lines (62), the opening (61) which can be torn open is arranged in a corner region of the lid wall (7), the weakening line (62) extending with a spacing, guaranteeing the opening (61), from the corner (E) from the one to the other upper circumferential edge (24, 34) forming the corner (E) and into an associated upper gusset part (27c), and the section of the weakening line (62) extending at least in the lid wall (7) being prefabricated as a weakening line which does not pass through the wall of the foldable box.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
Folding box (1), Particularly made of cardboard, with a
bottom wall (6) four upright side walls (2, 3, 4, 5) and a

cover wall (7),

wherein the cover wall (7) is folded from four folding
cover flaps (26, 27, 28, 29) which are connected to the

appertaining side wall at a bending line (22, 23, 24, 25)
in each case and essentially extend over the entire length

of the appertaining side wall (2, 3, 4, 5) in each case;

wherein the folding box, which can be folded from a one-piece
blank (8), has an aperture (61) which can be torn

open at a prefabricated line of attenuation (62) and is
disposed in a corner region of the cover wall (7), and

wherein the line of attenuation (62) extends, at a distance
from the corner (E) which guarantees the aperture (61),

from one of the upper peripheral edges (24, 34) which forms
the corner (E), to the other;

wherein that section of the line of attenuation (62) which
extends at least in the cover wall (7) is prefabricated as

a line of attenuation which does not penetrate the walls of
the folding box;

wherein the folding cover flaps (26, 27, 28, 29) are
connected to one another, at their side margins which face

towards one another, at first bending lines (33, 34, 35),
the length of which approximately corresponds to half the

breadth (b) of the appertaining side wall (3, 5);

wherein two first folding cover flaps (27, 29) which lie
opposite one another have two straight second bending lines

(44) in each case, which emanate from the inner corners
(46) of the first folding cover flaps (27, 29) and

intersect at a point of intersection (47) in the centre of
the outer margin (48) of the first folding cover flaps

(27, 29);

wherein the first folding cover flaps (27, 29) are, in each
case, folded onto one another about the second bending

lines (44) with lower and upper gusset parts (27a, 27b, 
27c; 29a, 29b, 29c), and wherein the two second folding

cover flaps (26, 28) are folded onto one another;
characterised in that,
of the second folding cover flaps (26, 28), the inner one
(26) has a length (L1) which is of somewhat longer

dimensions than half the breadth (b) of the side walls
(3, 5) carrying the first folding cover flaps (27, 29) and

is of shorter dimensions than the length (L2) of the outer
second folding cover flap (28),
so that the joints (60) which are present between the
gussets (27b, 27c; 29b, 29c) formed by folding about the

second bending lines (44) are covered by the inner (26) of
the two second folding cover flaps (26, 28).
Folding box (1), particularly made of cardboard, with a
bottom wall (6), four upright side walls (2, 3, 4, 5) and a

cover wall (7),

wherein the bottom wall (6) is folded from four folding
bottom flaps (18, 19, 20, 21) which are connected to the

appertaining side wall at a bending line (14, 15, 16, 17)
in each case and essentially extend over the entire length

of the appertaining side wall (2, 3, 4, 5) in each case;

wherein the folding box, which can be folded from a one-piece
blank (8), has an aperture (61) which can be torn

open at a prefabricated line of attenuation (62) and is
disposed in a corner region of the cover wall (7), and

wherein the line of attenuation (62) extends, at a distance
from the corner (E) which guarantees the aperture (61),

from one of the upper peripheral edges (24, 34) which forms
the corner (E), to the other;

wherein that section of the line of attenuation (62) which
extends at least in the cover wall (7) is prefabricated as

a line of attenuation which does not penetrate the walls of
the folding box;

wherein the folding bottom flaps (18, 19, 20, 21) are
connected to one another, at their side margins which face

towards one another, at first bending lines (30, 31, 32), 
the length of which approximately corresponds to half the

breadth (b) of the appertaining side wall (3, 5);

wherein two first folding bottom flaps (19, 21) which line
opposite one another have two straight second bending lines

(44) in each case, which emanate from the inner corners
(46) of the first folding bottom flaps (19, 21) and

intersect at a point of intersection (47) in the centre of
the outer margin (48) of the first folding bottom flaps

(19, 21);

wherein the first folding bottom flaps (19, 21) are, in
each case, folded onto one another about the second bending

lines (44) with lower and upper gusset parts (19a, 19b,
19c; 21a, 21b, 21c), and wherein the two second folding

bottom flaps (18, 20) are folded onto one another,
characterised in that,
of the second folding bottom flaps (18, 20), the inner one
(18) has a length (L1) which is of somewhat longer

dimensions than half the breadth (b) of the side walls
(3, 5) carrying the first folding bottom flaps (19, 21) and

is of shorter dimensions than the length (L2) of the outer
second folding bottom flap (20),
so that the joints (60) which are present between the
gussets (19b, 19c; 21b, 21c) formed by folding about the

second bending lines (44) are covered by the inner (18) of
the two second folding bottom flaps (18, 20).
Folding box according to claim 1 or 2,
characterised in that
the said box has a rectangular shape in horizontal cross-section,

and the two first folding bottom flaps (19, 21)
and/or folding cover flaps (27, 29), which lie opposite one

another, are disposed on the narrow sides.
Folding box according to one of the preceding claims,
characterised in that
the free corners of the inner of the two second folding
bottom and/or cover flaps (18, 26) are rounded or bevelled

(49).
Folding box according to one of the preceding claims,
characterised in that
the side walls (2, 3, 4, 5) are disposed on a blank (8) in
a row one behind the other and, at one of the two end side

walls (2a, 5a), particularly at an end side wall (5a) on a
narrow side, there is connected to a bending line (12), at

the latter, a side closure flap (13) which is connected to
the adjacent bottom and/or cover closure flap (21, 29) in

each case by a gusset (13a, 13b) which is connected in each
case, at a bending line (36, 37, 38, 39), to the folding

side flap (13) and the appertaining bottom and/or cover
closure flap (21, 29).
Folding box according to claim 5,
characterised in that
the free edge (42) of the gussets (13a, 13b) extends in an
angularly convex manner and intersects, preferably at a

distance (a), a prolongation (42a) of the free edge (43) of
the side closure flap (13).
Folding box according to one of the preceding claims,
characterised in that
the two second folding bottom and/or cover flaps (18, 20,
26, 28) are glued to one another, preferably by one or two

preferably strip-shaped gluing points (52, 53) disposed on
the inside of the outer bottom and/or cover closure flap.
Folding box according to one of the preceding claims,
characterised in that
the inner of the two second folding bottom and/or cover
flaps (18, 26) are glued, preferably by gluing points (54)

applied to the insides of the inner folding bottom and/or 
cover flaps (18, 26), to the first folding bottom and/or

cover flaps (19, 27), which form a gusset in each case.
Folding box according to one of the preceding claims,
characterised in that
the line of attenuation (62) extends into the appertaining
upper gusset part (27c), that section of the line of

attenuation (62) which extends at least in the cover wall
(7) being prefabricated as a line of attenuation which does

not penetrate the walls of the folding box.
Folding box according to one of the preceding claims,
characterised in that
the line of attenuation (62) is formed by a stamped portion
on the walls of the folding box.
Folding box according to claim 10,
characterised in that
the stamped portion is of continuous construction in its
longitudinal direction, at least in the region of the cover

wall (7).
Folding box according to claim 10 or 11,
characterised in that
the stamped portion is disposed on the inside or outside of
the walls of the folding box.
Folding box according to one of the preceding claims,
characterised in that
the first folding bottom and cover flaps (19, 21, 27, 29)
adjoin the narrow side walls (3, 5), and one (19c, 21b,

27c, 29b) of the two gussets in each case is of longer
dimensions, by a length L3, than the adjacent gusset (19b,

21c, 27b, 29c).
