<INVENTION-TITLE>
ROLLER-SKATING DEVICE
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A63C1700	A63C1704	A63C1706	A63C1724	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A63C	A63C	A63C	A63C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The roller-skating device according to the invention comprises at least two running rollers (16,25) which are attached in a manner permitting free rotation to a rigid rolling chassis (11,21) as a support for the roller-skater's foot and whose running surface is designed to be at least partly spherical, such as the surface of a sphere, a hemisphere, a spherical segment or an ovoid.
