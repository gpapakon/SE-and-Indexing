<INVENTION-TITLE>
A process for forming a polymer that contains metalsulphonate.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08C1900	C08C1920	C08F800	C08F800	C08F844	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08C	C08C	C08F	C08F	C08F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C08C19	C08C19	C08F8	C08F8	C08F8	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A gel-free cement of a neutralised sulphonated polymer 
having 10 to 50 meq. of sulphonate groups per 100 gm 

polymer is prepared by dissolving the sulphonated polymer, 
e.g. sulphonated EPDM, in a non-reactive solvent, e.g. 

n-heptane, quenching with isopropanol (2.5 to 5 wt. % based 
on non-reactive solvent) and neutralising with a neutralising 

agent dissolved in water at a level of less than 2.5 wt. % 
based on non-reactive solvent. 
</ABSTRACT>
