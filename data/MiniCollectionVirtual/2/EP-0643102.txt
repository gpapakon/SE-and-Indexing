<INVENTION-TITLE>
Syndiotactic polystyrene and mixtures obtained therefrom.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08F212	C08F214	C08F244	C08F244	C08F400	C08F4642	C08L2500	C08L2500	C08L2506	C08L5300	C08L5300	C08L5302	D01F602	D01F622	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
Polystyrene mixtures comprising   a) from 80 to 99% by weight of a syndiotactic homopolymer of styrene or a copolymer of styrene and further copolymerised, unsaturated comonomers, and b) from 1 to 20% by weight of a block copolymer comprising at least two monomer blocks or a star-shaped block copolymer of vinylaromatic monomers on the one hand and a conjugated diene or tert-butyl styrene on the other hand.   The syndiotactic polystyrene can be isolated by precipitation from these polystyrene mixtures with the aid of organic solvents.
</ABSTRACT>
