<INVENTION-TITLE>
Percussion primer for small arms, process for its preparation and its use.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C06B3300	C06B3314	C06C700	C06C700	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C06B	C06B	C06C	C06C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C06B33	C06B33	C06C7	C06C7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a percussion primer for small arms, the primer consisting of a special metal such as zirconium as a reductant (reducing agent) and a mixture of oxygen carriers having different oxidation potentials as the oxidant (oxidising agent). The preferred production method is based on the water serving for temporary desensitisation being flushed out by an organic solvent. Preferably, the primer is used in small-calibre small arms employing cartridges which are used, for the purpose of training, in numerous enclosed (self-contained) underground firing ranges.
</ABSTRACT>
