<INVENTION-TITLE>
Interlining from knitted fabric with weft yarns.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A41D2702	A41D2706	D04B2114	D04B2116	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A41D	A41D	D04B	D04B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
This invention describes an interlining based on a knitted fabric with weft threads composed of textured synthetic yarn which are embedded in each stitch row of the knitted fabric, the interlining being coated with hot-melt adhesive and the yarn of the stitches of the knitted fabric being a textured synthetic yarn.
