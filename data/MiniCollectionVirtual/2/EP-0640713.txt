<INVENTION-TITLE>
Iron for removing the lustre of the laundry
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>D06F7530	D06F7500	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>D06F	D06F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>D06F75	D06F75	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The iron (1) has a sole (2) and comprises a member (3) carrying fibres (4) for brushing the laundry (17) which is in contact with the sole (2). This member (3) is located in the rear of the iron (1), so that the fibres (4) are located in the vicinity of the plane (P) defined by the outer face of the sole (2) of the iron, part of the fibres (4) extending outside the iron beyond the said plane (P). Use for removing the lustre of laundry during ironing.

<CLAIMS>
A smoothing iron (1) for ironing fabric, presenting a sole plate 
(2), including a member (3, 20) carrying fibers (4) for brushing the 

fabric (17) which is in contact with the sole plate (2), said member 
(3, 20) being situated to the rear of the iron (1) so that the fibers 

(4) are placed in the vicinity of the plane (P) defined by the outer 
face of the sole plate (2) of the iron, a portion of the fibers (4) 

extending out from the iron, beyond said plane (P), the iron (1) 
being characterized in that it includes a cylindrical roller (3) 

presenting at least a portion of the brushing fibers (4) on the 
periphery thereof. 
A smoothing iron according to claim 1, characterized in that the 
axis (5) of the cylindrical roller (3) is substantially parallel to 

the plane (P) defined by the outer face of the sole plate (2) and 
perpendicular to the longitudinal direction of the iron, the roller 

(3) being mounted to rotate about its axis (5), each fiber (4) being 
fixed to the roller (3) by one end (6) towards the inside of the iron 

relative to the plane (P), its other end (7) extending out from the 
iron, beyond said plane (P). 
A smoothing iron according to claim 2, characterized in that the 
roller (3) can be retracted inside the iron so that the fibers (4) 

are placed entirely inside the iron away from the plane (P). 
A smoothing iron according to claim 3, characterized in that the 
iron includes a motor (10) for rotating the roller (3). 
A smoothing iron according to claim 4, and presenting a handle 
(13), the iron being characterized in that the control device (14) 

for the motor (10) is situated in the vicinity of the handle (13) of 
the iron. 
A smoothing iron according to any one of claims 1 to 5, 
characterized in that the brushing fibers comprise bristles (4).  

 
A smoothing iron according to any one of claims 1 to 6, 
characterized in that the brushing fibers are constituted in the form 

of a velvet. 
A smoothing iron according to any one of claims 1 to 7, 
characterized in that the brushing fibers are constituted by a felt 

fabric. 
</CLAIMS>
