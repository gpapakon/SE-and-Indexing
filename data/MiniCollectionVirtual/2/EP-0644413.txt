<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for quantitative analysis of liquid samples. A sample is dried and exposed to visible and/or infrared light. Diffusely or specularly reflected light from the sample and the sample-holder is detected and evaluated. The invention furthermore relates to a system for carrying out the method according to the invention and to sample-holders having diffusely or specularly reflecting surface.
