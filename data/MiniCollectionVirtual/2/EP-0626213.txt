<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method and device for sorting waste mixtures.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B03B900	B03B906	B07B1300	B07B1300	B07C534	B07C534	B07C536	B07C536	G01V810	G01V810	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B03B9	B03B9	B07B13	B07B13	B07C5	B07C5	B07C5	B07C5	G01V8	G01V8	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The sorting system uses electromagnetic and/or acoustic waves directed onto the waste material, the received waves being evaluated electronically, to identify objects within the waste material mixture. The latter is supplied to a transport path in loose form or in a bale. The waste material is irradiated with visible, and/or UV and/or IR light. The bale is opened to allow a major fraction to be identified and removed, with subsequent identification and sorting of the remainder. Pref. the major fraction of the waste material is identified from its texture, colour and/or structure.
</ABSTRACT>
