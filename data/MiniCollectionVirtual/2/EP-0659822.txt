<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the production of thermostable moulded parts.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Thermostable mouldings for brake linings, clutch linings and abrasive tools are produced by moulding and vulcanising a mixture containing a natural or synthetic rubber in the presence of a sulphur-free vulcaniser/activator system comprising phenolresols containing at least 90 mol% of p-(C3-C12)alkylphenols, based on the total content of phenols, and a substance or substance mixture which acts as a Bronsted or Lewis acid under the reaction conditions of the vulcanisation, at a temperature of 120 to 250 DEG C.
</ABSTRACT>
