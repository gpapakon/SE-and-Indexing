<INVENTION-TITLE>
Storage and sorting device for long products such as bars, pipes, reinforcing concrete rounds or the like.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a storage and sorting device for long products such as bars, pipes, concrete reinforcement bars and the like, characterised in that it comprises a plurality of independent magazines (1 - 10) and means for selectively forwarding said magazines and for discharging, also selectively at the same time and in independent sequences, the long products stored in these magazines.
