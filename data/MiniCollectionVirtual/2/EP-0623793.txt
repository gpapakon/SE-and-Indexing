<INVENTION-TITLE>
Refrigerator for treatment of food stuffs, especially chocolate masses.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A23G700	A23G702	F25D1300	F25D1306	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A23G	A23G	F25D	F25D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A23G7	A23G7	F25D13	F25D13	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Refrigerator for the treatment of foodstuffs, especially chocolate, the refrigerator comprising a plurality of modular structural units, having a fixed basic unit (1) which interacts with a feed device (2) and with a removal device (3) for moulds (s), and provided above the basic unit is a container (4) through which the moulds (s) run in vertical planes, the container (4) exhibiting, at least on one side, shaft-like components (6, 7) which form ventilation ducts and are arranged pivotably with respect to the refrigerator, and the pivotably arranged, shaft-like components (6, 7) being connectable to a cooling element (9) which is arranged displaceably with respect to the refrigerator.
