<INVENTION-TITLE>
Cooking plate temperature indicator.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01K500	G01K552	H05B102	H05B102	H05B368	H05B374	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01K	G01K	H05B	H05B	H05B	H05B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an arrangement for cooking plate temperature indication in glass ceramic cooking surfaces, the radiating heater elements arranged below the glass ceramic supporting surface having a temperature limiter which is connected to a regulator button, the rod regulator, arranged as temperature limiter (3), functioning as analog-value transmitter for an evaluation unit (13), the evaluation unit (13) being connected to an electronic unit (14) and the electronic unit (14) being connected on the output side to an analog-digital display (15).
