<INVENTION-TITLE>
SYSTEM FOR PRODUCING A RELEASE SIGNAL FOR A VEHICLE SAFETY DEVICE
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60R2101	B60R2101	B60R21013	B60R21013	B60R210132	B60R210132	B60R2116	B60R2116	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60R	B60R	B60R	B60R	B60R	B60R	B60R	B60R	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60R21	B60R21	B60R21	B60R21	B60R21	B60R21	B60R21	B60R21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a system which guarantees that a security device is released on time in the event of lateral impact of a vehicle against a kerb. To this end, a first threshold decider (1) compares a rotation rate ( omega x) measured in relation to the longitudinal axis of the vehicle, or a value derived therefrom, with a threshold value. A second threshold decider (2) compares an acceleration (ay) measured in the direction of the lateral axis of the vehicle with a threshold value. If both threshold deciders (1, 2) simultaneously indicate that their threshold values have been exceeded, a release signal (as1) for the safety device (4) is emitted.
</ABSTRACT>
