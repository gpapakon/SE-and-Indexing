<INVENTION-TITLE>
Luff rope guiding device in particular for a sail which can be furled on a boom.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B63H900	B63H908	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B63H	B63H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B63H9	B63H9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a luff rope (7) guiding device in particular for a sail (3) which can be furled on a boom (6) of a sailing boat, characterised in that it consists of a continuous groove (8), in which the luff rope (7) is engaged and moves freely, said groove (8) being arranged at the rear of the mast (2) and mounted freely pivotably about its axis by a given amplitude on either side of the plane of symmetry of the sailing boat. Application to sailing boats.
