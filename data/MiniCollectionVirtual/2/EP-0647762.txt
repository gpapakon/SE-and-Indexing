<INVENTION-TITLE>
Roller blind system.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E06B917	E06B917	E06B9174	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E06B	E06B	E06B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>E06B9	E06B9	E06B9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Roller shutter system with two lateral support parts for a draw-out mechanism or a bearing or the like of a roller shutter, it being possible for the support parts to be connected to the lateral guide rails for the roller shutter in each case, and the support parts being designed for supporting the draw-out mechanism or the bearing or the like. The roller shutter system comprises, for each support part (1), at least one covering (9) which can be fastened on the outside, at the end, of the respective support part (1).
