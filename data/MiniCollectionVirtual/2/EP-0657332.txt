<INVENTION-TITLE>
Windscreen wiper with connection pieces, especially for a motor vehicle.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60S138	B60S138	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60S	B60S	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60S1	B60S1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A windscreen wiper generally consists of a set of yokes (10,22) carrying a wiper blade (6), while being articulated to one another by means of connection pieces (26). According to the invention, a yoke (10,22) bears a non-retractable projection (48) which interacts with a locking bearing surface (60) provided on the connection piece (26).
