<INVENTION-TITLE>
Fixing device for stanchions on side-walls of lorries.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B62D3302	B62D3302	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B62D	B62D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B62D33	B62D33	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Apparatus for fixing stanchions to the side walls of lorries, consisting of a support piece (1) fixed to the platform (bed) of the lorry, of a locking piece (2) sliding along the stanchion (3) under the action of a lever (4) which has an eye (loop) for manual control and grasping, and against the action of a spring (m) connected by its ends to the locking piece (2) and to the stanchion (3), this support piece (1) axially having a mortice with lateral walls which converge from their lateral edges, the two chamfers converging in unison with those of the support piece into which it sinks progressively.
