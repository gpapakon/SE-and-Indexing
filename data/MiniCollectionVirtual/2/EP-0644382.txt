<INVENTION-TITLE>
Burner heated waterheater.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F24H148	F24H148	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F24H	F24H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F24H1	F24H1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Burner-heated water preparation means for preparing non-potable water and heating water, in which a heating-water storage means is provided which is connected to a heat exchanger, and a non-potable-water preparation means is provided. In order to achieve a high efficiency in such a water preparation means, provision is made for the heating-water storage means (10) to enclose the non-potable-water preparation means (1, 24), the non-potable-water preparation means (1, 24) being insulated from the heating-water storage means (10) by a layer of material which is a poor conductor of heat.
