Mobile apparatus for providing reclaimed abrasive blasting material.

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an apparatus for providing reclaimable abrasive blasting material having   - a unit (90) for reclaiming abrasive blasting material, comprising a cyclone pre-separator (9) for reciving abrasive substances (31), - a storage receptacle (13) for receiving the abrasive blasting material (36), - a pressurized abrasive blasting material container (15) for providing the abrasive blasting material (36) required for an abrasive blasting operation and - at least one mixing chamber (16) for distributing the abrasive blasting material (36) required.   The apparatus is characterised in that   - the cylone pre-separator (9) is connected to a cleaning and drying unit (10) and - a cleaning and a drying region (100, 200) having air-and material-permeable surface elements (101, 200) accommodated therein are arranged in cooperating fashion in the cleaning and drying unit (10). The cleaning region is arranged above the drying region (200).
