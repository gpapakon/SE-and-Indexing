<CLAIMS>
Cradle bridge with a flat roadway and upwardly-constructed side walls (SW) on the 
upper ends of which the upper booms (OG) are disposed, the bridge being divisible 

longitudinally of the roadway into any number of modules (B) of identical cross-section, 

characterized in that
 the individual bridge modules (B) can be nested for 
transportation so that a stack with a very small transportation height is produced. 
Cradle bridge according to claim 1, 
characterized in that
, in order to reduce the 
width for transportation, the side portions of the bridge can be pushed together. 
Cradle bridge according to claim 1, 
characterized in that
, in order to reduce the 
width for transportation, inner roadway plates (FI) can be folded towards the side walls 

(SW) onto the outer regions (FA) of the roadway and the side portions of the bridge 
can be pushed together. 
Cradle bridge according to any one of the preceding claims, 
characterized in that
 
each module (B) can be used as a separate bridge without the need for additional 

components. 
Cradle bridge according to any one of claims 1 to 3, 
characterized in that
 the 
individual bridge modules (B) are connected by bolts (BZ) or hammer couplings (HK). 
Cradle bridge according to any one of the preceding claims, 
characterized in that
 
transfer beam portions (VBT) or transverse beams (Q) can be housed in the upper 

bridge module of the stack. 
</CLAIMS>
