<INVENTION-TITLE>
Apparatus for nebulizing odoriferous liquids.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B05B700	B05B700	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B05B	B05B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B05B7	B05B7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an apparatus for nebulizing odoriferous liquids, which comprises a compact module enclosing an air generation system (7-13), and interchangeable cartridges (4) which contain an odoriferous liquid and which has a shape suitable for acting as a nebulizing chamber, the said cartridges incorporating a Venturi system (23) which includes a double conduit consisting of two longitudinally juxtaposed tubes: an air inlet tube (37) and a liquid inlet tube (38), the air inlet tube (37) being provided with a closure device (42) forming a horizontal air-outlet nozzle oriented towards the liquid inlet tube (38).
