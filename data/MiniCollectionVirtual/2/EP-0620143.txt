<INVENTION-TITLE>
Wiper blade for the windscreenwiper of a motor vehicle.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60S138	B60S138	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60S	B60S	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60S1	B60S1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention proposes a wiper blade (10), particularly for a motor vehicle windscreen wiper, the blade being produced by extrusion and of the type including, arranged successively along the median axis (X-X) of the transverse section of the blade, a heel (12), a clip protector (14) and a wiping part (16) in the shape of an arrow of which the tip (40) is intended to interact with a window to be wiped, and the wings (42) extend laterally towards the clip protector (14), characterised in that the transverse width (L) of the heel, the transverse width (L) of the clip protector and the transverse distance separating the ends (44) of the wings (42) are substantially equal.
