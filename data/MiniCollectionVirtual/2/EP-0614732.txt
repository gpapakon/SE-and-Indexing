<INVENTION-TITLE>
Rotational joint for a robot arm.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B25J906	B25J906	B25J1702	B25J1702	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B25J	B25J	B25J	B25J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B25J9	B25J9	B25J17	B25J17	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
An articulation for a robot arm, allowing pivoting about two axes, in which the linear actuators (5a, 5b) of the articulation are housed inside elements (1, 2) of the arm which the articulation connects. If the articulation comprises a ring (4) forming a journal cross, this ring has orthogonal grooves in which the spherical ends (7) of the rods (6) of the actuators housed in one and other element slide.
