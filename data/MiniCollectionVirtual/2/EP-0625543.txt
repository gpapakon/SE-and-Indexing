<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the preparation of polymer-bound rubber chemicals.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08F244	C08F244	C08J320	C08J322	C08K900	C08K908	C08L2100	C08L2100	C08L7500	C08L7500	C08L7504	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08F	C08F	C08J	C08J	C08K	C08K	C08L	C08L	C08L	C08L	C08L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C08F2	C08F2	C08J3	C08J3	C08K9	C08K9	C08L21	C08L21	C08L75	C08L75	C08L75	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A process for the preparation of polymer-bound rubber chemicals in which the rubber chemicals and optionally additives are finely distributed in a low-molecular-weight, polymerisable liquid, then, optionally after addition of a further component which reacts with the polymerisable liquid to form the polymer, the polymer formation is initiated, and the solid, homogeneous mixtures of rubber chemicals and polymer formed are optionally converted into a use form.
</ABSTRACT>
