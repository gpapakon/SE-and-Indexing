<INVENTION-TITLE>
Codable connector.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H01R13645	H01R13645	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H01R	H01R	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H01R13	H01R13	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A codable connector having a plug part and a socket with an insertion opening, especially for communications technology, in the case of which connector a coding plate is provided on the insertion opening, which coding plate has at least one projection, and in the case of which connector at least one recess is provided on the plug part, which recess interacts with the projection on the coding plate. Coding pins (34) which can be broken off or removed and reused are in this case assigned to the recess (32) in the plug part (31).
