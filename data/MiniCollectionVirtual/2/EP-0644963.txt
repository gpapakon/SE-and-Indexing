<INVENTION-TITLE>
MULTILAYERED PRINTING OF TEXTILE FABRICS.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B41M5035	B41M5035	D06P524	D06P524	D06Q100	D06Q104	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B41M	B41M	D06P	D06P	D06Q	D06Q	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B41M5	B41M5	D06P5	D06P5	D06Q1	D06Q1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A process is disclosed for both colour and metal-coating a textile fabric. The metal-coating is first applied, at least in zones, and is then totally or partially covered with a colour print by a thermal transfer process. Interesting new esthetic effects are thus obtained. The appearance of the colour print is modified by the underlying metal coat. Even natural fibre fabrics may be printed by this process.
</ABSTRACT>
