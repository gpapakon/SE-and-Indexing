<CLAIMS>
Cleansing apparatus, in particular for tentering a 
tentering frame comprising a textile basic fabric 

(2) from which pile threads (10) respectively fibers 
or loops project which develop a cleansing effect, 

and an attachment device (12, 12') for receiving 
said tentering frame,
characterized in that
 
along a border edge (6) on said basic material (2) an 

elongated trimming piece (4) is attached along two 
spaced apart attachment lines (6, 8) in such a way 

that the web width of said basic material (2) is 
larger between said attachment lines (6, 8) than the 

corresponding web width of said trimming piece (4) 
such that said basic material (2) is arched 

respectively bent between said attachment lines (6, 
8). 
Cleansing apparatus according to claim 1, 

characterized in
that
 said trimming piece (4) is 
sewn onto the surface of said basic fabric (2) 

which faces away from said pile threads (10). 
Cleansing apparatus according to claim 1 or 2, 

characterized in
that
 said trimming piece (4) is 
sewn onto or stitched through said basic fabric (2) 

by means of two spaced part attachment seams (6, 8), 
said one attachment seam extending along said border 

edge (6). 
Cleansing apparatus according to one of claims 1 
through 3, 
characterized in
that
 said trimming piece 
(4) and said basic fabric (2) are connected in their 

respective edges (6) in a rimmed respectively 
bordered way.  

 
Cleansing apparatus according to one of the 
preceding claims, 
characterized in
that
 said 
trimming piece (4) which is sewn on provides a 

curved cross section, and between basic fabric (2) 
and said trimming piece (4) an elongated hollow 

space (14) is formed which serves as fluid storage. 
Cleansing apparatus according to claim 5, 

characterized in
that
 the edge area of said basic 
fabric (2) is arched towards the outside. 
Cleansing apparatus according to claim 5, 

characterized in
that
 in said hollow space (14) a 
water storing material is received. 
Cleansing apparatus according to one of the claims 
1 through 4, 
characterized in
that
 said trimming 
piece (4) provides a relatively high rigidity such 

that said sewn on trimming piece (4) is arranged in 

a substantially flat way. 
Cleansing apparatus according to one of the 
preceding claims, 
characterized in
that
 on the edge 
said trimming piece (4) is attached on the face of 

said basic fabric (2) which provides said pile 
threads. 
Cleansing apparatus according to claim 7, 

characterized in
that
 said water storing material 
substantially fills out the hollow space. 
</CLAIMS>
