<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process and device for separating water and solids especially for producing reusable sand.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B03B900	B03B900	B09C100	B09C102	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B03B	B03B	B09C	B09C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a process and device for separating water and solids, especially for producing reusable sand. In order to minimise the disposal costs and reduce the banked-up volume, it is provided according to the invention that sand, for example introduced from a sand trap (5) into a receiver sump (1), is transferred by means of a first conveying device (8) after the supply of water into a screening device (2) and is separated from large solid bodies. The remaining water-solids mixture is fed via a second conveying device (10) to a hydro-cyclone (3) and to a fluidized-bed sorter (4). The remaining separation of the sand from the water takes place by means of a dewatering device (11).
