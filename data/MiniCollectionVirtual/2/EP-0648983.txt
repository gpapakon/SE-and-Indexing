Cross-piece for the fastening of an air outlet

<ABSTRACT>
In the case of a beam for the fastening of an air outlet to a housing (1) by means of a fastening element (7), the beam (4, 4.1) is to consist of a tube which has a receptacle (6, 11) for the fastening element (7).

<CLAIMS>
Cross-piece for the fastening of an air outlet on lateral walls (2, 3)
of a housing (1) by means of a securing member (7), which is inserted into

a receiving means (6, 11) on or in the cross-piece (4, 4.1), characterised in
that the cross-piece (4, 4.1) comprises a tube which is open-ended, and

stoppers (5) are inserted into the openings through the lateral walls (2, 3)
of the housing (1).
Cross-piece according to claim 1, characterised in that the tube has
an oval cross-section.
Cross-piece according to claim 1 or 2, characterised in that a sleeve
(6), having an internal thread (9), is inserted in the cross-piece (4) for

accommodating the securing member (7).
Cross-piece according to claim 3, characterised in that the sleeve
(6) abuts against the tube with an annular flange (10).
Cross-piece according to claim 1 or 2, characterised in that a holder
(11) is slipped over the cross-piece (4.1), the cross-piece (4.1) penetrating

a recess (12) in the holder. 
Cross-piece according to claim 5, characterised in that a
longitudinal bore (13) for accommodating th
e securing member (7) is
provided alongside the recess (12).
Cross-piece according to claim 6, characterised in that the
longitudinal bore (13) widens-out in a funnel-shaped manner at both ends.
