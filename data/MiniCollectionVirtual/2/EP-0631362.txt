<INVENTION-TITLE>
Reactive power-compensating arrangement.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H02J318	H02J318	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H02J	H02J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H02J3	H02J3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In order to compensate for the self-inductive reactive energy of a generator installation (21), capacitors (22) are connected to the mains circuit (16) via controlled switches (20). Control of this device is exercised by measurement and control means (17) which react to the apparent power or to the current.
