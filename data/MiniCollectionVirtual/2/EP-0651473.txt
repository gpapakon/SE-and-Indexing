<INVENTION-TITLE>
Slip ring or commutator motor.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H01R3900	H01R3916	H01R4306	H01R4308	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H01R	H01R	H01R	H01R	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H01R39	H01R39	H01R43	H01R43	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Wollastonite (CaSiO3) with a structure in the form of needles is added as a filler to the phenol resin moulding compound for the supporting body of the sliprings and of the commutator laminates in order to produce an asbestos-free slipring arrangement and commutator arrangement.
</ABSTRACT>
