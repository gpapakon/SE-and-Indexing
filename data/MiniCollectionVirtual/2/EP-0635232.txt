<INVENTION-TITLE>
Support system for bath tub or shower tray.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47K302	A47K302	A47K316	A47K316	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47K	A47K	A47K	A47K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47K3	A47K3	A47K3	A47K3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A support system for a bath tub or shower tray is described, consisting of a base plate having a central region and an acoustically uncoupled edge region and side walls supported on this edge region, which side walls for their part, in turn, support the edge of the tub or tray, base plate and side walls preferably being embodied as sandwich constructions and the space between at least two spaced layers being totally or partially filled by a highly flexible material.
