<INVENTION-TITLE>
Switching apparatus for the brakes of a railway vehicle
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B61H1330	B61H900	B61H1300	B61H900	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B61H	B61H	B61H	B61H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
A fitting apparatus for the brakes of a railway vehicle is described, having on each longitudinal side of the rail vehicle a pivot lever (1, 2) which is to be actuated in the same direction by a person facing the vehicle, the pivot levers (1, 2) being connected to one another and to the actuator (3) by means of flexible cables (4) which are led in a longitudinally displaceable fashion in hoses (5) or tubes.

<CLAIMS>
A switching apparatus for the brakes of a railway vehicle, 
having a respective pivot lever at each longitudinal side of the rail 

vehicle, which is to he actuated in the same direction by a person facing 
towards the vehicle, wherein the two pivot levers are connected to each 

other and to a control member in respect of movement thereof, 
characterised in that the pivot levers (1, 2) are connected to each other 

and to the control member (3) by flexible cables (4) which are 
longitudinally slidably guided in tubes (5) or pipes. 
A switching apparatus according to claim 1 characterised in that 
the pivot levers (1, 2) and the control member (3) are connected to gears 

(6) into which the cables (4) engage with a tooth arrangement (7). 
</CLAIMS>
