<INVENTION-TITLE>
PROCESS FOR MANUFACTURING TUBULAR BODIES FOR PACKAGING TUBES.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B29C5300	B29C5350	B29C6518	B29C6518	B29L2300	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B29C	B29C	B29C	B29C	B29L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B29C53	B29C53	B29C65	B29C65	B29L23	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Tubular bodies for packaging tubes must satisfy particular esthetic requirements. Extruded tubes satisfy such requirements better than tubes made of wound foils with a soldering seam. A process is disclosed for manufacturing a tube made of wound foils whose esthetic characteristics largely correspond to those of an extruded tube.
</ABSTRACT>
