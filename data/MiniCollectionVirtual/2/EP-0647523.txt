<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a laminated composite sheet, in particular for the production of passive electronic components of a multilayered structure, to a multicolour screenprint process for its production and to the use of said sheet for producing passive electronic components, the layer thickness of the laminated composite sheet being thin and uniform, the layer structure being improved and the production process being simplified. According to the invention, a laminated composite sheet is proposed which comprises a green ceramic substrate sheet, inner electrodes applied by screen printing and one or more compensating layers applied over the entire surface of the green ceramic substrate sheet and the inner electrodes by screen printing. The inner electrodes and the compensating layer or layers are applied one after the other to the green ceramic substrate sheet by screen printing.
