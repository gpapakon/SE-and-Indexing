<INVENTION-TITLE>
Automobile antenna.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H01Q100	H01Q100	H01Q132	H01Q132	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
Antenna for a motor vehicle including an antenna rod (2) having a metal conductor (8) and an external coating (6), and bearing, externally, over at least a part of its length, a connection means (7) extending helically about the longitudinal axis of the rod (2); the connection means (7) consists of the helically twisted outer surface of the outer coating (6).
