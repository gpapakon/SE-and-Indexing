<INVENTION-TITLE>
MOTORIZED CYCLE.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B62M1300	B62M1300	B62M2302	B62M2302	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B62M	B62M	B62M	B62M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B62M13	B62M13	B62M23	B62M23	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Cycle with at least two wheels, such as a bicycle, and fitted with a motor (12) on the frame (1) for driving a wheel (2) with which it is capable of being selectively coupled and uncoupled. According to the invention, the motor (12) is an electric motor using preferably direct current, supplied by storage batteries mounted on the cycle, for example in a case attached to the rack (10). Application to any kind of cycle or motorcycle with two or more wheels.
</ABSTRACT>
