<INVENTION-TITLE>
Use of soaps as antiageing agents in cosmetic and/or dermatological compositions.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The subject of the invention is the use of a soap formed of at least one alkali metal salt of saturated or unsaturated fatty acids as an agent for combating the signs of ageing of the skin from a cosmetic and/or dermatological point of view.
</ABSTRACT>
