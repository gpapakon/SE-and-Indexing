<INVENTION-TITLE>
Radiating lamp.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a radiating lamp (radiation lamp) having a radiator (1) of tubular shape which has electric contact elements, at least one end of the radiator being closed off by pinching and being surrounded in the region of the pinch (2) at least partly by a cap sleeve (3) of plastic. In order to specify a radiating lamp having a tubular radiator whose cap can be mounted very quickly and reliably and in the case of which a secure and reliable connection is ensured between the radiator and the cap in the entire region of use of the radiating lamp, the cap sleeve and the radiator have latching elements (6) which are in mutual engagement.
