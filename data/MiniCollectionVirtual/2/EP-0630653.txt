<INVENTION-TITLE>
Composition for promoting the formation and growth of bone marrow cells.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61K9127	A61K9127	A61L2700	A61L2714	A61L2750	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61K	A61K	A61L	A61L	A61L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61K9	A61K9	A61L27	A61L27	A61L27	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Composition for promoting the formation and development of bone cells. It contains deoxyribonucleic acid (DNA), to which are bound active carriers modulating the phenotypic expression of bone cells, as well as a physiologically acceptable amount of a pH stabiliser. The active modulatory carriers are chosen from tocopherols, carotenoids and xanthophylls.
</ABSTRACT>
