<INVENTION-TITLE>
Packaging insert for packaging chocolates or the like.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65D550	B65D550	B65D8560	B65D8560	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65D	B65D	B65D	B65D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65D5	B65D5	B65D85	B65D85	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A packaging insert made of cardboard for chocolate packaging and the like, designed as flat lidded boxes, has an insert base (1) in which a plurality of cutouts (3) are stamped, which each accommodate a nest (2) with a nest bottom (7) made of cardboard and recessed relative to the insert base (1). Each nest (2) is formed by a separate nest insert (6) which is made of cardboard and has the nest bottom (7) and nest walls (6) attached thereto. The nest walls (8) are fixed to the edge (4) of the cutout (3). Folded down from the edge (4) of each cutout (3) in the insert base (1) are a plurality of adhesive tabs (5). The adhesive tabs (5) of the insert base (1) are bonded to the nest walls (8).
