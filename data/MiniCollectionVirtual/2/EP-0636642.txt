<ABSTRACT>
Organosiloxanes containing at least one chemically bonded transition-metal complex of Pt, Pd, Rh, Ru, Os or Ir with a triazene, tetrazene, tetrazadiene, pentazadiene or triazene oxide ligand, are hydrosilylation catalysts which can be activated by light and heat.
</ABSTRACT>
