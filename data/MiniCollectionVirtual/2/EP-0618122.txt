Boarding device for a chair lift

<ABSTRACT>
A boarding device for a chair lift comprises a rolling belt (walkway) which brings the skiers to the boarding location where they are collected in movement by the seat. The rolling belt has two end drums 17 whose external perimeter bulges in the form of a barrel in order to centre the rolling belt and is provided with cavities 28 preventing the build-up of snow or ice.

<CLAIMS>
Loading device for a chairlift having a continuously moving 
cable (10), fixed grips (12) of the chairs (11) to the cable 

and a passenger (14) loading area (13) extending in the cable 
moving direction, which device comprises an endless belt (16), 

located on the loading area and driven at a speed less than the 
cable speed for carrying passengers, ready for embarkment and 

standing onto the belt, in the chair (11) moving direction at a 
speed less than the speed of the chair, which takes up and 

catches up the passengers, said endless belt (16) having two 
drums (17) with horizontal axes, located on the two ends of the 

belt and an endless flexible belt extending and driven by the 
two drums, characterized in that at least one of said drums 

(17) has a barrel like light cambered shape to centralize the 
belt on the drum in the cable (10) axis, and that the outer 

surface of the drum has holes (28) for housing the snow or the 
ice inserted between the drum and the belt. 
Loading device according to claim 1, characterized in that 
said holes comprise longitudinal grooves ((28) provided in the 

outer drum surface . (Fig. 2) 
Loading device according to claim 1 or 2, characterized in 
that said holes constitute a lattice shaped net. (Fig. 5) 
Loading device according to claim 1, 2 or 3, characterized 
in that the drum (17) is hollow and that the holes (28) are in 

communication with the inner drum space. 
Loading device according to claim 1, 2, 3 or 4, 
characterized in that the drum (17), in two parts, comprises a 

cylindrical core and a sleeve secured onto the core and 
providing said holes. 
Loading device according to any one of the preceding claims, 
characterized in that the holes are constituted by radial fins 

(29) spaced around the outer drum surface.  
 
Loading device according to any one of the preceding claims, 
characterized in that the drum is squirrel cage shaped. (Fig.4) 
Loading device according to any one of the preceding claims, 
characterized in that the drum is a perforated metal sheet. 

(Fig.6)
