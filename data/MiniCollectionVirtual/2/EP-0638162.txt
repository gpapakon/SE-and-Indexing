<INVENTION-TITLE>
MONOCHROMATOR.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01J300	G01J306	G01J312	G01J318	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01J	G01J	G01J	G01J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01J3	G01J3	G01J3	G01J3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A monochromator for examining very narrow band ranges of a light spectrum has a fixed inlet slot, a grid illuminated by said slot and rotatively driven by a grid drive, a fixed outlet slot arranged in the angular area of the generated spectrum and at least one cut-off filter arranged in the path of the rays, continuously and swivellingly driven by the grid drive transversely to the path of the rays for filtering out light of orders other than the observed order. The cut-off filter is coupled to the grid drive in such a way that it is synchronically swivelled over the same angle as the grid, covering the desired spectral range.
</ABSTRACT>
