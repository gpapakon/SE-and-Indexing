<INVENTION-TITLE>
Drawer with double extensible guide.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47B8804	A47B8810	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47B	A47B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47B88	A47B88	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In a drawer with a double extensible guide, having a fixed carcass rail (15), a middle rail (10) and a drawer rail (4), the middle rail (10) being guided on the carcass rail (15) and the drawer rail (4) being guided on the middle rail (10), and blocks (16) being provided to limit the extension lengths of the drawer rail (4) from the middle rail (10) and of the middle rail (10) from the carcass rail (15), the drawer rail (4) is designed as a flush-mounted rail and the carcass rail (15) can be mounted on the carcass (2) next to the drawer (3), the middle rail (10) extending in its width from the drawer rail (4) to the carcass rail (15) having, in the region located below the drawer (3), the counter guides (9) for the drawer rail and, in the region projecting laterally beyond the drawer, the counter guides (13) for the carcass rail (15).
