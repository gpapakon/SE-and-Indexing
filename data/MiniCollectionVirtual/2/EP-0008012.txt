<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for the production of hollow articles.

<ABSTRACT>
In the production of hollow articles from castable plastics, an elastic bellows filled with free-flowing particles is placed into the mould (8) as core (7). The core is made solid either within the mould or beforehand within a separate core mould by whirling or evacuating. If the particles are removed from the bellows, it is easy to withdraw the bellows from the hollow article, even if undercuts are present. This process is preferably used for producing pneumatic tyres.
