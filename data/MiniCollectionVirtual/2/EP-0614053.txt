<INVENTION-TITLE>
Installation and mounting of large scale kitchens
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F24C1530	F24C1508	F24C1508	F24C1500	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F24C	F24C	F24C	F24C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F24C15	F24C15	F24C15	F24C15	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
An installation and mounting device for fastening large-scale kitchen appliances has a horizontal appliance support running at a distance from the kitchen floor and containing an inner space extending through it. The appliance support is provided with openings, through which the installation lines running in the inner space of the appliance support can be guided to the attached large-scale kitchen appliances.

<CLAIMS>
Installation and mounting device for fastening canteen kitchen 
appliances (10, 11) to a horizontal appliance rack (20), which 

extends at a distance from the kitchen floor and through which an 
interior space (21) extends,
characterized in
 
that the appliance rack (20) is provided with openings (25), through 

which cables and conduits extending in the interior space (21) of the 
appliance rack (20) may be run up to the canteen kitchen appliances 

(10, 11). 
Installation and mounting device according to claim 1,
characterized in
 
that the appliance rack (20) is fashioned from high-quality sheet 

steel. 
Installation and mounting device according to claim 1 or 2,
characterized in
 
that the openings (25) are slightly smaller than the dimensions of 

the rear panel of the canteen kitchen appliances (10, 11). 
Installation and mounting device according to one of the preceding 
claims,
characterized in
 
that the openings (25) have substantially rectangular dimensions. 
Installation and mounting device according to one of the preceding 
claims,
characterized in
 
that the appliance rack (20) has a substantially rectangular cross 

section. 
Installation and mounting device according to one of the preceding 
claims,
characterized in
 
that the appliance rack (20) at its top edges has longitudinally  

 
extending, upwardly directed projections (27, 28), which are. 

overlapped and held by holding devices (13, 14) of the canteen 
kitchen appliances (10, 11). 
Installation and mounting device according to one of the preceding 
claims,
characterized in
 
that supports (41, 42) are provided at a distance from the 

longitudinal ends of the appliance rack (20). 
Installation and mounting device according to one of the preceding 
claims,
characterized in
 
that supporting surfaces (29) are provided in the bottom side region 

of the appliance rack (20), and that adjusting screws (17) extended 
through the canteen kitchen appliances (10, 11) are supported on the 

supporting surfaces (29). 
</CLAIMS>
