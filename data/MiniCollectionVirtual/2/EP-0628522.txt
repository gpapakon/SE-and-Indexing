<INVENTION-TITLE>
Concrete for road surfaces and process for preparation thereof
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
A concrete for carriageway coverings according to the 'ZTV Beton-StB 91' regulations consists of at least 340 kg/cu.m. cement and aggregates with a particle compsn. according to DIN 1045 and at least one essential particle group of above 8 mm, the novelty being that at least one of the essential particle groups of above 8 mm consists of concrete granulate recovered from old carriageway coverings. Also claimed is a process for reusing old carriageway coverings of concrete.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
Concrete for one-layered roadway platforms according to 
German regulation ZTV Beton-StB 91, Bauklasse SV, consisting 

of at least 340 kg/m
3
 of cement, and aggregates having 
a grain composition according to DIN 1045 and at least one 

required grain group of larger than 8 mm, characterized in 
that at least one of the required grain groups of larger 

than 8 mm consists of concrete granules obtained from 
detached and processed old roadway platform. 
A method for reusing old, detached and processed roadway 
platforms made of concrete, characterized in that the grain 

group of larger than 8 mm is used as an aggregate for the 
preparation of a one-layered new roadway platform made of 

concrete according to German regulation ZTV Beton-StB 91, 
Bauklasse SV, consisting of at least 340 kg/m
3
 of cement, 
and aggregates having a grain composition according to DIN 

1045 and at least one grain group of larger than 8 mm. 
The method according to claim 2, characterized in that 
prior to disrupting the old roadway, the present joints are 

ploughed out and wooden components are selected out. 
</CLAIMS>
