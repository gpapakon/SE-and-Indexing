<INVENTION-TITLE>
OUTLET DEVICE FOR A TOILET BOWL WITH A SIPHON TRAP
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E03D5012	E03D500	E03D1102	E03D1108	E03D1110	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
A device comprising a movable sealing flap (3) arranged between the toilet bowl (1) and the siphon trap (2) substantially in the plane (P) of the level of the water (E) in the siphon trap (2), and a pressurized water supply (5) located immediately beneath the flap (3). The device may be used to reduce the amount of water used in toilet flushing.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
Disposal device for a lavatory pan equipped with 
a water trap comprising a moveable closure shutter (3, 

7), characterized in that the shutter is arranged between 
the pan and the water trap, substantially in the plane 

(P) corresponding to the equilibrium water level in the 
water trap (2), and a pressurized-water supply means (5) 

just below the shutter (3, 7). 
Device according to Claim 1, characterized in 
that it includes a water-intake command means (4), such 

as a needle valve, slaved to the closing of the shutter 
(3, 7). 
Device according to one of Claims 1 or 2, characterized 
in that it includes voluntary command means (6) 

permitting actuation of the shutter (3, 7) using the hand 
or the foot. 
Device according to one of Claims 1 to 3, characterized 
in that the shutter (7) is a hollow component of 

flat shape, the lower face of which has a series of holes 
(72), and in that the water intake is via the inside of 

the shutter such that the water is injected into the 
water trap through these holes (72). 
Device according to one of Claims 1 to 4, characterized 
in that it includes additional means (80) for 

supplying water to the bottom part of the water trap (2). 
</CLAIMS>
