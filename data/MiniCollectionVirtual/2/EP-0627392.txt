<INVENTION-TITLE>
Chemical etching method for rocky substrates.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C04B4153	C04B4153	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C04B	C04B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C04B41	C04B41	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Process for chemical engraving on rocky substrates, where the reserves are printed on the support using a resin which resists chemical baths, or are treated photomechanically by starting with a photosensitive gelatin (applied onto the support beforehand) which resists chemical baths. Applications:     - decorative floor and wall coverings   - furniture   - town and professional sign panels   - commemorative monuments   - grave steles   - tombstones   - trophies - advertising and the like. or
</ABSTRACT>
