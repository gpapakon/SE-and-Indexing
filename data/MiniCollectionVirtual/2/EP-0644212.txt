<INVENTION-TITLE>
Chloroprene copolymers.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08F2200	C08F2236	C08F22000	C08F22004	C08F22200	C08F22202	C08F22240	C08F23600	C08F23618	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08F	C08F	C08F	C08F	C08F	C08F	C08F	C08F	C08F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C08F22	C08F22	C08F220	C08F220	C08F222	C08F222	C08F222	C08F236	C08F236	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Chloroprene copolymers containing from 1 to 40 % by weight of a copolymerised unsaturated carboximide, unsaturated carboxylic anhydride and/or unsaturated carboxylic acid have virtually no gel content and are suitable for the preparation of vulcanisates having improved thermal stability.
</ABSTRACT>
