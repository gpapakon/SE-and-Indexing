<INVENTION-TITLE>
Leadframe for integrated circuits.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H01L2348	H01L2348	H01L23495	H01L2350	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H01L	H01L	H01L	H01L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H01L23	H01L23	H01L23	H01L23	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Lead frames completely plated with palladium which have a nickel-phosphorus or a copper-tin alloy layer between the base body consisting, for example, of copper, [lacuna] can be bonded well and can be soldered well both before and after aging without previous tinning.
</ABSTRACT>
