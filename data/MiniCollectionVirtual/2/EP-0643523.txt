<INVENTION-TITLE>
Interactive command system for a multiservice station.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H04M1108	H04M1108	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H04M	H04M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H04M11	H04M11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
System allowing interactive control of application(s) by users in a multiservice installation making use of a telephone exchange (1) of a server for televisual programmes (4) and of user modules (6) each associating at least one telephone (2) and a television set (5), the server being equipped with means (12, 13) for allowing transmission of an interactive televisual programme, supplied by an operating assembly (11). The latter is linked to the telephone exchange and makes use of means (16, 17) for producing control commands from information sent by the exchange on reception of coded signals sent by the control device (18) of a module telephone, in connection with interactive televisual programme information displayed by the television set, associated with this telephone in the same module, upon the sending of the coded signals by the telephone in question.
