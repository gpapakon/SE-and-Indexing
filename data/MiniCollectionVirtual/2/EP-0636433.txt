<INVENTION-TITLE>
Straightening machine for rod- or striplike material.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B21D300	B21D305	B21F100	B21F102	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B21D	B21D	B21F	B21F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B21D3	B21D3	B21F1	B21F1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A straightening machine for straightening rod- or strip-like material (7) which has defects such as thickness and diameter fluctuations, twisted ovalities, profiles and ribs consists of at least one self-regulating system of straightening tools (1 to 3, 8, 9) in which a movably mounted straightening tool (8) subjected to force is supported on a fixed straightening tool (9) via the straightening stock and adjusts its straightening position automatically in accordance with the changing diameter of the straightening stock.
