<INVENTION-TITLE>
Cake-container, preferably made of carton.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65D500	B65D500	B65D8530	B65D8536	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65D	B65D	B65D	B65D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65D5	B65D5	B65D85	B65D85	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A container for receiving cakes or the like, made of a blank of foldable flat material, such as card, corrugated cardboard or the like, having a bottom wall (1), side walls (2, 3) linked to the bottom wall, and corner-connecting tabs (4) which are linked laterally to the side walls (2), the side walls (2, 3) being erected relative to the bottom wall (1), and the corner-connecting tabs (4) bearing against adjacent side walls (3), the corner-connecting tabs (4) having attached corner reinforcement tabs (8), and the corner reinforcement tabs connecting their corner-connecting tabs to the side walls (2) linked thereto at a distance from their attachment.
