<INVENTION-TITLE>
Composite packaging film and packaging pouch comprising such a film.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65D3302	B65D3302	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65D	B65D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65D33	B65D33	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
This composite sheet comprises at least two films (6, 7) adhesively bonded to each other. In at least one reinforcement region (8) of the sheet, the two films (6, 7) are adhesively bonded to each other only over a fraction (16) of the area of the reinforcement region, this fraction being distributed over the entire extent of this region. Application to composite sheets, made up of film that is easily tearable and film that is difficult to tear, intended for the packaging of foodstuffs.
