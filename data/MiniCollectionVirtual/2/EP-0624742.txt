<INVENTION-TITLE>
Shaft passage sealing with a barrier fluid.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Defective shaft passages (glands), for example those of liquid pumps, in which between two shaft seals (36, 37) there is arranged a chamber (38) containing a barrier fluid and connected to a reservoir (40), can be detected immediately by the triggering of an alarm. To this end, the surface (44) of the barrier fluid (41) in the reservoir (40), which is set to a nominal level, is covered, preferably with the interposition of a movable, hermetically sealing diaphragm (54), by a piston (46) which, when the level of fluid changes, acts on a signal transmitter (42) arranged outside the reservoir (10).
