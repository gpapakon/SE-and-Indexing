<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for providing high-precision volumetric flow at low flow rate and apparatus therefor.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B01L1100	B01L1100	F04B1100	F04B1100	G05D706	G05D706	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B01L	B01L	F04B	F04B	G05D	G05D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B01L11	B01L11	F04B11	F04B11	G05D7	G05D7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method and an apparatus for providing high-precision volumetric flows at low flow rate. It is the object of the invention to make available a method and an apparatus with which a continuous volumetric flow can be provided. This object is achieved by means of two parallel piston pumps which can be charged separately from each other via two valves, by means of two valves which allow the alternating charging of a common output conduit by the two piston pumps, and by means of two differential pressure meters for detecting the differential pressure across the two valves.
</ABSTRACT>
