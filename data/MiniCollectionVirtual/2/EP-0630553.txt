<INVENTION-TITLE>
Implement drive for a vehicle-implement combination.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A01B7100	A01B7106	A01D3463	A01D3464	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A01B	A01B	A01D	A01D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A01B71	A01B71	A01D34	A01D34	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In an implement-drive device for a vehicle/implement combination, the vehicle having at least one drivable running wheel and an engine (38) with a crankshaft (18) projecting towards the ground, and the implement being connected to the vehicle so as to be vertically movable and having at least one belt pulley (58) which can be driven from the crankshaft (18) via a single belt (62), an extension (40) having a drive pulley (44) driving the single belt (62) can be connected to the end of the crankshaft (18) projecting towards the ground.
