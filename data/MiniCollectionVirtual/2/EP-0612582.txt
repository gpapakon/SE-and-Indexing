<INVENTION-TITLE>
Linear transfer system.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B23Q4102	B23Q4102	B65G3700	B65G3702	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B23Q	B23Q	B65G	B65G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B23Q41	B23Q41	B65G37	B65G37	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In a linear transfer system with a circulating system (1) consisting of at least one rail track for guiding a work carrier (2) to at least one machining station (3), the machining station (3) is arranged at a by-pass (4) which is connected via switches (5, 6; 5a, 6a) to the circulating system (1). Here, a second by-pass (8) for guiding a material carrier (7) to the machining station (y) is to be allocated to the by-pass (4) at the machining station (3).
