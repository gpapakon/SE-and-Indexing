<ABSTRACT>
The positioning system uses a positioning pin (3) incorporated in each balancing weight (17) fitting into the material of the roller casing inside wall which is made of a softer material than that of the positioning pin. The positioning pin pref. has a pointed end (4) which penetrates the casing wall, upon application of pressure to the balancing weight (17) via a spring (19) associated with a radial fixing rod of an internal support structure (18).
</ABSTRACT>
