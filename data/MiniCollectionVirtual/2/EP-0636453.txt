<INVENTION-TITLE>
Speed protecting circuit for a weight balancing piston.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B23Q500	B23Q558	B23Q1100	B23Q1100	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B23Q	B23Q	B23Q	B23Q	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B23Q5	B23Q5	B23Q11	B23Q11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
- The subject of the invention relates to a safety device for a load-balancing ram (4). The device according to the invention includes:   . a system (13) designed to gain an appreciation of the speed of travel of the piston rod (5) of the ram, . and a means (14) able to control the operation of an item of equipment (15) for immobilising the rod of the ram when the speed of the rod as detected by the system (13) exceeds a predetermined critical value.
