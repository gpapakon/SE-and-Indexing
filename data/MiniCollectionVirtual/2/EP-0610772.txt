<INVENTION-TITLE>
Cassette for information medium, especially a magnetic tape, and a supplementary part therefor
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G11B23087	G11B23087	G11B2350	G11B2350	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G11B	G11B	G11B	G11B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G11B23	G11B23	G11B23	G11B23	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A cassette for recording carriers in tape form is provided in the vicinity of the tape running path with cleaning elements for the tape, it being possible to fit on the cassette casing supplementary casing parts which are provided on the side facing the tape with at least one cleaning element. The supplementary parts may be designed as a separate front part, including the entry opening for the scanning element, or as closure wall parts for specific insertion openings in the casing. Such separate cleaning elements can be used for all types of film or magnetic-tape cassettes.
