<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a cosmetic composition for the first stage of an operation of permanent-reshaping of hair, containing an N-(mercaptoalkyl)alkanediamide or one of its cosmetically acceptable salts as a reducing agent, and to its use in a process of permanent-reshaping of hair. It also relates to new N-(mercaptoalkyl)alkanediamide disulphides as well as to new N-(mercaptoalkyl)alkanediamides.
</ABSTRACT>
