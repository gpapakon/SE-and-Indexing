Opalescent glass

<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61K602	C03C3076	C03C400	A61K606	C03C3097	C03C400	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an opalescent glass which is distinguished by a continuous glass phase and a discontinuous glass phase distributed therein and which can be employed as a dental material or a constituent of dental materials.
</ABSTRACT>

Process for producing opalescent glass according
to any of Claims 1 to 3, characterized in that a glass

containing the components according to any of Claims 1 to
3 is produced and the glass produced is, if desired,

subjected to a heat treatment in the temperature range
from 950 to 1100°C.
Process according to Claim 4, characterized in
that the heat treatment is carried out for from 15

minutes to 4 hours.
Process according to Claim 4 or 5, characterized
in that the glass produced is granulated and the

granulated material is sintered at temperatures of up to
1100°C.
Process according to Claim 6, characterized in 
that the sintering is effected by heating the granulated

material to from 880 to 1070°C.
Use of the opalescent glass according to any of
Claims 1 to 3 as dental material or as additive to or

constituent of dental material.
Use according to Claim 8, wherein the opalescent
glass is used as additive to silicate glasses or silicate

glass-ceramics.
Use according to Claim 8 or 9, wherein the
opalescent glass is a constituent of inlays, onlays,

bridges or crowns.
