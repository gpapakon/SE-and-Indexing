<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for driving an electromagnetic consumer.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F02D4120	F02D4120	F02M5100	F02M5100	H01F708	H01F716	H01F718	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F02D	F02D	F02M	F02M	H01F	H01F	H01F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F02D41	F02D41	F02M51	F02M51	H01F7	H01F7	H01F7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A description is given of a device for driving an electromagnetic consumer. It comprises a series connection of the electromagnetic consumer and a first switching means, a freewheeling circuit, comprising a second switching means, for the electromagnetic consumer, and driving means for actuating the switching means. The second switching means can be switched by means of a current source.
