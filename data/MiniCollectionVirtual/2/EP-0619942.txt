<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for producing hard and extra-hard cheese.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A01J2500	A01J2512	A01J2515	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A01J	A01J	A01J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
In a system for producing hard and extra-hard cheese, a plurality of pressing devices are accommodated in a common trough (4.1) with a lid (5.1). The lid has a built-in cleaning system for cleaning the interior of the trough.
