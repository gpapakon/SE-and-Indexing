<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method of selectively bundling, marking and isolating elongated objects by means of a one-sided adhesive tape and a device for carrying out the method.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Device 1 for selectively bundling, marking and isolating elongate objects 2 by means of a one-sided adhesive tape 3, there being provided, according to the invention, at least one first and one second roller 4, 5 which are arranged in parallel and are designed to be adjustable in parallel relative to one another and which are jointly surrounded by a flat belt 6 on the casing side in the manner of a cigarette roller, the flat belt 6 being designed to be drivable.
