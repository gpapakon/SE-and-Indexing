<INVENTION-TITLE>
Blotting pad forming a support for a drawer to be used for storing a computer keyboard.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47B2100	A47B2103	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47B	A47B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47B21	A47B21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a support for a drawer designed to be adapted to a table top (4), such as a desk top. The shape of this support is generally that of a clamp enabling it to be fitted over the thickness of the desk top. The support comprises:   . a rigid plate (5) forming a blotter and intended to lie on the top face (41) of the desk top, . a frame (7) for supporting the drawer (2) and connected to the rigid plate (5) but a distance away from it and opposite it, the frame (7) being intended to be installed underneath the desk top, . and a clamp system (11) attached to the frame (7) and capable of being moved towards or away from the plate (5) to enable the support to be secured to the desk top.
