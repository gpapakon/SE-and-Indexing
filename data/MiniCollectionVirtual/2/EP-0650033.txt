<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method and apparatus for monitoring the functioning of a sensor.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F02D4122	F02D4122	G01D1800	G01D1800	G01D2100	G01D2100	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F02D	F02D	G01D	G01D	G01D	G01D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F02D41	F02D41	G01D18	G01D18	G01D21	G01D21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A method and a device for monitoring the operation of a sensor, particularly a sensor for detecting a measurement value in a motor vehicle, is described. When predetermined operating states are present, a measurement value is acquired and compared with a reference value. Errors are indicated when a measurement value provided by the sensor deviates from a reference value by more than a tolerance value. A measurement value acquired and stored at an earlier time, at which a corresponding operating state was present, is used as reference value.
