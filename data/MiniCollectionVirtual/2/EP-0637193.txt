<INVENTION-TITLE>
Connecting piece for the supply terminal of a cooking plate.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H05B368	H05B368	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H05B	H05B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H05B3	H05B3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A connecting piece for electrically connecting, in particular, cooking plates contains at least one perforation, open on at least one end face, as well as at least one screw-type connecting terminal. Said terminal has a metallic sleeve (10), is arranged in the perforation and has a screw (11) which is radially screwed into the sleeve (10). The screw head (12) is accessible from outside and contains a cylindrical screw-drive depression and a continuous screw-drive slot (14).
