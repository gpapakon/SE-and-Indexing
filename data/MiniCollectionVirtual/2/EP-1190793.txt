<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for treating the end sections of work pieces
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B23B500	B23B516	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B23B	B23B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B23B5	B23B5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The machining of the end sections of components (2) is carried out using a rotary tool holder that has a stator (1) and a rotor (4) with a built in electrical motor drive (5,7,8). The cutting tools (3) are mounted on a combined sensor and drive (12,170) that can be moved radially using commands from the controller.
</ABSTRACT>
