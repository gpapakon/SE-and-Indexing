<INVENTION-TITLE>
Paper sheet for abrasion-resistant laminates.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Surface paper sheet employed for manufacture of abrasion-resistant laminates comprising glass wool. According to the invention, when it is not impregnated with heat-curable resin, the sheet comprises up to approximately 7 %, on dry weight basis, of glass wool relative to its total dry weight. Application as covering protective sheet known as "overlay" sheet and decorative sheet.
</ABSTRACT>
