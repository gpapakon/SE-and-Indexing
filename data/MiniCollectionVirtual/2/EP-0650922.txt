<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the catalytic removal of CO from a H2 rich gas.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C01B300	C01B358	H01M806	H01M806	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C01B	C01B	H01M	H01M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C01B3	C01B3	H01M8	H01M8	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a multistage process for removing carbon monoxide in hydrogen-rich gas by an oxidation or methanisation of the CO over selective oxidation or methanisation catalysts containing noble metals with feeding in of air.
