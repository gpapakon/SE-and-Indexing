Location body for a deposit lock of a shopping carriage

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a receiving body (1) for a deposit lock of a shopping or luggage trolley, the receiving body being fastened to the trolley handle and the deposit lock being located in the cavity of the said receiving body, there projecting on each of the two sides of the receiving body a bolt (12,13) which corresponds in its cross-sectional shape and diameter to the cross-sectional shape and diameter of the cavity of the attachable handle profile.

<CLAIMS>
A location body (1) for a deposit lock (7) of a shopping or
baggage trolley, wherein the location body (1) is attached to the

trolley handle and contains the location lock in its cavity (6),
characterized in that while on each of the sides of the location

body (1) a pin (12, 13) projects whose cross-sectional shape and
diameter correspond to the cross-sectional shape and diameter of

the cavity of the handle profile which can be fitted on.
A location body according to claim 1, characterized in that
the pin (12, 13) is formed with at least one slot (14) which

terminates on the side of the head of the pin and can be widened
up by an expansion member.
A location body according to claim 1, characterized in that
the pin (12, 13) has an elongate cavity (15), which can be

widened up by the conical expansion member.
A location body according to claims 2 or 3, characterized in
that the expansion member can be screwed or knocked through the

cavity of the handle profile.
A location body according to one of the preceding claims,
characterized in that screwed into the pin (12, 13) is a screw

which at the same time extends through and retains that attaching
member which attaches the handle profile to the supporting arm of

the trolley.
</CLAIMS>
