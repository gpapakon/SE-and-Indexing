<ABSTRACT>
A crucible (12), esp. a support crucible for a quartz crucible for drawing silicon single crystals, consists of a base portion (18) and a sidewall portion (20) which are detachably assembled and which consist of different materials, the sidewall material not being prone to silicon carbide formation.
</ABSTRACT>
