Identification system for marking animals or objects

<ABSTRACT>
In order, in the marking of animals or objects with an identification system, consisting of a shaft 2 which can be inserted, with both ends exposed, into a hole in an animal or an object 12, a marking element 1 and a securing element 13, 14 which in each case can be fastened on one of the free ends of the shaft 2, to be able to mount automatically evaluatable information economically on the marking element 1 and moreover to facilitate the arrangement of the identification system and to improve the fastening, the identification system is developed according to the invention by the marking element 1 being designed in a pan-shaped manner and a plate-shaped information carrier 4 being fastenable in the pan 3 which faces away from the shaft in fastening position.

<CLAIMS>
Identification system for marking animals or
objects, having a shank (2) which can be inserted, with

its two ends exposed, into a hole in an animal or an
object (12), and having a marking element (1) and a

securing element, which elements can each be fastened at
one of the free ends of the shank (2), the marking

element (1) being designed in the form of a trough, and
it being possible for a plate-like information carrier

(4) to be inserted in the trough (3), which, in the
fastened position, is directed away from the shank (2),

and to be fastened there, characterized in that the
marking element (1) and the shank (2) are designed

integrally as a riveting element, and the securing
element has a securing cup (15, 22).
Identification system according to Claim 1,
characterized in that the information carrier (4) comprises

a disc-like element which is made up of a number
of layers (5, 6, 7).
Identification system according to Claim 2,
characterized in that a protective layer (8) is arranged

on the information carrier (4).
Identification system according to one of the
preceding claims, characterized in that, at its free end

of the trough (3), the marking element (1) has a flange
edge (10) which is folded over inwards.
Identification system according to one of the
preceding claims, characterized in that the se
curing
element is formed by a widened section (14) of the free

end of the shank (2). 
Identification system according to Claim 5,
characterized in that a washer (13) is arranged on the

shank (2).
Identification system according to one of the
preceding claims, characterized in that the securing

element is a spherical pushbutton.
Identification system according to one of the
preceding claims, characterized in that the securing

element has a rapid-action cap (26).
Identification system according to one of the
preceding claims, characterized in that an additional

element (17) is arranged in the region of the securing
element.
Identification system according to one of the
preceding claims, characterized in that an additional

information carrier (4) is arranged on the securing
element.
</CLAIMS>
