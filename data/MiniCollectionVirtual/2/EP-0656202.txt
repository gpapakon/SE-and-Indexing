<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to dyeing compositions containing, in a medium suitable for dyeing, sulphur-containing p-phenylenediamines of formula (I): 
<
IMAGE
>
  in which:   R1 and R2, which are identical or different, represent a hydrogen atom, a C1-C4 alkyl, C1-C4 hydroxyalkyl, C2-C4 polyhydroxyalkyl or C 1-C4 aminoalkyl radical;   R3 represents a hydrogen or halogen atom or a C1-C4 alkyl radical; R4 represents a C1-C4 alkyl, C1-C4 hydroxyalkyl, C2-C4 polyhydroxyalkyl, C1-C4 acetylaminoalkyl, C1-C4 aminoalkyl or C1-C4 dialkylaminoalkyl radical which is optionally quaternised;   n is equal to 1 or 2; and, when n = 2, the -SR4 groups are in the meta position relative to each other and R3 = H; and when n = 1 and R3 is different from hydrogen, then it is in the para position of the -SR4 group;  as well as the addition salts of these compounds with an acid; as well as processes for dyeing keratinous fibres using these compositions. The invention also consists of new sulphur-containing p-phenylenediamines, as well as new sulphur-containing p-nitroaminobenzenes for their preparation.

Composition according to any one of Claims 1 to
7, characterized in that all the oxidation dye precursors

together, are present in the composition in an amount of

between 0.1 and 7 % by weight relative to the total
weight of the said composition.

8, characterized in that it additionally contains at

relative to the total weight of the composition; antioxidants

relative to the total weight of the composition, penetration

agents, sequestering agents, conditioning agents,
preserving agents, fragrances and buffers.
