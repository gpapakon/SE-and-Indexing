<INVENTION-TITLE>
Non-stretched, foldable and portable plastic foil usable as paper substitute.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B29D700	B29D701	C08J518	C08J518	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B29D	B29D	C08J	C08J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B29D7	B29D7	C08J5	C08J5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A novel non-stretched, foldable and printable plastic film of a thickness of 30 to 100  mu m is proposed as a paper substitute. The said film comprises a polymer matrix containing an inorganic filler in particle form, the filler accounting for a portion of 20 to 60% by weight of the filled matrix and the cohesive forces between the filler and the matrix being less than the cohesive forces in the matrix itself.
</ABSTRACT>
