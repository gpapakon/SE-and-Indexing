<INVENTION-TITLE>
METHOD OF PRODUCING SYNTHETIC GRAVEL AND/OR CHIPPINGS CONTAINING SALT.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C04B1402	C04B1404	C09K318	C09K318	C09K1702	C09K1702	C09K1710	E01C1124	E01C1124	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C04B	C04B	C09K	C09K	C09K	C09K	C09K	E01C	E01C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C04B14	C04B14	C09K3	C09K3	C09K17	C09K17	C09K17	E01C11	E01C11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A mortar mixture, made up with mixing water, is used in particular for bitumen-bound road surfacings and consists of salt (preferably NaCl or KCl) having a grain size not exceeding 0.5 mm, mineral fines having a grain size not exceeding 1 mm, and cement.
</ABSTRACT>
