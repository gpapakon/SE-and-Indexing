<INVENTION-TITLE>
Steering lock for motor vehicles
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60R2502	B60R2502	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60R	B60R	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
Steering lock (antitheft device) for motor vehicle, comprising a body (10), a lock integral with a control cam (14) which can, under the action of a correct key, drive a locking part or bar (16) integral with a bolt in a translational movement parallel to the axis of the antitheft device from a locking position to an unlocking position, the antitheft device furthermore comprising a key feeler capable of radially displacing a slide (22) integral with a lever (26), first means (52) for pivoting the lever (26) for bringing the said lever to a condition of blocking the bar (16) in an unlocking position, characterised in that the antitheft device comprises second means (36, 70) for pivoting of the lever.

<CLAIMS>
Steering anti-theft device for a motor vehicle comprising a body (10), a lock 
integral with a control cam (14) able, under the action of a suitable key, to drive a 

locking piece or draw member (16) integral with a bolt in a translational movement 
parallel to the axis of the anti-theft device from a locking position to an unlocking 

position, the anti-theft device also comprising a key sensor able to radially move a 
slide (22) integral with a lever (26), first means (52) of pivoting the lever (26) in 

order to bring the said lever to a condition of locking the draw member (16) in an 
unlocking position, characterised in that the anti-theft device includes second means 

(36, 70) of pivoting the lever. 
Anti-theft device according to Claim 1, characterised in that the second means of 
pivoting the lever consist of a bearing face (70) for the draw piece (16) able to 

cooperate with a secondary arm (36) of the lever (26). 
Anti-theft device according to Claim 2, characterised in that the secondary arm 
(36) has an inclined face (60).
