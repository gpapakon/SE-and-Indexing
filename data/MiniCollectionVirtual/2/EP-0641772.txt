<ABSTRACT>
Anionic urethanes containing cyanamide groups and having relatively long hydrocarbon radicals are good retanning agents for leather, sizing agents for paper, finishing agents for leather and textiles and emulsifiers which dimerise or oligomerise after use, at the same time losing their ionic character and thus their hydrophilic properties.
</ABSTRACT>
