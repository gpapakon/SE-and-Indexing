Toilet in particular portable toilet.

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a toilet, in particular a portable toilet for boats, caravans or mobile homes, having a bowl-like seat exhibiting a discharge opening for faeces and a flushing-water container, having a receiving container which can be arranged beneath the seat part and has an inlet opening which is located beneath the discharge opening and is intended for the faeces, and having a valve housing which is located between the discharge opening and the inlet opening and has a flat valve slide. In order to improve the sealing of the slide valve and thus to increase the suitability of the toilet for usage, the valve slide (18) is designed as a push-through slide which exhibits, in its slide plate (5), an opening part (6) and a closing part (7), which can be pushed one after the other beneath the discharge opening (25).
