<INVENTION-TITLE>
Automatic parking garage for motor vehicles.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E04H622	E04H622	E04H628	E04H628	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E04H	E04H	E04H	E04H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a parking facility, in particular in the manner of a high-bay warehouse, having at least one storage and retrieval unit which transports the vehicles which have been parked on sub-pallets in an entrance station to a storage (parking) space and/or to an exit station, which parking facility exhibits an empty-pallet handling system with a pallet-removal device (11) and a displaceable pallet-stacking unit (27). In said parking facility, the storage capacity and retrieval capacity are improved.
