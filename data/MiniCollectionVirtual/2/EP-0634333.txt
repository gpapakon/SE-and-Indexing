<INVENTION-TITLE>
Cardboard packaging for washing powder or like.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65D546	B65D546	B65D5465	B65D554	B65D554	B65D564	B65D566	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65D	B65D	B65D	B65D	B65D	B65D	B65D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65D5	B65D5	B65D5	B65D5	B65D5	B65D5	B65D5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A packaging, consisting completely of cardboard, for washing powder and the like is described, the carrying handle also consisting of cardboard. The carrying handle (20) is attached to the lid (8, 10), and the package can be closed again with the aid of a retaining tab (17) so that the package can be carried.
