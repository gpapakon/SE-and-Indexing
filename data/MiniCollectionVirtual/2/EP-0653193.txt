<INVENTION-TITLE>
Subcutaneous locating device and related medical prosthesis.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device for subcutaneous location of a prosthesis provided with a tubular element, or catheter, (3) which can be punctured and is designed to be implanted in the body of a patient. According to the invention, the device comprises an outer envelope (15) exhibiting a main passage (25) running through it along one axis in order to receive, through the same, the tubular element, and a radial nail (17) mounted slidably in the envelope in order to puncture, under the control of an operator acting thereon, the tubular element and to block the envelope along the same. Application in particular to the location of temporary blood filters.
