<INVENTION-TITLE>
Paper collecting container.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65F104	B65F106	B65F116	B65F116	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65F	B65F	B65F	B65F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
A paper collector is composed of a collecting bag (10) which is made of a textile material and a rectangular container (12), which surrounds the latter, made of cardboard, which is open at the bottom and supports a lid (36) which is also made of cardboard. The container (12) has a narrow insertion slot (14) for paper sheets.
