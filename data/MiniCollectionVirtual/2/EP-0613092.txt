<ABSTRACT>
A portable electronic device for orientation and communication has a housing (2), which has a portable computer (8) with an integrated operator interface (10), communication devices including electronic modules for radio communication with identical or similar devices, and a docking station (6) for electric connection of the computer with the communication devices and the electronic modules and for mechanical fixing. The computer (8) and the electronic modules are housed in the housing so that they are protected from external influences, and the computer is in such a form that it can be removed from the housing for operation independently of the device.
