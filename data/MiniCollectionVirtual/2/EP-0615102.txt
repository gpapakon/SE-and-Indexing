<INVENTION-TITLE>
Air transfer system.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F24F1104	F24F1104	F24F1314	F24F1314	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F24F	F24F	F24F	F24F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F24F11	F24F11	F24F13	F24F13	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In an air transfer system having a tube length (1) in which there is arranged a throttle flap which is rotatably mounted about a pivot shaft (5), the pivot shaft (5) is to be assigned an energy store (8). The latter moves the throttle flap against an air flow into the open position. The pivot shaft (5) is assigned a device which closes the throttle flap against the force of the energy store (8).
