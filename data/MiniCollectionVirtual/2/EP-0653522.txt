Portable toilet particularly for boats, caravans or mobile homes.

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a portable toilet, in particular for boats, caravans or mobile homes, having a receiving container exhibiting a discharge opening, having a bowl-like seat part exhibiting a discharge opening, and having a bracket-like closure device for the releasable fastening of the seat part on the receiving container by means of a pivotable connecting body such that the discharge opening is in alignment with the inlet opening. In order to improve the closure device and to achieve an aesthetically pleasing configuration, the closure device (5) is made wholly from plastic and exhibits a closure plate (6) which can be hooked on the receiving container (2) and on which the connecting body (7) can be latched in, just as it can on the seat part (3).
