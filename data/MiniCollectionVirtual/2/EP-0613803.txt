<INVENTION-TITLE>
Table for the cab of a lorry.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60N300	B60N300	B60R704	B60R704	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60N	B60N	B60R	B60R	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60N3	B60N3	B60R7	B60R7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
2.1 In the prior art a table is provided in the region of the centre console, which table can be displaced vertically by means of a pivoting device and in a functional position is supported on the dashboard of the driver's cab. 2.2 According to the invention, the supporting plate is arranged on a lifting and pivoting cylinder which engages on an underside of the supporting plate eccentrically with respect to the centre of its surface, and the lifting and pivoting axis of which is aligned approximately perpendicularly relative to the supporting plate. 2.3 Use in lorries.
