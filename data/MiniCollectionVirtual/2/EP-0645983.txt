<INVENTION-TITLE>
BLOOD VESSEL MICROANASTOMOSIS METHOD AND AUTOMATIC GRIPPER ASSEMBLY THEREFOR.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61B1703	A61B17064	A61B17064	A61B17068	A61B17068	A61B1711	A61B17115	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61B	A61B	A61B	A61B	A61B	A61B	A61B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61B17	A61B17	A61B17	A61B17	A61B17	A61B17	A61B17	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A blood vessel microanastomosis device comprising a housing (1) with a gripper assembly (3) for grasping and bending clips (4, 5). Said housing (1) contains a mechanism for bending the clips (4, 5) by actuating the gripper assembly (3), said mechanism comprisin an outwardly accessible control member (16), a clip feeder (33) and a mechanism for transferring a clip (4, 5) from the feeder to between the jaws of the gripper (3) controlled by a member (9) accessible from outside the housing (1).
</ABSTRACT>
