<INVENTION-TITLE>
APPARATUS AND METHOD FOR INSPECTING COATING FILM
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01B1106	G01B1106	G01N2184	G01N2184	G01N2188	G01N2190	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01B	G01B	G01N	G01N	G01N	G01N	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01B11	G01B11	G01N21	G01N21	G01N21	G01N21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
An apparatus for inspecting the thickness or degradation of a coating applied to the surface of a container. The apparatus includes a lighting unit for irradiating a container having a coating; an imaging unit for receiving light reflected from the container or transmitting through the irradiated container and converting it to an image signal; and an arithmetic unit comparing the image data represented by this image signal with stored reference data to detect the film thickness of the coating on the container or its degradation. This apparatus detects the film thickness of the coating film or its degradation by utilizing the property that the quantity of scattered light is different depending on the degradation of the coating when inspection light passes through the container.
</ABSTRACT>
