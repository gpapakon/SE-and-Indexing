<INVENTION-TITLE>
Transport system for seeds.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A01C708	A01C708	B65G5334	B65G5360	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A01C	A01C	B65G	B65G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A01C7	A01C7	B65G53	B65G53	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A transport apparatus for seeds, in which seed is transported out of a container (10) to a seed-metering device (22) by means of a compressed-air stream, contains a tube (26) which is multiply bent on the discharge side and which is provided with straight portions (42, 44, 48) between the bends (34, 46, 50), a sieve (40) for the escape of air being arranged on the outer curved surface (36) of the first bend (34).
