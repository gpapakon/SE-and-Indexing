<INVENTION-TITLE>
2-Cyanoethyl-2'-hydroxyethylsulphone, a method for preparing it and its use for the production of 3-aminopropyl-2'-hydroxyethylsulphone
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Starting from 2-(2'-cyanoethylmercapto)ethanol, the corresponding sulphone is accessible via oxidation with hydrogen peroxide and, by hydrogenation of the nitrile group in the presence of sodium borhydride, 3-aminopropyl 2'-hydroxyethyl sulphone, which can be used to introduce reactive substituents into textile dyes.
</ABSTRACT>
