<INVENTION-TITLE>
Powdered food product and process for making it.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A23D700	A23D700	A23L122	A23L122	A23L124	A23L124	A23L148	A23L148	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a food product which comes in the form of a dehydrated oil-in-water emulsion which can be reconstituted by adding water so as once again to form a stable emulsion of the mayonnaise or salad dressing type, and to a process for preparing the said dehydrated emulsion.
</ABSTRACT>
