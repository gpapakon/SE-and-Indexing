<INVENTION-TITLE>
Female terminal for an electrical connection of flat contact pins

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an electrical flat connector contact socket consisting of a stamped sheet-metal part with a spring arm base which is in the form of a rectangular tube and to which a current outlet device is attached at one end and to which contact spring arms are attached to the other end as extensions, which are bent around into the interior of the spring arm base, of mutually opposite spring arm base walls, as well as forming a contact point within the spring arm base and forming an insertion opening for a contact connector pin in the bent-around region, arms which spring over being cut free from the spring arm base walls to which the contact spring arms are attached and being bent around in the spring arm base, acting on the contact spring arms in a resilient manner in the region of the contact point.

<CLAIMS>
Electrical flat plug contact socket consisting of a sheet 
metal stamping with a rectangular tubular spring arm base (1) to 

which a current output device (2) is connected at one end and to 
which contact spring arms (13, 14, 213) are connected at the 

other end as extensions of mutually opposed spring arm base walls 
(3, 4) bent into the interior of the spring arm base and form a 

contact point within the spring arm base and an inlet orifice for 
a contact plug pin in the bend region, characterised in that 

over-spring arms (22, 23, 222, 223) are cut free from the spring 
arm base walls (3, 4), to which the contact spring arms (13, 14, 

213, 214) are connected, and are bent into the spring arm base 
(1) while resiliently loading the contact spring arms (13, 14, 

213, 214) in the region of the contact point. 
Flat plug contact socket according to claim 1, characterised 
in that the over-spring arms (22, 23, 222, 223)
 are shorter than 
the contact spring arms and are preferably about half as long as 

the contact spring arms (13, 14, 213, 214). 
Flat plug contact socket according to claim 1 and/or 2, 
characterised in that the over-spring arms (22, 23, 222, 223) are 

designed as flat spring arms and have end regions which are bent 
toward one another. 
Flat plug contact socket according to one or more of claims 
1 to 3, characterised in that the contact point is located 

substantially half way along the contact spring arms (13, 14, 
213, 214) which extend away from one another in the insertion 

direction following the contact point. 
Flat plug contact socket according to one or more of claims 
1 to 4, characterised in that the contact spring arms (13, 14, 

213, 214) are arranged so as to extend in the opposite direction  
 

to the over-spring arms (22, 23, 222). 
Flat plug contact socket according to one or more of claims 
1 to 5, characterised in that the free ends of the contact spring 

arms (13, 14, 213, 214) are bent outwardly and in that these bent 
end regions (18, 19, 218, 219) are at a predetermined distance 

from the opposing spring arm base walls, preventing over-bending 
of the contact spring arms (13, 14, 213, 214). 
Flat plug contact socket according to claim 6, characterised 
in that the over-spring arms (22, 23, 222, 223) are connected to 

the spring arm base (1) at the level of the bent contact spring 
arm end regions. 
Flat plug contact socket according to one or more of claims 
1 to 7, characterised in that the spring arm base (1) has the 

form of a rectangular tube and in that the output device is a 
rectangular tubular line output device (2), the rectangular tube 

of the line output device (2) preferably having the same 
dimensions as the rectangular tube of the spring arm base (1) and 

the two rectangular tubes passing into one another with axial 
alignment and forming an integrally continuous rectangular tube. 
Flat plug contact socket according to claim 8, characterised 
in that contact members forming a contact point of the line 

output device (2) are arranged on the walls (3, 4, 103, 104) of 
the common rectangular tube to which the contact spring arms (13, 

14) and the over-spring arms (22, 23) are connected. 
Flat plug contact socket according to claim 9, characterised 
in that a spring arm base lateral wall (5, 105) connecting the 

two rectangular tube walls (3, 4, 103, 104) provided with the 
contact and over-spring arms (22, 23) and the line output device 

contact members (133, 132, 105) is axially divided and in that 
a locking device (8-11) is provided to prevent the rectangular 

tube from springing open due to introduction of a contact plug 
pin into the contact point.  

 
Flat plug contact socket according to claim 10, 
characterised in that the lateral wall (5, 105) is divided by 

a butt seam in the region of the spring arm base (1) and in that 
the locking device has complementarily designed mating dovetail 

locking members (8-11). 
Flat plug contact socket according to one or more of claims 
8 to 11, characterised in that the contact spring arms (13, 14) 

are connected to the axial end of the rectangular tubular spring 
arm base remote from the output device (2). 
Flat plug contact socket according to one or more of claims 
8 to 12, characterised in that the contact spring arms (13, 14) 

are bent axially in the form of a C. 
Flat plug contact socket according to one or more of claims 
8 to 13, characterised in that the contact spring arms (13, 14) 

extend substantially through the entire spring arm base (1) to 
the line output device (2). 
Flat plug contact socket according to one or more of claims 
8 to 14, characterised in that the over-spring arms (22, 23) are 

connected to the spring arm base walls (3, 4) close to the line 
output device (2) in the region of the spring arm base (1) remote 

from the inlet orifice. 
Flat plug contact socket according to one or more of claims 
8 to 15, characterised in that the line output device (2) is a 

cutting clamping contact device of which the inlet orifice (112) 
is located at the axial end of the common rectangular tube remote 

from the spring arm inlet orifice (12) and which comprises a 
plurality of mutually spaced cutting clamping webs (132, 133, 

105) of which the cutting clamping slots (134, 135, 130) are 
located in a common plane.  

 
Flat plug contact socket according to claim 16, 
characterised in that the axially divided lateral wall (5, 105) 

of the rectangular tube is designed, in the region of the line 
output device (2), as one of the cutting clamping webs of which 

the cutting clamping slot (130) is aligned with the butt seam (7, 
7') in the region of the spring arm base (1). 
Flat plug contact socket according to claim 16 or 17, 
characterised in that further cutting clamping webs (132, 133) 

are formed by sheet metal tabs which are cut free from mutually 
opposed rectangular tube walls (103, 104) connected to one 

another by the divided lateral wall and are bent toward one 
another in the interior of the tube. 
Flat plug contact socket according to one or more of claims 
8 to 18, characterised in that the common rectangular tube is a 

rectangular flat tube. 
Flat plug contact socket according to one or more of claims 
1 to 7, characterised in that the spring arm base (1) is U-shaped 

in cross section and in that the output device is a line output 
device (2) which is U-shaped in cross section, the U-shaped line 

output device (2) having the same dimensions as the U-shaped 
spring arm base (1) and the two U-parts passing into one another 

with axial alignment so as to form a single continuous U-part. 
Flat plug contact socket according to claim 20, 
characterised in that the contact spring arms (213, 214) are 

connected to the end edges of the U-legs of the spring arm base 
(1). 
Flat plug contact socket according to claim 20 and/or 21, 
characterised in that the over-spring arms (222, 223) are cut 

free close to the U-base web (6) of the U-shaped spring arm base 
(1) beginning in its legs (3, 4). 
Flat plug contact socket according to one or more of claims  
 

20 to 22, characterised in that sheet metal tabs (200) bent 
toward one another are connected to the edges of the two legs (3, 

4) of the U-shaped spring arm base (1) remote from the output

a locking device (203, 204) to prevent the U-shaped base (1)

Flat plug contact socket according to claim 23, 
characterised in that the bent sheet metal tabs abut against one 

another via abutting edges (201, 202) and in that the locking

(203, 204) at the abutting edges. 
Flat plug contact socket according to one or more of claims 
20 to 24, characterised in that a further contact plug pin inlet 

orifice (6a) is formed in the U-base web (6) axially remotely 
from the inlet orifice (212). 
Flat plug contact socket according to one or more of claims 

1 to 7, characterised in that the spring arm base (1) has the

Flat plug contact socket according to claim 26, 
characterised in that a spring arm base lateral wall (5)

Flat plug contact socket according to claim 27,

Flat plug contact socket according to one or more of claims

Flat plug contact socket according to one or more of claims 
20 to 31, characterised in that the line output device (2) is a

Flat plug contact socket according to claim 32 and/or 33,

are cut free from the legs of the U-shaped line output device
