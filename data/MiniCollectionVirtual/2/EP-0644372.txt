<INVENTION-TITLE>
Multi-chamber light with at least two juxtaposed cover lenses.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F21V1700	F21V1700	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F21V	F21V	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F21V17	F21V17	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The multi-function light comprises a plurality of reflection cavities (12), in the bottoms (12a) of which lamps (14) are arranged, at least two successive cavities (12) being closed by two separate cover lenses (19), each of these cover lenses (19) being at least partially bordered by an assembly rim (20) accommodated in a groove (22) which terminates the separating wall (21) between these two successive cavities (12), the said rim being adhesively bonded into the said groove, two juxtaposed rims (20) of the said two cover lenses being accommodated in one and the same groove (22) in the separating wall (21), the said rims (20) and the said groove (22) having complementary means (23, 24) for elastic locking of at least one of these rims (20) with respect to the said groove (22).
