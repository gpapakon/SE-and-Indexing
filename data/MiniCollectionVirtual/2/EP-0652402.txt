<INVENTION-TITLE>
Threaded ring.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F21V1700	F21V1710	F21V2136	F21V2136	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F21V	F21V	F21V	F21V	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
In a threaded ring which has, for mounting (1) on a lamp, a sleeve (3) and an open stirrup (2) whose free ends are latched to the sleeve (3), it is provided according to the invention for the notches (8, 9) to be fitted to the external circumference of the sleeve (3) and to be open at the top and for the free ends (5) of the stirrup (2) to run coaxially with respect to the sleeve (3).
