Water-dilutable two-component coating composition.

<ABSTRACT>
Water-dilutable (water-thinnable) two-component coating composition containing   a) a polyisocyanate component comprising one or more organic polyisocyanates, and b) an aqueous polyurethane dispersion prepared by free-radical-initiated polymerisation of polyurethane macromonomers containing carboxyl, phosphonyl or sulphonyl groups, lateral and/or terminal vinyl groups and optionally hydroxyl, urethane, thiourethane and/or urea groups.
</ABSTRACT>
