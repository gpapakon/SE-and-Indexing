<INVENTION-TITLE>
Skirting board ventilation outlet.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F24F1306	F24F13068	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F24F	F24F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F24F13	F24F13	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In the case of a skirting board ventilation outlet for introducing supply air into a room through a multiplicity of air holes (14), a base part (1) with at least one inlet for the supply air is to be assigned an outlet part (2) with the air holes (14).
