<INVENTION-TITLE>
Shaft element of an inspection chamber.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E02D2912	E02D2912	E03F500	E03F502	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the bottom shaft part (10) of an inspection shaft, having a shaft pipe (12) which exhibits a socket (14) arranged at the bottom. Formed in the outer surface of the shaft pipe (12) are two openings (26, 28) which are in connection with one another by virtue of a channel body (24; 50) which is held in a concrete filling (18) in the shaft pipe (12). The surface of the concrete filling (18) is covered on each side of the stoneware channel (50) by not more than two discharge plates (40).
