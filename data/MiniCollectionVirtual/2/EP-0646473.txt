<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a mixture of colour formers containing   (a) a compound of the formula (1) and/or of the formula (2) and/or of the formula (3) and   (b) a compound of the formula (4), or a mixture of colour formers containing at least two compounds of component (b).
