<INVENTION-TITLE>
Lubricating apparatus for flat bars of a wander flat carding machine.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>D01G1500	D01G1524	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>D01G	D01G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>D01G15	D01G15	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The process proposed is a new lubricating device for flat bars of a revolving-flat card, in which a recessed groove (5) having a soft absorbent transfer means (6) for lubricating the sliding surfaces of the flat bars is provided in the guides or flexible bows (2).
