<INVENTION-TITLE>
Indicating unit for an instrument panel.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01D1124	G01D1124	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01D	G01D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01D11	G01D11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The universal indicator module exclusively includes a plate (11) bearing an indicator member (2) and a support (10) for information associated with the indicator member (2), such as a needle and a dial, and a plug-in connector (17, 107) allowing dismantling via the front of the dashboard and connecting the indicator member (2) to management means (160) which control it and define its sensitivity. Application to motor vehicles.
