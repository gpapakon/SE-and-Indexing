<INVENTION-TITLE>
Panel for repeatedly positioning, fixing and release of non-selfadhesive sheets.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C09J500	C09J500	C09J702	C09J702	G09F702	G09F712	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C09J	C09J	C09J	C09J	G09F	G09F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C09J5	C09J5	C09J7	C09J7	G09F7	G09F7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a panel for repeatedly positioning, fixing and releasing non-self-adhesive sheets, the panel comprising a substrate provided with a pressure-sensitive adhesive coating which is not permanently self-adhesive. The pressure-sensitive adhesive coating which is not permanently self-adhesive preferably comprises hemispherical adhesive spots which are applied to the substrate in the form of a grid and the base of which is bonded to the substrate and the surface of which is directed towards the non-self-adhesive sheets. Suitable in particular as a coating material are hot-melt adhesives applied without a backing system and adhesive dispersions of high solids content.
