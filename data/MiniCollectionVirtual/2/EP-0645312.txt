<INVENTION-TITLE>
Filling system for cosmetic cream pots or same.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65B304	B65B304	B65D106	B65D106	B65D4900	B65D4900	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65B	B65B	B65D	B65D	B65D	B65D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65B3	B65B3	B65D1	B65D1	B65D49	B65D49	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Filling system for cosmetic cream pots and the like, which consists in the formation of an orifice (2) in the bottom of the pots (1) to be filled and in the introduction of the relevant product (3) through the said orifice (2) by means of a supply nozzle (4), with a view to the subsequent closure of the said orifice (2) by means of a stopper which may be pressure-mounted, this filling operation taking place when the relevant screwed lid (7) and any closure cap (6) are mounted securely on the pot (1).
