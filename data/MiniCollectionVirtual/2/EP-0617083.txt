<INVENTION-TITLE>
Homogeneous plastic webs or sheets, their use and process of their preparation.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08L2300	C08L2306	C08L2308	C08L2314	C08L2316	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08L	C08L	C08L	C08L	C08L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C08L23	C08L23	C08L23	C08L23	C08L23	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to homogeneous plastic webs or sheets containing a polymeric binder, conventional fillers and optionally auxiliaries, where the polymeric binder contains at least one ethylene-alkyl acrylate copolymer. The invention also relates to a process by means of which the plastic webs or sheets can be obtained. Plastic webs or sheets are used as wall, ceiling or floor coverings, decorative films or artificial veneers.
</ABSTRACT>
