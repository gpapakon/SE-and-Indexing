<INVENTION-TITLE>
Retaining clip for jewellery and badges with safety means.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A44B600	A44B600	A44B900	A44B916	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A44B	A44B	A44B	A44B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A44B6	A44B6	A44B9	A44B9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The retaining clip is U-shaped, seen from the side, and thus has two limbs and can be clipped with the open end over a material which has space between the limbs (2,3). The securing means comprises a resilient barb (4) which is firmly connected to one limb (2,3) and extends between the limbs (2,3) in the direction towards the bow of the U-shape.
