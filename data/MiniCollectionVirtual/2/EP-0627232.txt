<ABSTRACT>
The quick connection is intended to connect at least one sterile electrical, optical and/or fluid-conducting line to at least one non-sterile device in each case by a plug member on the line side and on the device side. The plug member on the device side is surrounded by at least one sterile film which is tightly clamped between corresponding sealing and clamping edges, which surround the line to be connected, of the plug members on the line and device sides. The plug member on the line side and/or on the device side has a separating or cutting device for severing the film in the region of the lines to be contacted.
</ABSTRACT>
