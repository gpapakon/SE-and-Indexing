<INVENTION-TITLE>
Curing agent for two-component polyurethane systems, especiallytwo-component polyurethane lacquers and a process for its production.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08G1800	C08G1878	C08G1879	C08G1880	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08G	C08G	C08G	C08G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C08G18	C08G18	C08G18	C08G18	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to curing agents for two-component polyurethane systems, in particular two-component polyurethane paints (surface coatings, lacquers, enamels, varnishes), comprising polyisocyanates containing biuret or isocyanurate groups, uretdione groups, urethane groups and tertiary amino groups, and to a process for the preparation thereof.
</ABSTRACT>
