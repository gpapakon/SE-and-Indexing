<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for neutralising the acid form of anionic surfactant, agglomerates and detergent thereby obtained.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C07C30300	C07C30332	C07C30900	C07C30917	C11D102	C11D128	C11D129	C11D1104	C11D1104	C11D1700	C11D1700	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C07C303	C07C303	C07C309	C07C309	C11D1	C11D1	C11D1	C11D11	C11D11	C11D17	C11D17	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
There is described a process for neutralizing essentially alkylbenzenesulphonic acid-free anionic surfactants using an alkali compound having a basic reaction, which is characterised in that the alkali compound is mixed in dry form with a powdered carrier material, the hydrolysis-sensitive acid form of a hydrolysis-sensitive anionic surfactant is reacted with this mixture with formation of an agglomerate without release of water and the surfactant is immobilised on the agglomerate. The agglomerate obtainable by this process can be employed in a detergent in addition to additional customary, but also particularly moisture-sensitive detergent components.
</ABSTRACT>
