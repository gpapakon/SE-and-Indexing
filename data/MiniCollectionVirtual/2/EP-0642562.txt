ECOLOGICAL INTEGRATION PROCESS AND COMPOSITIONS FOR ITS IMPLEMENTATION.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a process for the integration of mineral surfaces in an ecological environment by application of a water-based composition, grains, ameliorators, containing at least one pigment, at least one dispersible polymer in colloidal form and optionally at least one biological species capable of inducing vegetation in said environment, characterized in that the size and nature of the pigment or pigments are selected in order to promote chemical reactions, particularly acidic and basic, at the molecular sites of the walls and faces to be treated. The invention also relates to compositions implementing said integration process of mineral surfaces in the ecological environment.
</ABSTRACT>
