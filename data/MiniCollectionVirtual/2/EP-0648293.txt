<CLAIMS>
Modular system comprising prefabricated components (1,2,3,4,5,6,) 
for the manufacture of roads specifically identified by individual 

components (1,2,3,) incorporating ventilation and air extractor 
channels (8) for the prevention of smog. 
Modular system in accordance with requirement 1, whereby components 
(1,2,3) are equipped with a rain/icewater receptacle facility (7) 

comprising a dispersal system. 
Modular system in accordance with requirement 1 or 2, in which individual 
components (1,2,3) contain heating loops (9) for road surface heating 

to prevent the build-up of snow and ice. 
Modular system in accordance with requirements 1 to 3, whereby individual 
components (1,2,3) are equipped with control circuitry (10) for an 

automatic traffic guidance system. 
Modular system in accorddance with requirements 1 to 4, comprising 
individual components as roads (1,2,3) road supports (5) and support 

pylons (6).
