<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a fermentation cabinet (proofing cabinet) for raw dough pieces, in particular raw pretzels, as used in bakeries in order to ripen the dough pieces under defined environmental conditions. In order to create such an invention, it is necessary that the proofing cabinet is equipped with at least one loading area, a fermentation zone, a take-off zone and at least one discharge zone.
