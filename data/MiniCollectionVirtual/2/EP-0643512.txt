<INVENTION-TITLE>
Apparatus for the detection of the loss of frame synchronisation for multidimensional modulations.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H04B1404	H04B1404	H04L702	H04L702	H04L708	H04L708	H04L2538	H04L2538	H04L2738	H04L2738	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The device according to the invention makes it possible, for multidimensional digital modulations, to detect any loss of frame synchronisation, by checking that the value of a variable representing the decoding error with respect to the error due to noise does not exceed a certain threshold over a large number of successive frames. This variable (V(s)) is equal to a difference between a mean value (D(s)) of the decoding error and a mean value (E(s)) of the error due to the noise at the s
<
th
>
 symbol. Applications: digital modulation, modems.
