<INVENTION-TITLE>
Communication terminal device.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H04M1106	H04M1106	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H04M	H04M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H04M11	H04M11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Arrangement of communications terminals, for connection to an analog subscriber line unit TAE, comprising at least a telephone terminal FE with a signalling/transmission unit S/Ü-E1 and a data transmission device DÜE for connecting data terminal equipment DEE to the subscriber line unit TAE. The data transmission device DÜE has a control unit MSE and a signalling/transmission unit S/ÜE-E1. The data transmission device DÜE and the telephone terminal FE are configured in such a way that the control unit MSE of the data transmission device DÜE can effect a subscriber loop closure in the signalling/transmission unit S/Ü-E2 of the telephone terminal FE.
