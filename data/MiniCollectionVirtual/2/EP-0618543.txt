<INVENTION-TITLE>
Identification system.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
A description is given of an identification system, especially for persons (P, P'), having   - a recording substrate (1; G, 20), which carries at least one identifier (IA, IB, IC; K) characteristic of a specific person (P, P') or an object (G) and is not perceptible with the human sense organs, - a device (2; 22, 23) for impressing the identifier (IA, IB, IC; K) on the recording substrate (1; G, 20) and   - a device (8) for reading and evaluating the identifier, the recording substrate (1; G, 20) carrying a recording readable only in ultraviolet light of a predetermined wavelength range, the recording containing the identifier (IA, IB, IC; K) and the device for impressing (2; 22, 23) having a device for generating (7) and the device (8) for reading and evaluating having a device for reading the recording by means of the ultraviolet light.
