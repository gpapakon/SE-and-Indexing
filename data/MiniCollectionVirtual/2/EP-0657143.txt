<INVENTION-TITLE>
Dosing syringe prefilled with dental compositions.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61C500	A61C504	A61C504	A61C506	A61M531	A61M531	A61M5315	A61M5315	A61M534	A61M534	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61C5	A61C5	A61C5	A61C5	A61M5	A61M5	A61M5	A61M5	A61M5	A61M5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A supply and dosing syringe for viscous dental compounds, having a syringe body which contains a plunger and opens out in a delivery opening. The delivery opening adjoins the syringe body without constriction. This prevents separation of the compound components in the surface region of the emerging strand. The diameter of the syringe body preferably widens towards the delivery opening.
