<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for preparing solvent-free aqueous dispersions.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08J300	C08J300	C08L3300	C08L3306	C08L9300	C08L9304	C09J13306	C09J13306	C09J19300	C09J19304	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08J	C08J	C08L	C08L	C08L	C08L	C09J	C09J	C09J	C09J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C08J3	C08J3	C08L33	C08L33	C08L93	C08L93	C09J133	C09J133	C09J193	C09J193	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A process for preparing aqueous dispersions containing a synthetic, free-radical-polymerised polymer and a colophony resin, characterised in that an aqueous dispersion of the synthetic, free-radical-polymerised polymer is mixed with an aqueous preparation of a colophony resin having a carboxyl group content of from 0.1 to 0.5 mol, based on 100 g of colophony resin.
</ABSTRACT>
