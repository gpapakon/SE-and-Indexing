<CLAIMS>
Liquid ring machine, wherein a device 
(7;9;11;12) to delime its inner surfaces and/or sealing 

gaps, wetted by the operating liquid and/or the 
conveying medium, is provided, wherein the supply of a 

lime-dissolving rinsing liquid takes place separately 
from the supply of the operating liquid. 
Liquid ring machine according to claim 1, 
wherein the device (7;9;11;12) for deliming enables a 

direct wetting of the sealing gaps in the liquid ring 
machine with the lime-dissolving rinsing liquid. 
Liquid ring machine according to claim 1 or 2, 
wherein the device for deliming comprises at least one 

ring channel (7;12) arranged in a plate cam (1) for the 
distribution of the rinsing liquid. 
Liquid ring machine according to one of claims 
1 to 3, wherein the device for deliming comprises at 

least one ring channel (9) arranged in the impeller (2) 
for the distribution of the rinsing liquid. 
Liquid ring machine according to claim 3 or 4, 
wherein the ring channel (7;9) is constructed to be 

closed. 
Liquid ring machine according to claim 3 or 4, 
wherein the ring channel (9;12) is constructed to be 

open. 
Liquid ring machine according to claim 1 or 2, 
wherein the device for deliming comprises individual 

bores (11) which open directly into the sealing gap. 
Liquid ring machine according to claim 3 or 4, 
wherein the ring channel (7;9;12) for the rinsing 

liquid and the ring channel (10) for the operating 
liquid are staggered radially with respect to each 

other. 
Liquid ring machine according to one of claims 
3 to 6, wherein the ring channel (7;9;12) for the 

rinsing liquid is constructed as an annular groove  
 

surrounding the shaft outlet (13) of the plate cam (1). 
Liquid ring machine according to claim 3 or 
4, wherein the device for deliming comprises lead-in 

bores (8) and/or supply channels which open from the 
side plate directly into the ring channel (7;12). 
Liquid ring machine according to one of 
claims 1 to 10, wherein before the beginning of the 

rinsing procedure the operating liquid is to be 
drained. 
</CLAIMS>
