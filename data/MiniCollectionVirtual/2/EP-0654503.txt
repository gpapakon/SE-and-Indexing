<INVENTION-TITLE>
Moulded plastic article.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08L6700	C08L6702	G03C1795	G03C1795	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08L	C08L	G03C	G03C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C08L67	C08L67	G03C1	G03C1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A shaped article, in particular a carrier (support, substrate) for image-information layers comprising a biaxially stretched film of a polymer mixture comprising from 50 to 97% by weight of a linear polyester and from 3 to 50% by weight of a styrene-containing polymer which is not crosslinked in the styrene-containing part, where the percentages are based on the sum of polyester and styrene-containing polymer, is distinguished by particularly high covering power.
</ABSTRACT>
