<INVENTION-TITLE>
Apparatus incorporating an inverter, in particular for mini- or micro-computing.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G06F130	G06F130	G06F1114	G06F1114	H02J906	H02J906	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G06F	G06F	G06F	G06F	H02J	H02J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G06F1	G06F1	G06F11	G06F11	H02J9	H02J9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
This apparatus, intended more especially for powering a computer, is characterised in that a saving unit is built into an invertor. An apparatus according to the invention therefore accomplishes both the electrical safety function and the information saving function, this representing a great simplification for the user.
