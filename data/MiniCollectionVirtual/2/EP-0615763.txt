<INVENTION-TITLE>
Warming up and thawing device.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61M544	A61M544	H05B300	H05B300	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61M	A61M	H05B	H05B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention describes a warming up and thawing device for medical infusion and transfusion solutions stored in containers, which comprises a holding device (6) receiving the containers (7) and keeping them in motion during the warming-up and thawing process. Use of an infrared lamp as radiation source ensures quick and gentle thawing.
