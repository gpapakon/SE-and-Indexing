<ABSTRACT>
The invention describes a piezoresistive sensor structure in monolithic semiconductor material consisting of three or four electrodes and a resistance layer joining the electrodes, a mechanical stress acting on the latter changing the current distribution in the electrodes in such a manner that an electrical signal in the form of a differential current is measured in a simple and efficient manner.
