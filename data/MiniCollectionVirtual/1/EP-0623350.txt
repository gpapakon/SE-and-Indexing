<INVENTION-TITLE>
Use of Bradykinin-antagonists for the manufacture of a medicament for the prophylaxis and treatment of arteriosclerosis.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61K3800	A61K3800	A61K3804	A61K3804	A61P900	A61P910	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61K	A61K	A61K	A61K	A61P	A61P	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61K38	A61K38	A61K38	A61K38	A61P9	A61P9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The use of bradykinin antagonists for the prophylaxis and treatment of arteriosclerosis is described. The bradykinin antagonists to be used are distinguished by containing the amino acids D-Tic, D-Phe, D-Dic, D-Thi or D-Nal in position 7 of the peptide.
</ABSTRACT>
