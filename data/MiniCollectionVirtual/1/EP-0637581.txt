<INVENTION-TITLE>
Use of malonic anhydride for the preparation of 2,2-substituted, 4,6-dioxo-1,3-dioxanes.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C07C5154	C07C5154	C07D31900	C07D31906	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C07C	C07C	C07D	C07D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the use of malonic anhydride for the preparation of 2,2-disubstituted 4,6-dioxo-1,3-dioxanes and a method for preparing the dioxanes. The dioxanes are prepared by reacting, in the presence of a Lewis acid or a protic acid, malonic anhydride with a ketone of the formula R3C(O)R4, in which R3 and R4 denote alkyl or aryl groups or together form an unsubstituted or substituted cyclic system.
</ABSTRACT>
