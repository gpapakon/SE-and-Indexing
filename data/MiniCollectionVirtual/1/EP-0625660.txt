<INVENTION-TITLE>
Check valve.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F16K2702	F16K2702	F16L3728	F16L3738	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F16K	F16K	F16L	F16L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a check (non-return) valve with a valve body which can be screwed into an outlet for the medium and has a through passage and with a closure element which is pressed against a valve seat provided in the through passage under the action of a return spring, and with a device for connecting an outlet line to the valve body, the connection device having a pincer element which expands upon introduction of the outlet line at its inner end, which inner end is arranged behind a constriction of the through passage.
</ABSTRACT>
