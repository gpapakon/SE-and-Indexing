<INVENTION-TITLE>
MACHINE FOR TREATING THE SURFACE OF AT LEAST ONE TEXTILE WEB OF ENDLESS FABRIC, ESPECIALLY FOR NAPPING AND/OR EMERIZING OR THE LIKE
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>D06C1100	D06C1100	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>D06C	D06C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>D06C11	D06C11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a machine for treating the surface of at least one textile web of endless fabric, especially for napping and/or emerizing or the like, wherein the machine has at least a drum with a planetary-like, driven working roll (napping roll and/or emerizing roll or the like). The invention is characterized in that the drum (3) is mounted on a cylindrical support body (9) fixed on the machine frame (10) in such a way that it can rotate and be driven.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
Machine for surface treatment of at least one web of continuous textile material,
in particular for raising and/or emerising or the like, wherein the machine

has at least one drum with working rollers (napping rollers and/or emery rollers
or the like) arranged and drivable in planetary fashion,
characterised in that
 the drum (3) is rotatably and drivably mounted on a tubular
holding element (9) rigidly arranged in the machine frame (10).
Machine according to claim 1,
characterised in that
 the holding element (9) is rigidly secured in the machine
frame (10).
Machine according to at least one of claims 1 and 2,
characterised in that
 the holding element (9) is rigidly secured in the machine
frame (10) by means of a detachable connection (11).
Machine according to at least one of claims 1 to 3,
characterised in that
 the bearings (14) for at least one stripping roller (7) are secured
to the holding element (9).
Machine according to at least one of claims 1 to 4,
characterised in that
 the bearings (14) secured to the holding element (9) for at
least one stripping roller (7) arranged inside the drum (3) are arranged inside the

drum (3).
Machine according to at least one of claims 1 to 5,
characterised in that
 the dust funnel (8) is secured to the holding element (9).
Machine according to at least one of claims 1 to 6,
characterised in that
 the holding element (9) inside the drum (3) has apertures
(91) projecting for the most part into the dust funnel (8) arranged inside the

drum (3).
Machine according to at least one of claims 1 to 7,
characterised in that
 the holding element (9) is formed at least up to its emergence
from the drum (3) as part of a suction line leading to a suction device.
Machine according to at least one of claims 1 to 8,
characterised in that
 at least two bearing seats (92) for the round anchor plates
(31) forming the drum (3) are provided on the holding element (9).
Machine according to claims 1 to 6 and 9,
characterised in that
 at least one of the round anchor plates (31) mounted on the
holding element (9) has at least one hub (312) for driving the drum (3) and/or

one hub (314) for driving at least one of the stripping rollers (7) associated with
the outer periphery of the drum (3).
Machine according to at least one of claims 1 to 9,
characterised in that
 at least one of the round anchor plates (31) mounted on the
holding element (9) has a hub (312) for driving the drum (3) and a hub (314) for

driving at least one of the stripping rollers (7) arranged inside the drum (3).
Machine according to at least one of claims 1 to 10,
characterised in that
 the hubs (312; 314) of the round anchor plate(s) (31)
mounted on the holding element (9) are shaped at their outer periphery in accordance

with the respective drive means.
Machine according to at least one of claims 1 to 12,
characterised in that
 at least one supply and/or disposal line is arranged inside
the holding element (9).
</CLAIMS>
