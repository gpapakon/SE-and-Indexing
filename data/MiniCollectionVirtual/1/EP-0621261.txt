<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the purification of a methyldiethanolamine aqueous solution.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C07C21300	C07C21310	C07C21500	C07C21512	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C07C	C07C	C07C	C07C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C07C213	C07C213	C07C215	C07C215	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In this process, the formate content is measured in the MDEA solution circulating between the absorber and the desorber of the hydrogen sulphide wash, and on attaining a threshold value of 0.5 to 30 g/l a part stream of the MDEA solution is drawn off and passed over an ion exchanger for the purpose of removing formate. The part stream is then fed back into the solution circulation. The loaded ion exchanger is regenerated with an alkali metal hydroxide solution which is then passed into the ammonia stripper.
</ABSTRACT>
