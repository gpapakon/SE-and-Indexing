<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for depositing an adhesive tape at the beginning of a travelling web
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65H1910	B65H1910	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65H	B65H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65H19	B65H19	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
An adhesive tape having an adhesive layer essentially only on one side is used for making a web connection. The covering foil of this adhesive layer consists of two parts which are separated by a longitudinal seam. At least one narrow adhesive strip, likewise with a covering foil, is applied to the other side of the carrier of the adhesive layer and is preferably located on one edge of the adhesive tape.

<CLAIMS>
A means, in particular a drum car provided 
therewith, for applying an adhesive tape to the start 

of a web of a web reel or to the web reel parallel to 
the centre line thereof with a guide rail (86, 86') 

running parallel to the web reel (A), and a guide way 
(89),
 
characterised in that the reel of adhesive tape is 

contained in a cassette,
 
the cassette (K) is connected detachably and 

resiliently to a sliding block (91), the sliding block 
(91) sliding in the guide way (89) of the guide rail 

(86). 
A means according to Claim 1, characterised in 
that the cass
ette (K) has a crosspiece (96) 
transversely to the reel (R) of adhesive tape, which 

lifts off therefrom a cover film of the adhesive tape 
which is to be removed upon unwinding the adhesive 

tape, the reel of adhesive tape bearing the cover film 
on its radially outer side.
