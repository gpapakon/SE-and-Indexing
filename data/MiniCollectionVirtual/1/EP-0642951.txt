<INVENTION-TITLE>
Automatic control of a headlamp with non-visible emission e.g. U.V. for vehicle.

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an automatic control device for a motor vehicle headlight which can emit non-visible radiation. It comprises first detection means (6) which are sensitive to the ambient light, second detection means (7) which are sensitive to the speed of the vehicle, and means for operating the headlight only when the luminosity is less than a given luminosity threshold (SL) and when the speed of the vehicle is greater than a given speed threshold (SV).
