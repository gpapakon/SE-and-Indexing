<INVENTION-TITLE>
Radiotelephone with servo control of reception clock for base or terminal in function of value of reception quality.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H04J306	H04J306	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H04J	H04J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H04J3	H04J3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In a radio telephony installation comprising at least one fixed base linked to a predetermined telephone communication network, and including at least one base radiofrequency channel capable of providing radiofrequency intercommunication with at least one terminal radiofrequency channel of an independent terminal, in the event of a quality parameter below the threshold, the base or terminal processing facilities are capable of imposing a correction on the base or terminal receiving clock.
