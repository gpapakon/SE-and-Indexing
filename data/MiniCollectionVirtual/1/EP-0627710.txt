<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for reporting to a central station of an abnormal collision course of a vehicle.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G07C500	G07C508	G08G1123	G08G1123	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G07C	G07C	G08G	G08G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G07C5	G07C5	G08G1	G08G1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A device reporting an abnormal collision occurrence on a vehicle to a central station is proposed. The device consists of first means for the continuous recording of accelerations acting on the vehicle and of second means which evaluate the recorded measurement values according to fixed criteria and, if a collision occurrence to be reported is detected, activate a communication device assigned to the second means.
</ABSTRACT>
