Recording sheet for ink jet printing process

<ABSTRACT>
A recording sheet for ink-jet printing processes, comprising a support (carrier, base, substrate) and an ink take-up (absorption) layer containing a hydrophilic binder and a pigment, where the ink take-up layer contains a mixture of polyvinyl alcohol, polyvinylpyrrolidone, vinyl acetate homopolymer and/or vinyl acetate copolymer as binder and a quaternary ammonium compound.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
Receiving sheet for ink jet printing processes 
consisting of a support and an ink 
receiving layer 
containing a pigment and a hydrophilic binder 

characterized in that the ink receiving layer contains a 
quaternary ammonium compound and a mixture of vinyl 

acetate-homopolymer and/or vinyl acetate-copolymer and 
polyvinyl alcohol and polyvinyl pyrrolidone and the 

quantity ratio of the vinyl acetate homopolymer and/or 
copolymer to the polyvinyl alcohol/polyvinyl pyrrolidone 

mixture is 1:3 to 3:1. 
Receiving sheet according to claim 1, 
characterized in that the quaternary ammonium compound is 

a polyammonium compound with a cationicity of 15 to 30 ml 
of a 1 x 10
-3
 n solution of sodium polyethylenesulfonate. 
Receiving sheet according to claims 1 and 2, 
characterized in that the quaternary ammonium compound is 

a polydiallyldimethylammonium chloride. 
Receiving sheet according to claims 1 to 3, 
characterized in that the amount of quaternary ammonium 

compound does not exceed 10 wt%. 
Receiving sheet according to claims 1 to 4, 
characterized in that the vinyl acetate copolymer is a 

vinyl acetate/alkyl acrylate copolymer. 
Receiving sheet according to claims 1 to 5, 
characterized in that the amount of binder is 10 to 

80 wt%.  
 
Receiving sheet according to claims 1 to 6, 
characterized in that the ink receiving layer may contain 

an amorphous silicic acid, a clay, a zeolite, or other 
inorganic pigments. 
Receiving sheet according to claim 7, 
characterized in that the amorphous silicic acid has a 

pore volume of 1.0 to 2.5 ml/g and a particle size of 
%%le%% 5 Âµm. 
Receiving sheet according to claims 1 to 8, 
characterized in that the amount of pigment in the ink 

receiving layer is 15 to 80 wt%, in particular 30 to 
65 wt%. 
Receiving sheet according to claims 1 to 9, 
characterized in that the ink receiving layer is provided 

on a support of sized raw paper. 
Pigment-containing coating material for the 
manufacture of an ink receiving layer for an ink jet 

receiving sheet according to claims 1 to 10. 
</CLAIMS>
