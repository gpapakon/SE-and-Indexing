<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for shifting loads.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65G2500	B65G2502	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65G	B65G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65G25	B65G25	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A device (1) for shifting loads (2) on an essentially horizontal surface (4), in particular of transport vehicles, consists of a first longitudinal section (5) and a second longitudinal section (22) which are let into a passage (3) in the surface (4). The first longitudinal section (5) can be lifted and lowered via lifting elements (17, 19), whereas the second longitudinal section (22) is displaceable on a sliding layer (21) relative to the first longitudinal section (5) via a cylinder/piston unit (27, 28). A load (2) can thereby be displaced gradually in each case by a dimension a.
