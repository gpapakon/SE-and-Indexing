<INVENTION-TITLE>
Siloxane molecular composites, process for their preparation and their use.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08J312	C08J314	C08L7700	C08L7712	C08L7900	C08L7908	C08L8300	C08L8304	C08L8310	C08L10100	C08L10100	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
Molecular siloxane composites including a rigid reinforcing thermoplastic polymer dispersed on the molecular scale in a flexible organosiloxane/thermoplastic polymer copolymer, process for the preparation of the said composites and a material with siloxane-enriched surface obtained from the said molecular siloxane composites, and the use of the said composites and of the said material, in particular for aerospace construction.
</ABSTRACT>
