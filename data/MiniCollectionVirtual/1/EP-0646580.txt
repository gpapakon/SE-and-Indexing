<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to compounds with at least one vinyl ether group, which additionally have at least one further functional group, selected from acrylate, methacrylate, epoxide, alkenyl, cycloalkenyl and vinylaryl groups, in the molecule, compositions, in particular for stereolithography, containing these vinyl ether compounds and a process for the preparation of three-dimensional objects with the aid of the compositions.
</ABSTRACT>
