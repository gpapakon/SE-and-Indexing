Bus controller.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G06F1336	G06F13378	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a bus controller (5) for an electronic device (2), which has a dedicated intelligence unit (3) and a memory (4) with a multiplicity of individually addressable memory locations (for example 21, 40), which are connected to one another via a device bus (6), it being possible to connect the bus controller (5) at least to the memory (4) and a networking bus (1). According to the invention, the management of requests to be transmitted or received or of requests which have been transmitted or received is carried out by means of mutually separate, double-interlinked (double-chained) lists.
