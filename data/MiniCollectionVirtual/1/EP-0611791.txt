<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for treating the surface of articles.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08J700	C08J712	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08J	C08J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C08J7	C08J7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In a process for treating the surface of articles with a fluorine-containing gas in a reaction chamber, the articles are first treated in the reaction chamber with a low fluorine concentration which excludes damaging heating of the articles to be treated, and subsequently, after a first conditioning of the surface of the articles, the treatment is continued by increasing the fluorine concentration. This enables rapid fluorination without the risk of overheating of the articles.
</ABSTRACT>
