<INVENTION-TITLE>
PROCESS FOR THE MEASUREMENT OF DIELECTRIC AND MAGNETIC PARAMETERS OF A SOLID MATERIAL, INVOLVING SOLDERING OF THE MATERIAL ON A SPECIMEN HOLDER AND DEVICES FOR CARRYING OUT SAID PROCESS.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Process for the measurement of dielectric and magnetic parameters of a solid material, involving soldering of said material on a specimen holder, and devices for carrying out said process. According to the invention, a specimen (2) of material is soldered in an electrically conducting specimen holder (4, 6), the specimen holder is mounted in a measurement line (14) and said measurement line is connected to a measurement apparatus (32) capable of supplying data on the basis of which the parameters are calculated. Application in the measurement of dielectric permittivity and magnetic permeability of solid materials.
</ABSTRACT>
