<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for snap mounting of an electric apparatus on a standardized support rail
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H02B1015	H02B1052	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H02B	H02B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H02B1	H02B1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The device has a fixed nose and a movable nose (24), which engage behind parallel proceeding edges of the carrying rail. The movable nose is formed at a hook (15) which is arranged at the end of a bar (20). The bar proceeds through the switch, from the floor of the switch up to its operating side, and can be operated accordingly by a tool, so that the movable nose disengages from the carrying rail through twisting of the bar.
</ABSTRACT>
