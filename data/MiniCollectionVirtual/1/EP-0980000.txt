<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for determining the rotational position, speed and/or direction of the rotor of an electric motor
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01P342	G01P3487	G01P1302	G01P1304	H02K2906	H02K2908	H02P614	H02P616	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01P	G01P	G01P	G01P	H02K	H02K	H02P	H02P	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01P3	G01P3	G01P13	G01P13	H02K29	H02K29	H02P6	H02P6	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The arrangement has at least two rotary position sensors (1-3) offset in the direction of rotation of the rotor, whose outputs (6-8) are connected to an evaluation circuit (14) by common connecting cables (16). The sensors have arrangements (17-19) or resistances for influencing the position signals so that the sum of the signals has no ambiguity dependent on the rotor phase angle.
</ABSTRACT>
