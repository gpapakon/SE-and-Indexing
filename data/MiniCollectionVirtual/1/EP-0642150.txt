Optimised colour display device.

<ABSTRACT>
The device of the invention comprises a white-image source (2), a liquid-crystal matrix (5) for controlling the shade, and a liquid-crystal matrix (6) for controlling the saturation of the displayed images. A first embodiment is characterised in that it comprises, in cascade, a device (2, 10-11) for controlling the luminance of the displayed images, a device (5) for controlling the shade of these images, and a device (6) for controlling the saturation of these images.
