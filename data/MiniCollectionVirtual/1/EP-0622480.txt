<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for the dosing of pre-set quantities of fibre flocks of different quality and/or colour.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>D01G1300	D01G1300	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>D01G	D01G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>D01G13	D01G13	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A new method for the metering of predeterminable quantities of fibre flocks of different quality and/or colour in a mixing appliance having a plurality of metering devices (12a, 12b, 12c) is presented. To produce a non-uniform fibre sliver which can be non-uniform both visually and structurally, the instantaneous production rate at which the fibre flocks are added to the mixture of at least one predetermined mixture component is changed regularly in time between a minimum value (O, Pmin) and a maximum value (Pmax).
