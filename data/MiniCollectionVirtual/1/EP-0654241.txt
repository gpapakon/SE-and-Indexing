<INVENTION-TITLE>
Skimming device for oils, fats or the like.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47J4300	A47J4328	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47J	A47J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47J43	A47J43	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A device for skimming oils, fats and the like from the surface of a liquid is shown, having   a liquid receptacle (11) which has at least one bottom opening (15, 16) in its bottom wall (12), a closure disc (13) rotatable with respect to the bottom wall (12) and by which the bottom opening (15, 16) can be closed in a closed position, the closure disc (13) having at least one liquid passage opening (26, 27) which can be brought into coincidence with the bottom opening (15, 16) to achieve an open position, and an actuating device (14), by which the closure disc (13) can be rotated with respect to the bottom wall (12) of the liquid receptacle (11) from the upper side of the latter.
