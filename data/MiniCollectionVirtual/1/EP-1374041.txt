<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for running a plug-in on one or more computers, especially for monitoring purposes. The plug-in is transmitted to at least one computer (11.1, 11.2, 11.3) via a network (2). Afterwards, the plug-in prompts the at least one computer (11.1, 11.2, 11.3) to run this plug-in.
