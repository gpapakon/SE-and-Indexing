Mixtures of polymer with water.

<ABSTRACT>
Emulsifier-free synthetic resins and synthetic resin mixtures with water as binders for water-based printing inks and printer's varnishes, containing   a) an optionally neutralised or partially neutralised polymer resin A containing acid groups, obtainable by in-situ polymerisation of ethylenically unsaturated compounds containing acid groups with other ethylenically unsaturated compounds and condensation with monohydroxyl compounds and optionally neutralisation or partial neutralisation, and b) optionally a water-insoluble polymer resin B in the form of latex particles, obtainable by emulsion polymerisation of ethylenically unsaturated compounds in the presence of the polymer resin A.
</ABSTRACT>
