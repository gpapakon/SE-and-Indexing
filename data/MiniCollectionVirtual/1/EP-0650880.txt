Trolley for transporting a container.

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a trolley for receiving a container, in particular a basket, on its upper loading surface which forms a supporting surface for the container, the supporting surface, in particular the loading surface 2, being inclined with respect to the surface 3 on which the trolley 1 stands.
