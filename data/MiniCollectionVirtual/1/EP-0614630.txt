<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for applying cosmetic compositions.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A45D4026	A45D4026	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A45D	A45D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device for applying, in particular, cosmetic compositions. It contains an application element (3), which is designed as a hollow body, is made of an elastic material, and whose outer surface serves at least partially as an application surface (25, 26), and a supply container from which the cosmetic composition can be fed to the interior (29) of the application element (3) and from there to the application surface (25, 26). The device is characterised in that the application element (3) has, in the region of the application surface (25, 26), at least one passage opening serving as an outlet opening (27, 28) for the cosmetic composition.
