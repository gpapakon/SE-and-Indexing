Limited angle torque motor.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
A two-pole stepping motor has a control phase having a first winding (W3, W4) and has a further phase which is formed by a permanent magnet. The object is to enlarge the displacement range of the stepping motor. To this end, the phase having the permanent magnet is provided with an additional winding (W5, W6), the first winding and the second winding being connected in series.
