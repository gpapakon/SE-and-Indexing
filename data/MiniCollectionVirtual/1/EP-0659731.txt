<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the preparation of dimethyl carbonate.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B01J2706	B01J27122	C07B6100	C07B6100	C07C6800	C07C6800	C07C6900	C07C6996	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B01J	B01J	C07B	C07B	C07C	C07C	C07C	C07C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B01J27	B01J27	C07B61	C07B61	C07C68	C07C68	C07C69	C07C69	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
When preparing dimethyl carbonate by reacting methanol, carbon monoxide and oxygen in the presence of Cu compounds, the process is carried out according to the invention in distillation units and at least some of the water of reaction is removed from the stripping zone or the bottom phase.
</ABSTRACT>
