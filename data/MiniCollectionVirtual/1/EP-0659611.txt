<INVENTION-TITLE>
Luggage retaining device in a vehicle luggage compartment
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60R700	B60R700	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60R	B60R	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60R7	B60R7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In order to hold the luggage 13 on the floor of a luggage compartment (boot) 2 located behind a seat backrest 4 and under a rear wing (door) 5, the device includes a rectangular net 8 fastened at the front to tabs 9 sliding on substantially vertical guides 10, and at the rear on the wing 5, near to its lower edge 7. The tabs 9 are each connected to the wing 5 by an optionally compressible tie rod 12. The device is applicable to all vehicles equipped with a luggage compartment under a rear wing.

<CLAIMS>
Device for holding luggage placed on the floor of a 
vehicle luggage compartment, behind a seat-back and 

below an upward-hinging flap, comprising a 
substantially rectangular elastic net, the front 

portion of which is connected to the seat-back, or to 
the body of the vehicle near this seat-back,
 
characterised in that the rear portion of the net (8) 

is connected to the flap (5), near its lower edge (7), 
and
 
in that the lateral extremities of the front portion of 

the net (8) are each attached to a lug (9) which slides 
on one of two substantially vertical guides (10) and 

which is connected to the flap (5) by one of two 
vertical braces (12). 
Device according to Claim 1,
 
characterised in that the braces (12) are compressible. 
Device according to one of Claims 1 or 2,
 
characterised in that the two guides (10) are fastened 

to the seat-back (4). 
Device according to one of Claims 1 or 2,
 
characterised in that the two guides (10) are fastened 

near the seat-back, to lateral panels delimiting the 
luggage compartment (2). 
</CLAIMS>
