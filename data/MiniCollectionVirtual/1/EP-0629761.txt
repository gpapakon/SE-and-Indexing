<INVENTION-TITLE>
Molded hinge.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E05D500	E05D514	E05D900	E05D900	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E05D	E05D	E05D	E05D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
Cast (moulded) hinge for securing a door on a pin (8) which is retained in a receiving means of a cast member (1). In order to be able to produce such a cast hinge in a simple manner, it is provided that provision is made for two pairs of extensions (2, 3), between which the bolt (8) passes through an eyelet (9) of the door, and that the legs (11, 12) of the U-shaped clearances (4, 5) of the extensions (2, 3) converge towards the rounded web (6, 7), the two legs (11, 12) of a clearance (4, 5) enclosing an angle of approximately 10 DEG .
