<INVENTION-TITLE>
Private branch exchange.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G04G900	G04G900	H04M121	H04M121	H04M1247	H04M1247	H04M342	H04M342	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
A private branch exchange is provided with a time recording module which, during a connection set-up to an external subscriber routed via a telecommunications network, records the current time at the external subscriber end and displays it on a display device on the extension terminal of the internal subscriber concerned.
