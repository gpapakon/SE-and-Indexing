<INVENTION-TITLE>
Culinary preparation comprising at least two parts: a main part and a coated sauce portion.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A23L100	A23L100	A23L148	A23L148	A23P108	A23P108	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A23L	A23L	A23L	A23L	A23P	A23P	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A23L1	A23L1	A23L1	A23L1	A23P1	A23P1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The culinary preparation is characterised in that it consists of a whole which comprises at least two separate parts:   * a first part comprising at least one main portion such as minced meat (12), * a second part (52) comprising at least one sauce (21) which is coated with a sticky substance (42),   these two separate parts being placed against each other.
