HEATING SYSTEM COMPONENTS

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a heating air conditioning system, especially for an aeroplane, comprising a first fluid circuit (10) comprising a first heat exchanger (12), a second fluid circuit (20) comprising a second heat exchanger (22), and a third fluid circuit (30) comprising a third heat exchanger (32). According to the invention, the second and/or the third heat exchanger (22, 32) is connected downstream of the first heat exchanger using air flow technology.
</ABSTRACT>
