Linen for beds, sitting and/or lying furniture

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to linen 1 for beds, sitting and/or lying furniture, in particular a bed or cushion cover, composed of a patterned and/or printable natural-, synthetic- or mixed-fibre single fabric of warp and weft threads. In order to have in particular variably usable linen, the latter is designed as reversible linen usable on both sides. Either the left-hand or the right-hand single-fabric side is designed for summer or winter use, the single-fabric side 4 for winter use containing at least 50% of roughened weft and/or warp threads and the single-fabric side 3 for summer use containing at least 50% of smooth weft and/or warp threads.

<CLAIMS>
Item of linen for beds, seating and/or reclining furniture, 
particularly bed or cushion covers consisting of a patterned 

and/or printable natural chemical or mixed fibre simple 
woven structure consisting of warp and weft threads, with 

single-weave sides for summer or winter use, the single 
weave side for winter use having roughened threads while the 

single-weave side for summer use has smooth threads, 
characterised in that the linen item is constructed as a 

pull-through reversible linen item, whereby the single-weave 
side for winter use comprises at least 50% roughened warp 

and/or weft threads while the single-weave side for summer 
use comprises at least 50% smooth warp and/or weft threads 

and whereby the single-weave side (4) for winter use has 
roughened warp or weft threads having a thread thickness Nm 

10 to 50 and a thread density of 10 to 45/cm and the single-weave 
side (3) for summer use has smooth warp and weft 

threads with a thread thickness of Nm 10 to 80 and a thread 
density of 10 to 66/cm. 
A linen item according to Claim 1, characterised in that the 
warp and/or weft threads consist of a polyester material 

with a yarn numbering of about Nm 67 and a warp or weft 
density of about 45 threads/cm. 
A linen item according to Claim 1 or 2, characterised in 
that the warp or weft threads of the single-weave consist of 

cotton with a yarn numbering of about Nm 34 and a warp or 
weft density of 24 threads/cm. 
A linen item according to one of Claims 1 to 3, 
characterised in that the surfaces of the woven structure, 

outside the patterning (2) are bound in an atlas bind (1/4 
bind) and the patterned areas of the patterning (2) are 

bound in a 1/1, 1/3 to 1/7 bind.  
 
A linen item according to one of Claims 1 to 4, 
characterised in that it is constructed as a bed or cushion 

cover with at least two single-weave webs stitched securely 
to each other to bound a filling space adapted to be filled 

with a cushion through a filling aperture, the filling 
aperture being adapted to be closed by at least one interior 

closure element (5) which can be covered by an inclined 
strip or a stand-up hem (6). 
A linen item according to Claim 5, characterised in that the 
closure element (5) is constructed as an interior sliding 

clasp fastener. 
A linen item according to Claim 5 or 6, characterised in 
that the linen item has on each long side a stand-up hem 

(7).
