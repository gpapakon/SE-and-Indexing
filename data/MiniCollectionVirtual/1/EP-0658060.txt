<INVENTION-TITLE>
Detector for low-frequency tone signals.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H04Q130	H04Q146	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H04Q	H04Q	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H04Q1	H04Q1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Detector for low-frequency tone signals (f0) with a defined detection template. It includes a unit (B) having means (5, 6, 8, 9) for detecting the presence of the tone frequency sought and for producing a first DC voltage representative of the level of the signal after filtering and units (D, E, F) having means (13, 15, 29) for producing DC voltages representative of the analog signal received, and means (7, 17, 27) for verifying that the analog signal received adheres to the criteria fixed by the template.
