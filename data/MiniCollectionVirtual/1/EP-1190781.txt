<INVENTION-TITLE>
Apparatus and process for low emission cleaning a tank
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B08B908	B08B9093	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B08B	B08B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B08B9	B08B9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The equipment for the low emissions cleaning of a tank has a cleaning lance (26) with a controllable spray unit (30), and a rigid lance shaft (28) connected to a HP line for cleaning fluid. A control unit (38) is provided for the orientation of the spray unit, and a flange is provided for fitting on the outlet connection (24) of the tank. A branch (32) is installed on the flange and on one side has a connection for a drain hose (34) and on the other side a sealed opening for the lance shaft. An Independent claim is included for a procedure for the low emissions cleaning of a tank using the aforesaid cleaning lance and control unit to alter the orientation of the spray unit delivering the cleaning fluid into the tank.
</ABSTRACT>
