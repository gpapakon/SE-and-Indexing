<INVENTION-TITLE>
Measuring device with optical fibers.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
This device comprises at least one set of optical fibres (24) connected to measurement cells, at least one auxiliary optical fibre (30), and at least one selection element connecting the latter fibre to the set of fibres and intended selectively to make a connection between this fibre and one of the fibres in the set. The selection element comprises optical means (32, 34) for making the connection between the set and the auxiliary fibre, and retractable closure means (36) associated with the fibres in the set and intended to close the ends of these fibres which terminate at this element. Application in nuclear chemistry.
