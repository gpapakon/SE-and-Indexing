<INVENTION-TITLE>
High-rise storage rack.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65G104	B65G104	B65G112	B65G1127	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65G	B65G	B65G	B65G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65G1	B65G1	B65G1	B65G1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The shelving unit has two shelf columns, provided with compartments of which at least one column is provided with and input and removal aperture. Between the columns is a vertical conveyor forming a unit with them which has a horizontal conveyor device. With the aid of the conveyor device a plate can be transferred from the area of the input and removal aperture into a compartment controlled by the vertical conveyor. The bases (9) of the plates (7) are provided with at least one recess (11,12) by which the parts (20) of an auxiliary conveyor (21) in the area of the input and removal aperture (13) can be brought into engagement with objects (22) placed on he plates in order to put them into store or remove them individually or in multiples.
