<INVENTION-TITLE>
Dustbin
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65F100	B65F100	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65F	B65F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65F1	B65F1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The garbage can has several modules arranged in a column. Each module is composed of a sliding container (1) having handles (11), by pillars (2) and by a fixed cover (3). To assemble the garbage can, a pair of pillars is introduced into the base (4) and the corresponding container is slipped in the cover causing the container baskets (12) to slide in cover rails (31).
</ABSTRACT>
