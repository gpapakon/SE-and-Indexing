<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for horizontally projecting a laser beam.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01C500	G01C500	G01C1500	G01C1500	G02B2720	G02B2720	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01C	G01C	G01C	G01C	G02B	G02B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01C5	G01C5	G01C15	G01C15	G02B27	G02B27	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The device comprises a projector (4) which is mounted on the vertical part (3) of an oscillating equipment item (2, 3) provided with a counterweight (5) which, by the effect of gravity, keeps the projection permanently horizontal. The oscillations of the equipment item are damped by a manually controlled mechanical brake (6-7-8-9). The base (11) of the casing (1) which supports the oscillating equipment item is rotationally mounted on a base (10), with a graduation allowing the projecting angle to be read.
