<ABSTRACT>
Stabilised compositions comprising 1,1-dichloro-1-fluoroethane (HFA-141b), a styrene derivative and a costabiliser chosen from alcohols comprising less than 3 hydroxyl groups and non-functionalised alkenes. These compositions can be used in particular in premixes for polyurethane foams.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
Compositions comprising 1,1-dichloro-1-fluoroethane and a styrene
derivative corresponding to the general formula



where R1, R2, R3 and R4 independently represent a hydrogen atom or an alkyl
group containing from 1 to 3 carbon atoms, characterized in that they comprise a

co-stabilizing agent chosen from alcohols containing less than 3 hydroxyl functions
and non-functionalized acyclic alkenes containing at least 4 carbon atoms.
Compositions according to Claim 1, characterized in that the co-stabilizing
agent comprises at least one alcohol at least one alkene.
Compositions according to Claim 1 or 2, characterized in that the styrene
derivative is selected from those in which R1, R3 and R4 represent a hydrogen

atom.
Compositions according to Claim 3, characterized in that the styrene
derivative is alpha-methylstyrene.
Compositions according to any one of Claims 1 to 4, characterized in that
the amount of styrene derivative is from 0.01 to 1 % by weight relative to the weight

of the 1,1-dichloro-1-fluoroethane.
Compositions according to any one of Claims 1 to 5, characterized in that
the co-stabilizing agent comprises an aliphatic alcohol selected from methanol,

ethanol 1-propanol, 2-propanol and ethylene glycol.
Compositions according to Claim 6, characterized in that the alcohol is
methanol.
Compositions according to any one of Claims 1 to 7, characterized in that
the alkene is selected from 2-methyl-2-butene, 2-methyl-1-butene, 3-methyl-1-butene

and mixtures thereof. 
Compositions according to any one of Claims 1 to 8, characterized in that
they comprise the co-stabilizing agent in an amount by weight of from 0.0001 to

10% relative to the weight of the 1,1-dichloro-1-fluoro-ethane.
Compositions according to Claim 9, characterized in that they comprise
the co-stabilizing ag
ent in an amount by weight of from 0.001 to 1% relative to the
weight of the 1,1-dichloro-1-fluoroethane.
Compositions according to Claim 10, characterized in that in the case
where the co-stabilizing agent comprises an alcohol, the amount by weight of

alcohol does not exceed 0.1% relative to the weight of the 1,1-dichloro-1-fluoroethane.
Use of the compositions according to any one of Claims 1 to 11 as a
blowing agent for the preparation of polymer foams.
Use according to Claim 12, applied to polyurethane or polyisocyanurate
foams.
Premixes intended for preparing polyurethane or polyisocyanurate foams
comprising a polyol and a blowing agent, characterized in that the blowing agent

comprises a composition in accordance with any one of Claims 1 to 11.
Polyurethane or polyisocyanurate foams obtained by the use of a
blowing agent comprising a composition according to any one of Claims 1 to 11.
Polyurethane or polyisocyanurate foams obtained by the use of a premix
according to Claim 14.
