DEVICE FOR REPRODUCING OPTICAL RECORDING MEDIUM

<ABSTRACT>
An apparatus comprising an optical pickup for reproducing information stored on optical recording media, which automatically set conditions for reproduction of individual recording media loaded into a drive so as to obtain compatibility with different types of media. Even when an optical recording medium for low-density recording is loaded, the recorded information can be reliably reproduced.
</ABSTRACT>
