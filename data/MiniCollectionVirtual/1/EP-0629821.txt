<INVENTION-TITLE>
Floor that may be equipped with an unterfloor heating element and its manufacturing process.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F24D1302	F24D1302	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F24D	F24D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F24D13	F24D13	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a dwelling floor which is intended to be equipped with an underfloor heating element (7), such as an electric heating cable or film, consisting of a layer (1) of a thermal insulating material which rests on the ground (2) and of a concrete slab (3) on said layer (1), said heating element (7) being embedded in said slab (3). Said concrete slab (3) rests on said insulation layer (1) by means of supports (4) so as to form one or more spaces (6) for air circulation under said concrete slab (3).
