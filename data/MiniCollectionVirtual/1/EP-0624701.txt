<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for holding a window or door, which is pivotingly mounted in a frame.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E05C1700	E05C1704	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E05C	E05C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>E05C17	E05C17	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Stay for windows and doors which are retained pivotably in a frame, comprising a profile strip (3) and two U-profiles (4, 5) which are arranged in an articulated manner at in each case one of the ends of the profile strip (1), with leg openings directed towards one another, in each case one of the legs (6) exhibiting a bracing element (8), with the aid of which they can be fixedly clamped on the window (1) or the door, on the one hand, and on the frame (2), on the other hand. The bracing element (8) is formed by a threaded bolt with a large-surface-area pressure-exerting head (9).
