<INVENTION-TITLE>
Removal of higher alcohols from alkyl polyglycosides.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C07C2900	C07C2986	C07H1500	C07H1504	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C07C	C07C	C07H	C07H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C07C29	C07C29	C07H15	C07H15	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the extraction of higher alcohols from a mixture of these higher alcohols with alkyl polyglycosides, using ethane in the super critical state as the extractant. Ethane allows higher loads and substantially shorter extraction times than super critical carbon dioxide which has conventionally been used.
</ABSTRACT>
