<INVENTION-TITLE>
Arrangement for holding of multiple transmit- and/or receive modules.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H01Q2100	H01Q2100	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H01Q	H01Q	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H01Q21	H01Q21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an arrangement for holding a plurality of T/R modules, in particular for a front end of a phased-array (radar) antenna. A hollow supporting body is used in this case, through which a cooling gas or a cooling liquid can be passed. The T/R modules are mounted in a cost-effective and easily replaceable manner on opposite sides of the supporting body.
