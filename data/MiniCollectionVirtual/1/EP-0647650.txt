<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a process for the preparation of a heterogenous component of a metallocene catalyst from a suitably substituted metallocene of the 4th sub-group of the Period Table of the Elements and a poly(methylhydrogensiloxane) with participation of a catalyst. The metallocene components and a suitable aluminoxane can be used as a catalyst for the polymerisation of 1-olefins, cyclic olefins, diolefins and cyclic diolefins.
</ABSTRACT>
