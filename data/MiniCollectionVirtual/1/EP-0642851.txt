<INVENTION-TITLE>
Scale removing device.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B21B4504	B21B4508	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B21B	B21B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B21B45	B21B45	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In order to set the impact pressure of the water to the optimum for slabs in scale-removing plants, the proposal is that at least two pressurized-water piston pumps should be provided, that the piston pumps should be connected in parallel and that it should be possible to switch the pumps on and off separately.
