<ABSTRACT>
In order to be able to use a conventional paint spray gun for drying coated surfaces also, by means of a gentle air jet without sharp air-jet delimitation, there is mounted on the nozzle head 2 of the paint spray gun a tubular drying nozzle 7 which at one tube end has inlet slots 9 which are separated from one another by webs 10 which can be mounted on the nozzle head 2. If the trigger bow 4 is pulled only slightly, compressed air flows out of the nozzles 5, 6 of the nozzle head 2, which draws ambient air in via the inlet slots 9.
