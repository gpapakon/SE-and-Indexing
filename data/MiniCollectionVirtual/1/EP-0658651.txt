<INVENTION-TITLE>
Blade for coating webs of paper or cardboard.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B05C1102	B05C1104	D21H2500	D21H2508	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B05C	B05C	D21H	D21H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B05C11	B05C11	D21H25	D21H25	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The applicator assembly has a longitudinal carrier (2) secured in a holder (4). It is fitted with slits (8) along the lines of the dosing groove channels (12), to take thin blades (1) to define the dosing channels (12) between them. The blades (1) have a curved shape or form a curve, with their convex sides forming the laying surface against the web or roller to be coated.
</ABSTRACT>
