<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method of manufacturing a lateral bipolar transistor.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H01L2102	H01L21316	H01L21331	H01L2966	H01L2973	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H01L	H01L	H01L	H01L	H01L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H01L21	H01L21	H01L21	H01L29	H01L29	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Method of production for a lateral bipolar transistor, in which a highly doped emitter zone (7) and collector zone (8) as well as a base contact zone are formed by using a mask through implantation into a region (4) in the silicon layer of a SOI substrate, which region is provided with primary doping, and a base zone is produced between this emitter zone (7) and this collector zone (8), by use of a mask (10) through implantation of dopant.
