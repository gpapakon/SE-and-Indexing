<CLAIMS>
A lower end part (1) of doors or windows, with a
profile (2) that, in the installed state, has an upper

side (3), a lower side (4), and inner side (5) and an
outer side (6), wherein a U-profile (21) that is open

on the bottom is fixed in the region of the lower side
(4), and wherein a holding arrangement (24) for an

insertable strip (25) is arranged on the lower end of
the opposing limbs (22, 23) of the U-profile (21),


characterized by
 the fact that an expansion tape (26)
consisting of an expandable material is situated in

the U-profile (21), wherein the expansion of said tape
is prevented by the strip (25).
</CLAIMS>
