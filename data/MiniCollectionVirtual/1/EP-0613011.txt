<INVENTION-TITLE>
Mounting of a microsensor.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a mounting for a flat, parallelepipedal microcomponent, consisting of an assembly of silicon wafers (1, 2, 3) mounted on a support. A silicon plate (30) is fixed by a first small region (31) of its upper surface to one of the wafers of the microcomponent and, by a second small region (32) of its lower surface, to the support (20).
