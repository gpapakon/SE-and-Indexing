<INVENTION-TITLE>
Bulkhead for industrial rooms.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E04H500	E04H510	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E04H	E04H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>E04H5	E04H5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
a) Bulkhead (partition) for industrial premises (rooms). b) Partition characterised by: - a means for transversely clamping the top of the two piers against the panel, this means comprising:   - a longitudinal slideway (5) on the internal face of each pier (2), under the shoulder (21) supporting the panel (1), - a lock (6) in the form of a thin strip, housed so that it can move longitudinally in the slideways (5) of the two piers (2) and interacting with these in order to pull the two piers (2) against each other and press them against the panel (1).
