<INVENTION-TITLE>
Power generating plant.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H02M748	H02P900	H02P904	H02P904	H02P948	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H02M	H02P	H02P	H02P	H02P	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H02M7	H02P9	H02P9	H02P9	H02P9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The power generating plant (1) includes a motor (3) coupled to a generator (5) producing an alternating voltage (V2), a first convertor (6) producing a DC voltage (V3) from this alternating voltage (V2), a rechargeable electrical energy source (7) producing a DC voltage (V4) equal to the voltage (V3) and connected in parallel with the first convertor (6), and a second convertor (9) producing the alternating output voltage (V1) of the power generating plant (1) from this DC voltage (V3). This arrangement makes it possible to keep the rms value (U1) and the frequency (f1) of the output voltage (V1) constant whatever the variations in the electrical power absorbed by the consumer device (2) fed by the power generating plant. Application to the alternating-current electrical energy power supply of any consumer device.
