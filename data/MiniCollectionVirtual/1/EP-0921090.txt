<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for transporting goods with a portal-hoisting device
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B66C1900	B66C1900	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B66C	B66C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B66C19	B66C19	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Guides (3, 3') are provided for the wheels of the portal hoist, substantially level with the surrounding floor, so as to allow fork-lift trucks etc. to pass over them. The portal hoist has all-wheel steering. At crossing points of the guides, connecting pieces (4) are installed. Guides preferably form right-angled coordinates and wheels can be swivelled through 90 degrees.
</ABSTRACT>
