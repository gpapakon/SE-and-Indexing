<ABSTRACT>
The invention pertains to oxide particles with surfaces modified by covalent-bonded organic groups which provide for covalent bonding in polymer materials. These particles are especially advantageous in applications as fillers or modifiers in polymer materials, particularly polymer films.
</ABSTRACT>
