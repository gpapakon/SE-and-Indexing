Polyarylether agglomerates.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Polyarylene ether agglomerates obtainable from polyarylene ether flakes or polyarylene ether powders by the action of shear forces under pressure at temperatures between the glass transition temperature Tg and the melting point Tm of the respective polyarylene ether.
