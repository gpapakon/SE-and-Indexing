<INVENTION-TITLE>
3-AZABICYCLO(3.1.0)HEXANE DERIVATIVES AND PROCESS FOR THEIR PREPARATION
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C07D22100	C07D20952	C07D22122	C07D20900	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C07D	C07D	C07D	C07D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C07D221	C07D209	C07D221	C07D209	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Benzene sulphonic acid and toluene sulphonic acid 
salts of 2-cyano-3-azabicyclo[3.1.0]
hexane are novel com­
pounds. The cis and trans isomeric salts can be separated 

by virtue of their differing solubilities in water, methanol 
and ethanol, thereby providing a method of isolating rela­

tively pure cis and trans isomers of 2-cyano-3-azabicyclo­
[3.1.0]
hexane or 2-carboxy-3-azabicyclo[3.1.0]hexane. 
</ABSTRACT>
