<INVENTION-TITLE>
Organic silicon compound-containing compositions suitable for treating fibrous materials.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Compositions in the form of aqueous dispersions containing dihydroxypolyorganosiloxanes, amino-functional silanes and cyclic oligosiloxanes and/or products of the reaction of these components, are suitable for coating sheet-like structures made from fibre materials. The sheet-like structures are thus provided with good water barrier properties with good permanence thereof, good low-temperature flexibility and rebound elasticity.
</ABSTRACT>
