Acceleration sensor.

<ABSTRACT>
Acceleration sensor in which an inertial part (2) is constructed as a micromechanical component on springs (3) in the silicon layer (1) on an SOI-substrate, and in which means, such as piezoelectric resistors (4), for example, are present for the purpose of being able to detect a deflection of the inertial part (2) as a consequence of an acceleration of the substrate and to evaluate it in an electronic circuit.
