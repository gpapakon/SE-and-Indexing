<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for powder transporting.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B05B714	B05B714	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B05B	B05B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B05B7	B05B7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Powder-transporting device with eject pump. It contains a vacuum-channel segment (8) for sucking in powder (18), which can be connected to or disconnected from the outside air (30) quickly and suddenly via a valve (36), in order to switch off or switch on again the powder transport.
