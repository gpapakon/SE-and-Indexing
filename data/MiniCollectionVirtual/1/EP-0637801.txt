<INVENTION-TITLE>
Network communications system and corresponding protocol for access to information suppliers.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H04L2906	H04L2906	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H04L	H04L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H04L29	H04L29	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
System for communication (SCI) with at least one network (RE) comprising a host system (HOST) associated by means of a channel with at least one communication processor (PCi, PCj) characterised in that, with the host (HOST) processing the top layers (CH) and the processor (PCi, PCj) the bottom layers (CB) starting from the transport layer, it comprises means of access to the various transport layers comprising:     - a first module located at the bottom of the top layers (CH), - a second module (HI) belonging to the processor located at the top of the transport layer (C4), - model-specific access units operated via access unit commands carried by the channel placing the first and second modules in communication.   Application to telecommunications systems.
