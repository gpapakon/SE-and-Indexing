<INVENTION-TITLE>
Circuit for the correction of non-linearity of the final amplifier in a radio data transmission system.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H04B144	H04B144	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H04B	H04B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H04B1	H04B1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The appts. passes digital data (12) to an amplifier (14) with a lineariser (16). A command unit (UC) controls the transmitter and the amplifier. An output switch (20) is controlled by the command unit and can switch between the output antenna (10) and a load impedance (20). The command unit switches the output power to the matching load impedance during the linearisation phase.
</ABSTRACT>
