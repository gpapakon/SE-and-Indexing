Process for the purification of flue gases.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a process for purifying flue gases which are brought into contact with a sorbent for desulphurisation and removal of heavy metals and NH3 or NHx. With a view to good availability and economical utilization, the sorbent used is a material which is fired at temperatures of over 1200 DEG C and has cement-clinker-like properties.
</ABSTRACT>
