<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for working up solvent mixtures containing high boiling components and appropriate device.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B01D300	B01D300	B01D314	B01D314	D01F1300	D01F1300	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B01D	B01D	B01D	B01D	D01F	D01F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B01D3	B01D3	B01D3	B01D3	D01F13	D01F13	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A process and a plant are described for solvent recovery for the work-up of mixtures which contain organic solvents, high-boiling components, in particular salts, and possibly water. The plant includes a concentration apparatus (2), a separation apparatus (7) and a separation column (10).
