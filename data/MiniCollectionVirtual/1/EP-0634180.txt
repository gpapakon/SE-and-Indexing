<INVENTION-TITLE>
Adhesive medical bandage.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61L1516	A61L1530	A61L1558	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61L	A61L	A61L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61L15	A61L15	A61L15	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The adhesive bandage comprises a textile support (1) with a capacity for elongation, at least one of the faces of which is subjected to an operation of coating with a rubbery mass (2), while the face or faces thus treated receive an adhesive material (3).
