<ABSTRACT>
The invention concerns a mounting for a bobbin spindle, the mounting having two bearing units each consisting of a bushing (182) and a cover element. The bushing (182) and cover element (184) are connected to each other by spring pins (178). Each bearing unit (178) is contained in its respective chamber (162) by its cover element (178), bores (200) linking the space between the bushing and the inside surface (170) of the chamber to an oil supply (202, 204).
</ABSTRACT>
