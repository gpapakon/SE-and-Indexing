<INVENTION-TITLE>
METHOD AND SYSTEM FOR DETERMINING AN INFORMATION CONTENT OF AT LEAST TWO ELECTRONIC OBJECTS IN RELATION TO A DEFINED ELECTRONIC REFERENCE OBJECT
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G06F1730	G06F1730	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G06F	G06F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G06F17	G06F17	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
According to the invention, an information content of several objects in relation to a defined electronic reference object is determined in such a way that for each object a degree of similarity is determined by means of which the similarity of the object concerned with at least one other object and/or the reference object is described. When the information content is determined the degree of similarity and the object information content of an object concerned is taken into consideration.
</ABSTRACT>
