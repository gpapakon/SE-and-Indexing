<INVENTION-TITLE>
Monofilamentary synthetic fiber.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>D01F646	D01F646	D01F688	D01F690	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>D01F	D01F	D01F	D01F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>D01F6	D01F6	D01F6	D01F6	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A polyamide-based monofilamentary synthetic fibre is to be improved in respect of its mechanical and chemical properties. For this purpose there is proposed a monofilamentary synthetic fibre made of polyamide and polypropylene.
</ABSTRACT>
