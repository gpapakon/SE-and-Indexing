<INVENTION-TITLE>
Protective garment.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A41D19015	A41D19015	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A41D	A41D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A41D19	A41D19	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The glove is provided in the inner hand area with a thin metal foil (3) made of alloy steel. The metal foil can be made up of several individual overlapping scale-like plates (4-6) and enclosed between two material layers (1,2) made of cotton jersey. The outer material layer (2) has a preferably latex or plastics coating (7). The overlapping plates can be fixed by adhesive.
</ABSTRACT>
