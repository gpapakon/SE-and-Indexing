Rogowski coil to be used in an electrical installation wiht grounded metallic enclosure and method of manufacturing such a coil.

<ABSTRACT>
Rogowski coil, usable particularly in an electrical installation with an earthed metal casing, comprising a first toroidal support (1) at the surface of which is arranged at least one first metal helix (2), one end of the helix being linked to a return conductor, characterised in that the return conductor consists of a second metal helix (12) placed on a second toroidal support arranged close to the first toroidal support.
