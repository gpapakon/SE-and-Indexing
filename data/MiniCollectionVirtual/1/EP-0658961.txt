<INVENTION-TITLE>
Brush assembly and brush holder for electrical motor with build-in noise suppression and process for making same.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H01R3900	H01R3936	H01R3954	H02K1102	H02K1102	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H01R	H01R	H01R	H02K	H02K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H01R39	H01R39	H01R39	H02K11	H02K11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Brush assembly and brush holder (10) for an electric motor, the said brush (1) comprising (a) a wearing piece (2), (b) a spring (7) and (c) noise (interference)-suppressing means comprising electrically conducting turns surrounding a core (6) made of a material having a relatively high permeability, is characterised in that   1) the said core (6) and the said turns form a rigid block (4) by virtue of connecting means; 2) the said rigid block (4) is mechanically fastened to the said wearing piece (2); 3) the said brush holder (11) comprises means for preventing short-circuiting between the said wearing piece (2) and the said spring (7).   Process of manufacture by assembling.
