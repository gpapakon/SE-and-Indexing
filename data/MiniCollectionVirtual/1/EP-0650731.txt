<INVENTION-TITLE>
Product obtained from yeast cells of saccharomyces boulardii and its utilisation.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a product with the gas chromatogram shown in Fig. 1, obtainable by incubating cells of the yeast Saccharomyces boulardii at 30 to 38 DEG C in an aqueous yeast culture medium, removing the yeast cells, concentrating the cell-free supernatant, extracting the latter with an alkanol with 4 to 8 carbon atoms, evaporating the organic phase, dissolving the residue in water, dialysing the resulting solution against distilled water, concentrating the dialysate, precipitating the proteins from the latter and evaporating of the precipitant. The invention furthermore relates to the use of the product according to the invention for the production of antibiotics, antimycotics, immunosuppressants and antitumour agents.
