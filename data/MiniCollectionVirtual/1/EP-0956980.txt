<INVENTION-TITLE>
Motor-vehicle wheel
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60B2100	B60B2112	B60C500	B60C500	B60C1900	B60C1900	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60B	B60B	B60C	B60C	B60C	B60C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60B21	B60B21	B60C5	B60C5	B60C19	B60C19	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The surface of the foam (5) contacting the rim is an air impermeable layer (6). Preferred Features: The air impermeable layer is flexible and comprises rubber or plastic, especially a plastic sheet. It is a sheet of closed cell foam. It is adhered or vulcanized to the foam ring. It may be bonded by foaming on, optionally forming the impermeable layer as a skinning over of the foam, an elegant method. The impermeable layer is reinforced with, e.g., a fabric. A ribbed, studded or otherwise increased area may be conferred to the impermeable layer. Ribs or studs may themselves have a textured surface. Ring and layer have a contour fitting the rim (1). The layer is adhered to the rim using adhesive. The layer projects out from the ring, to be band clamped into place. Preferred Fitting: Reduced pressure is applied between rim and ring, during tyre fitting and/or during travel. The ring alternatively seats on the rim under pretension. The cross section of the ring is semicircular in cross-section.
</ABSTRACT>
