<INVENTION-TITLE>
FLUID FOOD.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A23L1304	A23L1304	A23L252	A23L260	A23L268	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A23L	A23L	A23L	A23L	A23L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A23L1	A23L1	A23L2	A23L2	A23L2	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Fruit-containing liquid foods enriched with nutrients, vitamins and minerals having a fruit content of 25 to 100% by weight, an acid content, calculated as tartaric acid, of at least 5 g/l and a pH of at most 4.5 additionally contain at least 2000 mg/l of dissolved calcium phosphate. Preferably, they contain other minerals. They are suitable for decreasing or preventing the occurrence of demineralisation of tooth enamel which is observed on the consumption of acid-containing beverages.
</ABSTRACT>
