<INVENTION-TITLE>
Wing stopper device for door or window
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E05C1700	E05C1744	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E05C	E05C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>E05C17	E05C17	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The door or window stopper comprises a one piece fastening plate which is connectable to a housing (21) in such a way that the latter covers at least almost entirely the fastening plate together with the stopping element. Only the pusher element (15), the clamping plate (18) and a free end of the stopping element protrude from the housing.
</ABSTRACT>
