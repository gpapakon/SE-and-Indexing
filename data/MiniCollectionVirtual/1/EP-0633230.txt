<INVENTION-TITLE>
Sight screen for a hermetically sealed casing.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Glass windows employed for producing sightglasses for a closed enclosure, such as dial manometers, where a pressure higher than atmospheric pressure may prevail. According to the invention such a window forming at least a part of a wall of a closed enclosure and liable to be subjected to pressure differences between its two faces includes at least one glass sheet (1) whose face directed towards the interior of the enclosure is coated with at least one layer of plastic (2).
