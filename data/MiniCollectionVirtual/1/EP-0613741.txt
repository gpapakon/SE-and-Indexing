<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process and apparatus for treating bentonite-bonded used foundry sand.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B22C500	B22C518	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B22C	B22C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B22C5	B22C5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A description is given of a process and an apparatus for treating bentonite-bonded used foundry sand. The process comprises the steps of precleaning the used sand and of subsequent thermal treatment of the precleaned used sand. The apparatus for carrying out the process has a gas-tight drum-type furnace for the thermal treatment of the precleaned used sand.
</ABSTRACT>
