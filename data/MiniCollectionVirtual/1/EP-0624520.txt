<INVENTION-TITLE>
Framework for holding bags and provided with a lid.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65B6700	B65B6712	B65F116	B65F116	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65B	B65B	B65F	B65F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65B67	B65B67	B65F1	B65F1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Framework with a lid which is produced from tubular parts and connecting devices and which can be used particularly for refuse-bag stands. In this lid, the same tube-segment profiles (3) are used as hinges as are also used for fastening the refuse bag to the tubular parts of the stand. On the underside, the lid is provided with projections (7) having a snap-fastening profiling for the fastening of a clamping profile (3).
