<INVENTION-TITLE>
Water distributor.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F24D310	F24D310	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F24D	F24D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F24D3	F24D3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a water distributor for splitting up a main line into a plurality of branches or heating circuits. The water distributor can be used universally for different heating systems as well as for water supply. To this end, the water distributor has a joining pipe (12) having a valve (14, 28) which can be actuated manually, thermally or electrically, a distributor pipe (15) which can be connected to the joining pipe (12) and has at least one branch (16, 18, 20) whose end is provided with a connection, and at least one adapter piece (22) which consists of a pipe piece having a valve (24) and whose one end has a connection for connecting to the branch and whose other end has a thread for the connection of an underfloor heating loop.
