<INVENTION-TITLE>
Disposable syringe.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61M5315	A61M5315	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61M	A61M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61M5	A61M5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Disposable syringe for the administration of liquid active substances (1) to living human beings or animals, comprising a hollow cylinder (2) filled with the active substance (1) on to which an injection needle (3) can be attached and which surrounds a piston (4) which can be moved by a piston rod (5) in the direction of the injection needle (3). The piston (4) is made of a thermoplastically processable rubber mixture and is delimited in the direction of the active substance (1) by a peripheral sealing lip (6) which touches the hollow cylinder (2) and is spring-deflectable in the radial direction.
