<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the production of concentrated aqueous formaldehyde solutions by pervaporation.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B01D6136	B01D6136	C07C4500	C07C4578	C07C4702	C07C47058	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B01D	B01D	C07C	C07C	C07C	C07C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B01D61	B01D61	C07C45	C07C45	C07C47	C07C47	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Concentrated aqueous formaldehyde solutions are prepared by pervaporation by leading an aqueous formaldehyde mixture over a hydrophilic, pore-free membrane and selectively removing water through the membrane.
</ABSTRACT>
