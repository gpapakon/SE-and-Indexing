<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to novel maleimide copolymers which, in addition to structural units derived from certain maleimide derivatives defined in greater detail in the present patent application, contain recurring structural units selected from the structural units of the formulae (Ia) and (Ib) 
<
IMAGE
>
  where, in the formulae (Ia) and (Ib),     A is a direct single bond or a divalent group of the formula -O-; R0 and R1, independently of one another, are each a hydrogen atom, a C1-C6-alkyl group or an aryl group having 6 to 14 ring carbon atoms;   R2 is a radical of the formula 
<
IMAGE
>
  in which   R is a tert-alkyl radical having 4 to 19 carbon atoms, [T] is a C1-C6-alkylene group or an arylene group having 6 to 14 ring carbon atoms and   p is one of the numbers 2, 3 and 4; or   A, together with R2, is a group of the formula 
<
IMAGE
>
  and where R3 and R4, independently of one another, are each a hydrogen atom, a halogen atom, a C1-C6-alkyl group or a C1-C6-alkoxy group, and the other radicals have specific meanings defined in greater detail in the present application. The polymers can form the basis of chemically reinforced positive photoresists which exhibit, inter alia, excellent contrast behaviour, a low shrinkage tendency in the irradiated areas and no significant delay time effects.
