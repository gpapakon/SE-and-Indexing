<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for allocating samples and/or measurement results of patients.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for allocating samples and/or measurement results of patients, patient information being arranged on a data carrier. The data carrier has the form of a memory device (1) which remains with the patient during the stay of the patient in a hospital, a clinic or the like. The memory device (1) is preferably fastened to a [lacuna] on the arm of the patient or the neck of the patient.
