<INVENTION-TITLE>
Electronic device to determine pressure and pressure differences in fluid media.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01L906	G01L906	G01L1904	G01L1904	G01M328	G01M328	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01L	G01L	G01L	G01L	G01M	G01M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01L9	G01L9	G01L19	G01L19	G01M3	G01M3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A pressure sensor bridge circuit (1) is driven by a constant current source (2). The bridge circuit is connected via an instrumentation amplifier (3) and drift correction amplifier to a signal processor (6) input. The signal processor (6) has an external EEPROM (7) containing control and measurement programs. A temperature sensor diode (8) is positioned remotely from the sensor (1). When ambient temperature measured by the sensor (8) is within operating limits for measurement, power from batteries (9) is applied to measurement chain on demand from the measurement program for the duration of measurement acquisition and displayed on an LCD display (10).
</ABSTRACT>
