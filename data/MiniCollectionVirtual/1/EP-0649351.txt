<INVENTION-TITLE>
CONTAMINATED WASTE PROCESSING METHOD, PARTICULARLY FOR HOSPITAL WASTE, AND PLANT THEREFOR.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61L1100	A61L1100	B09B300	B09B300	C08J1100	C08J1108	F23G502	F23G502	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61L	A61L	B09B	B09B	C08J	C08J	F23G	F23G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61L11	A61L11	B09B3	B09B3	C08J11	C08J11	F23G5	F23G5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A method and a plant for processing contaminated waste, wherein unsorted waste is fed directly into a reactor (101) supplied with a solvent for dissolving polymers in said waste, whereafter the dissolution reaction is performed in said reactor at a temperature no lower than the melting point of said polymers, and the resulting uniform sterilized liquid is recovered to enable a final incineration process. Said method is particularly suitable for processing hospital waste, whether it is liquid, aqueous or organic waste or composite waste.
</ABSTRACT>
