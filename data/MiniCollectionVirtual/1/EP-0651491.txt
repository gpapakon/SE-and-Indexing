<INVENTION-TITLE>
Surge arrester.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H02H904	H02H904	H02H906	H02H906	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H02H	H02H	H02H	H02H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H02H9	H02H9	H02H9	H02H9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The surge arrester (overvoltage limiter) contains a series circuit, which connects two live current conductors (L, N), formed by a varistor (V) and a disconnection device (AT1) which is thermally coupled thereto and has a specific switching temperature ( delta 1). A gas-discharge suppressor (A) or the like is connected in parallel with the disconnection device (AT1).
