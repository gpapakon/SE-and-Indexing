<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for lowering the fuel temperature in the tank of a motor vehicle.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F02D3300	F02D3300	F02M3700	F02M3700	F02M3720	F02M3720	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F02D	F02D	F02M	F02M	F02M	F02M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F02D33	F02D33	F02M37	F02M37	F02M37	F02M37	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In order to prevent an inadmissibly high temperature rise of the fuel in the tank (10) due to the cooling of the fuel rail (12) by fuel flowing through it, an electrically controllable valve (16) is provided in the fuel circuit, by means of which the quantity of fuel flowing through can be continuously varied.
