<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the recovery of silver from zinc leaching residues by flotation.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B03B700	B03B700	B03D100	B03D102	C22B300	C22B304	C22B900	C22B902	C22B902	C22B904	C22B1100	C22B1100	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B03B7	B03B7	B03D1	B03D1	C22B3	C22B3	C22B9	C22B9	C22B9	C22B9	C22B11	C22B11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A process is described for recovering silver from zinc leaching residue by flotation, the residue from neutral zinc leaching being separated off then attrited and the attrited residue being subjected to flotation.
</ABSTRACT>
