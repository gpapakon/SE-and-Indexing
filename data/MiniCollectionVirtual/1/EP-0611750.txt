<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the preparation of mixtures of 3-aminopropionitrile and ethylene cyanhydrine.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C07C25300	C07C25330	C07C25500	C07C25512	C07C25524	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C07C	C07C	C07C	C07C	C07C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C07C253	C07C253	C07C255	C07C255	C07C255	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Process for the preparation of mixtures of 3-aminopropionitrile, of the formula I,  H2N-CH2-CH2-CN (I
>
 and ethylene cyanohydrin, of the formula II, HO-CH2-CH2-CN (II
>
 by reacting bis(2-cyanoethyl) ether, of the formula III, NC-CH2-CH2-O-CH2-CH2-CN (III
>
 with ammonia at temperatures from 50 to 200@C and pressures from 1 to 500 bar.
</ABSTRACT>
