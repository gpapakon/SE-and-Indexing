<INVENTION-TITLE>
Contact element for terminal block and terminal block containing it.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a contact element (1) for a terminal block, comprising a head (3) having an opening (31), a nut (6) located beneath the lower face of the head (3), a screw (7) being screwed into the nut (6) through the opening (31) and located on the side of the upper face of the head (3), and a springy (elastic) part (5) formed by at least two electrical-contact blades. The contact element for a terminal block has an arm (4) extending on one side of the said head (3), the said springy part (5) being arranged at a distal end (41) of the arm and the said blades having a given nominal separation.
