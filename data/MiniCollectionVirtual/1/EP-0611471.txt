<INVENTION-TITLE>
DISPLAY FOR MEASURING INSTRUMENTS OR THE LIKE.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01D700	G01D700	G09F900	G09F900	G09F9302	G09F9302	G09G304	G09G304	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01D	G01D	G09F	G09F	G09F	G09F	G09G	G09G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01D7	G01D7	G09F9	G09F9	G09F9	G09F9	G09G3	G09G3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a display for measuring instruments, computers, etc., with multi-position read-out in which the individual positions (2) take the form of a pixel system with a plurality of bar-shaped segments; in order to display measurements, especially pressure measurements in both scientific form (mantissa with exponent) and with a floating point, it is proposed that at least the final position (2) be supplemented by further segments (11, 12, 13, 25, 26) to display exponents.
</ABSTRACT>
