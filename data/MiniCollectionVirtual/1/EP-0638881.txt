<INVENTION-TITLE>
Garage for two-wheeled vehicles.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G07B1502	G07B1504	G07F700	G07F700	G07F1710	G07F1710	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G07B	G07B	G07F	G07F	G07F	G07F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G07B15	G07B15	G07F7	G07F7	G07F17	G07F17	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a garage for two-wheeled vehicles, with a space which is enclosed on all sides for one or more two-wheeled vehicles and which has a door with a lock, through which a two-wheeled vehicle can be introduced and removed, the lock being designed as a card-reading lock for a magnetic or chip card or being connected to a card reader of this type, the lock being connected to a data-acquisition system and the door being openable by authorized parties only when the magnetic or chip card has been introduced. The door is permanently locked when the garage for two-wheeled vehicles is both empty and occupied.
</ABSTRACT>
