<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the valorization of waste material containing cellulosic fibers and synthetic polymer fibers.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a process for the recycling of scrap comprising fibre mixtures containing cellulose fibres, comprising the steps:   i) use of fibre mixtures containing cellulosic fibres and synthetic polymer fibres, and ii) microbial hydrolysis of these mixtures, in which the cellulosic fibres are completely broken down (degraded).   This process allows, in particular, the separation of fibre mixtures. To this end, the microorganisms and the hydrolysate are removed in a manner known per se after step ii) and the synthetic polymers which remain are recycled in a manner known per se.
</ABSTRACT>
