<INVENTION-TITLE>
Implantable defibrillator.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61N104	A61N104	A61N105	A61N105	A61N139	A61N139	H01B112	H01B112	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61N	A61N	A61N	A61N	A61N	A61N	H01B	H01B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61N1	A61N1	A61N1	A61N1	A61N1	A61N1	H01B1	H01B1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In an implantable defibrillator having a surface electrode in the form of a net, a spiral or a fabric of electronically conducting material, or an intracardiac electrode in the form of a helix of electronically conducting material, the electrode is completely embedded in a biocompatible, hydrophilic, electrolytically conducting polymer or surrounded by such a polymer.
</ABSTRACT>
