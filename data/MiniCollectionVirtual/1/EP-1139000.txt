<INVENTION-TITLE>
Flange fitting for process plant
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F16L2300	F16L2300	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F16L	F16L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F16L23	F16L23	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The flange unit consists of a flange, a pipe section welded with one end to the flange, and a fastener on the other end of the pipe section, for connection to adapters with different functions. The adapters are fastened via a union nut with cutting ring. The adapters are for steam, water, air, and a blind plug adapter or a nitrogen adapter is fastened to the other end of the pipe section.
</ABSTRACT>
