Prepreg sheet.

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a sheet-like semifinished product made from glass matt-reinforced thermoplastics, in which more than 50% by weight of the glass fibres have a length of greater than 2 mm and more than 5% by weight have a length of less than 2 mm, but virtually no fibres are longer than 50 mm, and to a process for the production of this semifinished product in which the thermoplastic and glass fibres are extruded and a sheet-like web is formed.
</ABSTRACT>
