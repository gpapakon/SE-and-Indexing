<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for treating solid residues from domestic and/or industrial waste incineration and solid product thus obtained.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A62D300	A62D336	A62D10108	B09B300	B09B300	C01F1100	C01F1122	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A62D	A62D	A62D	B09B	B09B	C01F	C01F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A62D3	A62D3	A62D101	B09B3	B09B3	C01F11	C01F11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
To treat solid residue in emissions from household and/or industrial rubbish incineration, they are fed to a mineral acid selected to give a solid where the calcium oxides and/or hydroxide react with the acid to give insoluble calcium salts.
</ABSTRACT>
