<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to new cosmetic compositions comprising, in a cosmetically acceptable carrier, at least one pseudolatex based on a polyblock polycondensate whose chain consists of the repetition of a polysiloxane sequence and a polyurethane and/or polyurea sequence, the said polyurethane and/or polyurea sequence containing, in addition, anionic or cationic groups. These compositions can be used especially in the hair, make-up or skincare cosmetic field.
</ABSTRACT>
