<CLAIMS>
Device (2;31) for the suspension of in particular 
elongate objects (8) preferably in upright position, with 

several holding element pairs arranged one next to another 
and each of two holding elements lying horizontally 

opposite one another between which there extends an 
insertion slit (7;41) for at least one object (8) to be 

suspended, whereby of these holding element pairs at least 
one holding element is formed by a holding arm 

(6a,6b;37,38) which extends obliquely upwardly against the 
other holding element which fulfils a counter-element 

function, whereby at least the upper end region of the 
holding arm (6a,6b;37,38) is laterally displaceable and is 

elastically or yieldingly loaded in the direction towards 
the other holding element, and whereby the mutually 

horizontally neighbouring holding arms (6a,6b;37,38) of 
mutually horizontally neighbouring holding element pairs 

are formed by the flanks of a common holding arm part 
(6c;43a) of elastically bendable material which is held on 

a base part (3;36) in the region between its flanks,
 
characterised in that,
 
the holding arm part (6c;43a) is a flat plate which is bent 

to a V-shape and is held in this V-shape by means of a 
holder arranged as a separate part in its apex region, 

which holder forms a carrier part (5) with which the 
holding arm part (6c;43a) is held to the base part (3;36). 
Device according to claim 1,
 
characterised in that,
 
the carrier part (5) comprises a carrier arm part (5a,5b) 

engaging beneath the apex (6d) of the common holding arm 
part (6c) in its longitudinal direction and a carrier arm 

part (5a,5b) engaging over the apex (6d), which carrier arm 
parts are preferably at least at their forward ends 

supported one against the other.  
 
Device according to claim 2,
 
characterised in that,
 
the lower carrier arm part (5a) is formed by an angled 

profile rod (5c) with upwardly diverging limbs. 
Device according to claim 2 or 3,
 
characterised in that,
 
the upper carrier arm part (5b) is formed by a preferably 

round rod (5d). 
Device according to any of claims 2 to 4,
 
characterised in that,
 
the forward ends of the carrier arm parts (5a,5b) are 

clamped to one another by means of a form part (14) 
engaging over them. 
Device according to claim 5,
 
characterised in that,
 
the form part (14) has a vertical limb (14a) and a 

horizontal limb (14b) of which the vertical limb has a hole 
(15) into which the rod (5d) engages and the horizontal 

limb (14b) engages below the lower carrier arm part (5a) 
and is preferably adapted to the form of the carrier arm 

part (5a). 
Device according to claim 5 or 6,
 
characterised in that,
 
the form part (14) is a press or injection moulded or die-cast 

part and preferably is of plastics, zinc or aluminium. 
Device according to any of claims 2 to 7,
 
characterised in that,
 
the rearward ends of the upper carrier arm part (5b) are in 

each case engaged over by a fixing (16). 
Device according to any of the preceding claims,
 
characterised in that,
  
 

the carrier arms (5) or the lower carrier arm parts (5a) 
are attached with their rearward ends to a vertical 

attachment plate (4) or a vertical attachment frame, which 
preferably has holes for its attachment to a wall or the 

like. 
Device according to claim 9,
 
characterised in that,
 
the fixings (16) are formed by a common form part, in 

particular by a profile sheet of nose-like shape, which is 
attached to the plate (4) or to a frame and extends over 

all carrier arms (5). 
Device (2;31) for the suspension of in particular 
elongate objects (8) preferably in upright position, having 

two holding elements lying horizontally oppositely to one 
another, between which there extends an insertion slit 

(7;41) for at least one object (8) to be suspended,
 
   whereby of these holding elements at least one holding 

element is formed by a holding arm (6a,6b;37,38) extending 
against the other holding element, fulfilling a counter-element 

function, preferably obliquely upwardly, 
whereby at least the upper end region of the holding arm 

(6a,6b;37,38) is laterally displaceable and is loaded 
elastically or yieldingly in the direction of the other 

holding element,
 
   whereby above or below the holding element pair 

(32,33) a further holding element pair (32,33), with 
holding elements lying horizontally mutually oppositely, is 

arranged, of which one holding element (37,39,38,40), 
arranged below or above the holding arm, is laterally 

displaceable and loaded in the direction towards the 
horizontally oppositely lying other holding element by 

means of a force,
 
   whereby the other, horizontally oppositely lying, 

holding element forms a counter-element or is likewise 
laterally displaceable and loaded by a yielding force  

 
against the one holding element,
 
   whereby the insertion slits (41,42) provided between 

the holding elements of the holding element pairs (32,33) 
are arranged one above the other
 
   and whereby the holding element arms (37,38,39,40) 

vertically neighbouring one another and of vertically 
neighbouring holding element pairs are formed by the flanks 

of a common holding arm part (6c,43a) of elastically 
bendable material, which is held on a base part (3;36) 

between its flanks,
 
characterised in that,
 
the holding arm part (6c;43a) is a flat plate which is bent 

to a V-shape and is held by means of a holder arranged as 
a separate part in its apex region in the form of a 

sideways tilted V which forms a carrier part (34,35) with 
which the holder arm part (6c,43a) is held on the base part 

(3;36). 
Device according to any of the preceding claims,
 
characterised in that,
 
the holding arm or holding arms (6a,6b;37,38,39,40) enclose 

an angle with the horizontal of approximately 15 to 70°, in 
particular approximately 30 to 55°, in particular 

approximately 45°. 
Device according to any of the preceding claims,
 
characterised in that,
 
the holding element of the further holding element pair 

(33) exercising the counter-element function is formed by 
a holding arm (39,40) arranged and formed to lie mirror-wise 

oppositely to the associated holding arm (39,40). 
Device according to any of the preceding claims,
 
characterised in that,
 
the holding element or holding arm, or the holding elements 

or holding arms (6a,6b;37,38,39,40) of the upper and/or 
lower pair have a horizontal width of a size such that  

 
several suspension loca
tions for the objects are formed 
arranged one behind another in the longitudinal direction 

of the insertion slit (7;41,42). 
Device according to any of the preceding claims,
 
characterised in that,
 
the holding element or holding arm or the holding elements 

or holding arms (6a,6b;37,38,39,40) of the upper and/or 
lower pair (32,33), in their free initial disposition, lie 

near to one another or contact one another or press against 
one another. 
Device according to any of the preceding claims,
 
characterised in that,
 
the holding part or parts (6c;43a) are of rubber or 

plastics and are preferably fibre or textile reinforced. 
Device according to any of the preceding claims,
 
characterised in that,
 
the holding element or holding elements exercising the 

counter-element function are attached to carrier parts 
(5;34,35) extending parallel to the insertion slits (7;41) 

and are connected by these parts to the base part (3;36). 
Device according to any of the preceding claims,
 
characterised in that,
 
several holding element pairs are arranged horizontally 

neighbouring one another and/or above one another. 
Device according to any of the preceding claims,
 
characterised in that,
 
in each case the holding element or the common holding arm 

part (6c;43a) and the associated carrier part (5;34,35) are 
connectable to one another by means of a preferably snap-in 

form-fitting mounting. 
Device according to any of the preceding claims,
  
 

characterised in that,
 
each holding arm part (43a) is held by two holding rods 

(44e) each arranged at a spacing (c) from the outermost 
base point (46) of the V-shape or preferably in middle 

region of the holding arms (37,39;38,40), and fixing the 
holding arms in a form-fitting manner in their divergent 

disposition. 
Device according to claim 20,
 
characterised in that,
 
a further holding rod (44d) is arranged in the region of 

the base of the V-shape, in particular at the inner base 
point of the V-shape. 
Device according to claim 20 or 21,
 
characterised in that,
 
the holding rods (44d,44e) engage over the associated 

holding arm part (43a) outwardly of the divergent holding 
arms (37,39;38,40) and thereby preferably engage each in a 

groove (40), or penetrate the holding arms in a hole (43b) 
or are embedded therein, in particular are vulcanized 

therein. 
Device according to any of claims 20 to 22,
 
characterised in that,
 
the rearward ends of the holding rods (44d,44e) project 

beyond the associated holder compartment (43a) and are 
attached to a base part (36) preferably releasably, in 

particular by means of a plug-in connection (49). 
Device according to any of the preceding claims,
 
characterised in that,
 
the carrier part (5;34,35) is formed by a U- or E-shaped 

bracket (44). 
Device according to any of the preceding claims,
 
characterised in that,
  
 

the carrier parts (5;34,35) are connected, preferably 
releasably, with a common, preferably plate-like or frame-like 

carrier base (3;36). 
Device according to claim 25,
 
characterised in that,
 
the carrier base is formed by a U-shape profile sheet (51) 

and the carrier parts (34,35) are attached to the profile 
cross-piece (51a). 
Device according to claim 25 or 26,
 
characterised in that,
 
the carrier parts (34,35) are connected with the carrier 

base (36) by means of a plug-in connection (49) effective 
preferably in a form-fitting manner. 
Device according to claim 27,
 
characterised in that,
 
the plug-in connection (49) effective in a form-fitting 

manner is formed by means of plug-in holes (53), associated 
with the free ends of the bracket limbs (44b), in the 

carrier base (36), preferably in the profile cross-piece 
(51a), and by bracket limb ends (44c) bent away 

transversely to the plane of the bracket (44), which in the 
plugged-in position engage behind the carrier base (36), 

preferably behind the profile cross-piece (51a). 
Device according to any of the preceding claims,
 
characterised in that,
 
the length (l) of the bracket limbs (44b) is adapted to the 

width (d) of the common holding arm part (43a).
