<INVENTION-TITLE>
Arrangement for a radio ripple control receiver in lighting masts.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H02J1300	H02J1300	H05B3702	H05B3702	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H02J	H02J	H05B	H05B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H02J13	H02J13	H05B37	H05B37	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
It is proposed for the arrangement of radio ripple control receivers in lighting poles to integrate the evaluation section of the radio ripple control receiver in the terminal protection device or tariff meter and to integrate the receiving section in the luminaire of the lighting pole.
