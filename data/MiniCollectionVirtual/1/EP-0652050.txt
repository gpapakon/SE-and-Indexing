<INVENTION-TITLE>
Filter means support for a reversible filter centrifuge.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B04B300	B04B302	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B04B	B04B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B04B3	B04B3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A reversible filter centrifuge with an unsupported and rotatably mounted filter drum in a housing, which filter drum has a filter drum floor (10) and a filter drum casing (11) which is connected thereto is provided with a filter drum cover (2) which closes off the free end side of the filter drum and can be displaced in the axial direction of the filter drum, an axially displaceable pusher plate (20) being provided within the filter drum adjacent to the filter drum floor (10) and located essentially parallel thereto, a hose-like filter means (12) being mounted with its first axial end on the pusher plate (20) and with its second axial end on the filter drum in the region of the free end side. The filter drum casing (11) is provided at its radially inner side with grooves (14), parallel to the axis, for carrying away filtrate.
