<ABSTRACT>
A magnetostrictive transducer has a magnetostrictive layer (2) arranged on a flexible element, for example a flexible rod (11). The flexible element (11) is deflected by an outer magnetic field. The thin magnetostrictive layer is composed of Tb(1-x)Dy(x)Te2.
</ABSTRACT>
