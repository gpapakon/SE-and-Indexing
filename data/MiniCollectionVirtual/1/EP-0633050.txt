<INVENTION-TITLE>
Use of deca-dodecasil 3R for adsorption and detection of carbone dioxide.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Use of decadodecasil 3R for the adsorption of carbon dioxide from a gaseous medium containing it. The invention is aimed more specifically at the use of decadodecasil 3R for the extraction of carbon dioxide and for the measurement and/or detection of CO2 in gaseous media.
</ABSTRACT>
