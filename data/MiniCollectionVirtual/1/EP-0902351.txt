<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method and device for determining distance values between vectors
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01B1100	G01B1100	G01C306	G01C306	G06K974	G06K976	G10L1500	G10L1508	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01B	G01B	G01C	G01C	G06K	G06K	G10L	G10L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01B11	G01B11	G01C3	G01C3	G06K9	G06K9	G10L15	G10L15	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The method involves storing reference vectors as a hologram in a crystal (7) using a system that has a laser (1), a beam splitter (2), lenses (3,4), expanded beam (14) and  optical modulator (5). The input is from a microphone (20). The crystal is exposed to the test signal which is combined with the reference for different wavelengths. The output is to a CCD matrix sensor. An Independent claim is included for a system for the determination of the spacing values.
</ABSTRACT>
