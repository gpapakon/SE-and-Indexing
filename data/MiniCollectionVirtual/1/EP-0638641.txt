<INVENTION-TITLE>
Miniature bioreactor with continuous renewal of nutrient medium.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C12M100	C12M100	C12M300	C12M300	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C12M	C12M	C12M	C12M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C12M1	C12M1	C12M3	C12M3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The reactor makes it possible to renew the nutrient medium in the culture chamber (1) continuously. The fluids are caused to flow in a closed circuit by an integrated pump (2) acting on a hydraulic circuit (6b) which pushes the fresh nutrient medium from the reservoir (4) into the culture chamber (1), then into the spent medium reservoir (3) separated from the fresh medium (4) by a flexible wall (5). The experimenter can observe the cells under culture without opening the rector or interrupting the replenishment.
