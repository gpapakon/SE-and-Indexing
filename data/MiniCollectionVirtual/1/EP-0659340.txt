<INVENTION-TITLE>
Preservation of column materials in aqueous solutions
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Combination composed of 2-methyl-4-isothiazolin-3-one hydrochloride and 5-bromo-5-nitro-1,3-dioxane for eliminating microorganisms and for preserving column materials in aqueous solutions. The biocide mixture is preferably added at concentrations of 0.001 to 0.3 % (w/v).
</ABSTRACT>
