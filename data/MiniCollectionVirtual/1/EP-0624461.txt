<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to laminated safety glass comprising a monolithic or laminated substrate made of glass and/or plastic, and a sheet of plastic comprising at least one outer layer of polyurethane having surface properties. According to the invention, the glass is provided on at least one area of the polyurethane layer with a bonding prelayer compatible with a subsequently deposited adhesive layer, this prelayer being deposited after an electrical treatment applied to at least that area of the polyurethane layer which is to receive the prelayer, this electrical treatment being chosen from treatments of the corona-discharge type.
