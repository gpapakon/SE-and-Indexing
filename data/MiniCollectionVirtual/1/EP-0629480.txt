<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method and apparatus for the manufacture of molds.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B29C3338	B29C3338	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B29C	B29C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B29C33	B29C33	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In the manufacture of moulds, in particular for injection moulding, blow moulding, foaming and spark erosion or the like, in certain cases also of machine components, a great advantage in terms of time and cost can be achieved by the mould (6, 9) being taken with a silicone intermediate step (5, 7) via a primary mould part (2) directly from the original functional sample part (1), without any consideration for shrinkage.
