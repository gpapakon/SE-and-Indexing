<INVENTION-TITLE>
Polymer resins and their use.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C03C2524	C03C2526	C08G5900	C08G5930	C08L6300	C08L6300	H05K103	H05K103	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to water-soluble or water-dispersible polymer resins and to the use thereof of film formers in aqueous sizes (sizing agents) for glass fibres, which are then used in flame-resistant moulding compositions.
</ABSTRACT>
