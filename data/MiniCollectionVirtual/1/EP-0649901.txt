<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to permanent cell lines of myeloid origin which have functional and antigenic properties of Langerhans cells. The cell lines can be cultivated in vitro stably and without loss of function and without limitation and are advantageously suitable for carrying out biological, immunological, medical, pharmacological, toxicological and/or cosmetic investigations.
</ABSTRACT>
