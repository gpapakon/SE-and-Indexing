<INVENTION-TITLE>
Under floor heating.

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an underfloor heating system comprising prefabricated structural elements consisting essentially of two plates (1), preferably deep-drawn plastics plates, placed mirror-invertedly one on top of the other and locked one into the other, between which heat-exchanger tubes (9) are laid and secured and air is guided through the interspace of the two plates over the heat-exchanger tubes (9) into the room in which the temperature is to be adjusted, for the purpose of a rapid reaction to a changed heat demand or temperature changes in this room.
