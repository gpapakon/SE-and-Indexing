<INVENTION-TITLE>
Automatic control for adjusting a vehicle power seat.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60N202	B60N202	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60N	B60N	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60N2	B60N2	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The device comprises a casing formed of a cell (11) equipped with an arrangement (110) for accommodating, supporting and electrically connecting at least one board which carries an electronic circuit and which is provided with a connector to the motor and to the energy source, of a chamber (12) equipped with means (120) for inserting a chip card and exchanging and storing with this card data relating to the position of the seat, of a duct (13) connecting the cell (11) and chamber (12) together and destined to receive an electrical coupling (131) for electrically connecting together the arrangement (110) of the cell (11) and the means (120) of the chamber (12), and of a fastener (14) for securing the casing to the seat. Application especially to the seat of a land motor vehicle.
