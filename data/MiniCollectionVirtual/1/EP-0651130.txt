<INVENTION-TITLE>
Packing sleeve for a well packer and method for constructing such a packer.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E21B1700	E21B1710	E21B3312	E21B33122	E21B33127	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E21B	E21B	E21B	E21B	E21B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>E21B17	E21B17	E21B33	E21B33	E21B33	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Packing sleeve (7) for the end sealing of the tubular, expandable envelope (8) of a well packer (6), with a cylindrical body (13) of elastic compressible plastic which has an eccentric, axially parallel main leadthrough (14) for a pipe section, anchor (3) or the like as well as at least one axially parallel secondary leadthrough (15-17) for a fluid-supply or fluid-disposal line (10-12) in the crescent-shaped cross-sectional area (18) of the body (13) remaining around the main leadthrough (14).
