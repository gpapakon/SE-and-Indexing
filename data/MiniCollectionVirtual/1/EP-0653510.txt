<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for treatment of printed waste paper.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>D21B100	D21B132	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>D21B	D21B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>D21B1	D21B1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a process for the treatment, in particular the cleaning after pulping, of waste paper, with pre-cleaning or pre-screening and at least dispersion of part of the waste paper, as well as flotation or washing of the same. The invention is characterised by the provision, carried out directly after the coarse cleaning/pre-screening in a screen basket pressure fine screen having a slot width of the screen basket of at most 0.15 mm, of a stock which is finally cleaned with respect to fine screening as the throughput of the pressure screen and routing of the severely contaminated overflow component of the pressure screen at least to a disperger stage connected downstream of a dewatering stage and a subsequent flotation and/or washing stage.
