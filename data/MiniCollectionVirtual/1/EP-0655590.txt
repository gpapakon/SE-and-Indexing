<INVENTION-TITLE>
Compression heat pump and method for operating it.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F24D1900	F24D1910	F25B4902	F25B4902	G05D2319	G05D2319	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F24D	F24D	F25B	F25B	G05D	G05D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F24D19	F24D19	F25B49	F25B49	G05D23	G05D23	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The heat pump, which is provided for heating purposes, comprises a compressor, a condenser, a restrictor and an evaporator which operates under the influence of the outside temperature (external temperature). The compressor is driven by an asynchronous motor (induction motor) the speed of which can be controlled in order to match the heating capacity of the heat pump to the heat requirements (heat demand) of the heating in the speed-controllable range. The result is a reduction in the additional heating capacity which is otherwise required in the case of low outside temperatures. Furthermore, in the case of higher outside temperatures the cyclic operation starts at a lower performance level. The result is a corresponding rise in the coefficient of performance.
