<INVENTION-TITLE>
Improved noise shaping coder in particular sigma/delta coder.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H03M302	H03M302	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H03M	H03M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H03M3	H03M3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The coder comprises, at the front end, an amplifier with gain which is adjustable by switched capacitances (A0, C1, C2, C4). Some of the elements of the adjustable-gain amplifier also form the switched-capacitance integrator of the coder (A0, C1, C2).
