<INVENTION-TITLE>
Mechanical release device.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60T720	B60T720	B60T1300	B60T1308	F16D6516	F16D6522	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60T	B60T	B60T	B60T	F16D	F16D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
A mechanical release device (1) for the automatic release of overrun brake devices in the reverse travel of trailers with overrun braking provides for a swivelling bell-crank lever (8), which is pivotably joined to the primary brake shoes (4) and secondary brake shoes (5) of the overrun drum brake. An automatic release of the overrun brake in reverse travel is ensured by the reciprocal assignment and guiding of the individual components of the release device (1).
