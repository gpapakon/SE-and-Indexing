<INVENTION-TITLE>
Gangsaw to saw granite blocks into slabs.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B28D102	B28D106	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B28D	B28D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B28D1	B28D1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The gang saw (multiple-blade supporting structure) includes four support devices produced using pairs of crossed links (4 and 5) which connect the rockers (3) to the supports (6 and 7) rendered integral with the blade holder (10) by the soleplates (8), with the advantage of considerably increasing the radius of curvature of the stroke of the blade holder during oscillation.
