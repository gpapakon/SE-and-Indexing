<ABSTRACT>
Device for rendering against payment a service, characterised in that it comprises means for displaying the values of at least a first set of parameters for determining the said service, and a single control unit able, on the one hand, to allow the selecting of the desired value of the said parameter, and on the other hand, to validate the choice made.

<CLAIMS>
A device for dispensing a service, against payment, from a 
plurality of possible choices as a function of various parameters 

(11, 12, 13, 14) each being capable of taking a plurality of values, 
said device comprising means (6) for displaying values proposed for 

each parameter of said service, said device being characterized in 
that it comprises a single control member (7, 17) adapted firstly to 

allow selection on the display means (6) of the desired value for 
each parameter and secondly to confirm parameter by parameter the 

values thus selected. 
A device according to claim 1, characterized in that the 
display means are formed by a screen (6) and in that the single 

control member comprises a knob (7) mounted to rotate in a plane 
substantially parallel to the screen (6). 
A device according to claim 2, characterized in that the knob 
(7) is provided with a push-button (17) adapted to effect the 

confirmation operation. 
A device according to any one of claims 1 to 3, characterized 
in that the values of each parameter are displayed in columns. 
A device according to any one of the preceding claims, 
characterized in that it comprises means (41) for displaying 

additional information, whose confirmation triggers the display of a 
set of parameters. 
A device according to any one of the preceding claims, 
characterized in that it comprises means (23) for entering a code of 

alphanumeric nature corresponding to a payment and means (22) for 
verifying said code in order to allow the said service to be 

dispensed. 
A device according to any one of the preceding claims, 
characterized in that it comprises automatic means (26) for 

preselection of a given value of each parameter.  
 
A device according to the preceding claim, characterized in 
that the said preselection can be modified. 
</CLAIMS>
