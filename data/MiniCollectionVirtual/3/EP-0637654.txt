<INVENTION-TITLE>
Panel from compacted cork, in particular for sound insulation, and cork slab for making this panel.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a panel, particularly for acoustic (sound) insulation, characterised in that it is composed of two juxtaposed slabs (20) of agglomerated (compacted) cork, each including, on their face opposite the other, (22), respective networks of projecting blocks (24) designed to allow the said projecting blocks (24) provided on the two slabs (20) to imbricate. The invention also relates to the basic slabs designed to form such a panel.
