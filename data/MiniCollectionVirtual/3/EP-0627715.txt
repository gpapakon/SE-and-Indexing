<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a deposit lock, especially for a shopping trolley, with an information carrier 2, especially a card, which can be attached temporarily in or on the deposit lock 1 and by means of which the lock can be released and which can be connected to a store computer, whilst information can be read off from the information carrier 2 and can be transmitted to the store computer and/or information which can be transmitted from the store computer can be memorised on the information carrier 2.
