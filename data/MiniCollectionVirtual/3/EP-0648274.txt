<INVENTION-TITLE>
METHOD OF PREPARING 3-HYDROXYPHENYLACETIC ACID
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C12P742	C12R1645	C12P740	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C12P	C12R	C12P	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention concerns a method for the preparation of 3-hydroxyphenylacetic acid by cultivating a microorganism of the species (Rhizoctonia), (Ceratobasidium) or (Pellicularia) in the presence of a phenyl compound of general formula (I), in which X = COOR, CH2OH, CH2NH2 or (II) and R = methyl, ethyl, hydrogen, sodium, potassium or ammonium.
</ABSTRACT>
