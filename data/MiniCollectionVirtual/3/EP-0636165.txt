<INVENTION-TITLE>
DETERGENT COMPOSITIONS CONTAINING LYSINE LAURYGLYCINATE.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61K830	A61K844	A61Q502	A61Q502	A61Q1910	A61Q1910	C11D102	C11D110	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61K	A61K	A61Q	A61Q	A61Q	A61Q	C11D	C11D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61K8	A61K8	A61Q5	A61Q5	A61Q19	A61Q19	C11D1	C11D1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The novel detergent compositions chiefly for use in body care products are characterized by having two amino acids in the form of lysine lauryglycinate. Such compositions have a particular affinity for the skin and hair together with a high foaming and cleaning potential. They also have anti-radical properties.
</ABSTRACT>
