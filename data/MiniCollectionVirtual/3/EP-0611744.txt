<ABSTRACT>
1,1,1,3,3-Pentafluoropropane is prepared in a particularly advantageous manner by subjecting 1,1,1,3,3-pentafluoro-2-dichloropropane or 1,1,1,3,3-pentafluoro-2,2,3-trichloropropane or any mixtures of these to catalytic hydrogenation.
</ABSTRACT>
