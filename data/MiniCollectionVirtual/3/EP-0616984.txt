<INVENTION-TITLE>
Lead-free crystal glass with a high light transmission
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C03C3097	C03C400	C03C3087	C03C400	C03C3076	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C03C	C03C	C03C	C03C	C03C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C03C3	C03C4	C03C3	C03C4	C03C3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Lead-free crystal glass, having a light transmission of at least 85%, a refractive index (nd) of more than 1.52, a density of at least 2.45 g/cm3, and a K2O + BaO + ZnO amt. of more than 10 wt.% comprises 50-75 (wt.%) SiO2, 2-15 Na2O, 1-15 K2O, 3-12 CaO, 1-10 BaO, 0-1- B2O3, 0-5 Al2O3, 0-5 Li2O, 0-5 MgO, 0-7 SrO, 0-7 ZnO, 0-8 TiO2, 0-5 ZrO2, 0.1-5 Nb2O5, 0-5 Ta2O5, and 0-2 F. The sum TiO2 + ZrO2 + Nb2O5 + Ta2O5 is 0.3-12 wt.% and the sum of alkalis is more than 10 wt.%..
</ABSTRACT>
