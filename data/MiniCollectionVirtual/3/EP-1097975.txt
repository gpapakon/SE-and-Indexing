<INVENTION-TITLE>
Self-adhesive masking tape
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B05B1504	B05B1504	C09J704	C09J704	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B05B	B05B	C09J	C09J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B05B15	B05B15	C09J7	C09J7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The product has a maximum traction force of over 10 N/cm (width) and the nonadhesive composite material has a transverse bending resistance of over 0.10 N/mm. The textile structure can be knitted, woven, nonwoven, braided, switchbonded or a combination of these. In one embodiment, the carrier material consists of viscose nonwoven, sewn over with a polyester thread with a 0.3% water absorbency.
</ABSTRACT>
