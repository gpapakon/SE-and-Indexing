<INVENTION-TITLE>
Intumescent composition of the two-components-type.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08K500	C08K5521	C09D518	C09D518	C09D16120	C09D16120	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an intumescent composition of the two-component type which comprises at least     (A) an amino-formaldehyde resin and (B) a phosphoric acid ester (phosphate) mixed with at least one additive selected from substances which are capable of water absorption, fillers, pigments, surfactants and mixtures thereof. This composition is suitable for use as a fire protection composition, in particular for coating or impregnating glass fibre mats.
</ABSTRACT>
