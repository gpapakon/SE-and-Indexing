<INVENTION-TITLE>
Modular assembly for selective collecting and transporting of refuse.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65F100	B65F100	B65F112	B65F112	B65F114	B65F114	B65F300	B65F300	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65F	B65F	B65F	B65F	B65F	B65F	B65F	B65F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65F1	B65F1	B65F1	B65F1	B65F1	B65F1	B65F3	B65F3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Assembly characterised in that each module (1), arranged transversely on the chassis of the vehicle and equipped at at least one of its ends with an opening for permitting lateral loading and/or unloading of the refuse, includes faces forming the bottom (2,13) of the lower compartment and the upper compartment which are equipped with a conveyor belt (5) driven in rotation in at least one direction, the face forming the bottom (2) of the upper compartment also being driven in an up-and-down movement inside the said container in order to permit the selective introduction at a constant height of refuse, by simple displacement of the movable plate (2) forming the bottom of the upper compartment above or below the opening of the chamber which is provided at head-height. Application to the collection of household (domestic) waste.
