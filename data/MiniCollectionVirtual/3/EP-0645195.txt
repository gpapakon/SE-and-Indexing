<INVENTION-TITLE>
Sonotrode manufacture method.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61C1300	A61C1300	B06B300	B06B300	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61C	A61C	B06B	B06B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61C13	A61C13	B06B3	B06B3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A method of manufacturing sonotrodes for the ultrasonic machining of workpieces, in particular high-performance ceramics, for producing precision mouldings, in particular ceramic tooth replacements, is characterised in that at least one of the pre-sonotrodes serving as a casting model for the sonotrodes is produced at least partly from a flexible, fully combustible model material. The method is distinguished by speed and high precision, so that the cost-effective manufacture of individually shaped dental appliances becomes possible.
