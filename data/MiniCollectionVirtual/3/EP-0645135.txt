<INVENTION-TITLE>
Sunscreen composition containing hemodialysate.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
An improvement in the condition of the skin and a reduction or prevention of the adverse consequences of exposure to the sun are achieved with a sunscreen composition which, besides cosmetic ancillary substances and sun protection filters and/or sun blockers, contains an effective amount of a deproteinized haemodialysate from the blood of mammals or an active fraction thereof. The composition is suitable for both prophylaxis and aftertreatment.
</ABSTRACT>
