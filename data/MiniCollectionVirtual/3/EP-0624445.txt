Shredder.

<ABSTRACT>
A shredder for reducing material from trees or garden and household waste is additionally provided in the discharge duct (A) with a barrier area having a multiplicity of openings for granulating the material to be put through. For a good throughput, the interior of the reduction chamber is provided with special features. The added barrier area, in the form of a perforated plate, produces a granulated end product of substantially the same size.
