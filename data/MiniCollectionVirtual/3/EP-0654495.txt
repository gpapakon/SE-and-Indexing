<INVENTION-TITLE>
Photoconductive film formed from a grafted silane polymer; process for preparing and process for the formation of an electrostatic image of this film and an optical valve based on this film
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G03G507	C08G7700	C08G7760	G03G507	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G03G	C08G	C08G	G03G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
Photoconductive grafted silane polymer with high carrier mobility and a photoconductive film formed from such a polymer. The grafted silane polymer corresponds to the formula: 
<
IMAGE
>
  in which:   R1, which is identical or different, is chosen from lower alkyl radicals, R2 is especially a lower alkyl, phenyl or -A-Het radical, A being a single bond, a divalent chain with delocalised  pi  electrons, a 1,2- or 1,3- or 1,4-phenylene nucleus, Het being especially a carbazolyl radical, provided that at least 10 % of the number of the radicals R2 correspond to the -A-Het- radical.   Application to electrostatic image reproduction, especially using an optical valve device.
</ABSTRACT>
