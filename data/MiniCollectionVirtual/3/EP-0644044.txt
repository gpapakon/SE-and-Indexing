<INVENTION-TITLE>
Packaging material and method of making it.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B32B904	B32B904	B65D6546	B65D6546	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B32B	B32B	B65D	B65D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B32B9	B32B9	B65D65	B65D65	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Packaging material having a sheet-like, padding inner layer (10) of a natural fibre nonwoven and two covering layers (12, 14), covering the said inner layer on both sides, characterised in that the covering layers comprise outer films consisting of material which is substantially completely biodegradable, and a method of making such a packaging material.
