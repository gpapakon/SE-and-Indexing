<INVENTION-TITLE>
Pipe seal.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F16L1700	F16L1702	F16L2102	F16L2103	F16L4700	F16L4710	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F16L	F16L	F16L	F16L	F16L	F16L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F16L17	F16L17	F16L21	F16L21	F16L47	F16L47	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Ring-sealed pipe seal between socket (1) and spigot (2) of a pipeline system, in the case of which the socket (1) is designed in a stepped manner on the inner side such that the internal diameter of the socket is greater at the collar-side end (7) of the annular groove (6) than at the socket-face-side end (8) of the annular groove (6). Consequently, retaining, fixing and supporting elements (5) of the sealing system can be fitted in the widened collar portion without the inner sealing gap having to be widened.
