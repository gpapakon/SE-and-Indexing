<INVENTION-TITLE>
Radiotelephone with recording of speech information
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H04M160	H04M160	H04M165	H04M1656	H04M172	H04M1725	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H04M	H04M	H04M	H04M	H04M	H04M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H04M1	H04M1	H04M1	H04M1	H04M1	H04M1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The apparatus includes a digital signal processor (3) which converts speech information transferred by radio into digital speech information. The digital speech information can be output via a converter unit as analogue speech signals or vice-versa. A signal for a digital memory (10) for digital recording of the speech information can be tapped in the data line (4) between the signal processor and a converter unit (5a,5b). Preferably, the digital memory is a semiconductor memory and has a transceiver unit (1).
</ABSTRACT>
