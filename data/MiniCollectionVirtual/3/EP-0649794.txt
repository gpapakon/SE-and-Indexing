<INVENTION-TITLE>
Plastic pallet and method of manufacturing the same.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a plastic pallet. It includes, in combination, an upper element or a central element provided with an array of stiffening grooves and a plurality of feet (mounts) (4,5,6,7,8) constituted by a set of vertical walls and of hollow longitudinal members (9,10,11) produced by extrusion, these being fastened, for example by welding or adhesive bonding, to the lower part of a series of aligned feet (6,7,8). The invention also relates to the method of manufacturing such a pallet.
