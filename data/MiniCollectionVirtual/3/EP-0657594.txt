<INVENTION-TITLE>
Panel for heat insulating of buildings.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E04B176	E04B176	E04D1316	E04D1316	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
A panel (1,3) for the heat insulation of buildings, in particular roofs, it being possible for the panel (1,3) to be clamped in between roof rafters (2), consists, according to the invention, of Styropor which is elasticised by pressing and has recovery properties.
