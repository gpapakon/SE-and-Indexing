<INVENTION-TITLE>
Kitchen stove with a glass-ceramic plate.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H05B368	H05B374	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H05B	H05B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H05B3	H05B3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Stove with a hob, preferably a glass-ceramic hob, equipped with hob-level input elements, a hob position controller, a digital display and, possibly, pot detection logic, which hob position controller (1), which can be selected on the input side by means of hob level input elements (6.1 to 6.4), supplies on the output side a databus to the heating power controller (7), a databus for digital indication (2) and a databus for pot detection (3), the hob position controller (1) corresponding with interface electronics (4, 5).
