<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a measurement device (102), in particular for measuring the level of glucose in the blood, intended to be combined with a removable sensor (108) comprising a plurality of active zones (144). In particular, the invention relates to a mobile connector (150) which electrically connects the removable sensor (108) to an electronic measurement circuit integral with the casing (104) of this measurement device. This electrical connection is produced by means of sliding electrical contacts.
