<INVENTION-TITLE>
Box-like package
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
A box-like package, in particular for pourable products, consists of a cardboard sleeve which is closed at the bottom or of a multilayer lamination of cardboard and/or plastic and/or metal and a cup-shaped lid which can be inserted in the sleeve and has a bottom, an essentially cylindrical side wall bearing against the inner side of the sleeve, and an upper annular part which has, on the outside, a step resting on the end face of the sleeve and a gripping edge drawn inwards. Such a package can be produced cost-effectively and in a segregated design in that the cup-shaped lid consists of a thermoformed part, comprising its bottom and side wall and made of cardboard or a cardboard lamination, and a separately produced annular part connected to the thermoformed part.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
Box-like pack in particular for free-flowing products, comprising a 
sleeve (1), which is closed at the bottom, made from cardboard or a multilayer 

composite of cardboard and/or plastic and/or metal and a cup-shaped 
cover (2) insertable into the sleeve and resting on the front face of the 

sleeve, said cover comprising a deep-drawn part (3) made from cardboard or 
cardboard composite with a bottom (4) and a substantially cylindrical side 

wall (5) engaging on the inside of the sleeve and a separately manufactured, 
upper ring part (6), which has an inwardly drawn gripping edge (13), and 

which is manufactured as a cup-shaped, deep-drawn moulding of cardboard or 
cardboard composite with a bottom (15) and a side wall, whereof the bottom 

(15) is partly punched out for forming the gripping edge (13) and which 
reversed is placed on the other deep-drawn part (3) and is joined to its 

side wall (5) by adhesion or heat sealing, characterized in that on its substantially 
cylindrical wall (16), the cup-shaped moulding is inwardly shaped 

to a double layer and with the double-layer wall externally engages over the 
side wall (5) of the other deep-drawn part (3), in the area thereof it is 

connected thereto and engages on the front face of the sleeve (1). 
Pack according to claim 1, characterized in that the bottom (15) of the 
ring part (6) is bent over inwards on the edge (19) of the punched blank (18) 

forming the gripping edge (13). 
Pack according to claim 1 or 2, characterized in that the bottom (15) 
of the ring part (6) is bent over inwards to the double layer on the edge 

(19) of the punched blank (18) forming the gripping edge. 
Pack according to one of the claims 1 to 3, characterized in that on its 
periphery, at the transition from the bottom (15) to the side wall (16), 

the ring part (6) has an inwardly drawn in, circumferential stacking step 
(20), which centres and fixes an engaged, similar pack in the area of its 

bottom. 
</CLAIMS>
