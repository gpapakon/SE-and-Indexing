<INVENTION-TITLE>
Work table.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47B2100	A47B2100	A47B2103	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47B	A47B	A47B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47B21	A47B21	A47B21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The frame (1) of the worktable has a horizontal rod (9) oriented transversely to a table user. Each table leaf (13, 14, 18) is equipped with a bearing element (10, 11, 15, 16, 28) for detachable fixture to the frame (1) and can be fixed to the rod (9) by means of this bearing element (10, 11, 15, 16). Furthermore, each table leaf (13, 14, 18) can be pivoted about the rod (9) and locked in each pivoted position by means (12).
