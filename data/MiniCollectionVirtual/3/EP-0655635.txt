<INVENTION-TITLE>
Microscope with illumination system.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G02B2106	G02B2106	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G02B	G02B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G02B21	G02B21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The illumination system of a microscope (1) illuminates with its light source (2) the viewing object with variable illumination (illuminance, illumination level) and focuses the light beams. The light source (2) can be displaced along the optical axis (10, 11) or along the beam path of the illumination system for the purpose of varying the illumination.
