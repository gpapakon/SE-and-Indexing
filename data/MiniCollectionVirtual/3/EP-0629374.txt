<CLAIMS>
Moist wiping cloth (20) for cleaning porous stoneware 
surfaces, having a plane textile backing (22), 

projecting from which are 1000 to 2600 individual, 
unbundled, spaced-apart, 2 to 12 mm long, elastic 

fibres (26, 36, 42, 44, 52) per cm
2
. 
Moist wiping cloth according to claim 1, characterized 
in that the fibres (26, 36, 42, 44, 52) have a length 

in the region of 4 to 10 mm. 
Moist wiping cloth according to claim 1 or 2, 
characterized in that the fibres (26, 36, 42, 44, 52) 

have a length of about 6 mm. 
Moist wiping cloth according to one of claims 1 to 3, 
characterized in that approximately 1400 to 2200 fibres 

(26, 36, 42, 44, 52) per cm
2
 are provided. 
Moist wiping cloth according to one of claims 1 to 4, 
characterized in that approximately 1800 fibres (26, 

36, 42, 44, 52) per cm
2
 are provided. 
Moist wiping cloth according to one of claims 1 to 5, 
characterized in that the fibres (26, 36, 42, 44, 52) 

are polyester fibres. 
Moist wiping cloth according to claim 6, characterized 
in that the backing (22) is likewise made of polyester. 
Moist wiping cloth according to one of claims 1 to 7, 
characterized in that the fibres (26, 36, 42, 44, 52) 

at their free end have a sharp raw edge (40). 
Moist wiping cloth according to claim 8, characterized 
in that the raw edge (40) extends obliquely relative to 

the longitudinal axis of a fibre (36).  
 
Moist wiping cloth according to one of claims 1 to 9, 
characterized in that the fibres (26, 36, 42, 44, 52) 

are cylindrical. 
Moist wiping cloth according to one of claims 1 to 10, 
characterized in that the fibres (26, 36, 42, 44, 52) 

are curved, in particular are curved in the shape of a 
serpentine. 
Cleaning appliance (10) for cleaning porous stoneware 
surfaces, which as a covering has a moist wiping 
cloth 
(20) according to one of claims 1 to 11. 
Cleaning appliance (10) according to claim 12 for the 
conservation cleaning of commercially used areas, such 

as office premises, showrooms and the like, 
characterized in that the dimensions of the moist 

wiping cloth (20) are 50 x 13 cm. 
Cleaning appliance (10) according to claim 12 for the 
conservation cleaning of fine stoneware, in particular 

for cleaning tiles in domestic households, 
characterized in that the dimensions of the moist 

wiping cloth (20) are 40 x 13 cm or 40 x 11 cm. 
</CLAIMS>
