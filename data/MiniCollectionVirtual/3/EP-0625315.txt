<INVENTION-TITLE>
Apparatus for preventing fluid-flow jam in a pipe and device using this apparatus for cigarette manufacture.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention describes an apparatus for preventing a flow build-up in a pipe, pressure fluctuations being compensated by means of an underpressure valve and an overpressure valve located above the latter, and small solid particles occurring at the same time being capable of being returned into the pipe via the underpressure valve. The apparatus is particularly suitable for use in cigarette manufacture.
