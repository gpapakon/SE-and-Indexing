<INVENTION-TITLE>
Storage container with guiding elements
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47F500	B65G104	B65G104	B65D1944	A47F500	B65D1938	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47F	B65G	B65G	B65D	A47F	B65D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
To achieve a smooth storage operation of storage containers (1), it is proposed to mount elements (6) in the form of guide rails on the side walls (4) of the storage container (1), which elements taper at their ends (7) towards the side walls (4) of the storage container (1).

<CLAIMS>
Storage container (1) having extensions fitted 
to both sides (4) as guide elements (6), which 

extensions extend over at least a partial region of the 
side walls (4) in the direction (2) of the depositing 

or withdrawal of stores and have a tapered region (7) 
towards at least a front end (3) of the storage 

container (1). 
Storage container (1) according to claim 1, 
wherein the side walls (4) and the guide elements (6) 

are formed from a section of the storage container (1). 
Rack bay (8) for a storage container (1) 
according to claim 1 or 2, having rack bay limits (10) 

for receiving the storage container (1), wherein the 
rack bay limits (10) at the front end of the rack bay 

have an outwardly bent element (11) at the level of the 
guide elements. 
</CLAIMS>
