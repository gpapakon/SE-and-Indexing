<INVENTION-TITLE>
Pressure and feeding screw for a meat grinder
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B02C1830	B02C1800	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a pressure and feeding screw (1) for meat grinders which is composed of two parts to be connected to one another and is made of different kinds of material. The basic body is a steel shaft (4) on which there is mounted a plastic pressure and feeding screw body (3), which is connected to the steel shaft (4). The steel shaft has attached to it a shaft pin (5) on which there is mounted the set of grinders.

<CLAIMS>
Pressure conveyor screw for meat-mincing machines for industrial use, which has two parts 
and consists of a shaft (4) and a conveyor screw body (3), whereby the pressure and transport 

screw (1) can be accommodated in the pressure casing (2) of the meat-mincing machine in a 
rotating way and in this connection the conveyor screw body (3) is positively linked to the 

shaft (4) and the shaft (4) has a shaft journal (5) with a cutting set (6), wherein 

the conveyor screw body (3) is manufactured from a non-metallic material 
Pressure and transport screw according to claim 1, wherein 

a synthetic material is used as the material for the conveyor screw body.
