<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for controlling the access for paying dynamic signaling, related to an interoffice trunk
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The access control method controls access (A1) across a highway (2) between two switching networks (1). Each application has a rights access attributed allowing access to the input only when the rights have been set.
