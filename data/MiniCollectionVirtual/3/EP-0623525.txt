<INVENTION-TITLE>
Packaging device for rotating brush.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65D7300	B65D7300	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65D	B65D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65D73	B65D73	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Packaging device for a brush for a drill, including a sheet of thermoformed plastic (5) defining a frustoconical dish (6, 7) accommodating the hub part (3) and the bristles (4) of the brush, and a flat portion (12) bearing an information label and equipped with a hanging hole (18). The handle (shaft) (1) of the brush passes through a hole (8) in the bottom (6) of the dish and receives a locking washer (9). This packaging allows purchasers to touch the bristles of the brush, while avoiding the brushes becoming entangled, which is what happens when they are displayed loose.
