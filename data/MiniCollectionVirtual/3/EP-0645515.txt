<INVENTION-TITLE>
Window wing or the like.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a window or the like which comprises an, in particular, rectangular frame and a wing which can be pivoted about one of the vertical frame members (called hinge side hereinbelow), the wing being articulated on the crossmembers of the frame via a pair of pivot scissor mechanisms. According to the invention, the wing (2) and the frame (5) are functionally connected to one another such that that vertical member of the wing (2) which remains in the plane of the frame (5) when the wing (2) is pivoted out (opened) can be displaced away from the hinge side (9).
