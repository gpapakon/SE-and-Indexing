<INVENTION-TITLE>
Apparatus for conditioning water by Ozone.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C02F178	C02F178	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C02F	C02F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C02F1	C02F1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a water treatment plant using ozone, in particular for treating drinking water, using an ozone reactor and feed pumps, valves and filters. In order to make possible continuous provision of treated water, in particular drinking water, according to the invention a plurality of vessels (2, 3) are assigned to the ozone reactor (1), which vessels are both intermediate vessels for the untreated water taken off and storage vessels for the treated water.
