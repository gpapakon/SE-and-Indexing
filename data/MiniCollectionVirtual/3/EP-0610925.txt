<INVENTION-TITLE>
Frustroconical hole shaped without cutting.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B21D2200	B21D2204	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B21D	B21D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B21D22	B21D22	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for shaping a frustoconical hole without cutting. In this method, a conical opening is expanded into a funnel shape by means of a mandrel. To prevent the surface of the workpiece from being deformed by displacement of the material, one or more spaced recesses are provided to absorb the flowing material.
