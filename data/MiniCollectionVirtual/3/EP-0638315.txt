<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to cardiology and represents an antihypoxant ENERGOSTIM based on cytochrome C, nicotinamide adenine dinucleotide and inosine. ENERGOSTIM completely restores energetic metabolism and abolishes hypoxic states of the cardiac tissues, of the brain, of the liver and of other organs.
</ABSTRACT>
