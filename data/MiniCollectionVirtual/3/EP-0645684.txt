<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for monitoring material flow processes in manufacturing.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G05B19418	G05B19418	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G05B19	G05B19	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Method for monitoring material flow processes by means of an automatic fault reporting system which, by means of speech output via a telephone, reports disturbances on-site to the person in charge of the installation and supports him/her in removing the disturbance.
