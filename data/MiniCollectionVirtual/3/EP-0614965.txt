<INVENTION-TITLE>
Light-duty detergent.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C11D312	C11D312	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C11D	C11D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C11D3	C11D3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The light-duty detergent contains 5 to 30% by weight of at least one surfactant, 5 to 60% by weight of kanemites as builder and conventional detergent auxiliaries. The kanemites used as builder can be prepared by reacting sodium silicate which essentially consists of delta -Na2Si2O5 with at least one acid in the pH range from 9 to 13 while stirring.
</ABSTRACT>
