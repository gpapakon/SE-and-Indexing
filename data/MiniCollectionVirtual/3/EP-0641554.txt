<ABSTRACT>
In summary, the technical invention can be classified as a novel further development of an orthopaedic system (crutch), by which     stability   force transmission   fixing of connection elements, and   seat and hold of the connection elements are considerably increased even under severe loads. Consequently, the present invention contributes to increased safety and reliability in orthopaedics. This contribution is also to be protected by the European patent right.
