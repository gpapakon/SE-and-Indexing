Longitudinally adjustable gas spring.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
A gas spring (6) can be connected to a hydraulic adjusting device (25) by locking means, the adjusting device (25) constituting an independent subassembly which has a release device (26) and an actuating device (27) connected to it via a hydraulic hose (28).
