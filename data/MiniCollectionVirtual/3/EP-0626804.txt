<INVENTION-TITLE>
NOVEL AQUEOUS EMULSION PREPARATION METHOD AND RESULTING AQUEOUS EMULSIONS.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
An aqueous emulsion preparation method wherein an organic phase containing a water-insoluble active principle and one or more anionic or cationic agents in a water-insoluble solvent is stirred into an aqueous phase containing a positively or negatively charged polymer, and the active principle containing particles are stirred until the desired size is obtained. Such aqueous emulsions are particularly useful in protection.
</ABSTRACT>
