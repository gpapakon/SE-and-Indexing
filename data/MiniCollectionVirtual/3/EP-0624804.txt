<INVENTION-TITLE>
Tracking system for estimating the pointing error of a HF antenna.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01S314	G01S314	G01S342	H01Q2500	H01Q2504	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01S	G01S	G01S	H01Q	H01Q	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01S3	G01S3	G01S3	H01Q25	H01Q25	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a tracking system intended to estimate the pointing error, along two orthogonal axes, of a UHF antenna towards a target, of the type including a radiating element (20) integral with the primary source of the antenna and particularly conveying a beacon signal transmitted by the target. The system includes:   - a waveguide (22) coupled to the radiating element (20) and including an output for a signal (S2) in odd tracking mode generated from the beacon signal in fundamental mode (S1), the waveguide (22) being followed by a mode reflector (23) supplying a signal (S3) in even fundamental mode; - a bandpass filter (25) tuned to the frequency of the signal in tracking mode (S2); - a phase shifter (27) of the signal in filtered tracking mode (S2) supplied by the bandpass filter (25); - means (29) of summing the output signal from the phase shifter (27) with the signal in fundamental mode (S3), the summing means (29) supplying a sum signal (S) for each given phase shift of the signal in filtered tracking mode; - means (30) for interpreting the sum signals (S) able to determine the pointing error along the two orthogonal axes of the antenna.
