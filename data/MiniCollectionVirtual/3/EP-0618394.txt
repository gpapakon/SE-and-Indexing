<INVENTION-TITLE>
Locking device for spigot and socket couplings.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F16L3700	F16L37084	F16L3728	F16L3732	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F16L	F16L	F16L	F16L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F16L37	F16L37	F16L37	F16L37	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a locking device (V) on plug-in couplings for pipes, hoses (1) or the like, in the case of which the male part (P, plug-in nipple 3), in the plug-in position, passes through an opening (8) of a locking plate (4), which can be shifted resiliently in the female part (M, coupling housing 6), and is locked by engagement of the opening border edge (9) on the outer surface (10) of the male part (P). In order to achieve reliable locking which is free of directional forces, the invention proposes an articulation-pin-free displacement of the locking plate (4) into the locking position.
