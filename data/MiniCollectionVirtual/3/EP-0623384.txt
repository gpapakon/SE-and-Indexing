<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the preparation of dehydrogenation catalysts.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B01J2354	B01J2362	B01J23652	B01J3700	B01J3702	C10G3500	C10G3509	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B01J	B01J	B01J	B01J	B01J	C10G	C10G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B01J23	B01J23	B01J23	B01J37	B01J37	C10G35	C10G35	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Process for the preparation of a catalyst, the said catalyst including at least one refractory inorganic support, at least one halogen or halogen compound in a weight concentration higher than 0.1%, and at least one metal of group VIII of the Periodic Classification (platinum, palladium, nickel and ruthenium) and at least one additional metal M chosen from germanium, tin, lead, iron, titanium and chromium, characterised in that the said metal M is introduced in the form of at least one organometallic complex. The catalyst prepared according to the invention is suitable for reactions of dehydrogenation, especially of paraffinic hydrocarbons to olefinic hydrocarbons.
</ABSTRACT>
