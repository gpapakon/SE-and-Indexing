<ABSTRACT>
Epoxy resin composition comprising   (A) compounds containing at least two 1,2-epoxide groups which are products of the reaction of   A1) compounds containing at least two 1,2-epoxide groups per molecule   A2) polyoxyalkylene-monoamines having a molecular weight of from 130 to 900 g/mol, and optionally A3) polyoxyalkylene-monoamines having a molecular weight of from 900 to 5000 g/mol, where the oxyalkylene groups in A2) and A3) are selected from oxypropylene and oxyethylene groups, and mixtures thereof, and optionally   A4) polycarboxylic acids,  and   (B) curing agents and (C) optionally conventional additives. ng
