<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the production of indole derivatives.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
In the prepn. of indoles (I) by decarboxylation of the corresp. indole-2-carboxylic acid (II), (II) is treated with HCOOH. Pref., (II) is treated with HCOOH contg. 0-50 vol. % water and heated 1-10, pref. 2-4 hrs. at 20-107, pref. 80-107 deg. C.
