Ink ribbon feeding cassette for thermal transfer printer
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Cassette (1) for feeding a thermal-transfer printing ribbon (10) in a thermal-transfer printer, the cassette (1) being designed for receiving and for retaining detachably and in their relative position of use a ribbon delivery roller (5) and a ribbon take-up roller (6), each provided with means (8, 9) for fastening in the printer, the cassette (1) leaving the said fastening means (8, 9) disengaged.

<CLAIMS>
Shoe (1) for loading a thermal transfer printing ribbon (10) wound, on the one hand,
around a hub (4) in an infeed roller (5) and, on the other hand, fixedly attached to the hub of a

receiving roller (6), the hubs being provided respectively with fixing ends (8, 9), the two
rollers (5, 6) being received and held in a detachable manner in the shoe (1) and the fixing

ends (8, 9) of their hubs axially protruding from the shoe (1).
Loading shoe according to claim 1, characterised in that it is disposed in a box.
Loading shoe according to claim 1, characterised in that it is placed in a capsule.
Loading shoe according to one of claims 1 to 3, wherein at least one of the ribbon rollers
(5, 6) is received in a housing (2, 3) of the shoe (1) at least provided with a friction edge (33)

arranged to cooperate with the roller (5, 6) when it is detached from the shoe (1) and thus to
tension the ribbon (10).
Loading shoe according to claim 4, wherein the housing (2) with the friction edge (33)
comprises a friction tab (32) with an internal wall (33) folded, beyond the zone (36) of

contact between the housing (2) and the roller (5, 6) in the plane (34) of separation of the
roller (5, 6), on the other side of this plane (34).
