<INVENTION-TITLE>
Apparatus for self-contained cooling of high temperature superconducting components, preferably sensors.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F25D1900	F25D1900	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F25D	F25D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F25D19	F25D19	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The object of the invention is to provide a cooling device for sensors, in which no fluctuations at all of the cooling machine take place at the measurement site. On the basis that discontinuous cooling satisfies requirements in a large proportion of application cases, an accumulator vessel, preferably a conical pressure vessel, which is filled with a working material, preferably Progan 8, is arranged according to the invention on the cooling head 4 of a coolant-gas cooling machine. The sensor-cooling surface 5 is likewise located on the pressure vessel 2. The measurement process takes place with the cooling machine switched off.
