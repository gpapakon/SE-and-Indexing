<INVENTION-TITLE>
ELECTRONIC UNIT FOR THE AUTOMATIC CONTROL OF ELECTROCHROMIC REAR-VIEW VEHICLE MIRRORS.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60R108	B60R108	G02F101	G02F1163	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60R	B60R	G02F	G02F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60R1	B60R1	G02F1	G02F1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a circuit for the automatic control of an electrochromic car rear-view mirror, containing a system for receiving images, a comparison circuit, an amplifier and a supply module. According to the invention, the supply module is designed as a voltage pulse stabiliser and is preferably provided with a module for the forced switch-on of the supply of the operating element of the mirror.
</ABSTRACT>
