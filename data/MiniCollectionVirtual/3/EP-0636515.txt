<INVENTION-TITLE>
Rear luggage carrier.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60R900	B60R906	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60R	B60R	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60R9	B60R9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a rear luggage carrier, especially for cars, in the case of which there can be mounted, in the rear region, a fastening device which projects beyond the rear in the direction of the longitudinal axis of the vehicle and has at least one extension element (30), a support (50) being mounted transversely to the longitudinal axis of the vehicle on the extension elements (30) and serving to accommodate bicycles, loads etc. It is made possible for the rear luggage carrier to accommodate large loads, display a high degree of safety and to be easy to handle in that at least one coupling device (33) is provided which is arranged on the vehicle (10), is at a distance from the fastening point(s) in the vertical direction of the vehicle and has at least one supporting strut (40) fastened to it, in that the supporting strut (40) can be connected at its end region facing away from the coupling device (33) to the extension element (30) and/or the carrier (50) and in that the carrier (50) can be fastened at at least two points on the vehicle (10) which are offset with respect to one another in a transverse direction.
