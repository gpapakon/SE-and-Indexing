<CLAIMS>
Pump with priming circuit comprising a valve with a 
seat (5) equipped with an orifice (6) and a shutter 

(7) fitted so as to be able to be applied to the seat 
(5) in front of the orifice (6), the shutter being a 

flexible blade (7) shrouded on one side, characterized 
in that the seat (5) is slipped into a slide (8) 

arranged on a priming element (9) fixed to the pump 
body and in that the flexibility of the flexible blade 

(7) increases from the shrouded side to the end 
opposite this side. 
Pump according to Claim 1, characterized in that the 
width of the blade (7) decreases from the shrouded 

side to the opposite end. 
Pump according to Claim 2, characterized in that the 
blade (7) comprises a triangular section (14). 
Pump according to Claim 1 or 2, characterized in that 
the thickness of the blade (7) decreases from the 

shrouded side to the opposite end. 
Pump according to one of Claims 1 to 4, characterized 
by two nipples (11) emerging from the seat (5) and 

penetrating into two holes (12) of the blade (7) 
situated closer to the opposite side than to the free 

end. 
Pump according to one of Claims 1 to 5, characterized 
by a heel (10) emerging from the edge of the orifice 

(6) farthest away from the shrouded side and 
projecting towards the blade (7). 
Pump according to one of Claims 1 to 6, characterized 
in that the seat (5) is in the form of an arc of a 

circle. 
</CLAIMS>
