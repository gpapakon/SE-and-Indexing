<ABSTRACT>
Transition-metal-containing organosiloxanes comprising at least three organosiloxane units containing at least one transition-metal complex of Pt, Pd, Rh, Ru, Os or Ir chemically bonded via ligands selected from amino, phosphino, and sulphido groups, with the exception of organosiloxanes containing exclusively Pt(0) complexes bonded via amino ligands, are employed as homogeneous hydrosilylation catalysts.
</ABSTRACT>
