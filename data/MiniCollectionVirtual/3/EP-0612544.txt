<INVENTION-TITLE>
SOLE PLATE FOR A SKIBINDING.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A63C900	A63C900	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A63C	A63C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a sole plate for a ski binding with a front jaw and a heel holder, which sole plate can be fastened on the upper side of a ski by means of screws and has a sole element associated with the front jaw and a sole element associated with the heel holder. The aim of the invention is to simplify this sole plate in its construction and yet to arrange the ski binding at a vertical spacing from the upper side of the ski. According to the invention, one sole element (5) has a flexible tongue (5b) which extends in the direction towards the other sole element (4) and projects with its front free end section (5d) into a downwardly and rearwardly open free area (4b) of this sole element (4).
