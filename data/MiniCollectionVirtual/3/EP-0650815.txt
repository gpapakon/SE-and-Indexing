Method and device for separating plastic waste.

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method and a device for separating the components of a mixture of plastics or polymers. This method consists in feeding a mixture of plastics into an extrusion screw 1 comprising a melting zone 11 followed by a compression zone 12 into which the molten fraction of the mixture escapes from a confinement space 14 via an orifice 15 to a discharge zone. Thus, it is possible to separate the constituents of a mixture by successively melting them.
