<INVENTION-TITLE>
Use of bismuth as fishing weight.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A01K9500	A01K9500	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A01K	A01K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A01K95	A01K95	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention lies in the use of bismuth, even in a low-purity form, as an environmentally compatible weighting material in stretches of water associated with ecology, in particular as weighting bodies for fishing, preferably in compact material bodies.
</ABSTRACT>
