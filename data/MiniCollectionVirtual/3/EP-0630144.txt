<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for providing services in a communication network.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H04N1327	H04N1327	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H04N	H04N	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H04N1	H04N1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention is based on the object of extending the access to teleservices. This object is achieved by a teleservice device according to the invention which offers the teleinformation belonging to teleservices in the fax format. Thus, fax units can now also be used for accessing teleservices.
</ABSTRACT>
