<INVENTION-TITLE>
Hydrophobic silica containing transition metals.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C01B3300	C01B3318	C08K300	C08K334	C08K336	C08L8300	C08L8304	C09C128	C09C128	C09C130	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The transition metal-containing hydrophobic silica has a BET specific surface area of from 40 to 450 m
<
2
>
/g, a carbon content, obtained by the hydrophobicisation, of at least 0.5% by weight, and a transition metal content of from 10 to 10,000 ppm by weight. The hydrophobic silica (silicic acid) is used as a thermal stabiliser in addition-crosslinking 2-component silicone rubber materials.
</ABSTRACT>
