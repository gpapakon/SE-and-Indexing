<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a cosmetic composition containing, in a cosmetically acceptable medium, at least one nonionic surfactant of the alkyl polyglycoside family and/or one polyglycerolated nonionic surfactant, and at least one maleic anhydride/alkyl(C1-C5) vinyl ether crosslinked copolymer, as well as to a cosmetic treatment process consisting in applying a cosmetically effective amount of this composition to keratinous substances.
</ABSTRACT>
