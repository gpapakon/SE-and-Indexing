<ABSTRACT>
Water-soluble or -dispersible polyurethanes which are suitable as thickeners for aqueous systems and are products of the reaction of a selected alcohol component a) comprising a hydrophilic/hydrophobic alcohol mixture with an isocyanate component b), and the use thereof, optionally in combination with other thickeners, for thickening aqueous systems.
</ABSTRACT>
