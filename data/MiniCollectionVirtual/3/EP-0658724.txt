<INVENTION-TITLE>
Combusiton chamber with ceramic lining.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F23M500	F23M502	F23M504	F23R300	F23R300	F23R342	F27D104	F27D104	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a combustion chamber (10), in particular for gas turbines, with a hollow-cylindrical supporting wall (12, 13), on the inside of which a lining (14) composed of individual ceramic elements (15, 16, 17, 18, 19) is arranged, the ceramic elements (15, 16, 17, 18, 19) being designed as trapezohedrons, the base areas of which are adapted to the geometry of the combustion chamber (10) and are fastened to the inside of the supporting wall (12, 13).
