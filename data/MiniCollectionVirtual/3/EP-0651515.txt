<INVENTION-TITLE>
Circuit arrangement for aiding the start-up of a transistor oscillator.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H03L300	H03L300	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H03L	H03L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H03L3	H03L3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
An aid for building up oscillations is proposed for a transistor oscillator having a negative current feedback resistor situated in the amplifier output, which aid comprises a switching transistor (T2) having a capacitor (C3) and a resistor (R1) which are connected in parallel with the negative current feedback resistor.
