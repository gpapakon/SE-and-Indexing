<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for separating liquid mixtures having an azeotropic point by combination of pervaporation and distillation.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B01D334	B01D336	B01D6136	B01D6136	C07D30700	C07D30708	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B01D	B01D	B01D	B01D	C07D	C07D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B01D3	B01D3	B01D61	B01D61	C07D307	C07D307	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Process for separating azeotropic liquid mixtures by combining pervaporation and distillation,   in a first process step a mixture being concentrated by pervaporation to the extent that the azeotropic composition is exceeded, and in a second process step the concentrated component being distilled by means of a distillation unit up to the required purity and the lower or higher-boiling mixture resulting from this being returned to the pervaporation.
</ABSTRACT>
