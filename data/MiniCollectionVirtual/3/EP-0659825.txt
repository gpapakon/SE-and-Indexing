<INVENTION-TITLE>
Thermoplastic/elastomer polyester and process thereof.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08L2300	C08L2316	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08L	C08L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C08L23	C08L23	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The object was to prepare thermoplastic elastomers based on blends of thermoplastic aromatic polyesters and dynamically vulcanised EP(D)M rubber in which the rubber need not be functionalised in advance. This object is achieved by converting a mixture containing the following components:     a) from 50 to 95 parts by weight of EP(D)M rubber, b) from 5 to 50 parts by weight of thermoplastic aromatic polyester containing aliphatic double bonds, c) from 0 to 45 parts by weight of unmodified thermoplastic aromatic polyester,   d) from 0 to 100 parts by weight of plasticiser oil,   e) from 0 to 100 parts by weight of reinforcing agent and   f) an effective amount of a crosslinking agent,  to a thermoplastic elastomer by dynamic vulcanisation. The polyester elastomers according to the invention have good resilience.
</ABSTRACT>
