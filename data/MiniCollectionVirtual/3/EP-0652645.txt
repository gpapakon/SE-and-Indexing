<INVENTION-TITLE>
Portable radio device with means for protecting its user from electromagnetic radiation.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H01Q124	H01Q124	H04B138	H04B138	H04B726	H04B726	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a radio device with an antenna. To prevent risks to health due to electromagnetic radiation for users of portable radio devices, the invention proposes that a further antenna element, which faces towards the head of the user of the radio device when the radio device is in operation, is disposed essentially parallel to the antenna axis in a portable radio device.
