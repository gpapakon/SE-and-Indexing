<INVENTION-TITLE>
Steering lock for motor vehicle.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60R2502	B60R2502	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60R	B60R	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60R25	B60R25	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Steering lock for motor vehicle, comprising a body (10), a lock integral with a control cam (14) which, under the action of a suitable key, can drive a locking part (16) which is integral with a bolt in a translational movement parallel to the axis of the steering lock from a locking position to an unlocking position, the steering lock furthermore comprising a key follower (feeler) which can radially displace a slide (22), and a blocking element (26) for the locking part (16) which can pivot under the action of a spring (52). The blocking element (26) includes, formed integrally, three articulation means (40, 46, 50) distributed along a line which is overall parallel to the axis of the steering lock.
