<INVENTION-TITLE>
Distributor for introducing gas into elastic tubes
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B01F304	B01F304	C02F320	C02F320	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
Distributor for supplying elastic tubes with gas, comprises a hollow member (4) with at least one connection point (6) for supplying gas, and at least one outlet point (5) for connection to an elastic tube (2). The outlet can be swivelled in a vertical plane. A non-return valve is pref. located on the outlet and/or the hollow member.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
Bottom distributor for introducing gas into elastic tubes substantially
including a hollow base body (4) of freely selectable length with at least one

connecting socket (6) for introducing gas and at least one outlet socket (5) for
the connection of an elastic tube (2) into which gas is to be introduced,

characterised in that the outlet socket (5) is pivotable in a vertical plane.
Bottom distributor as claimed in claim 1, characterised in that the hollow
base body (4) itself together with the outlet socket (5) provided thereon is

constructed to be pivotable about a longitudinal axis perpendicular to the
vertical.
Bottom distributor as claimed in claim 1 or 2, characterised in that the
outlet socket (5) tapers in the direction of its mouth (9), starting from the hollow

base body (4), into the connected elastic tube (2).
Bottom distributor as claimed in claim 3, characterised in that the outlet
socket (5) has holes (11) in the wall of its tapered region (10).
Bottom distributor as claimed in claim 1 or 2, characterised in that the
hollow base body (4) is constructed in the form of a tube (8) of predetermined

length and predetermined diameter, arranged on which is a plurality of outlet
sockets (5) spaced from one another and that the tube (8) has respective devices

at its ends (12 and 12'), to which further hollow base bodies (4) may be
connected. 
Bottom distributor as claimed in one of the preceding claims,
characterised in that non-return valves are provided in the outlet socket (5)

and/or in the hollow base body (4).
</CLAIMS>
