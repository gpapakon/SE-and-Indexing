<INVENTION-TITLE>
Windscreen wiper assembly with connection device and connection device for such an assembly.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60S132	B60S140	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60S	B60S	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60S1	B60S1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a windscreen wiper device of the sort including a windscreen wiper arm and a wiper blade fixed to one of the ends of the windscreen wiper arm in a removable fashion by means of a connection piece including a housing for the windscreen wiper arm as well as articulation means interacting with complementary fastening means provided on the windscreen wiper blade, the said connection piece being elastically deformable so that it can be fitted between the lateral cheeks of the middle part of the main yoke, characterised in that the connection piece consists of two elastically deformable lateral walls (6, 7) joined, on the side where the windscreen wiper arm is engaged, by a small bridge (8) and, on the opposite side, by a transverse wall (9), the lateral walls (6, 7) each having a projection (12) on the internal surface to interact with the hook provided at the end of the windscreen wiper arm and, on the outer face, each having a protuberance (10) for interacting with the windscreen wiper blade.
