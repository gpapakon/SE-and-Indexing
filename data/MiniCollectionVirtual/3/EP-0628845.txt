<INVENTION-TITLE>
Night vision binoculars with electric imagery.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G02B2300	G02B2312	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G02B	G02B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G02B23	G02B23	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The night-vision binoculars according to the invention includes an optical system for projecting an incident light flux, composed of an objective (5) coupled to a light intensifier tube (6) which is itself coupled, through an image reducer interface (8), to detection means (7) which are in the form of a matrix. The binoculars may be in the form of two modules, one of which fits into the other, one being a binoculars-body module (1) and the other a binoculars-sight module (2). Application to night-time vision using electronic imaging with inlaying of additional information.
