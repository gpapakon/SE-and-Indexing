<INVENTION-TITLE>
Wear plate with spherical clamps for ball mills.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B02C1700	B02C1722	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B02C	B02C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B02C17	B02C17	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The wear plate (shield) (5) of the invention is intended to be wedged on to the interior surface of the cylindrical shell of a ball mill. In a known manner, it includes at least one assembly face (11) which is provided with a housing intended to receive a wedge. According to the invention, the wedge is a ball, and the housing (12) is designed to receive part of the ball. More particularly, one assembly face (11) is provided with two identical housings (12) which are symmetric to one another relative to the longitudinal axis (5a) of the wear plate.
