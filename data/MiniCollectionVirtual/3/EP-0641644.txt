<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for controlling the drive of a hydraulic press and apparatus for carrying out the method.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B30B1516	B30B1516	B30B1524	F15B100	F15B102	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention proposes a method for controlling the drive of a hydraulic press and an associated apparatus for carrying out the method, in the case of which the drive of the piston/cylinder unit (2, 3) belonging to a press ram (1) takes place via a plurality of hydraulic devices (10, 12, 20) provided one behind the other. In this arrangement, depending on the operating phase of the press, the hydraulic devices are used, as required, as hydraulic motors or hydraulic pumps, which deliver pressure medium into the respective cylinder space (7, 8).
