Liquid collector for cooking hobs

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a heating apparatus (15), in which are arranged a cooking plate (1), heating elements, a support casing (2), characterised in that it comprises a means of collecting liquids in the vicinity of the interface between the support casing (2) and the cooking plate (1). Application for installable cooking hobs.

<CLAIMS>
A heating appliance (15) in which there are provided a cooking 
plate (1), heating elements, and a support housing (2), said heating 

appliance being characterized in that it includes means for 

collecting liquids in the vicinity of the interface between the 
support housing (2) and the cooking plate (1). 
A heating appliance (15) according to claim 1, characterized in 
that the collecting means is constituted by a channel (4) provided in 

the support housing (2). 
A heating appliance (15) according to claim 1 or 2, characterized 
in that it includes means for draining off liquids that have 

collected, said means being provided by at least one hole (5) 
communicating with the collecting means. 
A heating appliance (15) according to claim 2, characterized in 
that the channel (4) extends at least in part in the vicinity of the 

periphery of a bearing zone (6) for the cooking plate (1). 
A heating appliance (15) according to claim 4, characterized in 
that the bearing zone (6) is formed by a horizontal surface of the 

support housing (2) on which the cooking plate (1) rests. 
A heating appliance (15) according to claim 4 or 5, characterized 
in that the bearing zone (6) presents at least one groove (6a) for 

the flow of liquids towards the channel (4). 
A heating appliance (15) according to any preceding claim, 
characterized in that the support housing (2) presents a peripheral 

positioning member (7) with ribs (7a) against which the cooking plate 
(1) is disposed, so as to provide a flow space (7b) between the 

cooking plate (1) and the positioning member (7). 
A heating appliance (15) according to any preceding claim, 
characterized in that it includes trim (3) for fixing the cooking 

plate (1) to the support housing (2).  
 
A heating appliance (15) according to any preceding claim, 
characterized in that the cooking plate (1) is a ceramic plate. 
A heating appliance (15) according to any one of claims 1 to 9, 
characterized in that the cooking plate (1) is metal. 
A heating appliance (15) according to any preceding claim, 
characterized in that it is suitable for being built-in to a support. 
</CLAIMS>
