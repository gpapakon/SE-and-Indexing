<INVENTION-TITLE>
Kev switch module.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a monostable electrical switching appliance (10) having a switching device (14), having an operating element (26) for operating the switching device (14), and having a housing body (12) which accommodates the switching device (14) and has a guide (32) for the operating element (26), the guide (32) being formed by a spring device (30), the guide (32) furthermore holding the operating element (26) in a force-fitting manner, and the guide (32) deflecting with the operating element (26) when said operating element (26) is operated, and automatically resetting it to the rest position.
