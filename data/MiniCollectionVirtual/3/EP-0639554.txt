<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the preparation of propargyl ethers.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C07C4100	C07C4116	C07C4500	C07C4571	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C07C	C07C	C07C	C07C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C07C41	C07C41	C07C45	C07C45	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a process for the preparation of aromatic propargyl ethers by reacting phenolic compounds with 1 to 2 mols of propargyl chloride, based on 1 mol of phenolic OH groups, in the presence of 1 to 2 mols of base, based on 1 mol of phenolic OH groups, in organic solvents and in the presence of an iron catalyst.
</ABSTRACT>
