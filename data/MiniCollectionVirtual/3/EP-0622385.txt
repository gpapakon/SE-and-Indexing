Polymer blends.

<ABSTRACT>
Polymer mixtures comprising at least one amino-containing polymer A) and at least one polymer B) comprising ethylenically unsaturated monomers, obtained by reacting an epoxide with an amine in the presence of the ethylenically unsaturated monomers followed by free-radical polymerization. These polymer mixtures are employed for the preparation of binder supports for water-based printing inks and printing lacquers.
</ABSTRACT>
