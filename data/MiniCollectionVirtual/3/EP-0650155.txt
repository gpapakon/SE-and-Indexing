<INVENTION-TITLE>
Uniformly illuminated multi-layered composite system.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F21V800	F21V800	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F21V	F21V	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F21V8	F21V8	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to composite systems with light-guiding elements (1) which are illuminated from the edges, consisting of a plastics material A with a spectral decadic absorption measure according to DIN 1349 of a maximum of 0.035, and to which, if appropriate, information (7) is applied, and with covering elements (2) consisting of a plastics material B with a spectral decadic absorption measure in accordance with DIN 1349 of at least 0.04, a hollow space (3) being preferably located between light-guiding elements (1) and covering elements (2).
