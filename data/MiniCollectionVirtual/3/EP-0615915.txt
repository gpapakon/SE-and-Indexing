<INVENTION-TITLE>
Oversealing cap for bottle neck
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65D4162	B65D4100	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65D	B65D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to an oversealing cap (1) for a bottle neck (3), of the type including a head pad (4) intended to cover over the end face (5) of the neck (3) and a skirt (6) joined to the periphery of the pad (4). The pad (4) has the shape of a small dish (4) with a rim (9), the skirt (6) and the pad (4) being joined at the level of the rim (9) of the small dish. The cap (1) also includes a tamperproof (tamper-evident) disc (11) which covers over at least a part of the bottom of the small dish (4). It is possible to write on the small dish (4) by means of embossing.

<CLAIMS>
Capsule to be placed over a bottle-neck (3) and of 
the type having a cap patch (4) to cover the end surface 

(5) of the said neck (3) and a skirt (6) attached to the 
circumference of the said patch (4), characterised by the 

fact that the cap patch is in the form of a cup (4) with 
a raised edge (9), the said skirt (6) and the said patch 

being attached at the raised edge (9) of the said cup 
(4), being free of folds covering the end surface of the 

bottle and including at least one layer of a material 
selected from the group comprising aluminium, tin, PVC, 

polystyrene, polypropylene, coated papers, 
monocrystalline waxes and polyolefins. 
Capsule as described in claim 1, characterised by 
the fact that the cup (4) is placed over the adjacent end 

of the skirt (6). 
Capsule as described in claim 1, characterised by 
the fact that the cup (4) fits inside the adjacent end of 

the skirt (6). 
Capsule as described in one of claims 1 to 3, 
characterised by the fact that the skirt (6) is attached 

to the raised edge (9) of the cup by adhesion. 
Capsule as described in any one of claims 1 to 4, 
characterised by the fact that it also includes a tamper-proof 

disc (11) which covers at least part of the base of 
the cup (4). 
Capsule as described in claim 5, characterised by 
the fact that the tamper-proof disc (11) is made of 

stainless steel.  
 
Capsule as described in claim 6, characterised by 
the fact that the tamper-proof disc (11) has a thickness 

greater than 0.01mm and less than 1mm. 
Capsule as described in any one of claims 1 to 7, to 
be placed over the neck of a bottle having a ring (8) set 

at a distance 
d
 from the end surface, characterised by 
the fact that the cup has a depth substantially less than 

or equal to the distance 
d
.
