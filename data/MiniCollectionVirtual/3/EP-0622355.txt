A benzylhydrazone derivative represented by 
the general formula (I): 


[wherein X is CH∼OR⁵ or N∼OR⁵ (wherein R⁵ is a C₁₋₆alkyl 
group), Y is a C₁₋₆alkoxy group, a C₁₋₆alkylthio group, 

an unsubstituted amino group, or a mono- or di-substituted 
amino group having as the substituent(s) one 

or two C₁₋₆alkyl groups, each of Z's is a halogen atom, 
a C₁₋₆alkyl group or a
 halo-C₁₋₆alkyl groups, m is zero 
or an integer of 1 to 4, R¹ is a hydrogen atom, a

active ingredient.
