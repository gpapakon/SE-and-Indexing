<CLAIMS>
Wipe-resistant labelling device for objects of all kinds 
with several layers of material (6, 7) which can be 

provided with an item of information (5) by means of 
pressure, one of the layers of material (6, 7) being a 

scratchable film to which the item of information (5) can 
be applied, and a further layer of material (7) which is 

softer and thicker than the scratchable layer of material 
(6) is provided, characterised in that the scratchable film 

(6) encloses the further layer of material (7) all round. 
Wipe-resistant labelling device according to claim 1, 
characterised by a fastening means on the underside of the 

labelling device. 
Wipe-resistant labelling device according to claim 2, 
characterised in that the fastening means is an adhesive 

layer (9) after the fashion of a sticker. 
Wipe-resistant labelling device according to claim 2, 
characterised in that the fastening means is a hook layer 

(10) of a hook and pile fastener. 
Wipe-resistant labelling device according to claim 2, 
characterised in that the fastening means is a retaining 

device (8). 
</CLAIMS>
