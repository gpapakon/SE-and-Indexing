<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process and apparatus for the defined impregnation of honeycomb bodies with parallel flow channels.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B01D5386	B01D5386	B01J3700	B01J3702	B05B1306	B05B1306	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B01D	B01D	B01J	B01J	B05B	B05B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a process for the defined impregnation of honeycomb bodies and an apparatus for carrying out the process. The impregnation solutions are atomised by injector needles directly into the flow channels of the honeycomb bodies.
