OVEN DOOR.

<ABSTRACT>
Oven door (1) comprising: a frame (2) on which are mounted two glass panes (3, 4) parallel and spaced part from one another, the inner pane (4) being pivotably mounted in relation to the frame (2) by means of a hinge element. The oven door also comprises closure means for fast locking and unlocking located opposite the pivot pin (1b 
&
cir
&
 _) of the inner glass pane (4). The closure means maintain the inner glass pane (4) in a closed position in relation to the frame (2). According to the invention, the closure means consist of one or more individual closure spacers (7), capable of fast locking and unlocking, serving to maintain the inner glass pane (4) at a distance in relation to the inner frame surface (2a 
&
cir
&
 _), when the inner glass pane is closed. The hinge piece (6) comprises at least one plate (10), and the inner glass pane is secured at a distance from the plate (10) by at least one pin-like fastening spacer (8), so that the plate (10) contacts and bears on the inner face of the frame (2), when the inner glass pane is in the closed position.
</ABSTRACT>
