<INVENTION-TITLE>
SEPARATOR FOR BOUND PRINTED VOLUMES WITH A NOSE THAT PROJECTS OUT OF THE PLANE OF THE BOOK
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B42F2102	B42F2100	B42F2104	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B42F	B42F	B42F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B42F21	B42F21	B42F21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A separator for bound printed volumes has a nose (12) that projects out of the plane of the book. The separator is characterized in that it is designed as a separating leaf (10) whose inner edge opposite to the nose or noses is wider than the width (b) of the nose (12) and is provided with an adhesive material (16) for securing it to the inner side (22) of the book.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
A separator for bound printed volumes with noses projecting
out of the plane of the book, which is in the form of a separating sheet

(10) whose inner edge (14) that is opposite to the nose (12) or noses
(12, 12') is wider than the width (b) of the nose (12) and has adhesive

material (16) for fixing to the inside (22) of the book characterised in
that one or more folds (26) are provided at the side (24) of the

separating sheet (10), that is towards the inside (22) of the book, and
provided at the side (24) of the separating sheet (10), that is towards

the inside (22) of the book, are insert noses (30) which extend in a
direction opposite to the noses (12).
A separator according to claim 1 characterised in that the
adhesive material (16) is in the form of adhesive strips.
A separator according to claim 1 characterised in that the
separating sheets (10) have markings (26) in the form of marking lines

or perforations for adaptation to different book formats.
A separator according to claim 1 characterised in that the
separating sheets (10) have different numbers of noses (12) in different

positions.
</CLAIMS>
