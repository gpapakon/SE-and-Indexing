Process for the conversion of ester or carbonate groups to amide or urethanes groups.

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a process for the preparation of compounds containing aliphatically bonded amide or urethane groups in which compounds containing aliphatically bonded ester or carbonate groups are reacted with compounds containing at least one aliphatically bonded amino group in the presence of catalytically effective amounts of alkali metal alkoxides and alkali metal formates.
 
</ABSTRACT>
