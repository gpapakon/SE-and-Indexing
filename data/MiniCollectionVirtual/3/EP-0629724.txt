<INVENTION-TITLE>
Recyclable fabric made out of polyolefine thread
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>D04B114	D04B116	D02G340	D03D1500	D02G322	D03D1500	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>D04B	D04B	D02G	D03D	D02G	D03D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>D04B1	D04B1	D02G3	D03D15	D02G3	D03D15	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Recyclable (woven) fabric consisting of a polyolefin yarn composed of polypropylene fibres and 1-20% by weight of a lower-melting thermobonding fibre and its use for manufacturing textile sun protection articles, especially awnings.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
Textile sun protection articles, in particular 
awnings, which consist of a recyclable fabric woven from 

a polyolefin yarn consisting of polypropylene fibres and 
1-20% by weight of a lower-melting fusible binder fibre. 
Textile sun protection articles according to 
Claim 1 wherein the fusible binder fibre comprises 

polyethylene fibres. 
Textile sun protection articles according to 
Claim 1 which additionally contain an oil and water 

repellency finish. 
Textile sun protection articles according to 
Claim 3 wherein the oil- and water-repellent finish is a 

fluorocarbon resin. 
</CLAIMS>
