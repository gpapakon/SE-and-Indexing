<INVENTION-TITLE>
Ultrafine silicium and carbon containing powder.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C01B3100	C01B3136	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C01B	C01B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C01B31	C01B31	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an ultrafine powder containing silicon and carbon, which comprises spherical, X-ray amorphous particles which possess diamond-like properties. According to the invention, organosilicon compounds finely dispersed in a reaction zone are exposed to a temperature above 750 DEG C for from 0.5 to 10 seconds.
</ABSTRACT>
