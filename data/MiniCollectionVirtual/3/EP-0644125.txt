<INVENTION-TITLE>
Closure cap.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65D4104	B65D4104	B65D4134	B65D4134	B67B300	B67B320	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65D	B65D	B65D	B65D	B67B	B67B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
Stopper (closure cap) (1) for a bottle or flask (7) intended in particular to contain a liquid fluid, which stopper has the form of a shell (2) comprising a disc (3) and a skirt (3a) which has an internal screw thread (8) able to interact with a threaded part of the neck (6) of the bottle (7), this shell including a sealing ring (4) and a tamperproof ring (20). The sealing ring (4) is borne by the skirt (3a) of the shell of the stopper and includes a thick ring (5) on its internal periphery.
