<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for manufacturing a hole structure in a silicium substrate.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C23F110	C23F124	C25F300	C25F312	H01L2102	H01L21306	H01L213063	H01L21318	H01L2170	H01L21762	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C23F1	C23F1	C25F3	C25F3	H01L21	H01L21	H01L21	H01L21	H01L21	H01L21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In order to produce a hole pattern (6) in a substrate (1) made of n-doped, monocrystalline (single-crystal) silicon, the substrate (1) is provided, by means of electrochemical etching starting from a first principal plane (2), with pores (4) which have a depth corresponding to the depth of the hole pattern (6). There is generated, on the first principal plane (2) of the substrate (1), a mask (5) which defines the cross-section of the hole pattern (6) parallel to the first principal plane (2). With the aid of an etching operation which is selective with respect to mask (5), the hole pattern (6) is produced by removal of the silicon which forms the side walls of the pores (4) not covered by the mask (5).
