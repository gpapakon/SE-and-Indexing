<INVENTION-TITLE>
Door frame, particularly cladding therefor.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E06B104	E06B134	E06B716	E06B724	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E06B	E06B	E06B	E06B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>E06B1	E06B1	E06B7	E06B7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Doorframe, in particular cladding therefor, with profiles consisting of metal, e.g. aluminium, plastic, fibre-reinforced plastic or the like. At least one profile (6) exhibits a leg (7) parallel to the reveal (8) and a further leg (9) transverse thereto, the leg forming a rebate which exhibits a bevel (10) which runs along the profile (6) and through which, if appropriate, bores (12) are guided. The bevel (10) is covered by a seal (18) which runs along the bevel and is fixed in two grooves (16) running along the bevel (10). A further rebate (20), if appropriate provided with a further bevel (11), is covered by a further seal (18).
