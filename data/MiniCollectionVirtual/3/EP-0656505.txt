<INVENTION-TITLE>
Security installation for detecting human presence in dangerous areas.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F16P300	F16P314	G08B13189	G08B1319	G08B2900	G08B2914	G08B2916	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F16P	F16P	G08B	G08B	G08B	G08B	G08B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F16P3	F16P3	G08B13	G08B13	G08B29	G08B29	G08B29	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Installation characterised in that it comprises n infrared detectors and at least two (1, 2) arranged and oriented to detect a human presence in one and the same area (S), a comparator assembly for checking concurrence of the detections by the n detectors and an alarm set off by the comparator assembly (3) when at most n-1 detectors detect a human presence.
