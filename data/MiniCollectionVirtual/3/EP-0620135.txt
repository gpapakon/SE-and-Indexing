<INVENTION-TITLE>
Exchange station for electric road vehicles equipped with interchangeable accumulators.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60K104	B60K104	B60S500	B60S506	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60K	B60K	B60S	B60S	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60K1	B60K1	B60S5	B60S5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In addition to the necessary servicing facilities, in particular battery chargers, a battery exchange station for electric road vehicles, in a mobile, standard container (1) used as housing, contains the complete exchange appliance comprising a laterally traversable scissor lift (4) with a roller system (19) and a folding roller extension arm (20) which is coupled to a roller track (21) in the battery compartment of the vehicle, parked in front of an opened rolling door (10) of the container (1), and makes a non-positive connection with the said roller track. This allows the traction batteries to be easily transported from the vehicle to the charging locations and vice versa solely with the aid of the roller track drive situated on the scissor lift, it being likewise possible to couple the roller track (19) of the scissor lift (4) non-positively to the roller tracks of the charging locations.
