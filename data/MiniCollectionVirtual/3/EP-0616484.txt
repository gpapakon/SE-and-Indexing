<INVENTION-TITLE>
Magnetoresistive transducer und manufacturing method.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01R3306	G01R3309	H01L4308	H01L4308	H04R1500	H04R1500	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01R	G01R	H01L	H01L	H04R	H04R	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01R33	G01R33	H01L43	H01L43	H04R15	H04R15	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Magnetoresistive detector including a stack (1) of layers with different magnetic characteristics (multiple magnetic metal layers) in which a transport of electrons perpendicular to the layers is provided under the effect of an electric field. In order to provide several perpendicular transfers through the same stack of layers, the latter is clamped between two electrodes (4, 5) and the electrical conduction of the assembly is interrupted by a cutout in the structure in the form of "grecian" notches.
