<INVENTION-TITLE>
Apparatus for entangling filaments.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>D02J100	D02J100	D02J108	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>D02J	D02J	D02J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>D02J1	D02J1	D02J1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
For the swirling of a multiplicity of filaments (19) by means of the same number of swirl nozzles (5), there is presented a device which can be integrated in warping installations or the like and in which the swirl nozzles (5) are arranged on a crossmember (1) movable relative to the air-supply beam (2), the movement of the crossmember (1) taking place manually, via compression springs (8) connected to the swirl nozzle (5), or pneumatically. At the same time, the baffle plates (3) are fastened, for example to the air-supply beam (2), stationarily via a holder (4).
