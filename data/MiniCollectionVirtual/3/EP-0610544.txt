Cover for aquaria, terraria or the like

<ABSTRACT>
A cover for aquaria, terraria or the like is proposed, with an approximately rectangular frame (13) and a lid (15) which can be folded up and is mounted pivotably about a pivot axis (14) on the frame (13) and on the underside of which an illumination arrangement (17) is held. The cover has a drive arrangement (18) which can be activated to open the lid (15) and engages on the lid (15) and by means of which the lid (15) can be folded up automatically, at least over an initial opening angular range.
