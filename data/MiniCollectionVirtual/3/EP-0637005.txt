<INVENTION-TITLE>
Liquid crystal display device and procedure for reducing the brightness at wide viewing angles of such a device.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The subject of the invention is a liquid crystal display device comprising a light source (1) and a liquid crystal matrix (4) operating in transmission mode. According to the invention, it comprises an optical fibre plate (6; 6') between the light source and the matrix.
