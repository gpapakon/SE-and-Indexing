<INVENTION-TITLE>
Carton for products of a bakery.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65D546	B65D546	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65D	B65D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65D5	B65D5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A carton for packaging products of a bakery, consisting of a base plate (1) and upright walls (2, 3, 4) and an articulated closure element (11). In order to be able to slide products of a bakery into the carton without touching them and to carry the full carton conveniently in a horizontal position, the base plate is fitted on three sides with upright walls and a first closure element (5) which is connected to the walls and closes two thirds to three quarters of the space present between the base plate and the upright walls and is fitted with a first handle part (6) and has grooves (9, 10) on both sides, and the base plate is connected on its fourth side in an articulated manner to a second closure element (11) which has an edge profile which has a second handle part (12) and, on both of its sides, projecting lips which fit into the abovementioned grooves (9, 10).
