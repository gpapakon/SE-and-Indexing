<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for editing information for a car navigation system
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
This device for editing information for a computer, in particular for aiding navigation of a motor vehicle, in which the computer (1) is connected to information display means (2) and to information input means (3), is characterised in that the information input means comprise means (4, 5) for controlling the advance of the information in a window (6) of the display means (2), which means can be actuated by a user in order to select an information item and means (7) for capture, by the computer (1), of this information selected in the window.

<CLAIMS>
Information editing device for a computer, in
particular a motor vehicle navigation aid computer, wherein

the computer (1) is connected to information display means
(2) and to information input means (3), the information

input means comprising means (4, 5) of controlling the
scrolling of information in a window (6) of the display

means (2), which can be actuated by a user to select a
piece of information and means (7) of acquisition of this

information selected in the window by the computer (1),
characterised in that the information furthermore comprises

characters intended to become part of the composition of a
string of characters and symbols illustrating functions of

the computer, and in that the computer (1) is adapted, if
the acquired piece of information is a character, to allow

the acquisition of another piece of information in the
window in order to constitute a string of characters and,

if the information acquired is a symbol illustrating a
function of the computer, to determine the corresponding

operations to be carried out.
Device according to Claim 1, characterised in that

the information furthermore comprises a symbol illustrating
a correction of a character already acquired and in that

the computer (1) is adapted, during the acquisition of this
symbol, to bring an already acquired symbol into the said

window (6) and to allow its modification.
Device according to Claim 1 or 2, characterised in
that the means of control of the scrolling of information

and of acquisition of this information comprise keys. 
Device according to Claim 1 or 2, characterised in
that the means of control of the scrolling of information

and of acquisition of this information comprise a rotary
knurled wheel.
Device according to Claim 1 or 2, characterised in
that the means of control of the scrolling of information

and of acquisition of this information comprise a control
lever.
Device according to any one of the preceding
claims, characterised in that the computer is adapted to

limit the selection to authorised information according to
information already selected.
Device according to any one of the preceding
claims, characterised in that the window is movable over

the display means (2) in order to allow the selection of a
string of pieces of information.
Device according to any one of Claims 1 to 6,
characterised in that the selected information is displayed

in a location of the display means which is different from
that of the window.
Device according to any one of Claims 1 to 6,
characterised in that it comprises another device for

displaying the selected information.
</CLAIMS>
