<INVENTION-TITLE>
Current measuring method and device for bridge circuits.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01R1514	G01R1518	H02M75387	H02M75387	H02P700	H02P700	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01R	G01R	H02M	H02M	H02P	H02P	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01R15	G01R15	H02M7	H02M7	H02P7	H02P7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In the case of the method for measuring current in bridge circuits, especially full bridge circuits, two separate current-measuring devices (1, 2) are used in a bridge arm. In order to carry out the method, a device is provided in which two separate current-measuring devices (1, 2) are fitted in a bridge arm so that, by virtue of their arrangement, the working current (3, 4) flowing through the bridge and the freewheel current (4, 6) circulating, if applicable, are assessed at the correct amounts.
