<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the preparation of polyurethane foams.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08G1800	C08G1818	C08G1822	C08G1848	C08G10100	C08J900	C08J902	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08G	C08G	C08G	C08G	C08G	C08J	C08J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C08G18	C08G18	C08G18	C08G18	C08G101	C08J9	C08J9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A novel process for the production of polyurethane foams by reacting a) a polyisocyanate component with b) a salt group-free polyol component in the presence of c) at least one blowing agent, d) ester group-free, ether group-containing carboxylic acids in the salt form as catalysts, and optionally e) further auxiliaries and additives, preferably with foam backing of a plastic film as top (outer) layer in order to produce composite elements, and the composite elements produced in this way.
</ABSTRACT>
