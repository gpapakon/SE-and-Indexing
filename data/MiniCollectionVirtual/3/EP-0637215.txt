<INVENTION-TITLE>
FAST SNACK PREPARATION PROCESS FOR SNACK BARS.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A23L101	A23L101	A23L1214	A23L1217	A23L1317	A23L1317	A47J3704	A47J3704	F24C700	F24C706	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A23L1	A23L1	A23L1	A23L1	A23L1	A23L1	A47J37	A47J37	F24C7	F24C7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A snack preparation process allows fresh food portions to be prepared continuously or as they are ordered by exposing a flat layer of foodstuffs from several sides to direct or indirect infrared radiation with an adjustable intensity. The cooking chambers may take the form of a toaster (1) with a suspended grid (4), of absorption grilling pockets (6), of an infrared contact grilling plate (27, 28) or of grilling or frying devices provided with conveyor belts (12, 48), and they are suitable for preparing french fries and meat portions.
</ABSTRACT>
