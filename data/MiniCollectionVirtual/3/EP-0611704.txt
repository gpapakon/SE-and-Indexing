<INVENTION-TITLE>
Separation means for bottles in a bottle package and bottle package with separation means between the bottles.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65D548	B65D549	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65D	B65D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65D5	B65D5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A separating means for bottles in a bottle package, having at least one partition which is made of a blank of foldable flat material and has a central web to be arranged between rows of bottles and at least one flap to be arranged between rows of bottles perpendicular to those previously mentioned, each flap being swivellable about a swivel connection perpendicular to the central web and extending with a first flap section over approximately half the bottle diameter away from the swivel connection, the first flap sections of two central webs or central web sections (webs) to be arranged parallel to one another spaced apart by one row of bottles in the bottle package being swivellable on sides of the webs facing away from one another in the bottle package, and either the flaps on sides of the webs facing one another in the bottle package having foldable second flap sections with an extent of about half the bottle diameter away from the swivel connection and mutually complementary contours at the free ends, or only each flap of one web having a second flap section which can be swivelled onto one of the mutually facing sides of the webs in the bottle package with an extent of at least approximately half the bottle diameter away from the swivel connection and each flap of the other web having a third flap section, which can be swivelled onto the other one of the mutually facing sides, of shorter extent away from the swivel connection, or having no flap section.
