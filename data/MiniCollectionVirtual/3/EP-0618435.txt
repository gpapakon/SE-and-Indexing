Capacitive pressure sensor.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Pressure sensor having a matrix of diaphragms (11, 12, 13, 14) made of polysilicon, which are arranged over a structure of electrical conductors on the upper side of a silicon substrate for the determination of their variable electrical capacitance depending on the pressure loading, these diaphragms being present in at least two different sizes and, in each case, so many capacitances of these diaphragms of the same size being connected to form one sub-unit that the fundamental capacitance of these sub-units are equally large in each case.
