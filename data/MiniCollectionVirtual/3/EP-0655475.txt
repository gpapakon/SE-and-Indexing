<ABSTRACT>
Aqueous dispersions of organopolysiloxanes containing no organic transition metal compounds or organic compounds of metals from main groups III, IV and V, obtainable using the starting material     (A) an organopolysiloxane containing condensable groups, (B) an organopolysiloxane resin having a maximum molecular weight of 20,000 and (C) a compound containing basic nitrogen. g
