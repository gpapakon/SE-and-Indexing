<CLAIMS>
Device to clean the inside of wind instruments
 
with a cleaning body (2) which comprises a part (4) 

curved convex towards the outside, at the front end of 
which is attached a long broach part,
 
characterized in that
 
the cleaning body (2) has a plate-shaped part (3) on the 

top of which is arranged the curved part (4). 
Device according to claim 1, characterized in that the 
plate-shaped part (3) is a round disc. 
Device according to claim 1 or 2, characterized in that 
the curved part (4) is a section of a sphere, in 

particular a hemisphere. 
Device according to claim 2 and 3, characterized in that 
the diameter of the section of a sphere is less than the 

diameter of the round disc. 
Device according to any of claims 1 to 4, characterized 
in that the cleaning body (2) is made of sponge rubber, 

foam rubber and/or a plastic foam. 
Device according to any of claims 1 to 5, characterized 
in that the cleaning body (2), starting from the side of 

the curved part (4), is also covered by a cloth (7) in 
particular of leather or fabric. 
Device according to any of claims 1 to 6, characterized 
in that the long broach part is a flexible cord (1), in 

particular made of plastic. 
Device according to claim 7, characterized in that the 
free end of the cord (1) has a threading body (6).  

 
Device according to claim 7, characterized in that the 
free end of the cord (1) has a point (5). 
Device according to any of claims 7 to 9, characterized 
in that a second cord (1') engages on the side of the 

plate-shaped part (3). 
Device according to any of claims 1 to 6, characterized 
in that the long broach part is in particular a flexible 

rod (8). 
Device according to claim 11, characterized in that on 
the plate-shaped part (3) on the side facing away from 

the curved part (4) is attached a cloth (9), in 
particular of leather or fabric. 
</CLAIMS>
