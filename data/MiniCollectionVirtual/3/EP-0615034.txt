<ABSTRACT>
Rigid enclosure made of expanded polystyrene and prefabricated element for its construction. The enclosure results from the joining together of at least five elements (30) equipped on their longitudinal edges with assembly means constituted by pairs of ribs and grooves of matching shapes and dimensions.
