<INVENTION-TITLE>
Bitumen additive.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08J1100	C08J1104	C08L9500	C08L9500	C10C300	C10C300	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08J	C08J	C08L	C08L	C10C	C10C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a bitumen additive comprising molten and regranulated used plastics. In order to achieve ready solubility in bitumen, it is proposed that the used plastics comprise granules, ground in the solid state, having a particle size distribution of from 0 to 8 mm, in particular up to 5 mm, with a defined screen line, where the individual particles have a rough surface.
</ABSTRACT>
