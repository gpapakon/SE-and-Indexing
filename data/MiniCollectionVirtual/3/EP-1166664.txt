<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for making a hair strip
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A41G300	A41G300	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A41G	A41G	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A41G3	A41G3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The strip consists of hairs (1), which are connected at a set distance from both ends by strip (2) of a cast thermoplastic material. Projecting ends are cut off after the plastic has set. In addition, the hair may be sewn together using a thread (3) of pref. the same material. Fastening loops (4) or similar are connected to the plastic strip after the ends have been cut off.
</ABSTRACT>
