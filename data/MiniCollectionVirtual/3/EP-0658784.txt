<INVENTION-TITLE>
Opto-electronic device for coupling and uncoupling of radiation.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G02B6124	G02B6124	G02B630	G02B630	G02B642	G02B642	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G02B	G02B	G02B	G02B	G02B	G02B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G02B6	G02B6	G02B6	G02B6	G02B6	G02B6	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Opto-electronic device (component) for uncoupling (extracting) and coupling (launching, injecting) radiation out of a waveguide (2), via a mirror (3), through the substrate (1), in the case of which a plane-convex lens (4) is integrated into that surface (18) of the substrate (1) which is opposite the waveguide (2).
