<CLAIMS>
Straightening apparatus for wire, multiple wire or tubular 
products with a plurality of rotating straightening rollers (9) 

arranged in two parallel planes, whereof the journals are mounted 
in two body parts (2, 3) able to move relatively towards each 

other and forming a basic body (1), the body parts (2, 3) being 
arranged on guides and comprising a closing unit, characterised 

in that the body parts (2, 3) are held in a defined open position 
by springs (7) supported thereon and can be brought by the 

closing unit into a closed position with a predetermined defined 
zero position, the closed position being able to be secured in 

the defined zero position by a releasable locking connection. 
Straightening apparatus according to Claim 1, 
characterised in that the closing unit contains an eccentric (10) 

mounted to rotate in one body part (3) with an operating lever 
(13) attached thereto and a tie-rod (11) pivotally connected to 

the eccentric (10) and the other body part (2). 
Straightening apparatus according to Claim 2, 
characterised in that the operating lever (13) is designed as a 

protective cover covering all the straightening rollers (9) in 
the closed position. 
Straightening apparatus according to Claim 3, 
characterised in that the protective cover is transparent. 
Straightening apparatus according to Claim 3 or 4, 
characterised in that the protective cover comprises lateral 

transverse walls (22) with through slots (23) for the products 
to be straightened. 
Straightening apparatus according to one of Claims 1 to 
5, characterised in that the releasable locking connection 

consists of a leaf spring (18) and locking grooves (15) adapted 
thereto in the guides designed as parallel guide bolts (4), the  

 
leaf spring (18) being releasable from the biased locking 

position in the locking grooves (15) by unlocking pins (20). 
Straightening apparatus according to Claim 6, 
characterised in that for maintaining the defined open position, 

the springs (7) are located on the guide bolts (4). 
Straightening apparatus according to one of Claims 6 or 
7, characterised in that in the open position of the body parts 

(2, 3), the springs (7) and/or the guide bolts (4) are covered 
by protecting sleeves (8). 
Straightening apparatus according to one of Claims 1 to 
8, characterised in that the straightening chamber with the 

straightening rollers (9) is connected to a vacuum source. 
Straightening apparatus according to Claim 9, 
characterised in that the suction openings of the vacuum source 

in the direction of movement of the product to be straightened 
are located in the immediate vicinity behind the respective 

contact point of the straightening roller (9) and product to be 
straightened.
