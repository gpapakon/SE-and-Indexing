<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for providing instructions relating to a called number in a telecommunication network and network nodes therefor
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H04Q300	H04Q300	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H04Q	H04Q	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H04Q3	H04Q3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A connection (CON1,CON2) is made to a second nodal point (XDEST) from an originating terminal (TSRC) through a first nodal point (XSRC) connectable to an intelligent network device (SCP). XDEST tells XSRC that a number is unattainable, that a connection is set up on a section (CON2) linked to XSRC, that XSRC wants instructions re. the unattainable target subscriber and that a call is announced to the originating terminal or details of charges given.
</ABSTRACT>
