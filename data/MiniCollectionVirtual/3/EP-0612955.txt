<INVENTION-TITLE>
Lighting apparatus comprising a recyclable housing.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a lighting apparatus (1), of the type comprising a functional part and decorative elements, characterised in that the functional part (2) can be separated from the decorative elements which are in the form of at least one shaped article (3) made of recyclable material. More particularly, the technical part may be a casing with a bulb operating with an accumulator and a solar collector. The invention also encompasses the ranges of products obtained by recycling and converting the shaped articles and a method of manufacture of the shaped articles.
