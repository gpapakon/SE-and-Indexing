<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for spraying of high molecular polyisobutylene powder with inert carrier gas.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C02F168	C02F168	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C02F	C02F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C02F1	C02F1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Process for spraying high molecular weight polyisobutylene powder, an inert carrier gas being allowed to act in a mixing space on the high molecular weight polyisobutylene powder in such a manner that this is first vortexed and then discharged from the mixing apparatus together with the inert carrier gas. The process according to the invention is suitable in particular for combating uncontrolled oil discharges onto water.
</ABSTRACT>
