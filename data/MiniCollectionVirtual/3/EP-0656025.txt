<ABSTRACT>
A solution and a process are disclosed for metallising nonconductors by means of conductive polymers which contain water-soluble polymers besides the aromatic compounds required to produce the conductive polymers. As water-soluble polymers homopolymers or copolymers are used.
</ABSTRACT>
