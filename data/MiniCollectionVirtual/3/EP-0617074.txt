<INVENTION-TITLE>
Alveolar cellulose material containing a biocide.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47K702	A47K703	A47L1316	A47L1317	C08J900	C08J900	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47K	A47K	A47L	A47L	C08J	C08J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47K7	A47K7	A47L13	A47L13	C08J9	C08J9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Cellulose-based cellular material containing a biocidal agent and process for its preparation. Characteristically, microparticles of matrix type containing at least one biocidal agent are distributed in the cellulose network of the said material and provide therein a controlled release of the said biocidal agent(s) in the presence of moisture or of water.
</ABSTRACT>
