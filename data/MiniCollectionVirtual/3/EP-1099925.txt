<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for coating the interior surface of a gun barrel
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F41A2100	F41A2104	F41A2122	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F41A	F41A	F41A	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F41A21	F41A21	F41A21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The internal coating process for the barrel (1) of a weapon comprises applying an intermediate coating (5) by explosive cladding. A high melting point coating (3) is then applied over this by electroplating. The intermediate coating has higher thermal conductivity than the barrel and greater ductility than that of the high melting point coating.
</ABSTRACT>
