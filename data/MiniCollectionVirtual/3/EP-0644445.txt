<INVENTION-TITLE>
Aspherical lens.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a dioptric lens (objective) having a plurality of lens elements. The lens has a back diaphragm (rear diaphragm) (17) which acts as exit pupil (aperture diaphragm). The lens is distinguished in particular by the fact that the object-side surface of the object-side lens element has an aspherical component. In addition, a central lens and the image-side lenses are moved together closely. It is thus possible to achieve overall lengths of the order of magnitude of the focal length.
