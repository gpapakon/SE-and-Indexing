ROOF AIR-VENT PIPE MADE OF PLASTIC

<ABSTRACT>
The invention concerns a roof air-vent pipe (D) made of plastic, with a roof-covering plate (1) over which is fitted a dome (2) with a cap (6) which carries the vent pipe (3). In order to provide a vent pipe which is, in particular, efficient in use, the invention proposes that at least the external surfaces (8) of the dome (2) and the vent pipe (3) have a layer of copper covered with plastic.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
Roof vent (D) made of plastic with a dome (2) projecting 
beyond the upper side of a roofing tile (1) and with a 

ventilation tube (3) carried by the outer surface (8) of the 
dome (2) via a cap (6), characterised in that at least the 

outer surface (8) of the dome (2) and of the ventilation 
tube (3) carries a patina green layer (A) plus a protective 

lacquer layer (B) which is in turn covered with a copper 
lacquer layer (C). 
Roof vent according to claim 1, characterised in that the 
patina green layer (A) and protective lacquer layer (B) are 

applied wet-in-wet one on top of the other. 
Roof vent according to claim 1, characterised in that 
patina green and protective lacquer are applied in a single 

layer (A/B). 
Roof vent according to one or more of the preceding 
claims, characterised in that the copper lacquer layer (C) 

is 15-60 Âµm with 5-30% copper fraction in the binder. 
</CLAIMS>
