<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method and machine for dyeing manufactured articles of clothing.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>D06B500	D06B524	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>D06B	D06B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>D06B5	D06B5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Machine for dyeing ready-made articles of clothing, with a cylindrical dyeing vat (1) having a reinforced flat bottom and comprising: a basket for containing the articles to be treated, with a central vertical supporting shaft (2) for controlling the movement of the said basket, the latter being provided with at least one upper flap and with at least one lower flap; moreover, the basket (13) being provided with flow-breaker fins (19) arranged inside it; at least one actuating cylinder (16) of vertical axis, connected to the basket (13) by means of a crossmember (15), in the middle of which the shaft (2) passes, for the alternating vertical movement of the basket (13); an actuating cylinder (14) supported by the crossmember (15) and having the rod connected to a lever (18) articulated on the upper end of the shaft (2) for the alternating rotation of the basket (13) about the respective vertical axis; an actuating cylinder (17) connected to the basket (13) for allowing it to move in a horizontal translational motion; a pump (7) for supplying the dyestuffs and for the forced circulation of the dye bath; a resistance coil (8) for heating the dye bath to an adjustable temperature.
