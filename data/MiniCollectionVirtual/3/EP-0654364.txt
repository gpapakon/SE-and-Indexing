<INVENTION-TITLE>
Rubber composition.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08L2100	C08L2100	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08L	C08L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C08L21	C08L21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A rubber mixture for tyre treads which contains epoxidised polydiene in an amount of from 3 to 80% by weight, based on 100 parts by weight of rubber, and from 3 to 100 parts by weight of silicic acid and up to 100 parts by weight of carbon black.
</ABSTRACT>
