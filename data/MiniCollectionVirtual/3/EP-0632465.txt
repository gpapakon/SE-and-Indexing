<INVENTION-TITLE>
Low capacity read only memory.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G11C1714	G11C1718	H01L2358	H01L2362	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G11C	G11C	H01L	H01L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G11C17	G11C17	H01L23	H01L23	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Read-only memory for storing the choice of electronic functions in for example a vehicle, including a circuit (2) for writing at least one memory slot (1). The memory slot (1) includes a fuse in the form of a discrete component and the write circuit (2) is arranged so as to feed a fusing current into the fuse (1).
