<INVENTION-TITLE>
Sanitary towel forming a protection enveloppe before the use
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a sanitary towel (1) forming a protective envelope (15) before use and, optionally, a disposable package after use of the said sanitary towel, and which is of the type comprising a central fluid-absorbing element (2), arranged in an envelope which has, on its face in contact with the undergarment, means (7, 8) of fastening to the said undergarment, and at least one lateral flap (3) arranged along the said central element, capable of being folded down under the undergarment and fitted with at least one means (9) of fastening to the undergarment. According to the invention, the said protective envelope (15) or packaging formed by the sanitary towel (1) before and after use thereof consists of folding the said flap (3) onto the said central element (3) which is itself folded into three or into a C, without the folded central element (2) overlapping its own fastening means (7, 8) and without the said flap (3), once folded, overlapping the aforementioned means (7, 8) for fastening the said central element (3) and, if required, another flap.

<CLAIMS>
Sanitary towel (1) forming a protective envelope (15)
before use and if needs be disposable packaging after use,

of the type comprising a central element absorbing fluids

(2) disposed in an envelope having on its face in contact
with the undergarment means of attachment (7,8) to the said

undergarment, and at least one side flap (3) disposed along
the said central element, which can be turned back under

the undergarment and provided with at least one means of
attachment (9) to the said undergarment, characterized in

that the said protective or packaging element (15) formed
by the towel (1) before and after use thereof, is

constituted by folding the said flap (3) onto the said
central element (2), which is itself folded in three or

into a C, the folded central element (2) not overlapping
its own means of attachment (7,8) and the said flap (3),

once folded, not overlapping the aforementioned means of
attachment (7,8) of the said central element and, as the

case may be, of another flap.
Sanitary towel according to claim 1, characterized in
that it includes two side flaps (3 and 4) provided

respectively with means of attachment (9 and 10).
Sanitary towel according to claim 1 or 2, characterized
in that in the folded position it includes means of

protection (16) disposed on the aforementioned means of
attachment (7, 8, 9 and 10) both of the central element and

of the flap or flaps.
Sanitary towel according to one of the preceding
claims, characterized in that the aforementioned means of

attachment are adhesive patches and in that the
aforementioned means of protection is a siliconized strip. 
Sanitary towel according to one of claims 2 to 4,
characterized in that the two ends (13, 14) of the central

element (2) and the two side flaps (3, 4) are folded edge-to-edge.
