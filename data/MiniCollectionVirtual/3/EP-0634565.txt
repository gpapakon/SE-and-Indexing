<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for improving the cold stant behaviour of internal combustion engines.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
For storing the waste heat of internal combustion engines (10) and utilising it to preheat the internal combustion engine just prior to a cold start, the stored waste heat is introduced into the engine oil circuit (29) and/or into the starter battery.
