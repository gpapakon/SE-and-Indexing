<INVENTION-TITLE>
PROTECTIVE CASE FOR A MOBILE TELEPHONE
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A45C1100	A45C1124	H04B138	H04B138	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A45C	A45C	H04B	H04B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A45C11	A45C11	H04B1	H04B1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a protective case for a mobile telephone, essentially comprising a top part (2) and a bottom part (3), wherein the top part (2) and the bottom part (3) consist of hard, shock-resistant and break-proof walls (1). Excellent protection of a mobile telephone is thus ensured.
</ABSTRACT>
