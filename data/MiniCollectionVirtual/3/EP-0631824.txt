Container for dispensing liquid.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a container (1) for dispensing liquid (2), in particular for spraying or foaming dispensing, with a dispensing nozzle (D) arranged on the top side of the container (1) and a pump handle (15) for a pump (P), assigned to the container, with a pump cylinder (11), piston rod (16) and pump piston (22) for generating a pressure cushion above the liquid level (14), the pump chamber (21) having an air inlet valve (V1) and an air outlet valve (V2), and proposes, for achieving a construction which is favourable for use and functionally reliable, that the air outlet valve (V2) consists of two valves (34, 35) connected one after the other, between which a volume of air can be enclosed.
