<ABSTRACT>
Aqueous ink formulation and a process for printing sheetlike cellulose fibre materials or materials containing cellulose fibres with reactive dyes using a single-phase (one-phase) ink-jet process or some other non-contact minimal add-on spray technique and subsequent fixation of the reactive dyes on the fibre, characterised in that the aqueous ink formulation contains an alkali metal fluoride, an alkali metal formate, an alkali metal citrate, an alkali metal salt of a C2-C4-dicarboxylic acid or a mixture of the alkali metal salts mentioned.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
A process for printing sheetlike cellulose fiber 
materials or materials containing cellulose fibers 

with reactive dyes using a single-phase inkjet 
process or some other non-contact minimal addon 

spray technique and subsequent fixation of the 
reactive dyes on the fiber, which comprises using an 

aqueous ink formulation comprising an alkali metal 
fluoride, an alkali metal formate, an alkali metal 

citrate or a mixture thereof. 
The process of claim 1, wherein the alkali metal is 
lithium, sodium or potassium. 
The process of claim 1 or 2, wherein the alkali 
metal salt is sodium fluoride, potassium fluoride, 

lithium formate, sodium formate, potassium formate, 
sodium citrate, potassium citrate or lithium 

citrate. 
The process of claim 1 or 2, wherein the alkali 
metal salt is sodium formate. 
The process of claim 1 or 2, wherein the alkali 
metal salt is potassium fluoride. 
The process of at least one of claims 1 to 5, 
wherein the alkali metal salt or the mixture of the 

alkali metal salts is present in a concentration 
from 30 to 300 g per liter of ink formulation,  

 
preferably from 100 to 200 g per liter of ink 

formulation. 
The process of at least one of claims 1 to 6, 
wherein the ink formulation additionally contains 

one or more hydrotropic substances, preferably urea, 
a glycol monoalkyl ether or glycol. 
The process of at least one of claims 1 to 7, 
wherein the ink formulation additionally contains 

one or more salts containing water of crystallization, 
preferably lithium chloride, lithium sulfate, 

sodium sulfate or sodium citrate. 
The process of at least one of claims 1 to 8, 
wherein the ink formulation additionally contains 

one or more disperse dyes. 
The process of at least one of claims 1 to 9, 
wherein the fiber material to be printed is cotton, 

spun rayon, regenerated cellulose (viscose) or a 
polyester-cellulose blend fabric. 
An aqueous ink formulation for inkjet printing or 
for any other non-contact minimal addon spray 

technique, consisting essentially of 

a) an aqueous solution of a reactive dye, 
b) at least one alkali metal fluoride, alkali metal 
formate or alkali metal citrate,
 
and optionally of 
c) one or more disperse dyes, 
d) one or more salts containing water of crystallization, 
e) hydrotropic substances and/or 
f) further customary additives. 
The aqueous ink formulation of claim 11, wherein 
component b) is sodium formate or potassium 

fluoride. 
</CLAIMS>
