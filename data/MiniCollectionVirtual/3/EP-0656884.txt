<INVENTION-TITLE>
NOVEL POLY-IODATED COMPOUNDS, METHOD OF PREPARATION AND CONTRAST PRODUCT CONTAINING SAME.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61K4904	A61K4904	C07C23700	C07C23746	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61K	A61K	C07C	C07C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61K49	A61K49	C07C237	C07C237	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Novel poly-iodated compounds of general formula (I), in which R1, R2 the same or different, denote a group of formula (a) and R3 and R4, the same or different, denote a group of formula (b) with R1, R2, R3 and R4 comprising a total of at least ten hydroxy groups. The compounds according to the invention are for use as contrast products in radiography. The invention also concerns a process for the preparation of said compounds and a contrast product containing same.
</ABSTRACT>
