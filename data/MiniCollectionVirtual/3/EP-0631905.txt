Covering element.

<ABSTRACT>
Lining element for insulating acoustically annoying oscillations, including a foamed material body (1) with closed cells for sealing off a hollow body (3) which is provided with an aperture (2) on at least one side, the foamed material body (1) having a fastening device (4) and being capable of being fastened to the hollow body (3). The foamed material body (1) has a structure which is adapted to the shape of the aperture (2), the foamed material body (1) also having the fastening device (4) on the circumferential side (6) facing the delimitation (5) of the aperture (2), the fastening device (4) being formed by an endless, circumferential groove (7) which is open in the direction of the delimitation (5) and the groove (7) and the delimitation (5) being capable of being brought into engagement with one another in a sealing and positive manner.
