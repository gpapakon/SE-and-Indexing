<INVENTION-TITLE>
ROAD USE CHARGE COLLECTING DEVICE.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G07B1500	G07B1500	G07F700	G07F700	G07F708	G07F708	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G07B	G07B	G07F	G07F	G07F	G07F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
Road-use charge collecting device, with a value-card reader to be carried in the motor vehicle and having means for debiting a specific amount from a value card, inserted into the value-card reader, at preset time intervals.
