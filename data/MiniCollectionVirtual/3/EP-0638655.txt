<ABSTRACT>
The use of a Cu alloy, of compsn. 81-88 (pref. 83-86) wt.% Cu, less than 4 (pref. 2.5-3.7) wt.% Si, balance (pref. 10-13 wt.%), Zn and impurities, for (parts of) horse bits is claimed.
</ABSTRACT>
