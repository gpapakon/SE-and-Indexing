<INVENTION-TITLE>
Dishwasher with vertically slidable door
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47L1542	A47L1542	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47L	A47L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
Dishwasher, in particular for dishes and articles for medical use, which are disinfected, having a slidable door (2) guided by plain bearings or rollers (8), the plain bearings or rollers (8) being mounted on the door-side ends of closing rockers (6) rotatably mounted on the side walls of the housing (1).

<CLAIMS>
A washing machine, more particularly for dish-washing 
and for washing articles for medicinal use, 

which are disinfected, having a sliding door (2) having 
longitudinal guides (3) guided by plain bearings or 

rollers (8), characterised in that 

closure rockers (6) are pivotally mounted by means 
of pivots (7) on the side walls of the housing (1) 

of the washing machine 
the plain bearings or rollers (8) are mounted on 
the ends of the closure rockers (6) adjacent the 

door 
the sliding door (2) is pushed open and shut to 
seal the housing (1), without contact, when the 

closure rockers (6) are pivoted into the sliding 
position, 
the sliding door (2) is pressed against the seal 
of the housing (1) of the washing machine when the 

closure rockers (6) are pivoted into an angular 
position differing from the sliding position. 
A washing machine according to claim 1, 
characterised in that one end of a linear drive (11) is 

rotatably articulated by means of bolts (9) on the ends 
of the closure rockers (6) adjacent the machine, the 

other end of the linear drive (11) being screwed to a 
bearing (5) fixed on the washing machine housing (1). 
A washing machine according to at least one of the 
preceding claims, characterised in that a pull rod (11) 

is rotatably articulated, on the one hand, on the end 
of a closure rocker (6) adjacent the machine and, on  

 
the other hand, on the end of another closure rocker 

adjacent the machine. 
</CLAIMS>
