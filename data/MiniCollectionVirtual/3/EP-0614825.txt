Courier delivery container for mixed loads, e.g. for parcels and/or suspended clothing

<ABSTRACT>
Delivery (transportation) container comprising a frame (10) on legs (18) with four corner brackets (12) forming uprights on which are fastened a baseplate (14) and a top plate (16), characterised in that it comprises two closed faces (22) each including a lateral plate (24) equipped with housings (52) provided in order to receive the ends of removable support bars (30), linking means (58) being provided between the support bars and the plates.

<CLAIMS>
Transport container comprising on the one hand a
framework (10) on feet (18) with four uprights (12) upon

which there are fixed a base plate (14) and a top plate
(16), and on the other hand removable support rods (30),

characterised in that it comprises two closed faces (22)
and at least one open face (20) which can be blocked up,

the two closed faces (22) each comprising a lateral plate
(20) equipped with housings (52) provided to receive the

ends of the removable support rods (30), the length (L)
of the support rods (30) being greater than the distance

(l) separating the two lateral plates, connecting means
(58) being provided between the support rods and the

plates.
Transport container according to claim 1,
characterised in that the uprights (12) comprise means of

blockage of the bars (30) transversely to the open faces
(20).
Transport container according to claim 1 or 2,
characterised in that the rods (30) have a length (L)

equal to the distance separating the interior of the
wings (34 or 36) of two uprights (12) facing one another. 
Transport container according to any one of the
preceding claims, characterised in that the lateral

plates (24) comprise oblong holes (52) constituting
housings provided to receive the support rods.
Transport container according to claim 3 or 4,
characterised in that the rods (30) comprise pins (58)

constituting the connecting means for the support rods
with the plates, said pins projecting radially and being

so disposed that the distance separating two pins of the
same rod is greater than the distance (l) separating the

two lateral plates.
Transport container according to claim 5,
characterised in that the pins have a length comprised

between the small and the large diameters of the oblong
holes.
Transport container according to any one of the
preceding claims, characterised in that the lateral

plates (24) are fixed to the upright (12) by screws (46)
on an internal revetment (38) aligned at 90° and extending

the wing (34) of the side of the closed face in such a
way that these screws (36) constitute blocking means for

the rods in co-operation with an internal revetment at 90°
(40) extending the wing (36) of the uprights (12) on the

side of the open face (20). 
Transport container according to any one of the
preceding claims, characterised in that the base plate

(14), the opposed uprights (12) on either side of the
lateral plates (24) and this same lateral plate

constitute storage housings for the unused rods.
Transport container according to any one of the
preceding claims, characterised in that each upright (12)

comprises at its upper portion a plate (48) for receiving
the feet (18) of an identical container stacked on the

first.
Transport container according to any one of the
preceding claims, characterised in that it comprises

support means for documents and information provided on
the lateral plates (24).
