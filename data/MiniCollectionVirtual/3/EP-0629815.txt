<INVENTION-TITLE>
Module for a radiating device.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F21V1700	F21V1710	F21V21005	F21V21005	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F21V	F21V	F21V	F21V	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F21V17	F21V17	F21V21	F21V21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The rear wall of the radiator module is divided parallel to the symmetrical plane. The edges of the rear wall sections (3) giving mirror symmetrical profiled sections (5) along the dividing line, to give holding projections. Neighbouring and facing profiled sections are held together by clamps (6). On the outside of the side wall are profile parts which act as holder projections.
</ABSTRACT>
