<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a process for making thermoplastic hollow objects, in which first of all at least two part-objects (14, 30) are produced and are then joined together to form a complete hollow object, at least one of the part-objects being produced by compression moulding and bonded by welding or adhesion to at least one further part-object to form the hollow object.
