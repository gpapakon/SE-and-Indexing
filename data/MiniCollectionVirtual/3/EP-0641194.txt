<ABSTRACT>
An oil-in-water emulsion comprising at least a perfluoropolyether, a non-cationic fluorinated surfactant, a coemulsifier, a fatty alcohol and a gelled aqueous phase, is disclosed. A method for preparing the emulsion, its use for preparing cosmetic compositions, and cosmetic compositions containing said emulsion, are also disclosed.
</ABSTRACT>
