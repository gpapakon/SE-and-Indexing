<ABSTRACT>
The novel substances nordanoxamine, desferrinordanoxamine, desferrisalmycins A, B, C and D and also the known substances danoxamine and desferridanoxamine are prepared from the antibiotics salmycins A, B, C and D, which are obtained by fermentation. The substances are used as chelators and medicaments.
</ABSTRACT>
