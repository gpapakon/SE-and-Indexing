Electrical switch for an acoustical warning device.

<ABSTRACT>
In an electrical switch for an acoustic warning device on motor vehicles, the signalling horn is controlled by means of a push-button key via a slip ring structure or a belt-shaped conductor. In order to avoid electrical loading, the signalling horn is connected to the slip ring structure or the conductor belt via a field-effect transistor.
