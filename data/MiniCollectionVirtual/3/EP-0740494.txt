<INVENTION-TITLE>
Electrical circuit for the pulsed operation of discharge lamps
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H05B4100	H05B4104	H05B4128	H05B41288	H05B4138	H05B4138	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H05B	H05B	H05B	H05B	H05B	H05B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H05B41	H05B41	H05B41	H05B41	H05B41	H05B41	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The circuit has a DC source (OWF) coupled to a half bridge with a pair of semiconductor switches (S1,S2), a blind resistance (L,Ck) in series with the high pressure discharge lamp (EL) and a lamp ignition stage (Z). The blind resistance includes a non-linear reactance (L) which can be switched between 2 alternate values, with a ratio of about 40 between these reactance values.
</ABSTRACT>
