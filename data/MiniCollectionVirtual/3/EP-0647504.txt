<INVENTION-TITLE>
Surface tattoing device.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B24C100	B24C104	B24C700	B24C700	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B24C	B24C	B24C	B24C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B24C1	B24C1	B24C7	B24C7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Device (4) for tattooing surfaces, intended especially for marking windows of vehicles, comprising at least one stencil (5) and a spraying tool (7) connected to a source (8) of pressurised fluid and to a reservoir (9) of abrasive powder. According to the invention, it includes a fluid reservoir (10) interposed between the source (8) and the gun (7), having a given internal volume "V" and means (11) for bringing about a sequential and gradual regulation of the introduction and evacuation of the pressurised fluid into and from inside the reservoir (10) with a view to obtaining marking of the said glass to a given depth. Application to the marking of the windows of vehicles.
