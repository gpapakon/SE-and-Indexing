<INVENTION-TITLE>
Heating for vacuum-forming machines.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a heating system for vacuum forming machines for plastification of the thermoplastic sheets or films to be shaped. The heating system, more precisely the upper and/or lower heating system, is fitted with radiators (2) in the form of short-wave infra-red radiators having a radiation maximum in the region of wavelengths shorter than 2  mu m ( lambda  
<
 2  mu m). The infra-red radiators are expediently in the form of halogen lamps. These radiators produce relatively deep penetration into the surface of the plastic sheets. Preheating is unnecessary due to the speed of action of such radiators.
</ABSTRACT>
