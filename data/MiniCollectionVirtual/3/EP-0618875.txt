<CLAIMS>
At least partially degradable film composition 
characterized in that it includes a mixture made up: 


of alginate; 
of one or more components chosen from the group 
consisting of chitin, eggshell, cuttlebone extracts, 

pulverulent materials obtained by grinding shellfish, 
components based on pulverulent minerals, cellulose-based 

components, elastomers, vegetable fibres; 
of a substance providing di- or polyvalent 
cations. 
Composition according to Claim 1, characterized 
in that the said alginate is algal and more particularly 

of marine origin. 
Composition according to Claim 1 or 2, 
characterized in that the alginate is obtained from algae 

chosen from the group consisting of the Chlorophyceae, 
the Rhodophyceae, the Phaeophyceae, or of a mixture of 

these. 
Composition according to Claim 3, characterized 
in that the said algae are chosen from those of the genus 

Laminaria, Fucus, Ascophyllum, Sargassum and Saccorhiza. 
Composition according to Claim 4, characterized 
in that the said algae are chosen from the species Fucus 

vesiculosus, Fucus serratum, Laminaria digitata and 
Laminaria elongata. 
Composition according to one of Claims 1 to 5, 
characterized in that it includes an alginate and an 

elastomer. 
Composition according to one of Claims 1 to 5, 
characterized in that it includes an alginate and  

 
cellulose fibres. 
Composition according to one of Claims 1 to 5, 
characterized in that it includes an alginate, an 

elastomer and cellulose fibres. 
Composition according to either of Claims 6 and 
8, characterized in that the said elastomer consists of 

latex. 
Composition according to one of Claims 1 to 9, 
characterized in that the said substance is a substance 

providing Ca
++
 ions. 
Composition according to one of Claims 1 to 10, 
characterized in that it includes approximately 20 to 

50 % by weight of alginate. 
Film obtained from a composition in accordance 
with one of Claims 1 to 11. 
Film according to Claim 12, characterized in that 
it is treated with the aid of a tanning substance. 
Film according to Claim 13, characterized in that 
the said substance is an alum. 
Film according to Claim 14, characterized in that 
the said substance is a potassium alum. 
</CLAIMS>
