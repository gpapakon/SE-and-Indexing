<INVENTION-TITLE>
Diaphragm mounting device in water-drawing point next to a flow meter.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E03B700	E03B707	E03B900	E03B900	G01F134	G01F142	G01F500	G01F500	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E03B	E03B	E03B	E03B	G01F	G01F	G01F	G01F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>E03B7	E03B7	E03B9	E03B9	G01F1	G01F1	G01F5	G01F5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A device for mounting a diaphragm (7) in a water supply pipe element (1), characterised in that it includes an internal flange (6) for accommodating the diaphragm (7), made inside the pipe element, the diaphragm being provided with a peripheral rim (9) for bearing against the flange (6), a diffuser (11) forming a spacer, applied by one of its ends against the diaphragm (6) and a diffuser support (14) bearing at one of its ends against the end of the diffuser (11) away from the diaphragm (6) and including, at its other end, elastic locking lugs (18) which interact by elastic engagement with an annular housing (22) made in the pipe element.
