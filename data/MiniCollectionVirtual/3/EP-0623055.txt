<ABSTRACT>
The invention concerns a crusher with interlocking crushing rollers and crushing elements designed in the shape of ring segments (6) and fitted with teeth. The ring segments (6) occupy about half of the circumference of each roller (3, 4) and interlock with those parts (9) of the circumference of the other rollers (3, 4) which are not occupied by the ring segments (6). Each ring segment (6) is preferably designed as a radially extending part of a ring (16) fitted detachably to the drive shaft (5).
</ABSTRACT>
