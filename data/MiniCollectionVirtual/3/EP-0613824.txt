<INVENTION-TITLE>
Package with towel containing washing substances.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65D7552	B65D7558	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65D	B65D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an air- and moisture-impermeable form of packaging (package), consisting of a plastics film, a laminate or a metal foil, for one or more cloths composed of abrasion-resistant, absorbent material such as paper, web, in particular cotton web, synthetic fibre fabrics or felt and impregnated with detergent-active substances, the detergent-active substances comprising at least 0.5% by weight of anionic or nonionic surfactants, remainder water, and/or solvents and further auxiliaries.
</ABSTRACT>
