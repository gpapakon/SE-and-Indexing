<ABSTRACT>
In order to improve a machine tool, comprising a machine frame, a work carrier arranged on the machine frame for a workpiece to be machined, a tool carrier arranged on the machine frame for mounting a tool, and at least one drive with a slide for performing a relative movement between the tool and the workpiece in the direction of at least one axis, in such a way that this machine tool permits the highest possible dynamics, it is proposed that the drive comprise electric linear motors orientated parallel to the axis and arranged at a distance from one another in a transverse direction, that the slide extend between the two linear motors, and that a control system be provided which simultaneously operates the linear motors for traversing the slide.

<CLAIMS>
A machine tool comprising a machine frame (10), a work-piece
support (48) which is arranged on the machine frame (10) for

supporting a work-piece (12) that is to be worked, a tool
spindle (16) which is arranged such as to be moveable on the

machine frame (10) and serves for accommodating a tool (14), a
first carriage (18) which supports the tool spindle (16) and

which is moveable along a first axis (Y) and is guided on a
first carriage support (20), a second carriage (26) which is

moveable along a second axis (X) that extends transversely to
the first axis (Y) and which comprises the first carriage (20)

and is guided on a second carriage support (184, 186) that is in
the form of a yoke surrounding a second central opening (30),

wherein the second carriage support (184, 186) is part of the
machine frame (10), and a first and a second drive means (190,

192; 88, 90) for effecting relative movement between the tool
(14) and the work-piece (12) along the axes (Y, X),
characterised in that

the first carriage support (20) is in the form of a yoke
surrounding a first central opening (22) and the tool spindle

(16) extends through said first central opening (22) in
collision free manner at every position along the maximum path

length thereof along the first axis (Y), and 
in that
 the tool
spindle (16) extends through the second central opening (30) in

the second carriage support (184, 186) in collision free manner
at every position along the first axis (Y) and the second axis

(X) .
