<INVENTION-TITLE>
Multiple column press or similar press installation for sheet metal forming.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B21D4305	B21D4305	B30B1504	B30B1504	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B21D	B21D	B30B	B30B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B21D43	B21D43	B30B15	B30B15	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In order to reduce the bending moments within the region in which power is imparted to the press slide of a multiple column press, the gear means (5, 21, 22) are mounted in bearings (20) of transverse supports (19). The transverse supports (19) are fixedly connected to the press columns (4) which form a pair of columns composed of a tie rod (12) and tie rod sleeves (13).
