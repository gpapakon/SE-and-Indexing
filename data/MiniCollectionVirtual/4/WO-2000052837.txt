<INVENTION-TITLE>
TRANSMISSION POWER CONTROL DEVICE AND TRANSMISSIOM POWER CONTROL METHOD
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H03G330	H03G330	H04B104	H04B104	H04B7005	H04B7005	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H03G	H03G	H04B	H04B	H04B	H04B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H03G3	H03G3	H04B1	H04B1	H04B7	H04B7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
An effective value calculating section (22) receives a detection output signal of a transmission signal from a detecting circuit (21) and performs effective value calculation. A comparator (8) compares the detection output signal subjected to the effective value calculation with a reference signal and generates an attenuation adjusting signal for controlling the electric energy of the transmission signal which has been subjected to spread modulation and multiplexed.
</ABSTRACT>
