<INVENTION-TITLE>
Bed pan.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61G900	A61G900	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a bedpan intended to collect faecal matter and urine from bed-ridden individuals who cannot leave their bed, for example patients, characterised in that it includes at least two gripping means, a removable means making it possible to prevent mixing of the faecal matter and the urine of the patient, the shapes of the said bedpan being adapted to the human morphology.
