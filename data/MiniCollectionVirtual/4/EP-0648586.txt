Saw chain.

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a saw chain for power chain saws for cutting hard objects such as walls of brick, stone or concrete, one or more of the chain links (2, 11) being provided with cutting bodies (1), characterised in that the cutting bodies are connected in each case to a cutting-body carrier (8, 10) which in turn is connected to the chain link (cutting link).
