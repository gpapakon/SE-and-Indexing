<INVENTION-TITLE>
Closing plug for an opening.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B62D2500	B62D2524	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B62D	B62D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B62D25	B62D25	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a closing plug (1) for various assembly openings (12) in components (11), in particular in interior parts or accessory parts of motor vehicles. The closing plug (1) is designed in at least two parts and comprises a clamping part (2) and a covering cap (3), the two components being connected to each other in a jointed manner (4). A ball-and-socket joint (4) is provided as the joint for connecting the clamping part (2) and covering cap (3), the ball head (5) being integrally arranged on the top surface (6) of the clamping part (2) and the bearing (7) for the ball being arranged in the covering cap (3).
