<INVENTION-TITLE>
Protective layer for landfills and other sealing layers.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a protective layer for landfills and other sealing layers, in particular for such with plastic sealing sheets. The protective layer consists of a multiplicity of shaped bodies, threads or the like of plastic which are connected to one another to form an elastic flat-shaped structure. The protective layer has a voidage of 20 - 50% and is permeable to mineral substances on at least one side.
