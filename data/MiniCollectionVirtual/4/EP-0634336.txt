<CLAIMS>
A two-compartment container (1) comprising two chambers (2) 
and (3) stacked one upon another which are connected by a 

passage opening (4) closed by a stopper (5), having a screw 
cap (8) attached to the upper chamber (3), which partly 

embraces the outer wall of the upper chamber (3), 
characterized by a combination of the following features: 


a) The stopper (5) includes a coupling link (6) extending 
into the upper chamber (3), the end (7) of link (6) is 

constructed as a disk-shaped or rectangular unit; 
b) underneath this end part (7), hook-shaped and contoured 
supports (12) snap in forming the lower end of a handle 

(10) which has an enlargement (13) on its upper end 
protruding from the container (1); 
c) the handle (10) is connected to the closure cap (8) in one 
piece as a whole by an elastic membrane (9), which is 

turned inside as long as the chamber is closed, and 
turning outside after the stopper is removed. 
Two-compartment container according to claim 1, characterized 
in that the disk-shaped or rectangular end (7) of 
the 
coupling link (6) which is connected with the stopper (5) is 

undercut to catch the hook-shaped supports (12). 
Two-compartment container according to claim 1 or 2, 
characterized in that the container (1) has a reduced 

diameter in the region of the passage opening (4) between the 
chambers (2) and (3) related to the total diameter of the 

container (1).
