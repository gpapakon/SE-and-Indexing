<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for capturing the retinal reflex image
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61B312	A61B312	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61B	A61B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61B3	A61B3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
System comprises a beam splitter for transmission of light into the eye and for transmission of the back-scattered light reflected from the retina, a receiver for recording the back- scattered light, a projector to project an additional image on to the retina that is superimposed on the original image transmitted onto the retina and a scanning system for serial sampling of the images coming from the retina that are directed to an opto-electronic detector. The system is used in Ophthalmology.
</ABSTRACT>
