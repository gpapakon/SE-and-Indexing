Sound dampening open construction element

<ABSTRACT>
Sound insulation element of open construction, especially for use in vehicle construction, for example oil sump and cover plate, with at least two metal layers arranged one over another.

<CLAIMS>
Open component for noise insulation, in particular 
for use in vehicle construction, for example oil sump and 

metal cover plate, wherein the component has at least two 
metallic layers, in particular metal plates, disposed one 

above the other, characterized in that at least one of 
the two layers has a coating which is applied to the side 

which is adjacent to the other layer and in that the 
coating is composed of aluminium. 
Open component according to Claim 1, characterized 
in that the two layers have a different thickness. 
Open component according to Claim 1, characterized 
in that the two layers can be joined to one another by 

pressure joints. 
</CLAIMS>
