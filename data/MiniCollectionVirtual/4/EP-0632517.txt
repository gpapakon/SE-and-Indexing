<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a dipole probe. This dipole probe consists of an antenna and a mixer connected to this antenna via a stripline. The antenna is a dipole antenna. The dipole antenna is formed on a susbtrate and the substrate is fixed in a first waveguide approximately perpendicularly to the longitudinal axis of the waveguide. The output of the dipole antenna is designed as a coplanar line.
