Layer-elements of one or more layers deposited on substrates and their manufacture.

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to single- or multilayer elements applied to supports, comprising   a) a modified support, where the modification means application of reactive groups with reactivity in the same direction over the entire surface, and b) one or more layers of organic polymer materials containing reactive groups which are reactive in the same direction in each layer, where the reactive groups in the first layer are ready to react with the reactive groups of the support and, in the case of several layers, each further layer in turn contains reactive groups which are ready to react with those of the previous layer, and where the reactive groups form covalent bonds.   Such layer elements supplied to supports are produced in such a way that the individual layers are applied from solutions of the organic polymer materials in suitable solvents to modified supports by sequential chemisorption (formation of covalent bonds).
</ABSTRACT>
