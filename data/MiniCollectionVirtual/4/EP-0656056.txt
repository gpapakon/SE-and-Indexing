<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the production of a DNA fragment comprising the gene of the malolactic enzyme of Lactococcus lactis, and to the cloning and expression of said gene in E. coli, and in yeasts, particularly of the genus Saccharomyces and Schizosaccharomyces.
