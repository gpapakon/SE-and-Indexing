<INVENTION-TITLE>
CONTAINER FOR SUPPORTS FOR MEDICAMENTS TO BE USED CYCLICALLY.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61J700	A61J704	B65D8304	B65D8304	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61J	A61J	B65D	B65D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61J7	A61J7	B65D83	B65D83	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a container (5) for supports for medicaments to be used cyclically which must be administered in a given sequence, which is of simple construction and makes it possible to control extraction at a given time. An annular support (5) can be laid in a peripheral groove (2) between the outer wall (3) and a central region (4) of the container (5). A sector of the central region (4) facing the interior of the groove (2) has a succession of grooves or projections (6) with each of which a correspondingly shaped groove or projection of the support can engage.
</ABSTRACT>
