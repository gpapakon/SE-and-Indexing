<INVENTION-TITLE>
Cooker hob.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F24C1510	F24C1510	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F24C	F24C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F24C15	F24C15	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The cooker hob has a frame-like structure, partly consisting of extruded profiles, with a base part (1) which accommodates connecting and/or switching subassemblies and a carrier part (2) which forms the cooking area or carries a glass-ceramic plate (3). According to the invention, the base part (1) is composed of extruded-profile sections (4) and has a flat shoulder (5) running all the way round, on which the likewise frame-like carrier part (2) is mounted and fastened.
