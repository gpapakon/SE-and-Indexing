<INVENTION-TITLE>
Adapting element.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A47C762	A47C762	A47C770	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A47C	A47C	A47C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A47C7	A47C7	A47C7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
An adapting element (15) for fastening various functional elements (21), such as armrests (21), onto a working chair, which adapting element can be fixed onto or under the seat (10) of the chair on the left and/or right and projects partly laterally beyond the seat (10), the outer edge of the region (15.1) projecting beyond the seat (10) describing an arc of a circle about the centre (M) of the seat (10).
