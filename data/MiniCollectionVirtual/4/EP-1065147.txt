<INVENTION-TITLE>
Accessory for pallet
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65D1900	B65D1900	B65D1902	B65D1910	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65D	B65D	B65D	B65D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65D19	B65D19	B65D19	B65D19	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The palette system comprises end frames (11) which fit over a pair of stands (17) at each end of the palette. The side arms (13) of these carry fastening eyes (14). Side walls (15) are inserted between these, either by inserting hooks (20) into the eyes or by using bars (18) with a right-angled bend at the top which are inserted through fastening eyes on both the end frames and side walls.
</ABSTRACT>
