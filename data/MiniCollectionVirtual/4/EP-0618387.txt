Seal.

<ABSTRACT>
Seal for insertion into the gap (1) between two axially adjacent annular supporting surfaces (2,3), comprising two coaxial inflexible supporting rings (4,5) and a sealing ring (7) of elastomeric material which can be inserted into the interspace (6) between the supporting rings (4,5), the supporting rings (4,5) and the sealing ring (7) resting against the supporting surfaces (2,3) on both sides and being axially compressible between the supporting surfaces (2,3). The supporting rings (4,5) are connected to one another by at least one web (8), the web (8) passing through the sealing ring (7) at a distance from the supporting surfaces (2,3).
