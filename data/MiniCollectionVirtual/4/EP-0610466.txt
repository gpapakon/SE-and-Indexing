<INVENTION-TITLE>
USE OF DL-VALIN, ITS ESTERS AND/OR SALTS TO CONTROL THE GREASY APPEARANCE OF HAIR OR SKIN.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61K830	A61K844	A61Q500	A61Q500	A61Q1900	A61Q1900	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61K	A61K	A61Q	A61Q	A61Q	A61Q	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61K8	A61K8	A61Q5	A61Q5	A61Q19	A61Q19	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention concerns the use of DL-valin, its esters and/or its salts, preferably at a concentration of 0.05 to 10 % by wt., to control the greasy appearance of hair or skin.
</ABSTRACT>
