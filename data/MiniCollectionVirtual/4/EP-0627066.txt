<INVENTION-TITLE>
INITIATION DEVICE FOR A PYROTECHNIC SYSTEM.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F42B3900	F42B3920	F42C1500	F42C1536	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F42B	F42B	F42C	F42C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The field of the invention concerns initiation devices for a pyrotechnic system. The initiation device (7) according to the invention is characterized in that it comprises a primer-holder system (12) arranged inside a casing (10), said primer-holder system is susceptible of passing from a safety position to an armed position through the action of motor means (22) and is immobilized in its safety position by at least one first lock (17a) which is released by a shape memory actuator when the environmental temperature exceeds a given value. Application to the deconfinement of ammunition in storage aeras.
</ABSTRACT>
