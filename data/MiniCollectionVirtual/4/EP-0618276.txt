<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method of depositing a film of composite material on a electroconductive surface by electropoymerisation.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08F258	C08F258	C09D544	C09D544	H01B112	H01B112	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08F	C08F	C09D	C09D	H01B	H01B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C08F2	C08F2	C09D5	C09D5	H01B1	H01B1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Process for depositing, by electropolymerisation, a film of a composite material on an electrically conductive surface, according to which use is made of a mixture including (a) a monomer, (b) a substance forming a doping agent, (c) an auxiliary electrolyte and (d) a solvent, this mixture is subjected to electrolysis and the cathode or anode is polarised at a potential situated in the region corresponding to the electron transfer between the metal surface and the monomer. Before the electrolysis a solution is formed from the abovementioned mixture, in which the abovementioned substance is present either in the state of anion, cation, molecule, preferably organic molecule, and/or complex and dissociated into ions.
</ABSTRACT>
