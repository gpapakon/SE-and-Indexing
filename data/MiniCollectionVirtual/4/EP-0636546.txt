<CLAIMS>
Transporting mount for picture frames (4) or the
like for installation in a transporting container (1) or

for fitting on a frame made of profile rails (24), said
frame actually constituting the structure of the

transporting container (1), having at least four frame-fastening
elements (3, 3a) and having longitudinal

adjustment guides (5) and transverse adjustment guides
(6) which are fitted in the transporting container (1) or

in the frame made of profile rails (24) and on which at
least three frame-fastening elements (3) are guided such

that they can be adjusted and secured, characterized in
that the frame-fastening elements (3, 3a) are each

designed as corner angles for enclosing in each case one
corner or as a straight, side angle for enclosing in each

case part of the edge of the picture frame or the like,
in that each of the frame-fastening elements (3, 3a), on

the inner surfaces which come into contact with the
picture frame (4) or the like, are provided with a padded

covering (7) or are formed from padding material, in that
each of the frame-fastening elements (3, 3a) is of

U-shaped design in cross section, that is to say each has
a base leg (8), which serves for bearing the picture

frame (4) or the like, a border web (9), which butts
against the border of the picture frame (4) or the like,

and a cover leg (10), which grips over the picture frame
(4) or the like from above, and in that the distance of

the cover leg (10) from the base leg (8) can be adjusted
and secured.
Transporting mount according to Claim 1,
characterized in that the base leg (8) and the border web

(9) of the frame-fastening element (3, 3a) are fixedly
connected to one another, and if appropriate are also

designed integrally with one another, and in that the
cover leg (10) can be raised on the border web (9) and, 

preferably, is fastened releasably thereon.
Transporting mount according to Claim 1 or 2,
characterized in that the cover leg (10) is fastened on

the border web (9) by means of a pivoting mechanism or of
a pivoting/sliding mechanism.
Transporting mount according to one of Claims 1
to 3, characterized in that the cover leg (10) is

fastened on the border web (9) by means of a screwed-connection.
Transporting mount according to Claim 4,
characterized in that the screwed-connection (11) has a

profile strip (12), which is fitted on, or integrated in,
the border web (9) and has a longitudinal groove (13),

and a sliding block (14) which is fitted on the cover leg
(10) and has a tightening screw (15) for bracing in the

longitudinal groove (13).
Transporting mount according to Claim 4,
characterized in that the screwed-connection (11) has a

guide rail (16), which is fitted on the border web (9),
and a guide sleeve, which is fitted on the cover leg

(10), can be positioned displaceably on the guide rail
(16) and has a tightening screw for bracing on the guide

rail (16).
Transporting mount according to one of Claims 1
to 6, characterized in that a handle (17) is provided on

the cover leg (10) for the purpose of handling the cover
leg (10).
Transporting mount according to one of Claims 1
to 7, characterized in that one frame-fastening element

(3a) is arranged in a stationary manner and at least
three frame-fastening elements (3) are guided on longitudinal

and transverse adjustment guides (5, 6) such that
they can be adjusted and secured.
Transporting mount according to one of Claims 1
to 8, characterized in that the frame-fastening elements

(3) are provided on their border webs (9) with adjustment
mounts (20), which are fitted on appropriately arranged

longitudinal adjustment guides (5) or transverse 
adjustment guides (6).
Transporting mount according to Claim 9, characterized
in that the adjustment mounts (20) are designed

as sliding blocks with tightening screws, and the longitudinal
adjustment guides (5) and transverse adjustment

guides (6) are designed as profile rails.
Transporting mount according to either of Claims
9 and 10, characterized in that the longitudinal

adjustment guides (5) and transverse adjustment guides
(6) are fitted on the narrow sides (21) of a transporting

container (1).
Transporting mount according to one of Claims 9
to 11, characterized in that, for at least one frame-fastening

element (3), a longitudinal adjustment guide
(5) or a transverse adjustment guide (6) is arranged on

an extension rail (22) which, for its part, can itself be
adjusted on a transverse adjustment guide (6) or a

longitudinal adjustment guide (5).
Transporting mount according to one of Claims 9
to 12, characterized in that the transverse adjustment

guide (6) on the narrow side (21) at the base has a
parallel slide guide (23) or roller guide for supporting

heavy picture frames (4) or the like.
