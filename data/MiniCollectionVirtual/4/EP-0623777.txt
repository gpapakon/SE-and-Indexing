<INVENTION-TITLE>
Articulated pipe clamp
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F16L3308	F16L3302	F16L3302	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F16L	F16L	F16L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
Fastening clamp consisting of a strip, generally of metal, wound round itself and at each of its ends including complementary means (3, 4) for clamping and/or fastening. The strip (1) consists of three separate portions (1a, 1b, 1c), the ends of the middle portion (1b) being articulated to the facing ends of each of the other two portions (1a and 1c).

<CLAIMS>
Clip formed by a wound strip generally of metal and provided at each of its end with
complementary clamping and/or fastening means (3, 4), the stnp (1) comprising at

least two portions the ends of which are hinged on to one another about a substantially
radial axis, characterised in that the strip comprises only three portions (1a, 1b, 1c), the

central portion (1b) being hinged on to the other two portions (1a and 1c) by means of
pivots (5, 6).
