<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the use of water- and solvent-free surfactant concentrates containing mono-, di- or trialkanolammonium salts of alkyl ether sulphates and/or alkyl sulphates. The surfactant concentrates with a very high basic surfactant content are homogeneous and have such a low viscosity that they can easily be processed further at temperatures to 70 DEG C to give concentrated homogeneous and free-flowing liquid formulations with surfactant contents of 70% and higher. The concentrated liquid formulations are used in detergents, dishwashing compositions, cleaners and body cleansers.
</ABSTRACT>
