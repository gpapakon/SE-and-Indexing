Substrate-free conductive pigments.

<ABSTRACT>
Substrate-free, electrically conductive pigment comprising from 90 to 98% by weight of tin oxide and from 1 to 10% by weight of at least one dopant (doping substance) consisting of an oxide of an element from main group III, IV or V of the Periodic Table with the exception of tin, which can be prepared by intimately mixing tin dioxide with at least one dopant and calcining the resulting mixture at from 800 DEG  to 1000 DEG C for from 30 to 120 min.
</ABSTRACT>
