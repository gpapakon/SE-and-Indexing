<INVENTION-TITLE>
Container for the humidification of respiratory gas.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61M1610	A61M1616	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61M	A61M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61M16	A61M16	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a container (1) for the humidification of respiratory gas. A riser (6) leads at the back wall (4) of the container from its upper end to its floor (5) and extends by a horizontal part (6') along the floor (8). The horizontal part (6') of the riser (6) has small holes (10) which extend through the floor (8) of the container (1) and thus produce a flow connection from the interior of the container (1) to the riser (6).
