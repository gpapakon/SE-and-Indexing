<INVENTION-TITLE>
Calibration method for measuring devices and measuring device with control unit for carrying out this method.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01D1800	G01D1800	G01N2125	G01N2127	G01N3300	G01N3300	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01D	G01D	G01N	G01N	G01N	G01N	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01D18	G01D18	G01N21	G01N21	G01N33	G01N33	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
To calibrate measurement devices, a number of calibration values are measured at time intervals. Using a predetermined number of most recently measured calibration values, future calibration values, by means of which measurement values are corrected, are calculated by means of a nonlinear function. Main fields of application are irradiation IR absorption photometers.
