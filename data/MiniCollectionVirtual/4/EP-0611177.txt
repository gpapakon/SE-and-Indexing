<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method and device for freezing substances contained in vessels.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A23L336	A23L336	A23L3375	B65G2700	B65G2704	F25D310	F25D311	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A23L	A23L	A23L	B65G	B65G	F25D	F25D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A23L3	A23L3	A23L3	B65G27	B65G27	F25D3	F25D3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The container containing the substance to be frozen is placed on a conveyor support, advantageously having vibrating circuits (7), and is moved along a defined path while being held partially immersed in a liquefied gas (13) poured out (11) onto the support. Application in particular to the pharmaceutical industry.
