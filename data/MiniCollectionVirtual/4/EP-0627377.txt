<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for splicing webs.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65B4100	B65B4100	B65B4112	B65H1918	B65H1918	B65H3704	B65H3704	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65B	B65B	B65B	B65H	B65H	B65H	B65H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65B41	B65B41	B65B41	B65H19	B65H19	B65H37	B65H37	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Device for splicing material webs (50), especially webs of packaging material, in which, in order to splice an initial portion of a new material web (6) to the running-out end portion of an old material web (5), this initial portion of the new material web (6) is joined with an adhesive strip (27) which is received releasably on a pivotable pressure plate (12,13). The pressure plate (12) pivotable into the region of the running-out material web (5) cooperates with a separately pivotable pressure roller (14) which can engage at least partially through a recess (26) of the pressure plate (12), strip off the adhesive strip (27) having the initial portion of the new material web (6) and at the same time press it onto the running-out end portion of the old material web (5).
