<INVENTION-TITLE>
System and method for adaption of data transmission rate depending on line quality and a modem suitable for use in such a system.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H04L100	H04L100	H04L120	H04L120	H04L2502	H04L2502	H04M1100	H04M1100	H04M1106	H04M1106	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H04L	H04L	H04L	H04L	H04L	H04L	H04M	H04M	H04M	H04M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H04L1	H04L1	H04L1	H04L1	H04L25	H04L25	H04M11	H04M11	H04M11	H04M11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
According to this system, the local (1) and remote (3) modems having, respectively, possible data rates of 4800 bits/s to 19200 bits/s and from 4800, 9600 to 14400 bits/s can communicate according to their common 4800, 9600 to 14400 bits/s data rates by adapting their data rate according to the quality of the transmission medium (85). Application: data transmission.
