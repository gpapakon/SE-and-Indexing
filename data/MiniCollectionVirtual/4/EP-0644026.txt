<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for manufacturing blanks of pavement markers or bituminous material, and device for carrying out this process.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B29C3300	B29C3300	B29C3500	B29C3516	B29C3700	B29C3700	B29C3902	B29C3902	E01F904	E01F906	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B29C33	B29C33	B29C35	B29C35	B29C37	B29C37	B29C39	B29C39	E01F9	E01F9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In the process for manufacturing blanks for carriageway marking material and bitumen, the constituents of the material are melted and mixed in a cooker (20), the liquid material is introduced into a mould (26) covered by a release agent and the set material is transported to the place where it is to be used, is heated there and applied to a carriageway. A plurality of moulds (26) which are coated with a release agent and can be used many times are provided. After setting, the material is removed from the moulds (26) and wrapped in a polyethylene film.
</ABSTRACT>
