<INVENTION-TITLE>
Ramming protection.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E04F1902	E04F1902	E04F1904	E04F1904	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E04F	E04F	E04F	E04F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>E04F19	E04F19	E04F19	E04F19	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The guard is fixed to the floor (2), being particularly for a factory wall (1). A metal casing (4) forming the impact surface is anchored to the core (3), and also serves to mould the latter. Bent-over top and bottom ends (7) of the casing forming undercuts can be used to form the anchors. The bottom end can be bent over twice towards the inside to form a channel-section containing a strip (9) of soft foam material. The top end can be bent over inwards once, forming a lengthwise seam between guard and wall tapering towards the top.
</ABSTRACT>
