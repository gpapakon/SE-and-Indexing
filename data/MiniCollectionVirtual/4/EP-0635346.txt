Process for reusing plastic scraps.

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a process for reprocessing plastics scrap which, in the comminuted (granulated, shred) state, is admixed to building materials, in particular for road construction, as a filler. To improve the bonding between the comminuted plastics scrap and the mineral building materials, the plastics scrap is to be mixed with granular mineral materials and at the same time be heated to a temperature in the range of its melting point. The plastics/mineral mixture thus prepared can be further processed with building materials for road construction without any problems.
</ABSTRACT>
