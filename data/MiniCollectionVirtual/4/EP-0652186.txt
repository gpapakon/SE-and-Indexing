<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a heat-resistant material as an asbestos substitute in equipment for handling hot glass, glass-ceramic and ceramic articles, which material comprises fibre-reinforced glass or fibre-reinforced glass-ceramic.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
Use of a fibre-reinforced glass or of a fibre-reinforced 
glass ceramic as temperature resistant material on devices for 

handling hot glass, glass-ceramic and ceramic objects, in 
particular as asbestos replacement.

carbon and/or SiC. 
Use of a fibre-reinforced glass or of a fibre-reinforced 
glass ceramic according to at least one of Claims 1 to 6,  

 
characterized by a fibre content of 10 to 70% by volume. 
Use of a fibre-reinforced glass or of a fibre-reinforced 
glass ceramic according to at least one of Claims 1 to 7 for 

grippers, pushers, sliders, turners, resting plates, sliding 
rails, sliding parts, conveyor-belt segments, transport rollers, 

scrapers, extending bars, deflectors, gripper claw inserts, lehr 
inserters, rests for shear parts, transportation devices, 

positioning gauges, moulds or mould parts, knives, deflection 
rollers or parts thereof, in devices for handling hot glass, 

glass-ceramic or ceramic objects. 
Use of a fibre-reinforced glass or of a fibre-reinforced 
glass ceramic according to at least one of Claims 1 to 7 for the 

deforming or shaping of hot glass objects or glass gathers.
