<INVENTION-TITLE>
Pyroelectrical container.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F16L900	F16L912	F16L1111	F16L11118	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F16L	F16L	F16L	F16L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F16L9	F16L9	F16L11	F16L11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Container (vessel) for fluid or fluidized media, the container wall having at least one active layer (1) made of pyroelectric and/or ferroelectric and/or antiferroelectric and/or magnetoelectric and/or piezoelectric material, electrodes (2, 3) being provided to supply an a.c. voltage to the layer.
