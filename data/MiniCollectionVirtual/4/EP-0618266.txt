<CLAIMS>
Thermoplastic moulding compositions prepared from

A) 10 to 60 parts by weight of polyalkylene
terephthalate,
B) 2 to 45 parts by weight of thermoplastic
polyurethane and
C) 2 to 45 parts by weight of rubber-elastic polymer
with a glass transition temperature (T
g
) of
below 0Â°C, obtainable from


C.1) 10 to 95 parts by weight of monomers
from the group
C.1.1) 50 to 99 parts by weight of styrene and
C.1.2) 1 to 50 parts by weight of
acrylonitrile on
C.2) polybutadiene,

wherein the sum of A+B+C is 100.
Moulding composition according to claim 1 which
additionally contains conventional additives such as

lubricants and mould release agents, processing
stabilisers, fillers and reinforcing agents, dyes and

pigments.
Use of moulding compositions according to claims 1 and
2 for the production of mouldings. 
Mouldings produced from moulding compositions
according to claims 1 and 2.
Insulation material produced from moulding
compositions according to claims 1 and 2.
