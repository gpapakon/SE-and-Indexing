<INVENTION-TITLE>
Closure assembly for connection and break-out of cables, in particular bundles and glass fibres of glass fibre cables.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G02B644	G02B644	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G02B	G02B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G02B6	G02B6	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention involves a closure assembly for connecting, branching and splitting optical cables. This closure assembly has a split sealing body with a plurality of cable bushing adapters, a console for a cassette holder and such a cassette holder. The closure hood with sealing action can be placed on the sealing body, which closure hood is sealed with respect to the sealing body by means of a sealing ring which runs around the sealing body and receives the cassette holder, the console and at least partially the sealing body.
