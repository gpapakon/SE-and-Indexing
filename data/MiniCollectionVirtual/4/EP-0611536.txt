Pad for chair, in particular for lawn chair or the like.

<ABSTRACT>
This cushion comprises at least one component (6a) forming a track fastened to the cover (3), with a slide piece moving in said component and connected to a stuffed component (10). The track component and the slide piece consist of the tracks (6a) and two cursors (6b,6c) respectively of zip fasteners (6), the two cursors (6b,bc) being fastened to each other and arranged in opposite directions.
