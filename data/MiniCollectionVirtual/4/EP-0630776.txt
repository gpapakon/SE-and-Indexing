<INVENTION-TITLE>
Hybrid-drive apparatus for motor vehicle.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60K600	B60K630	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60K	B60K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60K6	B60K6	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Hybrid drive device (apparatus) for a motor vehicle, comprising on the one hand a heat engine (2) driving an alternator (5) capable of powering an electric motor (6) and/or of charging a battery (8) and, on the other hand, an inverting rectifier for starting (12, 12') which starts up the heat engine (2) using the battery (8) via the alternator (5), characterised in that the alternator (5) is used as a motor to transmit energy to an inertia flywheel (14).
