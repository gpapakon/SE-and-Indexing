<INVENTION-TITLE>
Rabbitmeat-based foodstuff and process for producing it.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A23L131	A23L131	A23L1318	A23L1318	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A23L	A23L	A23L	A23L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a food product based on rabbit meat and to a method for preparing this food product. Food product characterised in that it essentially consists of the meat of at least one whole boned rabbit, with the giblets (offal) removed, which has been rolled, steeped in brine and smoked. The invention is more especially applicable to promoting the use of rabbit meat in the form of charcuterie (smoked and cooked meat products).
</ABSTRACT>
