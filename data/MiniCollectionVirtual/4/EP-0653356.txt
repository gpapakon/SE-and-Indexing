<CLAIMS>
Two-compartment container (1) moulded as one-piece unit, 
consisting of two compartments (2) and (3) stacked one upon 

another which are connected by a passage opening (4), closed 
by a stopper (5) to keep the contents of the two compartments 

separate during storage, characterized in that the stopper (5) 
being connected, by 

the upper end (7) of a coupling link (6), to the lower end 
(8) of a pouring spout (9) which is, in one-piece, connected 

to a covering cap (10) sealing the upper compartment (3), by 
an elastic membrane (11) which is turned inside, when the 

container is closed, and turned outside, when the container 
is opened; and the pouring spout (9) is joined with an 

encompassing sealing cap (12) reaching to the collar region 
of the upper compartment (3), by a screw thread (13), whereof 

the sealing cap (12) is fitted at its junction point to the 
upper part of the container (3) with a safeguard against 

rotation (14) to prevent premature removal of the sealing cap 
(12) prior to admixture of the contents of the compartments 

(2) and (3), and consisting of a serration (14 A) on the 
inside of the rim of sealing cap (12) which overlaps the 

covering cap (10), and at least one corresponding cross-bar 
(14 B) attached to the covering cap (10).  

 
Two-compartment container according to claim 1, characterized 
by a reduced diameter, compared with the total diameter of 

the container (1), in a section (18) between the container 
parts (2) and (3), containing the passage opening (4). 
Two-compartment container according to claim 1 or 
2, characterized in that the sealing cap (12) is 

connected with the shoulder of covering cap (10) through 
welding cones or points (19). 
</CLAIMS>
