<INVENTION-TITLE>
Probe for intracorporal fragmentation of stones.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61B1722	A61B1722	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61B	A61B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61B17	A61B17	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In a probe (1) for intracorporal fragmentation of stones (3), in particular of stones present in ureters and bile ducts, fragmentation is carried out by electrohydraulic shock waves (10) generated by spark discharge between poles (8, 9) at the distal end of the probe. The poles (8, 9) are surrounded by a screen (11) intended to prevent radial spread of the shock waves (10).
