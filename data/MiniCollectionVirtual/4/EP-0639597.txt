<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the preparation of flexible, urethane-group containing foams.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08G1800	C08G1810	C08G1870	C08G1883	C08J900	C08J900	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08G	C08G	C08G	C08G	C08J	C08J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C08G18	C08G18	C08G18	C08G18	C08J9	C08J9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Flexible (soft) foams containing urethane groups are produced by reacting   1) modified polyisocyanates which are products of the reaction of polyurethanes with polyisocyanates prepared in the presence of acylating and/or alkylating agents, with 2) compounds having a molecular weight of from 400 to 10,000 which contain at least two hydrogen atoms which are active towards isocyanates, 3) water and/or organic blowing agents, optionally in the presence of   4) compounds having a molecular weight of from 32 to 399 which contain at least two hydrogen atoms which are reactive towards isocyanates, as crosslinking agents, and optionally in the presence of 5) auxiliaries and additives known per se. up
</ABSTRACT>
