<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a piston-connecting rod unit (1) for internal combustion engines. At its head (4), the piston has a connecting-rod bearing (5) to receive a journal (7) connected rigidly to the connecting rod (8). An offset design of the connecting-rod head (9) allows the connecting rod (8) and the piston (1) to be connected to one another easily.
