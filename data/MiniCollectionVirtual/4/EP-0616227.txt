<INVENTION-TITLE>
Digital directional resonance magnetometer.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The magnetometer of the invention comprises a logic unit (50) receiving a resonance detection signal, a digital processor (60) delivering a number N, a digital/analog convertor converting this number into current serving to set up a feedback. The measurement of the field is given directly in digital form by the processor. Application in magnetometry.
