<INVENTION-TITLE>
Noise reduction for speech recognition.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G06F1518	G06F1518	G06N300	G06N300	G10L1500	G10L1502	G10L1508	G10L1516	G10L1520	G10L2100	G10L2102	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
For speech recognition in a noise-laden environment, noise reduction must be carried out. The speech recognition must be robust against noises. Use additional information from suitable signal representations at the input of the neural network for noise reduction.
