<INVENTION-TITLE>
Reclosable folded cardboard package for dry products
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a folded cardboard package including front and rear panels (2) and side panels (5) between the said front and rear panels, and flaps forming the bottom and the top of the said package, and having a recloseable closure (19) of substantially prismatic shape including a hinge line (20) on the top of the said package and pre-cutout lines (21) on the front and rear panels ending on the said hinge line and on one of the side panels (5) having a cutout line (23), the closure including, under the cutout line of the abovementioned side panel and integral with the latter, a pre-cutout gripping tab (24) comprising a locking tab (25) provided in order to engage in a slot formed in a layer of cardboard arranged on the said side panel under the said gripping tab.

<CLAIMS>
Pack made of folded cardboard and comprising front (2) 
and rear (3) panels and side panels (4, 5) between the said 

front and rear panels, and flaps (9, 10, 11, 15) forming 

the bottom and top (6, 7, 8, 14) of the said pack and 
having a reclosable closure (19) of substantially prismatic 

shape which is delimited by a hinge line (20) on the top of 
the said pack, by precutting-out lines (21, 22) on the 

front and rear panels terminating on the said hinge line, 
and by a cutting-out line (23) on one of the side panels, 

characterised in that the closure (19) comprises, under the 
cutting-out line (23) on the aforesaid side panel and 

integral with the latter, a precut-out gripping tab (24) 
comprising a locking tab (25) which is intended to engage 

in a slot (26) formed in a layer of cardboard (12) applied 
against the said side panel (5) under the said gripping 

tongue (24), and in that the aforesaid side panel (5), in 
which the said closure (19) is formed, and the layer of 

cardboard (12) are situated at two opposite ends of the 
blank (1) from which the pack is produced. 
Pack according to claim 1, characterised in that, on the 
precutting-out lines (21, 22) of the front and rear panels, 

the precutting-out segments are longer near the hinge line 
(20) and the lateral panel (5). 
Pack according to one of claims 1 or 2, characterised in 
that the width of the gripping tab (24) corresponds to 

substantially half the width of the side panel (5). 
Pack according to one of claims 1 to 3, characterised in 
that the locking tab (25) has the shape of a semicircle. 
Pack according to one of claims 1 to 4, characterised in 
that the side panel (5) comprises, under the gripping tab  

 
(24), an unhooking arrangement (27) to make it easier to 

get hold of the said tab. 
Pack according to any one of claims 1 to 5, 
characterised in that the hinge line (20) comprises a 

cutout along a semicircular line (28). 
</CLAIMS>
