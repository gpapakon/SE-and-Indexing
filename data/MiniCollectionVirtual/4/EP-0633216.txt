<INVENTION-TITLE>
Spreader lifting frame for a crane for handling containers in ports, train-terminals or similar installations.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B66C110	B66C110	B66C1300	B66C1316	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B66C	B66C	B66C	B66C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B66C1	B66C1	B66C13	B66C13	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Spreader lifting frame (1) for a crane for handling containers in ports, train terminals and similar installations which is suitable for the weighing of containers. Here, the lifting frame (1) has the advantage of being exceptionally light. It is provided with couplings (3) for anchoring to a spreader as well as with an annular structure (4) with transport rings (2), brackets (7) in the abovementioned structure (4), weighing cells (9) on the brackets (7), and T-shaped plates (11), the horizontal arms (13) at each T-shaped plate (11) being supported on corresponding weighing cells (9), and the vertical part (15) of the T-shaped plate (11) being firmly connected to the corresponding coupling (3) for anchoring to the spreader.
