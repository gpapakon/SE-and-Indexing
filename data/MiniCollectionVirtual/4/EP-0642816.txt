<INVENTION-TITLE>
Distillation apparatus, especially a rotary evaporator.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B01D300	B01D308	B01L700	B01L702	B01L900	B01L904	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B01D	B01D	B01L	B01L	B01L	B01L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B01D3	B01D3	B01L7	B01L7	B01L9	B01L9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In a distillation apparatus, in particular a rotary evaporator having a heating bath for an evaporation flask which can be coupled to a rotary motor drive, a cooler, at least one receiving flask for the distillate and with protective devices for the glass components and the heating bath, for safe use and energy savings, the arrangement of a completely enclosing receiving housing (1) for heating bath (4), evaporation flask (3), cooler, receiving flask and the rotary drive (2) is provided and the arrangement of the construction of part-regions of the receiving housing (1) assigned to the heating bath (4), evaporation flask (3), cooler and receiving flask is provided, which part-regions, to achieve operating accesses (5) are arranged on the receiving housing (1) so as to be able to pivot and/or slide and/or push in.
