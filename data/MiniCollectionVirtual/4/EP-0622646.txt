<INVENTION-TITLE>
Opto-electronic integrated circuit.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G02B612	G02B612	G02B6125	G02B6125	G02B630	G02B630	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G02B	G02B	G02B	G02B	G02B	G02B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G02B6	G02B6	G02B6	G02B6	G02B6	G02B6	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Optoelectronic integrated circuit, preferably on a substrate (support) provided with grooves for fixing optical fibres, the waveguide running in the integrated circuit having at least one arc which runs through virtually 180@, so that the ends, provided for light entry or light exit, of the waveguide are guided to the same light entry/exit surface of the integrated circuit.
