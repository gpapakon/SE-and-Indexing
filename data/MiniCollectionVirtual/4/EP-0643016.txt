<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for disinfection and sterilization of water with UV light.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C02F132	C02F132	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C02F	C02F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C02F1	C02F1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device (1) for disinfecting and sterilising water, preferably water which is located in a boiler (20) or container (special), having a lamp (11) which emits ultraviolet light and dips into the water. Arranged inside the device (1) is an optoelectric sensor (14) which is located in the radiation region of the lamp (11) and responds to ultraviolet light, and which is capable of outputting a signal in the event of failure of the lamp (11) to radiate.
