<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a process for the preparation of widely known triazolinones of the formula (I) 
<
IMAGE
>
 by oxidation of the corresponding triazolinethiones to triazolesulphonic acids of the formula (III) 
<
IMAGE
>
 in which M is hydrogen or a metal equivalent, and reaction of these triazolesulphonic acids with water. The invention furthermore relates to the novel intermediates of the formula (III).
</ABSTRACT>
