<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method and apparatus for access restriction to a mobile radio system.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H04W402	H04W402	H04W818	H04W818	H04W4800	H04W4804	H04W7600	H04W7604	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H04W	H04W	H04W	H04W	H04W	H04W	H04W	H04W	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H04W4	H04W4	H04W8	H04W8	H04W48	H04W48	H04W76	H04W76	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
To restrict the access of mobile stations (MS) to a mobile radio system with a plurality of mobile stations (MS), the radio cell exhibiting a plurality of locating areas (LA) and the mobile station (MS) being authenticated on a service request by the mobile station (MS), a current locating identification (LAIa) identifying the respective locating area (LA) is transmitted to the mobile station (MS), where it is stored. A locating identification (LAI) of at least one locating area (LA) allowed for the mobile station (MS) is stored in an identification module (SIM). The current locating information (LAIa) is compared with the locating identification (LAI) stored there and access to the mobile radio system is inhibited when they do not agree.
