<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for lifting and positioning a ceiling panel.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E04F2118	E04F2118	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E04F	E04F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>E04F21	E04F21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The device (1) for lifting and positioning a ceiling panel (43) on the ceiling (45) of a room comprises a vertical guide (9) and a lifting cylinder (27) which lifts the panel-receiving means (25) with the panel (43) located thereon to the ceiling (45) and presses it thereon until a fastening means (47) fixes the panel on the ceiling (45). The device (1) can be displaced to all sides on rollers (5), with the result that the ceiling panel (43) can be positioned without difficulty.
