<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for preparing dough
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A21C100	A21C114	B01F1502	B01F1502	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A21C	A21C	B01F	B01F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A21C1	A21C1	B01F15	B01F15	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The kneading appts., for the prodn. of dough, has a delivery mechanism for the kneaded dough with a rotating plate covering the whole of the base of the kneading vessel and rotating with it. The plate is mounted to a central rod, which raises the plate continuously together with the kneaded dough to the level of the upper edge of the vessel, where there is a guide rail extending radially from the vessel edge to the axis.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
Machine for making dough, consisting of a kneading 
arrangement (1, 2), a dough basin (3) mounted for 

rotation, and a discharging arrangement for the dough 
once it has been fully kneaded, characterised in that 

the discharging arrangement features a plate (4) 
which covers the entire floor of the dough basin and 

rotates with the dough basin, and which is mounted on 
a lifting rod (5) which is disposed axially at the 

centre of the basin (3) and which continuously raises 
the plate (4) and the fully kneaded dough as far as 

a position near the upper rim of the dough basin (3) 
and incorporates a guide rail (6) which extends 

radially from the rim of the dough basin as far as 
the axis thereof. 
Machine according to claim 1, characterised in that 
the lower face of the guide rail (6) is provided with 

a cutting edge. 
Machine according to claims 1 and 2, characterised in 
that the guide rail (6) has a chamfered shape, with 

the concave side facing the dough. 
Machine according to claims 1, 2 and 3, characterised 
in that the guide rail (6) is curved in the 

longitudinal direction. 
</CLAIMS>
