<INVENTION-TITLE>
Wall construction for a booth of a painting installation.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B05B1512	B05B1512	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B05B	B05B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B05B15	B05B15	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Wall construction for a booth of a painting installation, which comprises vertical stays (22, 24) and also panel-shaped wall elements (52, 52'), in which, in order to have to use expensive materials only for these wall elements (52, 52'), the vertical stays (22, 24) form, together with transverse struts (34) fastened thereto and arranged at a distance from one another in the vertical direction, a supporting framework, the stays and transverse struts (34) of which form compartments closed in each case by a panel-shaped wall element (52, 52'), and the framework being covered by the panel-shaped wall elements (52, 52') on its side facing the booth interior, and lastly the wall elements having panels made of a material which is more corrosion-resistant than the material of the framework.
