<INVENTION-TITLE>
Filling device.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B67C302	B67C304	B67D300	B67D300	B67D501	B67D502	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B67C	B67C	B67D	B67D	B67D	B67D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B67C3	B67C3	B67D3	B67D3	B67D5	B67D5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Device for filling liquids, in particular liquid flushing (rinsing) agents, liquid detergents or liquid foodstuffs, from large transport containers (2) into bottles (18), characterised by a suction pump (4) with an intake line (3) projecting into the transport container (2), by a vessel (9) with a measuring chamber (6) with overflow, the measuring chamber being connected to the delivery side of the suction pump (4), by a filling chamber (13) with a filling stub (12) for the liquid which is to be filled into the bottles and a signal generator or signal generators which are actuated when a bottle (18) assumes the filling position in the filling chamber (13), and by a filling line (10) which is provided with a solenoid valve (11) and leads from the measuring chamber (6) to the filling stub (12). The filling device is suitable for the repeated filling of bottles by customers in supermarkets.
