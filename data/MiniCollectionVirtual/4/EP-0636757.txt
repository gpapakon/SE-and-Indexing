<INVENTION-TITLE>
A method for fastening textile skirting boards in rooms.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E04F1904	E04F1904	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E04F	E04F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>E04F19	E04F19	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Method for fastening textile skirting boards in rooms, in the case of which method adhesive bonding is carried out by means of an acrylate dispersion adhesive such that the rearside of the skirting-board strip and/or the corresponding wall surface is coated with the adhesive and the skirting-board strip is fitted on the wall in a manner known per se. Application takes place by means of a gun, using an adhesive-containing cartridge.
</ABSTRACT>
