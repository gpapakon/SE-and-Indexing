<INVENTION-TITLE>
Shock absorbing flooring tile.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E01C500	E01C518	E01C522	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E01C	E01C	E01C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>

<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a shock-absorbing floor covering tile, of the type consisting of granulates of natural or synthetic elastic materials such as rubber, the said granulates being agglomerated by pressure into a mat (1) by means of a binder. The tile according to the invention is essentially characterised in that the upper wall of the mat (1) is covered with a sheet (2) or lining having edges (3) which at least partially encloses the sides (4) of the mat.
