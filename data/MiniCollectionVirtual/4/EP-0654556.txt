Lock device.

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a lock device for a washing automat (1)/washing machine which comprises a washing-machine housing (2) with a loading orifice (3) and with a washing-machine door (4)/window or closing lid which closes the loading orifice (3), a locking catch (6) being arranged preferably on the washing-machine door (4), on the window or on the closing lid, and the device parts serving for controlling and adjusting the locking element or locking elements being located preferably on the washing-machine housing (2). The essence of the invention is that the locking element or locking elements (9, 13) has or have a release position as well as a first locking position or precatching position and a second locking position or operating position.
