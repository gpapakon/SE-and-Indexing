Container for transporting and mixing of any product.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a container for transporting and mixing any products. This container includes at least two compartments (1, 2) closed off by a capsule (7) and capable of opening into a spout (3) which is common to the two compartments (1, 2) and can be closed off by a plug (4), the spout (3) and the plug (4) forming a connection region between the two compartments (1, 2) when the capsule (7) is withdrawn, in order to allow the products to be mixed. This container allows any products such as water and syrups to be transported and mixed before use.
