<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for analysing a light flux given by a coded spectral modulation sensor.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01D526	G01D526	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01D	G01D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01D5	G01D5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Analysis device for a spectral modulation encoding sensor (14) illuminated by an incoherent light source (10), this device comprising a demodulator interferometer (18) with diffraction gratings (32) which are in a first-order Littrow mounting for the central wavelength of the flux emitted by the source (10), the flux leaving the demodulator interferometer (18) being focused by a lens (30) onto a linear array (20) of photodetectors.
