<INVENTION-TITLE>
Apparatus for packaging a group of articles
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65B2100	B65B2106	B65B5900	B65B5900	B65G1900	B65G1922	B65G4728	B65G4728	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The machine has chains with carriers (2,3). At least two carriers engage on each bottle (1). The distance between the carriers, and between carriers and longitudinal center axis of the bottles, are adjustable. The carriers are formed as fingers with threaded spindles for adjustment. The end of each carrier facing the bottles has a turnable roller (4) engaging simultaneously on two following objects.
