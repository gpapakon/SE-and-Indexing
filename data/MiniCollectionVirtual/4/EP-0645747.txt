<INVENTION-TITLE>
Laser writable label.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G09F302	G09F302	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a laser-writable multilayered label, having a first layer (21) and a second layer (22), differing optically from the first layer (21), the first layer (21) being removable by means of laser radiation to correspond to a desired typeface or print image, making the surface of the second layer (22) visible. The laser-writable multilayered label according to the invention is characterised in that between the layers (21, 22) there is arranged a transparent plastic film, forming a backing layer (20), and in that the layers (21, 22) are applied to this backing layer (20) as coatings.
