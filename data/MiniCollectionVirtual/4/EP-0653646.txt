<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to an optical apparatus as a driving aid. According to the invention, a lidar consists of a piece of Erbium-doped glass (5) pumped by a fraction of the light flux (3) generated by a xenon-discharge lamp (1) with a concentrator (2). The assembly is arranged in a lamp or a spotlight whose facilities it uses, and makes it possible to activate a receiver (17) and circuitry generating parameters for assistance with navigation (26).
