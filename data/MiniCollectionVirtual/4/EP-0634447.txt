<INVENTION-TITLE>
Plasticised polymer mouldings from modified polyvinyl butyrals.

<ABSTRACT>
The preparation of plasticized polymer mouldings from modified polyvinylbutyrals (PVBs) containing sulfonate-carrying monomer units in the polymer main chain, by acetalation of sulfonate-containing polyvinyl alcohols or mixtures thereof with sulfonate-free standard polyvinyl alcohols, isolation of modified polyvinylbutyrals in solid, finely divided salt form, and mixing thereof with plasticizers with plastication and thermoplastic shaping, preferably to give flat films. The flat films are used as interlayer films having improved rheological and mechanical properties for production of laminated silicate glass sheets (panes, windscreens, windows).
</ABSTRACT>
