Compositions of the type water-in-oil emulsion containing one or more silicone oil and one ore more cationic polymer

<ABSTRACT>
Water-in-oil emulsions (I), containing water and optionally water-soluble components, lipids and optionally lipophilic components, silicone oil(s), surfactant(s) and cationic polymer(s). Water-in-oil emulsions (I), containing (a) water and optionally water-soluble components and (b) lipids and optionally lipophilic components at an overall content of at least 20 wt.% of (I), additionally contain (c) silicone oil(s) at at least 7.5 (preferably at least 10) wt.% of (I), (d) surfactant(s) selected from alkyl methicone copolyols and/or alkyl dimethicone copolyols and (e) cationic polymer(s).
</ABSTRACT>
