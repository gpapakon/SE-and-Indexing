<INVENTION-TITLE>
Container foldable in one level.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B65D536	B65D536	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B65D	B65D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B65D5	B65D5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A container (1), foldable in one plane, is proposed, which serves, in particular, for use as a spectacle case or the like. For this purpose a housing is provided, which is triangular in cross-section and whose wall sections can be folded onto one another in one plane.
