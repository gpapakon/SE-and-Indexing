<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a dispenser for liquids. Said dispenser consists of at least one cabinet-shaped store module (1) and a cabinet-shaped dispenser module (2) that is joined thereto. A pull-out carriage (3) can be drawn out of the front of the store module (1) and serves for receiving a store tank (8) which can be put in through one of the two open side faces (6). The dispenser module is provided with a dispenser chamber (17) that is accessible from the front. The store module (1) has a square plan with an edge length (a) that equals the depth (t) of the dispenser module (2) having a rectangular plan. The dispenser module (2) can be alternatively joined to each of the three side faces (11, 18, 19) of the store module (1).

<CLAIMS>
Dispensing device for liquids, consisting of at least one cabinet-shaped
storage module (1), and a cabinet-shaped dispensing module (2)

which can be connected thereto and has a dispensing space (17) which is
accessible from the front side, where the storage module (1) has a square

plan contour having an edge length (a) which is equal to the depth (t) of the
dispensing module (2), which is constructed with a rectangular plan contour,


characterised in that
 the storage module has a pull-out carriage (3)
intended for the accommodation of an exchangeable storage container (8),


in that
 the pull-out carriage (3) of the storage module (1) has a base trough
(4) fitted with a roller bed (7), the roller rotation axes of which are arranged

in the pull-out direction, 
in that
 the base trough (4) is connected to a front
plate (5) and two side surfaces (6), and 
in that
 at least one of the two side
surfaces (6) of the pull-out carriage (3) is open.
Dispensing device according to Claim 1, 
characterised in that
 both
side surfaces (6) of the pull-out carriage (3) are open.
Dispensing device according to Claim 1, 
characterised in that
 the
pull-out carriage (3) is provided with base running rollers (10) on its underside.
Dispensing device according to Claim 1, 
characterised in that
 a
window (13) is cut out of each of the facing side walls (11, 18 and 19; 12) of

the storage module (1) and of the dispensing module (2).
Dispensing device according to Claim 4, 
characterised in that
 a
window (13) is provided in each of all three side walls (11,18,19) of each

storage module (1) and in both side surfaces of the dispensing module (2).
Dispensing device according to Claim 1, 
characterised in that
 the
storage module (1) and the dispensing module (2) have the same height. 
Dispensing device according to Claim 1, 
characterised in that
 a dispensing
module (2) is connected together with two storage modules (1) in

each case.
Dispensing device according to Claim 1, 
characterised in that
 a
plurality of dispensing installations for different liquids are provided in the

dispensing module (1).
