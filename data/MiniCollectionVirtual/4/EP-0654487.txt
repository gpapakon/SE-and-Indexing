<ABSTRACT>
A highly effective release agent for thermally transferrable inscription and decoration systems contains a copolymer as principal constituent. The in particular soap-free copolymer to be used in accordance with the invention exhibits not only excellent colour stability during heating, but also exhibits a release action corresponding to the values of soap-containing montan wax.
</ABSTRACT>
