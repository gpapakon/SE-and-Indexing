<INVENTION-TITLE>
Pressure regulator
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G05D1616	G05D1600	G05D1606	G05D1604	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G05D	G05D	G05D	G05D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G05D16	G05D16	G05D16	G05D16	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Pressure regulator for a gas-heated heating appliance having a blower which is connected via throttles to an air inlet and a gas inlet, and whose pressure side is connected to a burner, the discharge side of an air diaphragm (20) being connected to a space (43) of the pressure regulator (29) bounded by a control diaphragm (10) and a servodiaphragm (11), and a space (45) which is bounded by the control diaphragm (10) being subjected to pressure in the underpressure chamber, and the servodiaphragm (11) controlling a valve (12), which influences the flow to a space (41) bounded by a regulating diaphragm (8), which controls a gas regulating valve (5). In order to ensure a constant regulator pressure pR, provision is made for a holding part (15) having an internal thread (49) to be pressed into one of the two diaphragms (10, 11) bounding a space (43), a screw (14), which determines the spacing of the two diaphragms (10, 11), being held adjustably in the internal thread (49).
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
Pressure controller for a gas-fired heater having a blower (21) connected
via throttles with an air inlet and a gas inlet, the pressure side of the

said blower being connected with a bumer, with the efflux side of an
throttling point (20) in the air stream being connected with a space (43)

of the pressure controller (29), such space being delimited by a control
diaphragm (10) and a servo diaphragm (11), and a space (45) delimited

by the control diaphragm (10) is charged with the pressure in the low-pressure
chamber (23) and/or ahead of the throttling point 20 on the

suction side of the blower (21), and the servo diaphragm (11) controls a
valve (12) which influences the afflux to a space (41) delimited by a

control diaphragm (8) controlling a gas control valve (5), 
characterized
in that
 one of the diaphragms delimiting a space 43, namely the control
diaphragm (10) and servo diaphragm (11), comprises a pressed-in retaining

member (15) with an intemal thread (49) holding an adjustable
screw (14) which determines the distance between the two diaphragms

(10, 11).
</CLAIMS>
