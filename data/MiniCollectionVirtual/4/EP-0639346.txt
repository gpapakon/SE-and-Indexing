<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method and apparatus for examining the eye.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Observation or imaging of the endothelium (E) of the cornea (9) by means of a pencil of light (10) produces a powerful front reflex (Ifront) and a much weaker reflex (Iendothelium) on the corneal endothelium (E). On observation and recording of an image of the endothelium there is great interference from the very close front reflex. In order to eliminate this disadvantage there is provided an adjustable diaphragm (7) which can be inserted on one side into the ray path of the imaging optics in order to blank out the front reflex (Ifront).
