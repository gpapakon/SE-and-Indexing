<INVENTION-TITLE>
Blend of water-in-oil emulsifiers and its use in cosmetic and pharmaceutical preparations
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Water-oil emulsifier comprises a fatty alcohol mixt. (A), comprising 10-28C fatty alcohols in the following amounts in the composition (wt.%); 10C (up to 1.0); 12C (1.0-3.0); 14C (12.0-16.0); 16C (16.0-21.0); 18C ( 11.0-14.5); 20C (6.0-10.0); 22C (7.0-11.0); 24C (2.5-5.0); 26C (0.5-2.5); and 28C (0.5-1.1); a mixt. (B) of 20-24C 2-alkyl-alkanols (5-23); and phytosterols (C) (6-13).
</ABSTRACT>
