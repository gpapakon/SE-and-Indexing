<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the separation of propene-propane mixtures.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C07C700	C07C711	C07C900	C07C908	C07C1100	C07C1106	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C07C	C07C	C07C	C07C	C07C	C07C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C07C7	C07C7	C07C9	C07C9	C07C11	C07C11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
To separate propylene-propane mixtures, the unsaturated hydrocarbon is absorbed using water. The absorption can be assisted by addition of phase-transfer reagents to the water.
</ABSTRACT>
