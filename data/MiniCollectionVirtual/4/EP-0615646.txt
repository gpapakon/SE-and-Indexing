<CLAIMS>
Device for a lifelike representation of speaking persons with a lifesized 
doll (20) which is a replica of the person and with a projection 

device (26) for sound and light, projecting a movie of the moving face 
of said person from the outside onto at least particle areas of the face, 

at least lips and eyes, of the doll (20) and reproducing audio signals at 
the same time and synchronously thereto, which audio signals are from 

a recording of the person, characterized in that the projection device 
(26) for sound and light is arranged in the foot of the doll (20) or 

under the doll (20) and in that the projection is performed from outside 
onto the face via a mirror, which is arranged near the doll (20), 

preferably which is held in the doll's hand. 
Device according to claim 1, characterized in that a loudspeaker (28) for 
reproducing the audio signals is arranged in the doll. 
Device according to claim 1, characterized in that the face of the doll 
(20) is a least transparent, preferably milky-transparent, in at least 

some areas. 
Device according to claim 1, characterized in that the projection from 
the outside is done from below in an acute angle onto the face of the 

doll (20) and in that distortions during take and/or reproduction are 
compensated for by a supplementary optic. 
Device according to claim 1, characterized in that the doll (20) has a 
slightly opened mouth and regularly positioned eyes. 
Device according to claim 1, characterized in that the projection device 
(26) for light is arranged in
 a base of the doll (20). 
</CLAIMS>
