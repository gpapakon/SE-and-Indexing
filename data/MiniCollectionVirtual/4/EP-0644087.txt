<INVENTION-TITLE>
Air bag crash protecting device.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60R2116	B60R2116	B60R2123	B60R21231	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60R	B60R	B60R	B60R	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60R21	B60R21	B60R21	B60R21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
An air-bag collision protecting device for motor vehicles is installed on the front-seat passenger side behind an outlet aperture (3), which is arranged in the dashboard (2), for the air bag (4). The limiting surface (7), facing the passenger sitting in the front passenger seat, of the air bag has, when the air bag is inflated, an approximately trapezoidal shape with a limiting surface which tapers in an upward direction.
