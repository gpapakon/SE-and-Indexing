<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for forming a parallel pipe-shaped cavity for receiving a component in a support plate.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H01L2102	H01L21308	H01L2148	H01L2312	H01L2313	H01L2314	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H01L	H01L	H01L	H01L	H01L	H01L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H01L21	H01L21	H01L21	H01L23	H01L23	H01L23	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In the case of a method for producing a parallelepipedal indentation for holding a component in a support plate, a mask having at least one rectangular window (3, 3') is arranged on a silicon wafer (1) whose surface lies in the (100) plane, the sides of the window (3, 3') being aligned parallel to 
<
100
>
 directions and the side lengths being a predetermined value smaller than the intended edge lengths of the indentation (2, 2'). Anisotropic etching is carried out until the edges of the indentation (2, 2') reach the predetermined length.
