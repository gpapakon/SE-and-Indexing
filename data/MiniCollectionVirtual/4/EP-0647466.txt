Cellulosic membranes.

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a process for producing cellulosic membranes in the form of flat films, tubular films or hollow fibres having improved biocompatibility by using cotton fibres, in particular linters, which are treated in a single-stage process with gaseous molecular oxygen in an alkaline bath; furthermore the invention relates to cotton fibres treated in this manner in particular linters, membranes produced therewith and use thereof for haemodialysis.
</ABSTRACT>
