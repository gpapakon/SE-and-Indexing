Process and device for sludge disposal.

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a process and an apparatus for disposing of sewage sludge by mixing a partially dewatered, stabilised sewage sludge with a mineral raw material. In order to achieve treatment of the sewage sludge to give a stable product which can be reused as required, utilising the potential of the valuable material in the sewage sludge and with simultaneous decrease of its hazard potential, it is provided that the sewage sludge and granular raw material containing clay minerals are mixed and kneaded together in a volume ratio of up to 4 : 1 until the raw material mixture is converted into a malleably deformable state, the malleable raw material mixture is deaerated under vacuum and shaped to form mouldings.
