<INVENTION-TITLE>
Multimeter with automatic setting of the measuring function.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01R1500	G01R1508	G01R1509	G01R1512	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01R	G01R	G01R	G01R	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01R15	G01R15	G01R15	G01R15	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In the case of a multimeter with automatic setting of the measuring function and automatic recognition of the measuring range, the electrical quantity to be measured is recognised at high input resistance. When the quantities to be measured are recognised as impedances, a low input resistance is automatically switched to, the quantity to be measured is measured at low resistance and subsequently displayed, and, when the measuring range changes, the start of the measuring procedure is again returned to.
