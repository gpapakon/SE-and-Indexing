<INVENTION-TITLE>
Motor, in particular electric motor, with an adjustable axial play between the rotor and the stator.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H02K5167	H02K5167	H02K1500	H02K1516	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H02K	H02K	H02K	H02K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H02K5	H02K5	H02K15	H02K15	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The axial play is adjusted by means of a spacer sleeve (4) which is held in a pre-installed manner on a collar (3.1) of an insulating end disc (3); in order to adjust individual axial play, the spacer sleeve (4) is displaced axially relative to the collar (3.1) from its pre-installed position by means of a screw-threaded guide (3.14), and is fixed in its end position by means of a latching lock or by self-locking.
