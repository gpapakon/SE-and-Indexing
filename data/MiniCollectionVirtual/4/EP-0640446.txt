<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a box (1) used as a so-called tool cassette. The essential feature is that the tool holder (5, 6) is fitted in one of the housing parts (3, 4) by means of a snap-in-connection (7), thus enabling easy assembly of the tool holders (5, 6). In addition, a rotary guide consisting of a sliding block (40) and a guide slot (41) concentric with the axis of rotation is provided.
