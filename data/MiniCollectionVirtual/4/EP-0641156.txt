<INVENTION-TITLE>
Holder for plate-like objects, in particular circuit boards, and unloading/loading device for this holder.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H05K1300	H05K1300	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H05K	H05K	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H05K13	H05K13	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In the case of a holder for plate-like objects, in particular printed circuit boards and shaped etched parts, accommodation is provided for at least one plate-like object, and the plate-like objects are arranged suspended one behind the other or alongside one another in the holder. In this case, the plate-like objects are preferably fixed with a gap between one another. An unloading and/or loading device for plate-like objects has such a holder. In this case, this holder is preferably arranged above the transportation or handling device from which the plate-like objects are removed or into which they are fitted.
