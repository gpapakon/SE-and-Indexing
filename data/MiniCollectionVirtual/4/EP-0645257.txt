<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a connection element (4) for a printer housing (1) whose housing parts (2, 3) are connected to each other by at least one connection element (4) in which a receiving device in the form of a T-shaped groove is integrated. Additional units (8) can be releasably coupled to the receiving device either directly or via an anchoring plate (5).

<CLAIMS>
A printing apparatus, in particular a dot matrix, ink-jet, laser or 
thermal transfer printer, with a printer housing (1) and at least one connecting 

means (4) provided on the printer housing (1) and including a receiving 
device (15) for receiving an anchoring means (5) of an accessory device or 

an attachment (8), 
characterized in that
 the printer housing (1) is composed 
of at least two housing shells (2, 3) which are connected with each 

other by the connecting means (4) such as to provide a slot (1') through 
which the receiving device (15) is accessible. 
The printing apparatus as claimed in claim 1, 
characterized in 
that
 the receiving device (15) is accessible from outside. 
The printing apparatus as claimed in any one of the preceding 
claims, 
characterized in that
 the connecting means (4) is integrally formed 
with the receiving device (15). 
The printing apparatus as claimed in any one of the preceding 
claims, 
characterized in that
 the receiving device (15) is configured as a T-slot. 
The printing apparatus as claimed in any one of the preceding 
claims, 
characterized in that
 at least one anchoring means (5) is adapted to 
be attached to or within the receiving device (15) of the connecting means 

(4).  
 
The printing apparatus as claimed in any one of the preceding 
claims, 
characterized in that
 the anchoring means (5) is adapted to be releasably 
attached to or within the connecting means (4), in particular by 

clamping, threading, suspending, snap-fitting, etc. 
The printing apparatus as claimed in any one of the preceding 
claims, 
characterized in that
 the anchoring means (5) is an anchor plate, 
preferably a flat steel anchor plate. 
The printing apparatus as claimed in any one of the preceding 
claims, 
characterized in that
 at least one attachment (8) is adapted to be 
coupled to the anchoring means (5). 
The printing apparatus as claimed in any one of the preceding 
claims, 
characterized in that
 the attachment (8) is releasably attachable to 
the anchoring means (5). 
The printing apparatus as claimed in any one of the preceding 
claims, 
characterized in that
 the attachment (8) is releasably attached to 
the receiving device (15), in particular by threading, rotating, suspending, 

snap-fitting. 
</CLAIMS>
