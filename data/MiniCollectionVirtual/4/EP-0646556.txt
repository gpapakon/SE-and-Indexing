<INVENTION-TITLE>
Match head formulations, matches coated with such a formulation and sticking surface suitable therefor.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C06B2900	C06B2902	C06F300	C06F300	C06F308	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C06B	C06B	C06F	C06F	C06F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C06B29	C06B29	C06F3	C06F3	C06F3	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Safety or nonsafety matchhead composition including an oxidising agent, a fuel, one or more cylinders, characterised in that the oxidising agent is a mixture of an oxidiser of perchlorate type in small quantity and of micronised pyrolusite in high proportions as substitution for a number of other products conventionally from a part of matchhead compositions. Matches coated with such a composition.
</ABSTRACT>
