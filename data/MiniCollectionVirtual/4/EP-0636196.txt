<INVENTION-TITLE>
FRONT CLIP FOR A SAFETY SKI BINDING
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A63C9085	A63C908	A63C908	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A63C	A63C	A63C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A63C9	A63C9	A63C9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A front clip for a safety binding with a housing (2, 3) which can be secured to a ski and has a base (3) and a top (2) and at least one adjustable release spring (4). In the housing top (2) there are two substantially horizontally pivotable sole-clamps (14a, 14b) fitted on a shared, substantially vertical bolt (13) on which bears the release spring (4) and which is arranged in the housing (2, 3) along a longitudinal and rearwardly widened guide surface (130).
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
A front clamp assembly for a safety binding, comprising 
a housing (2,3) adapted to be secured to a ski and having a 

lower housing part (3) and an upper housing part (2) and at 
least one adjustable release spring (4), said upper housing 

part (2) accommodating two substantially horizontally pivotable 
sole clamp members (14a,14b) mounted on a substantially 

vertical common bolt (13) subjected to the action of said 
release spring (4) and guided in said housing (2,3), characterized 

in that said bolt (13) is disposed for being 
guided along a longitudinally directed and rearwards enlarged 

guide face (130) formed in a central guide plate (30) 
disposed in the upper housing part (2) and associated to the 

lower end section (113) of said bolt (13). 
A front clamp assembly according to claim 1, characterized 
in that said guide face (130) is formed in a longitudinal 

slot (131) provided at its front end with a rounded 
end face (132), and at its rear end, with a conical widening 

(133). 
A front clamp assembly according to any of claims 1 or 
2, characterized in that in the skiing position of the front 

clamp assembly (1), said bolt (13) is guided on a first and//or 
a second guide face section (130a,130b), and in the outwards 

swivelled condition of at least one of said sole clamp 
members (14a,b), on said second (130b) or on a third guide 

face section (130c) of said guide face (130), said second 
guide face section (130b) being formed on a straight section 

of said longitudinal slot (131), and said third guide face 
section (130c), on the boundary walls of said conical widening 

(113). 
</CLAIMS>
