<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for the preparation of isobornyle (meth)acrylate.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B01J2714	B01J2719	C07B6100	C07B6100	C07C6700	C07C6704	C07C6900	C07C6954	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a process for the preparation of isobornyl acrylate or isobornyl methacrylate by reacting camphene with acrylic acid or methacrylic acid in the presence of phosphomolybdic heteropolyacid as catalyst.
</ABSTRACT>
