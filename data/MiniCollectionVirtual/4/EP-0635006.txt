<CLAIMS>
Fluorescent lanthanide metal chelates selected from
compounds of the following formulas I through X:


Fluorescent chelates of claim 1 for europium and
terbium.
Fluorescent chelates of claim 2 for europium
selected from compounds of formulae I, II, III, IV, V,

VI, VII, VIII, IX and X.
Fluorescent chelates of claim 2 for terbium selected
from compounds of formulae II, III, IV, VII and X.
In a time resolved fluorescent based assay,
enzymatically treating esters of chelates of formulae I

through X of claim 1 to render the treated chelates as
either fluorescent or non-fluorescent when combined with

a lanthanide metal.
In the assay of claim 5, said esters of chelates are
selected from phosphate and galactoside esters.
In the assay of claim 6, the esters are phosphate
esters.
In the assay of claim 7, the chelate is 4-hydroxy-7-trifluoromethyl-3-quinoline
carboxylic acid (HTQC).
In the assay of claim 5, the resultant compound
being 4-methylumbelliferone which exhibits minimal

fluorescence and its ester which forms a fluorescent
chelate with a lanthanide metal is 4-methylumbelliferyl

phosphate.
</CLAIMS>
