<INVENTION-TITLE>
Modular total knee prosthesis
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61F238	A61F238	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61F	A61F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61F2	A61F2	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The knee prosthesis, comprising a tibial base (2), a femoral condyle component and an intermediate insert (4) has the under-surface of the insert plate (8) and the upper surface (9) of the tibial base made with kidney-shaped cavities (20, 22) of the same width but of different lengths. The cavities are designed to receive a kidney-shaped wedge (27) with a central hole (30) fitting over a projecting stud (24) in the tibial base cavity.
</ABSTRACT>
