<INVENTION-TITLE>
Element of modular wall for a container cover-barrier and in particular for a nuclear reactor vessel plug.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G21C1300	G21C13036	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G21C	G21C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G21C13	G21C13	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The wall element (35a, 35c, 35d) of the enclosure includes a means (46a, 46c, 49d) for sampling or injecting fluid from or into a chamber (44) defined by the enclosure and the outer surface of a container (32) containing a fluid at high temperature. The sampling means (46a, 46c, 46b) includes openings (47a, 47c, 49d) on the internal surface of the wall element and at least one sampling or injection pipe (48, 50) which emerges on the outer surface of the wall element. The wall element may be a thermally insulated modular panel for covering the vessel head (32) of a pressurised water nuclear reactor.
