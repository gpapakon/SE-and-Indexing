<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for intermittent feed of workpieces
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B21D4305	B21D4305	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B21D	B21D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B21D43	B21D43	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A device (1) for the intermittent feed of workpieces, for example in a press (2) or similar production machine or in other processing operations to be carried out in steps, has two feed bars (3) which can be moved backwards and forwards in the feed direction, have gripping parts (3a) or gripping regions for gripping the workpieces (100), to grip the workpieces can be moved towards one another and, to perform an advance in this gripping position, can be moved jointly in the feed direction and then release the workpieces by moving apart and can be displaced back into the starting position by means of a return movement. Engaging on each feed bar (3) are the pivotable ends (5) or end regions of at least two levers (4), which can be pivoted in the plane of motion of the feed bar (3) or a plane parallel thereto, the levers (4) of one feed bar (3) being pivotable in the opposite direction to those of the other feed bar (3), and these levers (4) are each connected up by means of a longitudinal guide (7). It is particularly expedient if each of the pivoting levers (4) engaging on one particular feed bar (3) are connected to one another mechanically, in particular by means of a tie bar (8) and so as to be rotatable relative to the said tie bar and can thus be moved synchronously. This provides an effective drive, particularly for the gripping movement, which can be kept free from backlash in a simple manner and takes up little space.

<CLAIMS>
A device (1) for the intermittent feed of workpieces, 
the device including two gripper bars (3) which are 

reciprocable in the direction of feed and have 
gripper elements or gripper zones for gripping the 

workpieces, wherein said gripper bars are 
intermittently movable toward each other for gripping 

the workpieces and for advancement are jointly 
movable in the direction of feed and then are movable 

back to the initial position by a diverging movement 
and return movement, wherein each gripper bar (3) is 

acted on by the pivotable ends (5) or end zones of at 
least two levers (4) pivotable parallel to each other 

in the plane of movement of the gripper bar (3) or in 
a plane parallel thereto, said levers (4) of the one 

gripper bar (3) being pivotable in an opposite 
direction to those of the other gripper bar (3) and 

being coupled by way of a longitudinal guide (7) of 
the gripper bars (3), 
characterized in that
 each 
gripper bar (3) is guided in the direction of its 

longitudinal extent on a drawbar and/or push bar (8) 
by which the levers (4) acting indirectly on the 

respective gripper bar (3) are mechanically 
interconnected and movable in synchronism. 
A device as claimed in claim 1, characterized in that 
the stationary pivot bearings (9) of the pivotable
 
levers (4) are arranged on the sides facing away from 

each other of the gripper bars (3) or in the space 
between the gripper bars (3). 
A device as claimed in claim 2, characterized in that

bars (3).
