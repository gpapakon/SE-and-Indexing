<INVENTION-TITLE>
Kitchen appliance to be used as a cutting board.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a kitchen appliance that serves as a cutting board and as a receptacle for the food which has been cut up.   The cutting board (2) and the food receptacle (3) are secured reversibly together by fastening means, and the cutting board (2) is positioned in the centre of the food receptacle (3), which itself is formed by a bag (5) of a sufficiently flexible material serving as a bag in which to place the cut food when closed, and as a work surface when open. Application to the cutting of bread and meats.
