<INVENTION-TITLE>
Combustion air cleaning device for internal combustion engine.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F01N500	F01N504	F01N708	F01N708	F02M3502	F02M3508	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F01N	F01N	F01N	F01N	F02M	F02M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F01N5	F01N5	F01N7	F01N7	F02M35	F02M35	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Cleaning device (2) making it possible, in an exhaust pipe (1) of an internal-combustion engine, to remove particles of dust contained in the combustion air (oxidiser) of this engine, characterised in that it comprises, in the direction of flow of the particles of dust, a tube (3) penetrating into the exhaust pipe (1), an elbow (4), a divergent nozzle (5) and a cylindrical part (6) in which a non-return valve (8) is located.
