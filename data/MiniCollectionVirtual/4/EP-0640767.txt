<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the provision of a small centrifugal pump which, given identical dimensions and identical rotational speed of the pump rotor, produces a higher pumping capacity (pumpage) than known small centrifugal pumps. In the novel centrifugal pump there is provided, downstream of the pump rotor (3), a duct (5) into which the particles of the liquid are forced. In the process they are repeatedly captured and accelerated by the blades (9) of the pump rotor, the particles of the liquid moving in the duct along a helical (screw-like) trajectory.
