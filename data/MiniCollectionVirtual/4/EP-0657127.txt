Mattress.

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a mattress with a mattress body (1) composed of foam, which has zones (A to G) of varying hardness. To create a simpler and thus less expensive mattress, it is proposed according to the invention that the zones (A to G) of varying hardness are formed by shaped bodies (2a, 2b, 2c, 2d) formed into the mattress body (1).
