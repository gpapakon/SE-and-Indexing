<INVENTION-TITLE>
IMPLANT FOR PARTIAL OR TOTAL REPLACEMENT OF PREFERABLY CARTILAGENOUS OR OSSEOUS ORGANS.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
An implant is described for partial or total replacement of preferably cartilaginous or osseous organs such as a joint, vertebrae, bone or bone portion, etc. said implant is designed to absorb shock forces and reduce compression of the organ treated and/or of adjacent organs. Said implant (3) comprises at least one portion (8) made of an elastically deformable material, and comprises a cavity (7). Application to bone implants.
</ABSTRACT>
