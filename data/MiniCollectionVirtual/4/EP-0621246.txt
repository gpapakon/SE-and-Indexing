<INVENTION-TITLE>
Glass articles containing lead, especially containers of crystal glass coated with a barrier layer of amorphous glass and process for fabricating such articles.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C03C1702	C03C1702	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C03C	C03C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C03C17	C03C17	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Glass articles containing lead, especially a container made of lead crystal. The surface intended to come into contact with an external body is coated with a thin layer of binary glass of amorphous structure formed by a mixture of metal oxide and of an oxide of an associated glass-forming element, the said layer being resistant to chemical agents while forming a barrier to lead in normal use conditions. Process for producing such articles.
</ABSTRACT>
