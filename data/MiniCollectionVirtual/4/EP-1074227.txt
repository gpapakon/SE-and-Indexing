<INVENTION-TITLE>
Stent for bifurcation
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61F206	A61F206	A61F282	A61F284	A61F290	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61F	A61F	A61F	A61F	A61F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61F2	A61F2	A61F2	A61F2	A61F2	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The stent (1) has a tubular cladding (2) and at least one branched section (5) to free a passage in the vessel (3) branch (3.1). The branched section has at least two support elements (6,7), which fold radially out from the cladding in different directions to support the vessel branch, in the style of a saloon door. Alternatively three or folding support elements may be provided, each folding in a different direction. An Independent claim is included for a method for implanting the stent.
</ABSTRACT>
