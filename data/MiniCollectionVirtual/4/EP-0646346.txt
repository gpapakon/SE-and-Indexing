<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for measuring respiratory gas parameters.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61B508	A61B5087	G01F166	G01F166	G01N2902	G01N2902	G01N33483	G01N33497	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device for measuring respiratory gas parameters, with a breathing tube, ultrasound sensors and pre-amplifier electronics located in a separate housing. In order to avoid dangerous cross-infections between patients on multiple use of the measuring device, the ultrasound sensors are firmly integrated in the breathing tube, so that they form an integral part with the latter. At least the breathing tube with the integrated ultrasound sensors is designed as an exchangeable part for single use.
</ABSTRACT>
