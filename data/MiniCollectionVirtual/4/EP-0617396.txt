<ABSTRACT>
During the master-copy production (1) by means of templates using various taking geometries (3,4,5,6), the subjects of the templates (2) become visible in the desired observation region (7) at great distances (7) in the desired spectral colours, when the focal point (6) of the optical reference system (9) is in the viewing point (7) of the observer and the focal point (5) of the optical master system (10) is in the central luminous point of the white-light lamp.
