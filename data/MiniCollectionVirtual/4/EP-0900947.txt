<INVENTION-TITLE>
Actuating device for machine elements subject to wear, particularly for brakes of rail vehicles
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B61H1500	B61H1500	F16D6514	F16D6532	F16D6538	F16D6556	F16D6600	F16D6600	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B61H	B61H	F16D	F16D	F16D	F16D	F16D	F16D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B61H15	B61H15	F16D65	F16D65	F16D65	F16D65	F16D66	F16D66	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
>
The piston connecting rod (5) is hollow and in it the spindle (14) is rotatably arranged, the head area (16) of which is connected rotatably with the brake. A spring (23) on the piston (2) and supporting on the spindle acts against the securement against rotation (19) of the spindle. A path pick-up is provided which assesses the movement between the spindle and the piston and a signal from it is passed by a cable (22) to a control unit which is connected control-wise with the operation of the device securing against rotation (19).
</ABSTRACT>
