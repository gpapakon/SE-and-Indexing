<INVENTION-TITLE>
Agent for the production of yeast-leavened baked goods.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A21D200	A21D216	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A21D	A21D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A21D2	A21D2	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Agent for the production of yeast-leavened baked goods, which contains   a) stearoyl lactyl lactates in the form of their sodium, potassium or calcium salts, b) polyglycerol mono-fatty acid esters of fatty acids having 8 to 22 carbon atoms in a ratio of a:b equals 1:9 to 9:1, and   c) if appropriate, additionally glycerol fatty esters.   The agents can be prepared in a technically simple fashion and are highly effective.
</ABSTRACT>
