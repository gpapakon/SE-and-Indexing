<INVENTION-TITLE>
Water-in-oil emulsions containing polyols and their use in cosmetology.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an emulsion containing an aqueous phase dispersed in an oily phase using a mixed ester of isostearic acid and of succinic acid with glycerol and at least 10% by weight of polyol with respect to the total weight of the emulsion. This emulsion is intended in particular for the cosmetic and/or dermatological treatment of the hands.
</ABSTRACT>
