<ABSTRACT>
Manufacturing process for a machine support (1) with a spring element (2) made of rubber, in which the spring element (2) is fixed on a ring element (3) made of an elastically deformable material. The ring element (3) is produced from a strip-shaped portion of a metal sheet (13) and, after being converted to the form of a ring (14), is converted to the shape of the ring element (3).
