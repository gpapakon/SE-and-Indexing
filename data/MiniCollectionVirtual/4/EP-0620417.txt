<INVENTION-TITLE>
Encapsulated length measuring device.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G01D526	G01D5347	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G01D	G01D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G01D5	G01D5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In this device, two sealing means are provided, namely sealing lips (5, 6) which are arranged in the form of a roof in a hollow body (1) and which are additionally covered by a flexible band (8) which rests externally against the hollow body (1) and is firmly held against it.
