<INVENTION-TITLE>
Cup for hip prosthesis.
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
In this cup, the retention ring (4) comprises a cylindrical bore (21), the diameter (d) of which is less than that of the femoral head, in order to provide longitudinal retention of the latter, and a plurality of rounded studs (22), projecting radially and having maximum radius RB, whilst the skirt (11), integral with the core (3) or with the cup (2), comprises a region (9) with contact bearing surfaces for the studs (22), which surfaces are designed to exert a radial compression on the corresponding stud, locally deforming the ring and ensuring a higher degree of retention.
