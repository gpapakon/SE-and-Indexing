Process for the preparation of benzoyl amino anthraquinone compounds
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Benzoylaminoanthraquinone compounds (I) are prepared by reacting anthraquinone compounds which have at least one primary amino group (II) with benzoyl halides (III) in the presence of an organic solvent, using an alkyl benzoate as the organic solvent.
</ABSTRACT>
