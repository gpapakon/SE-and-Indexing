<INVENTION-TITLE>
Catalysts eliminating nitrogen oxides in an oxidizing environment.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B01D5394	B01D5394	B01J2900	B01J29072	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B01D	B01D	B01J	B01J	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B01D53	B01D53	B01J29	B01J29	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
New catalysts based on copper supported by a zeolite system with an Si/Al atomic ratio 
>
 100. These catalysts are advantageously employed for the selective reduction of nitrogen oxides using hydrocarbons in an oxidising medium.
</ABSTRACT>
