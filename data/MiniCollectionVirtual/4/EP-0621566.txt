<INVENTION-TITLE>
Entrance checking system for sporting activity fields.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G06K1904	G06K1904	G07C900	G07C900	G07C902	G07C902	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G06K	G06K	G07C	G07C	G07C	G07C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G06K19	G06K19	G07C9	G07C9	G07C9	G07C9	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
An access-checking system for skiing areas, sports installations or the like comprises data carriers (10, 10') assigned to equipment parts (11, 12, 13) of the users and checking stations (1) monitoring the said data carriers contactlessly. Each data carrier (10) is provided on or in equipment, for example ski (11) or ski stick (12), necessary for performing the sport, and the checking station (1) has an antenna (5) assigned to the ground (2).
