<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for making gray cast iron for the surface-remelting treatment.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C21C100	C21C108	C21D500	C21D500	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C21C	C21C	C21D	C21D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C21C1	C21C1	C21D5	C21D5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Process for making grey iron for the production of articles intended to receive a surface remelting treatment. The process consists chiefly in introducing, during the filling of the casting ladle, an inoculant comprising elements of the rare-earth type, preferably cerium, in the form of a ferrosilicon filled with cerium.
