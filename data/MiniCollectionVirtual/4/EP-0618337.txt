<INVENTION-TITLE>
Cylinder lock, key for such a lock and a key therefor.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E05B1500	E05B1506	E05B3500	E05B3504	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E05B	E05B	E05B	E05B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>E05B15	E05B15	E05B35	E05B35	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The lock (1) includes:     - a body (4) exhibiting a longitudinal opening (5); - means for fastening the body (4) onto the edges (6) of an opening in a manner such that it cannot be removed from the outside; - a cylinder (barrel) (9) designed to receive a key in a corresponding hole (11) and to turn with the latter inside the body (4), the cylinder (9) passing through the said opening.   It also includes a fixed annular sleeve (19) inside the body (4) and exhibiting an internal tapping (20) designed to receive radial wards of a key when the said key is turned in the lock (1), the stem of the said key itself turning inside the opening in the said sleeve (19).
