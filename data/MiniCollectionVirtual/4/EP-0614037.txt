<INVENTION-TITLE>
Ceiling mount.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>F16M1102	F16M1106	F16M1108	F16M1302	F16M1302	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>F16M	F16M	F16M	F16M	F16M	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>F16M11	F16M11	F16M11	F16M13	F16M13	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A ceiling mount (1) having a ceiling securing means (2) and at least one mount arm (3) in each case exhibits, for pivotable connection, rotary articulations (7) with first and second bearing parts (8, 9). In order that the angular pivoting range can be adjusted in a simple manner, one of the bearing parts (8) exhibits an adjustable stop (12) in its circumference, and the other bearing part (9) exhibits a fixed stop (21) which comes into engagement therewith when the mount arm is pivoted.
