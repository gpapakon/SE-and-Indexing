Colour paste for the manufacture of inwardly printed laminated glass sheets.

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a pigment paste for the production of internally printed, curved laminated glass (safety glass) sheets. Existing pigment pastes cause problems when internally printed laminated glass sheets are fired, with respect to adhesion and absence of optical errors, or require a high degree of technical complexity. Pigment pastes of the invention, which overcome the previous disadvantages, are characterised by a printing medium which comprises, in addition to solvents, 5,6,8-trihydroxy-1,4-naphthoquinone.
</ABSTRACT>
