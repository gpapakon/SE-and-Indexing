<INVENTION-TITLE>
CONTROLLER FOR AUTOMOBILE
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B62D504	B62D504	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B62D	B62D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B62D5	B62D5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A controller for automobile which is provided with a relay (46) which outputs and controls a driving current to a large-current load (40) from a battery (41), a smoothing capacitor (42) which is connected between the load (40) connecting side of the relay (46) and an earth, and a precharging circuit (60) which closes the contact of the relay (46) in a predetermined period of time after a capacitor (42) is charged with electric charges.
</ABSTRACT>
