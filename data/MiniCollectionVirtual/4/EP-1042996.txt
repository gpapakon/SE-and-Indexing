<INVENTION-TITLE>
Stent apparatus
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61F206	A61F206	A61F282	A61F284	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61F	A61F	A61F	A61F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61F2	A61F2	A61F2	A61F2	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
The stent arrangement has a tubular stent (10), a guide wire (17) that pushes a pushing tube (18), to push the stent with respect to the guide wire. A coupling device releasably connects the stent and the pushing tube. The coupling device has a wire loop fastened to the stent, which fits inside a slit (25) in the pushing tube and is blocked by the guide wire.
</ABSTRACT>
