<CLAIMS>
A refillable tube containing paste refillable from 
one of the ends (9;14) of the tube (2;8;37;31) equipped 

with a cylindrical opening of large diameter having an 
internal female thread (3;10;16), in which a cap (4;12) is 

screwed on by means of a male thread (11) located on its 
lower part (35),
 
   characterized in that the female thread (3;10;16) is 

part of a plug (15;25) approximately ring-shaped, which 
enters into the end (9;14) of the tube and which is 

fastened to the filling end (9;14) of the tube by friction 
soldering. 
A refillable tube containing paste in accordance 
with claim 1, characterized in that the plug (15) has a 

flange (19;29) of a width almost equal to the thickness (2) 
of the tube (37;31) in order to limit its sinking in the 

tube. 
A refillable tube containing paste in accordance 
with any one of the preceding claims, characterized in that 

the cap (12) has a neck (12) for dispensing the product, 
such that the filling of the tube and the dispensing of the 

product might be done by the same end (9) of the tube. 
</CLAIMS>
