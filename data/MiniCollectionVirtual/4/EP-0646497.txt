<INVENTION-TITLE>
Box for spiral cable.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B60R1602	B60R1602	B60R16023	B60R16027	H01R3500	H01R3502	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B60R	B60R	B60R	B60R	H01R	H01R	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B60R16	B60R16	B60R16	B60R16	H01R35	H01R35	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Spiral cable box (20) for accommodating a spiral cable (22), especially an electric airbag spiral cable, having a first box part and a second box part which can be rotated with respect to one another and enclose an annular cable storing chamber, the box parts having cable bushings (26, 27) for the guiding to the exterior of the ends (28, 29), which are held in the cable bushings, of the spiral cable (22) from the box and the cable storing chamber being delimited radially from the outside by an outer cylinder wall (25) of the first box part and radially on the inside by an inner cylinder wall (24) of the second box part, the spiral cable box (20) also having an elastic pressing device (33) for holding the spiral cable (22) away from the inner cylinder wall (24) having a resilient device which acts in the region of the cable bushing (26) in the inner cylinder wall (24) on the spiral cable (22), the elastic pressing device (33) being designed to act on the spiral cable (22) on both sides of the cable bushing (26).
