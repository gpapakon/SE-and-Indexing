<INVENTION-TITLE>
Process for coating an object and object coated by this process.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B05D118	B05D118	B05D302	B05D302	C09D17504	C09D17504	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>B05D	B05D	B05D	B05D	C09D	C09D	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B05D1	B05D1	B05D3	B05D3	C09D175	C09D175	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In order, in a process for covering articles with a skin of plastic, in particular with a plastic having a high polyurethane content, a process for structuring such skins and in skins structured in this way and articles covered with a skin, to achieve a significant surface improvement with low environmental pollution and thus in particular to achieve an improvement in the strength and/or keeping quality of the article covered with a skin, it is proposed according to the invention in the process for covering articles with a skin that the article is coated with at least one polyurethane polymer containing free reactive groups dissolved in a solvent and during or preferably after the coating a chemical reaction, in particular a polymerization of the polyurethane polymer, takes place in the skin applied to the article.
