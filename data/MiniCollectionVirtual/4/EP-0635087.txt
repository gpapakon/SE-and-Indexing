<INVENTION-TITLE>
WALL FIXTURE.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>E04B288	E04B296	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>E04B	E04B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>E04B2	E04B2	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A wall fixture (1) is provided for connecting the weather strip (2) to the external wall (3). The wall fixture (1) consists of at least one anchoring member (1.1) and a connection member (1.3). The anchoring member (1.1) passes through the sealing strip (2.1) and is connected intimately and frictionally with the external wall (3) and frictionally with the connection member (1.3) in such a way as to secure a profiled strip (2.3), said strip being designed to clamp and support the weather strip elements located between the external wall (3) and the profiled strip (2.3) with the inserted sealing strips (2.1, 2.2).
</ABSTRACT>
