<INVENTION-TITLE>
Apparatus for relaxing the legs by cooling.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A61F700	A61F700	A61H1500	A61H1500	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A61F	A61F	A61H	A61H	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A61F7	A61F7	A61H15	A61H15	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
- The invention relates to a relaxation apparatus especially for the legs. - According to a preferred embodiment, the apparatus comprises a roller (30) made of hydrophilic material, intended to be applied onto a region of the leg (Ja) to be relaxed and moistened, a liquid tank (5) including at least one orifice facing the roller (30), allowing the liquid to flow, and a ventilation system (1) comprising an electric motor (6) and a fan (7) which blows an air jet (4) onto the moistened region so as to dry it and produce lowering of the surface temperature. The apparatus can be supplied by the mains or by self-contained means: cells or batteries.
