<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a bioelectrical arrangement, with the use of a radiation-sensitive biological material which is embedded in a lyotropic liquid crystal. According to the invention, provision is made for the arrangement to consist of at least one surfactant, of bacterial rhodopsin or membrane parts containing bacteriorhodopsin, and water.
