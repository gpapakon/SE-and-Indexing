<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method for excluding multiple use bottles from their usage circuit.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B07C5342	B07C5342	B65B5702	B65B5702	B65G4308	B65G4308	B65G4734	B65G4742	G01N2188	G01N2190	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>B07C5	B07C5	B65B57	B65B57	B65G43	B65G43	B65G47	B65G47	G01N21	G01N21	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
In order to detect stress cracks in the bottom (2) of PET bottles (1), the bottom is exposed to light. The light scattered on the stress-crack zone is measured by a photodetector (15) which is fixed at an angle with respect to the light incidence direction. The measured value allows good assessment of whether the amount and depth of the stress cracks require removal of the bottle from the recycling process.
