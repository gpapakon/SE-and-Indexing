<INVENTION-TITLE>
Volumetric machine with planetary movement
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The machine comprises a cylindrical piston (1) of axis  DELTA p, which is rotary and situated in a cylindrical capsule (5) of axis DELTA c, the piston (1) having a profile having Sp axes of symmetry, the said capsule (5) defining a hollow volume (4) of which the cross section through a plane perpendicular to its axis  DELTA c, has Sc axes of symmetry, Sp and Sc being different from one another by one, the axes  DELTA p and  DELTA c, which are parallel, being separated by a distance E, the piston (1) and the capsule (5) delimiting between them at least three chambers (A, B, C) and the capsule including at least one suction inlet (9, 10, 11) and one delivery outlet (13, 14, 15) and the machine is characterised in that it comprises a ferromagnetic gear (2, 30) of axis  DELTA p integral with the piston (1) and including Np teeth, located inside a ferromagnetic ring (6, 32) of axis  DELTA c and integral with the capsule (5), the said ring being equipped with NB electric coils (20, 33) arranged radially, in that the ratio Np DIVIDED NB is equal to the ratio Sp DIVIDED Sc, and in that the NB electric coils (20, 33) of the said ring (6, 32) are powered in succession.
