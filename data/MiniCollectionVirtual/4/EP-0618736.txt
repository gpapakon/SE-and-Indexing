<INVENTION-TITLE>
Method and device for scrambling and descrambling of a television transmission.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>H04N7167	H04N7167	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>H04N	H04N	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>H04N7	H04N7	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A receiver device includes a pseudo-random generator (18) initialised by a control word (CW) and a smart card reader (19). According to the invention, it includes means (5) for reading the control word in a table of numbers contained in a read-only memory of a smart card (17) by using one way of doing so from among several different ways, as well as means (6) for receiving from the transmitter a signal indicating which is the choice made at a given instant as far as the said way is concerned. Application to television descrambling a single retransmission of a determined event.
