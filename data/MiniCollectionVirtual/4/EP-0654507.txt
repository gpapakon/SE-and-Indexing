<INVENTION-TITLE>
Pigments containing zirconium oxide and an oxide of cerium, praseodymium and/or terbium, their preparation and their use.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C01G2500	C01G2500	C01G2502	C09C100	C09C100	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C01G	C01G	C01G	C09C	C09C	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C01G25	C01G25	C01G25	C09C1	C09C1	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Coloured pigments of orange-yellow type which are characterised in that they are based on zirconium oxide and on at least one rare earth chosen from cerium, terbium and praseodymium. These pigments can be employed as colouring matter in plastics, paints, varnishes, rubbers, ceramics, glazes, papers, inks, cosmetic products, dyes and laminated coatings.
</ABSTRACT>
