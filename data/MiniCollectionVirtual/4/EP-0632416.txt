Money container for cash-registers

<ABSTRACT>
According to the invention, a banknote holder is to be arranged on cashboxes for cash registers quickly and simply, in such a way that there is no possibility of damage to the banknote holder during transport and stacking. According to the invention, the banknote holder is integrated within the cashbox, the cashbox base part being produced in one piece with the banknote holder by injection moulding.

<CLAIMS>
Cash box for cash registers, with a narrow box base part (2) and 
a closable box lid (8), wherein the box base part (2) is lockable by 

a stationary receiving device, and with a banknote holder (1) 
arranged within the cash box, characterised thereby that the banknote 

holder (1) is integrally connected with the box base part (2). 
Cash box according to claim 1, characterised thereby that the 
box base part (2) is produced together with the banknote holder (1) 

in the injection moulding process. 
Cash box according to claim 1 or 2, characterised thereby that 
the banknote holder (1) is formed by a prolongation of the inner 

front wall (3) of the box base part (2). 
Cash box according to one of claims 1 to 3, characterised 
thereby that a banknote receiving slot (4) is associated with the 

banknote holder (1). 
Cash box according to claim 4, characterised thereby that the 
banknote receiving slot (4) has a lower closure (5). 
Cash box according to claim 4 or 5, characterised thereby that 
the banknote receiving slot (4) is arranged between banknote holder 

(1) and outer front wall (6) of the box base part (2). 
Cash box according to one of claims 1 to 6, characterised 
thereby that the banknote holder (1) projects above the upper front 

boundary of the box base part (2). 
Cash box according to one of claims 1 to 7, characterised 
thereby that the inner front wall (3) of the box base part (2) is 

provided with a respective cut-out (7) laterally of the banknote 
holder (1). 
Cash box according to one of claims 1 to 8, characterised  
 

thereby that at least one clip is associated with the banknote holder 
(1). 
Cash box according to claim 9, characterised thereby that the 
clip is formed by a stamping-out in the box base part (2). 
</CLAIMS>
