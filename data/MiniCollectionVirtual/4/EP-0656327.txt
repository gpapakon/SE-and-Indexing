<INVENTION-TITLE>
Cement for the manufacture of sprayable concrete and process and device for manufacturing sprayable concrete from this cement.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>B28C900	B28C904	C04B1402	C04B1430	C04B2000	C04B2000	C04B2800	C04B2802	</CLASSIFICATIONS>

<ABSTRACT>
For the efficient manufacture of sprayable concrete, use is made of a gypsum-free and anhydrite-free cement with an addition of a mineral, pH-neutral setting accelerator containing aluminium oxide hydroxide (hydrated aluminium oxide), into which the earth-moist aggregates can be mixed.
