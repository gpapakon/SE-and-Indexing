<INVENTION-TITLE>
Computer with a status indicator unit.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>G06F1132	G06F1132	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>G06F	G06F	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>G06F11	G06F11	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
A computer is proposed, in particular a personal computer, having a status indicating unit for indicating status messages with improved fault analysis after a computer failure (outage, malfunction). To this end, the computer is provided with a buffered status monitoring unit (2), which designates and stores a plurality of status messages in accordance with the chronological order of their acquisition, and, in the case of a computer failure, the status monitoring unit (2) indicates the status messages via the status indicating unit (3) in a manual or automatic mode of operation. The invention is used in personal computers.
