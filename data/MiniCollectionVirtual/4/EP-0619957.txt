<INVENTION-TITLE>
Device for adapting a boot to the shape of the foot.
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>A43B500	A43B504	A43B504	A43B514	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>A43B	A43B	A43B	A43B	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>A43B5	A43B5	A43B5	A43B5	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Boot device capable of matching, on the inside, the shape of the foot, particularly at certain points, with a view to protecting said points against possible impacts. It includes at least one leaktight and deformable casing (envelope) (3) inserted in the upper (10) of the said boot (1) opposite the zone or zones of the foot which are to be protected, the said casing (3) communicating with a reservoir (21) containing a fluid product (24), the said leaktight and deformable reservoir (21) being contained in a non-extendable chamber (2) and being capable of being compressed by injection of a gas by a blowing means (23) in the said chamber (2) or in an inflatable casing arranged, inside said chamber (2), in contact with the said reservoir (21).
