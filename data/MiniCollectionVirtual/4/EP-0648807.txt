<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to novel binders which can be cured at elevated temperatures and are compatible with cellulose-containing materials, for example wood, and are suitable for the production of wood materials, such as chipboard. They comprise tannins and weakly acidic compounds.
</ABSTRACT>

characterized in that it contains as curing catalyst an inorganic acid or a substance 

which is acid in aqueous medium with a pka value of more than 7.5.

A binder according to one of claims 1 to 3, characterized in that it contains the
