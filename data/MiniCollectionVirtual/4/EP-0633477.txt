<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a device for the vectorial measurement of UHF signals of like pulsatance, of the six-port junction type, comprising a power divider (10) and a phase-shifting circuit (11), two of the six ports of this device being measurement ports capable of being connected up to two sources of signals to be characterized, the other four ports being connected to four detectors, one of the latter being a matched detector (14) and the other three (12, 13, 15) being unmatched detectors.
