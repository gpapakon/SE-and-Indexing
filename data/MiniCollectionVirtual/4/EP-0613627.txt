Shopping basket
</INVENTION-TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
A number of ribs (20) on its inside wall surfaces have supports (21) for false bottoms (7, 8) inserted through the open top. Position adjustable partitions (9, 9A, 9B) are provided on the false bottoms and/or the bottoms (2) of the basket (1) itself. There can be several supports one above the other on each rib and which are adjustable for height. The false and basket bottoms can have guide rails along which the partitions are adjusted.
</ABSTRACT>

<CLAIMS>
Shopping basket for storing and transporting
household articles and groceries,


having at least one holding hoop or holding handle and
at least one false bottom, which can be inserted from

above into the shopping basket, for dividing the
interior, which false bottom is supported in a height-adjustable

manner on the walls of the shopping basket
and, when required, can be removed from said basket,

characterized
in chat the false bottom (5) has, or its outsides,
movable tongues (6a, 6b, 6c, 6d) in order to adapt the

false bottom to conically formed walls (3) of the
shopping basket (1) without leaving a gap.
Shopping basket according to Claim 1,
characterized in that the false bottom (5) is provided

with flexible supporting straps (9), and in that the
shopping basket (1) has a number of fastening

elements (7), which number corresponds to the number of
supporting straps, on which fastening elements the

supporting straps can be supported in a non-positive
manner.
Shopping basket according to Claim 2,
characterized in that rings or hooks are provided as

fastening elements (7), and in that the supporting
straps (9) are provided with a multiplicity of

openings (10) into which the rings or hooks can be
fitted. 
Shopping basket according to Claim 2,
characterized in that touch-and-close fasteners are

provided as fastening elements (7).
Shopping basket according to Claim 2 or 4,
characterized in that the fastening elements (7) are

provided with clips and/or clamps by means of which the
fastening elements can be clamped/fitted onto the edge

of the basket.
Shopping basket according to one of Claims 1
to 5, characterized in that the false bottom (5) is

provided with cutouts and/or small spatial units for
accommodating eggs.
Shopping basket according to one of Claims 1
to 6, characterized in that at least one partition (11)

is provided which can be locked in various positions on
a false bottom (5) and/or on the container bottom (2).
Shopping basket according to Claim 7,
characterized in that the false bottom (5) has

cutouts (12), and in that the at least one
partition (11) has pegs which correspond to these

cutouts, so that the at least one partition can be
locked in the cutouts by means of these pegs.
Shopping basket according to one of Claims 1
to 8, characterized by a holder, which can be fitted or

placed onto the outside of the shopping basket (1), for
holding goods.
Shopping basket according to one of Claims 1
to 8, characterized by a holder, which can be fitted or

placed onto the outside of the shopping basket (1), for
holding goods, and in that this holder is designed as a

bottle holder for at least two bottles, or as a net.
Shopping basket according to one of Claims 1
to 10, characterized in that the shopping basket has a

pocket (15) at least on one outer side.
Shopping basket according to one of Claims 1
to 11, characterized in that the shopping basket has a

holding device (17, 18) on an outer side for the
fastening of at least one false bottom (5). 
Shopping basket according to one of Claims 1
to 12, characterized in that shopping basket is

provided with wheels or castors and an extendable
holding handle.
</CLAIMS>
