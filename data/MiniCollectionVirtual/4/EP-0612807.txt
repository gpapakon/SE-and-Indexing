<INVENTION-TITLE>
Thermoplastic polyalkylene terephthalate moulding compositions
</INVENTION-TITLE>
<CLASSIFICATIONS>C08L6700	C08L6702	</CLASSIFICATIONS>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>C08L	C08L	</CLASSIFICATIONS-THIRD>
<CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>C08L67	C08L67	</CLASSIFICATIONS-FOURTH>
<ABSTRACT>
Thermoplastic polyalkylene terephthalate moulding compositions prepared by mixing   A) from 99.9 to 80.0 % by weight of one or more thermoplastic polyalkylene terephthalates with   B) from 0.1 to 20.0 % by weight of an epoxy-isocyanurate resin in the melt.
</ABSTRACT>
